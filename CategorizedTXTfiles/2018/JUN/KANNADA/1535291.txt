      Posted On: 13 JUN 2018 4:50PM by PIB Bengaluru


    Afghanistan to make a historic Test Match debut against India in Bengaluru tomorrow; Sports Minister Rathore to inaugurate the fixture


    Afghanistan will make their Test cricket debut against India in Bengaluru tomorrow at the Chinnaswamy stadium. On hand to witness the historic occasion will be Indiaâ€™s Sports Minister, Col. Rajyavardhan Rathore.


    The one-off test match series has been named the Indo-Afghanistan Friendship series, and will be on from 14 th to 18 th June. Afghanistan earned their status as a Test-playing nation in June last year. The series is expected to give a massive boost to cricket in Afghanistan.


    Col. Rathore, who is an Olympian Medallist, is a keen cricket buff. He is also expected to visit other Sports academies in Bengaluru during his stay. Since Col. Rathore is also the I&B Minister, he will be meeting I&B Ministry officers posted in the city.
