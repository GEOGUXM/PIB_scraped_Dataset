      Vice President’s Visit to Kolkata from Tomorrow
      Posted On: 27 JUN 2018 12:50PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, June 27, 2018


    Hon’ble Vice President of India, Shri M Venkaiah Naidu is scheduled to reach Kolkata on a two-day visit tomorrow.


    On Thursday afternoon, Shri Naidu will first visit Saha Institute of Nuclear Physics and interact with scientists. Later, the Vice President will receive books and meet guests at Raj Bhawan.


    On June 29, 2018, the Vice President will be present as the Chief Guest at the 12 th Statistical Day and closing ceremony of 125 th birth anniversary of Prof. P. C. Mahalanobis in the morning. This programme will be held at Indian Statistical Institute. In the afternoon, he will leave the city for New Delhi.
