      Ministry of Home Affairs

      CELEBRATION OF 4TH INTERNATIONAL DAY OF YOGA
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2018 5:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, Date: 21 June 2018


    On the occasion of 4 th International Day of Yoga today, Headquarters, South Bengal Frontier, Border Security Force organized Yoga activities in the morning at BSF Camp Digberia, Badu Road, Madhyamgram (North 24 Parganas) West Bengal as per Common Yoga Protocol (CYP) under Ministry of AYUSH. The event was inaugurated by Dr. Rajesh Mishra, IPS, Inspector General in presence of Shri A S Kathat, Dy Inspector General South Bengal Frontier BSF, Ms Usha Kiran, Dy Inspector General of CRPF and other senior official from CISF, SSB, NSG. Approximately 1000 Officers, Subordinate Officers and other ranks of CAPFs comprising BSF, NDRF, CRPF, CISF, SSB, NSG attended the Yoga session with great enthusiasm and spirit.


    Dr. Rajesh Mishra, IPS, Chief Guest of the event, thanked all the participants from different Forces for enthusiastically participating in grand event held on the occasion of International Yoga Day. All CAPF personnel took pledge to perform yoga regularly for a healthy body and mind.
