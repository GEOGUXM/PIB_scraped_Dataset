      Ministry of External Affairs

      Passport Mela on Saturday
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2018 3:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, June 25, 2018.


    Regional Passport Office under Ministry of External Affairs has decided to organize Passport Mela on June 30, 2018 at the Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Head Post Office, Barrackpore, for the benefit of passport seeking applicants residing in district of North 24 Parganas.


    Applicants are requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original & self attested xerox copy).


    The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in .


    Applications in Normal category (Fresh/Re-issue) will be entertained in the Mela and appointments for June 30, 2018 will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.in on June 26, 2018 from 3.30 PM onwards.


    The applications have to pay the passport fees through online payment system. Maximum 50 appointments will be entertained in the mela.
