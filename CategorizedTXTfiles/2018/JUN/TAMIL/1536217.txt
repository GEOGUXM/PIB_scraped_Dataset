      PIB Headquarters

      DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2018 6:30PM by PIB Chennai


    It is proposed to conduct “DAK ADALAT” at divisional level on 29.06.2018 at Office of the Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Chennai City Central Division, Sivagnanam Road, Chennai 600 017 at 03.30 p.m. The public may forward the complaints pertaining to this Division to the undersigned by furnishing the information such as date of booking of Registered letter, Parcel, Insured, VP and Money orders, Office of booking, address of both sender and addressee. Complaints, if any relating to Savings Bank accounts and Cash certificates etc., may also be sent. They may attend the Dak Adalat on 29.06.2018 at 03.30 p.m.


    The delivery areas of the following Post offices come under the jurisdiction of this Division: T.Nagar, Mylapore, Greames Road, Tiruvallikkeni, Royapettah, Teynampet, Nungambakkam, Gopalapuram and Choolaimedu.
