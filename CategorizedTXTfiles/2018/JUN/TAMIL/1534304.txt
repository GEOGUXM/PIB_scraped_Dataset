      PIB Headquarters

      Press Release
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2018 6:25PM by PIB Chennai


    It was notified last week that due to migration to new software, 101 post offices under Tambaram Postal division covering the areas of Tambaram, Avadi, Ambattur, Tiruvottiyur, Ponneri, Gummidipoondi etc. will not function of 2 nd June & 4 th June 2018 to facilitate successful migration.


    Now it is informed that due to Administrative reasons the above migration has been postponed and it is intimated that all the Post Offices will function as usual and all the transactions will be accepted during the working hours of the above post offices on 02.06.2018 & 04.06.2018
