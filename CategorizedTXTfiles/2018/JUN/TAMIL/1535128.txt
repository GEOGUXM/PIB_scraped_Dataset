      Dak Adalat
      Posted On: 12 JUN 2018 2:41PM by PIB Chennai


    “Dak Adalat” will be conducted by the Senior Superintendent of Post offices, Chennai City North Division at the office premises at No. 5 (Old No.3), IV Floor, Ethiraj Salai, Egmore, Chennai – 600 008 at 12.00 hours on 20.06.2018 (Wednesday) .


    The members of the public are requested to send their unsettled grievances / pending complaints relating to postal services in respect of post offices under the administrative jurisdiction of Chennai City North Division on or before 18.06.2018.
