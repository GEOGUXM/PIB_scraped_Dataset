      Diploma Programmes offered at CIPET: IPT - CHENNAI
      Posted On: 13 JUN 2018 7:03PM by PIB Chennai


    Central Institute of Plastics Engineering & Technology (CIPET) is an ISO 9001:2008 QMS certified, NABL & NABCB accredited premier national Institution functioning the Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers, Govt. of India fully devoted to Skill Development, Technology Support, Academic & Research (STAR) activities for the growth of Polymer & allied industries in the country. CIPET operates on hub & spokes model with 40 locations spread across the country.


    CIPET centre at Chennai is the first institute established by Government of India in the year 1968. CIPET Chennai offers Post Graduate. Under Graduate, Diploma, Post Graduate Diploma as well as Vocational Skill Development training programs in the niche areas of Plastics Engineering & Technology to fulfill the human resource requirements of polymer and allied industries in the country.
