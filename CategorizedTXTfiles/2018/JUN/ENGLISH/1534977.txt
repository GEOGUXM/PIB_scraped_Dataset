      Vice President undergoes a Cataract surgery
      Posted On: 10 JUN 2018 1:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has undergone a successful Cataract surgery at L.V. Prasad Eye Institute (LVPEI), in Hyderabad today. Dr. Pravin Krishna Vaddavalli and his team conducted the surgery and was discharged thereafter.


    He was advised complete rest for 03 days and also as a precaution visitors were advised not to visit during this period.
