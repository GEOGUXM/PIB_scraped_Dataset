      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 07 JUN 2018 1:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri KeshariNathTripathi, Governor of West Bengal to discharge the functions of the Governor of Tripura, in addition to his own duties, during the absence on leave of Shri Tathagata Roy, Governor of Tripura.
