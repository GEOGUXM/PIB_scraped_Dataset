      PM to visit SantKabir Nagar on June 28, 2018
      Posted On: 27 JUN 2018 12:43PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will visit Maghar in SantKabir Nagar district of Uttar Pradesh on June 28, 2018.


    The Prime Minister will offer floral tributes at SantKabir Samadhi, on the occasion of the 500 th death anniversary of the great saint and poet, Kabir. He will offer Chadar at SantKabirMazaar.


    The Prime Minister will visit the SantKabir Cave. He will unveil a plaque to mark the laying of Foundation Stone of Kabir Academy, which will highlight the great saintâ€™s teachings and thought.


    Later, at a public meeting in Maghar, the Prime Minister will witness a cultural programme and address the gathering.
