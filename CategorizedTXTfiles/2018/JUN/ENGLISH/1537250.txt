      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      President summoned Rajya Sabha from 18th July 2018
      Posted On: 30 JUN 2018 6:36PM by PIB Delhi


    The President has summoned the Rajya Sabha to meet on Wednesday, the 18 th July, 2018 at New Delhi. Subject to exigencies of business, the Session is scheduled to conclude on Friday, the 10 th August, 2018.
