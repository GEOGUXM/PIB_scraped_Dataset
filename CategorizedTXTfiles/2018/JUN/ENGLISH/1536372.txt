      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Three day Photo Exhibition on ‘Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao campaign, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and Khelo India Khelo’inaugurated in Chandigarh
      
      Photo Journalists play a significant role in depicting the reality in a natural way :The Union Minister Shri Vijay Sampla

      Posted On: 23 JUN 2018 6:26PM by PIB Delhi

    The Union Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment, Shri Vijay Sampla stressed the need to inspire more girls to take up the field of Photo Journalism as the Photo Journalists play a significant role in depicting the reality in a natural way.


    He was addressing the Photo Journalists after inauguratinga three day Photo Exhibition on ‘Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao campaign, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and Khelo India Khelo’ in Chandigarh today organised by The Photo Journalists Welfare Association. He congratulated the organisers of Photo Exhibition for highlighting these issues along with their press photography duties which are too demanding. He also urged them to motivate more girls to enter this enterprising field of Photo Journalism which is full of challenges as well as opportunities.


    The president of The Photo Journalists Welfare Association, Shri Akhilesh Mishra, spoke about the activities undertaken by the association in highlighting the issues of social concern.


    As many as 88 Photographs of 49 Photo Journalists are displayed in the exhibition from all over India and abroad.


    The Union Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment, Shri Vijay Sampla also interacted with Media persons .
