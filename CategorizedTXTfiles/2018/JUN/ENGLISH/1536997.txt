      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to dedicate and lay foundation stone for key projects in AIIMS
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2018 3:54PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will visit the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) in New Delhi on June 29, 2018.


    He will lay the Foundation Stone of the National Centre for Aging at AIIMS. This will provide the older population with multi-speciality healthcare. It will have 200 general ward beds.


    During the same event, the Prime Minister will inaugurate the 555-bed Super Speciality Block in Safdarjung Hospital. He will also dedicate the 500-bed New Emergency Block in Safdarjung Hospital; the 300-bed PowerGrid Vishram Sadan in AIIMS; and the Connection Motorable Tunnel between AIIMS, Ansari Nagar and Trauma Centre.
