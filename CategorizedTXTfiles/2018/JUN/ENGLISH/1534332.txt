      Ministry of Education

      Union HRD Minister felicitates 67 meritorious students of class XII at Gunn Gaurav Samman Samaroh- 2018
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2018 8:18PM by PIB Delhi


      The Union Minister of Human Resource Development, Shri Prakash Javadekar today felicitated 67 students of class XII who topped in different categories from Delhi and NCR. These students belonged to KVs, JNVs, government, private and open school, varied social strata and students with special needs and have scored more than 82% marks and above in class XII 2018 exam results . The programme signifies the effort of the government to bring the meritorious students to the main stream and provide encouragement.


      Gunn Gaurav Samman Samaroh organised today by CBSE under the aegis of MHRD is the 3rd consecutive event of this nature which felicitates meritorious students from diverse backgrounds. The programme is an acknowledgement of the consistent effort and merit of the students who have demonstrated the grit and determination to overcome hurdles in achieving high academic performance.


      During interactions with the students, Shri Javadekar appreciated the extra ordinary effort put in by each student and saluted their grit and determination. He advised the students to maintain consistency , focus, and discipline to reach up to their goals. He also said that education is not simply mugging up and writing it down, rather it is the development of ability to analyze , comprehend and communicate. He further said that it is very important to give space to value education, skills , experimental learning , and physical education.


      The Minister also said that this felicitation programme is unique in a way as it promotes meritorious students from all sections of the society. It promotes Equality in education and social justice also. Our priority is to improve the quality of education in government schools so that people get access to finest education with minimal fees, Shri Prakash Javadekar added.


      There were 5 toppers from Delhi-NCR (including Private Independent schools), 5 students from Kendriya Vidyalaya (Delhi-NCR), 6 students from Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (Delhi-NCR), 5 SC/ST category and 6 students from Children with Special Needs Categories, 16 students from Lower Income group and 7 students from Delhi Government Schools and 12 students from NIOS. These students were identified on the basis of the performance and income criteria as recommended by the internal committee of CBSE.


      Smt. Rina Ray, Special Secretary (SE&L), Smt. Anita Karwal, Chairperson CBSE, and other senior officials of the MHRD, KVS, JNV and CBSE were also present on the occasion.
