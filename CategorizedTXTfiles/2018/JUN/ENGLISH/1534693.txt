      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister on a two-day visit to J&K from 7th June
      Posted On: 06 JUN 2018 7:09PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Home Minister Shri Rajnath Singh will be on a two-day visit to Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) from tomorrow i.e. 7 th June, 2018.


    During his visit, Shri Rajnath Singh will meet the Chief Minister of J&K Ms Mehbooba Mufti. He will review security arrangements for Shri Amarnath Ji Yatra - 2018.


    The Home Minister will also hold meetings with various delegations during the visit.
