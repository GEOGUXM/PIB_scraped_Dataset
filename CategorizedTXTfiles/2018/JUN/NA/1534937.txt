      Ministry of Defence

      IAF Jaguar Incident
      Posted On: 08 JUN 2018 8:16PM by PIB Delhi


    On 08 th Jun 2018, at around 0930 hrs at Air Force Station Jamnagar an incident involving a Jaguar aircraft happened while on the landing roll. Aircraft sustained minor damages with no injuries to the pilot after he ejected safely. A Court of Inquiry has been ordered to ascertain the cause of the incident.
