      Prime Minister's Office

      PM visits former PM Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee at AIIMS
      Posted On: 11 JUN 2018 9:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, visited former Prime Minister Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee at AIIMS today.


    The Prime Minister interacted with family members of Shri Vajpayee. He also spoke to Doctors and inquired about the health of Shri Vajpayee.


    Shri Narendra Modi was at AIIMS for about 50 minutes.
