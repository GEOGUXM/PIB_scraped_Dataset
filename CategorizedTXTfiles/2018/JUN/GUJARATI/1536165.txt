      International Yoga Day Celebration at HPCL
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2018 4:39PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 21-06-2018


    International Day of Yoga, is celebrated annually on 21 June since its inception in 2015. 4 th International yoga day was observed and celebrated across the various locations of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited. Large number of employees participated by doing Yog-Asanas at locations spread across the length and breadth of the country.
