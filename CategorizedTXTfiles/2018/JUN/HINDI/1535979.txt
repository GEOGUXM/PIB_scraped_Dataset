      KVIC adopts Jagatpur village in Delhi for Swachhata Abhiyaan
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2018 12:37PM by PIB Delhi


      Khadi and Village Industries Commission leading a sanitation drive in Delhi


      The Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) has adopted Jagatpur village in Delhi for its Swachhata Abhiyaan. The sanitation drive by KVIC began with a plantation drive and ended with cleanliness drive along the bank of the Yamuna river.


      Speaking to the villagers, KVIC Chairman, Vinai Kumar Saxena said that he was enthused with their response. He said that with such enthusiastic participation of the public, Jagatpur is destined to be one of the most beautiful villages in Delhi and NCR.
