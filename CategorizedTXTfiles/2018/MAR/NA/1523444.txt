      President's Secretariat

      Press Comunique
      Posted On: 09 MAR 2018 12:28PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, as advised by the Prime Minister, has accepted the resignation of the following Ministers from the Union Council of Ministers, with immediate effect, under clause (2) of Article 75 of the Constitution :-

     1. Shri Ashok Gajapathi Raju Pusapati, and
     2. Shri Y.S. Chowdary

    Further, as advised by the Prime Minister, The President has directed that the work of the Ministry of Civil Aviation will be looked after by the Prime Minister.
