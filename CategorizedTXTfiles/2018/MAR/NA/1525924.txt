      Ministry of Shipping

      Krishnapatnam Harbour
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2018 4:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of India has notified Krishnapatnam Seaport in Andhra Pradesh as an authorized Immigration Check Post w.e.f08 February, 2018 for entry into/ exit from India with valid travel documents for all classes of passengers.


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Shipping Shri Mansukh L. Mandaviya in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
