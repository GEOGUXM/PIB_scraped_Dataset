      President’s Ram Navami Greetings
      Posted On: 24 MAR 2018 6:13PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of Ram Navami has said: -


    “On the occasion of Ram Navami, I extend my greetings and best wishes to all my fellow citizens.


    Lord Rama’s life is a message in itself. A message of adhering to the values of righteousness, treading the path of truth, devotion to duty, restraint and selfless service. Let us embrace these moral values and make the world beautiful, clean and joyful with our good deeds.”
