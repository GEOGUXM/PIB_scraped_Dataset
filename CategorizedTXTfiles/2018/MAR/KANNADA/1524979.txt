    “ Swachhata Symposium” inaugurated by Sh. Hardeep Singh Puri, Hon’ble Union Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (Independent Charge)


    Bangalore, Dt. 17.03.2018


    Shri Hardeep Singh Puri, Hon’ble Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs inaugurated the “ Swachhata Symposium” in Bangalore today.


    The Minister had interaction with Swachhagrahis who have done exemplary work in the area of ‘swachhata’. A formal launch by MoHUA of an innovative mobile-based Municipal Solid Waste Collection & Transportation App, development in partnership with Minid tree, and a detailed Project Report (DPR) planning tool, developed in partnership with GIZ was also organized.


    The Bruhat Bengalure Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) also shared a brief overview of Memorandum of Understanding (MOUs) signed by them with companies from France and Netherlands, for waste to energy projects.


    An exhibition to showcase various best practices in sanitation and solid waste management in various ULBs of Karnataka was also setup during the event.


    Shri Mahendra Jain, Addl.Chief Secretary,UDD,GOK,Shri Vinod Kumar Jindal, Jt.Secy & MD,SBM(U) and Many Senior officers were present during the function.
