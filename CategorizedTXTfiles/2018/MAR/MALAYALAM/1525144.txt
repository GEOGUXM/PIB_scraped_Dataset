      Summer Vacation For C.A.T
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2018 4:59PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT), Ernakulam Bench will remain closed from 16 st April to 10 th May, 2018 on account of Summer Vacation. During the vacation period, the Bench of Administrative Member Shri E.K. Bharat Bhushan will hold sittings on 18 th April, 25 th April and 02 nd May 2018 at 11.00 a.m. to hear urgent and such other matters as the Bench may direct. The Registry of the Tribunal will function as usual, but only the applications of urgent orders will be received from 10.30 am to 1.00 pm during the aforesaid period of vacation.
