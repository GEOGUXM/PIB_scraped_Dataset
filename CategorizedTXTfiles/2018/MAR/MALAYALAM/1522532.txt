      REGIONAL DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2018 4:43PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A Regional ‘‘DAK ADALAT'' of Central Postal Region will be held at the Office of the Post Master General, Central Region, Gandhi Nagar(Maveli Road), Kochi on 13 th March, 2018 at 11.00 am. Complaints relating to Postal and Counter Services, Savings Bank, Money Order etc. will be taken up in the Adalat.


    Cases already taken up and settled in previous Dak Adalats will not be entertained. Complaints can be sent in envelopes super scribed “Regional Dak Adalat: March 2018” and addressed to “ Smt. B. Sudha, Assistant Director(PG), Office of the Postmaster General, Central Region, Kochi-682020,” so as to reach there on or before 09 th March, 2018.
