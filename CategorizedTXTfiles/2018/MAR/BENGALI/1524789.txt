      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      New DG takes over in PIB Kolkata
      Posted On: 16 MAR 2018 2:18PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, February 1, 2018


    Shri Anil Kumar Saxena has taken over as Director General, Press Information Bureau, Kolkata today. Prior to this, he was in PIB Delhi in different assignments. He is an 1984 batch Indian Information Service officer.
