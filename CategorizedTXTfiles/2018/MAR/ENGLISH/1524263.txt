      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of Professor Stephen Hawking
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2018 12:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Professor Stephen Hawking.


    “Professor Stephen Hawking was an outstanding scientist and academic. His grit and tenacity inspired people all over the world. His demise is anguishing. Professor Hawking’s pioneering work made our world a better place. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
