      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Department Related Parliamentary Standing Committee on Commerce Invites Memoranda on the subject ‘Impact of Chinese Goods on Indian Industry’
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2018 6:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Department Related Parliamentary Standing Committee on Commerce headed by Shri Naresh Gujral, M.P. , Rajya Sabha has taken up the subject ‘Impact of Chinese Goods on Indian Industry’ for detailed examination and has, accordingly, decided to invite memoranda there on within 15 days.


    Those desirous of submitting memoranda or giving oral evidence before the Committee may visit the website of the Rajya Sabha ( http://rajyasabha.nic.in ) under the link “Committees” for details. All correspondence may be sent to Shri Narendra Kumar, Additional Director, Rajya Sabha Secretariat, Room No. 535, Parliament House Annexe, New Delhi-110001 (Tel: 011-23034057, FAX: 011-21410347), E-mail: narendra.rs@sansad.nic.in/ rsc-comm@sansad.nic.in .
