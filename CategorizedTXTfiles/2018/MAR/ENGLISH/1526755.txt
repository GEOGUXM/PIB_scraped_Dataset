      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Corruption Charges
      Posted On: 28 MAR 2018 3:23PM by PIB Delhi


    No consolidated data is maintained by this Department for the number of cases pertaining to charges of disproportionate assets against top officials of the entire Government of India. However, as per available information, sanction for prosecution on the specific charge of disproportionate assets has been issued against 01 Indian Administrative Service officer in the last three years.


    Prosecution Sanction under Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 has been issued against 03 Indian Police Service officers in the last three years.
