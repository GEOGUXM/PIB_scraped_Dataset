      Ministry of Finance

      Merger of State-owned General Insurance Companies
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2018 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Minister of Finance in his Budget Speech for 2018-19 announced that the t hree Public Sector General Insurance Companies viz. National Insurance Company Limited, United India Insurance Company Limited and Oriental India Insurance Company Limited will be merged into a Single Insurance Entity and will be subsequently listed. The merger is expected to lead to effective utilization of infrastructure and improved solvency margin.
