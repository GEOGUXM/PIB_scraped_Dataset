      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Raising of New Battalions in BSF and ITBP
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2018 5:32PM by PIB Delhi


    Out of the proposed 15 additional battalions (6 Bns of BSF and 9 Bns of ITBP), the Government has sanctioned raising of 6 additional battalions to BSF on 19.01.2018 and the proposal for raising of 09 additional battalions in ITBP is under consideration.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Kiren Rijiju in written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
