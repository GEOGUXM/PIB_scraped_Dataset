      Prime Minister's Office

      PM extends his greetings on Ram Navami
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2018 10:41AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted people on the occasion of Ram Navami.


    PM said, " राम नवमी के पावन अवसर पर सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं। Greetings on Ram Navami."
