      Ministry of Shipping

      Transportation of Goods through Waterways
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2018 6:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The total quantity of cargo transported on National Waterways (NWs) since 2008-09 upto January, 2018 is 134.61 million tonnes. There has been a gradual increase in the overall quantity of cargo transported on NWs in the last few years. The year wise details are given below­­: -
