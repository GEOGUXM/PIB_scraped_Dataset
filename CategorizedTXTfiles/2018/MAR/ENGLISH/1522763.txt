      President of India to address annual convocation of Aligarh Muslim University tomorrow
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2018 5:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Uttar Pradesh (Aligarh) tomorrow (March 7, 2018) to address the annual convocation of the Aligarh Muslim University.
