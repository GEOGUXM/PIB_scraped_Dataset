      Czech Minister of Industry & Trade calls on Union Minister of Railways & Coal, Shri Piyush Goyal
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2018 5:23PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of Industry and Trade of the Czech Republic, Tomas Huner today called on Union Minister of Railways & Coal Shri Piyush Goyal in New Delhi today. The Czech Minister was accompanied by Ambassador of the Czech Republic to India Milan Hovorka, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic Miloslav Stašek, Senior Director General, Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Czech Richard Hlavatý and Vice President, Czech Chamber of Commerce Borivoj Minar.


    The two sides had wide ranging discussions on many specific areas of mutual interest in rail sector and deepening collaboration and strengthening ties on technical cooperation. The Czech delegation wanted to get insights, suggestions & to particularly discuss the role of the Czech Companies in enhancing of trade exchange and Mutual Corporation with India.
