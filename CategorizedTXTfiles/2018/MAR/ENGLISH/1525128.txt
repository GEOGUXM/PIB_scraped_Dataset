      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      All India Civil Services Powerlifting, Weightlifting and Best Physique Tournament to be organised from 23rd -26th March
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2018 3:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Civil Services Cultural & Sports Board (CCSCSB) is organising All India Civil Services Powerlifting, Weightlifting and Best Physique Tournament 2017-18 at Grih Kalyan Kendra, Raja Bazar, Near Gole Market, New Delhi. The Tournament will be held from 23 rd March to 26 th March 2018. Last date of receipt of entry is 21 st March, 2018.


    It is expected that a total of 25 teams from across the country are likely to participate. As per AICS rules, only civilian employees of the Central Government and regular State Government employees may participate in this tournament.


    The aim of holding such tournament is to give an opportunity to the civil servants to meet and know each other and compete on the field of play. The conduct of such tournament also strives to create awareness among the employees about the virtue of physical fitness and the need to release stress and tension of the present day fast paced life through sports activities.
