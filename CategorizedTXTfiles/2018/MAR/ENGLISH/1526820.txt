      Ministry of Tourism

      14 States/UTs have deployed ‘Tourist Police’: Tourism Minister
      Posted On: 28 MAR 2018 5:05PM by PIB Delhi


    At Present, 14 States have deployed ‘Tourist Police’ in one form or the other. The States are Delhi, Goa, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, Odisha, Punjab, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.


    Ministry of Tourism does not have any scheme for providing financial assistance to States/UTs for deploying ‘Tourist Police’.


    This information was given by Shri K. J. Alphons, Union Minister of State (I/C) for Tourism in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
