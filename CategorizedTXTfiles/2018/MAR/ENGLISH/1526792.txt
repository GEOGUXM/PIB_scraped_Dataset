      Ministry of Finance

      Statement on quarterly of the trends in receipts and expenditure of 3rd quarter of Financial Year -2017-18
      Posted On: 28 MAR 2018 4:08PM by PIB Delhi


    The Statement on quarterly review of the trends in receipts and expenditure in relation to the Budget at the end of 3 rd Quarter of Financial Year 2017-18 as required under Section 7(1) of the Act has been laid in Rajya Sabha today on 28 th March, 2018.


    The Statement has been uploaded on Department of Economic Affairs Website.
