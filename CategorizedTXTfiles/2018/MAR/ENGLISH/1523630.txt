      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets CISF personnel on the Raising Day of CISF
      Posted On: 10 MAR 2018 10:25AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the CISF personnel on theRaising Day of the Central Industrial Security Force.


    “Greetings and best wishes toCentral Industrial Security Forceon their Raising Day. CISF has distinguished itself by effectively catering to the security needs of vital establishments, many of which are driving India’s resurgence as well as connecting the nation”,the Prime Minister said.
