      Ministry of Shipping

      V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust creats New Record inSingle Day Handling at North Cargo Berth - I
      Posted On: 21 MAR 2018 5:58PM by PIB Delhi


    V O Chidambaranar Port Trust created a new record by handling 48,701 Metric Tonnes of Thermal Coal in a day at North Cargo Berth-I from the vessel MVVishva Nidhi on 20 th March, 2018, surpassing the previous Single Day record of 42,508 Metric Tonnes of Thermal Coal at North Cargo Berth -I from the vessel MV Pangea on 14 th January this year. M/s. Sical Logistics Ltd., were the Steamer and Stevedore Agent.


    North Cargo Berth -I, the captive berth of M/s. Neyveli Thermal Power Plant has handled 3.28Million Tonnes of Thermal Coal during the financial year 2016-17 and 3.11 Million Tonnes of Thermal Coal during the current financial year up to February, 2018.
