      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Rs. 44830.4 lakh funds released to the State Government of Chhattisgarh under Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub Schemefrom 2014-15 to 2017-18
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2018 3:03PM by PIB Delhi


    A total of Rs. 44830.4 lakh has been released to the State Government of Chhattisgarh under Special Central Assistance (SCA) to Tribal Sub Scheme (TSS) from 2014-15 to 2017-18. The year-wise details can be seen as under:

                                             
S. No.  Year      Fund released (Rs. in lakh)
                                             
                                             
                                             
1.      2014-15   10778.00                   
                                             
                                             
                                             
2.      2015-16   11904.31                   
                                             
                                             
                                             
3.      2016-17   10488.52                   
                                             
                                             
                                             
4.      2017-18   11659.57                   
                                             
                                             
                
Total   44830.40
                
                

    This information was given by Minister of State for Tribal Affairs Shri JaswantsinhBhabhor in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
