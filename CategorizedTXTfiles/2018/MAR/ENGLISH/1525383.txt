      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Complaints against fake Scheduled Castes Certificates
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2018 5:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The National Commission for Scheduled Castes (NCSC) is investigating the complaints as per the duties assigned to it by Article 338(5) of the Constitution.


    Accordingly, the Commission sends its recommendations to the concerned authorities wherever deemed necessary.
