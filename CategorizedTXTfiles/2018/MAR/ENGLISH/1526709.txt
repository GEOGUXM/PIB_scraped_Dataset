      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Full utilization of Funds for Schemes implemented through VOs for welfare of STs
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2018 8:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The budget allocation for the financial year 2016-17 for the following schemes implemented through Voluntary Organizations (VOs) for undertaking welfare of STs was Rs. 120.00 Crore and 100% budget allocation was utilized for the purpose: -


    (1) Grant-in-aid to voluntary organizations working for the welfare of Scheduled Tribes,


    (2) Strengthening Education among ST Girls in Low Literacy Districts


    (3) Vocational Training in Tribal Areas


    NGO run projects funded by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs are considered based upon the annual inspection carried by the District Authorities. Further, to ensure proper utilization of funds by the NGOs / VOs, the projects / schemes, Scrutiny of proposals by multidisciplinary State Level Committee every Year is being done. Besides, Mandatory submission of Audited statements of accounts as well as Utilization Certificate pertaining to the releases and field inspections by Officers from State Government / Central Government is being done. In addition to the above, the accounts of grantee institutions are open for inspection by representatives/officers from the office of Comptroller and Auditor General of India, or concerned State Govt. at any time.


    This information was given today by the Union Minister of Tribal Affairs Shri. Jastwantsinh Bhabhor today in Lok Sabha.
