      PM salutes women power on Women's Day
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2018 12:45PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has saluted women power on Women's Day.


    PM said, "Salute to Women Power on Women's Day. We are proud of the achievments of women. India is moving from women development to women-led development."
