      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of renowned Sufi singer Shri Pyarelal Wadali
      Posted On: 09 MAR 2018 3:12PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of the renowned Sufi singer, Shri Pyarelal Wadali.


    “Saddened by the demise of Shri Pyarelal Wadali. His renditions attained great popularity globally. His work will continue to draw people towards the joys of Sufi music. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
