      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Rs. 4502 crores released in the last four years to State Governments/UTs under the scheme Post-Matric Scholarship for ST students
      
      5992172 beneficiaries of the scheme from 2014-15 to 2016-17
      
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2018 4:27PM by PIB Delhi


    The Scheme ‘Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Tribe Students’ is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme being implemented by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs through the State Governments/UT Administrations. The details of the funds released to State Governments/UT Administrations is as under:

                     
Year   Funds Released
                     
                     
       (Rs. in lakh) 
                     
                     
                     
Total  450238.25     
                     
                     

    The total number of beneficiaries under the scheme from 2014-15 to 2016-17 is 5992172.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Tribal Affairs Shri Jaswantsinh Bhabhor in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
