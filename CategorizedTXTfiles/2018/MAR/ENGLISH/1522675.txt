      Prime Minister's Office

      PM expresses grief on loss of lives due to accident in Bihar
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2018 2:09PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has expressed his grief on loss of lives in road accident in Bihar.


    PM said, "Saddened by the loss of lives due to a road accident in Araria, Bihar. My thoughts are with the families of the deceased. I pray for the quick recovery of those injured."
