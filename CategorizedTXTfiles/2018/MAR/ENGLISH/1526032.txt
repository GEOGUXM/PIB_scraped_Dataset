      PM pays tributes to Bhagat Singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev on their martyrdom day
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2018 9:51AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Bhagat Singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev on the day of their martyrdom.


    “The martyrdom of Bhagat Singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev was a watershed moment in our history. Every Indian is proud that these three great men belong to our land. At the peak of their youth they sacrificed their lives so that others can live a life of freedom and dignity”, the Prime Minister said.
