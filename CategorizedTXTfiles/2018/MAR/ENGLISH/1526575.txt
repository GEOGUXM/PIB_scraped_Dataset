    The main reasons for not achieving 100% full immunization coverage are lack of awareness for benefit of Immunization, apprehensions of minor Adverse Effect Following Immunization (AEFI), child travelling, programme gaps, refusal for vaccination etc.


    The Minister of State (Health and Family Welfare), ShAshwini Kumar Choubey stated this in a written reply in the RajyaSabha here today.
