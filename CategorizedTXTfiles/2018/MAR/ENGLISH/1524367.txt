      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Construction of Bunkers along LOC
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2018 5:34PM by PIB Delhi


    In order to mitigate the hardships being faced by people living on the International Border/LoC due to Cross Border Firing, the Ministry of Home Affairs had approved a pilot project for construction of 60 bunkers at a cost of Rs. 3 crore during the year 2016-17 which has since been completed. Recently, following the visit of Home Minister, the Government of India has approved construction of another 14460 bunkers at a cost of Rs.415.73 crore, the details of which are given in the Table.

    The cost of construction of these Bunkers will be reimbursed to the State Government.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
