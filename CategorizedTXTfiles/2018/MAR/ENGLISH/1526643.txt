      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Digitization of Targeted Public Distribution System
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2018 6:31PM by PIB Delhi


    In order to plug the leakages and bring about greater efficiency and transparency in the Public Distribution System, Government is implementing a scheme on ‘End to end computerisation of TPDS Operations’. The scheme comprises digitization of ration cards/beneficiaries, online allocation, computerisation of supply chain management, etc. As reported by States/UTs, digitization of all 23.2 crore ration cards having more than 80 crore beneficiaries has been completed in all States/UTs.


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri C.R. Chaudhary in a written reply in the Lok Sabha, today.
