      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Funds to Assam
      Posted On: 21 MAR 2018 5:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region is providing funds to Government of Assam under the Special Packages of Rs.750.00 crore for Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC), Rs.350.00 crore for Karbi Anglong Autonomous Territorial Council (KAATC) and Rs.200.00 crore for Dima Hasao Autonomous Territorial Council (DHATC).
