      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Actual Expenditure with respect to National Commission for Scheduled Tribes from 2014-15 to 2016-17 was Rs. 21.64 crore
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2018 3:03PM by PIB Delhi


    The total expenditure with respect to National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST) from 2014-15 to 2016-17 was Rs. 21.64 crore. The year wise break-up can be seen as under:

                                 
Year     Actual Expenditure (Rs.)
                                 
                                 
                                 
2014-15  6,18,27,500             
                                 
                                 
                                 
2015-16  6,29,22,176             
                                 
                                 
                                 
2016-17  9,16,88,967             
                                 
                                 
                                 
Total    21,64,38,643            
                                 
                                 

    This information was given by Minister of State for Tribal Affairs Shri Jaswantsinh Bhabhor in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
