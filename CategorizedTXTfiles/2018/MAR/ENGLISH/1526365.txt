      President of India to visit Uttar Pradesh tomorrow
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2018 11:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind will visit Varanasi ( Uttar Pradesh) tomorrow (March 26, 2018) to lay foundation stone of five projects of the National Highway Authority of India and distribute appointment letters to candidates selected during a job fair organised by the Vyavasaik Shiksha Evam Kaushal Vikas Vibhag of the Government of Uttar Pradesh. The President will also receive the first copy of the Sanskrit translation of the book ‘Charaiveti! Charaiveti!!’, written by Shri Ram Naik, Governor of Uttar Pradesh, before returning to Delhi.
