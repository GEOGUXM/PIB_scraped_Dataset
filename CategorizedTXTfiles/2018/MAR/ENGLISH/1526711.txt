      Ministry of Finance

      CBDT extends the date for linking of Aadhaar with PAN till 30th June, 2018
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2018 9:33PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) had allowed time till 31 st March, 2018 to link PAN with Aadhaar while filing the Income Tax Returns. Upon consideration of the matter,

      ​the ​CBDT further extends the time for linking PAN with Aadhaar till 30 th June, 2018.
