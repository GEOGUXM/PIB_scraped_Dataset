      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique-Governor of Bihar gets additional charge of Governor of Odisha
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2018 12:10PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri Satya Pal Malik, Governor of Bihar to discharge the functions of the Governor of Odisha, in addition to his own duties, until regular arrangements for the office of the Governor of Odisha is made vice Shri S.C. Jamir, who is completing his tenure on March 20, 2018.
