      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Increase in Budgetary Allocation for Ministry of Tribal Affairs over last 4 consecutive years
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2018 6:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Budgetary allocation for the Ministry of Tribal Affairs has been increasing over the years as per details given below:

                                        
Sl. No.  Year     Budget Allocation (BE)
                                        
                                        
                  (Rs. in crore)        
                                        
                                        
                                        
1        2013-14  4295.94               
                                        
                                        
                                        
2        2014-15  4497.96               
                                        
                                        
                                        
3        2015-16  4819.21               
                                        
                                        
                                        
4        2016-17  4826.50               
                                        
                                        
                                        
5        2017-18  5329.32               
                                        
                                        

    Efforts are made by seeking more funds / increase in the budgetary allocation of the Ministry every year so as to increase the fund allocation to different Schemes of the Ministry for the socio-economic development of the STs.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Tribal Affairs Shri Jaswantsinh Bhabhor in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.
