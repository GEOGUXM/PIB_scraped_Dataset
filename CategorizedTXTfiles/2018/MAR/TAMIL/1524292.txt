      PIB Headquarters

      Dak Adalat
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2018 3:09PM by PIB Chennai


    "Dak Adalat" will be conducted by the Senior Superintendent of Post offices, Chennai city North Division at the office premises at no.5 (old No.3) IV Floor, Ethiraj Salai, Egmore, Chennai 600 008 at 10,00 hours on 23.03.2018 (Friday).


    The members of public are requested to send their unsettled grievances/pending complaints relating to postal services in respect of post offices under the administrative jurisdiction of Chennai City North Division on or before 15.03.2018.
