      Special Arrangements for Filing of Returns of Income for the Assessment Years 2016-17 & 2017-18
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2018 7:24PM by PIB Chennai


    Based on detailed data analytics carried out with reference to non-filers and stop-filers of income tax returns, letters have been sent from the Income-tax Department reminding those tax-payers who have not filed returns of income for the assessment years 2016-17 and 2017-18, of their obligation to file the return of income. Huge response is being received from tax-payers seeking clarification, guidance, assistance, etc.


    In view of this and in order to facilitate the tax-payers in filing Return of Income particularly those who are self-employed or having income from salary / pension, special arrangements are made to file returns in Aayakar Seva Kendras (ASK) at Chennai viz. Aayakar Bhavan, 121, Mahathma Gandhi Road, Nungambakkam, Chennai – 600 034, at BSNL Building, No. 16, Greems Road, Chennai – 600006 and at 130-B, Mudichur Road, West Tambaram, Chennai – 600045 from 26 th March 2018 to 31 st March 2018, from 9.15 a.m. to 5.45 p.m.


    For further details or clarifications, tax-payers may contact the Public Relations office at 044-28338014 / 28338314.
