      Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana for students of class VI to IX
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2018 3:46PM by PIB Chennai


    Department of Posts has launched a philately scholarship scheme Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana ( S cholarship for P romotion of A ptitude & R esearch in S tamps as a H obby) on 03.11.2017 for generating interest regarding Philately amongst students. Students of class VI to IX having good academic record and having either Philately Deposit Account or Member of Philately Club at schools are being awarded scholarship on the basis of a Philately quiz and a Philately project. The amount of scholarship is Rs. 6000/- per annum and it will be deposited in the quarterly basis (Rs. 1500/- per quarter). Department of Posts will conduct this scholarship program every year in future.


    A total of 629 students participated in the written quiz for Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana on 18.01.2018 and 167 students were selected to submit a philately project. Out of 167 projects the best 40 projects (10 from each class VI, VII, VIII, and IX) were selected for awarding of scholarship for the year 2017-2018. The prize winners will receive certificates and scholarship amount of Rs. 6000/- will be deposited in their Post Office Savings Bank account. Shri M. Sampath, Chief Postmaster General, Tamilnadu Circle will distribute the certificates to the prize winners at Chennai on 20.03.2018.


    A Special cover on Stephen Hawking will also be released by the Chief Postmaster General during this occasion
