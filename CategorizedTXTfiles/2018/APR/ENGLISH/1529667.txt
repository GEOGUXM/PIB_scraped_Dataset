      Posted On: 19 APR 2018 6:05PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Thaawarchand Gehlot, Union Minister for Social Justice and Empowerment presented the annual awards of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar International Foundation to eminent personalities who have excelled in the fields of Social and Economic empowerment, Art and Culture, Sports & Games, Health & Education, Poverty Alleviation etc. at a function here today. Shri Gehlot congratulated all award winners for their exemplary works and praised the efforts of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar International Foundation in doing humanitarian work among the economically weaker section of society.


    Dr. B.R. Ambedkar International Foundation strives to accomplish its objectives by designing and implementing several innovative and bold solutions that will bring in long-term sustainable changes in the living condition of the poor and needy. It has been recognizing eminent personalities at its award function annually.
