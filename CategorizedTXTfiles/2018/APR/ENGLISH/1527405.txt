      Prime Minister's Office

      PM announces ex-gratia relief of Rs. 10 lakh each to the next of the kin of those killed in Mosul
      Posted On: 03 APR 2018 1:57PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has announced ex-gratia payment of Rs. 10 lakh each to the next of the kin of those who were killed in Mosul, Iraq.
