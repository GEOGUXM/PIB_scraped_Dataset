      India’s Fastest Train Gatimaan Express Extended up to Bundelkhand Region : To Boost Tourism in the Region
      Posted On: 02 APR 2018 6:25PM by PIB Delhi


    India’s fastest train Gatimaan Express has been extended up to Bundelkhand Region w.e.f 1 st April, 2018. It has been notified that the Train no 12049/50 Nizammuddin-Agra Cantt Gatimaan Exp. has been extended upto Gwalior/Jhansi for facilitating passengers. The extension of this prestigious premium train will facilitate movement of tourists to various tourist attractions in and around Gwalior and Jhansi of Bundelkhand region. It will boost the tourism in the region.
