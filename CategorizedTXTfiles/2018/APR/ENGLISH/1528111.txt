      Ministry of Finance

      Development of Various Infrastructure Schemes
      Posted On: 06 APR 2018 5:44PM by PIB Delhi


    As per Budget 2017-18 and 2018-19, the Revised Estimates for Capital Outlay on Infrastructure Sector for the years 2016-17 and 2017-18 are Rs 3,58,634 crore and Rs 4,94,313 crore respectively, while Budget Estimate for the year 2018-19 is Rs 5,97,143 crore.
