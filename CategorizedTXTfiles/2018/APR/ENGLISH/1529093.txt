      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people on Ambedkar Jayanti
      Posted On: 14 APR 2018 10:25AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted people on Ambedkar Jayanti.


    “Greetings on Ambedkar Jayanti. PujyaBabasaheb gave hope to lakhs of people belonging to the poorest and marginalised sections of society. We remain indebted to him for his efforts towards the making of our Constitution. सभी देशवासियों को अम्बेडकर जयंती की शुभकामनाएं। जय भीम!”, the Prime Minister said.
