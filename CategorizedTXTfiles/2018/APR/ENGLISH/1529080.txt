      ECI Officers’ Committee Submits its Report on Alleged News Leak of Election Schedule relating to Karnataka Elections during Commission’s Announcement
      Posted On: 13 APR 2018 8:07PM by PIB Delhi


    The Officers’ Committee, constituted by the Election Commission of India on 27 th March 2018 to probe alleged news leak of Election Schedule related to Karnataka Elections during Commission’s Announcement, today submitted its report to the Commission in New Delhi.
