      President of India to visit Jammu & Kashmir on April 18 and 19
      Posted On: 17 APR 2018 4:41PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Jammu & Kashmir on April 18 and 19, 2018.


    On April 18, 2018, the President will grace and address the 6 th convocation of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University in Katra. On the same evening, he will attend a civic reception at Amar Mahal, Jammu.


    On April 19, 2018, the President will visit the Shree Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine, before returning to Delhi.
