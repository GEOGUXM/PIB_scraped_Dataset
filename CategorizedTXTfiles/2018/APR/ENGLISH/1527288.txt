      Ministry of Finance

      Final Monthly collection figures of GST
      Posted On: 02 APR 2018 6:08PM by PIB Delhi


    The revenue collection figures under GST including CGST, SGST, IGST and cess for the period July 2017 – February 2018 paid in the period July 2017 – March 2018 is as follows:


    (Figures in Rs. Crores)

                         
Month      GST Collection
                         
                         
                         
August     93,590

February   88,047        
                         
                         
                         
March      89,264        
                         
                         
                         
Total      7,17,638      
                         
                         

    Besides the above Rs. 27,811 crores were collected as IGST and cess on imports in the month of March. Every month the Department of Revenue released figures for revenue collection under GST which were released usually the day next to the day when settlement of IGST was done. The figures were normally released between 24 th -26 th of the month. The above figures are month end figures for collection under GST.
