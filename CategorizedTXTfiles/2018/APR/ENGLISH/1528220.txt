      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Sathish Kumar Sivalingam on winning the Gold Medal in the Men's 77 kg weightlifting event
      Posted On: 07 APR 2018 4:33PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated weightlifter Sathish Kumar Sivalingam on winning the Gold Medal at Commonwealth Games.


    "India’s weightlifters are also lifting our position in the medal’s tally. Well done Sathish Kumar Sivalingam for clinching a Gold in the 77 kg weight category. He has entered the history books by winning consecutive Golds in 2 CWGs, Glasgow 2014 and Gold Coast 2018", the Prime Minister said.
