      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Disproportionate Assets and Income of Officials
      Posted On: 05 APR 2018 6:26PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Bureau of Investigation has registered 23 cases (20 Regular Cases and 03 Preliminary Enquiries) relating to Disproportionate Assets against top officials, i.e. Joint Secretary and above for Central Government, General Manager and above for Public Sector Undertaking and Public Sector Banks during the last three years, i.e., 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018 (upto 28.02.2018).


    It will not be appropriate to state that cases of corruption have grown during the last three years.
