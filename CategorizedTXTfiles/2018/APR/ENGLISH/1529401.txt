      Envoys of Four Nations Present Credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 17 APR 2018 6:37PM by PIB Delhi


    Envoys of Bahrain, Lebanon, Argentine Republic and Iraq presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (April 17, 2018).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. His Excellency Mr Abdulrahman Mohamed Ahmed Al Gaoud, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Bahrain


    2. His Excellency Mr Rabie Narsh, Ambassador of Lebanon


    3. His Excellency Mr Daniel Chuburu, Ambassador of Argentine Republic


    4. His Excellency Dr Falah Abdulhasan Abdulsada, Ambassador of Republic of Iraq
