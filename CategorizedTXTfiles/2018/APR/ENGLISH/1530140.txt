      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Cabinet apprised of the Memorandum of Agreement between India and World Health Organisation represented by its Regional Office for South-East Asia acting through its Country Office in India
      Posted On: 25 APR 2018 1:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet Chaired by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has been apprised of the Memorandum of Agreement (MoA) between India and World Health Organisation represented by its Regional Office for South-East Asia acting through its Country Office in India. The MoU was signed on 13 th March, 2018 at New Delhi.


    The bilateral MoA shall encourage cooperation between India and WHO. This will facilitate in improving the public health status of the people in India.
