      President of India to grace a function on the occasion of 127th birth anniversary of Babasaheb Dr BR Ambedkar in Mhow
      Posted On: 13 APR 2018 5:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Madhya Pradesh (Mhow) tomorrow (April 14, 2018). The President will pay his tributes to Babasaheb Dr BR Ambedkar at his memorial in Mhow, the birth place of Dr Ambedkar, and grace a function being organised on his 127 th birth anniversary.
