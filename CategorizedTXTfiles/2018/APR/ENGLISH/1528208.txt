      Prime Minister's Office

      PM wishes people on World Health Day
      Posted On: 07 APR 2018 10:28AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has wished people on World Health Day.


    PM said, "Good health is the foundation of human progress. This World Health Day, I wish all of you remain in the best health and continue to scale new heights of growth.


    I welcome the theme ‘Universal health coverage: everyone, everywhere’ that has been chosen by WHO and others. It is the quest for Health For All that inspired us to create Ayushman Bharat, the largest healthcare programme in the world."
