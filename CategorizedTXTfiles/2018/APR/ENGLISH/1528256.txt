      PM congratulates Indian women’s Table Tennis team on winning the Gold Medal
      Posted On: 08 APR 2018 8:13PM by PIB Delhi

      Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated the Indian women’s Table Tennis team on winning the Gold Medal at the Commonwealth Games.
      
      "A well deserved and historic Gold for the Indian women’s Table Tennis team! Congratulations to the team. Delighted at this accomplishment", the Prime Minister said.
