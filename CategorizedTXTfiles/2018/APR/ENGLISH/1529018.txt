      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Anjum Moudgil for bagging the Silver in the women's 50m Rifle 3 Positions event
      Posted On: 13 APR 2018 5:54PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Anjum Moudgil for bagging the Silver in the women's 50m Rifle 3 Positions event


    The Prime Minister said, “Proud of Anjum Moudgil for bagging the Silver in the women's 50m Rifle 3 Positions event”.
