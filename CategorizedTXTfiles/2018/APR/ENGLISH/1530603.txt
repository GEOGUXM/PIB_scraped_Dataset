      President of India to visit Madhya Pradesh on April 28 and 29
      Posted On: 27 APR 2018 6:06PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Madhya Pradesh on April 28 and 29, 2018.


    On April 28, 2018, the President will grace the 27 th convocation of Dr Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, Sagar. He will also grace the Satguru Kabir Saheb Mahotsav being organised on the occasion of 620 th Pragatosav of Satguru Kabir in Sagar.


    On April 29, 2018, the President will grace an ‘Asangathit Shramik Sammelan’ organised by Government of Madhya Pradesh, in Guna. He will also address a function related to Mini Smart City Mission of Government of Madhya Pradesh in Guna, before returning to New Delhi.
