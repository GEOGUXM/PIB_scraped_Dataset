      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Centre extends ceasefire for one more year with NSCN/NK and NSCN/R
      Posted On: 20 APR 2018 7:36PM by PIB Delhi


    A ceasefire is in operation between Government of India, National Socialist Council of Nagaland – Neopao Konyak/Kitovi (NSCN/NK) and National Socialist Council of Nagaland – Reformation (NSCN/R). It was decided to extend the Suspension of Operation with NSCN/NK and NSCN/R for a further period of one year with effect from 28 th April, 2018, i.e till 27 th April, 2019.


    This was signed on 19 th April, 2018 by Shri Satyendra Garg, Joint Secretary, Ministry of Home Affairs on behalf of Government of India. Shri Jack Jimomi, Supervisor, GPRN/NSCN on behalf of NSCN/NK and Shri Viniho Kiho Swu, Co-Supervisor and Shri Toshi Longkumer, Member on behalf of NSCN/R.
