    NCL : In the command area of NCL, spread over the states of Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh, a number of works have been taken up for upgradation of transport infrastructure for smooth evacuation of coal.

     1. Complete Track Renewal(CTR) of old railway siding tracks for Bina (UP), Jayant (MP) & Block-B (MP) Areas.
     2. Construction of various railway sidings for smooth dispatch of coal.
     3. Extension of existing Public Railway line from Jayant (MP) to Amlohri (MP) – Nigahi (MP).
     4. Diversion of KBJ Line (Railway line) at Khadia Area (UP).

    MCL: MCL is in the process of constructing various railway lines, roads, coal handling plants, SILOs and Washeries for improving its coal evacuation. The details are as follows:

     1. Jharsuguda- Barpali- Sardega rail line- Newly constructed line

    A 52.92 KM Jharsuguda- Barpali- Sardega rail line taking-off from Jharsuguda station has been constructed by MCL at a project cost of Rs 1007.12 Cr for evacuation of coal to the tune of 70 MT from Basundhara- Garjanbahal coalfield of Sundargarh district.

     1. Construction of 3rd line from TLSB to Talcher Yard of Talcher coalfield

    The subject rail line of a length of 4 KM has been constructed and commissioned by MCL at a project cost of Rs 47.55 Cr to increase the coal dispatch capacity from Talcher coalfields.

     1. Auto Signaling/ IBS system between Talcher and Paradip Port

    MCL has invested Rs 72.42 Cr for introduction of Automatic signaling system/IBS system between Talcher and Paradip Port so as to enable faster dispatch of coal from Talcher coalfields of MCL.
