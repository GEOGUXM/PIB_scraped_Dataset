      Summer camp on letter writing and philately
      Posted On: 19 APR 2018 4:23PM by PIB Chennai


    Philatelic Bureau, Anna Road HO is organizing a Summer Camp for School Children to sharpen their skills in letter writing and teach them the nuances of Philately so that they can learn to rise as “Stamp Collector/ an amateur Philatelist”. The summer camp is mainly intended for the benefit of school children from Class VI to IX. Students of higher classes can also attend.


    The duration of camp would be 3 half days and the session would start at 09.30 am and conclude at 12.30 pm. Batches will run from Wednesday to Friday, every week. The 1 st batch will be from 25 th of April 2018 to 27 th of April 2018. Subsequent batches would be held on 2 nd to 4 th of May; 9 th to 11 th of May; 16 th to 18 th of May; 23 rd to 25 th of May 2018. The summer camp will be held in the premises of Anna Road Head Post Office, Chennai 600 002. The Parents have to make their own arrangements for the pickup and drop of their children.


    In the first day of camp, children will be provided inputs on Philately, practical training will also be given to the participating students to prepare stamp book/ exhibits. Selected Exhibits prepared by the students will be displayed in the Permanent Philatelic Exhibition of Anna Road HO, for a period of one month. On the second day, inputs will be provided to improve the skill of letter writing among the students and letters written by great people will also be discussed. Students will be writing personal letters to their grandparents or friends and posting them at Anna Road Head Post Office, itself. On the third day, students will be taken through the complete journey of a letter in Post Office right from letter box clearance to processing and delivery of letters. They will also get a glimpse of different activities of a Post Office including money order booking and payment.


    ­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­ Teachers / Heads of Schools or parents of interested children are requested to contact the Philatelic Bureau, Anna Road HO, Chennai 600002 for registration. Application format is available on www.chennaipost.gov.in . Filled in Applications can be posted to “The Chief Postmaster, (Philatelic Bureau), Anna Road HO, Chennai 600 002” or can be forwarded to the e mail id – philatelypromotion@gmail.com . A sum of Rs.250/- only will be charged per student for which the students will be provided a Philatelic Deposit Account and Philately material. The amount can be paid at Philatelic Bureau, Anna Road on the first day of the camp.
