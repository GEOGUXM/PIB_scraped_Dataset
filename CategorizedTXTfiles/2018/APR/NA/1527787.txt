      PM remembers Babu Jagjivan Ram on his birth anniversary.
      Posted On: 05 APR 2018 11:08AM by PIB Delhi

      Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has saluted Babu Jagjivan Ram on his birth anniversary.
      PM said, " Self-made and industrious, the contribution of Babu Jagjivan Ram can never be forgotten. As a freedom fighter and veteran administrator, his service to India was impeccable. Babuji was a true democrat, refusing to bow to authoritarianism. India remembers him on his Jayanti. "
