      President’s greetings on the eve of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi, Puthandu Pirappu
      Posted On: 13 APR 2018 4:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi, Puthandu Pirappu which are being celebrated on 14 th and 15 th April, 2018 has said:-


    “ On the auspicious occasion of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi, Puthandu Pirappu, I extend my warm greetings and good wishes to all my fellow citizens. These festivals symbolize the importance and respect given to hard work in our tradition.


    These festivals, celebrated in various ways in different parts of our country, reflect our diversity and pluralistic traditions and highlight the unity and understanding so characteristic of our social ethos.


    On this joyous occasion, let us resolve to work hard to bring peace, prosperity and happiness and also to spread the message of unity and fraternity for the progress of our beloved nation ”.
