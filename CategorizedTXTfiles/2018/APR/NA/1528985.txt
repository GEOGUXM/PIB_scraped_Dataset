      Vice President Greets People on the eve of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Puthandu, Masadi, Vaishkhadi and Bahag Bihu
      Posted On: 13 APR 2018 4:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted the people on the auspicious occasion of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Puthandu, Masadi, Vaishkhadi and Bahag Bihu. In a message, he said that these festivals reflect the rich agrarian traditions and composite culture of our country.


    Following is the text of Vice President's message :


    "I convey my warm greetings and good wishes to the people of our country on the joyous occasion of ‘Vaisakhi, Vishu, Puthandu, Masadi, Vaishkhadi and Bahag Bihu’.


    Celebrated with traditional fervor and enthusiasm throughout the country these festivals reflect the rich agrarian traditions and composite culture of our country.


    May these festivals bring peace, harmony, prosperity and happiness in our country."
