      Ministry of Finance

      Press Communique: New Accountant General
      Posted On: 10 APR 2018 3:50PM by PIB Mumbai


    Shri. S. K. Jaipuriyar, IAAS, (1992) has assumed charge of Principal Accountant General (Audit)-III, Maharashtra, Mumbai from 2 April 2018.


    Shri. S. K. Haipuriyar, is B.A. (Hons.). He belongs to the 1992 batch of Indian Audit & Accounts Service. Prior to the present assignment, he held position of Additional Director General (Finance) in Prasar Bharati, New Delhi.
