      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Series of programmes to mark the 4 years of completion of NDA Govt.
      Union Minister of State for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare makes a visit to Guwahati

      Posted On: 27 MAY 2018 6:02PM by PIB Guwahati

    To mark the 4 years of completion of the NDA Government, the Union Minister of State for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat is visiting Guwahati and will be participating in a number of programmes tomorrow.


    The Minister as a part of the programme will interact with the Radio Jockeys of various FM stations of Guwahati viz. Radio Gupshup, FM Rainbow, Big FM & Radio Mirchi.


    The Minister will also address the press on the various policies, programmes and achievements of the Government. Besides that he will also be participating in an interactive session with the editors of Assam along with the Chief Minister of Assam Shri Sarbananda Sonowal. The Minister will also hold a review meeting related to the Agriculture Department in North East in general and Assam in particular with the officials of Agriculture and other related Departments.
