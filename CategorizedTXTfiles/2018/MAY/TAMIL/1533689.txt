      “Nidhi Aapke Nikat”(PF Near You) on 11th June, 2018
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2018 6:38PM by PIB Chennai


    Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation, Ministry Of Labour and Employment, Govt. Of India) is conducting “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” (PF Near You) at its Regional office, Chennai on 10th of every month for speedy redressal of long pending grievances of the Subscribers/Employers covered under the Employees’ Provident Funds & Miscellaneous Provisions Ac, 1952.


    Accordingly, the “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” for the month of June 2018 will be conducted on Monday, the 11th June, 2018 in the Regional Office of the Employees’ Provident Fund organisation, No.37, Royapettah High Road, Chennai 600 014. The following will be time slots allotted for “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” Programme for Subscribers/Members/Pensioners will be from 10.30 A.M. to 1.00 P.M., for Employers will be from 3.00 P.M. to 4.00 P.M. and for Exempted establishments located in jurisdiction will be from 4.00 P.M. to 5.00 P.M.


    The Subscribers / Employers who desire to get their long pending unresolved grievances redressed or have any other suggestions on improvement of service/organisation, they may register their names with the Public Relations Officer duly furnishing their PF code Number / Account Number, nature of grievance etc on or before 01st of June 2018. The aforesaid timings are only for guidance and the employers may also present their grievances, if any, earlier during the day.
