      PIB Headquarters

      National Career Service Centre for SC/ST
      Posted On: 02 MAY 2018 4:14PM by PIB Chennai


    The National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, 3 rd Floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Building, 56, Santhome High Road, Chennai-600 004, (Tel 044-24615112) under M/o Labour & Employment, DGE, Government of India, inviting proposals from reputed firms/Institutions of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, for imparting the DGE sponsored scheme of 25th Phase Special Coaching Scheme under shorthand to 60 nos SC/ST candidates, Interested Institutes/Firms need to meet the following criteria such as will-trained faculties, training infrasture, location etc.


    The Selected institute will impart training in (1) General Awareness, (2) General English (3) Numerical Ability, (4) Shorthand and Typing, (5) Basic Knowledge in Computer in order to make the eligible SC/ST educated Job Seeker of Tamil Nadu viable for facing the different Competitive Examinations. The selected Institutes / Firms will be paid professional Charges of Rs.800/-per candidate per month.


    The interested institutions/Firms may apply to the Sub-Regional Employment officer, NATIONAL CAREER SERVICE CENTRE FOR SC/ST. CHENNAI (FORMERLY CGC) 3 rd Floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Building, 56, Santhome High Road, Chennai-600 004 (Tel o44-24615112) on or before 31st May 2018
