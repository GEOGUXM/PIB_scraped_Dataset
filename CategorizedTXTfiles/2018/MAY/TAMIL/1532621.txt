      Ministry of External Affairs

      Opening of new Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in the Third Phase
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2018 5:39PM by PIB Chennai

     1. The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) and the Department of Posts (DOP) have decided to utilize the Head Post Offices (HPO)/ Post Offices in the country as Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) for delivery of passport related services to the citizens of the country. The objective of this partnership is to extend passport related services on a larger scale and to ensure wider area coverage.
     2. In the first two phases, it was decided to open 251 POPSK in the country out of which 192 have become functional as on 14 May, 2018.
