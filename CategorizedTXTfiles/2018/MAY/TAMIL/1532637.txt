      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Application are invited by National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, Chennai
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2018 6:10PM by PIB Chennai


    Application are invited by National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, Chennai a subordinate office of Directorate General of Employment, M/O Labour & Employment, Govt. of India, for imparting following Training Programmes:


     1. Free and Stipendiary Special Coaching Scheme for SC/STs:


    Duration: 11 Months


    Stipend: 1000 per Month per trainee


    Minimum Edu. Qualification: 10, +2 Pass or equivalent from a recognized Board/ University or ITI (One Year after 10 th )


    Age Limit: 18-30 years


    Course Fee: Free


     1. 'O' Level One Year Computer Training (Software):


    Duration: 12 Months


    Stipend: Rs. 1000/- per Month per trainee


    Minimum Edu. Qualification: 10, +2 Pass or equivalent from a recognized Board/ University or ITI (One Year after 10 th )


    Age Limit: 18-30 years


    Course Fee: Free


    Note: Date of issuing of Application: 7th May 2018


    Last date of receiving application: 8th June 2018
