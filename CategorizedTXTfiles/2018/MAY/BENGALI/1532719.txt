      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Anti-terrorism Day will be observed on May 21
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2018 2:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 18 May, 2018


    This year May 21 (Monday) will be observed as anti-terrorism day like every year. The objective behind observance of this day is to wean away the youth from terrorism and the cult of violence by highlighting the sufferings of common people and showing as to how it is prejudicial to the national interest. Mass education programmes, lectures, talks, discussions will be organised by government and non-government organizations to highlight the ill-effects of violence and terrorism. Anti-terrorism pledge will be taken in all government offices, public sector undertakings and other public institutions on that day. Besides, Doordarshan and All India Radio and other media will also highlight the aim of observing anti-terrorism day.
