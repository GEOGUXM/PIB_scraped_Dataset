      Ministry of Culture

      Book Bazar to be organized by the Sahitya Akademi at Kolkata
      Posted On: 22 MAY 2018 4:24PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 22, 2018.


    Sahitya Akademi, The National Academy of Letters, an autonomous of the Government of India, Ministry of Culture, is going to organize a Book Bazar at its eastern regional office premises at 4 D. L. Khan Road, Kolkata 70025 on 25 May – 2 June 2018 (10.00 am-5.30 pm). The Book Bazar will remain close on Sunday only. In the Book Bazar, Sahitya Akademi publications in Hindi, English, Urdu, Assamese, Bengali, Bodo, Manipuri, Nepali and Odia will be sold with the special discount up to 60%. Eminent Bengali writer Sri Sirshendu Mukhopadhyay will inaugurate the Book Bazar on 25 May 2018 at 3.00 pm.


    In this context it is also to mention that Sahitya Akademi has a library with rich resources in their office complex. The membership enrolment process is going on. Sahitya Akademi is also going to organize a “Book Discussion Programme” once in every month where the members can participate and share their views with eminent litterateurs.
