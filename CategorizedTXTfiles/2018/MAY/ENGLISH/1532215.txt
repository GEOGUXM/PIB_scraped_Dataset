      PRESS COMMUNIQUE
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2018 8:40PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri Om Prakash Kohli, Governor of Gujarat to discharge the functions of the Governor of Madhya Pradesh, in addition to his own duties, during the absence, on leave, of Smt Anandiben Patel, Governor of Madhya Pradesh.
