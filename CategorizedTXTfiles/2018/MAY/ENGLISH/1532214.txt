      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the loss of lives due to capsizing of a boat in Andhra Pradesh’s East Godavari district
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2018 10:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives due to capsizing of a boat in Andhra Pradesh’s East Godavari district.


    “Anguished by the capsizing of a boat in Andhra Pradesh’s East Godavari district. Condolences to the bereaved families. I pray for the safety of those missing ", the Prime Minister said.
