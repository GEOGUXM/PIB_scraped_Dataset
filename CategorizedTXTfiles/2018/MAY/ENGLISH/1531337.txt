      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Shri Gadkari to Lay the Foundation Stone of four National Highway Projects in Telangana Tomorrow
      Posted On: 04 MAY 2018 4:34PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of Road Transport and Highways, Shipping, Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation Shri Nitin Gadkari will lay the foundation stone of four National Highway projects in the state of Telangana tomorrow. The ceremony to be held in Hyderabad Public School grounds, Ramnathapur, Hyderabad will be attended by Chief Minister K Chandrashekhar Rao.


    Costing Rs 1523 crore, these projects include six laning of Aramgarh-Shamsabad section of NH 44 between Hyderabad and Bangalore, rehabilitation and upgradation of NH 765D from Hyderabad outer ring road to Medak, construction of 4-lane flyover at Amberpet-X Roads, and construction of 6-lane elevated corridor from Uppal to Narapally on the Hyderabad-Bhopalpatnam section of NH 163.
