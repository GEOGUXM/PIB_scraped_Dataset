      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Union Minister of State for Tribal Affairs (MoS) Shri. Jasvanthsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor to visit Samoa
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2018 6:59PM by PIB Delhi


    As part of goodwill gesture and to strengthen relations between India and Samoa, the Union Minister of State for Tribal Affairs (MoS) Shri. Jasvanthsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor will embark on a weeklong visit (18 th to 25th May) to Samoa, Pacific Island country from tomorrow. This is the first Ministerial level visit from Government of India. During the visit MoS Shri. Bhabhor will call on the Prime Minister and Deputy Prime Minister of Samoa.The visit is aimed to strengthen the cordial relations between India and Samoa.
