      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointments and Approvals
      Posted On: 03 MAY 2018 8:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the following appointments:


     1. Dr. Sameer Sharma, IAS, (AP:85), Additional Secretary, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, as Director General, Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs, in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary;


     1. Ms. Shefali Shah, IRS (IT:85), Additional Secretary, Ministry of Culture, as Additional Secretary, Department of Consumer Affairs;


     1. Shri Sanjeev Nandan Sahai, IAS (UT:86), presently in the cadre, as Additional Secretary, Ministry of Power;


     1. Shri Rakesh Kumar Gupta, IAS (JK:86), presently in the cadre, as Secretary, Union Public Service Commission, in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary, vice Shri T. Jacob, IAS (TN:84) on his superannuation on 31.05.2018;


     1. Ms. Rajni Sekhri Sibal, IAS (HY:86), Director General, National Institute Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development (NIESBUD), as Additional Secretary, Ministry of Home Affairs;


     1. Dr. Rakesh Kumar Vats, IAS (WB:86), Additional Secretary, Department of Health & Family Welfare, as Chairperson, National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA), under the Department of Pharmaceuticals in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary;


     1. Ms. Mala Dutt, IES (86), presently in the cadre, as Additional Secretary & Financial Adviser, Ministry of Defence,


     1. Shri Sanjeev Kumar, IDAS (86), presently in the cadre, as Additional Secretary, Department of Rural Development;


     1. Shri M. Srinivas Rao, IAS (AM:87), presently in the cadre, as Chief Executive Officer, National e-Governance Division, Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary;


     1. Dr. Rakesh Sarwal, IAS (TR:88), presently in the cadre, as Additional Secretary, Department of Health & Family Welfare vice Dr Rakesh Kumar Vats, IAS (WB:86);


     1. Ms. Anjali Bhawara, IAS (PB:88), presently in the cadre, as Additional Secretary, Ministry of Corporate Affairs;


     1. Ms. Juthika Patankar, IAS (UP:88), presently in the cadre, as Director General, National Institute of Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development (NIESBUD) in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary, vice Ms. Rajni Sekhri Sibal, IAS (HY:86);


     1. Shri K. Sanjay Murthy, IAS (HP:89), Joint Secretary, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, as Joint Secretary, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs;


     1. Shri Devesh Chaturvedi, IAS (UP:89), Joint Secretary, Department of Personnel & Training, as Joint Secretary, Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers' Welfare.


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has further approved to extend the Central Deputation tenure of the following officers:


     1. Shri Arun Goel, IAS (PB:85), Additional Secretary & Financial Adviser, Ministry of Labour & Employment, by a further period of one year beyond 04.05.2018 i.e. upto 03.05.2019; and


     1. Ms. S. Radha Chauhan, IAS (UP:88), Chief Executive Officer, GeM SPV by a further period of one year beyond 31.05.2018 i.e. upto 30.05.2019.
