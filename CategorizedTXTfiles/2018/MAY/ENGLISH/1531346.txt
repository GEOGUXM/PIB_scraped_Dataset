      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Approval
      Posted On: 03 MAY 2018 8:51PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the proposal for appointment of Dr. Ashok Lahiri as Full Time Member in the Fifteenth Finance Commission subject to the condition that he steps down from his present position in Bandhan Bank.


    On his appointment as Full Time Member, Dr. Lahiri will be accorded the status of Minister of State for the purpose of warrant of precedence only.
