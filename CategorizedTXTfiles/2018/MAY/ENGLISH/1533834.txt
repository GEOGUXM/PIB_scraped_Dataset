      Election Commission

      Biennial Election to Rajya Sabha due to retirement of sitting members from Kerala
      Posted On: 30 MAY 2018 12:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The term of office of 3 members of the Council of States (Rajya Sabha) elected from Kerala, is due to expire in the month of July, 2018 as per the following details:

                                                             
S.No.              State   Name of Member  Date of retirement
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
1.                 Kerala  1. Joy Abraham  01.07.2018        
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
                 
2. P.J. Kurien   
                 
                 
                 
3. C.P. Narayanan
                 
                 

    The Election Commission has decided to hold the Biennial Election to the Council of States from Kerala in accordance with the following schedule: -

1.                                                                                        
     Issue of Notifications                         4 th June, 2018 (Monday)              
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Last date of making nominations                11 th June, 2018 (Monday)             
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Scrutiny of nominations                        12 th June, 2018 (Tuesday)            
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Last date for withdrawal of candidatures       14 th June, 2018 (Thursday)           
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Date of poll                                   21 st June, 2018 (Thursday)           
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Hours of poll                                  9.00 A.M. to 4.00 P.M                 
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Counting of Votes                              21 st June, 2018 (Thursday) at 5.00 PM
                                                                                          
                                                                                          
1.                                                                                        
     Date before which election shall be completed  25 th June, 2018 (Monday)             
                                                                                          
                                                                                          

    ( Originally issued by the Election Commission of India vide Press Note No. ECI/PN/41/2018 dated 28.05.2018 )
