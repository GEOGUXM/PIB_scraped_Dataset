      Ministry of Corporate Affairs

      Shri Rajiv Mehrishi, Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG)of India to deliver the CCI Annual Day Lecture on “Competition Law 2.0: Way Forward”
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2018 3:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The Competition Commission of India (CCI) celebrates its Annual Day every year on 20 th May. To commemorate the day, a Lecture Series has been instituted where in distinguished speakers are being invited to deliver the Annual Day Lecture.


    In the past, eminent persons such as Dr. Raguram Rajan, former Governor of RBI and Mr. N. Narayana Murthy from Infosys delivered the Annual Day Lecture inyear 2014 & 2015. In 2016 & 2017, then Hon’ble Finance Minister of India, Shri Arun Jaitley and Hon’ble Mr. Justice J.S. Khehar, then Chief Justice of India had delivered the Annual Day Lecture respectively on the occasion of the CCI’s Annual Day.


    This year, on 20 th May, 2018, Shri Rajiv Mehrishi, Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG)of India will be the Chief Guest on the occasion and will deliver the Annual Day Lecture on the subject of “Competition Law 2.0: Way Forward”.
