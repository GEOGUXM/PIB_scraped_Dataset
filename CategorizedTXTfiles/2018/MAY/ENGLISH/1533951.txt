      Envoys of five nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2018 12:40PM by PIB Delhi


    Envoys of Azerbaijan, Lesotho, Cyprus, Samoa and Jamaica presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (May 31, 2018).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Mr Ashraf Farhad Shikhaliyev, Ambassador of the Republic of Azerbaijan


    2. Mr Bothata Tsikoane, High Commissioner of the Kingdom of Lesotho


    3. Mr Agis Loizou, High Commissioner of the Republic of Cyprus


    4. Ms Faalavaau Perina Jaqueline Sila-Tualaulelei, High Commissioner of the Independent State of Samoa


    5. Mr Aubyn Hill, High Commissioner of Jamaica
