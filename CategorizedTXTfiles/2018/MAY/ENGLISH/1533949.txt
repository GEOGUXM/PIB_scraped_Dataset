      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Union Sports Minister Grants Financial Assistance of Rs. Five Lakh to Manipuri Fencer Kh. Kalambia Chanu For Medical Treatment
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2018 12:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of State (I/C) Youth Affairs and Sports Col Rajyavardhan Rathore has sanctioned financial assistance of Rs. 5 Lakh (Five Lakh) to Fencer Ms. Kh. Kalambia Chanu of Manipur for her medical treatment from Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS).


    Ms. Chanu is suffering from multiple Sclerosis and could not continue her sports and living in indigent conditions.
