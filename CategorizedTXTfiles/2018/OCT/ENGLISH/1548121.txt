      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Chairman NCSK Administers ‘Swachhta Pledge’ to the Employees
      Posted On: 01 OCT 2018 5:54PM by PIB Delhi


    Chairman of National Commission For Safai Karamcharis (NCSK) Shri Manhar Valjibhai Zala administered the ‘Swachhta Pledge’ to the employees of the commission in its premises in Lok Nayak Bhawan, Khan Market Delhi today.


    At the outset, Chairman read out the Swachhta Pledge’ messages received from Ministry. The Vice Chairman Shri Hanraj Hans, Members Smt. Manju Diler and Shri Dilip K. Hathibed and Secretary Shri Narain Das were also present on the occasion.


    Besides, on the occasion Chairman highlighted the importance of cleanliness by citing the example of the life of Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi and requested all employees to ensure cleanliness around their workstations. He has appealed to every employee to dedicate 100 hrs a year towards cleanliness.


    A cleanliness drive was undertaken by the Chairman, Vice Chairman, Members, Secretary and officers/staff of the Commission in its office premises located Lok Naya Bhawan, Khan Market, New Delhi.
