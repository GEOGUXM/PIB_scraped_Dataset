      NITI Aayog to hold Workshop on Best Practices in International Arbitration
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2018 4:53PM by PIB Delhi


    In an effort to strengthen dispute resolution mechanisms and to not only encourage ‘Make in India’ but also to ‘Resolve in India’, a workshop is being organised on 'Best Practices in International Arbitration’ by NITI Aayog, in collaboration with ICC International Court of Arbitration in New Delhi on 10 October 2018.


    The workshop would cover basic concepts of international arbitration and include best practices on drafting, selection of arbitrators, enforcement of arbitral awards, role of courts in the arbitration life cycle, among other topics, with information sharing sessions to be conducted by world class faculty and practitioners from the United Kingdom, Singapore, Paris and India.


    Shri Justice A K Sikri, Judge, Supreme Court of India, Shri P.P. Chaudhary, MoS, Ministry of Law and Justice and Shri Amitabh Kant, CEO, NITI Aayog shall be present in the Inaugural Session of the workshop.
