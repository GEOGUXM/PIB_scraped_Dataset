      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Shri S. S. Deswal appointed as DG, ITBP; to hold additional charge of DG, SSB
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2018 6:34PM by PIB Delhi


    The ACC has approved the appointment of Shri S. S. Deswal, IPS (HY: 84) as Director General, Indo Tibetan Border Police (ITBP). He took charge from Shri R K Pachnanda, the outgoing DG of ITBP, who superannuated today.


    An MHA order said that Shri Deswal will hold the additional charge of the post of DG, Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) till further orders.
