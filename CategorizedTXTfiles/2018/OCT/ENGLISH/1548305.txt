      PRESS COMMUNIQUE
      Posted On: 03 OCT 2018 11:46AM by PIB Delhi


    At a ceremony held today (October 3, 2018) at 1045 hrs in the Durbar Hall, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Shri Justice Ranjan Gogoi was sworn in as the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of India. He took and subscribed to the oath of office before the President.
