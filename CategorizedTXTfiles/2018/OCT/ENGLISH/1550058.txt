      PM greets nation on the occasion of Vijayadashmi
      Posted On: 19 OCT 2018 10:21AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashmi.“विजयादशमी के शुभ अवसर पर सभी देशवासियों को हार्दिक बधाई ”, “ Vijayadashami greetings to everyone”, the Prime Minister said.
