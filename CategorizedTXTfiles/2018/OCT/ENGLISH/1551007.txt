      President of India to visit Himachal Pradesh on October 29 and 30
      Posted On: 28 OCT 2018 4:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Himachal Pradesh on October 29 and 30, 2018.


    On October 29, 2018, the President will grace the first convocation of Dr Rajendra Prasad Government Medical College in Kangra.


    On October 30, 2018, the President will grace the 24 th convocation of Himachal Pradesh University in Shimla.
