      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      2000 Participants from SAI, NSS and NYKS to take part in“Run for Unity”
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2018 5:54PM by PIB Delhi


    Department of Sports, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports would participate in “Run for Unity” on 31 st October,2018, as part of observance of “RashtriyaEktaDiwas” (National Unity Day)along with 2000 participants from Sports Authority of India (SAI), National Service Scheme (NSS) and National Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) at India Gate, NewDelhi.


    Sports Celebrities namely Sh. Zafar Iqbal (Olympian, Hockey), Mrs. Mary Kom (Olympian, Boxing),Sh. Sharad (Para Athelete), Ms. Dipa K Karmakar(Gymnastic),Mr. Abhishek Verma(Archery) and Ms. Sonia Lather will take part in the “Run”.


    Major Dhyanchand National Stadium has geared up for conducting the “Run for Unity”.The Ministry has arranged 2000 T-Shirts for the participants and a Flag for “Flag Off for the Run”.
