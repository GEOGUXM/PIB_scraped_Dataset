      Prime Minister's Office

      PM felicitates medal winners of 2018 Asian Para Games
      Posted On: 16 OCT 2018 5:39PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today met and felicitated the medal winners of the 2018 Asian Para Games.


    Speaking on the occasion, the Prime Minister expressed happiness at receiving the medal winners. Appreciating their performance, he remarked that their mental strength was a key factor in their success. The Prime Minister complimented them for contributing to raising India's profile on the global stage.


    The Prime Minister also expressed appreciation for the coaches of the medal winners.


    He urged the athletes to keep up their spirit of optimism, and persevere to achieve even greater heights.
