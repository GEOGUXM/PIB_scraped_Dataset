      President's Secretariat

      PRESS COMMUNIQUE
      Posted On: 17 OCT 2018 9:40PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, as advised by the Prime Minister, has accepted the resignation of Shri M.J. Akbar from the Union Council of Ministers, with immediate effect, under clause (2) of Article 75 of the Constitution.
