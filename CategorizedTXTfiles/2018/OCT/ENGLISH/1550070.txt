      Ministry of Culture

      Red Fort to remain closed for Public for 3 days
      Posted On: 19 OCT 2018 4:52PM by PIB Delhi


    Archaeological Survey of India(ASI), organisation under Ministry of Culture, Government of India, has ordered that the Red Fort, Delhi shall remain closed for the general public and visitors in view of VVIP movement in Ramleela and Redfort on 19 th , 20 th and 21 st of October, till the function in over.
