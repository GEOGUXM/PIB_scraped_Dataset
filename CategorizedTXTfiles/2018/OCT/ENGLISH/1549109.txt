      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Regional Conference on Good Governance Initiatives begins in Kohima tomorrow
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2018 5:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG), Government of India, in association with Government of Nagaland, is organizing a Regional Conference on Good Governance Initiatives, in Kohima, Nagaland beginning tomorrow. The Chief Minister of Nagaland, Shri Neiphiu Rio will inaugurate the 2-day event. Shri KV Eapen, Secretary, DARPG will also address the participants. Delegates from eight North Eastern States will participate in the Conference. There will be seven Technical sessions in the 2-day conference.


    On the first day, 12 Presentations will be made in five sessions which will be followed by Panel Discussions. Sub themes of the technical sessions include: Capacity Building/ Technology/ Best Practices by Aspirational Districts. On Day-2, five Presentations will be made on Transforming Aspirational Districts and Good Governance Initiatives followed by panel discussions. Chief Secretary, Government of Nagaland, Shri R. Binchilo Thong and Additional Secretary, DARPG, Smt. Vasudha Mishra will address the Valedictory Session.


    The Department has organized 29 Regional Conferences till date. This Conference is an effort to create a common platform to share experiences in the formulation and implementation of best practices in citizen centric governance, improved public service delivery through e-Governance, transparent, accountable and citizen-friendly effective administration.
