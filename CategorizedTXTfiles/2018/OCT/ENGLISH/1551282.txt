      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister Shri Rajnath Singh chairs a review meeting on recruitment, promotion and vacancy position in CAPFs & Delhi Police
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2018 7:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Home Minister Shri Rajnath Singh chaired a review meeting with the Director(s) General of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and Commissioner of Delhi Police today at North Block, New Delhi. The Minister of State for Home Affairs Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir and Union Home Secretary were also present in the meeting.


    During the meeting, the vacancy position, various issues related to direct recruitment and promotion to different posts in CAPFs and Delhi Police were discussed.


    While taking the stock of the vacancy position and recruitment procedure of the CAPFs, Union Home Minister called for initiation of special mechanisms for reducing the delays and fast tracking the recruitment activities in time bound manner.


    Union Home Minister expressed his satisfaction on the progress made by the CAPFs in recruitment during last two years and also desired that departmental promotion should be processed at the earliest within powers vested to respective Director(s) General of the CAPFs and Delhi Police.
