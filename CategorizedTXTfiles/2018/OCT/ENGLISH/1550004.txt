      Envoys of Four Nations present Credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 18 OCT 2018 1:37PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassadors of Kuwait, Belgium, Portugal and Cambodia presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (October 18, 2018).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


     1. Mr Jasem Ibrahem JM Al-Najem, Ambassador of the State of Kuwait

     2. Mr Francois Delhaye, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Belgium
     3. Mr Carlos Jose de Pinho e Melo Pereira Marques, Ambassador of Portugal
     4. Mr Ung Sean, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia
