      Vice President greets people on the eve of Dussehra
      Posted On: 18 OCT 2018 10:11AM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu greeted people on the auspicious occasion of Dussehra. In message, he said that Dussehra festival reminds us of the victory of Good over Evil. The festival plays an important role in promoting amity and camaraderie amongst the people and reaffirms our faith in the noble and virtuous life of Lord Rama.


    Following is the text of Vice President's message :


    "I extend my warm greetings and good wishes to the people of our country on the auspicious occasion of Dussehra.


    Dussehra festival reminds us of the victory of Good over Evil. The ten days Ramleela on the life of Lord Rama conveys the eternal message that good always triumphs over evil. The festival plays an important role in promoting amity and camaraderie amongst the people and reaffirms our faith in the noble and virtuous life of Lord Rama.


    May this festival bring peace, harmony and prosperity in our country.
