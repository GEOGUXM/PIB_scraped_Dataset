      MAHATMA GANDHI’S BIRTH ANNIVERSARY AND SWACHHATA HI SEVA OBSERVED IN PIB KOLKATA
      Posted On: 02 OCT 2018 7:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, October, 2, 2018


    Press Information Bureau, Kolkata celebrated 150 th birth anniversary of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi with speeches, songs and recitations on Gandhi’s teachings and ideas along with campaigning for Swachh Bharat Mission. Several cultural performances including songs and puppet dance by the Song and Drama Division were held to mark the occasion. Quiz competition on swachhata was also held.


    Describing Gandhiji as the apostle of peace and true believer of non-violence, Shri Chinmay Chakravarty, Joint Director, PIB, threw some light on the life and ideals of the father of the Nation and on the importance of cleanliness. He urged all to fulfill Mahatma Gandhi’s dream of a clean and hygienic India as well as to get inspired by Gandhiji for truthfulness. Speakers spoke about the historical contributions and the monumental role played by Mahatma Gandhi for our country achieving freedom from the British colonial and Mahatma’s teachings of strengthening bonds of communal harmony, brotherhood, amity and reviving our secular traditions. Dr. Suresh Banerjee who also was the chief guest, spoke on different aspects of life and philosophy followed by Gandhiji.


    Dignitaries included, Shri Ajay Mhamia, Dy. Director, PIB, Shri Khurshid Mallick, Dy. Director, Publications Division, Smt. Chitra Gupta, Dy. Director, PIB, Shri Samrat Bandhopadhyay, Dy. Director, PIB among others.
