      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Regional Outreach Bureau has organised exhibition on Mahatma Gandhi at Gandhi Bhawan in Barrackpore.
      Posted On: 01 OCT 2018 6:31PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 1 st October, 2018


    Regional Outreach Bureau has organised an exhibition on Mahatma Gandhi at Gandhi Museum at Barrackpore today. The Chairman of Gandhi Museum Shri Narayan Basu inaugurated the exhibition organised by the Kolkata branch of Union Information & Broadcasting Ministry’s media unit. The Vice Chancellor of Vishwabharati University Dr. Sabujkali Sen was also present amongst dignitaries in the inaugural function. The exhibition will be continued till October 5, 2018 (Friday). Besides, a sit-and-draw competition was also organised by Regional Outreach Bureau at the venue.


    In this connection, it may be stated that the Union Government will observe 150 th birth anniversary of the ‘Father of the Nation’ through various programmes.
