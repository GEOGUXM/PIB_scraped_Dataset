      Ministry of Defence

      NCC ANNUAL TRAINING CAMP CONCLUDES
      Posted On: 15 OCT 2018 2:04PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, October 15, 2018


    No 1 (Bengal) Air Sqn NCC, Kolkata, commanded by Wg Cdr Rajesh Sharma just concluded its annual training camp on October 14, 2018. The main objective of the camp was to provide training to senior wing cadets of different schools & colleges, to prepare them to compete at All India Vayu Sena Camp scheduled to be held at Jodhpur from October 24 to November 04, 2018.


    The institute provided all infrastructure facilities to groom the overall personality of the cadets, with swimming pool, sports facilities like football, basketball, badminton, volleyball and gym. All senior cadets were provided flying experience in Virus SW80 microlite at the Behala Flying Club.
