      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Regional Conference on Good Governance Initiatives
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2018 12:46PM by PIB Guwahati


    The Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG), Government of India in association with Government of Nagaland is organizing a Regional Conference on October 10- 11, 2018 at Kohimaon Good Governance Initiatives. The Chief Minister of Nagaland ShriNeiphiu Rio will inaugurate the 2-day event at 10 A.M. on October 10, 2018. Shri KV Eapen, Secretary, D/o Administrative Reforms & Public Grievances will also address the participants. Delegates from 8North Eastern States will participate in the Conference.There will be 7 Technical sessions in the 2-day conference.


    On the Day1, 12 Presentations will be made in 5 sessions which will be followed by Panel Discussions. Sub themes of the technical sessions include: Capacity Building/ Technology/ Best practices by Aspirational Districts.On Day2, five Presentations will be made on Transforming Aspirational Districts and Good Governance Initiativesfollowed by Panel Discussions.Chief Secretary, Government of Nagaland ShriR. Binchilo Thong and Additional Secretary DARPG Smt. Vasudha Mishra will address the Valedictory Session.


    The Department has organized 29 Regional Conferences till date. This Conference is an effort to create a common platform to share experiences in the formulation and implementation of best practices in citizen centric governance, improved public service delivery through e-Governance, transparent, accountable and citizen-friendly effective administration.
