      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Run for unity organised by NHAI, Nagpur
      
      Union Minister Sh. Nitin Gadkari flagged off the event

      Posted On: 31 OCT 2018 5:00PM by PIB Mumbai

    National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) Regional Office, Nagpur today organised ‘Run for unity’ to celebrate Rashtriya Ekta Diwas & to mark the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.


    Union Minister of Road Transport, Highways & Shipping Sh. Nitin Gadkari flagged off ‘Run for Unity’ at Ram Nagar , Nagpur. Prior to this, Sh. Chandrashekhar, Regional officer, NHAI administered the National Integration pledge to officers & staff persons of NHAI, Nagpur at its office in Narang Tower, in Civil line. NHAI officials & staff persons participated in large numbers in the event.
