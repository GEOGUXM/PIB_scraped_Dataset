      EPFO, RO, Ahmadabad is celebrating Vigilance Awareness Week 2018
      Posted On: 26 OCT 2018 3:43PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 26-10-2018


    EPFO, RO, Ahmadabad is celebrating Vigilance Awareness Week 2018 from 29/L0/2018 to 03/1t/2018. Shri Ajeetkumar, RPFC-I, APFCs and all the staff members of RO, Ahmadabad took oath of integrity pledge on 29 / 10 /2O 18 at 11,00 a.m. Arrangements have been made for the customers/citizens for taking oath online pledge at Ro, Ahmadabad during the whole week.


    As part of the -awareness drive posters/Banners are placed at various prominent places in the city for bringing awareness in public against corruption & benefit on various EPF Schemes.
