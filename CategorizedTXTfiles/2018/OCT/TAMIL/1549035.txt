      ‘DAK ADALAT’
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2018 3:55PM by PIB Chennai


    It is proposed to conduct DAK ADALAT on 15.10.2018 at 1600 hours at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002. The members of the public are requested to send their grievances, if any regarding Postal services (Money order / Registered Articles / Savings Bank / Savings Certificate etc.,) availed at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002, either in person or by post or through email at doannaroadhpo.tn@indiapost.gov.in , with the caption DAK Adalat, on or before 12.10.2018. They may also attend the Dak Adalat in person on 15.10.2018 at 1600 hours.
