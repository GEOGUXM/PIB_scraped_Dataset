      Pensioner’s meet for quick redressal of grievances
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2018 3:15PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The first monthly meeting of pensioners or family pensioners of the Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute (CMFRI), Central Institute of Fisheries Technology (CIFT) and Central Tuber Crops Research Institute (CTCRI) will be held at CMFRI at 10.30 am on October 22, for a prompt and quick redressal of pensioner’s grievances.


    Officials from the concerned institutes, PPO issuing branch and senior official of the bank concerned will be present at the meeting. Pensioners and family pensioners or their representatives of the respective institutes may plead their cases of grievances, if any, for effective redressal and to take possible necessary action at the level of the institute and bank.
