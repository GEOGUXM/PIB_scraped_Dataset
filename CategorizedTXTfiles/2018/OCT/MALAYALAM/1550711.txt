      Telecom Consumer Outreach Programme at Kochi Municipal Town Hall
      Posted On: 25 OCT 2018 3:36PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) is organizing a Telecom Consumer Outreach Programme at 3.00 p.m on Monday, the 26 th October 2018 at Kochi Municipal Corporation Town Hall. During the Programme, officers from Regional office of Telecom Regulatory Authority of India will address the gathering. Public are invited to attend the Consumer Outreach Programme to know about their rights and duties as subscribers of Cellphone, Landphone, Cable TV, etc. Entry to the programme will be free; registration will start at 2.00 p.m.


    Kerala has a mobile subscriber base of 43.01 million and landline subscribers of 1.94 million. There are 6 Telecom Service Providers in Kerala Service area. Many consumers are not aware of the regulations issued by TRAI to protect the interest of the consumers. Consumers should be aware of the grievance mechanism, procedure for filing appeal, procedure for porting the subscription to another service provider by retaining the same number, procedure to stop unsolicited commercial communication and unwanted calls, and rules related to value added services. The Digitization of cable TV network already completed in the entire country. Advantages of the above system will also be discussed during the above programme.
