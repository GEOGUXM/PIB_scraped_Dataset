      President of India to address the 1st convocation of National Institute of Food Technology Entrepreneurship and Management, Sonepat, Tomorrow
      Posted On: 09 FEB 2018 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Haryana (Sonepat) tomorrow (February 10, 2018) to grace and address the 1 st convocation of National Institute of Food Technology Entrepreneurship and Management (NIFTEM).
