      Ministry of Defence

      RRM Visits Andaman & Nicobar Command
      Posted On: 26 FEB 2018 9:32PM by PIB Delhi


    Raksha Rajya Mantri Dr. Subhash Ramarao Bhamre is on a three day visit to Andaman & Nicobar Command from 25 February 2018. On his maiden visit to the Command, he was familiarised with the operational capabilities and uniqueness of the first and only joint services Operational Command of the country. During his visit, Dr. Bhamre interacted with the Lt Governor, Admiral DK Joshi (Retd) and Commander-in-Chief, Andaman and Nicobar Command Vice Admiral Bimal Verma.


    Dr. Bhamre went on a windshield tour of the operational areas of Andaman and Nicobar Command. He also interacted with the troops at Port Blair during tea organised at HQ ANC.
