      Delhi Zoo to Introduce Electronic Machines For Sale of Entry Tickets
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2018 6:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The National Zoological Park, New Delhi, will introduce seven Electronic Machine from tomorrow, i.e February 7, 2018 for sale of entry tickets to the visitors.


    Two Point of Sale (POS) machines are already working in the booking section of National Zoological Park for cashless payment. This will facilitate the visitors to get tickets without waiting.
