      President of India to Visit Madhya Pradesh Tomorrow
      Posted On: 10 FEB 2018 6:09PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Madhya Pradesh (Gwalior) tomorrow (February 11, 2018) to grace a function, organised by the Indian Red Cross Society, Haryana Chapter to declare Gwalior as ‘Divyang friendly district’ and to distribute artificial limbs. He will also deliver the 4 th Dr Ram Manohar Lohia Memorial Lecture at ITM University, Gwalior, and grace the convocation of Jiwaji University, before returning to Delhi.
