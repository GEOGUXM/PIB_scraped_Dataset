      Prime Minister's Office

      PM expresses grief over loss of lives in air crash in Moscow
      Posted On: 11 FEB 2018 10:39PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has expressed his condolences on the loss of lives in an air crash in Moscow.


    PM said, "Deepest condolences on the tragic loss of life in today's air crash in Moscow. Our thoughts are with the families of the victims."
