      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates team India on winning the Under-19 World Cup
      Posted On: 03 FEB 2018 4:21PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated the young cricketers of team India on winning the Under-19 World Cup.


    “Absolutely thrilled by the stupendous achievement of our young cricketers. Congratulations to them on winning the Under-19 World Cup. This triumph makes every Indian extremely proud”, the Prime Minister said.
