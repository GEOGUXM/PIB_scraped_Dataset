      PM pays tributes to Dr. Zakir Hussain on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 08 FEB 2018 12:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Dr. Zakir Hussain, former President of India , on his birth anniversary.


    “India remembers Dr. Zakir Hussain on his birth anniversary. His contribution towards India’s freedom movement was tremendous. He also distinguished himself as a scholar and educationist”, the Prime Minister said.
