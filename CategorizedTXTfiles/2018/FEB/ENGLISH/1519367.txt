      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      MoUs signed by BIS
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2018 6:52PM by PIB Delhi


    BIS has signed MoUs with National Standards Bodies (NSBs) of 23 countries (List given below) . The objective of the MoUs is to facilitate closer cooperation between the bodies with the common aim of strengthening core activities of standardization and conformity assessment which helps the national economy by providing safe, reliable and quality goods, minimizing health hazards to consumers, protecting the environment, promoting exports and imports etc.
