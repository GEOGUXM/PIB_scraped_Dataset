      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      WCD Ministry considering setting up National Women Entrepreneurship Council
      Posted On: 09 FEB 2018 4:31PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Women and Child Development is considering to establish National Women Entrepreneurship Council (NWEC). NWEC will promote entrepreneurship as it would be the umbrella organization for socio-economic gender parity, financial inclusion and economic empowerment of women in India.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Women and Child Development, Dr. Virendra Kumar in reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
