      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      559 Swadhar Greh presently functional in the country with 17231 beneficiaries
      Posted On: 09 FEB 2018 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    T he Ministry of Women and Child Development is administering Swadhar Greh Scheme for relief and rehabilitation of women in difficult circumstances including the victims of sexual harassment. Similarly, a centrally sponsored scheme namely Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS) is being implemented for care, protection, rehabilitation and reintegration of children in difficult circumstances including sexually harassed children.
