      Envoys of four Nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2018 12:46PM by PIB Delhi


    
    Envoys of Bolivia, Republic of Korea, Paraguay and Republic of Congo presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (February 20, 2018).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -

     1. His Excellency Mr Sergio Dario Arispe Barrientos, Ambassador of the Plurinational State of Bolivia
     2. His Excellency Mr Shin Bongkil, Ambassador of the Republic of Korea
     3. His Excellency Mr. Fleming Raul Duarte Ramos, Ambassador of Paraguay
     4. His Excellency Mr Andre Poh, Ambassador of Republic of Congo
