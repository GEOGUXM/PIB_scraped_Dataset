      Shri Pankaj Pandey Assumes Charge As Director, PIB
      Posted On: 16 FEB 2018 4:27PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Shri Pankaj Pandey assumed charge as Director of Press Information Bureau, Cochin under the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting. Prior to this appointment, he was Director (News) in Doordarshan, Lucknow. He is an Indian Information Service officer of 2002 batch. He has worked in different media units of the Ministry like All India Radio and Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity. He has also served at SAARC headquarters, Kathmandu during 2010-13 on foreign deputation.
