      SSP QC Teams bag Awards
      Posted On: 31 DEC 2018 1:51PM by PIB Chennai


    Two Quality Circles of Salem Steel Plant participated in the National Convention on Quality concepts (NCQC 2018) conducted at Gwalior recently. The Evergreen QC from CRM Mechanical department bagged the Par Excellence Award while the New Tech QC from SMS Mechanical department won the Excellence Award. Shri AV Kamlakar, Executive Director, congratulated the winning teams at a function held recently. He appreciated the Award winning employees for their achievements. He pointed out that employees’ contribution through Quality Circles help in a big way to improve the plant’s performance.
