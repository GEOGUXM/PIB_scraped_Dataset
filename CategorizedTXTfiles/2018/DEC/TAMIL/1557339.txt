      Aadhaar Enrolment Centre at EPFO Royapettah
      Posted On: 26 DEC 2018 12:20PM by PIB Chennai


    An Aadhaar Enrolment Centre has been opened at the premises of the Regional Office of Employees Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO), in Royapettah by M/s. Electronics Corporation of Tamil Nadu (ELCOT.)


    Those who wish to correct their Names / Address / Date of Birth / Change or updation of mobile numbers in their Aadhaar for the purpose of KYC updation in UAN can do so by visiting the PF office at Royapettah, Chennai-14 by bringing originals of PAN Card / Driving licence/Passport/Voter ID/Birth Certificate with them as documentary proof. All the PF members are requested to utilize this facility for updating or correcting their Aadhaar card says Regional P.F. Commissioner-I, Shri. Salil Sankar in a press release issued by Employeesâ€™ Provident Fund Organisation.
