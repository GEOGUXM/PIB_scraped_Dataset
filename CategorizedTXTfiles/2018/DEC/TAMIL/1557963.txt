      Bye-election for Thiruvarur constituency to be held on 28.01.2019
      Posted On: 31 DEC 2018 5:57PM by PIB Chennai


    Bye-election for Thiruvarur assembly constituency to be held on 28.01.2019. After taking into consideration various factors like local festivals, electoral rolls, weather conditions etc., the election Commission has decided to hold bye-elections to fill these vacancies as per the programme mentioned as under: -
