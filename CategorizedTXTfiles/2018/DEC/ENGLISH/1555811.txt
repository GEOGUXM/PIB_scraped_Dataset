      Ministry of Shipping

      Revamping of Kolkata Port and Haldia Dock
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2018 3:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The Minister of State for Shipping and Finance Shri Pon Radhakrishnan in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today informed that the Government is preparing a plan to invest and revamp Kolkata port and Haldia dock to boost industrial and commercial business in West Bengal.
