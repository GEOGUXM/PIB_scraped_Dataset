      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Shri A. N. Jha appointed as Finance Secretary
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2018 11:51AM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved designating Shri A. N. Jha, IAS (MN:1982), Secretary, Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance as Finance Secretary.
