      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Operationalisation of ICDS –CAS to address problem of under-nutrition
      Posted On: 28 DEC 2018 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


    Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) common application software has been in operation in 57 districts covering 7 States/UTs till July, 2018. As on 31 st November, 2018, ICDS-CAS has been rolled-out in 64 districts across 9 States/UTs.


    ICDS-CAS Software enables the identification of malnourished children based on auto-plotting of Growth Charts. The drill–down dashboard available at National, State, District, Block level enables timely identification and interventions to address the problem of under-nutrition.
