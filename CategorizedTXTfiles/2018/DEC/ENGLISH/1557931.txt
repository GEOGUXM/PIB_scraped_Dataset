      Vice President Greets people on the advent of Year 2019
      Posted On: 31 DEC 2018 4:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted the people on the advent of Year 2019. In a message, he said that on the joyous occasion of beginning of Year 2019, let us collectively resolve to dedicate ourselves towards building a peaceful, prosperous, inclusive and sustainable world.


    Following is the text of Vice President’s message :


    “I extend my warm greetings and best wishes to the citizens of country on the advent of Year 2019.


    On the joyous occasion of beginning of Year 2019, let us collectively resolve to dedicate ourselves towards building a peaceful, prosperous, inclusive and sustainable world.


    May the Year 2019 bring peace, prosperity and happiness in our lives and make our country progress on all the cherished goals.”
