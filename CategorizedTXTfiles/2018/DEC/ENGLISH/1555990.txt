      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Shri Ashok Kumar Singh assumes charge of secretary,
      
      National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST)

      Posted On: 14 DEC 2018 5:56PM by PIB Delhi

    Shri Ashok Kumar Singh, Lok Sabha Secretariat Service, Additional Secretary, Lok Sabha Secretariat has assumed charge as Secretary, National Commission for Scheduled Tribes (NCST) on 14 th December, 2018 forenoon.
