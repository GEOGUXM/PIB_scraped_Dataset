      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Shri Thaawarchand Gehlot unveils life size painting of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar at DAIC, New Delhi
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2018 1:08PM by PIB Delhi


    In order to mark the “Mahaparinirvan Diwas” of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar on 06 th December, 2018, the Union Minister of Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Thaawarchand Gehlot and the Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment, Shri Vijay Sampla unveiled a life size painting of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar at Dr. Ambedkar International Centre (DAIC), 15 Janpath, New Delhi.
