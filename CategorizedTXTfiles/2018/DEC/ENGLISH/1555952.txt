      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Safe City projects of more than Rs 2919 Crores appraised under Nirbhaya fund so far
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2018 4:28PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has appraised projects on Safe City worth Rs. 2919.55 crores under Nirbhaya fund for eight major cities of the country to make them safer for women. The city-wise details are as follows:

                                                    
Sl. No.  City       Appraised amount (Rs. in crores)
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
1        Delhi      663.67                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
2        Mumbai     252.00                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
3        Chennai    425.06                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
4        Ahmedabad  253.00                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
5        Kolkata    181.32                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
6        Bengaluru  667.00                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
7        Hyderabad  282.50                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
8        Lucknow    195.00                          
                                                    
                                                    
                                                    
         Total      2919.55                         
                                                    
                                                    

    The projects are proposed to be implemented by MHA during the period from 2018-19 to 2020-21.


    The above Information was given by Minister of State for Women and Child Development,DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR in reply to an Unstarred Question in the Lok Sabha, today.
