      Shri Bharat Bhushan Vyas takes Oath of Office and Secrecy as Member, UPSC
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2018 2:49PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Bharat Bhushan Vyas, IAS (J&K 1986) (Retd.) took the Oath of Office and Secrecy as Member, Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) today. The Oath was administered by Shri Arvind Saxena, Chairman, UPSC.


    He was born on 15/11/1957. He has done B.Sc. (Botany). He joined Indian Administrative Service in 1986. He served in State & Centre in various capacities and retired as Chief Secretary, Jammu & Kashmir. Before assuming charge as Member, UPSC, he was serving as Adviser to the Governor, Jammu & Kashmir.
