      Prime Minister's Office

      PM pays tributes to Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 25 DEC 2018 10:28AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid his tributes to Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his Jayanti.


    “ Tributes to AtalJi on his Jayanti. We reiterate our commitment towards creating the India he dreamt of ”, the Prime Minister said.
