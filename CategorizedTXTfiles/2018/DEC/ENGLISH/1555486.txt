      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 11 DEC 2018 10:57AM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, as advised by the Prime Minister, has accepted the resignation of Shri Upendra Kushwaha from the Union Council of Ministers, with immediate effect, under clause (2) of Article 75 of the Constitution.
