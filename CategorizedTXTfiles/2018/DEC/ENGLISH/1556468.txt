      Ministry of Home Affairs

      RAF Battalions
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2018 4:56PM by PIB Delhi


    The total number of sanctioned RAF Battalions is 15 including 5 Battalions sanctioned in 2016. The bases of these Battalions are in the States of Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Telangana, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Bihar, Haryana, Karnataka, Delhi and Uttar Pradesh.


    This was stated by the Union Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today.
