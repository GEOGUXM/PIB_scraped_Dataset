      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Appointment of Shri Ramphal Pawar as Director, NCRB
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2018 4:26PM by PIB Delhi


    Consequent upon curtailment of his present tenure as Joint Secretary, NATGRID, the competent authority has approved appointment of Shri Ramphal Pawar, IPS (WB: 88), as Director (ADG level), NCRB against the existing vacancy from the date of joining the post and up to tenure extendable as per IPS Tenure Policy or till further orders, whichever is earlier.
