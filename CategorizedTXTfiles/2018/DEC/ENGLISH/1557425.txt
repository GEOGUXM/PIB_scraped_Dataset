      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Industrial Corridor in Punjab
      Posted On: 27 DEC 2018 11:49AM by PIB Delhi


    Amritsar Kolkata Industrial Corridor (AKIC) Project planned by the Government of India covers seven States including Punjab. In consultation with State Government, Rajpura- Patiala has been identified as an Integrated Manufacturing Cluster (IMC) for development in the State of Punjab. No other Industrial Corridor is planned in Punjab.


    This information was given by the Minister of State of Commerce and Industry, C. R. Chaudhary, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha.
