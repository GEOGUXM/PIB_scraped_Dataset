      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of former US President George H .W. Bush
      Posted On: 01 DEC 2018 4:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of former US President George H .W. Bush.


    “Condolences to the Bush family and the people of USA on the passing away of former President George H .W. Bush. He was a leading statesman at a critical period of world history.


    Former President George H. W. Bush was passionate about strong relations between India and USA. His presence will be sorely missed. May he rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
