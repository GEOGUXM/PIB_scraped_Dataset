      Department of Space

      Agreement with ROSCOSMOS
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2018 5:07PM by PIB Delhi


    ISRO and ROSCOSMOS have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on 5 th October 2018 on ‘Joint Activities in the field of Human Spaceflight Programme’. Specific missions to International Space Station on Soyuz have not been included in the MoU.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
