      Ministry of Railways

      Enhancement of Combined Reservation Quota of lower berths for Senior Citizens and Female Passengers
      Posted On: 27 DEC 2018 5:29PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Railways has decided that the combined reservation quota of lower berths earmarked for senior citizens, female passengers 45 years of age and above and pregnant women may be enhanced as under:-
