      Ministry of Rural Development

      Women Participation Under Mgnregs
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2018 5:47PM by PIB Delhi


    The women participation rate in Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) has been 55% in FY 2015-16, 56% in FY 2016-17, 53% in FY 2017-18 and 53% in 2018-19 (as on 07.12.2018) which has been above the statutory requirement of 1/3 rd women participation under MGNREGS, as per the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005.


    The State/UT-wise details of employment provided to women under MGNREGS during the last three years are given at Annexure .


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Rural Development, Shri Ram Kripal Yadav today in Lok Sabha.
