      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Foodgrain Prices Under National Food Security Act
      Posted On: 11 DEC 2018 5:51PM by PIB Delhi


    The highly subsidized prices of foodgrains under the National Food Security Act,2013 (NFSA) i.e. Rs.1/2/3 per kg. For coarsegrains/ wheat/ rice have been continued upto June, 2019.


    Currently, about 80.72 crore persons are covered under NFSA for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains. The budget allocation for food subsidy in 2018-19 is Rs.169123 crore.


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri C.R. Chaudhary in Lok Sabha.
