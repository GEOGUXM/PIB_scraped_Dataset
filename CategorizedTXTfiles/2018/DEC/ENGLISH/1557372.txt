      Prime Minister to visit Himachal Pradesh tomorrow
      Posted On: 26 DEC 2018 4:31PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Narendra Modi, will visit Himachal Pradesh on December 27, 2018. He will join in the celebrations to mark one-year of the State Government. He will release a document highlighting the achievements of the Government. The Prime Minister will address a public rally in Dharamshala in Kangra district. He will also interact with the beneficiaries of various Government schemes on the occasion.
