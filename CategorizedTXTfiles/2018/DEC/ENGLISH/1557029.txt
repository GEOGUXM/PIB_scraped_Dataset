      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Committee constituted to decide parameters and operational modalities for pilot study on Conditional Cash Transfer in lieu of THR
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2018 7:14PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Women and Child Development has decided that a Pilot on Conditional Cash Transfer is to be carried out in few blocks of two districts each in Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan. The decision has been taken based on the direction of National Council on Indiaâ€™s Nutrition Challenges. The National Council during t he 3 rd Meeting held on 14 th November, 2018 under the Chairmanship of Dr. Rajiv Kumar, Vice Chairman, NITI Aayog decided to do the pilot for Conditional Cash Transfer in lieu of Take Home Ration.


    A Committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Dr. Vinod K. Paul, Member, Health & Nutrition, NITI Aayog for deciding the parameters and operational modalities for Conditional Cash Transfer . The members of the Committee are from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Department of Financial Services, Department of Food and Public Distribution, NITI Aayog, State of Rajasthan, State of Uttar Pradesh, UNICEF, BMGF, DIMAGI etc.
