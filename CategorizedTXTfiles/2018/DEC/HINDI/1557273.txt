      Prime Minister's Office

      PM pays tributes to Pt. Madan Mohan Malaviya on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 25 DEC 2018 10:26AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid his tributes to Pt. Madan Mohan Malaviya on his Jayanti.


    “ Remembering Mahamana on his Jayanti. He made everlasting contributions to our freedom movement and in education ”, the Prime Minister said.
