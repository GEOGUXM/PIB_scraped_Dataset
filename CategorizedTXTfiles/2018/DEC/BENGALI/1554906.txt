      Ministry of Railways

      SER observes Mahaparinirwan Diwas
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2018 6:52PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 06, 2018


    South Eastern Railway (SER) observed the 63 rd Mahaparinirwan Diwas of Bharat Ratna Dr. B. R. Ambedkar at its Headquarters at Garden Reach in Kolkata today.


    Sri Anirban Datta, Addl. General Manager, South Eastern Railway paid floral tribute to the Bharat Ratna Dr. B. R. Ambedkar. The Principal Head of the Departments along with other Senior Railway officers and staff of members paid homage to Dr. B. R. Ambedkar.


    Mahaparinirwan Diwas of Bharat Ratna Dr. B. R. Ambedkar was also observed at four Divisional offices of Adra, Kharagpur, Chakradharpur and Ranchi.
