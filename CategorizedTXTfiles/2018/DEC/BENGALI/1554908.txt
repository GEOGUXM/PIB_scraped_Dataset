      Ministry of External Affairs

      Post Office Passport Seva Kendra to be inaugurated at Coochbehar on December 14
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2018 6:54PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 6 th December, 2018


    A Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) will be inaugurated at Coochbehar Head Post Office in Coochbehar on December 14, 2018 (Friday). Initially, on December 13 and December 14, appointments will be given since 10 a.m. to 05 (five) and 10 (ten) candidates only respectively. From December 17 (Monday), 40 appointments will be processed from 10 a.m. on a daily basis. Appointments will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.in from December 10, 2018 at 1530 hours and onwards. The applicants have to pay passport fee through online payment system.


    Further, applicants are to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (in original as well as self attested photocopy) while reporting for the appointment. Detailed information of the documents required and other details can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in .
