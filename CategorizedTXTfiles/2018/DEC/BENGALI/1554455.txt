      Logistics Databank Service starts at Kolkata Port Trust
      Posted On: 03 DEC 2018 12:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 3, 2018


    A Logistics Databank Service has been launched by Kolkata Port Trust (KoPT) at Kolkata on Saturday (December 01, 2018).


    Chairman of KoPT Shri Vineet Kumar formally inaugurated the new facility and said, this project will make use of radio frequency identification technology. The aim of having a Logistics Data Bank is to track and view the movement of containers across ports to the Container Freight Stations and Inland Container Depots and end-users, he further informed. This project is being implemented in KoPT by Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor Development Corporation (DMICDC).


    The dignitaries present on the occasion included CEO & MD of DMICDC Shri Alkesh Kumar Sharma.
