    Kolkata, 3 rd October, 2018


    On the occasion of 74 th Foundation Day of Anthropological Survey of India (AnSI), veteran anthropologist and the former Director of Kerala Institute for Research Training and Development Studies (KIRTADS) Prof P.R.G. Mathur delivered the keynote address on Saturday (December 1, 2018). The Director of AnSI Prof. Vinay Kumar Srivastava was present among the dignitaries.


    The AnSI is the only research organization to pursue anthropological research in Central Government under the Ministry of Culture. Since its inception, AnSI has been following its mandate of pursuing research in socio-cultural and biological aspects of the people of India in a holistic perspective, with an emphasis on the matters of contemporary relevance and national significance.
