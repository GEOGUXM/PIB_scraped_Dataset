      JHARGRAM-PURULIA MEMU PASSENGER AND TATANAGAR-KHARAGPUR PASSENGER TO STOP AT CHIRUGODA
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2018 6:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 06, 2018


    68023 Jhargram-Purulia MEMU Pasenger and 58022 Tatanagar-Kharagpur Pasenger will stop at Chirugoda w.e.f 07.12.18 on an experimental basis for six months. 68023 Jhargram-Purulia MEMU Passenger will arrive Chirugoda at 05.49 hrs and will depart at 05.50 hrs. 58022 Tatanagar-Kharagpur Passenger will arrive Chirugoda at 19.14 hrs and will depart at 19.15 hrs.


    This decision was taken by South Eastern Railway in Kolkata today.
