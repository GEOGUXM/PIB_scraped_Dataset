      Ministry of External Affairs

      Passport Seva Camp at Sundarban Kristi Mela O Loko Sanskriti Utsab
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2018 5:07PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 12, 2018


    The Regional Passport Office under Ministry of External Affairs is organising Passport Seva Camp at Sundarban Kristi Mela O Loko Sanskriti Utsab, Kultali, South 24 Parganas from December 20 to 29, 2018.


    Applicants have been requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original & self attested Xerox copy).


    The detailed information of the documents required can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in .


    Applications in normal category (fresh/re-issue) will be entertained in the mela. Fifty applications on daily basis will be entertained from December 20 to 29, 2018.


    Appointments for the period from December 20 to 29, 2018 will be in the above mentioned website on December 17, 2018 at 3.30 pm.


    The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system. Only those applicants who have obtained appointments for the said period through online mode will be entertained.
