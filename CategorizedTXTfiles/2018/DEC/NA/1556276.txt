      Ministry of Education

      Shri Prakash Javadekar to inaugurate Sangeet Kala Sangam, a joint Presentation of KVS & NVS at New Delhi tomorrow
      Posted On: 17 DEC 2018 6:43PM by PIB Delhi


    Selected Teachers of Art & Music from Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan & Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti are going to share a common platform at Dr. Ambedkar International Centre in New Delhi tomorrow. An evening of Music and Arts organized under the aegis of Human Resource Ministry will be inaugurated by the HRD Minister Sh. Prakash Javadekar.


    Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Navoday Vidyalaya Samiti are two sister organizations of HRD Ministry. The main objective of KVS is to provide uninterrupted quality education to the wards of transferable employees of central government, while NVS provides quality education to talented students specially from rural background. Both the organizations have established high benchmarks in the field of School Education.


    This ‘Sangeet Kala Sangam’ is going to provide a National Platform to the Art and Music Teachers of KVS, where they will have an opportunity to showcase their talent before a large audience.
