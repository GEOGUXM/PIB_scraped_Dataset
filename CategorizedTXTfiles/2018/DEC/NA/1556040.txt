      PM remembers Sardar Patel on his Punya Tithi
      Posted On: 15 DEC 2018 10:09AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has remembered Sardar Patel on his Punya Tithi.


    “ लौह पुरुष सरदार वल्लभभाई पटेल को उनकी पुण्यतिथि पर मेरी विनम्र श्रद्धांजलि।


    Remembering the great Sardar Patel on his Punya Tithi. His thoughts, rich work and strong effort towards India’s unity inspire generations of Indians”, the Prime Minister said.
