      Christmas Vacation for Central Administrative Tribunal
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2018 3:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT), Ernakulam Bench will remain closed from 24 th December to 28 th December 2018 on account of Christmas vacation. During the period, the vacation Bench will hold sitting on 24 th December at 11.00 a.m. to hear urgent matters. The Registry of the Tribunal will function as usual during the vacation period and will receive only applications of urgent nature between 10. 30 a.m. and 1.00 p.m.


    IE-AB (07.12.2018)
