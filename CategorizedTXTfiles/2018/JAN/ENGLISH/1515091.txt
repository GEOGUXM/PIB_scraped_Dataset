      Ministry of Shipping

      Drafts available at Indian Ports
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2018 6:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The Maritime Agenda 2010-20 has suggested that Indian ports should strive to achieve a draft of at least 14 mtrs and up to 17 mtrs according to the potential of bigger size vessels calling at a particular port. The Minister informed that the 10 Major ports already achieved a minimum draft of 14 mtrs and the remaining Major port except Kolkata have plan to achieve this level. Details of the existing draft available at Major Ports is as follows:-
