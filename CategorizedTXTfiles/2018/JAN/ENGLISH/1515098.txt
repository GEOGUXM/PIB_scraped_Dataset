      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      NH Construction and Maintenance Proposals received from Chhattisgarh
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2018 6:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Minister of State for Road Transport & Highways, Shipping and C&F Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya while answering a question in Rajya Sabha today informed that during last three years, proposals amounting to Rs. 7497 crore and Rs.100 crore, respectively were sanctioned for Construction and Maintenance of the National Highways in the State of Chhattisgarh. The Minister said that during the last three years, funds amounting to Rs. 2102 crore and Rs. 208 crore were allocated under Development and Maintenance & Repair for the National Highways in the State of Chhattisgarh, respectively.
