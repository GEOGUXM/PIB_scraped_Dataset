      19 Ayurvedic Herbs Collection Centers have been approved/suggested across the country during 2016-17 and 2017-18
      Posted On: 05 JAN 2018 6:22PM by PIB Delhi


    Under ‘Medicinal Plants’ component of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), the Ministry of AYUSH is supporting various activities relating to cultivation, establishment of nurseries, Post-Harvest Management, IEC activities and marketing of medicinal plants/herbs throughout the country including hilly areas. Under ‘Medicinal Plants’ component of NAM Scheme there is also a provision to establish Herbs Collection Centres at the Village/District/State level.
