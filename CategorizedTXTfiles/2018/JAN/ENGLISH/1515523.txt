      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Widening of Highways in Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 04 JAN 2018 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government proposes to widen the highways, peripheral highways and bypass roads around the city of Madurai in Tamil Nadu. DPR for following projects are in progress. These projects are likely to be awarded during 2018-19 by NHAI.

     1. Madurai Ring Road from km 0.000 to km 82.600
     2. Madurai - Natham Road from km 0.000 to km 35.650
     3. Madurai - Theni road from km 0+000 to km 44+000
     4. Thirumangalam - Rajapalayam section from km 0+000 to km 81+436

    This information was given by the Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.
