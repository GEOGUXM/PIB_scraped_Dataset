      PM greets people of Tripura on their Statehood Day
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2018 10:02AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Tripura on their Statehood Day.


    “Tripura is blessed with a glorious history and industrious youngsters, whose aspirations we are committed to fulfilling”, the Prime Minister said.
