      PM greets people of Meghalaya on their Statehood Day
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2018 10:07AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Meghalaya on their Statehood Day.


    “I will always cherish the affection of the people of Meghalaya. The state’s natural beauty is enchanting. I pray for good health and wellbeing of the people of Meghalaya”, the Prime Minister said.
