      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Aanchal Thakur on winning India’s first international medal in skiing at FIS International Skiing Competition in Turkey
      Posted On: 10 JAN 2018 10:50AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Aanchal Thakur on winning India’s first international medal in skiing at FIS International Skiing Competition in Turkey.


    “Well done Aanchal Thakur for winning an international medal in skiing! The entire nation is ecstatic on your historic accomplishment at the FIS International Skiing Competition in Turkey. Wishing you the very best for your future endeavours”, the Prime Minister said.
