      Ministry of Culture

      ASI has conducted survey to trace 50 missing monuments in the country: Culture Minister
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2018 3:50PM by PIB Delhi


      Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has conducted survey regarding missing monuments in the country and status is as under:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
  Number of monuments/sites affected due to rapid urbanization:  14                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
  Number of monuments/sites submerged under reservoir/dam:       12                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
  Number of monuments/sites which are untraceable:               24
