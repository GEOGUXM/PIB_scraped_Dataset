      PM greets people of Manipur on their Statehood Day
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2018 10:05AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Manipur on their Statehood Day.


    “May Manipur continue to scale new heights of growth in the times to come”, the Prime Minister said.
