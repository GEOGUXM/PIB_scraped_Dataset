      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      8 metropolitan cities selected by the Government to address issues of women safety
      Posted On: 05 JAN 2018 5:59PM by PIB Delhi


    To address the issues of women safety including in public spaces and public transport, Government of India has selected eight metropolitan cities namely, Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad and Lucknow in the meeting of ‘Steering Committee’ held on 30 th November, 2017 under the chairmanship of Secretary, Ministry of Home Affairs.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Women and Child Development, Dr. Virendra Kumar in reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
