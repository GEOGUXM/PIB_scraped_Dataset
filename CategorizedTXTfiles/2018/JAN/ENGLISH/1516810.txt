      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Financial Assistance of Rs. 2 lakhs to Ms. Gohela Boro, Archer from scheme ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons’
      Posted On: 16 JAN 2018 2:09PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of State for Youth Affairs & Sports (Independent Charge) Col. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore has sanctioned ex-gratia assistance of Rs. 2.00 lakh from the scheme ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons’ to Ms. Gohela Boro, National level Archer for her medical treatment.


    The Scheme ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons’ provides for lump sum financial assistance for medical treatment to outstanding sportspersons now living in indigent circumstances.
