      PM bows to Swami Vivekananda on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 12 JAN 2018 11:12AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has bowed to Swami Vivekananda, on his Jayanti .


    “ I bow to Swami Vivekananda on his Jayanti. Today, on National Youth Day I salute the indomitable energy and enthusiasm of our youngsters, who are the builders of New India ”, the Prime Minister said.
