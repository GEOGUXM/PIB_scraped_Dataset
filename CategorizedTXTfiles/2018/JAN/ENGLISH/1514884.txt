      Ministry of Finance

      Cessation of 8 % GOI Savings (Taxable) Bonds, 2003 with effect from the close of banking business on Tuesday, the 02nd January, 2018.
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2018 5:14PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of India (GoI) announced here today that 8% GOI Savings (Taxable) Bonds, 2003 shall cease for subscription with effect from the close of banking business on Tuesday, the 02 nd January, 2018.
