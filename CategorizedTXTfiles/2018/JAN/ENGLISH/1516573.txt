      President of India to visit Maharashtra on January 13 and 14, 2018
      Posted On: 12 JAN 2018 6:10PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Maharashtra on January 13 and 14, 2018.


    On January 14, 2018, the President will inaugurate the ‘Economic Democracy Conclave’ at Thane, being organised by Rambhau Mhalgi Prabodhini with an aim to create a platform for young entrepreneurs and self-employed individuals and provide them motivation, networking and mentoring. Later in the day, the President will visit the Global Vipassana Pagoda in Mumbai, before returning to Delhi.
