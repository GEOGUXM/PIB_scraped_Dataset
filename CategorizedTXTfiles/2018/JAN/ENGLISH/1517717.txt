    Shri Anil Baijal, Lt. Governor of Delhi has inaugurated the Dynamic Facade Lighting of India Gate in the presence of Dr. Subhash RamraoBhamre, Minister of State for Defence and Shri Hardeep S. Puri, Minister of State(I/C) Housing & Urban Affairs and other officials of various departments, here today. This work has been executed by Central Public Works Department.


    A total of 130 RGB narrow / wide beam energy efficient LED fittings with computerized control, have been installed for Dynamic Facade Lighting due to which there is 75% energy saving as compared to earlier conventional light fittings. The life of this light fittings is about 1 lakh burning hours. This Dynamic Facade Lighting has 16 million colours combinations which will provide aesthetic appearance of India Gate during night time.
