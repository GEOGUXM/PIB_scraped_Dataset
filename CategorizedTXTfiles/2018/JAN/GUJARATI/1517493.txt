      PM pays tributes to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 23 JAN 2018 4:15PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on his birth anniversary.


    “The valour of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose makes every Indian proud. We bow to this great personality on his Jayanti”, the Prime Minister said.
