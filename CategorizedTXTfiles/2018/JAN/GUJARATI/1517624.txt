      PM salutes the skills, strengths and fortitude of the girl child on National Girl Child Day
      Posted On: 24 JAN 2018 3:04PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted the skills, strengths and fortitude of the girl child on National Girl Child Day.


    “We salute the skills, strengths and fortitude of the girl child. We are proud of the accomplishments of the girl child in various fields”, the Prime Minister said.
