      PM greets the nation on New Year
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2018 4:20PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has extended his New Year greetings to the nation.


    “Wishing you all a happy 2018! I pray that this year brings joy, prosperity and good health in everyone's lives.”, the Prime Minister said.
