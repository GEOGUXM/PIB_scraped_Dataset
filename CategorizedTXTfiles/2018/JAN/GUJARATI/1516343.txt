      Prime Minister's Office

      PM pays homage to Former PM Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri, on his Punya Tithi
      Posted On: 11 JAN 2018 2:07PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid homage to Former Prime Minister of India Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri, on his Punya Tithi.


    “We pay homage to Shastri Ji on his Punya Tithi. His impeccable service and courageous leadership will be remembered for generations to come”, the Prime Minister said.
