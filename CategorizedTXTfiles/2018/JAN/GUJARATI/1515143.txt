      Prime Minister's Office

      PM meets officials from PMO and SPG
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2018 9:48PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today met and exchanged new year greetings with PMO officials and SPG personnel, at Lok Kalyan Marg. Over 1000 people were present at the interaction. The Prime Minister appreciated the good work being done by them, and encouraged them to keep up their efforts in future as well.
