      The Last Date for Submission of Aadhar and Mobile details to POs is 31-3-2018.
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2018 4:25PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Last Date for Submission of Aadhar and Mobile details to POs is 31-3-2018.


    As per Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Gazette Notification dated 29-09-2017 all Post office Saving Bank accounts holders, Public Provident Fund (PPF) accounts holders, Kisan Vikas Patra holders etc. are requested to lik Aadhar Number and Mobile Numbers into their Post office Savings Bank accounts, Public Provident Fund (PPF) accounts, Kisan Vikas Patras immediately at nearby Post offices. The last date for submission of Aadhar and Mobile details to POs is 31.03.2018. This may please be noted.


    For Seeding of Aadhar Numbers and Mobile Numbers, you are requested to contact your nearest post office and kindly co-operate.
