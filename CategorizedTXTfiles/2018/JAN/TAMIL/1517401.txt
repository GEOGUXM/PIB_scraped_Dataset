      JUSTICE MANMOHAN SINGH ASSUMES CHARGE AS CHAIRMAN OF IPAB
      Posted On: 22 JAN 2018 4:17PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri Justice Manmohan Singh has assumed Charge as Chairman of IPAB on 1.1.2018 and the Bench of Intellectual Property Appellate Board has started its hearing on 18.1.2018 after a gap of 20 months from May 2016. The Bench consisting of Shri Justice Manmohan Singh, Hon’ble Chairman and Shri Sanjeev K. Chaswal Hon’ble Technical Member TM started hearing at New Delhi 18.1.2018. The Bench heard about 48 cases on its first day of hearing.


    The Board will be hearing the matters in Delhi, Chennai, Mumbai, Kolkata and Ahmedabad in coming months. A hearing schedule upto the month of May 2018 is available in the official website:// www.ipabindia.org


    The Intellectual Property Appellate Board (IPAB) has been constituted to hear appeals against the decisions of the Registrar under Trade Marks Act, 1999, Geographical Indications of goods (Registration and Protection) Act, 1999 besides Patent Act, 1970. The Copyright Board has been merged with IPAB vide Finance Bill 2017.


    Presently IPAB is having Hon’ble Shri Justice Manmohan Singh as Chairman and Hon’ble Shri Sanjeev K. Chaswal as Technical Member (Trade Marks). The IPAB has started hearing trade marks cases on 18.01.2018 after a gap of 20 months.
