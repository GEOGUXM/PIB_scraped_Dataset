      MVVS Murthy takes charge as Director General (South) of Press Information Bureau
      Posted On: 16 JAN 2018 3:40PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri. MVVS Murthy, who belongs to the Indian Information Service of 1984 batch, has taken charge as Director General (South), Press Information Bureau, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India with effect from today. He is replacing Shri K. Muthu kumar who has been transferred to Mumbai.


    Prior coming to Chennai, Shri. Murthy has worked in different media like AIR, DAVP, DFP and PIB at New Delhi, Hyderabad and Bangalore.
