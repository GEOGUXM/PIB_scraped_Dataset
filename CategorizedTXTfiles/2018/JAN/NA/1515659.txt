      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Shri Nitin Gadkari to inaugurate ASEAN India Pravasi Bharatiya Divas at Singapore tomorrow
      Posted On: 05 JAN 2018 4:22PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Nitin Gadkari, Union Minister of Road Transport & Highways, Shipping and Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation is in Singapore to inaugurate the ASEAN India Pravasi Bharatiya Divas tomorrow, the 6 th of January 2018.


    Pravasi Bharatiya Divas is celebrated every year to strengthen the engagement of the overseas Indian community with the Government, reconnect them with their roots and celebrate their achievements and contributions. Shri Gadkari will attend the session on ASEAN India Economic Partnership at the Marina Bay Sands Expo and Convention Centre tomorrow morning.
