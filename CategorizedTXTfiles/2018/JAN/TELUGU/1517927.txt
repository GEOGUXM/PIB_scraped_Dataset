      Prime Minister's Office

      MANN KI BAAT ON JANUARY 28, 2018
      Posted On: 25 JAN 2018 6:41PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi will address the nation through “Mann ki Baat” on January 28th, 2018 (Sunday) at 11.00 a.m. through the entire All India Radio network encompassing medium wave, shortwave, FM, Vividhbharati and LRS and will be relayed by all AIR stations of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.


    Telugu version of this programme Mann Ki Baat “ మనసులో మాట ” will be broadcast immediately after the PM’s speech and repeat broadcast of telugu version will also be made at 8.00 p.m. from all the stations of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh states on the same day informed M. Rama Rao, Programme Head-AIR.
