      STATE LEVEL DAK ADALAT ON JANUARY 24
      Posted On: 11 JAN 2018 6:36PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Department of Posts, Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Telangana Circle, Hyderabad is conducting a ‘96 TH Dak Adalat’ on January 24 (Wednesday) to hear the complaints and grievances of the public relating to postal services. The Adalat will be held at 11.00 a.m. at the Conference Hall, 2 nd Floor, O/o the Chief Postmaster General, Telangana Circle – 500 001.


    The complaints and grievances from the public may be sent in postal envelop or cover, duly super scribed with the words ‘96 th Dak Adalat’ and addressed to Shri K. Venkata Rami Reddy, Assistant Postmaster General (PG), O/o the Chief Postmaster General, Telangana Circle, Hyderabad – 500001. The last date for receiving complaints and grievances is 19 th January, 2018, according to a press note issued by Asst. Postmaster General, Telangana Circle, Hyderabad – 500 001.
