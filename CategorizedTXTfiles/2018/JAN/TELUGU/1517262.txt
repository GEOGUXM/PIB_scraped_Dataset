      NEW MODEL FOR ORGANIZING LEGAL SERVICES CAMP IN TIRUMALGIRI ON JANUARY 23, 2018
      Posted On: 19 JAN 2018 7:03PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The National Legal Services Authority (Hon'ble Supreme Court of India), New Delhi, has formulated A "New Model for Organizing Legal Services Camp" in pursuance of the same.


    The authority intends to conduct Legal Services Camp on 23-01-2018 at Thirumalagiri foot ball ground at 10:00am, Secunderabad, with core themes such as right of Labour, Rights of Senior Citizens and Rights of Women and Children.


    In this camp the concerned departments i.e., Department of Labour, Deparment of Social Welfare, Deparment of Health, Deparment of Women, BC Welfare, Social Welfare, Minority Welfare, Youth Department, Civil Supplies Department, and NGOs involved in social services and skill development for Women are participating.


    The beneficiaries who are attending in the said camp can present their applications according to the eligibility norms fixed by the above departments and are also required to present copies of the documents like Aadhar card, Voter ID, Ration card and passport size photos. The applicaitons will be processed through Legal Services Authority, of the concerned department.


    The public attending the camp can also avail services of the District Legal Services Authority, Hyderabad, for any type of legal issues through the Panel of Lawyers who are participating in the camp.
