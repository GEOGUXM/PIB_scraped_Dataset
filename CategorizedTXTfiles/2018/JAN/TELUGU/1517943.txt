      REPUBLIC DAY-2018 CELEBRATIONS SARDAR VALLABHBHAI PATEL NATIONAL POLICE ACADEMY
      Posted On: 26 JAN 2018 6:01PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The 69 th Republic Day Celebrations was held in the Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel National Police Academy with a big fanfare. The Director of the Academy, Mrs. D.R. Doley Barman took the salute of the parade put up by the uniformed staff of the Academy and unfurled the National Flag.


    During the occasion, the Director reminded the IPS Probationers of 70 RR that on our shoulders lies the heavy responsibility of upholding the values of justice and equality so that the citizens of India reaffirm their faith in the Indian Constitution.


    The Director presented the medals to the winners of Union Home Minister’s Medal for Excellence in Police Training, President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service and Indian Police Medal for Meritorious Service announced in 2017 and also congratulated the Equestrian Team of National Police Academy which was declared the Overall Champion in the 36 th All India Police Equestrian Championship held in 2017.
