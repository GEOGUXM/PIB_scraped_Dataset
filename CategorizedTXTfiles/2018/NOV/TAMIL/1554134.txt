      ONE MORE PERSON ARRESTED IN FAKE GST BILL TRADING RACKET
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2018 6:06PM by PIB Chennai


    In continuation of further investigations on the issue of fake GST invoices racket, the officers of Headquarters Preventive Unit, Chennai North GST and Central Excise Commissionerate have kept a watch on the activities of bill traders.


    The officers have noticed that Dilip Kumar, aged 45 years, who has availed input tax credit in fake companies based on fake invoices received and passed on the same to other companies so that they can avail input tax credit without receipt of goods.


    After the confession of the offence, he has been arrested on 27.11.2018. Prima facie it is evidenced that he has received fake GST invoices on the commodities like Steel, Chemicals. Plastics, electronic goods, Leather goods etc. and passed on the same, with an intention of fraud and to pass on the Input Tax Credit without movement of Goods. Further investigation is in progress.


    The Department has intensified action on such fraudsters to curb the evasion of GST by bill traders in future.
