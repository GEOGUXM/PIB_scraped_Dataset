      Gandhi Corner in Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 07 NOV 2018 12:44PM by PIB Chennai


    A dialer phone, that you get to hear Gandhiji’s voice, a miniature of Sevagram Ashram in Maharashtra, Petti charkha(s), interesting artefact replicas,images depicting Gandhiji’s visit(s) to Tamil Nadu and his interaction with regional leaders, public and many more will catch the eye of passengers travelling through Chennai Airport hereafter.


    ‘GandhiCorner’, an exhibition commemorating the 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi was inaugurated in domestic arrival by Ms.Anjali Mohapatra, President, AAI Kalyanmayee on 26.10.2018.


    The exhibition with giant screens playing exclusive videos/ films of Gandhi, is intended at sensitising passengers -essentially younger generation on the life and works of Father of our Nation.


    The exhibition which has been receiving rave reviews from passengers has been conceived by Chennai Airport in collaboration with National Gandhi Museum, Raj Ghat, New Delhi will be continue during the government observance of 150th birth anniversary celebrations as notified by Ministry of Culture.
