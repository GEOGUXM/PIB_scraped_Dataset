      CFSI to give free DVDs on the occasion of Children’s Day
      Posted On: 07 NOV 2018 1:20PM by PIB Chennai


    As a part of Children’s Day celebrations, Children’s Film Society, Chennai will be supplying DVDs of children’s films free of cost to schools for screening to their children. Interested schools can contact CFSI on 24981159 to get copies of DVDS.


    Children’s Film Society, India is a sole organization under Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, GOI, producing films exclusively for children. The films are not only entertaining but also inculcate some moral values in young minds says Ms. V S Agila, Asst. Distribution Officer, in a press release issued by Children’s Film Society, India, Chennai.
