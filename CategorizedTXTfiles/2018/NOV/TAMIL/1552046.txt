      GST Special Helpdesks for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME)
      Posted On: 07 NOV 2018 12:48PM by PIB Chennai


    Government of India has launched a support and outreach initiative for Micro, small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) in 80 districts across the country on 2 November, 2018. As part of this initiative, GST and Central Excise Department has established GST Special Helpdesks in Thiruvallur and Vellore districts of Tamil Nadu to assist Micro, small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) in all matters relating to Goods and Services Tax (GST) . The officers will also help them in overcoming technical difficulties in paying tax, availing input tax credit and claiming GST refunds. For the next hundred days the department undertakes to process the MSME refund claims on top priority. In addition, the department is planning to conduct special outreach programmes in these two districts in cooperation with MSME trade bodies.
