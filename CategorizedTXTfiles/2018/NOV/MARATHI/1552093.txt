      Ministry of Defence

      Army Recruitment Rally in December
      Posted On: 07 NOV 2018 1:47PM by PIB Mumbai


    Panaji, November 7, 2018


    An Army Recruitment Rally is being conducted at Kolhapur, Maharashtra from December 6 to December 16, 2018. Recruitment is being conducted for Soldier General Duty (GD), Soldier Clerk / Store Keeper Technical, Soldier Technical, Soldier Technical Aviation / Ammunition, Soldier Technical (NA and Vet), Soldier Tradesman, JCO (RT) (Religious Teacher) and Havildar (Surveyor Auto Cartographer) categories. The Rally is applicable to the candidates of six districts of Maharashtra i.e. Satara, Sangli, Solapur, Sindhurdurg, Kolhapur and Ratnagiri and North Goa and South Goa districts.


    Candidates are required to register and apply online on the Indian Army website www.joinindianarmy.nic.in between October 7, 2018 to November 20, 2018. Admit Cards of Registered candidates will be posted to their e-mail ID after November 21, 2018. Candidates are advised to check educational qualification before applying for specific category.
