      Ministry of Railways

      CELEBRATION OF CONSTITUTION DAY BY N.F.RAILWAY CONSTRUCTION ORGANISATION
      Posted On: 26 NOV 2018 3:51PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, November 26, 2018.


    Officers and staff of N.F.Railway Construction today observed the Constitution Day in their HQ premises. Sri N.K.Prasad, General Manager/Construction/N.F.Railway read out the Preamble of our Constitution which was followed by other officers and staff.


    It may be mentioned here that on 26 th day of November 1949, the Constituent Assembly adopt, enact and gave ourselves this Constitution.
