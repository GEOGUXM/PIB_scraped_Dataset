      Ministry of Communications

      Special discounts for outstanding BSNL bills
      Posted On: 08 NOV 2018 4:42PM by PIB Kolkata


    - Kolkata, 8 th November, 2018


    BSNL has started a new scheme ‘Project Dhanalakshmi’ for providing special discounts from 1-3 % against outstanding bills on landline/broadband, enterprise business and mobile services during Dussehra and Deepavali. Calcutta Telephones BSNL Customer Service Centre/ Bill Collection centre will remain open on Sundays/Holidays up to 23-11-2018 for collection against outstanding bills. This was stated in a press release issued by BSNL today.
