      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      CGHS doctor to present study on rheumatic heart disease among Indian school children at U.S.A
      Posted On: 01 NOV 2018 6:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    - Kolkata, 1 st November, 2018


    Dr. P.C. Hazra, a Senior Chief Medical Officer under Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) has been invited by the American Heart Association at Chicago, Illinois in U.S.A to present a clinical study on symptom-based screening of Rheumatic Heart Disease (RHD) and development of an effective tool for that purpose. Dr. Hazra had conducted the study on 8646 number of school-going children at Garbeta in West Midnapore district.
