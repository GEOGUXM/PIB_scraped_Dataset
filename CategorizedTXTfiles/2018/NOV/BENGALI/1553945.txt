      Ministry of Railways

      A Public and Passenger Awareness Campaign displayed by the Eastern Railway in Press Club, Kolkata
      Posted On: 26 NOV 2018 9:45PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, November 26, 2018.


    In order to bring awareness regarding safety at railway stations and railway level crossings among the public a Public and Passenger Awareness Campaign was displayed by the Eastern Railway in Press Club, Kolkata today. Inaugurating the display, Shri Harindra Rao, General Manager, Eastern Railway said, the Public safety is the top most priority of the Railways. People must be aware of safety measures being taken by the Railways and must follow them to avoid untoward incidents. Our life is most precious and we must take care of it, he added.
