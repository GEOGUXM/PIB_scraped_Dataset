      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      WCD Ministry nominates three Members of NCW
      Posted On: 16 NOV 2018 5:30PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Women and Child Development, In pursuance of Section 3 of the National Commission for Women Act, 1990, has nominated the following as Members of the National Commission for Women:

     1. Smt. Chandramukhi Devi.
     2. Smt. Soso Shaiza
     3. Smt. Kamlesh Gautam,

    They shall hold office for a period of three years or till the age of 65 years or until further orders from the date of assumption of charge of office, whichever is the earlier.
