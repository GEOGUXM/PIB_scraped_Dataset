      Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers

      Union Minister Shri D.V. Sadananda Gowda assumes charge of the Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers
      Posted On: 14 NOV 2018 1:23PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of Statistics & Programme Implementation, Shri D. V. Sadananda Gowda, has assumed charge of the Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers, in addition to his existing portfolio, here today.


    Dignitaries present on the occasion included Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping and Chemicals & Fertilizers, Shri Mansukh L. Mandaviya, along with other senior officers of the Ministry.
