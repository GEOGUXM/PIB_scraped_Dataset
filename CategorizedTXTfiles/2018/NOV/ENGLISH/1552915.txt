      Bharat electronics Ltd pays Rs. 64.41 Crorefinal Dividend
      Posted On: 15 NOV 2018 7:23PM by PIB Delhi


    Navratna Defence PSU Bharat Electronics Ltd (BEL) has paid a total dividend of 200 per cent to the Government of India for financial Year 2017-18. ShriM V Gowtama, Chairman & Managing Director ofBELpresented the 40 per cent final dividend cheque for Rs. 64.41crorefor the year 2017-18, payable on the shares held by the President of India to Raksha Mantri SmtNirmala Sitharaman in New Delhi today. The Interim Dividend of 160 per cent (on face value of Re. 1 per equity share) was paid to the Government of India in February 2018.
