      Vice President Greets people on the eve of Milad-un-Nabi
      Posted On: 20 NOV 2018 4:07PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted people of our country on the auspicious occasion of Milad-un-Nabi/Id-E-Milad. In a message, he said that Prophet Mohammad showed humanity the righteous path of compassion and universal brotherhood.


    Following is the text of Vice President’s message:


    “I convey my warm greetings and best wishes to the people of our country on the occasion of Milad-un-Nabi/Id-E-Milad – celebrated as the Birthday of Prophet Mohammad.


    Prophet Mohammad showed humanity the righteous path of compassion and universal brotherhood.


    May the eternal teachings of Prophet Mohammad continue to guide people in building a peaceful and harmonious society.”
