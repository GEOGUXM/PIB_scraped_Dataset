      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles loss of lives due to bus accident in Mandya, Karnataka
      Posted On: 24 NOV 2018 5:37PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives due to bus accident in Mandya, Karnataka.


    “Deeply pained by the bus accident in Karnataka’s Mandya. My thoughts are with the families of the deceased. May God give them strength in this hour of sadness”, the Prime Minister said.
