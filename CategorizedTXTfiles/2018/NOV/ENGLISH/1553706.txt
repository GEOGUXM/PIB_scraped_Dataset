      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Mary Kom on winning Gold in Women’s World Boxing Championships
      Posted On: 24 NOV 2018 5:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Mary Kom for winning a Gold in Women’s World Boxing Championships.


    “A proud moment for Indian sports. Congratulations to Mary Kom for winning a Gold in the Women’s World Boxing Championships. The diligence with which she’s pursued sports and excelled at the world stage is extremely inspiring. Her win is truly special”, the Prime Minister said.
