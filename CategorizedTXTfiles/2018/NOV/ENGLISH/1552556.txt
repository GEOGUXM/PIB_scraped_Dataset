      Envoys of Four Nations present Credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 13 NOV 2018 1:07PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassadors/High Commissioners of Vietnam, Latvia, Jordan and Sri Lanka presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (November 13, 2018).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Mr Pham Sanh Chau, Ambassador of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam


    2. Mr Artis Bertulis, Ambassador of the Republic of Latvia


    3. Mr Mohamed Salam Jameel A. F. El-Kayed, Ambassador of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan


    4. Mr Kalupage Austin Fernando, High Commissioner of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
