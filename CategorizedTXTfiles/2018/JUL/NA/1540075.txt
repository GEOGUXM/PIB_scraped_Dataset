    The steps taken by the Government to make NCC attractive to students in the Country are:-

  * Free institutional training is imparted by service personnel to all NCC Cadets.             
                                                                                                
  * Provision of free set of uniform to cadets studying in Govt. institutions.                  
                                                                                                
  * Refreshment allowance to cadets after NCC parades from State Govt. funds.                   
                                                                                                
  * No fees charged from Students (Full cost is borne by the Central/State/Institute).          
  * Membership to Cadet Welfare Society (CWS).                                                  
                                                                                                
  * Issue of A, B & C certificates which has varying benefits / concessions for:-               
  * Higher Education.                                                                           
  * (ii) Employment.                                                                            
                                                                                                
  * Opportunity to visit foreign countries through NCC youth Exchange Programme.                
                                                                                                
  * Exposure to adventure activities like rock craft, mountaineering, etc.                      
                                                                                                
  * Provision of training in SSB coaching, personality development, etc.                        
                                                                                                
  * Opportunity for handling of weapons, equestrian training, ship building, Microlites, etc.   
                                                                                                
  * Concession/priority in selection into Armed Forces.                                         
                                                                                                
  * State Governments are also providing varying benefits to their cadets with NCC certificates.
