      Ministry of Finance

      Creating Income Generating Activities and Employment under Mudra Yojana
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2018 4:03PM by PIB Delhi


    Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) provides access to institutional finance to unfunded micro / small business units by extending loans upto Rs.10 lakh for manufacturing, processing, trading, services and activities allied to agriculture, which help in creating income generating activities and employment. Overdraft facility upto Rs.5,000/- under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is also covered under PMMY.
