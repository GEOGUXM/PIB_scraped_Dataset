      Ministry of Shipping

      Promotion of Inland Water Transport
      Posted On: 30 JUL 2018 5:51PM by PIB Delhi


    The Minister of State for Shipping, Road Transport and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today informed that the development of viable National Waterways is being undertaken for the movement of cargo and passenger vessels. Besides, under the Sagarmala Programme, a Central Sector Scheme is being implemented for providing support to Major/Non-Major Ports/State Governments for creation of infrastructure to promote movement of cargo/passengers by sea/National Waterways.
