      President of India to grace the Shiromani Kabir Prakat Diwas celebrations in Fatehabad tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 JUL 2018 2:15PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Haryana (Fatehabad) tomorrow (July 15, 2018) to grace the Shiromani Kabir Prakat Diwas (Kabir Jayanti). This function is being organised by the Dhanak Sabha Fatehabad and the Sant Kabir Dhanak Samaj Karamchari Welfare Association, Haryana.
