      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Foreign Funds
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2018 5:19PM by PIB Delhi


    Registration of 18864 FCRA registered associations/institutions has been cancelled from the year 2011 till now and they have been barred from receiving foreign funds. List of such associations/ institutions is available on the website: www.fcraonline.nic.in .


    One time opportunity to upload the pending Annual Returns (containing details of their income and expenditure, receipts of funds from abroad and balance sheets) without penalty was given to associations vide public notice dated 12 th May 2017. This amnesty window for uploading the pending Annual Returns was provided for one month starting from 15 th May 2017 till 14 th June 2017.


    As on date, 2547 NGOs are yet to adhere to the Government orders to submit their pending Annual Returns. List of such NGOs is available on the website: www.fcraonline.nic.in .


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today.
