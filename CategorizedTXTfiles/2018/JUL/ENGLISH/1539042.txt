      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      FDI Inflow reaches 61.96 USD Billion in 2017-18
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2018 5:24PM by PIB Delhi


    Measures taken by the Government on FDI policy liberalization along with improvement in ease of doing business climate has resulted in growth of total FDI inflows. The country has been recording highest ever FDI since 2015-16. It increased from 55.56 USD billion in 2015-16 to 60.22 USD billion in 2016-17 and reached to 61.96 USD billion in 2017-18. These figures surpass the FDI inflows of any of the preceding financial years. Year wise increase in the total FDI inflows is given below:
