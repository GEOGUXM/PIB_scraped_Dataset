      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Funds Under PMKSY
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2018 12:54PM by PIB Delhi


    Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) has various components such as Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP), Command area Development and Water Management (CADWM), Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP), Per Drop More Crop (PDMC)) and Watershed Development (WD).


    The details of Central Assistance (CA) released under various components of PMKSY and Flood Management Programme (FMP) which includes anti-erosion scheme for the State of Assam during 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 are as under:
