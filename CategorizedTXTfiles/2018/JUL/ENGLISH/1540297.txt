      Ministry of Railways

      Shri Ravindra Gupta, Member (Rolling Stock), Railway Board chairs the Conference of Principal Chief Mechanical Engineers in Vigyan Bhawan
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2018 5:45PM by PIB Delhi


      The Conference of the Principal Chief Mechanical Engineers of Indian Railways was held at Vigyan Bhawan on 23 rd & 24 th July, 2018. The conference was chaired by Shri Shri Ravindra Gupta, Member (Rolling Stock), Railway Board. The conference was attended by Principal Chief Mechanical Engineers from Zonal Railway, Production Units & Executive Directors of Railway Board & RDSO. Issues relating to improving passenger safety, comfort & experience, enhancing serviceability of foreign stock & improving the environment responsiveness of Indian Railways were discussed & policy decisions were taken.
