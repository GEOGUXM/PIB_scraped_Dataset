      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Terrorists Killed in J&K
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2018 4:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The number of terrorists killed and civilian killed in terrorist violence incidents in Jammu and Kashmir during last three years and current year (upto 22.07.2018) is as under: -

                                                            
Year              No. of terrorists killed  Civilians killed
                                                            
                                                            
                                                            
2015              108                       17              
                                                            
                                                            
                                                            
2016              150                       15              
                                                            
                                                            
                                                            
2017              213                       40              
                                                            
                                                            
                                                            
2018              110                       18              
                                                            
                                                            
(upto 22nd July)                                            
                                                            
                                                            

    The Government regularly reviews the security situation in the State of Jammu and Kashmir. In order to prevent activities of militants, numerous steps have been taken including strengthening of operational grid with enhanced human intelligence and use of technical intelligence grid. Besides, Police and civil authorities of Jammu and Kashmir have been advising people through various media to stay away from encounter sites. The Government has also continuously encouraged policies to mainstream the youth, including providing employment opportunities to wean them away from militancy.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in a written reply to question in the Lok Sabha today.
