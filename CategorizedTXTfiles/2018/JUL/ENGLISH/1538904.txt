      Ministry of Minority Affairs

      Evil of Terrorism Can be Countered with the Philosophy of Sufism- Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi
      
      Official Web Portal of Dargah Khwaja Sahib Ajmer Launched

      Posted On: 17 JUL 2018 6:54PM by PIB Delhi

    Union Minister for Minority Affairs Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi has emphasised that the philosophy of Sufism spreads the thoughts of peace and harmony. While launching the official web portal of Dargah Khwaja Sahib Ajmer here today, Shri Naqvi said that the present government is doing efforts for inclusive growth of every section of the society. He said that in the last four years the government has worked with the resolve of development without discrimination.


    Shri Naqvi also said that in the last four years the Central Government has started several programmes for the upliftment and skill development of the youth, belonging to the notified Minority Communities. He mentioned Prime Minister Jan Vikas Karyakram, Mudra Yojana and Ujjawala Yojana, among others.


    The function was attended by the Secretary, Ministry of Minority Affairs, Shri A. Luikham, Joint Secretary, Jan-e-Alam and KC Samariya, President Dargah Khwaja Sahib, Ajmer Shri Ameen Pathan, other members of Dargah committee, Justice Zakiullah Khan and Shri Satish Punia. The web address can be accessed at https://gharibnawaz.minorityaffairs.gov.in
