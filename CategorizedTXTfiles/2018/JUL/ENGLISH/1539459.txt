      Ministry of Railways

      Decline in the number of train accidents
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2018 4:41PM by PIB Delhi


    Consequential train accidents have come down from 118 in 2013-14 to 73 in 2017-18. Year-wise details of these consequential train accidents are given below:

                                                                              
Type of accidents                  2013-14  2014-15  2015-16  2016-17  2017-18
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Collisions                         4        5        3        5        3      
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Derailments                        53       63       65       78       54     
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Manned Level Crossing Accidents    4        6        6                 3      
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Unmanned Level Crossing Accidents  47       50       29       20       10     
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Fire                               7        6                 1        3      
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Miscellaneous Accidents            3        5        4                        
                                                                              
                                                                              
                                                                              
Total                              118      135      107      104      73
