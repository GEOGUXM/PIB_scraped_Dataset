      PM congratulates Dipa Karmakar on winning Gold in vault event at FIG World Challenge Cup
      Posted On: 08 JUL 2018 7:16PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Dipa Karmakar on winning Gold in vault event at FIG World Challenge Cup.


    “India is proud of Dipa Karmakar! Congratulations to her on winning a well-deserved Gold in the vault event at the FIG World Challenge Cup in Mersin, Turkey. This win is a prime example of her tenacity and never-say-die attitude”, the Prime Minister said.
