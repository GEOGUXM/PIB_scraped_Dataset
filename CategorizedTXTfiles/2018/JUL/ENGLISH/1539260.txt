      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Change in Civil Service Recruitment and Allocation Rules
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2018 4:27PM by PIB Delhi


    There is no proposal to alter the existing mode of recruitment to civil services through Civil Services Examination (CSE) which is conducted according to the CSE Rules notified by the Government every year.


    The number of vacancies and the number of candidates allocated to various services through Civil Services Examination during the last 3 years is given below :-

                                                                         
S.No  CSE Year  Vacancies  Allocated to various services as per CSE Rules
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
1     2015      1164       1041                                          
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
2     2016      1209       1058                                          
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
3     2017      1058       Final result declared on 27.04.2018           
                                                                         
                                                                         

    There is no proposal for any special examination. For Civil Services Examination-2018, a total of 782 vacancies (tentative) has been advertised by UPSC on 07.02.2018 in its notice of Examination for 24 (twenty four) participating services.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, DrJitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
