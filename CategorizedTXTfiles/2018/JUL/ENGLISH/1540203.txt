      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Details of Irrigation Projects under AIBP
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2018 12:55PM by PIB Delhi


    During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) - Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP), having ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh ha. and balance estimated cost of Rs.77595 cr. have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases up to December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works. Funding mechanism through NABARD has been approved by the Government for both Central and State share. State-wise details of these 99 projects are at Annexure .
