      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Driving Training Institutions
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2018 4:39PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways is implementing the scheme of Institute of Driver Training and Research (IDTR). Proposals have been received from the states of Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana, Maharashtra, Manipur, J&K, Jharkhand and Sikkim for setting up of these institutes, and the Ministry has sanctioned Institute of Driver Training and Research one each in these States.


    The Minister of State for Road Transport and Shipping and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya said this in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
