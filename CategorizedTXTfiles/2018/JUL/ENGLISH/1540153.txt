      Ministry of Culture

      Closing of Red Fort for Public and General Visitors from the morning of 8th August 2018 to 15th of August 2018 till the Independence Day Function is over
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2018 6:34PM by PIB Delhi


    As per the Order issued by Archaeological survey of India, there will be no entry inside the Red Fort for Public and General Visitors from the morning of 8 th August 2018 to 15 th of August 2018 till the Independence Day Celebration is over.
