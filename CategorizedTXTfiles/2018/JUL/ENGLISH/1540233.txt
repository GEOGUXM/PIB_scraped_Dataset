      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      National Overseas Scholarships for Scheduled Castes
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2018 3:23PM by PIB Delhi


    The National Overseas Scholarship is a Central Sector scheme for Scheduled Caste (SC) students. There is no state wise quota of slots under this Scheme. Advertisements inviting applications under the scheme are published in leading newspapers and Employment News twice a year, once at the beginning of the year and then once again later during the financial year. The details of the scheme are also available on the website of the Department. Apart from this the features of the scheme are also broadcasted through radio channels of All India Radio in the programme ‘Sawarati Jaye Jeewan ki Rahen’.
