      President of India to address the 64th Annual Convocation of IIT Kharagpur tomorrow
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2018 5:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit West Bengal (Kharagpur) tomorrow (July 20, 2018) to grace and address the 64 th annual convocation of IIT Kharagpur.
