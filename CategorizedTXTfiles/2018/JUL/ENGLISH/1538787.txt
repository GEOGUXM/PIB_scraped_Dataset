      CIC bilingual Mobile app launched
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2018 6:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Information Commission (CIC) has launched a newly updated version of mobile app in Hindi also, - “CIC App”, which is available in Google Play Store and Apple store with the following features:


    (i) Applicant can submit his/her 2 nd Appeal(s)/ Complaint(s) / link paper(s) in both the languages i.e. in Hindi and English


    (ii) The appellant can search and trace dak, 2nd Appeal/Complaint in both the languages.


    (iii) Real time status is available in both the languages (Hindi & English)


    (iv) Appellant can download copy of Hearing notice, Facilitation memo & decision.
