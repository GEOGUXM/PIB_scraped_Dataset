      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 13 JUL 2018 4:39PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri Kaptan Singh Solanki, Governor of Haryana to discharge the functions of the Governor of Himachal Pradesh, in addition to his own duties, during the absence, on leave, of Acharya Devvrat, Governor of Himachal Pradesh.
