      Prime Minister's Office

      PM pays tributes to Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee on his Birth Anniversary
      Posted On: 06 JUL 2018 12:27PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee on his birth anniversary today.


    "On his Jayanti, I bow to Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee .


    Dr. Mookerjee is remembered as a fine educationist, a wonderful administrator and a stalwart who fought for India's freedom as well as unity", the Prime Minister said.
