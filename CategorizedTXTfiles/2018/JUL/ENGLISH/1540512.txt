      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles loss of lives due to a bus accident in Maharashtra
      Posted On: 28 JUL 2018 4:12PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives due to a bus accident in Raigad district of Maharashtra.


    “Pained by the loss of lives due to a bus accident in Maharashtra's Raigad district. My condolences to those who lost their loved ones”, the Prime Minister said.
