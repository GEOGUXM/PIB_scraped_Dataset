      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Union Sports Minister Grants Financial Assistance of Rs. Five Lakh to Archer Gohela Boro For Medical TreatmentUnion
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2018 12:38PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of State (I/C) Youth Affairs and Sports Col Rajyavardhan Rathore has sanctioned financial assistance of Rs. 5 Lakh (Five Lakh) to National Level Archer Ms. Gohela Boro for her medical treatment. The financial assistance has been given from Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS).


    This is in addition to financial assistance of over Rs 3.37 Lakh already provided to her upto January, 2018.


    Ms. Gohela Boro is suffering from systemic lupus erythematosus combined with other multiple disorder. She belongs to very poor family in village Amguri in Kokrajhar.
