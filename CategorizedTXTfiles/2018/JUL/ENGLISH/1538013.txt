      President of India to Visit Goa on July 7 and 8, 2018
      Posted On: 06 JUL 2018 4:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Goa on July 7 and 8, 2018. This will be his first visit to Goa as the President of India.


    On July 7, 2018, the President will address the 30 th annual convocation of the Goa University. On the same evening, he will also attend a civic reception hosted by the State Government in his honour.


    On July 8, 2018, the President will visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Old Goa, and Shri Mangueshi Temple in Mardol, before returning to Delhi.
