      Ministry of Education

      CBSE invites online applications for Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET)
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2018 6:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Board of Secondary Education- CBSE has started inviting online applications for the 11 th edition of Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) 2018. The test will be conducted in twenty languages in 92 cities all over the country. The detailed Information Bulletin containing details of examination, syllabus, languages eligibility criteria, examination fee, examination cities and important dates will be available on CTET official website www.ctet.nic.in w.e.f. 01-08-2018 and the aspiring candidates are requested to download the Information Bulletin from above mentioned website only and read the same carefully before applying. The aspiring candidates have to apply online only through CTET website www.ctet.nic.in. The online application process will start from 01-08-2018 onwards. The last date for submitting online application is 27-08-2018 and fee can be paid upto30-08-2018 before 3.30.p.m.
