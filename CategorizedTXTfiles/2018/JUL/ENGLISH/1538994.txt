    Ministry of Finance as well as NITI Aayog has been requested to consider uniform levy of GST on Rail Container traffic at par with road container traffic. Service of Goods Transport Agency (GTA) for transport of goods by road are taxed uniformly @ 5% GST with no Input Tax Credit (ITC).


    This information was given by the Minister of State of Railways Shri Rajen Gohain in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
