      430 YEAR OLD BAOBAB TREE IN GOLCONDA HEALTHY: HORTICULTURE REPORT
      Posted On: 30 JUL 2018 6:19PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Dr. Milan Kumar Chaubey, the Superintending Archaeologist, Archaeological Society of India (ASI) in a press release stated that the 430 year old Baobab tree at Naya Qila, Golconda Fort is healthy. The tree has been inspected by Horticultural experts and found to be healthy, he said.
