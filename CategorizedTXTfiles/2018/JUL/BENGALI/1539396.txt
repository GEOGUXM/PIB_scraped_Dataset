      Ministry of Finance

      CAG reports on West Bengal accounts submitted for laying in the Assembly.
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2018 11:41AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, Date: 19 July 2018


    Office of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India (CAG) has forwarded audit reports on State Finances and General and Social Sector, containing audit comments and observations of the accounts and transactions, pertaining to the activities of the West Bengal government for the Financial Year ending on 31 st , March, 2017. The reports have been forwarded for laying in the State Legislature.


    Shri Amar Patnaik, Principal Accountant General (G&SSAudit), West Bengal informed here today.
