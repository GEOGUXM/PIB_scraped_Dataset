      Ministry of Culture

      Special Lecture on Acharya S N Bose to mark his 125th birth anniversary
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2018 3:13PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 23 rd July, 2018


    A special lecture on ‘Science, Society and Acharya Satyendranath Bose’ will be delivered by Professor Samit Kumar Roy at Bhasha Bhavan Auditorium in National Library Complex on July 24 (Tuesday). Professor Roy is the Director of SN Bose National Centre for Basic Sciences. Barisha Vigyan O Sanskriti Kendra is organising this session in which a quiz contest for school children will also be held. This is to mark the 125 th birth anniversary of Acharya S N Bose who was born on January 1, 1894. In this connection, it may be stated that the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi had addressed on the curtain raiser ceremony of the commemoration of Prof. S.N. Bose’s 125th birth anniversary, at Kolkata via video conference.
