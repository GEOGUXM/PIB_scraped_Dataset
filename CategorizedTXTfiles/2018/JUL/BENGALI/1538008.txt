      Ministry of Finance

      Rs. 43 Crore fraud in GST, two Directors arrested
      Posted On: 06 JUL 2018 4:17PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 6, 2018.


    Howrah CGST Commissionerate today unearthed a GST fraud based in the Hooghly and Howrah area involving a case of fraudulent issuance of fake GST invoices involving tax evasion of approximately 43 Crores. The investigation was based on an intelligence report. Accordingly, two Directors along with a key person have been arrested in connection with this case. Further investigation is underway to uncover the extent of this fraud.


    It was found that such GST invoices were issued to 63 GST Taxpayers. Investigation at the recipient's end has revealed that invoices were issued without delivery of any goods or services. These recipients have availed inadmissible. Input Tax Credit and on admitting the irregularity, are discharging the tax liability on this count. Some of these recipients are also found to be non -existent though they have issued further invoices and passed on credit to other taxpayers without delivery of goods or services.


    As per Section 132 of the CGST Act, issuance of an invoice or bill without supply of goods or services and wrongful availment of Input Tax Credit is a punishable offence.
