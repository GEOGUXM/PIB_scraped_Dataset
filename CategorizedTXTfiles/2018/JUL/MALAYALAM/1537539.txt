      Governor briefed about Centre's policy initiatives by Additional Director General of PIB& ROB Kerala & Lakshadweep Region
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2018 3:38PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    
    
    Shri. D Murali Mohan, Additional Director General, Press Information Bureau ( PIB) & Regional Outreach Bureau, (ROB ), Kerala and Lakshadweep (Region), made a courtesy call to the Governor of Kerala Justice (Retd) P. Sathasivam. Shri. Murali Mohan briefed the activities of PIB and ROB in disseminating information about the policy initiatives of the Central Government to the Governor. He pointed out that special focus is given to publicize the flagship progammes and welfare schemes of the Government.
