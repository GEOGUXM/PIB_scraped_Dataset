      Shri E. Mariappan takes over as Additional Director General, Press Information Bureau, Chennai
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2018 5:52PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri E. Mariappan has taken over the charge today as Additional Director General, PIB, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Chennai. Before assuming the charge, he was Additional Director General, PIB, Hyderabad. He will also be the Additional Press Registrar of the Registrar of Newspapers for India in Chennai Office.


    Shri. Mariappan will also head the Regional Outreach Bureau (ROB) of the I &B Ministry. The ROB has been formed, clubbing three media units of the ministry viz. DAVP (Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity), DFP (Directorate of Field Publicity) and S &DD (Song & Drama Division). Shri Mariappan will also look after the Directorate of Publications Division (DPD) in Chennai.


    He belongs to 1990 Batch of Indian Information Service. Having worked in different media units including PIB, All India Radio and Doordarshan.
