      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Shri Kiren Rijiju expresses concern over the death of Arunachali youth while travelling by train in Uttar Pradesh
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2018 5:34PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Kiren Rijiju has expressed concern over the tragic incident in which a young man named Tage Dule of Ziro, Arunachal Pradesh lost his life due to injuries suffered while travelling by train in Uttar Pradesh on 3 rd August, 2018.


    The Minister immediately took up the matter with the offices of Chief Minister, Uttar Pradesh and the Union Railway Minister with request for investigation into the incident to ascertain the facts and circumstances leading to the death of the youth. Shri Rijiju sought action against the culprits, if there was any malafide intent. The Minister sought that the probe should ascertain whether the incident is related to discrimination against the North-East people.


    Shri Rijiju also asked Government of Uttar Pradesh and Ministry of Railways to extend all necessary support to the family members of the deceased in this hour of crisis. Further, Shri Rijiju expressed his condolences to the family members of the deceased.
