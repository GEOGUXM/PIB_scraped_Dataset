      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Recognition for Skill Development Services
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 3:58PM by PIB Delhi


    Based on the proposal received from the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurships (MOSDE), a new Service , – Indian Skill Development Service (ISDS) was formed with the approval of the Cabinet on 08.10.2016 for the Training Directorate of the MOSDE (erstwhile Directorate General of Employment & Training under the Ministry of Labour & Employment). Intake of officers would be through an All-India Examination conducted by UPSC.


    Posts of all the Central Group ‘A’ Services are at the disposal of the Central Govt. and hence do not belong to any State. As on 01.01.2017, total duty posts in Allied Services (Excluding All India Services and Indian Ordnance Factories Service) is 17,587 as per detail below:

    Since 2014, three new Services in the name of Indian Enterprise Development Service under M/o MSME, Indian Petroleum and Explosive Safety Service under M/o Commerce and Indian Naval Material Management Service under M/o Defence have been formed.
