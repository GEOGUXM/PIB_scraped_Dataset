      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Difference in Income Limit for Availing OBC Scholarships
      Posted On: 02 AUG 2018 3:02PM by PIB Delhi


    Welfare Schemes for OBCs/EBCs/DNTs are funds limited and there is a huge demand from the States. To award scholarship to the most needy students, the income ceiling have been fixed accordingly.


    The details of income limit for parents of OBC and other students in the following schemes implemented by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment are as under:


    (Amount in lakhs)

                                               
Name of scheme           SCs   OBCs  EBCs  DNTs
                                               
                                               
                                               
Pre-Matric Scholarship   2.50  2.50  -     2.00
                                               
                                               
                                         
Post-Matric Scholarship  2.50  1.00  1.00
                                         
                                         

    There is a proposal under examination for enhancement of income ceiling for parents under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students in India from Rs. 1.00 lakh to Rs. 1.50 lakh.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Krishan Pal Gurjar in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
