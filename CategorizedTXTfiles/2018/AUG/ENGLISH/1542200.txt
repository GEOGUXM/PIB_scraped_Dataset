      Ministry of Railways

      Accurate Signalling System in Railways
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 3:23PM by PIB Delhi


    In signaling, there is continuous improvement through technological upgradation. The best equipments suitable for Indian conditions available world-wide are being adopted as a continuous process. However, whenever such systems are provided it is ensured that indigenization of the systems takes place in phased manner.


    A work for modernization of complete Signaling system on Indian Railways, which includes works of Automatic Train Protection (ATP) has been included in the works programme 2018-19 for implementation on complete 60,000 Route Kilometres (RKMs) on Broad Gauge (BG) network of Indian Railways subject to expenditure on this project to be made only after following due processes/mandatory approvals and sanctions.
