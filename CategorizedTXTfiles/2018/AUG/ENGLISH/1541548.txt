      Ministry of Railways

      Corrigendum to the Press Release "Shri Piyush Goyal Approves a New Promotion Policy for Railway Sportspersons" dated 02nd August, 2018
      Posted On: 03 AUG 2018 5:31PM by PIB Delhi


    
    In the PIB Press Release issued on 02 nd August, 2018 titled "Shri Piyush Goyal Approves a New Promotion Policy for Railway Sportspersons", the second para of release namely:-


    " In order to give due recognition to the effort put in by the sportspersons, who have made two appearances in Olympics, won a medal in Asian Games/Common Wealth Games, it has now been decided to award them with a promotion to the officer rank. Further Railway sportsperson who are winners of any of the awards like Arjuna/Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award would also be entitled to the said promotion"


    
    Should be read as:-


    "In order to give due recognition to the efforts put in by the sportspersons two appearances in Olympics alongwith either a medal in Asian Games/CWG or any of the awards like Arjuna/Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award would entitle a Railway sportsperson to a promotion to the officer rank"


    Others content of the Press Release remain unchanged. Inconvenience caused is regretted.
