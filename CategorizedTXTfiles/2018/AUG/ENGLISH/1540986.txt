      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      61.12 Lakh Benefited Through PMRPY
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2018 2:13PM by PIB Delhi


    Pradhan Mantri Rojgar Protsahan Yojana (PMRPY) has been initiated by the Ministry of Labour and Employment in the year 2016-17 for incentivizing employers by paying the entire employerâ€™s contribution (12 % or as admissible) towards the EPS and EPF for all sectors w.e.f. 01.04.2018 to all eligible new employees and is applicable for all sectors for the next 3 years. The total number of beneficiaries under the scheme as on 25.07.2018 is 61.12 lakhs. Details of State-wise, Sector/ Industries-wise/Month- wise beneficiaries under the scheme are at Annexure-I, II and III respectively.
