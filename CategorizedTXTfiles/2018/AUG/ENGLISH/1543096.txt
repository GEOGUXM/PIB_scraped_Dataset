      Ministry of Defence

      Honorary Commissions for Army Personnel
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2018 11:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The list of Army personnel who have been honoured with honorary commissions on the occasion of 72st Independence Day -2018 is attached to this e-mail.


    Note :- All due care has been taken in preparation of the list of candidates who have made it to the merit and conferred Honorary Captain and Honorary Lieutenant on active list on occasion of Independence Day 2018. However, this list should not be taken as an authority for claiming Honorary Commission. The final authority on conferment of Honorary Commission is the Draft Gazette Notification of India, which can be obtained from respective Record Offices or Headquarter Commands.
