      Ministry of Finance

      Extension of Last Date for filing GSTR-3B for July, 2018 extended till 24th August, 2018 for all class of taxpayers
      Posted On: 21 AUG 2018 6:56PM by PIB Delhi


    The last date for Filing of Return in FORM GSTR-3B for the month of July, 2018 has been extended till 24 th August, 2018 for all class of taxpayers by the Competent Authority. The relevant Notification for the same shall be issued shortly.
