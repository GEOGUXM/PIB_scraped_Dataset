      Prime Minister's Office

      PM speaks to Kerala CM regarding flood situation in the state; offers all possible assistance
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 10:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has spoken to Chief Minister of Kerala Shri Pinarayi Vijayan regarding flood situation in the state.


    The Prime Minister said, “Spoke to Kerala CM Shri Pinarayi Vijayan and discussed the situation arising due to floods in various parts of the state. Offered all possible assistance to those affected. We stand shoulder to shoulder with the people of Kerala in the wake of this calamity”.
