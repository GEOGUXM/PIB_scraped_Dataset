      Prime Minister's Office

      PM speaks to Kerala CM regarding flood situation in the state.
      Posted On: 16 AUG 2018 11:15AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has spoken to Chief Minister of Kerala Shri Pinarayi Vijayan regarding flood situation in the state.


    “Spoke to Kerala CM Shri Pinarayi Vijayan again this morning. We discussed the flood situation in the state. Have asked Defence Ministry to further step up the rescue and relief operations across the state. Praying for the safety and well-being of the people of Kerala”, the Prime Minister said.
