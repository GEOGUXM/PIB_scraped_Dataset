      President of India pays floral Tributes to Shri V.V. Giri on his birth Anniversary
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 1:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid floral tributes to Shri V.V. Giri, former President of India, on the occasion of his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (August 10, 2018).


    The President, the family members of Shri V. V. Giri, officers and staff of Rashtrapati Bhavan, paid floral tributes in front of the portrait of Shri V.V. Giri.
