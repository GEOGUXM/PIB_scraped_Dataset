      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Central Rule in J & K
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2018 5:15PM by PIB Delhi


    On 20.6.2018, the Governor of Jammu and Kashmir, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 92 of the Constitution of Jammu and Kashmir and, with the concurrence of President of India, issued a proclamation inter alia assuming to himself all the functions of the Government of the State of Jammu and Kashmir.


    The Details regarding number of terrorist attacks and loss of lives after the imposition of Central rule are as under (20.06.2018 to 19.07.2018):-
