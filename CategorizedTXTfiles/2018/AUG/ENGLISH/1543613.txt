      PM greets nation on the occasion of Idu’lZuha
      Posted On: 22 AUG 2018 10:13AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted fellow citizens on Idu’lZuha. “ Best wishes on Id-ul-Zuha. May this day deepen the spirit of compassion and brotherhood in our society, ” the PM says.
