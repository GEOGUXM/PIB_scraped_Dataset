      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Census 2021
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2018 3:09PM by PIB Delhi


    The Office of Registrar General and Census Commissioner, India in the Ministry of Home Affairs has initiated the preparatory work for conducting next Population Census 2021.


    Meetings with stakeholders including Central Government Ministries/ Departments, State Governments and data users in the country are underway. Based on inputs from all the stakeholders, the questionnaire for census will be decided and pilot testing done.


    In Census, data are collected on all persons who are present in the country during the period of Census enumeration.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in a written reply to question in the Lok Sabha today.
