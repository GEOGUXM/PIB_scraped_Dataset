    During 2016-17, the Committee headed by the Minister (WR), Govt. of Chhattisgarh, in consultation with States identified ninety-nine on-going Major/Medium irrigation project under PMKSY-AIBP having an ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh ha. prioritised for completion by December, 2019 with a balance estimated cost of Rs.77595 cr. along with Command Area Development & Water Management (CAD&WM) works. Funding mechanism through NABARD has been approved by the Government for both Central and State share. This includes small and marginal land holdings also.


    This information was given by Union Minister of State for Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation Shri Arjun Ram Mehghwal in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
