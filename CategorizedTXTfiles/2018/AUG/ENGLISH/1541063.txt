      Ministry of Railways

      Overhauling of Railway Coaches
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2018 5:37PM by PIB Delhi


    All the coaches running on Indian Railways are periodically overhauled as prescribed in maintenance manuals / instructions.


    The Government has allocated â‚¹9277 crore for the periodic overhauling/servicing of railway coaches in Budget Estimates 2018-19.


    Acquisition of new coaches for replacement of over-aged stock is a continuous process on Indian Railways. Coaches are replaced after attaining their prescribed codal life or prematurely in case of heavy damage to structural members.
