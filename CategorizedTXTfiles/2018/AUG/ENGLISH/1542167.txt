      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Declaration of irrigation projects as National Projects
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 1:16PM by PIB Delhi


    Sixteen projects in the country have been declared as National Project as per the guidelines of the scheme. Further, the State Governments from time to time send requests for inclusion of the projects in the scheme of National Projects. However, their inclusion depends upon appraisal/approval by Advisory Committee of Ministry of Water Resources, RD & GR from techno-economic angle, investment clearance, fulfillment of criteria of the scheme of National Projects, availability of funds etc. The status of requests received from the State Governments in this regard is as under:
