      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC appointments
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 11:22AM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the following appointments:

    1). Shri Raghvendra Singh, IAS (WB:83), Secretary, Ministry of Culture as Secretary, Ministry of Textiles vice Shri Anant Kumar Singh, IAS (UP:84);


    2). Shri Anant Kumar Singh, IAS (UP:84), Secretary, Ministry of Textiles as Secretary, Department of Land Resources in the vacancy caused on superannuation of Shri Dinesh Singh, IAS (UP:82) on 30.06.2018; and


    3). Shri Arun Goel, IAS (PB:85), Special Secretary, Ministry of Culture as Secretary, Ministry of Culture vice Shri Raghvendra Singh, IAS (WB:83) on his appointment as Secretary, Ministry of Textiles.
