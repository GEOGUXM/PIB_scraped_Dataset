      President of India to inaugurate ‘one district one product’ summit in Lucknow tomorrow
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 5:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Uttar Pradesh (Lucknow) tomorrow (August 10, 2018) to inaugurate the ‘One District One Product’ Summit.
