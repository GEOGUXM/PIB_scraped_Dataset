      President of India hosts ‘At Home’ for freedom fighters on the 76th anniversary of Quit India Day
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 8:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, hosted an ‘At Home’ reception for freedom fighters on the 76 th anniversary of Quit India Day today (August 9, 2018) at Rashtrapati Bhavan.


    On the occasion, the President honoured and felicitated 89 freedom fighters from across the country in the presence of the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi; Union Minister for Home Affairs Shri Rajnath Singh and other dignitaries.
