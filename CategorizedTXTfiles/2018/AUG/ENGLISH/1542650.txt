      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to visit Mumbai on 11th August, 2018
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 5:26PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will visit Mumbai on 11 th August, 2018.


    He will deliver the Convocation Address, at the 56 th Annual Convocation of the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.


    After the Convocation, the Prime Minister will inaugurate the new building of the Department of Energy Science & Engineering; and Centre for Environmental Science and Engineering, at IIT Bombay.
