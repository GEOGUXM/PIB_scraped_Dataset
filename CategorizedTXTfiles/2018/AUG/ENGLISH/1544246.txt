      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Cabinet approves Trade Cooperation Framework between India and Rwanda
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2018 1:39PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has given its ex-post facto approval for the trade cooperation framework between India and Rwanda. The trade cooperation framework was signed on 23 rd July, 2018.


    The trade cooperation framework will facilitate better trade and economic relations between the two countries.
