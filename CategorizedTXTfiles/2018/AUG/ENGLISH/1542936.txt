      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Change in Holiday on account of Id-ul-Zuha (Bakrid)
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2018 11:31AM by PIB Delhi


    All Central Government Administrative Offices located at Delhi/New Delhi shall remain closed on 23 rd August, 2018 on account of Id-ul-Zuha (Bakrid) holiday (in place of 22 nd August, 2018).
