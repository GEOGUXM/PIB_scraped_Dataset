      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      National Commission for Backward Classes (Repeal) Bill, 2017 Passed in Parliament
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2018 7:39PM by PIB Delhi


    National Commission for Backward Classes (Repeal) Bill, 2017 is passed by Rajya Sabha today. Union Minister for Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Thaawarchand Gehlot today moved in Rajya Sabha the Constitution (One Hundred and Twenty Third Amendment) Bill, 2017 and the further Amendments made by the Lok Sabha. This Amendment Bill is to repeal the National Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993 as passed by the Lok Sabha.


    The Bill was passed by the Rajya Sabha with an Amendment (without clause-3) at its sitting held on 31 st July, 2017 and transmitted to the Lok Sabha for its concurrence. The Lok Sabha passed the Bill with alternate amendments and further amendments at its sitting held on 2 nd August, 2018.
