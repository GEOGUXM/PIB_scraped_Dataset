      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      EPFO Informed 4,98,056 Members About Contributions
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2018 2:10PM by PIB Delhi


    SMS/E-mail to the members, if contributions are not deposited by their employers with Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) for a given month within due date, started from 27 th June, 2018 for contribution due for wage month May paid in June, 2018. Total SMS/E-mail sent to the members upto 20 th July, 2018 are 4,98,056.


    Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance ( UMANG) App, where various services of EPFO have been linked also, inter-alia, provides information regarding member’s Provident Fund (PF) balance, claim status, Know Your Customer (KYC) status, etc.


    This information was given by Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar, Union Minister of State (I/C) for Labour and Employment in written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
