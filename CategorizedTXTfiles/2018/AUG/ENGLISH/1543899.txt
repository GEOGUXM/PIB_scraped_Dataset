      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Indian Rowing Team on winning Gold at Asian Games- 2018
      Posted On: 24 AUG 2018 7:49PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated the team of Sawarn Singh, Dattu Baban Bhokanal, Om Prakash and Sukhmeet Singh on winning Gold in Rowing Men's Quadruple Sculls event at the 18th Asian Games 2018 in Jakarta - Palembang, Indonesia.


    “Great rowing by our team. They have enhanced India’s pride by winning the Gold in the Rowing Men's Quadruple Sculls event. Congratulations to the team of Sawarn Singh, Dattu Baban Bhokanal, Om Prakash and Sukhmeet Singh”, the Prime Minister said.
