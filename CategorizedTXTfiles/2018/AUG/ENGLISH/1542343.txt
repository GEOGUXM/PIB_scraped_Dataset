      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Dr. R. G Anand appointed Member, National Commission for Protection of Child Rights.
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2018 5:49PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of India has appointed Dr. R. G Anand as Member, National Commission for Protection of Child Rights with effect from 13 th July 2018 for a period of three years, or until on attaining the age of 60 years, or until further orders, wherever is the earlier.
