      Bye-Election Schedule for the Bijapur Local Authorities Constituency in Karnataka Legislative Council Announced
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2018 4:47PM by PIB Delhi


    The Election Commission of India (ECI) has announced the bye-election schedule for the Bijapur Local Authorities' Constituency of Karnataka Legislative Council today. The bye-election is to be held to fill a casual vacancy that arose in the constituency due to the resignation of the incumbent Shri Basangouda R. Patil (Yatnal) on 18 th April, 2018. The date of retirement for the seat would be 05.01.2022.
