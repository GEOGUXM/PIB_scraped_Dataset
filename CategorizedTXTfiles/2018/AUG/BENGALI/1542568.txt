      Ministry of Defence

      ANC to Organise ‘Spirit of Freedom’ Run 2018 ANC to celebrate Independence Day at Saddle Peak
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 2:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 10, 2018.


    On the occasion of 72 nd Independence Day of the country, Andaman and Nicobar Command is organising ‘Spirit of Freedom’ run with the aim to spread awareness about the harmful effects of use of plastic and towards making A&N ‘Plastic Free Islands’. The event includes non-stop run covering a distance of 290 kms from Port Blair to Diglipur. The run will be followed by a trek to Saddle Peak on 15 Aug 18 where the National Flag will be unfurled. Twenty personnel of the Army, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard and Defence Civilians will participate in the event.


    The event is planned in the format of relay wherein every runner will run for 10kms and handover the baton to the next runner. The event will be flagged off on August 12, 2018 at Netaji Stadium at 10:00AM, by Vice Admiral Bimal Verma, AVSM, ADC, Commander -in-Chief, Andaman & Nicobar Command. Students from the schools located in the Islands will participate in the flag off and will run with participants till Science Centre, Port Blair.


    A social outreach program has also been planned during the event, where an outreach team will be visiting 19 schools enroute Port Blair to Diglipur. They will be addressing the students and will be giving a talk on ill effects of usage of plastic and how to contribute towards making the Islands truly ‘Plastic Free’.


    In coordination with forest department, the team will also hand over saplings to the school children as part of ‘Adopt a Tree’ program of the Government of India. During the interaction, the students will be told about the various career prospects in the Army/Navy/Air Force and Coast Guard.


    Civilian volunteers willing to participate in the run can join the team at any segment and run for any distance to support the cause. The volunteers are to make their own arrangement for transportation to return back to their start point. The team will pass through various locations from 13 August to 15 August 18.
