      Ministry of Railways

      SADBHAVANA DIWAS OBSERVED AT SER HEADQUARTERS
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2018 6:36PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 th August, 2018.


    SADBHAVANA DIWAS was observed at South Eastern Railway Headquarters, Garden Reach this morning (20/8/2018).


    Shri Anirban Datta, Additional General Manager, South Eastern Railway administered pledge to all staff and officers of S E Railway headquarters to work for the emotional oneness and harmony of all the people of India regardless of caste, region, religion or language and to resolve all differences through dialogue and constitutional means without resorting to any violence.


    Smt Zarina Firdausi, Principal Chief Personnel Officer and other Principal Head of the Departments along with large number of staff and officers of South Eastern Railway were present at the oath taking ceremony.
