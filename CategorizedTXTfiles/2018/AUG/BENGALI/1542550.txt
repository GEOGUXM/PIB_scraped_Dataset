      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      ROB to organize an exhibition on ‘Saaf Niyat Sahi Vishwas’
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 1:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 10, 2018.


    Regional Outreach Bureau, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India, Kolkata will organize an exhibition on ‘Saaf Niyat Sahi Vishwas” at Birla Industrial and Technological Museum (BITM), Kolkata. The exhibition will start on August 13, 2018 and will continue till August 17, 2018.


    Smt. Rupa Ganguly, MP (Rajya Sabha) will inaugurate the exhibition on August 13, 2018 at 12 Noon.
