      The ASthi Kalash Yatra of Late Prime Minister Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee
      Posted On: 23 AUG 2018 7:44PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 23, 2018.


    The Asthi Kalash of Late Prime Minister Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee will be immersed in Gangasagar tomorrow. Today Kalash Yatra started from Amtala of South 24 Pargana District and will reach Gangasagar tonight. Today many people paid their tribute to Atalji in many places while Kalash Yatra was passing through Diamond Harbour Road. Union Minister Smriti Irani attended the programme.
