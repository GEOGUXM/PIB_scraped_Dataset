      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Holiday on Account of Id-ul-Zuha
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2018 6:32PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 20, 2018


    On the basis of the report of Ruiyat Hilal (moon deciding Committee), headed by Shahi Imam, Jama Masjid, Delhi, Syed Ahmed Bukhari intimating that witnesses of sighting of moon have been received from different cities of India, it has been decided that all Central Government Administrative Offices located at Delhi/New Delhi will remain closed on August 22, 2018 instead of August 23 on account of Id-ul-Zuha (Bakrid).


    Similarly, Masjid-e-Nakhoda Markazi Rooyat-e-Hilal Committee of Kolkata has also declared Eid-ul-Zoha on August 22, 2018 on the basis of the sighting of the moon. The Convenor of the Committee, Nasser Ibrahim has informed this by a press release.


    Hence, the Central Government Administrative Offices located at various places of West Bengal will remain closed on August 22, 2018.
