      Ministry of Home Affairs

      CBI Urges for Fighting Against Corruption
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2018 4:04PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 10, 2018


    Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) is working for eradication of corruption in every Central Government office.


    In order to continue their fight against corruption, it has urged everybody to complain to anti-corruption branch of CBI, Kolkata, if it is found that a Central Government employee is demanding money for any official work.


    In a message CBI has urged people to complaint to its Anti-corruption Branch, Kolkata on numbers - 09051613440 or 033-2289-4408.
