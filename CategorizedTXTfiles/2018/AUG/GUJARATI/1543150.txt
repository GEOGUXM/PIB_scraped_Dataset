      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Book exhibition-cum-sale by Publications Division
      Posted On: 16 AUG 2018 12:56PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Publications Division of Ministry of Information and Broadcasting Ahmedabad has organised a book exhibition-cum-sale on the occasion of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan at Ravi Shankar Raval Kala Bhavan, Ground Floor, Near Gymkhana Club, Law Garden, Ahmedabad from 15th to 17th August, 2018, from 11 AM to 6 PM.


    A special discount from 10 % to 90 % is being offered for books on Mahatma Gandhi, Freedom Struggle, Children's books, among other books.
