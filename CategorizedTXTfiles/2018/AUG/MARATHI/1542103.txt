      Ministry of Communications

      Pension Adalat by Department of Posts on 18th September, 2018
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2018 5:20PM by PIB Mumbai


    As per Guidelines of Department of Pension and Pensioners Welfare, Department of Post will be organising a Pension Adalat on 18 th September, 2018.


    The Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai will hold Pension Adalat at 11 :00 a.m. in the Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, 2nd Floor, GPO Building, Mumbai-400 001.


    Pensioner / Family Pensioner may send their complaints addressed to the Sr. Accounts Officer, O/o Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai at above address on or before 15th August, 2018 in the proforma attached below. It is conveyed that t he applications received after the said date will not be considered in the Pension Adalat.
