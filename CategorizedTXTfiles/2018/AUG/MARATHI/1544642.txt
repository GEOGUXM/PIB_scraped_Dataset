      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Short notice for Recruitment by Staff Selection Commission in Western Region
      Posted On: 31 AUG 2018 3:29PM by PIB Mumbai


    Staff Selection Commission is issuing Recruitment Notice, for various Selection Posts in Central Government department all over India on 05.09.2018 with the closing date of online receipt of application as 30.09.2018, the notice is available on website www.sscwr.net and in Employment News dated 05.09.2018.


    The posts in Gujarat, Maharashtra and Goa which are within the jurisdiction of Staff Selection Commission, Western Region, Mumbai are also included in the advertisement.


    For details regarding the Eligibility Conditions, Scheme of the examination Guidelines for filling up online applications form etc. aspirants must refer the detailed Notice of the examination in Western Regional office website: www.sscwr.net


    Eligible candidates are required to apply online at http:// ssconline.nic.in only before the last date. No Other mode of submission of application is acceptable.
