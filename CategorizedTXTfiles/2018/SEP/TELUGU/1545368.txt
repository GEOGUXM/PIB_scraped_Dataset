      MEDAK POPSK IN TELANGANA STATE STARTED FULL-FLEDGED OPERATIONS FROM 6TH SEPTEMBER, 2018 PROCESSING OF APPLICATION WILL COMPLETE ON THE SAME DAY ITSELF
      Posted On: 07 SEP 2018 6:45PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Medak situated at Head Post Office, Medak is functioning with effect from 17.03.2018 and has been running in Camp Mode". In the earlier Camp Mode operations, the applicants had to wait for 7-10 days before their passport applications could be processed. To avoid the delay, the Post Office Passport Seva Kendra, Medak is now converted to PSP online mode from 06-09-2018. Processing of applications submitted at POPSK, Medak will be completed on the same day itself on par with other Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs) in the State.


    Passport applicants in the surrounding areas of Medak are requested to note the change and make best use of the facility.
