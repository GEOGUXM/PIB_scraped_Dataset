      Civilians’ day out with Armed Forces as part of ‘Parakram Parv’
      Posted On: 30 SEP 2018 5:22PM by PIB Hyderabad


    As part of Parakram Parv, various weapons were showcased to general public.It was a rare opportunity for students, youngsters and the general public to witness the armoury used by the Indian Armed Forces. They also got a hands-on experience with weapons like the 5.56 INSAS LMG, an assault rifle used by the Armed forces on the battlefield.The unique experience was part of ‘Parakram Parv’, during which the Indian Army showcased equipment and weapons to the general public at the Parade Ground in Secunderabad on Saturday.
