      SPACE TECHNOLOGY HELPED IN SUCCESS OF SURGICAL STRIKES: SHRI A.R.K. REDDY (RETD) LIEUTENANT GENERAL
      Posted On: 28 SEP 2018 4:28PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Shri A.R.K.Reddy, Lieutenant General (Retd) said that India’s expertise in the area of Space Technology was largely responsible for the success of “Surgical Strikes”, the country conducted across the border.


    Speaking at a symposium organised by the Regional Outreach Bureau (ROB) under the Ministry of I & B, as part of the “Parakram Parv”, in commemoration of the second anniversary of the Surgical strikes.Shri Reddy said that , due to the state of the art satellite technology, available, the Indian Army could effectively convert real time intelligence to actionable intelligence and carry out the strikes accurately.


    He said the role of the Indian Army in planning the highly classified surgical strikes was laudable . The army succeeded in ensuring exfiltration, and those involved in the strikes were retrieved successfully, he said. Shri Reddy, went on to say that the surgical strikes showcased the might of the Indian Army and the valour of our soldiers.


    Recounting the earlier wars in which India was engaged, the 1965 war with China, or the Bangladesh war in 1971, or Operation Vijay in Kargil in 1999 he said, Indian army never attacked the enemy first, but retaliated effectively, wherever it was engaged. Shri Reddy, shared his personal experiences in the army.


    Earlier, welcoming the gathering Shri T.V.K.Reddy, ADG (Region).Press Information Bureau, said that youth should opt for a career in the armed forces, which is more satisfying and fulfilling.


    Shri P.Ratnakar, Deputy Director, PIB Hyderabad proposed vote of thanks. Several officials of ROB and PIB were also present.
