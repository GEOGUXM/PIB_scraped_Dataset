      PARAKRAM PARV’ TO BE CELEBRATED AT SECUNDERABAD
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2018 7:59PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The Indian Armed Forces will observe ‘Parakram Parv’ from 28-30 September 2018 to mark the second anniversary of the surgical strikes against terror camps across the Line of Control (LoC) and to “showcase the courage, valour and sacrifice of the Armed Forces”. Indian Army had conducted surgical strikes in 2016, which had strategic ramifications and were aimed to dissuade inimical adversary from adopting the path of violence and to ensure an environment of peace for the Nation.


    The event will be organised in all important Military Stations/Cantonments across the country to exhibit the might of the Indian Armed Forces.


    As part of the celebrations, an Equipment Display showcasing different types of Military equipment and weapons will be on display for general public at Parade grounds, Secunderabad from 10:00 am to 03:00 pm on 29th September 2018 for general public. All Army Public Schools and Kendriya Vidyalas under Telangana and Andhra Sub Area will be celebrating Parkram Parv and organizing special events on 29th September 2018.
