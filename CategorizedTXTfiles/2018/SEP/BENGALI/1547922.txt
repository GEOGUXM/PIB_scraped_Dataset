      Ministry of Defence

      Parakram Parv observed in Kolkata
      Posted On: 29 SEP 2018 1:34PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 29, 2018


    “Parakram Parv” was observed by the Eastern Command to mark the second anniversary of the surgical strikes against Pakistan. The event was organized to highlight the courage, valour and sacrifice of the officers and Jawans of the Indian Army. Various exhibitions were on show including the Army weapons and equipments, the selfi wall to feel the Army Uniform and the signature wall. Motivational movies were also shown to the students and NCC cadets. Other programmes, mainly the Band Display, Symposium and projection of memorable Army videos were also planned for the day.


    NCC cadets, students and people in large numbers visited the event and interacted with Army personnel there to understand their sacrifices towards protecting the nation from enemies. The day-long event started at 10 AM. The Kolkata event was a part of the celebrations all over West Bengal and the North-East region by the Eastern Command.
