      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      POSHAN MELA HELD AS PART OF RASHTRIYA POSHAN MAAH
      Posted On: 13 SEP 2018 12:49PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: September 12, 2018


    Poshan Mela as part of the Rashtriya Poshan Maah, the outreach activity by Government of India which is being celebrated this month as the National Nutrition Month was organised today at Cultural Research Institute in Kolkata. The Poshan Mela organised by the Tribal Development Department, Government of West Bengal with support from the Government of India was to create awareness about tribal food and their nutritive value and dietary advantages. The programme was to encourage people about nutritious food in traditional diet and the need of nutritious food for new born children.


    Cultural Research Institute (CRI) in West Bengal has lined up a number of events to showcase the necessity of nutrition in day to day life of people, with particular emphasis on tribal concentrated areas of the state. Various tribal communities, Oraon, Mahali, Munda and Santhal also took part in the Poshan Mela to show balanced diets in tribal cuisine.


    In the seven tribal concentrated districts, Jhargram, Bankura, Purulia, Paschim Medinipur, Alipurduar, Jalpaiguri and Dakshin Dinajpur, district level seminars are to be held in the coming weeks of this month to highlight food nutrition requirements. In addition to the seminars plantation drives are to be held in Eklavya Model Residential Schools and Pandit Raghunath Murmu Abashik Schools for promotion of drumstick plantation as a livelihood.
