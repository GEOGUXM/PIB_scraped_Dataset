      Ministry of Tourism

      India Tourism Kolkata Heritage Walk and Swachhata hi Seva drive
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2018 3:13PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 17, 2018.


    As part of inaugural session of Paryatan Parv, Indiatourism Kolkata organised aHeritage Walk along with Swachhata hi Seva Drive as part of inaugural Paryatan Parv 2018 yesterday.


    As part of fortnight long Paryatan Parv activity, the Indiatourism Kolkata, Ministry of Tourism in association with Institute of Hotel Management, Kolkata, Travel Trade and Hospitality stakeholders and other enthusiast has organised a Heritage Walk along with Swachhata hi Seva Drive as part of inaugural Paryatan Parv 2018.


    A decorated procession started from iconic Victoria Memorial Hall after a Swachhata activity, walked through the famous Queen’s Way, Cathedral Road, Chowringhee covering Heritage monuments like St. Paul’s Cathedral, Kolkata Race Courseand Indian Museum.


    120 participants were present in this inaugural session of Paryatan Parv including IHM students, Travel Trade and Hospitality stakeholders, Regional Level Guides, officials of Indiatourism Kolkata, faculties of IHM etc. Besides, a famous film; tv-series actor has also participated in this walk.
