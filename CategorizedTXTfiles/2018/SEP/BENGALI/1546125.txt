      Ministry of Home Affairs

      BSF M&C Frontier Seizes Contraband Items worth More than Rs 7 Crores, Apprehends 35 Smugglers including 7 Bangladesh Nationals
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2018 2:47PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 14, 2018


    Border Security Force (BSF) M&C Frontier (FTR), with its Headquarters at Masimpur, Silchar under the leadership of Shri N S Jamwal, IG has seized contraband items worth more than Rs 7 crores and apprehended 35 smugglers including seven Bangladeshi nationals so far in the current year and has been instrumental enough in breaking the nexus between Indian and Bangladeshi smugglers.


    BSF has always appreciated and given credit of its success to the border people. BSF is not only effectively manning international borders, but also doing well in other fronts including anti-Naxal operations, NRC duties, election duties etc.


    BSF is also extending its full support and cooperation to the people of nation in times of needs, natural calamities etc.


    As a part of border management, BSF is also connecting to the people of remotest part of the country and have been successful in winning their hearts and minds.
