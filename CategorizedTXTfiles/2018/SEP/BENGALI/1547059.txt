      Ministry of External Affairs

      POPSK at Kharagpur on September 27
      Posted On: 24 SEP 2018 3:37PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 24, 2018.


    Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Kharagpur is going to be inaugurated on September 27, 2018 at Kharagpur Technology Mukhya Dakghar, IIT Campus, Kharagpur, West Mednipore, Pin – 721 302. Applicants are requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (Original & self attested Xerox copy). The details information of the documents required can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in . Ten appointments will be entertained on 27.09.2018 at 12 PM onwards and 30 appointments on daily basis from the next working day i.e. 28.09.2018. Appointments for the same will be released on POPSK website www.passportindia.gov.in from 25.09.2018 at 15.30 PM onwards. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payments system.


    Only those applicants who have obtained appointments through the online mode ( www.passportindia.gov.in ) will be entertained. Tatkaal, On-Hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed.
