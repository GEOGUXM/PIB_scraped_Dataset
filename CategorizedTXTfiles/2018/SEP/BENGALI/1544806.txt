      Ministry of Electronics & IT

      IT companies have been playing crucial role in making digital inclusion: Ravi Shankar Prasad
      Posted On: 03 SEP 2018 1:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 01, 2018.


    IT companies have been playing crucial role in making digital inclusion and gender equality of the people from across the country. IT companies are also contributing in making people digitally literate, stated, Shri Ravi Shankar Prasad, Union Minister of Electronics, IT and Law and Justice while laying foundation stone of the Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) Kolkata Incubation Facility here in the city today.


    The Minister informed that Digital India is the most transforming feature of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led Central government. Make in India, Skill India, Start Up, Stand Up, Digital Services and Digital Payments are some of the initiatives of the Centre which have made people's life more convenient and empowered, he added. He said that Aadhaar has ensured transparency and good governance in the country. Shri Prasad emphasised that Aadhaar data is completely safe and secured though he urged the IT giants to give cyber security and data safety top priority.


    Dr. Omkar Rai, Director General, STPI; Shri P.K.Das, Director, STPI Guwahati and Shri Samir Bhattacharya, Additional Secretary, Department of Information Technology and Electronics, government of West Bengal were also present on the occasion.
