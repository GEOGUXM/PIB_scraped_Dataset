      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Language prize ceremony at PIB and CBFC
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2018 6:58PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata : 14 th September, 2018 .


    Ms. Jane Namchu, Additional Director General (Media & Communication), presided over and gave away prizes at the Official Language award ceremonies held at PIB and Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) in Kolkata today. Excelling officials of both the offices, who participated in various Hindi competitions held during the past week, were awarded in these events. Smt. Asha Gaurisaria, senior advocate, High Court, Calcutta and also a newspaper columnist and Ms. Asmita Chaudhuri, Advisory Panel Member of CBFC were present as chief guest and key speakers. Shri Chinmay Chakravarty, Joint Director of PIB and Shri Partha Ghosh, Regional Officer of CBFC and other officials attended the events.
