      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Recruitment Advertisement of Staff Selection Commission
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2018 2:35PM by PIB Kolkata


    - Kolkata, 05 September, 2018


    The Staff Selection Commission has published its Advertisement Notice No “Phase-VI/2018/Selection Posts” for recruitment against various selection posts in Central Government Ministries and Departments on September 5, 2018 with closing date as September 30, 2018 on the website of its headquarters and all regional and sub-regional offices. Under this advertisement, the Eastern Regional Office of the Commission advertises 20 categories of Posts comprising 258 vacancies. The advertisement is available on the website of the SSC(ER) www.sscer.org and will remain effective till 30 September, 2018. Online applications are being invited through www.ssconline.nic.in from all the eligible candidates on and from September 5, 2018 to September 30, 2018 (upto 1700 hours). No other mode of submission of application is acceptable. The detailed information/instructions/guidelines for filing application/Scheme of Examination, etc. are provided in the Advertisement Notice.
