      Ministry of Communications

      Dak Adalat
      Posted On: 11 SEP 2018 4:45PM by PIB Chennai


    “DAK ADALAT” will be conducted at divisional level on 17.09.2018 at Office of the Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Chennai City Central Division, Sivagnanam Road, Chennai 600 017 at 03.30 p.m. The public may forward the complaints pertaining to this Division to the Senior Superintendent of Pos, Chennai City Central Division, Chennai 600 017 by furnishing the information such as date of booking of Registered letter, Parcel, Insured, VP and Money orders, Office of booking, address of both sender and addressee. Complaints, if any relating to Savings Bank accounts and Cash certificates etc., may also be sent.


    The delivery areas of the following Post offices come under the jurisdiction of this Division: T.Nagar, Mylapore, Greames Road, Tiruvallikkeni, Royapettah, Teynampet, Nungambakkam, Gopalapuram and Choolaimedu.
