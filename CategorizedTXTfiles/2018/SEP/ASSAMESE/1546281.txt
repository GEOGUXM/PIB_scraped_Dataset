      Ministry Of Law & Justice

      National Judicial Pay Commission To Hold Regional Conference In Guwahati Tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2018 4:13PM by PIB Guwahati


    Second National Judicial Pay Commission is holding its first Regional Conference tomorrow on 15th September in Guwahati. Judicial officers of Lower Judiciary of North East will take part in the conference and will give their inputs. The Conference will be mainly on subjects like Pay Scale, Emolument, Service Conditions, Retirement Benefits, Post Retirement Jobs in Judiciary, Infrastructure and Environment of the Lower Court and Judicial working conditions.


    Judges of Seven States of North East will be taking part in the conference.
