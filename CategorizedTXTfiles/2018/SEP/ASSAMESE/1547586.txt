      Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation

      Union Minister Shri Sadananda Gowda To Inaugurate Workshop of Trainers For Socio Economic Survey At Guwahati
      Posted On: 26 SEP 2018 3:04PM by PIB Guwahati


    The National Sample Survey Office (NSSO), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, Government of India is holding All India Workshop of Trainers (AIWOT) for the 77th Round of its Socio Economic Surveys at Guwahati during 27-28 September, 2018. All India Workshop of Trainers (AIWOT) for Time Use Survey will also be conducted on 29th September 2018 at Guwahati for officers of the NSSO.


    Shri D.V. Sadananda Gowda, Minister for Statistics and Programme Implementation, Government of India will inaugurate the workshops on 27th September, 2018. Shri Vijay Goel, Minister of State for Statistics and Programme Implementation, will also grace the occasion. Other dignitaries attending the Workshop include Dr. Manoj Kumar Panda, Acting Chairman, National Statistical Commission, Shri K.V. Eapen, IAS, Secretary, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, Members of National Statistical Commission, senior officers of NSSO and the State Directorates of Economics & Statistics.


    Socio-Economic Surveys conducted by the NSSO during its different rounds are the primary source of various indicators used by the Government for planning, policy formulation and decision making. The subjects being covered in this survey are ‘Land and Livestock Holdings of Households and Situation Assessment of Agricultural households’ and ‘Debt and Investment’. The NSSO and the State Directorates of Economics & Statistics will carry out the 77th Round survey programme jointly for a period of one year starting from 1st January, 2019. In addition, the Time Use Survey (TUS) will be launched from 1st January 2019.
