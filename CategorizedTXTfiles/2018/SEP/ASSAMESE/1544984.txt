      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Message of Swachh Bharat better spread by our daily activities: DG North-east Workshop on Swachh Bharat Urban organised in city
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2018 3:54PM by PIB Guwahati


    In a workshop organised by the Regional Outreach Bureau (ROB), Guwahati, the Director General, Northeast Zone, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Shri Kuldeep Singh Dhatwalia urged all the participants that the message of cleanliness and ‘Swachhta hi Seva’ is best accomplished if we inculcate the habit of cleanliness in our day-to-day activities. The workshop was an orientation event organised for the officers of the Field Outreach Bureau of the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting under the Guwahati region.


    Shri Dhatwalia also reiterated the message of the Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi under the Swachh Bharat Mission. He also reminded everyone that cleanliness has several implications including on health and our life.


    Earlier, the Head of ROB, Guwahati, Dr Ahmed Muyeen Farooqi informed all that the ROB will be holding five awareness programmes in September at Khanapara, Pub Sarumotoria, Birubari, Zoo road and Maligaon, all within the GMC limits. The workshop was attended by the resource persons from Guwahati Municipal Corporation and Swachh Bharat Mission, Guwahati. The All India Radio News and DDK news heads were also present in the event.
