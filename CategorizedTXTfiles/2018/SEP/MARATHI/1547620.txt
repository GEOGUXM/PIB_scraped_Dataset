      Ministry of Defence

      Indian Air Force to organise static display at air force station sonegoan on the occasion of second anniversary of the Surgical Strike
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2018 11:44AM by PIB Mumbai


    To commemorate Second Anniversary of Surgical Strike a static display is being organized at Air Force Station Sonegoan Nagpur. Indian citizens are welcome to visit Air Force Station Sonegoan Nagpur on 28 Sep 18 and 29 Sep 18 from 0900h to 1600h to witness the static display. Visitors must carry valid photo identity proof like Aadhaar Card, PAN card, Driving License or any other photo identity issues by Govt of India.
