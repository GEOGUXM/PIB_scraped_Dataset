      KVIC to observe Swachhata Hi Seva till October 2
      Posted On: 15 SEP 2018 8:36PM by PIB Mumbai


    The cleanliness drive, “Swacchata Hi Seva” is being observed from 15th September to 2nd October 2018, by Khadi and Village Industries Commission at its head office Erla Road, Vile Parle, Mumbai and all state and divisional offices. The Chief Executive Officer, KVIC, Smt. Preeta Verma administered the Swacchata pledge at the KVIC head office today and exhorted all present to keep the surroundings clean. The CEO also planted a sapling in the office premises on the occasion.


    Joint Chief Executive officer, KVIC, Shri K.S. Rao; Deputy CEO, Shri. Y K Barabatikar; all Program Directors, Officers and employees participated in large numbers.
