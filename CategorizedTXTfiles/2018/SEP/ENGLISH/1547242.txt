      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      National Sports Awards 2018 and National Adventure Awards 2017 conferred
      Posted On: 25 SEP 2018 7:00PM by PIB Delhi


    President of India Shri Ram NathKovind conferred National Sports Awards 2018and National Adventure Awards, 2017 in a ceremony held at RashtrapatiBhawanhere today. Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Youth Affairs and Sports Col. Rajyawardhan Rathore was among the dignitaries present on the occasion.
