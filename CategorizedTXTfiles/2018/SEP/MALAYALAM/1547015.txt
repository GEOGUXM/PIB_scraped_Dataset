      Swachhata Hi Seva
      Posted On: 22 SEP 2018 11:16AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    As part of the Swachhata Hi Seva campaign, awareness programme on better sanitation practices of using toilets, hand washing, health and hygiene awareness was conducted in the Cochin Fisheries Harbour at Thoppumpady by the employees of Medical Dept.


    Students of RLV College, Thripunithura painted graffiti on the five walls of 110 Sub-station of Cochin Port in Willingdon Island.


    Cleanliness activities were carried out at KPK Menon Road to Hospital junction in Willingdon Island and from Marine building to BTP Gate were carried out by Medical Dept. and Marine Dept. respectively.


    Disposal of old papers at the control room of Traffic Dept. was carried out. Civil Engg. Dept. started the renovation works of toilets at Marine building.
