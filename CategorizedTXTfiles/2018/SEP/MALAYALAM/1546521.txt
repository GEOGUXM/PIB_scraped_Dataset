      Awareness programme conducted at Vypeen Fisheries Harbour
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2018 4:32PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Employees of Cochin Port Trust conducted awareness programme on the importance of keeping the harbour and water bodies clean at Kalamukku Fisheries Harbour, Vypeen on 17th September, 2018. The fishermen were urged to keep the harbour clean, reduce the use of plastic and to stop others from throwing waste to the water bodies.


    The Civil Engineering Department started the first phase of cleaning residential area in RNAS area in Willingdon Island. Swachhata Pledge was administered in all departments.
