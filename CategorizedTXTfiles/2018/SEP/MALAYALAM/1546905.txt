      SWACHHATA HI SEVA : CLEANING AT BOT JUNCTION, WILLINGTON ISLAND
      Posted On: 19 SEP 2018 7:51PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    As part of the Swachhata Pakhwada & Swachhata Hi Seva in Cochin Port Trust all officers and staff of Civil Engineering & Finance Departments carried out old files/records today . Mass cleaning was carried out by volunteers from all departments near Cochin Port Trust Walkway and BOT junction in Willingdon Island. The overgrown vegetation on the sides of busiest junction in Willindon Island was cleared alongwith flex banners, etc. The dried up branches of trees on the roadside were cut and removed and BOT junction was given a new look.
