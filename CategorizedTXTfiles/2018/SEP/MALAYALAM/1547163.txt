      Sports Person Ms. P.U. Chithra received offer of appointment from Southern Railway, Palakkad Division
      Posted On: 24 SEP 2018 11:40PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Shri Pratap Singh Shami, Divisional Railway Manager, Palakkad Division today(24.09.2018) handed over the Offer of appointment to Ms. P. U. Chithra, Ace Middle Distance Runner of the country. Ms P. U. Chithra is being appointed as Senior Clerk in Southern Railway, Palakkad Division. Additional Divisional Railway Manager, Shri D. Sai Baba, Senior Divisional Electrical Engineer (Traction Distribution) and Secretary of Sports Association of Palakkad Division, Shri S. Jayakrishanan were also present on the occasion held today at Divisional Railway Manager's Office.
