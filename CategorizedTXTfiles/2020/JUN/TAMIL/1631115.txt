      DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 12 JUN 2020 3:43PM by PIB Chennai


    It is proposed to conduct Dak Adalat on 22.06.2020 at 1500 hours at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600002. The members of the public are requested to send their grievances, if any regarding Postal services (Money order / Registered Articles / Savings Bank / Savings Certificate etc.,) availed at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600002, either in person or by post or through email at doannaroadhpo.tn@indiapost.gov.in , with the caption DAK ADALAT, on or before 19.06.2020. They may also attend the Dak Adalat in person on 22.06.2020 at 1500 hours, a ccording to a press release issued by Shri R Balachander, Chief Post Master, Anna Salai, Head Post Office, Chennai.
