      National Statistical Office (Field Operations Division), Mohali to celebrate Statistics Day, 2020 through video conferencing
      Posted On: 27 JUN 2020 7:01PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, 27 June 2020


    On account of COVID-19 pandemic,National Statistical Office (Field Operations Division), Mohali under Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, Government of India will be celebrating Statistics Day through video conferencing.


    To pay tribute to Sh. Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis known as "Father of Statistics" on his birth anniversary, the Government of India has been celebrating Statistics Day on 29th of June every year since 2007.Celebrating Statistics Day is also aimed at creating awareness among the younger generation on the role of statistics in socio-economic planning and policy formulation for the development of the country.


    In continuation to the steps taken by the Ministry for improving the data system on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the theme selected for Statistics Day, 2020 covers SDG-3 (Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages) and SDG-5 (Achieve Gender equality and empower all women and girls).
