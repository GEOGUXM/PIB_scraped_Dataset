      Fact Check: No shortage of fertiliser in Manipur
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2020 6:06PM by PIB Guwahati


    A report published in a section of press in the state yesterday said that farmers of Seijang, Imphal East were agitated over shortage of fertilisers in the state.


    ( Fix a date so that we can take our own lives: Seijang farmers, Imphal Free Press dated 23.6.2020, link: https://ifp.co.in/fix-a-date-so-that-we-can-take-our-own-lives-seijang-farmers#:~:text=By%20IFP%20Bureau%20%7C%20Jun%2023,%2C%202020%2C%2010%3A20%20p.m.&text=Unable%20to%20get%20their%20hands,they%20can%20take%20their%20lives. )


    Director of Agriculture, Manipur government has since assured the far m ers that there will not be any shortage of Urea in the state. Speaking to media persons, Director Agriculture said that the situation is being monitored constantly. He added that Manipur requires around 15,000 tones of urea every year but the Central Government usually supplies 20,000 tones to the State, which is more than sufficient. Chief Minister and Agriculture Minister of Manipur have a l ready directed the officials to inspect the private Urea dealers and their godowns to ensure smooth supply of fertilisers during this farming season.
