      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      DISINFORMATION ON RECRUITMENT OF PORTERS
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2020 9:01PM by PIB Guwahati


    News articles are appearing in some media suggesting that in Sikkim, Army is carrying out recruitment of porters due to border tensions.


    It is clarified that recruitment of porters is an annual activity and is not connected with any other border related issues.


    All are informed that recruitment of such personnel is being carried out for people of Sikkim, Bihar and North Bengal for which due process including advertisements was set into motion months ago for the knowledge of public in the region.
