      Regional Outreach Bureau to observe International Yoga Day through Online platform
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2020 4:24PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Regional Outreach Bureau,(ROB) under Ministry of Information & Broadcasting will observe this year's International Yoga Day tomorrow (June 21,2020) through online platform considerinng the prevailing Covid-19 pandemic situation. The theme of International Yoga Day 2020 is Yoga at Home and Yoga with Family, as decided by the United Nations. ROB is asociating with National Ayush Mision and Govt. of Kerala in organising Webinar on diseases in all districts and online Yoga Video and Quiz competions. The topic for essay competition for High School category is "Yoga as a habit", and for Higher Secondary category is "Yoga as a treatment".
    
    Field Outreach Bureau (FOB) Palakkad will organise an online workshop on 21 June 2020 in connection with the International Yoga Day. Yoga classes and demonstrations will be conducted as part of the seminar. Competition for students also will be organised along with the seminar.
    
    FOB Ernakulam will conduct a webinar on GoogleMeet in the evening. FOB Kottayam is to organize online seminars with focus on importance of doing Yoga and meditation by both students and elders during these trying times of Covid-19.The webinar is arranged in coordination with Malavedan community wattsapp group , Narnamohizhi Panchayat in Ranni and the women's wing of Yogakshema Sabha, Alappuzha District. The sessions will be led by techers from Art of Living.
