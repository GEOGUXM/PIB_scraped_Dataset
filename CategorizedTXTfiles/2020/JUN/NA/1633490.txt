      PM greets people on Ashadhi Bij, the Kutchi New Year
      Posted On: 23 JUN 2020 10:24AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the special occasion of Ashadhi Bij, the Kutchi New Year.


    “On the special occasion of Ashadhi Bij, greetings to the Kutchi community. This is a community known for its great culture and bravery. I pray that the coming year is filled with happiness and good health”, the Prime Minister said.
