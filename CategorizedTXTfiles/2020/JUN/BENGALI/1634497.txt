      Ministry of Railways

      CANCELLATION OF ALL TICKETS BOOKED FOR REGULAR TIME-TABLED TRAINS FOR JOURNEY DATES FROM 01.07.2020 TO 12.08.2020
      
      SPECIAL MAIL/EXPRESS TRAINS SHALL CONTINUE TO BE OPERATED

      Posted On: 26 JUN 2020 4:06PM by PIB Kolkata
      Kolkata, 26 June, 2020
      It has been decided that, all tickets booked for regular time-tabled trains for journey dates from 01.07.2020 to 12.08.2020 may be cancelled and full refunds generated.
      All originating long distance Mail/Express and Intercity trains (including Premium trains) and Passenger trains having reserved accommodation shall remain cancelled from 01.07.2020 to 12.08.2020.
      The Special Mail/Express trains shall continue to be operated.
