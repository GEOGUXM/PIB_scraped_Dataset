      Ministry of Shipping

      MV BULK JAPAN Carrying 38207 of ST Coal Berths at HDC, SMP
      Posted On: 26 JUN 2020 6:55PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 June, 2020


    The Shyama Prasad Mookerjee Port, Kolkata (Erstwhile Kolkata Port Trust), under the able guidance of the ministry of Shipping is ever committed to improve the parcel size at the port.


    Today, bulk carrier, MV BULK JAPAN, with the carrying capacity 82951 tonnes Deadweight tonnage and with the length of 228.29 metres and width of 32.26 metres berthed at Haldia Dock Complex of Shyama Prasad Mookerjee Port (SMP), Kolkata. The ship is carrying a parcel of 38207 MT, which is one of the highest parcel loads handled within the impounded dock of HDC, SMP, Kolkata in recent times.


    The ship was built in 2006. The home port is Monrovia and is sailing under the flag of Liberia.
