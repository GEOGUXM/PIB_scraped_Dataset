      45 RAKES WITH MORE THAN 1.20 LAKH TONNES FOOD GRAINS REACH SER JURISDICTION IN JUNE
      Posted On: 29 JUN 2020 5:30PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 29 June, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has taken every possible measure to ensure uninterrupted supply of food grains and other essential commodities across the country in the wake of outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic. As per the guidelines provided by the Ministry of Railways, SER has intensified its entire network for unabated movement of goods trains.


    In the month of June, 2020 till date, more than 1.20 lakh tonnes of food grains like rice and wheat have been unloaded from 45 rakes which arrived at different stations and sidings of the SER. Out of this, more than 70,000 tonnes of rice was unloaded from 24 rakes while more than 50,000 tonnes of wheat was unloaded from 21 rakes. The rakes were unloaded at different locations of SER namely Midnapur, Kalaikunda, Bankura, Purulia, Bokaro Steel City, Namkon, Tatisilwai, Tatanagar, between June 1 and June 28, 2020.
