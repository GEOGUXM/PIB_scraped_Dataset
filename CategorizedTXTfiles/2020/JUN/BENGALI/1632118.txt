      Ministry of Railways

      ER'S SEAMLESS FREIGHT SERVICES TRANSPORTING ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 17 JUN 2020 6:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 17 June, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and other merchandise for maintaining the supply chain and availability of essential commodities as well as other inputs during lockdown period, ER is operating freight trains on 24x7 basis, transporting food grains, sugar, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, steel, coal & minerals etc.


    THE TOTAL LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON 16.06.2020 IS AS FOLLOWS:-


    Total Unloading : 60 Rakes


    Cement – 5 Rakes


    Clinker – 6 Rakes


    Coal and mineral - 37 Rakes


    Fertilizer – 2 Rakes


    POL – 1 Rake


    Rice – 3 Rakes


    Slag – 2 Rakes


    Tar – 1 Rake


    Wheat – 3 Rakes


    Total Loading : 63.6 Rakes


    Coal- 24 Rakes


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Cement – 6 Rakes


    Container- 1 rakes


    Stone – 18 rakes


    Ballast- 5 Rakes


    Ash – 2 Rakes


    Jute & others – 2.6 Rakes


    Sand – 2 Rake


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff are well taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer are affluently available at all working points. Proper sanitization is also being done wherever required.
