      ER’S FREIGHT SERVICES CONTINUES TO MAINTAIN SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 08 JUN 2020 9:04PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 08 June, 2020


    To maintain the supply of essential commodities and necessary inputs at different areas of the country in the midst of lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) has taken a number of initiatives to continue the freight services round the clock, transporting food grains, sugar, fertilizers, petroleum products, minerals, steel, cement etc and coal for power generating sector.
