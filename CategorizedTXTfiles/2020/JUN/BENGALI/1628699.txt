      Ministry of Railways

      ER IS CONTINUING ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 02 JUN 2020 6:31PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 02 June, 2020


    Eastern Railway is continuing running of freight trains on round the clock basis to maintain supply of essential commodities at different corners of the country since initiation of Nation-wide lock down. Freight trains carrying food grains, fertilizer, steel, cement, stone, petroleum products, coal etc are reaching at different areas to cater to the demand of consumables and other inputs.


    

    
    Eastern Railway has run 225 freight trains on Monday (May 1, 2020), the brake up of which is as follows:
    
    Ash - 4 Rakes
    
    Ballast - 1 Rake
    
    Cement – 19 Rakes
    
    Clinker – 3 Rakes
    
    Container – 15 Rakes
    
    Coalandmineral – 51 Rakes
    
    Fertilizer – 3 Rakes
    
    POLproduct - 5 Rakes
    
    Maize – 5 Rakes
    
    Onion – 2 Rakes
    
    Foodgrains&Rice – 15 Rakes
    
    Steel – 9 Rakes
    
    Salt – 2 Rakes
    
    Stone – 10 Rakes
    
    Sugar – 2 Rakes
    
    Slag – 1 Rake
    
    Wheat – 2 Rakes
    
    Parcel - 5 Rakes
    
    Besides, 71 Empty Rakes were despatch to different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities. Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff are well taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitization is also being done wherever required.
