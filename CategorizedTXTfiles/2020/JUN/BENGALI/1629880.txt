      Ministry of Railways

      RUNNING OF SPECIAL TRAIN BETWEEN ROURKELA-BHUBANESWAR-ROURKELA
      Posted On: 06 JUN 2020 4:01PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 June, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to run one pair of Special train between Rourkela and Bhubaneswar from June 08, 2020 to June 30, 2020.


    Rourkela-Bhubaneswar-Rourkela Special (02861/02862) will run five days a week, except Saturday and Sunday. This Special Train will run according to the existing timings and stoppages of 22839/22840 Rourkela-Bhubaneswar-Rourkela Intercity Express.


    02861 Rourkela-Bhubaneswar Special leaving Rourkela at 5:10 am will reach Bhubaneswar at 12:30 pm, the same day. In the return direction, 02862 Bhubaneswar-Rourkela Special leaving Bhubaneswar at 2:10 pm will arrive Rourkela at 9:40 pm, the same day. This special train will have two AC Chair Car and 10 General Chair Car (reserved) coaches.
