      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO SUPPLY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2020 6:43PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 June, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuing its freight services round the clock to maintain supply of essential commodities, consumables as well as other inputs to different corners of the country. The freight service of ER is thus helping in meeting up the demand with the availability of such commodities in those areas.


    THE FREIGHT LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON FRIDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:

    TOTAL LOADING: 65.3 Rakes


    Coal- 24 Rakes


    Steel - 7.9 rakes


    Cement – 7 Rakes


    Stone – 15 Rakes

    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitiser were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation was done wherever required.
