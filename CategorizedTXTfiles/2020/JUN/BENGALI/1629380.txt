      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS WILL RUN FOR FEW MORE DAYS
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2020 5:55PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 04 June, 2020


    After the introduction of lock down to resist spread of COVID-19, Eastern Railway (ER) started running time tabled Parcel Express since April 10, 2020 in different routes for swift transport of essential commodities, consumables, medicine, medical equipment etc to different corners of the country. The CPRO’s office of this railway has informed that Parcel Express Trains which were earlier scheduled up to May 31, 2020 have been extended from June 4, 2020 and will run up to June 15, 2020 (journey commencing stations) on nominated days.
