      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES FOR SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 06 JUN 2020 4:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 June, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuing its freight services round the clock in the midst of lockdown to maintain the supply chain of essential commodities, consumables and other inputs at different corners of the country. The freight service of ER is helping in meeting up the demand with the availability of essential commodities and other inputs at different areas.
