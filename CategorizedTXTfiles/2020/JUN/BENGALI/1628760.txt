      Ministry of Railways

      SER WITHDRAWS STOPPAGES OF SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 02 JUN 2020 8:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 02 June, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has withdrawn the stoppages of the below mentioned trains as requested by Govt. of Jharkhand, Govt. of West Bengal and Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, to ensure better medical screening of the passengers on arrival. Until further advisory since June 4, 2020, the trains will not stop in between stated a press release from CPROs office.
