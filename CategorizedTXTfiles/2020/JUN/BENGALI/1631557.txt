      Ministry of Railways

      REVISED TIMINGS OF SOME SPECIAL TRAINS AT TERMINAL STATIONS OVER ER
      Posted On: 14 JUN 2020 7:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 14 June, 2020


    Arrival & departure time of some special trains at terminal stations will be revised over Eastern Railway w.e.f. 16.6.2020 as under:-


    • 02301 UP Howrah – New Delhi AC special train will leave Howrah at 16.45 hrs. instead of 17.05 hrs.


    • 02302 DN New Delhi – Howrah AC special train will arrive Howrah at 10.30 hrs. instead of 10.05 hrs.


    • 02303 UP Howrah – New Delhi (via Patna) special train will leave Howrah at 7.45 hrs. instead of 8.00 hrs.


    • 02304 DN New Delhi - Howrah (via Patna) special train will arrive Howrah at 17.30 hrs. instead of 17.15 hrs.


    • 02381 UP Howrah – New Delhi (via Gaya) special train will leave Howrah at 8.00 hrs. instead of 8.15 hrs.


    • 02382 DN New Delhi – Howrah (via Gaya) special train will arrive Howrah at 17.30 hrs. instead of 17.15 hrs.


    • 02023 UP Howrah – Patna special train will leave Howrah at 14.35 hrs. instead of 14.45 hrs.


    • 02024 DN Patna – Howrah special train will arrive Howrah at 13.35 hrs. instead of 13.25 hrs.


    • 02307 UP Howrah – Jodhpur special train will leave Howrah at 23.25 hrs. instead of 23.40 hrs.


    • 02308 DN Jodhpur – Howrah special train will arrive Howrah at 4.30 hrs. instead of 4.15 hrs.


    • 02377 UP Sealdah – New Alipurduar special train will leave Sealdah at 23.00 hrs. instead of 23.20 hrs.


    • 02378 DN New Alipurduar – Sealdah special train will arrive Sealdah at 7.05 hrs. instead of 6.45 hrs.


    Stoppage time of the above trains at intermediate stations will remain unchanged.
