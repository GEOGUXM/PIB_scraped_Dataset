      MAULAUNA ABUL KALAM AZAD UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY PROVIDES BOOKS TO AMPHAN AFFECTED STUDENTS
      Posted On: 15 JUN 2020 7:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 th May, 2020


    Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) has provided books and other learning materials to the students of Pakhirala High School of Gosaba, a remotely located school in the fringe of Sundarban. Numbers of students of this school have either lost their books and other study materials or found them in an unusable pathetic condition after the super cyclone Amphan. On a request from the Headmaster of the School the Vice Chancellor of MAKAUT Prof. (Dr.) Saikat Mitra took the imitative of providing books and learning materials to the affected students.


    Today the COVID19 and Amphan Contingency team of MAKAUT reach out the school and delivered the study material like 160 books, 120 note books, Pens, pencils, erasers, school bags, hand sanitizers and sanitary napkins for girl students. The Head Master along with the other staffs of the school was there to receive the materials. Around 40 students of class X will be benefitted with these resources.
