      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S SEALDAH-GUWAHATI-SEALDAH AND HOWRAH-GUWAHATI-HOWRAH PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS TO CONTINUE RUN
      Posted On: 15 JUN 2020 8:31PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 June, 2020


    After the introduction of nationwide lockdown Eastern Railway (ER) has started running time table Parcel Express trains since April 10, 2020 in different routes in different phases for swift transport of essential commodities, consumables, medicine, medical equipment etc. These Parcel Express trains were earlier scheduled to run up to June 15, 2020 (journey commencing station).


    Now, it has been planned to extend the running of 00313/00314 Sealdah-Guwahati-Sealdah and 00303/00304 Howrah-Guwahati-Howrah Parcel Express trains for another six months from today with revised days of run, timings and composition.


    00313 Sealdah-Guwahati Parcel Express (Bi-Weekly) will leave Sealdah at 1:50 pm on Tuesday & Friday from June 19, 2020 to December 15, 2020 and will reach Guwahati at 12:00 hrs on next day. In return direction, 00314 Guwahati–Sealdah Parcel Express (bi-Weekly) train will leave Guwahati at 9:00 pm on Wednesday & Saturday from June 20, 2020 to December 16, 2020 and will arrive Sealdah station at 9:00 pm on the next day. This Parcel Express train will stop at Bandel, Barddhaman, Khaltipur, Malda Town, New Jalpaiguri, New Coochbehar and New Bongaigaon stations en route in both directions.


    00303 Howrah-Guwahati Parcel Express (Bi-Weekly) will leave Howrah station at 14:00 hrs on Wednesday & Saturday from June 17, 2020 to December 12, 2020 and will reach Guwahati at 12:00 pm on the next day. In return direction, 00304 Guwahati–Howrah Parcel Express (Bi-weekly) train will leave Guwahati at 9:00 pm on Thursday & Sunday from June 18, 2020 to December 13, 2020 and will arrive Howrah station at 9:00 pm on the next day. This Parcel Express train will stop at Srirampur, Bandel, Barddhaman, Khaltipur, Malda Town, New Jalpaiguri, New Coochbehar and New Bongaigaon stations en route in both directions.
