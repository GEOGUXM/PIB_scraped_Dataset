      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS CARRY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES TO DIFFERENT AREAS
      Posted On: 09 JUN 2020 7:13PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 09 June, 2020


    Eastern Railway is running freight trains as well as Parcel Express trains on round the clock basis to maintain supply of essential commodities at different corners of the country during lockdown period. Freight trains carrying food grains, sugar, fertiliser, cement, stone, petroleum products, coal etc are reaching at different areas to cater to the demand of people.


    ER has run a total of 270 freight trains on Monday (June 8, 2020). The details are as follows:


    Ash -3 Rakes
    
    Automobile- 1 Rakes
    
    Ballast- 2 Rakes
    
    Cement- 26 Rakes
    
    Clinker -4 Rakes
    
    Container- 26 Rakes
    
    Coal&mineral – 55 Rakes
    
    EdibleOil- 2 Rakes
    
    Fertilizer- 8 Rakes
    
    POLproduct- 5 Rakes
    
    FoodGrain&Rice – 7 Rakes
    
    Steel – 8 Rakes
    
    Stone – 21 Rakes
    
    Slag – 2 Rakes
    
    Wheat- 2 Rakes
    
    Besides, 98 Empty Rakes were dispatched to different freight operating
    points for loading of essential commodities.
    
    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of
    keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing and other
    precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were
    available in all working points. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
