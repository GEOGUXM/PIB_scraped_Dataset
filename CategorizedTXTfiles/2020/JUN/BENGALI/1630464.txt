      Ministry of Communications

      S Mervin Alexander, New Postmaster General, West Bengal Circle
      Posted On: 09 JUN 2020 4:59PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 09 June, 2020


    The West Bengal Circle (including Sikkim and Andaman & Nicobar Islands), Department of Posts has a new Post master General. His name is Shri S Mervin Alexander. Shri Alexander is an officer of the 1987 batch of the Indian Postal Service. He had worked in the States of Tamil Nadu, Kerala besides, at the Postal Directorate, New Delhi.


    An alumnus of National Defence College, Shri Alexander was Joint Secretary, Dept. of Atomic Energy in his last assignment. As Joint Secretary, he was also Director in the Boards of three PSUs like, India Rare Earths Limited (IREL), Uranium Corporation of India Limited (UCIL) & Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL).


    He also functioned as Governing Council member of Tata Memorial Centre (Tata Cancer Hospital) & B. Barooah Cancer Institute, Guwahati, both autonomous Institutions under Department of Atomic Energy (DAE).
