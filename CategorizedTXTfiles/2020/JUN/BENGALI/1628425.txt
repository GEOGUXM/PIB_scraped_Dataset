      Ministry of Railways

      CHANGE IN TIMINGS OF HOWRAH-BHUBANESWAR AND RANCHI-PATNA SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 01 JUN 2020 8:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 01 June, 2020


    To comply with the prescribed protocols in train travel under present situation, it has been decided that 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Express Special and 02366 Ranchi-Patna Express Special Trains will run as per revised timings from tomorrow (June 2, 2020).


    Revised Timings of 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Express Special w.e.f. 02-06-2020


    02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Express Special leaving Howrah at 2:25 pm will reach Bhubaneswar at 9:20 pm on the same day.


    The departure timing from Kharagpur will be at 4:20 pm, from Jaleswar at 5:10 pm, from Balasore at 5:47 pm, from Soro at 6:12 pm and from Bhadrak at 6:52 pm in SER system.


    Revised Timings of 02366 Ranchi-Patna Express Special w.e.f. 02-06-2020


    02366 Ranchi-Patna Express Special leaving Ranchi at 3:25 pm will reach Patna at 11:20 pm on the same day.


    The departure timing from Muri will be at 4:33 pm and from Bokaro Steel City at 5:40 pm in SER system.
