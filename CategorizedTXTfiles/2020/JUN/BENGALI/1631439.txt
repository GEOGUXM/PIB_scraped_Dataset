      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUIES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 13 JUN 2020 8:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 13 June, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously running its freight trains and on round the clock basis to maintain supply of essential commodities at different corners of the country since the initiation of nation-wide lock down. Freight trains carrying food grains, fertilizer, steel, cement, stone, petroleum products, coal etc are reaching far and wide to cater to the requirements of consumables and other inputs.


    ER has run 281 freight trains on Friday, June 12, 2020, the breakup of which is:


    
    ASH – 1 Rake
    BALLAST – 3 Rakes
    CEMENT – 22 Rakes
    CLINKER - 3 Rakes
    CONTAINER - 21 Rakes
    COAL&MINERAL- 62 Rakes
    EDIBLEOIL- 1 Rake
    FERTILIZER – 6 Rakes
    IRON&STEEL – 10 Rakes
    MAIZE – 5 Rakes
    POL PRODUCT- 3 Rakes
    ONION - 1 Rake
    FOODGRAIN – 5 Rakes
    SUGAR- 4 Rakes
    STONE- 21 Rakes
    SAND – 1 Rake
    SLAG – 2 Rakes


    Besides, 110 Empty Rakes were dispatched to different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities. Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
