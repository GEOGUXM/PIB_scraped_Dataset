      Shri Pradip Kumar Tripathi Assumes Charge as Secretary in Ministry of Steel
      Posted On: 01 JUN 2020 3:22PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Pradip Kumar Tripathi, IAS (J&K:1987) has taken over as Secretary, Ministry of Steel, Govt. of India, here today. Prior to this, Shri Tripathihad been posted as Special Secretary and Establishment officer in Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT).
