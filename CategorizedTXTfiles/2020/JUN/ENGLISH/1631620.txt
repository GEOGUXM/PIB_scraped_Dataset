      PM greets people of Odisha on Raja Parba
      Posted On: 14 JUN 2020 2:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Odisha on the special festival of Raja Parba.


    "Greetings on the very special festival of Raja Parba. May this occasion strengthen the spirit of joy and brotherhood in society. I also pray for the good health and well-being of my fellow citizens", the Prime Minister said.
