      Prime Minister's Office

      PM speaks to Assam CM regarding Baghjan fire tragedy; assures all possible support
      Posted On: 10 JUN 2020 4:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi spoke to Assam Chief Minister Shri Sarbananda Sonowal to discuss the situation in the wake of Baghjan fire tragedy and assured all possible help from the centre.


    In a tweet Prime Minister’s Office said, “PM spoke to Assam CM Shri Sarbananda Sonowal to discuss the situation in the wake of the Baghjan fire tragedy. PM assured all possible support from the Centre. The situation is being monitored closely.”
