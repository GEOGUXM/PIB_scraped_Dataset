      Conditions are becoming favourable for further advancement of Southwest Monsoon
      
      Scattered to fairly widespread rain/thundershowers very likely to continue over western Himalayan region
      
      Isolated heavy falls also very likely over Uttarakhand, East Uttar Pradesh and South Rajasthan during next 24 hours

      Posted On: 05 JUN 2020 2:02PM by PIB Delhi

    Significant Weather Features


    Conditions are becoming favourable for further advancement of Southwest Monsoon into some more parts of Central Arabian Sea, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Puducherry & Karaikal, Southwest & Eastcentral Bay of Bengal; entire Southeast Bay of Bengal and some parts of Westcentral Bay of Bengal during next 2 days. 2. Under the influence of the Western Disturbance, scattered to fairly widespread rain/thundershowers very likely to continue over western Himalayan region and scattered rain/thundershowers over plains of northwest India during next 2 days and decrease thereafter. 3. Isolated thunderstorm accompanied with lightning, gusty wind over these regions during next 2 days. Isolated heavy falls also very likely over Uttarakhand, East Uttar Pradesh and South Rajasthan during next 24 hours. 4. Widespread rain/thundershowers along the west coast and Isolated to Scattered rainfall activity over Gujarat State, Interior Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu during nezt 4-5 days. 5. Isolated heavy falls also very likely over Kerala and Konkan & Goa during next 3 days and over Coastal Karnataka during next 24 hours.
