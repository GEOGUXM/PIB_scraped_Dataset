      India Post Arranges for Innovative Competition to Encourage Creativity and the Habit of Writing
      Posted On: 18 SEP 2020 4:59PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 18, 2020


    India Post, West Bengal Circle is organising a slogan writing, painting and calligraphy competition from Thursday (September 18, 2020). The last date of submission for this competition is October 31. The successful entries will be announced on November 14, 2020. Anybody interested to participate, may purchase ‘Competition Postcard’ from the post office counters by paying Rs 2/- for each such postcard.


    The first prize amounts to Rs 10,000, the second prize, Rs 7,500 and the third prize is Rs 5,000 in each category.


    The theme of the Slogan Writing Competition is ‘What is the Most Important Thing I did during the Time of Covid-19’. The answer is required to be written in the competition postcards within 50 words in Hindi/English/Bengali.


    For the painting competition, any one postage stamp on Mahatma Gandhi may be painted on the postcard.


    For the Calligraphy competition, the lines for Calligraphy design are:


    “Where the mind is without fear and the head is held high


    Where knowledge is free


    Where the world has not been broken into fragments


    By narrow domestic walls”


    For the Hindi version, the following is to be written:


    “सारे जहाँ से अच्छा, हिन्दोस्ताँ हमारा
    हम बुलबुलें हैं इसकी, यह गुलिस्ताँ हमारा”


    For the Bengali, the lines to be written are:


    চিত্ত যেথা ভয়শূন্য, উচ্চ যেথা শির,
    জ্ঞান যেথা মুক্ত, যেথা গৃহের প্রাচীর


    আপন প্রাঙ্গণতলে দিবসশর্বরী
    বসুধারে রাখে নাই খণ্ড ক্ষুদ্র করি,


    The Competitors should have to drop the designed postcards in the earmarked letterbox of the nearest post office for successful participation in the competition. The details of the competition are also available in the website— https://www.westbengalpost.gov.in
