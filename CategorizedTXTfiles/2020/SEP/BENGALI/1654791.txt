      Ministry of Railways

      COMMENDABLE PERFORMANCE OF RAILWAY PROTECTION FORCE OF SOUTH EASTERN RAILWAY
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 12:17AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 15, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER)â€™s RPF (Railway Protection Force) personnel are playing a significant role in providing security to all the rail travellers as well as in guarding the railway property and its vital installations.


    During the nationwide COVID-19 crisis, the RPF personnel of this railway, in the month of August, 2020 have conducted regular drives against miscreants involved in theft of passenger belongings. Preventive measures were also taken to control the theft of railway materials and booked consignments.


    During the month of August 2020, a total number of 20 miscreants were arrested under the RP (UP) Act and Railway property worth Rs. 1,06,143/- was recovered. Moreover, 57 offenders were booked under the different sections of the Railways Act and Rs.15,750/- was realised as fine.


    Six left behind luggage of the passengers were recovered by the RPF during the month and one case of smoking in train was detected by the RPF under the Tobacco Act and a fine amounting to Rs.200/- was realised.


    RPF personnel of SER have taken active part to conduct 154 cleanliness drives and also removed 30 encroachments at various places in the SER jurisdiction.


    During the month of August 2020, the RPF personnel rescued eight minor boys and one minor girl and the rescued minors were handed over to their relatives with proper verification and acknowledgement.
