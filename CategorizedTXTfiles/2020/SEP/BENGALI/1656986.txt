      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES SPECIAL DRIVE FOR ELIMINATION OF PLASTIC WASTE
      Posted On: 20 SEP 2020 5:39PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 20, 2020


    Intensive cleanliness drive is continuing at the different Divisions of the Eastern Railway or ER (Howrah, Sealdah, Asansol & Malda Divisions) and Workshops (Liluah, Kanchrapara & Jamalpur Workshops) o­n the occasion of observance of Swachhta Pakhwara from September 16 to 30, 2020. Campaigns are also being organized for maintaining cleanliness at the railway premises as well as eradication of single use plastic & discourage use of plastics. Special focus has been given for cleaning of all the drains, toilets, water vending points and railway tracks. Cleaning and sanitization of passenger interfaces with special emphasis to high contact areas are also going o­n at different stations for prevention of the spread of Covid-19.


    Webinars (Web Conferences) have also been organized by different divisions of ER to share the ideas of waste management, controlling non-biodegradable waste etc. All the staff & officers and Bharat Scouts & Guides teams of ER joined hands in the special cleanliness drive and spread the message for maintaining cleanliness for a better environment.
