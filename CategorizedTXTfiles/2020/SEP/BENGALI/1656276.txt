      Department of Posts to Bring Out Special Covers to Commemorate 9 Manifestations of Devi Durga
      Posted On: 18 SEP 2020 5:38PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 18, 2020


    The Department of Posts, West Bengal Circle has decided to bring out nine special covers to commemorate the nine manifestations of Goddess Durga to celebrate the ensuing Durga Puja. One special cover will be released on each day of the Navratri festival and will be dedicated to the particular form of the Goddess specific to the day. It will be starting from October 17, 2020 and will continue till October 25, 2020.


    The said special covers will be available for sale at Kolkata GPO and other philatelic bureaus of West Bengal Circle along the e-Post Office at Sansad Marg, H.O., New Delhi.


    The special covers may be also purchased as a collection of nine. The online portal www.epostoffice.gov.in may be used by the overseas customers for purchasing these unique symbols of Indian culture.
