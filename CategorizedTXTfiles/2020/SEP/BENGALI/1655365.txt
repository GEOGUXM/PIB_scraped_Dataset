      Ministry of Shipping

      Swachhta Pakhwada Activities at Syama Prasad Mookerjee Port, Kolkata
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 8:01PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 16, 2020


    With the aim to create ownership on Swachhta-related activities during the Pakhwada, Syama Prasad Mookerjee Port, Kolkata (SMP, Kolkata) has taken up an ambitious and innovative plan to involve various port users, partners and other stakeholders for a multi-pronged campaign on Swacchta during the Pakhwada, keeping in view COVID-related social distancing norms.


    Today, 2020 Shri Vinit Kumar, Chairman, Syama Prasad Mookerjee Port, Kolkata administered the Swachhta pledge through electronic platform to the senior officers and employees of SMP, Kolkata. Shri A. K. Mehera, Dy. Chairman, SMP, Kolkata was also present.


    The cleanliness campaign is being carried out by SMP, Kolkata both at Kolkata Dock System and Haldia Dock Complex with special focus and emphasis on “Plantation of saplings and Construction of toilet block for general public at Brooklyn” which will be constructed, maintained and operated by Sulabh Sauchalaya. Webinars are also being organised in this connection.
