      ER CONTINUING SPECIAL DRIVE FOR ELIMINATION OF PLASTIC WASTE ON THE OCCASION OF SWACHHTA PAKHWARA-2020

      Posted On: 19 SEP 2020 11:03PM by PIB Kolkata

    Kolkata, September 19, 2020


    Intensive cleanliness drive is continuing at different Divisions (Howrah, Sealdah, Asansol & Malda Divisions) and Workshops (Liluah, Kanchrapara & Jamalpur Workshops) over ER on the occasion of observance of Swachhta Pakhwara from 16 to 30 September,2020. Campaigns are also being organized for maintaining cleanliness at Railway premises as well as eradication of single use Plastic & discourage use of plastics. Special focus has been given for cleaning of all drains, toilets, water vending points and railway tracks. Cleaning and sanitization of Passenger interfaces with special emphasis to high contact areas are also going on at different stations for prevention of spread of Covid-19.


    Webinars (Web Conferences) have also being organized by different divisions of ER to share ideas on waste management, controlling non-biodegradable waste etc. All the staff & officers and Bharat Scouts & Guides teams of ER joined hands in the special cleanliness drive and spreading the message for maintaining cleanliness for a better environment.
