      Ministry of Railways

      COMMITTED FREIGHT SERVICES OF ER FOR UNINTERRUPTED SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 20 SEP 2020 5:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 20, 2020


    Eastern Railway’s freight services are continuing on round the clock basis to ensure supply of essential commodities and other items in different parts of the country ever since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown.


    
    Railway freight services are having certain unique specific benefits. It is appealed to the business community to opt more for Railway mode of freight transportation as these are safe & secured way of transportation of commodities. The freight trains are running with enhanced speed to maintain strictly the punctual services. The freight trains are transporting regularly foodgrains, sugar, edible oil, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, steel, coal and minerals.


    
    Eastern Railway has run a total of 263 freight trains on 19.9.2020 with breakup given as follows:-


    
    Ash – 1 Rake


    Auto - 4 Rakes


    Ballast &Railway Material Consignment – 3 Rakes


    Cement – 26 Rakes


    Clinker – 6 Rake


    Container – 16 Rakes


    Coal and mineral – 85 Rakes


    De-oiled cake – 1 Rake


    Edible Oil – 1 Rake


    Jute – 2 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 8 Rakes


    Maize – 5 Rakes


    Onion – 1 Rake


    POL – 6 Rakes


    Rice – 2 Rakes


    Salt – 5 Rakes


    Steel – 8 Rakes


    Sugar – 2 Rakes


    Stone – 12 Rakes


    Wheat – 6 Rakes


    
    Besides, 63 empty rakes were ran out to different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    
    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff have been given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitization is also being done wherever required.
