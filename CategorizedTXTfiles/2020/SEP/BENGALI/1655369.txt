      Ministry of Railways

      ER TO ATTACH AN ADDITIONAL AC THREE TIER COACH WITH SEALDAH-NEW ALIPURDUAR-SEALDAH SPECIAL
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 11:36PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata September 16, 2020


    The eastern railway (ER) authority has decided to attach one additional AC 3-tier coach temporarily with 02377/02378 Sealdah-New Alipurduar-Sealdah Special, leaving Sealdah from September 18, 2020 to October 29, 2020 & leaving New Alipurduar from September 19, 2020 to October 30, 2020.
