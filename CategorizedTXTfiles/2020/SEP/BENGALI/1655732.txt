      Ministry of Railways

      SER RUNS 4439 TRIPS OF PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS TO ENSURE UNINTERRUPTED SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2020 6:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 17, 2020


    Indian Railways is poised to fight against the Coronavirus pandemic by adopting various measures including the movement of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains for maintaining uninterrupted supply of essential commodities to every nook and corner of the country in the wake of Covid-19. As a part of commitment to the nation, South Eastern Railway (SER) has geared up its entire network to run Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains towards different destinations in the country. SER, during this national crisis, has already run 4439 trips of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains carrying essential commodities like food items, groceries, medicines, medical equipment, fish, fruits, cotton goods, gunny bags, vegetables, onion, ginger, garlic and other daily need items to different parts of the country from April 2 to September 15, 2020. SER during the above period, has carried 89,936 tonnes of parcel traffic containing 33,15,204 number of packages to different places in the country.


    These Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains are running between Shalimar-Ranchi, Shalimar-Mumbai CSMT, Howrah-Secunderabad, Howrah-KSR Bengaluru, Shalimar-Porbander and Tatanagar-Itwari. This apart, the Parcel trains running between KSR Bengaluru-Dimapur and KSR Bengaluru-Guwahati are also passing through SER jurisdiction.


    SER is also transporting parcel goods in 10 pairs of Special Trains which are running in the path of existing Mail/ Express Trains from June 1, 2020.
