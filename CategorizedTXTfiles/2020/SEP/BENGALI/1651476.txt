      Ministry of Railways

      REGULATION OF SER SPECIAL TRAINS ON COMPLETE LOCKDOWN DAYS IN WB
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2020 11:36PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 4, 2020


    In view of complete lockdown on September 7, 11 and 12, 2020 in West Bengal, it has been decided that the special train services will be regulated as under:-


    CANCELLATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02021 Howrah-Barbil Special leaving Howrah on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02022 Barbil-Howrah Special leaving Barbil on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02074 Bhubaneswar-Howrah Special leaving Bhubaneswar on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special leaving Howrah on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02213 Shalimar-Patna Special leaving Shalimar on 07.09.2020 and 11.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02214 Patna-Shalimar Special leaving Patna on 08.09.2020 and 10.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02245 Howrah-Yesvantpur Special leaving Howrah on 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02246 Yesvantpur-Howrah Special leaving Yesvantpur on 06.09.2020, 10.09.2020, 11.09.2020, 13.09.2020 and 14.09.2020 will remain cancelled.


    WITHDRAWAL OF STOPPAGES:


    Ø 02801 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on 06.09.2020, 10.09.2020 and 11.09.2020 will not stop at Hijli and Purulia.


    Ø 02802 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on 06.09.2020, 10.09.2020 and 11.09.2020 will not stop at Purulia and Hijli.


    Ø 02823 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    Ø 02824 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on 06.09.2020, 10.09.2020 and 11.09.2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    SHORT TERMINATION/SHORT ORIGINATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02703 Howrah-Secunderabad Special scheduled to leave Howrah on 07.09.2020, 11.09.2020 and 12.09.2020 will be short originated from Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah. Hence, the service between Howrah and Bhubaneswar will remain cancelled on these dates.


    Ø 02704 Secunderabad-Howrah Special leaving Secunderabad on 06.09.2020, 10.09.2020 and 11.09.2020 will be short terminated at Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah and the service between Bhubaneswar and Howrah will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02834 Howrah-Ahmedabad Special scheduled to leave Howrah on 11.09.2020 will be short originated from Rourkela at 06.35 am on 12.09.2020. There will be no service between Howrah and Rourkela.
