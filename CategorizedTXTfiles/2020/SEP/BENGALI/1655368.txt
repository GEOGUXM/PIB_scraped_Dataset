      Ministry of Railways

      SUMITA SARKAR JOINS AS NEW PRINCIPAL FINANCIAL ADVISER, EASTERN RAILWAY
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 8:34PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 16, 2020


    Ms. Sumita Sarkar, an Indian Railway Accounts Service (IRAS) officer of 1986 Batch, has joined as Principal Financial Adviser, Eastern Railway (ER).


    Prior to this, Ms Sarkar was posted as Joint Secretary & Financial Adviser in the Department of Scientific & Industrial Research and the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research, under the Ministry of Science & Technology, at New Delhi.


    Ms Sarkar has also worked in the Railway Board in the capacity of Executive Director Finance (Stores) and as Financial Adviser & Chief Accounts Officer, Workshop & Stores, South Eastern Railway (SER). The other charges held by her during her career include that of Director in the Ministry of Home Affairs and Financial Adviser & Chief Accounts Officer in the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation.


    Earlier, in ER, she had held the charge of Sr. Divisional Accounts Officer, Howrah and Dy. Financial Adviser & Chief Accounts Officer/Stores & Workshop, amongst several other posts.
