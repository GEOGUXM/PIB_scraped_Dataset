      Posted On: 05 SEP 2020 6:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 5, 2020


    In the month of August, 2020, South Eastern Railway (SER) has loaded 14.58 million tonnes of freight against 13.41 million tonnes loaded in August, 2019, registering a growth of 8.72%. SER has also surpassed the freight loading target of 12.77 million tonnes for the month of August, 2020 by loading 14.17% more than the target.


    The main commodities of freight loading in August, 2020 were 8.60 million tonnes of Iron Ore, 2.59 million tonnes of Coal, 1.56 million tonnes of Pig Iron & Finished Steel etc.


    SER generated a revenue of Rs. 1103.46 crores from freight loading in the month of August, 2020 as against Rs. 1099.02 crores generated from freight sector in August, 2019.


    During the first five months of the current financial year i.e. April-August of 2020-21, SER has loaded altogether 60.30 million tonnes of freight. Revenue generation from freight loading during the above period has been Rs. 4670.34 crores.
