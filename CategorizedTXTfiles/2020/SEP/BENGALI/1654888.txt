      CPFC, EPFO Calls Employers to Avail Subsidy under PMGKY
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 12:03PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 16, 2020


    The Central Provident Fund Commissioner, Employees Provident Fund Organization (EPFO), Ministry of Labour & Employment, Govt of India, in a communication issued on September 11, 2020, has called upon the employers of the establishments registered with the EPFO to avail the subsidy under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY). This Yojana has been made available from March 2020 to August 2020 under the Atma-Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan by filing their Electronic Challan-cum-Return (ECR) as per Para 38 of the EPF Scheme, 1952, on or before September 15, 2020. By this, the benefits of the subsidy can be credited to the Aadhaar-seeded Universal Account Numbers of all eligible members and to update Form No. 5A (Return of Ownership) with bank details.
