      Ministry of Finance

      Press Note
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 8:20PM by PIB Kolkata


    In accordance with Article 151 of the Constitution of India and Section 19A of the Comptroller & Auditor General of India’s (Duties, Powers and Conditions of Services) Act, 1971, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India is required to forward ‘Audit Reports’ on the accounts of the State Government to the Governor who causes them to be laid on the table of the State Legislature.


    Accordingly, the Audit Report of the Comptroller & Auditor General of India on Revenue Sector for the year ended 31 March 2018, Government of West Bengal (Report no. 2 of the year 2020) was forwarded to the State Government on 12 th August 2020 for laying in the State Legislature.
