      Ministry of Railways

      SER CANCELS 02021 / 02022 HOWRAH-BARBIL-HOWRAH SPECIAL TRAIN
      Posted On: 09 SEP 2020 4:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 9, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) authority has informed that due to the poor occupancy, 02021/02022 Howrah-Barbil-Howrah Special leaving Howrah and Barbil on and from September 12, 2020 will remain cancelled from the both ends.
