      GOVERNMENT EXTENDS TENURE OF SHRI L.C. GOYAL AS CMD, ITPO
      Posted On: 02 SEP 2020 6:42PM by PIB Chennai


    The Government has given one year extension upto September 1, 2021 to Shri L.C. Goyal as Chairman and Managing Director, India Trade Promotion Organization(ITPO). He has been serving as the Chairman & Managing Director of India Trade Promotion Organization (ITPO) since September, 2015.


    Shri Goyal is a 1979 batch retired IAS officer of Kerala cadre served as the Union Home Secretary in 2015 apart from holding several key positions at the Centre and in the State of Kerala.
