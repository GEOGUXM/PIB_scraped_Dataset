      Ministry of Education

      “Acharyadevo bhava”- Address by Honourble Union Minister of Education Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ in commemoration of Teacher’s Day on a Live Webinar digital platform hosted by NAAC
      Posted On: 10 SEP 2020 3:36PM by PIB Bengaluru


    Honourable Union Minister of Education Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ will be addressing the Faculty of Higher Educational Institutions across the country on a Live Webinar, ‘Acharyadevo Bhava’ hosted by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bengaluru on 11 th September, 2020 at 06.00 pm.


    The “Acharyadevo Bhava” programme will be inaugurated by the Hon’ble Minister. On this important occasion, Prof D P Singh, Chairman, University Grants Commission, Prof Virander S Chauhan, Chairman, Executive Committee, NAAC will be participating in the programme. Prof S C Sharma, Director, NAAC will coordinate the event.


    Participants can register for this mega academic event from the link provided on the NAAC website www.naac.gov.in/ . The programme will be streamed live via YouTube: https://youtu.be/mNAH6YlUai0 . The program can be accessed live on facebook & twitter NAAC Twitter: @NAAC_India, NAAC Facebook: @NAAC94 and Union Minister Twitter: @DrRPNishank, Union Minister Facebook: @DrRPNishank_. The HEIs may kindly browse through the NAAC website www.naac.gov.in for continued updation and take forward the vision and mission of NAAC.
