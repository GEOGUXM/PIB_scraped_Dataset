      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Covid-19 Effect on Import of Oil
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2020 2:19PM by PIB Delhi


    Oil and gas sector PSUs have informed that COVID 19 pandemic has resulted in unprecedented fall in demand which in-turn has reduced revenue consequent to lockdown imposed in the country.


    Consumption of all petroleum products has increased as the country is going towards unlock process. Month-wise consumption of petroleum products and Import of crude oil are given below:
