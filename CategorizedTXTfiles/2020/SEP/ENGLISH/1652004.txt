      Prime Minister to inaugurate Patrika Gate in Jaipur
      Posted On: 07 SEP 2020 4:42PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi shall inaugurate the Patrika Gate in Jaipur through video conference at 11 AM, on the 8 th September 2020.


    The Iconic Gate is built by the Patrika Group of Newspapers on the Jawaharlal Nehru Marg in Jaipur.


    Prime Minister Shri Modi shall also release two books written by the Group Chairman on the occasion.


    The programme will be telecast live on DD News.
