      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      The 36th National Games allotted to Goa have been postponed due to Covid-19 pandemic
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2020 4:33PM by PIB Delhi


    The 36 th National Games allotted to Goa, which was scheduled from 20.10.2020 to 04.11.2020, had been postponeddue to outbreak of the Covid-19 pandemic.


    Central assistance of Rs.97.80 crore has been released to the Government of Goa for creation / development of sports infrastructure for 36 th National Games.


    The first Khelo India University Games was successfully conducted at Odisha from 22ndFebruary to 1st March, 2020 with total athlete participation of 3182 from 158 universities in 17 sports disciplines.


    Athletes and teams are well aware about the National Games through the State Governments, concerned National Sports Federations, Sports Promotion Boards, etc. Once the dates of National Games are finalised, Indian Olympic Association and the Organizing Committee of National Games use various publicity platforms such as print media, electronic media, social media to promote the National Games among spectators throughout the country.


    This information was given by Union Minister of State (IC) for Youth Affairs and Sports, Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.
