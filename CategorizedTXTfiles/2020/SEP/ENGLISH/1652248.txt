      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of Professor Govind Swarup
      Posted On: 08 SEP 2020 1:49PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Professor Govind Swarup.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, "Professor Govind Swarup was an exceptional scientist. His pioneering works in radio astronomy have attained global commendation. Anguished by his passing away. My thoughts are with his near and dear ones. "
