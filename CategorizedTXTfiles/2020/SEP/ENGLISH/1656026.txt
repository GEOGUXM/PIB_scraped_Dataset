      PM condoles demise of Lok Sabha MP Balli Durga Prasad Rao Garu
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2020 8:35PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Lok Sabha MP, Balli Durga Prasad Rao Garu.


    “Saddened by the demise of Lok Sabha MP, Balli Durga Prasad Rao Garu. He was an experienced leader, who made effective contributions towards the progress of Andhra Pradesh. My thoughts are with his family and well-wishers in this sad hour. Om Shanti.”, the Prime Minister said.
