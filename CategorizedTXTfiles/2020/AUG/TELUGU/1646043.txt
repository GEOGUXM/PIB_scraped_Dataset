      Ministry of Food Processing Industries

      74th INDEPENDENCE DAY Celebration at Food Corporation of India, Telangana
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2020 1:57PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Sri Ashwani Kumar Gupta, General Manager, Food Corporation of India, Telangana hoisted the National Flag on the occasion of 74 th Independence Day of the country at its Regional Office, HACA Bhavan premises in presence of officers and staff. In his Independence Day address Sri Gupta said that all countrymen including the employees of the corporation should value the sacrifices of our forefathers due to which we are breathing in free air. He also highlighted the role of FCI in providing food grains to each and every citizen of the country and successful implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojna even during this tough time of COVID-19 pandemic which has been acknowledged by the Honâ€™ ble Prime Minister of India too. Sri Gupta also appreciated restless efforts made by the officers and staff of Telangana region in procurement and movement of huge quantum of food grains to needy states of the country in adverse conditions. He also emphasized to understand the true value of Independence and maintain the spirit, dedication and temperament towards work as has been showcased by all the employees during such crisis period.
