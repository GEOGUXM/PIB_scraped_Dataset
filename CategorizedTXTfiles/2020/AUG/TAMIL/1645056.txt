      Gold worth Rs.22.21 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2020 4:28PM by PIB Chennai


    On Sunday night, Saminathan Kannan, 41, and Thangavel Sivasankar, 49, both of Thanjavur arrived from Abu Dhabi by Air India Express Flight IX-1642 were intercepted by customs officers at exit. On personal search, one gold paste pouch was found concealed in shirt sleeve of each person. One Kamarudeen Shajahan, 57, of Trichy arrived from Abu Dhabi by Air India Express Flight IX-1638 was intercepted at the exit. On personal search, one gold paste pouch was found concealed in his inner wear. On extraction, 402 grams of gold valued at Rs.22.21 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is under progress , according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport, Chennai.
