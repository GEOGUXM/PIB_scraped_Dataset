      1.48 Kg gold worth Rs. 82.3 Lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport. Two arrested
      Posted On: 05 AUG 2020 12:01PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday morning, one Khaleel Ahamed, 24, who arrived from Dubai by Air India express flight IX-1644 was intercepted at exit. On personal search one packet gold paste was recovered which was kept concealed inside his underwear. On extraction 280 grams of gold valued at Rs.15.60 lakhs was recovered and seized.


    Earlier on Monday night Air Intelligence officers apprehended two persons outside airport who panicked after seeing the officers and started running away. On their personal search, five packets of gold in paste form were recovered. They were identified as Kaja Mohideen,39, and S Peer Mohideen, 33, both from Chennai and informed that the packets were handed over to them by five passengers who had arrived from Sharjah by Air India flight AI-1984.The passengers also confirmed they had handed over one packet each to them. On extraction gold weighing 1.2 Kg valued at Rs. 66.73 lakhs was recovered and seized. Both receivers were arrested.


    Total 1.48 Kg gold valued at Rs. 82.3 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is under process, according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
