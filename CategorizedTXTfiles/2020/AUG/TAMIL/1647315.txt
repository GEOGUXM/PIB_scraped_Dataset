      State Finance Audit Report of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2020 5:14PM by PIB Chennai


    In accordance with Article 151 (2) of the Constitution of India, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India is required to forward his Audit Report on the accounts of the Government of Tamil Nadu to the Hon’ble Governor, who causes it to be laid on the table of the State Legislature.


    Accordingly, the State Finance Audit Report of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India, Government of Tamil Nadu for the year ended 31 st March 2019, was handed to the Hon’ble Governor of Tamil Nadu on 18 th August 2020 for laying before the State Legislative Assembly. This is stated in the press release issued by the Principal Accountant General.
