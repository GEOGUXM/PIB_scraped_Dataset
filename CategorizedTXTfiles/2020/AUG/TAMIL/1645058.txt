      Special Service and Features

      NATIONAL CAREER SERVICE CENTRE FOR SC/ST, CHENNAI
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2020 4:31PM by PIB Chennai


    The National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, 3 rd floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board building, 56. Santhome High Road, Chennai – 600 004 (Tel 044-2461 5112) under M/o Labour & Employment, DGE, Govt. of India, inviting proposal from reputed Firms/Institutions of Chennai, Tamil Nadu for imparting the DGE sponsored scheme of 27 th Phase Special Coaching Scheme under Shorthand to 60 Nos. of SC/ST candidates. Interested institutes/Firms need to meet the following criteria such as well- trained faculties, training infrastructure, location etc.


    The Selected institute will impart training in (1) General Awareness, (2) General English (3), Numerical Ability (4) Shorthand and Typing and (5) Basic Knowledge in Computer in order to make the eligible SC/ST educated job seekers of Tamil Nadu viable for facing the different Competitive Examinations. The selected Institutes/Firms will be paid professional charges of Rs.800/- per candidate per month.


    The interested institutions /Firms may apply to the Sub-Regional Employment Officer, NATIONAL CAREER SERVICE CENTRE FOR SC/ST, CHENNAI, No.56 , 3 rd floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board building, 56. Santhome High Road, Chennai – 600 004 (Tel Ph- 80720 96783), on or before 20 th August 2020, according to a press release issued by Shri S K Sahoo, Sub Regional Employment Officer, NCSC for SC/ST Chennai.
