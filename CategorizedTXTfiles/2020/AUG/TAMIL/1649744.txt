      KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA CLEBRATES NATIONAL SPORTS DAY
      Posted On: 30 AUG 2020 2:58PM by PIB Chennai


    The National Sports Day was held on Saturday 29th January 2020 to the birth anniversary of hockey legend Major Dhyan Chand who is regarded as nation's greatest hockey player of all time.


    Kendriya Vidyalaya IIT Chennai celebrated the National Sports Day with great zeal, excitement amidst all the challenges prevailing around.


    Dr. M. Manickasamy Principal of the Vidyalaya in his speech emphasised on the importance of sports in a child's life and how they can be kept engaged with various sports activities mentally and physically fit. One need not go out for a heavy work out instead simple exercises can be performed at home and stay fit and safe.


    The chief guest, Mr. KrishnamachariSrikkanth a former captain of the Indian cricket team and former chairman of the selection committee gave an inspirational speech. In his speech he expressed that sports education trains the students to face defeat with a smile and maintain the humility even in victory. It is the healthiest means of refreshments and recreation. Sport can teach values such as fairness, teambuilding, equality, discipline, inclusion, perseverance and respect.


    A Short film on the life of hockey legend Major Dhyan Chand was screened and a glimpse of all the posters prepared by the students of our Vidyalaya was shown.
