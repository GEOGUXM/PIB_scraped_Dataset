      PM greets nation on the occasion of Eid al-Adha
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2020 9:11AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the occasion of Eid al-Adha.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said,


    "Eid Mubarak!


    Greetings on Eid al-Adha. May this day inspire us to create a just, harmonious and inclusive society. May the spirit of brotherhood and compassion be furthered."
