      Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change

      Clarification over India's Carbon Emissions
      Posted On: 28 AUG 2020 11:27AM by PIB Delhi


    Certain media reports have misquoted a Ministry official about falling of India’s carbon emissions for 2020. It is clarified that the statement was in the context of predicted fall in global Carbon Dioxide emission, not country’s emission, which was reported a couple of months ago based on the Global Energy Review 2020 report of International Energy Agency.
