      Nominations for Sardar Patel National Unity Award extended till 31st October 2020
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2020 5:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The online nomination process for Sardar Patel National Unity Award, the highest civilian award in the field of contribution to the unity and integrity of India, has now been extended till 31.10 .2020. The nominations are being received online in the Ministry of Home Affairs website https://nationalunityawards.mha.gov.in .


    Government of India has instituted the award in the name of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. The Award seeks to recognize notable and inspiring contributions to promote the cause of national unity and integrity and to reinforce the value of a strong and united India.
