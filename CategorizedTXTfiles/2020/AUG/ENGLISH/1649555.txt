      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates National Sports and Adventure Awards, 2020 winners
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2020 6:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, congratulated National Sports and Adventure Awards, 2020 winners today.


    The Prime Minister said, “Congratulations to all the talented sportspersons who have been conferred the National Sports and Adventure Awards 2020! There is much to learn from these athletes. Their successes inspire many budding sportspersons. Best wishes to the award winners for their future endeavours.”
