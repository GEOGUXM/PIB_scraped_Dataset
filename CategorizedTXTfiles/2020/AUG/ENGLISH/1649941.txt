      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates chess players for winning the FIDE Online Chess Olympiad
      Posted On: 30 AUG 2020 9:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi congratulated chess players for winning the FIDE Online Chess Olympiad.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, “Congratulations to our chess players for winning the FIDE Online Chess Olympiad. Their hard work and dedication are admirable. Their success will surely motivate other chess players. I would like to congratulate the Russian team as well.”


    Congratulations to our chess players for winning the FIDE Online #ChessOlympiad. Their hard work and dedication are admirable. Their success will surely motivate other chess players. I would like to congratulate the Russian team as well.

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) August 30, 2020
