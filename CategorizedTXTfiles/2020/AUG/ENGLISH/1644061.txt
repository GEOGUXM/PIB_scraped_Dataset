      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah says “Modi government is committed for the holistic development ofour weaver’s community”
      
      “For the first time since 2014, true skills of our hardworking weavers are being nurtured and they are being given their due credit”
      
      “Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi in 2015 declared 7th August as National Handlooms Day to bring weavers into the mainstream of India's development”
      
      “PM Modi’s mantra of ‘Vocal for Local' will surely boost the morale of handloom sector”
      
      “Let us all pledge to support “Vocal for Handmade” to help realise Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi’s dream of a self-reliant India”
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2020 2:29PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah has said that“Modi government is committed for the holistic development of our weaver’s community”.In his tweets Shri Amit Shah said that“For the first time since 2014, true skills of our hardworking weavers are being nurtured and they are being given their due credit. To further promote them, Prime MinisterShri Narendra Modi in 2015 declared 7th August as National Handlooms Day to bring them into the mainstream of India's development”.


    Union Home Ministeralso emphasized that “PM Modi’s mantra of ‘Vocal for Local' will surely boost the morale of handloom sector.Let us all pledge to support‘Vocal for Handmade’to help realise Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi’s dream of a self-reliant India”.
