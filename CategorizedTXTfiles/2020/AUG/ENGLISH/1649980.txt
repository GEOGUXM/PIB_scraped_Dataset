      All India Weather Warning Bulletin (MID-DAY)
      
      Heavy to very heavy rainfall very likely at isolated places over West Rajasthan, Sub-Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim, Assam & Meghalaya and Saurashtra & Kutch
