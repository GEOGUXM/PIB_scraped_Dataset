      Ministry of Finance

      Borrowing options to meet the GST Compensation requirement for 2020-21
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2020 3:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The two borrowing options to meet the GST Compensation requirement for 2020-21 consequent to the discussions in the 41 st meeting of the GST Council held on 27 th August, 2020 has been communicated to States, as per the document attached with this press note, to communicate their preference within seven working days. A meeting of State Finance Secretaries with the Union Finance Secretary and Secretary (Expenditure) is scheduled to be held on 1 st September, 2020 for clarifying issues, if any.
