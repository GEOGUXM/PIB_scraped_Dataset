      Ministry of Finance

      Tariff Notification No. 83/2020-Customs (N.T.) in respect of Fixation of Tariff Value of Edible Oils, Brass Scrap, Poppy Seeds, Areca Nut, Gold and Silver

O. 748 (E), dated the 3 rd August, 2001, namely:-
