      PM remembers Arun Jaitley on his first Punya Tithi
      Posted On: 24 AUG 2020 11:59AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has remembered Arun Jaitley on his first Punya Tithi.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, "On this day, last year, we lost Shri Arun Jaitley Ji. I miss my friend a lot.


    Arun Ji diligently served India. His wit, intellect, legal acumen and warm personality were legendary."
