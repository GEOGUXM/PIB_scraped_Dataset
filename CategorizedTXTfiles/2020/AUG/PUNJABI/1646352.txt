      The Government of India has sanctioned one lakh crore rupees for agriculture infrastructure during Corona Pandemic: PM Shri Narendra Modi
      Posted On: 16 AUG 2020 6:26PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, 16 August 2020


    The Government of India has sanctioned one lakh crore rupees for agriculture infrastructure during Corona Pandemic, said Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi in his address to the nation on 74 th Independence Day. He further said, the priority of a self-reliant India is a self-Reliant farming sector and self-reliant farmers.India's farmers not only supply foodgrains for the citizens of India but also for other countries in need. We have to free them of shackles and we have done that. Farmers could not sell their produce in any part of the country or the world according to their wishes on their terms but now these shackles will not be there.
