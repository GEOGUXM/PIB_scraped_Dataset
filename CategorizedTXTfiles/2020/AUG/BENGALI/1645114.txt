      Ministry of Railways

      SER REGULATES SPECIAL TRAINS ON COMPLETE LOCKDOWN DAYS IN WEST BENGAL
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2020 6:52PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 11 August, 2020:


    In view of complete lockdown on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 in West Bengal, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to regulate the special train services as under:-


    CANCELLATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02021 Howrah-Barbil Special leaving Howrah on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02022 Barbil-Howrah Special leaving Barbil on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02074 Bhubaneswar-Howrah Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special leaving Howrah on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02214 Patna-Shalimar Special leaving Patna on August 20 and 27, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02213 Shalimar-Patna Special leaving Shalimar on August 21 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02246 Yesvantpur-Howrah Special leaving Yesvantpur on August 20 and 27, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02245 Howrah-Yesvantpur Special leaving Howrah on August 21 and 28, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    SHORT TERMINATION/SHORT ORIGINATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02703 Howrah-Secunderabad Special scheduled to leave Howrah on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will be short originated from Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah. Hence, the service between Howrah and Bhubaneswar will remain cancelled on these dates.


    Ø 02704 Secunderabad-Howrah Special leaving Secunderabad on August 19, 20, 26 and 27, 2020 will be short terminated at Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah and the service between Bhubaneswar and Howrah will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02834 Howrah-Ahmedabad Special scheduled to leave Howrah on August 21 and 28, 2020 will be short originated from Rourkela at 06.35 am on August 22 and 29, 2020 instead of Howrah. Hence, the service between Howrah and Rourkela will remain cancelled.


    WITHDRAWAL OF STOPPAGES:


    Ø 02801 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 19, 20, 26 and 27, 2020 will not stop at Hijli and Purulia.


    Ø 02802 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on August 19, 20, 26 and 27, 2020 will not stop at Purulia and Hijli.


    Ø 02823 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 20, 21, 27 and 28, 2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    Ø 02824 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on August 19, 20, 26 and 27, 2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    In addition to above, train services of August 31, 2020 will also be regulated as per previous notification.
