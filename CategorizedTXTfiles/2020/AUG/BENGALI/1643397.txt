      Ministry of Railways

      ER CANCELS FEW SPECIAL TRAINS DUE TO LOCKDOWN IN WB ON AUGUST 8
      Posted On: 04 AUG 2020 9:02PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 04 August, 2020


    Eastern railway (ER) authority has informed that in view of the lockdown declared in entire West Bengal on August 8, 2020 by the State Government, the following special trains will remain cancelled:


    1) 02024 Patna–Howrah Special Train journey commencing on August 8, 2020.
    2) 02023 Howrah–Patna Special Train journey commencing on August 8, 2020.
    3) 02302 New Delhi–Howrah AC Special Train journey commencing on August 7, 2020.
    4) 02301 Howrah–New Delhi AC Special Train journey commencing on August 8, 2020.
    5) 02377 Sealdah-New Alipurduar Special Train journey commencing on August 7, 2020 & August 8, 2020
    6) 02378 New Alipurduar-Sealdah Special Train journey commencing on August 7, 2020 & August 8, 2020.


    7) 02308 Jodhpur–Howrah Special train journey commencing on August 6, 2020.
    8) 02307 Howrah–Jodhpur Special train journey commencing on August 8, 2020.
    9) 02303 Howrah-New Delhi Special train journey commencing August 8, 2020.
    10) 02382 New Delhi–Howrah Special train journey commencing on August 7, 2020.
    11) 02357 Kolkata-Amritsar Special train journey commencing on August 8, 2020.
    12) 02358 Amritsar-Kolkata Special journey commencing on August 10, 2020.

    ER has requested the passengers to plan their journey accordingly.
