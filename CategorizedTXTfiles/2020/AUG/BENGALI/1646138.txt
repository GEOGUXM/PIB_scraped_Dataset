      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      PIB Kolkata Celebrates the 74th Independence Day
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2020 6:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 15, 2020


    The Press Information Bureau, Kolkata today celebrated the 74 th Independence Day with dignity and honour. The Director General, Eastern Zone, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting (MIB) Shri R N Mishra unfurled the tricolour and paid homage to all the sung and unsung heroes of our freedom movement. He also saluted all the Covid warriors of the country and appreciated the activities of the staff of MIB of the Eastern Region who are relentlessly working, despite the hindrances created by COVID-19.


    The Additional Director General, Eastern Zone, MIB along with the other officials of the Press Information Bureau, Kolkata was present on the occasion.
