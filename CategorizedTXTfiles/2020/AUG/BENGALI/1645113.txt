      Ministry of Culture

      BITM to Organise Online Quiz Competition, Patriotic Essay Competition as Gestures to Independence Day Celebration
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2020 6:51PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 11 August, 2020


    Birla Industrial & Technological Museum (BITM) under the Union Ministry of Culture is gearing itself up to celebrate Independence Day in a befitting way. In the wake of the rapid spread of Coronavirus, the museum is mostly following the online ways to celebrate most of the special events.


    This time, BITM is going online to celebrate the Independence Day. Besides flag hoisting and sapling plantation in the morning at 10 am on August 15, 2020, there will be online events for the students like, Quiz Competition where students from Class VIII to XII will be able to participate. The Topic is ‘History of Indian Independence’. For participation the interested students will have to visit www.bitm.gov.in from 11 am to 11.30 am.


    This apart, there will be a ‘Patriotic Essay Competition’ within 500 words on the topic: ‘Role of Patriotism in your Life’. The time for this is, in between 10 am and 3 pm, the same day. The interested students from Class VIII to Class XII may make online submission of their write-ups in pdf format at the bitmeducationsection@gmail.com


    Besides, there is an arrangement of Patriotic Poetry Competition where students may write poems within 14 lines and submit in the pdf format at bitmeducationsection@gmail.com from 10 am to 3 pm. The students from Class VI to VIII are eligible to participate in this competition.
