      Ministry of Science & Technology

      Celebration of the 74th Independence Day at Science City, Kolkata
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2020 4:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 15, 2020


    Science City, Kolkata celebrated the 74th Independence Day with hosting of the tricolor and planting of saplings by Director, Shri Subhabrata Chaudhuri and other officials. People from different walks of life attended the programme with due adherence to Covid protocols, like wearing of masks, keeping social distance during all activities, washing and sanitization after each event.


    As a part of this celebration, Science City also organised online contests for students from Class V to XII, like painting competition on the topic “Pride of the Nation: Defence Forces of India”, Essay writing competition on “Swachha Bharat, Sundar Bharat” for junior groups, and “Daughters of India, Who Served Mother India” for senior groups. The underlying spirit of celebration of the Independence Day in Science City this year is to make all members of the society feel ‘Ek Bharat, Shreshtha Bharat’ (एक भारत, श्रेष्ठ भारत) in the midst of pall of gloom cast during this current pandemic.
