      Ministry of Home Affairs

      BSF SEIZES BIRI IN BORDERING AREA, KARIMGANJ
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2020 7:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 29, 2020


    On Friday (August 28, 2020), acting on specific Intelligence, troops of 07 Battalion, Border Security Force, Mizoram and Cachar Frontier seized 210 packets of Biri (S K Nasiruddin Brand) along with one Bajaj auto rickshaw at the bordering area of Jagannathi, Karimganj, Assam.


    The total value of the seizure is Rs.2, 44,500. Further, the seized items and articles were handed over to the Custom Office at Karimganj.
