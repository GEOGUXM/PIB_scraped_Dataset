      Ministry of Railways

      KHARAGPUR DIVISION PERFORMS COMMENDABLE LOADING IN PARCEL CARGO EXPRESS TRAINS
      Posted On: 02 AUG 2020 5:23PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 02 August, 2020


    Indian Railways, aptly called the 'Lifeline of the Nation' is poised to fight against the Coronavirus pandemic and reach people living in nook and corner of the country with the supply of essential commodities by adopting various measures including movement of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains.


    As a part of commitment to the nation, Kharagpur Division of South Eastern Railway (SER) has geared up its entire network to run Parcel Cargo Express Trains towards different destinations of the country. During the month of July, 2020, Kharagpur Division loaded a record number of 113 rakes containing different essential commodities. In that period, Kharagpur Division loaded 7744 MT in Parcel Cargo Express trains as against 5158 MT loaded in the month of June, 2020, thus achieving a growth of 50%. Revenue earned by Kharagpur Division by carrying parcel goods has been Rs. 2.86 crores as against Rs. 1.68 crores earned in the month of June, 2020 thereby registering a growth of 70%.


    The essential commodities carried by the parcel cargo express trains included food items, groceries, medicines, medical equipment, PPE Kits, fish, fruits, cotton goods, mosquito net, vegetables, flowers, cloth, tarpaulin etc.
