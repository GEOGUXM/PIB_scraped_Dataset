      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN 4 PAIRS OF SPECIAL TRAINS AS PER REVISED DEPARTURE/ARRIVAL TIMINGS
      Posted On: 27 AUG 2020 4:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 27, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER), in a bid to make high-speed movement of the train services for reaching the passengers to their destinations quickly, has decided to accelerate the sectional permissible maximum speed limit from 110 kmph to 130 kmph between Andul and Kharagpur on Howrah-Kharagpur section in Kharagpur Division.


    With the above acceleration of sectional maximum permissible speed, four pairs of trains viz., 02245/02246 Howrah-Yesvantpur-Howrah Special, 02021/02022 Howrah-Barbil-Howrah Special, 02810/02809 Howrah-Mumbai CSMT - Howrah Special and 02073/02074 Howrah-Bhubaneswar- Howrah Special trains will run at a maximum permissible speed of 130 kmph instead of existing speed of 110 kmph on the above section between Andul and Kharagpur with the revised departure/arrival timings to and fro Howrah from August 28, 2020.


    Ø 02245 Howrah-Yesvantpur Special will leave Howrah at 10.55 am instead of 10.50 am. In the return direction, 02246 Yesvantpur-Howrah Special will arrive at Howrah at 4:40 pm instead of 4.45 pm.


    Ø 02021 Howrah-Barbil Special will depart Howrah at 06.25 am instead of 06.20 am. In the return trip, 02022Barbil-Howrah Special will reach Howrah at 8.50 pm instead of 8.55 pm.


    Ø 02810 Howrah-Mumbai CSMT Special will leave Howrah at 8.05 pm instead of 8.00 pm and the corresponding down train 02809 Mumbai CSMT-Howrah Special will arrive Howrah at 05.45 am instead of 05.50 am.


    Ø 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special will depart Howrah at 2.30 pm instead of 2.25 pm and 02074 Bhubaneswar-Howrah Special will arrive Howrah at 12.35 pm instead of 12.40 pm.


    However, the Arrival/Departure timings of the aforesaid Special trains at other stations en route will remain unchanged.
