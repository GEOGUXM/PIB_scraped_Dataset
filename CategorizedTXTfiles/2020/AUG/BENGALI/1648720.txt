      Ministry of Finance

      Webinar on Faceless Assessment and Taxpayer’s Charter organized in Kolkata
      Posted On: 26 AUG 2020 4:30PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 26, 2020


    Faceless Assessment Scheme was rolled out in the Income Tax Department as a pilot project in September, 2019. Initially, limited number of cases were picked up for faceless assessment which was being done at 8(eight) centres in the country. Hon’ble Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi had announced the extension of the scheme to the entire department on 13 th August 2020. The scheme is also being extended to the first appellate authority i.e. Commissioner of Income Tax(Appeals) from 25 th September, 2020


    A webinar was conducted on 26-08-2020 by the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal & Sikkim Region addressing all stakeholder’s about the faceless assessment scheme. Representatives of the Chambers of Commerce, Trade and Industry, Tax Practitioners as well as officers of the department attended the Webinar organized here today. The Webinar was organized to promote the awareness amongst the stakeholder’s about the new faceless assessment scheme. The Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal & Sikkim, Shri Bishwanath Jha along with his colleague Shri K. K. Singh, Chief Commissioner of Income Tax (ReAC) answered the questions raised by the participants in the Webinar. The department intends to hold more such outreach programmes in coming days to educate the tax payers about the new faceless assessment scheme.
