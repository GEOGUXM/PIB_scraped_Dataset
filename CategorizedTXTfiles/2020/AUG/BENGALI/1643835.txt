      Ministry of Railways

      SER CROSSES FREIGHT LOADING TARGET IN JULY
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2020 6:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 August, 2020


    In the month of July, 2020, South Eastern Railway (SER) loaded 14.38 million tonnes of freight against the target of 13.76 million tones, thereby registering an increase of 4.51%. The main commodities of freight loading in July, 2020 were 8.96 million tonnes of iron ore, 2.00 million tonnes of coal, 1.51 million tones of pig Iron & finished Steel, 1.03 million tonnes of cement etc. As a result, SER generated revenue of Rs. 1092.01 crore from the freight loading in July, 2020.


    During the first four months of the current fiscal i.e., April-July of 2020-21, SER loaded altogether 45.72 million tonnes of freight. The revenue generation from freight loading during the above period was Rs. 3566.88 crore.
