      SER RESTORES SPECIAL TRAINS DUE TO CHANGE OF LOCKDOWN DAYS IN WB
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2020 5:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 August, 2020


    The Government of West Bengal had previously announced complete lockdown in the State on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020, according to which the Special Train services of South Eastern Railway (SER) were regulated. But now as per the revised notification, the complete lockdown in West Bengal has been withdrawn on August 16, 17, 23 and 24, 2020.


    Consequently, the Special train services cancelled earlier will be restored to run by SER on August 16, 17, 23 and 24, 2020, stated the SER authority in a press release.
