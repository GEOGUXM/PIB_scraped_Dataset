      Ministry of Finance

      PRESS NOTE
      Posted On: 25 AUG 2020 8:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    The Principal Accountant General (Audit-I), West Bengal, Shri Sarat Chaturvedi has informed that the State Finances Audit Report of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India containing analysis of the Finances of the State and audit observations on Financial Management & Budgetary Control of the State Government and the Financial Reporting for the year ended March 31, 2019 has been forwarded to the State Government today (August 25, 2020) for laying in the State Legislature.


    In accordance with the Article 151 of the Constitution and Section 19A of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India’s (Duties, Powers and Conditions of Services) Act, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India is required to forward ‘Audit Report’ on the accounts of the State Government to the Governor who helps them to be laid on the table of the State Legislature. The Comptroller and Auditor General of India also compiles the Finance Accounts of the State Government together with the Appropriation Accounts. These are examined by the concerned Principal Accountant General (Audit) under the overall direction of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India in accordance with the requirements of the C&AG’s (SPC) Act 1971.
