      Ministry of Railways

      SER REGULATES SPECIAL TRAINS ON COMPLETE LOCKDOWN DAYS IN WEST BENGAL
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2020 5:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 01 August, 2020


    In the view of the complete lockdown on August 5, 8, 16 , 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 in West Bengal, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to regulate the special train services as under:-


    CANCELLATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02245 Howrah-Yesvantpur Special leaving Howrah on August 5, 8, 16 and 23, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02246 Yesvantpur-Howrah Special leaving Yesvantpur on August 7, 10, 16, 18, 23, 25 and 30, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02021 Howrah-Barbil Special leaving Howrah on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02022 Barbil-Howrah Special leaving Barbil on 05.08.2020, 08.08.2020, 16.08.2020, 17.08.2020, 23.08.2020, 24.08.2020 and 31.08.2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02214 Patna-Shalimar Special leaving Patna on August 4, 15, 22 and 29, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02213 Shalimar-Patna Special leaving Shalimar on August 5, 17, 24 and 31, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02074 Bhubaneswar-Howrah Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    Ø 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special leaving Howrah on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 will remain cancelled.


    WITHDRAWAL OF STOPPAGES:


    Ø 02801 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 4, 7, 15, 16, 22, 23 and 30, 2020 will not stop at Hijli and Purulia.


    Ø 02802 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on August 4, 7, 15, 16, 22, 23 and 30, 2020 will not stop at Purulia and Hijli.


    Ø 02823 Bhubaneswar-New Delhi Special leaving Bhubaneswar on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    Ø 02824 New Delhi-Bhubaneswar Special leaving New Delhi on August 4, 7, 15, 16, 22, 23 and 30, 2020 will not stop at Hijli.


    SHORT TERMINATION/SHORT ORIGINATION OF TRAINS:


    Ø 02809 Mumbai CSMT-Howrah Special scheduled to leave Mumbai CSMT on August 14 and 21, 2020 will be short terminated at Rourkela instead of Howrah.


    Ø 02810 Howrah-Mumbai CSMT Special scheduled to leave Howrah on August 5, 2020 will be short originated from Rourkela at 02.11 pm on August 6, 2020 instead of Howrah. Hence, the service of this train from Howrah to Rourkela will remain cancelled on August 5, 2020.


    Ø 02703 Howrah-Secunderabad Special scheduled to leave Howrah on August 5, 8, 16, 17, 23, 24 and 31, 2020 will be short originated from Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah. Hence, the service between Howrah and Bhubaneswar will remain cancelled on these dates.


    Ø 02704 Secunderabad-Howrah Special leaving Secunderabad on August 4, 7, 15, 16, 22, 23 and 30, 2020 will be short terminated at Bhubaneswar instead of Howrah and the service between Bhubaneswar and Howrah will remain cancelled.
