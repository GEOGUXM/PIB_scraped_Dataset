      West Bengal Circle of DOP celebrates Independence Day
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2020 10:27PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15th August, 2020


    

    Department of Post, West Bengal circle today celebrated the Independence Day with flag hoisting ceremony at its headquarters-General Post Office in Kolkata.


    

    Department salutes the brave hearts of Bengal, specially the health workers, police personnel, civil administration and postal staff, for their tireless fight against Covid 19.


    

    Shri Mervin Alexander, Chief Postmaster General, West Bengal Circle elaborated on the work carried out by the postal staffs. He said that in this war against the Covid-19, Postal department has stepped forward for the nation. They deliver the postal items and maintain small scale savings scheme activities during this pandemic situation. Sri Alexander has released a special cover on CORONA FIGHTERS BRAVE HEARTS OF BENGAL at Kolkata General Post Office today on the occasion of Independence Day in presence of other senior officers of Postal Department.
