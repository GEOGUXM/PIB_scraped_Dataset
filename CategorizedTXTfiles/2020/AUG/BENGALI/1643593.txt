      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S TIRELESS EFFORTS FOR LOADING & UNLOADING OF FREIGHT TRAINS
      Posted On: 05 AUG 2020 7:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 05 August, 2020


    To maintain backend supply, Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains on round the clock basis to transport essential commodities in different parts of the country, since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown. The freight trains are carrying food-grains, fertilizer, coal & mineral, onion, salt, iron & steel, POL, container, clinker, cement, ballast, ash, sand, stone, maize to cater to the demand of consumables and other inputs.


    ER has run a total of 264 freight trains on August 4, 2020. The breakup is given as follows:-


    Ash – 2 Rakes


    Automobile – 1 Rake


    Ballast & Railway Material Consignment – 3 Rakes


    Cement – 21 Rakes


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container – 16 Rakes


    Coal and mineral – 77 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 7 Rakes


    Maize – 5 Rakes


    Petroleum Oil – 5 Rakes


    Foodgrains & Rice – 5 Rakes


    Onion – 1 Rake


    Salt – 3 Rakes


    Sand – 2 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Stone – 2 Rakes


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    Tar – 2 Rakes


    Wheat – 7 Rakes


    Besides, 98 empty rakes were run out to the different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
