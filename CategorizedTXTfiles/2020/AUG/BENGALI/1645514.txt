      SER UNLOADS 28 RAKES CONTAINING AROUND 59 THOUSAND TONNES OF FOOD-GRAINS AND SALT AT ADRA DIVISION IN JULY 2020
      Posted On: 13 AUG 2020 4:51PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 13 August, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has taken all possible measures in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic to ensure uninterrupted supply of food-grains and other essential commodities. As per the guidelines given by the Ministry of Railways, SER has geared up its entire network for uninterrupted movement of the goods trains.


    During the month of July, 58,833 , thousand tonnes of food-grains and salt were unloaded from 28 rakes which arrived at different stations of Adra Division of SER. Altogether 24,977 tonnes of rice was unloaded from 14 rakes and 26,952 tonnes of wheat from 10 rakes. Moreover, 6,904 tonnes of salt was unloaded from four rakes.


    The rakes were unloaded at different locations of Adra Division of SER like Purulia, Bankura, Bokaro Steel City, Adra, Chandil during the period from July 1 to July 31, 2020.
