      112 RAKES CONTAINING MORE THAN 2.68 LAKH TONNES OF FOOD GRAINS AND SALT REACH KHARAGPUR DIVISION OF SER IN THE WAKE OF COVID-19
      Posted On: 05 AUG 2020 7:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 05 August, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has continued its endeavour to ensure transportation of food grains and other essential commodities through its freight services during the nationwide lockdowns in the wake of COVID-19 pandemic. As per the guidelines given by the Ministry of Railways, SER has geared up its entire network for unabated movement of goods trains. During the period from March 20 to July 31, 2020, more than 2,68,128 lakh tonnes of food grains and salt were unloaded from 112 food rakes which arrived at different stations and sidings of Kharagpur Division of SER. Altogether 1,46,034 tonnes of wheat from 61 rakes and 1,02,942 tonnes of rice from 43 rakes were unloaded. Moreover, 19,152 tonnes of salt was unloaded from eight rakes.


    The rakes were unloaded at different locations of Kharagpur Division of SER like Midnapur, Kalaikunda, Kharagpur, Rupsa, Sankrail Goods Terminal Yard etc. during the period mentioned above. The concerned staff of the SER have been working at various stations and goods sheds for the safe unloading and loading of food grains and essential commodities. Security persons have also been deployed for the security of food grains and other commodities.
