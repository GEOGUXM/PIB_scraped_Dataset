      ROURKELA-BHUBANESWAR-ROURKELA SPECIAL TRAIN TO CONTINUE TILL THE END OF SEPTEMBER
      Posted On: 31 AUG 2020 6:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 31, 2020


    The South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to continue the running of Rourkela-Bhubaneswar-Rourkela Special Train (02861/02862) five days a week except Saturday and Sunday till September 30, 2020.


    02861 Rourkela-Bhubaneswar Special will leave Rourkela at 05.10 am and will reach Bhubaneswar at 12:30 pm. In the return direction, 02862 Bhubaneswar-Rourkela Special will leave Bhubaneswar at 2.10 pm and will reach Rourkela at 9.40 pm at night.
