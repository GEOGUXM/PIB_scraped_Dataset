      Ministry of Railways

      SOUTH EASTERN RAILWAY OBSERVES “SADBHAVNA DIWAS”
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2020 4:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 20, 2020


    “Sadbhavna Diwas” was observed at South Eastern Railway (SER) Headquarters, Garden Reach today morning through Virtual Media.


    Shri Sanjay Kumar Mohanty, General Manager, SER administered “Sadbhavna Diwas” pledge to all officers and staff of SER Headquarters to work for the emotional oneness and harmony of all the people of India regardless of caste, region, religion or language and to resolve all differences through dialogue and constitutional means without resorting to any violence.


    Smt. Zarina Firdausi, Principal Chief Personnel Officer and other Principal Head of the Departments along with a large number of staff and officers of SER took part in the oath taking ceremony of “Sadbhavna Diwas” through virtual media.
