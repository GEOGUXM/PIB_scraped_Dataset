      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      SC/ST candidates to get free stipendiary coaching at Nagpur
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2020 12:54PM by PIB Mumbai


    Office of the National Career Service Centre through its Coaching cum guidance Centre, Nagpur working under Directorate General of Employment & Training (DGET), Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India has appealed to educated unemployed candidates from Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribes (SC/ST) community to avail the free-stipendiary coaching in typewriting, competitive Exam preparations & computer operations.


    The free coaching includes special coaching in type-writing, Short-hand (English), General Awareness, General English & Computer operation. The duration of this training will be 11 Months. It is likely to commence from 1 September 2020. Education Qualification is minimum HSC passed while age limit is between 18 to 27 years. The amount of stipend is Rs. 1,000/- per month. Besides above training, there will be courses of 1 year duration related to software and hardware of computer. These coursers will require educational eligibility on the same line as above. The age limit for courses related to software and hardware of computer is between 18 to 30 years.


    To get more details and for availing the benefits of these schemes, interested candidates can fill the form online on link https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd6gD7qe88Oio59N-d283XZ3b7ORp0fGfLcxdiit7o-9Cj4lw/viewform or contact Whatsapp number 9730645446 till 18 August 2020, informed P.S.Pachpor, Sub-regional Employment Officer, National Career Service Centre
