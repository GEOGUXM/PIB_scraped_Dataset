      13 Highway Projects, a Road Safety Project inaugurated by Union Minister for Transport
      Posted On: 17 AUG 2020 3:43PM by PIB Imphal


    13 highway projects and a Road safety project of Manipur were inaugurated today through video conference by Union Minister for Road Transport, Highways and MSMEs Shri Nitin Gadkari in presence of Manipur Chief Minister Shri N Biren Singh. The function was also attended by MoS (I/c) for DONER and PMO Dr Jitendra Singh, MoS RTH Gen (Retd) V K Singh, Ministers of the Manipur State, several MPs, MLAs and senior officers from the Centre and the State.


    The projects come as a major boost to the infrastructure of this border state and will enhance the quality of 316 kilometers of highways in the state. The projects seek to enhance connectivity, convenience and economic growth of the region. Shri Gadkari assured the people of the state that no efforts will be spared to ensure good infrastructure in the region.


    Speaking on the occasion, Chief Minister Shri Biren Singh said that under the leadership of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, the region is witnessing fast economic development for the last few years.
