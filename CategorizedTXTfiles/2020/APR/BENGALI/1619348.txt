      Ministry of Railways

      RPF OF ER DISTRIBUTES COOKED FOOD AMONG THOUSANDS OF NEEDY PEOPLE
      Posted On: 29 APR 2020 7:48PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 29, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) continues its Samaritanâ€™s drive of distributing cooked meals to the needy people following the nation-wide lockdown due to COVID-19. Food distribution was done to poor, destitute, beggars, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever else comes in search of food at and near the Railway stations every day. The food was prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same was distributed by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) was distributed among 2305 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Budge Budge etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Rampurhat, Dumka, Asansol, Barakar, Madhupur, Jasidih and Malda. Out of this, RPF/NGO/other organizations prepared food for 1180 persons, and the rest for 1125 persons was prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1465 persons was prepared and the same distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 515 persons was prepared by RPF/NGO/Others & the rest food for 950 persons was the contribution by IRCTC.
