      Ministry of Railways

      RPF OF ER PROVIDES FOOD TO SEVERAL STARVING PEOPLE OF CITY & STATE
      Posted On: 21 APR 2020 8:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 21, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) continues to provide cooked meals to needy people in the midst of nation-wide lockdown due to the outbreak of COVID-19. Food distribution is being done to poor, destitute, beggars, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever else comes in search of food at and near the railway stations every day. The food is being prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2183 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ballygunge, Budge Budge etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, New Farakka, Malda, Durgapur, Barakar, Cossimbazar, Raniganj, Asansol, Barakar, Madhupur and Jasidih. Out of this large quantity of food, RPF/NGO/other organizations prepared food for 1058 persons, and the rest food for 1125 persons were prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1215 persons have been prepared on date and the same will be distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 265 persons is being prepared by RPF/NGO/Others and the rest food for 950 persons is the contribution of IRCTC.


    This apart, food grains and vegetables were also distributed by RPF/ER personnel among 270 families near Dum Dum Jn., New Farakka, Durgapur and Jamtara Railway stations.
