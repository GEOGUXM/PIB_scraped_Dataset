      Ministry of Railways

      JAMALPUR-HOWRAH PARCEL EXPRESS TRAIN CARRIES OVER 12 TONS OF GHEE
      Posted On: 25 APR 2020 3:34PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 25, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is running time tabled Parcel Express Trains in eight different routes for transporting essential commodities and other merchandise to different places during the lockdown period. These Parcel Express Trains are running in Sealdah – Guwahati – Sealdah, Howrah – Guwahati – Howrah, Sealdah – Malda Town – Sealdah, Howrah – Jamalpur – Howrah, Howrah – New Delhi – Howrah, Sealdah – New Delhi – Sealdah , Howrah – Mumbai CSMT – Howrah and Howrah- Bhopal -Howrah routes.


    Jamalpur-Howrah Parcel Express train has become a means of sending ‘Ghee’ from Bhagalpur and Sahibganj area to Kolkata and Howrah markets for maintaining smooth supply of this popular consumable at both these cities and other areas of West Bengal. Howrah-Jamalpur-Howrah Parcel Express is running daily from Howrah station since April 10 April. It will run till May 3. Betweer Jamalpur and Howrah, the train will run between April 11 and May 4, 2020. Through Jamalpur-Howrah Parcel Express train, 682 tins of Ghee (total weight 12.07 tons) has been sent till date from Bhagalpur and Sahibganj areas for catering the need of people.
