      F C I CHUCHURA GODOWN DISBURSED 5000 METRIC TON OF RICE UNDER PRADHANMANTRI GARIB KALYAN YOJNA TO THE DISTRIBUTORS
      Posted On: 30 APR 2020 8:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 30 April, 2020


    Distributors of Chuchura Sadar and Chandan Nagar division under Hooghly district have lifted 4919.4 metric tonnes of rice under Pradhanmantri Garib Kalyan Yojna till 29 th April, from Food Corporation of Indiaâ€™s Chinsurah ware house. 10 distributors operate in this area. FCI distributes food grains from three divisions in Hooghly district. Apart from Chuchura the other two divisions are Sreerampur and Dankuni.


    Photo: Loading unloading is in progress at FCI Chuchura warehouse


    Hooghly district has received 12 wagons of rice dispatched from Andhra Pradesh and Telengana and many more wagons are on their way. Out of which four wagons have been received by the Chuchura warehouse. Incharge of the FCI Chuchura depot Shri Biswajit Das stated that they are working day and night for distribution of food grains so that the target of providing food grains to the poor under PMGKY can be fulfilled.
