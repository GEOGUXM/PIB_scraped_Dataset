      Posted On: 28 APR 2020 5:13PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 28, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) continues to provide cooked meals to needy people following the Nation-wide lockdown due to COVID-19. Food distribution is being done to poor, destitute, beggars, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever else comes in search of food at and near the Railway stations every day. The food is being prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2047 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Budge Budge etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Nabadwip Dham, Asansol, Barakar, Madhupur, Jasidih and Malda. Out of this, RPF/NGO/other organisations prepared food for 922 persons and the rest food for 1125 persons was prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1240 persons is prepared on date and the same distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 290 persons had been prepared by RPF/NGO/Others & the rest food for 950 persons was contribution from IRCTC.
