      Ministry of Railways

      RPF/ER PROVIDES FOOD TO THOUSANDS OF NEEDY PEOPLE IN STATE
      Posted On: 22 APR 2020 6:23PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 22, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) continues to provide cooked meals to needy people following the nation-wide lockdown in view of spread of COVID-19. Food distribution is being done to poor, destitute, beggars, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever else comes in search of food at and near the railway stations each day. The food is being prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2521 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ballygunge, Budge Budge etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Malda, Durgapur, Barakar, Cossimbazar, Raniganj, Asansol, Madhupur and Jasidih. Out of this large quantity of food, RPF/NGO/other organizations prepared food for 1396 persons, and the rest for 1125 persons were prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1215 persons is being being prepared on date for distribution among the starving people at various places. Out of this, food for 265 persons is being be prepared by RPF/NGO/Others & the rest for 950 persons is the contribution from IRCTC.


    This apart, food grains and vegetables were also distributed by RPF/ER personnel among 280 families near Krishnapur, Durgapur & New Farakka Railway stations.


    In this context, it is to be mentioned that RPF/ER of Sealdah Division alone has distributed more than 50,000 meals among needy people till Tuesday (April 21, 2020).
