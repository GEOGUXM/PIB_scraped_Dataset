      Ministry of Railways

      RPF/ER PROVIDES FOOD TO THOUSANDS OF VAGRANT PEOPLE
      Posted On: 24 APR 2020 7:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 24, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) continues to provide cooked meals to needy people in the wake of nation-wide lockdown due to COVID-19. Food is distributed among the poor, destitute, beggar, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever else comes in search of food near the Railway stations every day. The food is prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2475 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like, Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ballygunge, Budge Budge etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Ambika Kalna, Malda, Panagarh, Durgapur, Barakar, Cossimbazar, Raniganj, Asansol, Madhupur, Jasidih and Dumka. Out of this, RPF/NGO/other organisations prepared food for 1350 persons and the rest for 1125 persons was prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1225 persons has been prepared and the same distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 275 persons was prepared by RPF/NGO/Others & the rest food for 950 persons was the contribution from IRCTC.


    Besides, food grains and vegetables were distributed jointly by RPF/ER & Permanent Way Inspector (PWI) personnel among 200 needy families at Naihati Railway station. RPF, GRP and Station Superintendent of Sonarpur station also distributed foodgrains and vegetables among 150 needy families near Sonarpur Railway Station.
