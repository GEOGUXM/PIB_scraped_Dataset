      UTTARPARA-KOTRANG MUNICIPALITY STARTS DOOR-TO-DOOR TESTING TO IDENTIFY COVID-19 CASE
      Posted On: 17 APR 2020 7:42PM by PIB Kolkata

      Kolkata, 17th April, 2020
      COVID-19 outbreak has posed a threat and extraordinary health emergency worldwide. India is also facing the wrath of this virus. Government of India is taking several steps along with the States/UTs for the prevention, containment and management of COVID-19 in the country. Similarly municipalities across India are innovating new ideas to spread the message for containment of the disease.
      In an attempt to identify the suspected COVID-19 infected patients in Hooghly district, administration has initiated a door to door health checkup drive. As a part of that extensive effort health checkup initiative was undertaken today at various places under Uttarpara-Kotrang Municipality. Primarily it has been decided that the municipal area will be divided into four zones. The doctors and health workers will go to each house of the designated area and do thermal scanning. As a follow up action all essential and necessary data will be collected regarding health and ailments. The data regarding the medicines they consume will also be taken. After doing the data analysis if needed concerned person will be sent to government hospitals for further investigation.
      Four medical teams have been constituted for this purpose. Authorities expect this initiative would help in instilling confidence among the citizens.
