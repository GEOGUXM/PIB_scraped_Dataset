      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S ROUND THE CLOCK FREIGHT SERVICES TO MAINTAIN THE SUPPLY CHAIN
      Posted On: 28 APR 2020 4:20PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 28, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and other merchandise for maintaining the supply chain and availability of essential items as well as other consumables during this nation-wide lockdown period, Eastern Railway (ER|) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.
