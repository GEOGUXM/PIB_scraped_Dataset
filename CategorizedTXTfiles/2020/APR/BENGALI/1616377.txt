      Ministry of Railways

      SER ENSURES PROMPT TRANSPORTATION OF MEDICAL ITEMS DURING PRESENT CRISIS PERIOD
      Posted On: 20 APR 2020 4:27PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 20, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) is running Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains to different parts of the country for uninterrupted supply of medicines, medical equipment and other essential commodities during the present nationwide lockdown. During such an emergency situation, transportation of medicines and medical equipment has been given the top most priority by this Railway.


    Till last Friday, SER has loaded altogether 2.82 tonnes of medicines, medical equipment and masks through different parcel express trains from originating stations within the jurisdiction of this railway . The transportation was executed through parcel express trains running between Shalimar-Ranchi, Shalimar-Mumbai, Howrah-Yesvantpur and Howrah-Secunderabad for destinations viz. Ranchi, Rourkela, Chennai, Bengaluru, Durg, Nagpur, Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, Mumbai etc. All the parcel express trains will continue to run till May 3, 2020 to meet the various demand of essential commodities.


    The unique initiative of running parcel express trains by railways has ensured swift movement of essential items to keep the supply chain steady during this crisis period caused due to outbreak of COVID-19. Railway employees are working round the clock for seamless transportation of these essential and life saving commodities.
