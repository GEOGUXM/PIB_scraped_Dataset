      Ministry of Railways

      ER OPERATES 24 HRS FREIGHT SERVICES TO MAINTAIN THE SUPPLY CHAIN
      Posted On: 27 APR 2020 4:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 27, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and other merchandise to maintain the supply chain and availability of consumables and essential items during this nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for the power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON SUNDAY:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 36 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 22 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Steel – 1 Rake


    Rice – 7 Rakes


    Sugar - 3 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 1 Rake


    Cement - 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 30 RAKES


    Coal – 19 Rakes


    Steel – 4 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Stone - 4 Rakes


    Cement - 1 Rake


    Twenty eight empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were maintained keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at plenty in all working points. Proper sanitisation was done wherever required.
