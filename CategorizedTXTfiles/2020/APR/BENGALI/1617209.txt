      Ministry of Railways

      HOWRAH-JABALPUR PARCEL EXPRESS EXTENDED UP TO BHOPAL
      Posted On: 22 APR 2020 6:34PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 22, 2020:


    Howrah-Jabalpur-Howrah Parcel Express train which was notified vide press release issued on Tuesday (April 21, 2020) has been extended up to Bhopal to transport essential commodities to different corners of the country during the lockdown period.


    Hence, Howrah-Bhopal-Howrah Parcel Express train will leave Howrah at 9:00 pm, on April 27, 2020 and in return journey will start from Bhopal at 3:00 pmon April 29, 2020. This Parcel Express train will stop at Shrirampur, Bandel, Barddhaman, Durgapur, Asansol, Madhupur, Jasidih, Patna, Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Jn, Varansai, Prayagraj Jn, Satna, Maihar, Katni Jn, Jabalpur, Narshinghpur, Kareli, Gadarwara, Pipariya, Itarsi Jn, Hoshangabad and Habibganj en route.
