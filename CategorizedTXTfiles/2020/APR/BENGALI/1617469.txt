      Ministry of Railways

      ER ENSURES FAST TRANSPORTATION OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 23 APR 2020 4:27PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 23, 2020


    To ease the lives of people during this unprecedented crisis following nation-wide lockdown, ER is operating its freight trains on 24x7 basis, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON WEDNESDAY :


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 37 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Rice – 2 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 1 Rake


    Salt – 1 Rake


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Cement – 2 Rakes


    POL – 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 26.3 RAKES


    Coal – 21 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Ballast – 1 Rake


    Jute and others – 0.3 Rake


    Thirty Four empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Sanitization had also been done of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs & Engines.
