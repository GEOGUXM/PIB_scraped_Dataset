      Ministry of Railways

      RPF, ER DISTRIBUTES FOOD AMONG 5223 STARVING PEOPLE
      Posted On: 17 APR 2020 7:31PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 17, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) has rendered yeomanâ€™s service by distributing food among the thousands of starving people every day since imposition of lockdown in the country in the wake of COVID-19.


    The food is being prepared by RPF/RPSF/NGO, Commercial Department & IRCTC and the same is distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food were distributed among 3210 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata, like, Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ballygunge etc. and far-off places like Krishnanagar, Ranaghat, Cossimbazar, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Nabadwip Dham, Durgapur, Kalipahadi, Malda, Asansol, Madhupur and Jasidih. Out of this large quantity of food, RPF/RPSF/NGO prepared food for 1285 persons, Commercial Department for 800 and the rest food for 1125 persons were prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 2013 people has also been prepared on date and the same will be distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 263 persons will be prepared by RPF/RPSF/NGO, Commercial Department for 800 persons & the rest food for 950 persons will be contributed by IRCTC.
