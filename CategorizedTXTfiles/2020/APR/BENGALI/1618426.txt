      Ministry of Railways

      ER’s Parcel Express trains Carry more than 30 tonnes of Medicine, Medical Equipment & Face Masks
      Posted On: 26 APR 2020 5:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 26, 2020:


    Eastern Railway (ER) is running Parcel Express trains in eight different routes for carrying essential items in the wake of outbreak of COVID-19 and the resultant lockdown. ER started operation of Parcel Express trains from April 10, 2020. Total trip originated from ER up to April 25, 2020 is 134 and transported about 581 tonnes of consignments. These parcel traffic of ER includes essential commodities & consumables as well as medicine & medical equipment.


    ER’s Parcel Express trains have also become a means of speedy transport of medicines, medical equipment & PPE materials to different corners of the country. Till April 25, 2020, Medicine, Medical Equipment & Face Mask weighing a total of 30.46 tonnes have been dispatched from ER as originating parcel traffic to different corners of the country. This has helped in augmenting medical infrastructure at different areas to resist spread of COVID-19.
