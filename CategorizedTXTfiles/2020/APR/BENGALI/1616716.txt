      Ministry of Railways

      ER ENSURES SEAMLESS TRANSPORTATION OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 21 APR 2020 4:54PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 21, 2020


    To ease the lives of people during the nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING ON MONDAY:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 38 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Rice – 6 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 2 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    POL – 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 39 RAKES


    Coal – 29 Rakes


    Container – 6 Rakes


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Ballast – 1 Rake


    Forty empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff have been taken care of keeping in mind the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer are available in plenty in all working points. Sanitization has also been done of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs & Engines.
