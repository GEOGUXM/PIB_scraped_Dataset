      Ministry of Railways

      ER Prepares around 80 K Face Masks & 1K Coveralls in-house
      Posted On: 24 APR 2020 5:24PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 24, 2020:


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously producing protective gears in-house as a part of relentless effort to resist spread of COVID-19.


    As a supplement to market procurement, ER is also producing protective equipment to obtain self-sufficiency keeping in view the increased requirement of those in the wake of this dreadly disease. Masks and sanitizers are being produced at different workshops, divisions of ER and distributed among the field staff / health workers on duty. Coveralls, Paramedical suits have been prepared and distributed among the physicians / paramedical staff and health workers.


    With the concerted effort of different divisions, workshops, loco sheds, ER has produced a total of 1077 Nos. of Coveralls, 1064 Para Medical Suits, 79217 Face Masks, 5459 litres of Sanitizers till Thursday (April 23, 2020), as a part of COVID-19 preparedness.
