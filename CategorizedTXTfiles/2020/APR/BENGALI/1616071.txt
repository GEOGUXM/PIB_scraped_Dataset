      Ministry of Railways

      ER IN FREIGHT SERVICES TO CARRY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES ROUND THE CLOCK
      Posted On: 19 APR 2020 5:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 19, 2020


    During this unprecedented crisis owing to the spread of COVID-19 following nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON FRIDAY IS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 37 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Rice – 4 Rakes


    Fertilizer – 2 Rakes


    Sugar - 1 Rake


    Cement – 1 Rake


    Container – 2 Rakes


    TOTAL LOADING – 32.8 RAKES


    Coal – 25 Rakes


    Container – 4 Rakes


    Steel – 1 Rake


    Cement – 1 Rake


    Jute – 0.8 Rake


    Ballast – 1 Rake


    37 empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff have been taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer are affluently available in all working points. Sanitization is done of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs & Engines.
