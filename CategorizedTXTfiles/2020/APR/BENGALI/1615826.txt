      Ministry of Railways

      RPF of ER DISTRIBUTE FOOD AMONG 3388 STARVING PEOPLE
      Posted On: 18 APR 2020 7:50PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 18, 2020


    Railway Protection Force (RPF) of Eastern Railway (ER) is rendering benevolent service by distributing food among the thousands of starving people every day since imposition of lockdown in the country in the wake of COVID-19. The food is being prepared by RPF/RPSF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2183 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata, like, Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ballygunge and far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Seoraphuli, Nabadwip Dham, Durgapur, Malda, Asansol, Madhupur and Jasidih. Out of this large quantity of food, RPF/NGO/other organizations prepared food for 1058 persons, and the rest of the food for 1125 persons were prepared by IRCTC.


    Moreover, dinner for 1205 persons has also been prepared on date and the same will be distributed among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places. Out of this, food for 255 persons has been prepared by RPF/NGO & the rest food for 950 persons is the contribution from IRCTC.
