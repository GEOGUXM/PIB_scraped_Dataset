      Ministry of Railways

      142 GOODS TRAINS OF ER PLY TO ENSURE SMOOTH SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 16 APR 2020 6:20PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 16, 2020


    Since the imposition of Nation-wide lockdown in the wake of COVID-19, Eastern Railway (ER) is running its freight services every day to ensure uninterrupted supply of essential commodities like food grains, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, POL, coal for power plants etc. to different parts of the country. On Wednesday, ER ran a total of 142 freight trains loaded with different commodities as detailed below:


    Coal & Minerals – 51 Rakes


    Container – 22 Rakes


    Rice – 7 Rakes


    Fertilizer – 1 Rake


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    POL – 5 Rakes


    Cement – 3 Rakes


    Clinker – 1 Rake


    Parcel – 2 Rakes


    Automobile – 1 Rake


    Ballast/Railway Materials Consignment – 1 Rake


    Rest 46 Rakes were despatched empty to facilitate loading of the same at other places.


    TOTAL TRAINS LOADED ON WEDNESDAY: 29 Rakes


    Coal – 28 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    TOTAL TRAINS UNLOADED ON WEDNESDAY: 43 Rakes


    Coal and mineral – 32 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Rice – 7 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    POL – 1 Rake


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff have been well taken care of as per the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap and sanitizers are being made available affluently to all staff at all working points. Sanitisation of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs and Engines has also been done on regular basis.
