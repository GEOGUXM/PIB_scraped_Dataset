      Ministry of Railways

      SER COMES WITH 369 TRIPS OF PARCEL EXPRESS TO ENSURE SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 28 APR 2020 4:17PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 28, 2020


    Indian Railways, the 'Life Line of The Nation' is fighting against the Corona virus pandemic in different ways by adopting various initiatives including movement of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains and Freight Trains for maintaining uninterrupted supply of essential commodities to every nook and corner of the country in the midst of nation-wide lockdown in the wake of outbreak of this menace. As a part of commitment to the nation, South Eastern Railway (SER) has already geared up its entire network to run Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains towards different destinations in the country. SER, during this trying period, has already run 369 trips of Parcel Express Trains from April 2 to 26, 2020, carrying essential commodities viz. food grains, medicines, medical equipments including PPEs, masks, sanitizer, coveralls & gloves, fish, egg, vegetables, grocery items, fruits, sugar, salt, betel leaves, fabrics, plastic goods, poly bundle, e-commerce items, machine parts, cotton goods etc. amounting to 4,431.13 tonnes of parcel traffic containing 1,59,407 packages to different places in the country . These Parcel Express Trains will continue to run till May 3, 2020.


    This challenging and robust task has come to due to the dedicated workforce of frontline warriors of this railway, viz., loco pilots, guards, trackmen, station masters and other employees working at different stations and divisional Headquarters who are putting their exemplary services to the nation despite this lockdown. SER is committed to run more freight and parcel trains for transportation of daily and essential items until the crisis situation in the country is over.
