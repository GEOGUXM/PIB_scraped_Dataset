      Ministry of Railways

      ER ENSURES FAST TRANSPORTATION OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 24 APR 2020 4:26PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 24 April, 2020


    To ease the lives of people during the nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON THURSDAY


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 37 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Rice – 3 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 3 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Clinker – 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 30.2 RAKES


    Coal – 20 Rakes


    Steel – 4 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Stone - 3 Rakes


    Ballast - 1 Rake


    Jute – 0.2 Rake


    Thirty six empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff was taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in plenty in all working points. Sanitization had been done of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs & Engines.
