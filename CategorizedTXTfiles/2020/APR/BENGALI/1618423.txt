      Ministry of Railways

      FAST TRANSPORTATION OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES BY ER
      Posted On: 26 APR 2020 5:29PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 26, 2020


    To ensure supply of essential commodities during this unprecedented crisis following nation-wide lockdown, ER is operating its freight trains on 24x7 basis, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON SATURDAY:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 39 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 24 Rakes


    Container – 1Rake


    Rice – 9 Rakes


    Salt - 2 Rakes


    Sugar - 1 Rake


    Tar - 1 Rake


    Railway Material Consignment - 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 29 RAKES


    Coal – 18 Rakes


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Stone - 5 Rakes


    Ballast - 1 Rake


    Twenty nine empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were well taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer are available in plenty all working points. Proper sanitization is also done wherever required.
