      Ministry of Railways

      ER OPERATES FREIGHT SERVICES TO CARRY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES ROUND THE CLOCK
      Posted On: 20 APR 2020 6:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 20, 2020


    Despite nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE ON SUNDAY WAS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 33 RAKES


    Coal & Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Rice – 4 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Steel – 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING – 30.5 RAKES


    Coal – 27 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Jute – 0.5 Rake


    Ballast – 1 Rake


    75 empty rekes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff are well taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizers are available in all working points in plenty of numbers. Sanitization is also done of the crew booking lobbies, Guard’s Cabs & Engines.
