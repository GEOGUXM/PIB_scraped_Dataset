      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT OPERATIONS CONTINUE UNABATED TO ENSURE MARKET STABILITY
      Posted On: 29 APR 2020 5:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 29, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and other merchandise for continuing the supply chain and make essential items available to all citizens in the wake of nation-wide lockdown due to outbreak of COVID-19, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating its freight trains round the clock, transporting food, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products and coal for power generating sector.
