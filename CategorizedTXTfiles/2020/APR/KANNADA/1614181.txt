      Beneficiaries praise Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package
      Posted On: 13 APR 2020 11:42PM by PIB Bengaluru


    More than 32 crore poor people have received financial assistance of Rs. 29,352 crore under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, which was announced by Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman, Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs. A relief package of Rs 1.70 Lakh crore was announced under PM Garib Kalyan Yojana by the minister on 26 March 2020 to protect poor people from the impact of lockdown.


    The relief package, targeted to help poor people in fight against COVID-19 pandemic, is inclusive of many components. Some of the components are: Implementation of PM Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana to ensure adequate availability of grains and pulses for the poor, cash transfer under PM- KISAN Yojana worth Rs. 2000 and delivery of free gas cylinders etc.


    During these tough times, government has provided free ration of food grains to 5.29 crore beneficiaries under PM Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana and 97.8 lakh free Ujjwala cylinders are delivered. First instalment under PM-KISAN has been transferred to 7.47 crore farmers amounting to 14,946 crore rupees.


    The beneficiaries are appreciating successful implementation of relief package under PM Garib Kalyan Yojana. The promised financial assistance is timely reaching their Jan Dhan accounts, which they find helpful in lockdown due to COVID-19.


    Many beneficiaries have shared their stories through various mediums appreciating the timely distribution of relief package.
