      India is ready to do whatever possible to help our friends to fight the pandemic says PM
      Posted On: 10 APR 2020 12:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi said that India is ready to do whatever possible to help it's friends to fight against pandemic.


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi replied to the tweet from the Israel Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, who expressed his gratitude for India’s decision to supply Chloroquine to Israel


    In his tweet Prime Minister, Modi said “We have to jointly fight this pandemic. India is ready to do whatever is possible to help our friends. Praying for the well-being and good health of the people of Israel.”
