      PIB Headquarters

      PIB’s Daily Bulletin on COVID-19
      Posted On: 02 APR 2020 6:25PM by PIB Delhi


    (Contains Press releases concerning Covid-19, issued in last 24 hours and Fact checks undertaken by PIB)


    Updates on COVID-19 from Ministry of Health and Family Welfare


    Various actions have been initiated by Government of India along with the States/UTs for the prevention, containment and management of COVID-19 in the country. As of now, 1965 confirmed cases and 50 deaths have been reported. During the last 24 hours, 328 new confirmed cases and 12 new deaths have been reported. 151 persons have been cured/discharged from the hospitals after recovery.


    https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=1610353


    PM interacts with CMs to bolster efforts to tackle COVID-19


    Prime Minister today interacted with Chief Ministers of states via VC to discuss measures to combat COVID-19. He thanked the States for supporting the decision of lockdown due to which India has achieved some success in limiting the spread of COVID-19. PM however forewarned that the global situation remains far from satisfactory and informed about speculations of a possible second wave of spread of the virus in some countries. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=1610246


    Prime Minister will share a video message with the people at 9 AM tomorrow i.e. 3 April 2020. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610350


    President of India to hold Discussions with Governors, LGs and Administrators of States and UTs on Covid-19 Response Tomorrow


    https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=1610249


    AarogyaSetu: A multi-dimensional bridge


    A mobile app has been developed to bring the people of India together in fight against COVID-19.App called ‘AarogyaSetu’ will enable people to assess themselves the risk for their catching Corona Virus infection. It will calculate this based on their interaction with others, using cutting edge Bluetooth technology, algorithms and artificial intelligence. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=1610301


    MHA writes to States to widely publicize the Penal Provisions regarding Violations of Lockdown Measures to fight COVID-19
    Union Home Secretary has written to all States to widely publicize among the authorities and public, the penal provisions under the DM Act and IPC regarding violations of lockdown measures, to fight COVID-19. It has been stressed that strict action must be taken by authorities on violators of lockdown measures.


    https://pib.gov.in/PressRelesePage.aspx?PRID=1610308


    MHA writes to States/UTs to ensure smooth Disbursal of Money to beneficiaries of PM-GKY during 21-day Lockdown to fight COVID-19


    Home secretary has written to all States/UTs requesting them to take all necessary measures to ensure smooth disbursal of money to the beneficiaries of PM-GKY, while observing norms of social distancing.


    https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610329


    Relief for third party Motor Insurance & Health policy holders


    The Government has provided relief for third party Motor Insurance & Health policy holders. These policies falling in the period from March 25 to April 14, 2020, can be renewed up till April 21,2020.


    https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610210


    Railways maintains its freight corridors fully functional for essential commodities


    Indian Railways has maintained its freight corridors fully functional. Total 143458 wagons of freight were loaded in the last three days. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610226


    Parcel Trains providing big boost to transportation of essential commodities


    10 routes for Time Tabled Parcel Trains and tentative 18 new routes are planned for Special Parcel Trains to keep supply chains running. So far, Indian Railways has loaded 30 Special Parcel Trains. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610205


    Ex-Servicemen mobilised to fight COVID-19 pandemic


    RajyaandZilaSainik Boards are identifying and mobilising maximum Ex-Service Men volunteers to assist State and District Administration in performing public outreach activities such as contact tracing, community surveillance, management of quarantine facilities. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610111


    NCC offers its volunteer cadets for national duty to fight COVID-19


    National Cadet Corps has offered a helping hand to civilian authorities in the country’s fight against COVID-19 by extending the services of cadets under ‘Exercise NCC Yogdan’. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610145


    MHA writes to States/UTs to take measures to fight Fake News


    Following up on the Supreme Court’s observations, Secretary, MHA has written to States/UTs to take effective measures to fight Fake News. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610146


    PIB sets up COVID19 Fact Check Unit
    PIB has set up a portal - COVID 19 Fact Check Unit which will receive messages by email and send its response in quick time. PIB will also release a daily Bulletin every day to inform regarding Government’s decisions and developments and progress on COVID 19. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610111


    FCI ramps up foodgrain supplies
    FCI is ensuring uninterrupted supply of wheat and rice throughout the country. 352 rakes have moved carrying about 9.86 LMT foodgrains since the lockdown began.

    Dr. Harsh Vardhan reviews actions taken and further preparedness on COVID 19
    Health & FWMinisterheld a VC with IMA members and reviewed the status of the preparedness in terms of availability of beds, isolation wards, laboratory readiness for high test volumes. https://pib.gov.in/PressReleseDetail.aspx?PRID=1610003


    MHA requests States/UTs to implement Lockdown Measures fully

    CBSE’s directions about promotions and Board Examinations
