      Telephone Conversation between PM and Prime Minister of Japan
      Posted On: 10 APR 2020 3:05PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi spoke on telephone today with H.E. Shinzo Abe, Prime Minister of Japan.


    
    The two leaders discussed the global health and economic challenges emerging out of the COVID-19 pandemic. They also discussed the steps taken in their respective countries to deal with this crisis.


    
    Both leaders expressed appreciation for the support and facilitation provided during the present crisis to each otherâ€™s citizens present in their respective territories, and agreed to continue such coordination.


    
    The two leaders agreed that the India-Japan partnership could play a critical role in helping the world find solutions to the challenges arising out of the pandemic.
