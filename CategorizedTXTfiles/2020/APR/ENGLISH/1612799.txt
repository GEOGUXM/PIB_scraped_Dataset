      PM remembers Jesus Christ on Good Friday
      Posted On: 10 APR 2020 10:59AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has remembered Jesus Christ’s commitment to truth, service and justice on Good Friday.


    “Lord Christ devoted his life to serving others. His courage and righteousness stand out and so does his sense of justice. On Good Friday, we remember Lord Christ and his commitment to truth, service and justice”, the Prime Minister said.
