      PM condoles passing away of versatile actor Irrfan Khan
      Posted On: 29 APR 2020 6:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of versatile actor Irrfan Khan.


    “Irrfan Khan’s demise is a loss to the world of cinema and theatre. He will be remembered for his versatile performances across different mediums. My thoughts are with his family, friends and admirers. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
