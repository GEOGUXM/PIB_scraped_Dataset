      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles passing away of veteran actor Rishi Kapoor
      Posted On: 30 APR 2020 12:12PM by PIB Delhi


      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of veteran actor Rishi Kapoor.


      “Multifaceted, endearing and lively...this was Rishi Kapoor Ji. He was a powerhouse of talent. I will always recall our interactions, even on social media. He was passionate about films and India’s progress. Anguished by his demise. Condolences to his family and fans. Om Shanti”, the Prime Minister said.
