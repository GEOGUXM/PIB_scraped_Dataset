      Ministry of Home Affairs

      MHA issues order to make domiciles of Jammu and Kashmir eligible for all Government posts in the Union Territory
      Posted On: 04 APR 2020 9:17AM by PIB Delhi


    Union Ministry for Home Affairs (MHA) had issued anorder on 31.03.2020 for adaptation and modification of State Laws specific to the erstwhile State of Jammu and Kashmir, to further facilitate the application of Central Laws to the newly formed Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.


    MHA has furtherissued an order to make domiciles of Jammu and Kashmir eligible for all Government posts in the Union Territory.
