      Kind gesture of a senior citizen
      Posted On: 22 APR 2020 6:36PM by PIB Chennai


    Today at Madurai Reserve Lines SO, a senior citizen Ms Karthika Balanayagam age 65 yrs old has booked 32 money orders for Rs 100/- each to PM and all state CMs/Union Territory of India for treatment of corona virus and relief fund. She is very poor and the donated amount is her own money kept in her collapse bag for her food and not collected from any others.


    She is also planning to send her money to all countries suffering by corona through bank for treatment and fight against corona virus.


    The eMOs booked at counter by Smt M.Devi, PA and emo Forms prepared by all postmen of Madurai Reserve Lines SO.
