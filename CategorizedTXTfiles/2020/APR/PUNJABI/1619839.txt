      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      District Magistrate Faridkot responds to a news report regarding three passengers returning from Hazur Sahib not taken to quarantine zone
      Posted On: 30 APR 2020 5:06PM by PIB Chandigarh


    In response to a news report that a PRTC bus coming from Hazur Sahib
    dropped three passengers on the way at Kot Kapura, Faridkot instead of
    taking them to Quarantine centre, District Magistrate Faridkot has confirmed
    that that all three passengers were intercepted by the police & taken to
    government quarantine facility.
    In a tweet, District Magistrate Faridkot clarified that
    “Dist admin picks up passengers from designated places, when they are
    dropped by bus. These people after being dropped by bus (going to moga) tried
    to go their home by private vehicle. They were intercepted by police and
    brought to government quarantine facility”
    The clarification has also been tweeted from official Twitter handles
    namely @PIBChandigarh and @ROBChandigarh.
