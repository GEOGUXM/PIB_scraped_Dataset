      Ministry of Micro,Small & Medium Enterprises

      NSIC provides 3 Ply Face Mask and Hand Sanitizer for sanitation workers in Guwahati
      Posted On: 22 APR 2020 3:42PM by PIB Guwahati


    National Small Industries Corporation Limited(NSIC), Guwahati provided 3-ply face mask and hand sanitizer for distribution among sanitation workers to Guwahati Municipal Corporation (GMC) . Dr Avinash Choudhary, Zonal General Manager, NSIC handed over the materials to Shri Devajyoti Hazarika, IAS, Commissioner of GMC today under its CSR activity.
