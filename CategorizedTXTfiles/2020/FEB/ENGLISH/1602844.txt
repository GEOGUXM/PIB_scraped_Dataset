      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Non-Distribution of Imported Onions
      Posted On: 11 FEB 2020 6:24PM by PIB Delhi


    Based on the direction of Government, MMTC contracted import of 41,950 metric tonnes (MT) of onions against which 36,124 MT of import materialized. States/UTs were offered imported onions. However, as on 03.02.2020, only about 2501 MT imported onion, in total, were taken by States/ UTs that include Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Telengana, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, West Bengal, Himachal Pradesh, Goa, Haryana, Meghalaya and Jammu & Kashmir.


    This information was given in a written reply bytheMinister of State Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, Shri Danve Raosaheb Dada Rao in Lok Sabha today.
