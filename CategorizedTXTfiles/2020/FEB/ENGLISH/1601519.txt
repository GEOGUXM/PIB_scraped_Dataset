      President of India to Inaugurate New Global Headquarters of Ram Chanda Mission In Rangareddy District of Telangana Tomorrow
      Posted On: 01 FEB 2020 4:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Telangana on February 1 and 2, 2020 to inaugurate the new global headquarters ‘Kanha Shanti Vanam’ of Shri Ram Chandra Mission in Rangareddy District on February 2, 2020.
