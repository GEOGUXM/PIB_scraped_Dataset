      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Status of Projects Funded by ADB
      Posted On: 05 FEB 2020 12:40PM by PIB Delhi


    As per the information received from Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, an amount of 80 million US Dollars was sanctioned by Asian Development Bank (ADB), under North Eastern Region Capital Cities Development Investment programme (NERCCDIP) Tranche-III, out of which 20 million US Dollar was surrendered during the implementation as it was not required. From the balance 60 million US Dollars, 56.38 million US Dollars have been utilised till the closure of the loan on 22 nd June, 2019. Progress made and status of these projects upto January, 2020 is given below:

2       Tripura  7                5                          2                        98.75%                          
                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                      

    In order to complete the ongoing projects, the North Eastern Region Urban Development Programme (NERUDP) Scheme has been extended for two more years i.e. upto 22.06.2021 with funding by Government of India as 90% grant.


    Classification of cities does not fall under the purview of Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today.
