      President of India to lay foundation stone/inaugurate various developmental projects of Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu tomorrow
      Posted On: 16 FEB 2020 1:56PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Union Territory of Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu on February 17-18, 2020.


    On February 17, 2020, the President will lay the foundation stone/inaugurate various developmental projects of Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu in Daman. On the same day, he will also inaugurate the Jampore Sea Front Road from Moti Daman Jetty to Jampore Beach and an Ayushman Bharat Wellness Centre in Daman.
