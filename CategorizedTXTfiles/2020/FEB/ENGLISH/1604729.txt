      Prime Minister's Office

      PM pays tributes to the former Prime Minister of India Shri Moraji Desai on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 29 FEB 2020 1:35PM by PIB Delhi


    PM paid tributes to the former Prime Minister of India Shri Moraji Desai on his birth anniversary. The Prime Minister said “He always did politics based on discipline and principles, for which he will always be remembered.”


    पूर्व प्रधानमंत्री मोरारजी भाई देसाई को उनकी जयंती पर शत-शत नमन। उन्होंने हमेशा अनुशासन और सिद्धांतों पर आधारित राजनीति की, जिसके लिए वे हमेशा याद किए जाएंगे। pic.twitter.com/fwKrPTv8zZ
