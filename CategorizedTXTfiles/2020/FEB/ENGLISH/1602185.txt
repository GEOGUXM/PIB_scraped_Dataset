      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Khelo India Scheme
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2020 12:51PM by PIB Delhi


    The Khelo India Scheme covers all sportspersons, including sportspersons of rural India. The State-wise details of athletes selected so far under Khelo India Scheme are given at Annexure – I .


    The following facilities are being provided by the Government to sportspersons under the Khelo India Scheme:

     1. Out of pocket allowance
     2. Coaching
     3. Lodging and boarding
     4. Sports kits
     5. Domestic travel
     6. Insurance and medical benefits
     7. Sports science management
     8. Educational tie-up

    Under the Khelo India Scheme, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has sanctioned 179 sports infrastructure projects including playfields in rural areas, for development of sports infrastructure facilities in the States / Union Territories (UTs) of the country. State/UT wise details are placed at Annexure – II . Further, this Ministry has introduced geo-tagging of all playfields in the country and sharing the information with the public, which is in progress. So far 3005 playfields have been geo-tagged across the country, which includes 94 in West Bengal.
