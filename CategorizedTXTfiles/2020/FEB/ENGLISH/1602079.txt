      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007
      Posted On: 05 FEB 2020 5:22PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment undertook a study on ‘Functioning and Effectiveness of Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens (MWPSC) Act, 2007’ during 2019.


    Following are the extract of the findings of the Study:

(a)    Cases ongoing at the tribunal level                                 96.66%

4. (a)    strongly agreed                                                     41.90%

(c)    not agree                                                           19.34%    
                                                                                     
                                                                                     

    Thus, the Tribunals settled more than 80% of the cases in favour of the petitioners and more than 80% of the petitioners have shown their satisfaction to the orders of the Maintenance Tribunals.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Rattan Lal Kataria in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
