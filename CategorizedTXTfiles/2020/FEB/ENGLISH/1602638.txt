      Ministry of Micro,Small & Medium Enterprises

      Vendor Development Programme
      Posted On: 10 FEB 2020 4:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vendor Development Programs (VDPs) are aimed at building the capacity of Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) and enhancing their participation in public procurement process.


    The procurement from Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) by Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) increased from 23.11% in 2017-18 to 26.31% in 2018-19.
