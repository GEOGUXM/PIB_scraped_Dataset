      Ministry of Railways

      Track Electrification
      Posted On: 05 FEB 2020 5:24PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Railways has planned to electrify balance Broad Gauge (BG) routes of Indian Railways (IR) by December, 2023.


    The time line for completion of Electrification of entire BG routes of IR is as under:

    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
