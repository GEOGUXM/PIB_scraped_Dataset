      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Manpower Augmentation in Anganwadis
      Posted On: 07 FEB 2020 4:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government at present has not proposed the engagement of more manpower under the Anganwadi Services Scheme.


    Joint guidelines for construction of 4 lakh Anganwadi Centre (AWC) buildings across the country in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) were issued on 17.02.2016. Under the revised guidelines, provision for construction of 4lakh new AWC buildings @ Rs.7.00 lakh per AWC building has been made. Out of this, Rs.5 lakh are provided by Ministry of Rural Development. Central share for construction of AWC building is Rs.1 lakh per AWC building for all States/UTs, which is reimbursable on completion of construction work and remaining Rs.1 lakh is borne by the respective State/UT.Further, provision has been made for construction of toilets in 70,000 AWCs with drinking water facilities in 20,000 AWCs from 2017-18 to 2019-20 under Swachhata Action Plan. Apart from this, there is a provision of up-gradation of AWC buildings @ Rs.2 lakh per AWC building under the scheme. Funds for construction of AWC building, construction of Toilets in AWCs, providing drinking water facilities and for up-gradation of AWC buildings are released to States/UTs.


    The Government with effect from 1 st October, 2018 hasenhanced the honorarium of Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) from ₹3,000/- to ₹4,500/- per month; AWWs at mini-AWCs from ₹2,250/- to ₹3,500/- per month; Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) from ₹1,500/- to ₹2,250/- per month. The Government has also introduced performance linked incentive of ₹250/- per month to AWHs. They are paid performance linked incentive of ₹500/- per month for using ICDS-CAS (Common Application Software) under POSHAN Abhiyaan.


    For providing basic amenities and infrastructure at AWCs across the country, the Government has released funds of an amount of Rs.6735.65 lakhs in F.Y. 2017-18, Rs.7266.54 lakhs in F.Y. 2018-19 and Rs.138.54 lakh during current year for construction of toilets and providing drinking water facilities in AWCs. Funds to the tune of Rs. 25774.90 lakh in financial year 2017-18 and Rs.14904.70 lakh in financial year 2018-19 have been released to States/UTs for up-gradation of AWC buildings. Ministry has also issued directions to States/UTs to delegate powers to District Magistrates/Collectors for the recruitment of Anganwadi Workers, Anganwadi Helpers and Supervisors on 14 th March, 2018 for smooth functioning of Anganwadis.


    This information was given by the Minister of Women and Child Development, Smriti Zubin Irani, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
