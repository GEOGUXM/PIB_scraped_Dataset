      Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation

      Consumer Price Index Numbers on Base 2012=100 for Rural, Urban and Combined for the month of January 2020
      Posted On: 12 FEB 2020 5:30PM by PIB Delhi

In addition to this, Consumer Food Price Index (CFPI) for all India Rural, Urban and Combined are also being released for January 2020.     3 . Provisional indices for the month of January 2020 and also the final indices for December 2019 are being released with this note for all-India and for State/UTs. All-India provisional General (all-groups), Group and Sub-group level CPI and CFPI numbers for January 2020 for Rural, Urban and Combined are given in Annexure I. The inflation rates of important categories of items are given in Annexure II. State/UT wise provisional General CPI numbers for Rural, Urban and Combined are given in Annexure III. State/UT–wise Group CPIs are available on the Ministry’s website ( www.mospi.gov.in ).


    4. Price data are collected from selected towns and selected villages by the Field Operations Division of NSO, MoSPI. Price data are received through web portals, maintained by the National Informatics Centre.
