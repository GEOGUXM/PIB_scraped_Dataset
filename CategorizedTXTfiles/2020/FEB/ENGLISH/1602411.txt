      27,916 Startups Recognized by DPIIT
      Posted On: 07 FEB 2020 4:56PM by PIB Delhi


    Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) has recognized 27,916 startups, as on 1 st February 2020.


    In the state of Rajasthan800 startups have been recognizedby the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, as on 1 st February 2020.
