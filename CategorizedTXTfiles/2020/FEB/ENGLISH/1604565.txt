      President of India to Visit Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh from February 28 to March 2
      Posted On: 27 FEB 2020 6:07PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh from February 28 to March 2, 2020.


    On February 28, 2020, the President will address the first convocation of the Central University of Jharkhand in Ranchi.


    On February 29, 2020, the President will visit Vikas Bharti, Bishunpur in Gumla, Jharkhand.


    On March 2, 2020, the President will deliver the convocation address at the Guru Ghasidas University in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.
