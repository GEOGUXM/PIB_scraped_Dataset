      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      National Commission for Protection of Child Rights recommends disclosure of information linked with the child sexual abuse victim is restricted and punishable under section 23 of the POCSO Act 2012
      Posted On: 18 FEB 2020 5:17PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, February 18, 2020


    National Commission for Protection of Child Rights has observed that various media reports are revealing key information such as address, photograph, family details, school name and neighbourhood having connect with the identification of the child sexual abuse victims. It has recommended that disclosure of information linked with the child sexual abuse victim is restricted and punishable under section 23 of the POCSO Act 2012. It has recommended ‘Editors’ of various media entities to ensure strict compliance of such significant provision of the POCSO Act 2012 in the interest of children.
