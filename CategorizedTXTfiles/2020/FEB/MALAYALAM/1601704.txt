      Kochi Area Boat Pulling Regatta-2020 tomorrow
      Posted On: 03 FEB 2020 3:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Kochi Area Boat Pulling Regatta-2020 will be conducted by Southern Naval Command tomorrow (04th February 2020). The races would be held on “ Whaler” boat in the Ernakulam Channel between Venduruthy Vikrant bridge and North Jetty of the Naval Base. Six teams from the Southern Naval Command would compete in four categories for the prestigious boat pulling trophy.


    Vice Admiral Anil Kumar Chawla, Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Southern Naval Command would be the Chief Guest and would be giving away prizes to the winners.
