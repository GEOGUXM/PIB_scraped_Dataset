      Passport Mela in Aluva , Kottayam and Thrissur
      Posted On: 05 FEB 2020 3:11PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Passport Seva Kendras (PSK), in Aluva, Kottayam and Thrissur will remain open on 15 th February, 2020 on account of passport mela. Normal applications with prior online appointments will be accepted. Appointments are now available online and can be booked through passportindia.gov.in or mPassport Seva App. For more information, contact 94477 31152
