      1.16 Kg gold worth Rs. 49 lakhs concealed in meat chopper handle seized by Customs At Chennai Airport. One arrested.
      Posted On: 15 FEB 2020 6:07PM by PIB Chennai


    On Saturday morning based on information one Mohammed Khan Pathan,41, of Rayachoty( AP) who arrived from Riyadh via Sharjah by Air Arabia flight G9 471 was intercepted at exit on suspicion of carrying gold. On examination of his checked in baggage, one Meat Chopper Cleaver was found concealed in his personal effects. On lifting the chopper appeared to be unusually heavy. On closer examination, the handle portion of the chopper was found to be wrapped with black adhesive tape. On removing the tape the handle appeared to be specially made. On dismantling the handle one rectangular gold cut bar was found ingeniously concealed inside. The gold assayer certified the cut gold bar to be of 24 K purity weighing 1.164 Kg and valued at Rs . 49.03 lakhs . The recovered gold was seized under Customs Act 1962. The passenger was arrested.


    Further investigation is under progress according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
