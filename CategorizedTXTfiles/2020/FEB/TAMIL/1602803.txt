      Shri Bimal Kumar Jha assumes charge as Deputy Chairman, V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust, Tuticorin
      Posted On: 11 FEB 2020 5:04PM by PIB Chennai


    On 07.02.2020, Shri Bimal Kumar Jha has assumed the charge as Deputy Chairman of V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust, Tuticorin, succeeding Shri N. Vaiyapuri, who retired on superannuation on 31.10.2019.


    Prior to the new assignment, he served as the Secretary of Paradip Port Trust and Deendayal (Kandla) Port Trust during 2012-19, and also held additional charge as Deputy Chairman of Deendayal Port Trust (Erstwhile Kandla Port Trust) from 24.7.2018 to 16.4.2019.


    Mr. Bimal Kumar Jha, holds a First Class Degree in Maths (Hons.) from St. Xavier’s College, Calcutta University, Calcutta. He also holds Master Degree in Business Management with specialisation in Personnel Management from Department of Business Management, University of Calcutta. He is also UGC (NET) qualified in the field of Human Resource management, Industrial Relations and Labour Welfare. He had also participated in the training programme on ‘Port Management’ from Flanders Port Training Center (APEC) at Antwerp, Belgium.


    Mr. Bimal Kumar Jha started his professional career in Port sector in the year 1994 and had served in various capacities at Cochin Port Trust from 2001 to 2012.


    Having rich experience in Port & Shipping Sector for more than 25 years, he possesses practical knowledge in the Port operations and Management. His notable expertise includes Human Resources Management, Industrial Relations, Training & Education, Public Relations & Corporate Communications, Labour Welfare, Legal & Financial Affairs, according to the Press Release issued by V. O. Chidambaranar Port Trust.
