      Bureau of Indian Standards, Southern Regional Office Organised Licensees Meet for packaged Drinking Water Licensees
      Posted On: 13 FEB 2020 1:03PM by PIB Chennai


    “The Southern Regional Office of the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) conducted Licensees Meet for the licensees of Packaged Drinking Water from Thiruvallur, Kanchipuram and Chennai districts on 12 Feb 2020 at Hotel Savera, Chennai, in association with Greater Tamilnadu Packaged Drinking Water Manufacturers Association.


    The meeting was attended by 160 licensees of Packaged Drinking Water. Dr R K Tyagi, Head Chennai Branch Office – II, BIS inaugurated and Sh. T Kalaivanan, Deputy Director General South, BIS presided over the function. Technical presentations were made by officers of the Bureau on various requirements as per IS 14543 and operation of BIS licence.


    An exclusive presentation was given on the topic “Introduction of Licensing requirements for the scheme of Certification of containers as per IS 15410 used in Packaged Drinking Water”, according to a press release issued by Deputy Director, Bureau of Indian Standards.
