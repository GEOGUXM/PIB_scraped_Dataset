      1.45 Kg gold worth Rs. 61 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 12 FEB 2020 12:42PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday based on specific informationone Umar Farook Ali , 56, of Ramanathapuram, arrived from Colombo by UL 125 was intercepted at exit . On personal search2 bundles of gold paste were recovered fromrectum. On extraction 270 grams of gold valued at Rs.11.37 lakhs was recovered and seized.


    In another case today morning one NoorulAmeen , 48, of Ramanathapuram, and Syed Mohamed ,51,of Chennai arrived from Dubai by flight EK542 and 6E66 respectively. They were intercepted at exit . On personal search 6 bundles of gold paste were recovered from rectum and 2 gold cut bits totally weighing 98 grams recovered from pant pockets. On extraction 536 grams of gold was recovered. Total 634 grams of gold valued at Rs. 26.69 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Earlier on Monday one Abbas , 34, of Chennai, arrived from KualaLumpur by Batik Airlines Flight No. ID6018 was intercepted at the exit .On search of his luggage trolley10 gold cut bitsweighing 547 grams valued at Rs.23 lakhs were found concealed in hollow pipe of the trolley and same were recovered and seized.


    In three cases total gold weighing 1.45 Kg valued at Rs.61 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is in progress , according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
