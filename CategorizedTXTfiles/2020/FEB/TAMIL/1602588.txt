      Gold and Cigarettes worth Rs. 19.3 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 10 FEB 2020 11:55AM by PIB Chennai


    On Sunday one Musthafa, 37, of Thanjavur and Sikkander, 45, of Chennai, who arrived from Dubai by Emirates flight EK 542 were intercepted at exit. On personal search of Mustafa 3 crude gold chains weighing 348 grams valued at Rs. 14.6 lakhs were recovered from his trouser pocket and seized Customs Act 1962.


    On examination of checked in bag of Sikkander, 45, 98 cartons of Gudang garam cigarettes (23520 sticks) valued Rs. 4.70 lakhs were found concealed among his personal effects. Same recovered and seized under Customs Act and COTPA 2003.


    Further investigation is under process , according to a press release issue by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
