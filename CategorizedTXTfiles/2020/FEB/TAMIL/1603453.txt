      APPRENTICESHIP MELA IN 7 DISTRICTS ACROSS TAMIL NADU
      Posted On: 17 FEB 2020 1:13PM by PIB Chennai


    The Regional Directorate of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (RDSDE) has planned for recruitment drive to select candidates for apprenticeship through ITIs in seven regions of Tamil Nadu from 19 th February to 4 th of March. Addressing Media in Chennai today Joint Director of RDSDE Shri. K. Srinivasa Rao said over 20,000 candidates will be selected through this drive and training will be given to them in various trades at different Private and Government Public Sector undertakings.


    The selected candidates would be trained in PSUs like BHEL, ICF, NLC and other corporates in the States. Those selected would also be given stipend amount of Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 12,000 depending upon the industries policy and trade which would last for one or two years depending upon the trade he added. He said, sessions on skills needed to match the requirement of the industry would be included in the curriculum and the candidates would be given opportunity to hone their skills in the industry.


    Shri Srinivasa Rao said, those who have passed 8 th standard can appear for the objective type written test and interview to be short listed for training in different trades. After completion of apprenticeship training the candidates are eligible to appear for All India Trade Test (AITT) exam and National Apprenticeship Certificate is issued to the successful candidates. The certificates would also help them find job placements in the industry in India and abroad, he said.


    He said, the Recruitment is being planned at ITIs at Chengleput, Salem, Coimbatore, Uludurpettai, Madurai, Thanjavur and Tirunelveli from 19 th of February to 4th of March.
