      1.58 Kg gold worth Rs. 66.5 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport.
      Posted On: 10 FEB 2020 6:46PM by PIB Chennai


    On Monday one Shake Abdullah,28, of Ramanathapuram arrived from Dubai by Indigo Flight 6E-66 was intercepted at exit. On personal search 4 bundles of gold paste recovered from his rectum. On extraction 486 grams of gold valued at Rs. 20.4 lakhs was recovered and seized.


    On Sunday night three passengers, Abdul Razzak,40, of Cuddalore, Inhanullah,33, and Sintha, 49 both from Ramanathapuram, arrived from Colombo by Sri Lankan Airlines Flight UL121 and UL 127 respectively. All three were intercepted at the exit. On personal search 10 bundles of gold paste were recovered from rectum. On extraction 1.07 Kg gold was recovered. Further two gold cut bits weighing 24 grams were also recovered from pant pocket of one of them. Total 1.09 Kg gold valued at Rs. 46.10 Lakhs was recovered and seized.


    In four cases 1.58 Kg gold worth Rs. 66.5 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. Further investigation is in progress. according to a press release issue by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
