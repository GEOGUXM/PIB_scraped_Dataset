      I kg gold worth Rs. 44.1 lakhs seized by Customs at Airport
      Posted On: 23 FEB 2020 1:35PM by PIB Chennai


    On Saturday Kalander Abbas, 36, of Chennai arrived from Colombo by Srilankan Airlines flight UL-125 and Asikdeen, 24, also of Chennai arrived from Dubai by Indigo Flight 6E-66 were intercepted at the exit. On personal search 5 bundles containing gold paste were found concealed in rectum. On extraction 681 grams of gold valued at Rs.29.3 was recovered and seized.


    In another case Mohamed Sadusha,22, of Tiruchirappalli, who arrived from Singapore by Indigo Airlines 6E54 was intercepted at exit. On personal search two pouches of gold paste 407 grams was found concealed inside specially stitched pockets inside bottom of his jeans. On extraction 344 gms valued at Rs.14.8 akhs were recovered.


    In total 1.025 Kg gold valued at Rs. 44.1 lakhs was recovered and seized under the Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is in progress , according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
