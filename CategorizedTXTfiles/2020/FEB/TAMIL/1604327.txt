      Army recruitment rally at arunai engineering college stadium, Tiruvannamalai (tamil nadu) from 15 apr 2020 to 25 apr 2020
      Posted On: 25 FEB 2020 5:45PM by PIB Chennai


    Indian Army will conduct a Recruitment Rally from 15 Apr 2020 to 25 Apr 2020 at Arunai Engineering College Stadium, Tiruvannamalai (TN) to enroll candidates into the Army from 11 Districts of Tamil Nadu under the aegis of Recruitment Office (Headquarter), Chennai i.e Cuddalore, Vellore, Tirupathur, Ranipet, Tiruvannamalai, Chennai, Tiruvallur, Vilupuram, Kallakurichi, Kancheepuram, Chengalpattu and Puducherry District of Puducherry (UT) for various categories that include :-


    Soldier Technical


    Soldier Nursing Assistant/Soldier Nursing Assistant Veterinary


    Soldier Clerk


    Store Keeper Technical


    Soldier General Duty and


    Soldier Tradesman


    Candidates will have to apply online only on www.joinindianarmy.nic.in and the registration will start from 01 Mar 2020 and will close on 31 Mar 2020. Admit card will be issued online after 31 Mar 2020. Date of reporting at rally site will be intimated on admit card and the applicant can take print out of admit card after 31 Mar 2020 from www.joinindianarmy.nic.in . Recruitment process being fully automated, is fair and transparent. Candidates must guard against Touts/fraudsters, claiming to help them pass. Only their hard work and preparation will ensure their selection as per merit.
