      FRAUDSTERS CONVICTED IN A BANK CASE OF CBI
      Posted On: 27 FEB 2020 6:04PM by PIB Chennai


    The Hon’ble Addl. Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Egmore, Chennai, convicted two Private persons in a Bank fraud case in RC 10/E/1995 - CBI/EOW/Chennai (CC 12619/2003) on 27.02.2020.


    The allegation of the case registered by CBI, EOW, Chennai, on 30.12.1995 was that the accused persons entered into a criminal conspiracy at Madras, Delhi and other places during 1993 to cheat the Indian Overseas Bank to the tune of Rs.2.70 crores by producing four false and forged Demand drafts purported to have been issued by different Branches of Indian Overseas Bank and 5 DDs of Bank of Navo Scotia, Mumbai, issued in favour of M/s Subhadra Impex, Chennai and M/s C.P.K. Traders, Chennai.


    After completion of investigation, charge sheet was filed before the Addl. Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Egmore, Chennai, in CC 12169/2003 U/s.120-B r/w sec.420, 420 IPC, 467, 467 r/w, 471 and 201 IPC substantive offences thereof.


    After the trial, the Hon’ble Court passed the Judgment against the accused persons as indicated below.
