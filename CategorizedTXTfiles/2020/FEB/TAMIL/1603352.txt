      2.91Kg gold worth Rs.1.24 Croresseized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 16 FEB 2020 5:06PM by PIB Chennai


    On Saturday night four passengers, two Sri Lankanladies Fathima, 48, and FathimaFareena Rizwie,43, along with two Indians Abdul Hameed,34,and Rasik Ali,31, both of Ramanathapuramarrived from Colombo by Air India flight AI 274 were intercepted at exit.On personal search eleven bundles containing gold in paste form were recovered from their rectum. On extraction 1.284 Kg of gold was recovered.


    On Sunday three passengers two Indians Nazeer Ahamed,28, of Chennai and Baisul Rahman,23, of Ramanathapuram and one Sri Lankan Yaseer, 49, arrived from Colombo by Sri Lankan Airlines flight UL 125 were intercepted at exit. On personal search twelve bundles of gold in paste form were recovered from their rectum. On extraction1.324 Kg was recovered.


    In another case on Sunday one Noorul Huk,39, of Ramnad who arrived from Dubai by Indigo Flight 6E 66 was intercepted at exit. On personal search three Strips of gold paste were found concealed inside waist portion of his jeans. On extraction303 grams was recovered.


    In all eight cases,total 2.91Kg gold of 24K purity valued at Rs. 1.24Crores was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is under process, according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
