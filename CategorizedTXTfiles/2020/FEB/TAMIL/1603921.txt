      Only institute in India, deals with the youth affairs is RGNIYD, says Union Minister Kiren Rijiju
      Posted On: 21 FEB 2020 2:55PM by PIB Chennai


    Youth population in India contribute to 20% of world population which shows how much energy we have in our country, says Union Minister of State for Youth Affairs and Sports, Shri. Kiren Rijiju while delivering the convocation address at the Second Convocation of Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development in Chennai, today


    While appreciating the students and parents on this occasion he elaborated about the changes happened in the education. In the last years, we have witnessed huge difference in education. Technology has greatly changed the education, not only the learning methodology but the teaching methodology too, he said


    While talking about the several schemes introduced by the government under the leadership of Prime Minister Shri. Narendra Modi, Minister said that there are several initiatives taken by the Government for creating New India, which is not possible without the involvement of youngsters and educational institutions like RGNIYD


    He said that education institutions play an important role in pivoting government schemes for the development of nation


    The only institute in India which deals with the youth affairs and youth development is RGNIYD and this institute is of national importance, he added


    He conveyed his wishes to RGNIYD, to evolve as an International Centre for youth affairs and youth development.
