      1.23 Kg of Gold worthRs. 54.6 Lakhs seized by Customs at Airport. One Arrested
      Posted On: 26 FEB 2020 2:32PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday one Kalandar Maideen Abdul Rahuman, 21,of Ramanathapuram arrived from Kuala Lumpurby Indigo Airlines flight 6E-1818was intercepted atexit.On personal search bundles of gold paste were found concealed in rectum. On extraction 334 grams of gold valued at Rs.15 lakhs was recovered.


    On Monday one Salman Faris, 24, of Ramanathapuram arrived from Singapore via Colombo Srilankan Airlines flight UL-121, was intercepted at exit. On personal search four gold bars weighing 100 grams each and one 10 gram gold cut bit were recovered from his wallet. Total 410 grams of gold valued at Rs. 18.17 lakhs was recovered.


    In another case on Monday one Srilankan lady Fathima Fasmiya, 39, had arrived from Colombo by Srilankan Airlines flightUL-121 was intercepted at exit. On persistent questioning she admitted to have concealed four bundles containing gold paste in her rectum. On extraction 485 grams of gold valued Rs. 21.5 lakhs was recovered.


    In three cases 1.23 Kg of gold of 24 K purity valued at Rs. 54.6 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. One passenger was arrested.


    Further investigation is under progress, according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
