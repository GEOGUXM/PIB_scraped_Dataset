      Diplomatic Outreach event on 21-22 Feb. in Chennai
      Posted On: 19 FEB 2020 5:16PM by PIB Chennai


    The Ministry of External affairs and Tamil Nadu State Government are jointly hosting a “ Diplomatic Outreach’ event focused on ‘Economic and Investment Opportunities in Tamil Nadu’ on 21-22 February 2020 in Chennai.


    Chief Minister Shri. Edappadi K. Palaniswami will inaugurate the event and deliver his address during the inaugural session. It is expected that 50 foreign diplomats and senior government officials will attend.
