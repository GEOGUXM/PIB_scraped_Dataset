      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      SAI to conduct Selection Trials for Atheltics, Kabaddi, Wrestling and Hockey in Mumbai
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2020 6:10PM by PIB Mumbai


    
    Sports Authority of India (SAI) under Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is conducting selection trials for induction of talented sportspersons at Sports Ground, National Centre of Excellence (NCOE), Mumbai during February 23 - 25, 2020. Interested sportspersons (women & men) , willing to take part in the selection trial have to report at SAI Sports Ground, Kandivali, Mumbai on February 23, 2020 at 9 AM. The selection trials are being conducted for four disciplines; Athletics (Sprints, Middle Distance & Long Distance Events), Kabaddi, Wrestling and Hockey (Girls).


    

    Age and performance criteria for various disciplines is available here.


    Attendees/Participants are required to carry the following documents in order to enroll for the 3 day Selection Trials

     1. DOB Certificate
     2. Aadhaar Card
     3. Sports certificates
     4. Medical Fitness Certificate
     5. Character Certificate from the Institute
     6. Education Qualification Certificate
     7. Passport size Photographs (4)

    .
    All documents should be in original as well as attested copies
