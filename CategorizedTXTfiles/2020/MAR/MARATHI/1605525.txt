      Ministry of Textiles

      International Women’s Day to be celebrated in Nashik by Mumbai Office of Development Commissioner for Handlooms
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2020 3:06PM by PIB Mumbai


    Mumbai Office of Development Commissioner for Handlooms under Ministry of Textiles, Government of India will celebrate International Women’s Day in Maharashtra state. One day function for the same will be organised on 8th March in Nashik near Hanuman Mandir, Bajrang Dal Chowk, Nagade in taluka Yeola.


    Various programmes will be organised on that day between 11 a.m. and 5.00 pm in which women weavers will be felicitated. Moreover there will be Interaction with weavers especially women weavers and presentation of local success stories.


    There will also be an Exhibition of designs, samples, products etc. developed by WSC, Mumbai. Various major initiatives taken for development of handloom sector will also be showcased.


    Drawing competition will also be organised on this occasion. It will be inaugurated by Member of Parliament, Dr. Bharati Pawar.


    National Awardees, Sant Kabir Awardees, National Merit Certificate Holders, All India Handloom Board Members, designers are requested to attend this function.
