      Ministry of Railways

      "Cancellation of Weekly Special" Jabalpur-Coimbatore and Coimbture-Jabalpur Train
      Posted On: 18 MAR 2020 5:53PM by PIB Mumbai


    The Railway Board and West Central Railway has decided to cancel the services of Train No. 02198 Jabalpur Jn. - Coimbatore Jn. Weekly Special on 21 st & 28 th March 2020 (Saturday) andTrain No. 02197 Coimbatore Jn. - Jabalpur Jn. Weekly Special on 23 rd & 30 th March 2020 (Monday) due to the outbreak of Pandemic of COVID-19.
