      Covid-19: No public enquiry at regional passport office Cochin and PSKs and POPSKs
      Posted On: 18 MAR 2020 5:38PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    As a precautionary measure to prevent the spread of Novel Corona Virus (Covid-19) , the regional passport officer at Cochin has informed that public enquiry will be stopped at the regional passport office ,Cochin, Passport Seva kendras at Aluva, Alappuzha, Cochin, Kottayam and Thrissur and Post Office Passport Seva Kendras at Chengannur, Kattappana, Olavakkode and Nenmara with immediate effect till further intimation. Further, the office also requested people who have been advised self isolation by health department to desist from visiting Passport Seva Kendras during their quarantine period.
