      Central Media units to celebrate International Women’s day
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2020 2:54PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Press Information Bureau and Regional Outreach Bureau, Thiruvananthapuram, under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting will jointly hold a Round Table on Women – Education, Employment & Empowerment as part of International Women’s Day celebrations. It will be inaugurated by Smt. A. Shainamol IAS, Director, Spices Board, Government of India at 9.30 am at LBS Institute of Technology for Women (LBSITW), Poojappura, Thiruvananthapuram on 07th March 2020.


    Dr. Shiju.P.P, Associate Professor, LBSITW will deliver the keynote address. The participants in the round table include Dr. Gita Gopal, Former Gender Advisor, Govt.of Kerala & Former Lead Evaluation Expert, World Bank, Smt. Babitha Marina Justin, Asso. Professor in English Indian Institute of Space Science & Technology, Smt. Geethanjali Krishnan, Sr. Manager, Technopark & Writer, Ms. Jancy James, Managing Director, Strava Technologies (P) Ltd. Smt. K.A. Beena, Dy. Director, ROB, Thiruvananthapuram will be the moderator.
