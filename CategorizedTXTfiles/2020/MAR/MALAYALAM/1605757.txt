      International Women’s Day Celebrated at CMFRI
      Posted On: 07 MAR 2020 5:57PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    International Women’s Day was celebrated at the Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute (CMFRI) on Saturday. Anuvinda Anil, an MBBS student who donated her liver to her father, was the chief guest at the function. A single girl child, she shared her inspiring story from her life and made a presentation on organ donation.


    The programme was organised by the Women Cell at the CMFRI. Dr Sunil Mohamed, Principal Scientist and Head of Molluscan Fisheries Division of CMFRI presided over the function. Dr Miriam Paul Sreeram and Dr Sandhya Sukumaran also spoke on the occasion.
