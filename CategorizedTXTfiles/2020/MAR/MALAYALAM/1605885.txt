      Regional Dak Adalat
      Posted On: 10 MAR 2020 5:04PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A Regional Dak Adalat of Central Postal Region of Kerala Circle will be held at the Office of the Post Master General, Central Region, Gandhi Nagar (Maveli Road), Kochi on 25th March, 2020 at 11.00 am. Complaints pertaining to the post offices under Central region (ie Post Offices under Mavelikara, Alappuzha, Changanassery, Kottayam, Idukki, Aluva, Ernakulam, Irinjalakuda, Thrissur and Lakshadweep divisions) relating to counter services, savings bank, money orders etc. will be taken up in the adalat.


    Cases taken up in the previous adalats will not be entertained. Complaints can be sent in envelopes super scribed “Regional Dak Adalat: March 2020” and addressed to “ Smt. K. Kalaivani, Assistant Director(PG), Office of the Postmaster General, Central Region, Kochi-682020,” so as to reach there on or before 20th March, 2020.
