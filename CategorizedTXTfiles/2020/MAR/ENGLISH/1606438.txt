      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to Interact with SAARC leaders tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2020 8:50PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will interact with SAARC leaders tomorrow i.e 15 March 2020 at 1700 hrs, through video conference on measures to deal with COVID-19. He will lead India at the video conference of all SAARC nations to chalk out a strong common strategy to fight COVID-19 in the region.
