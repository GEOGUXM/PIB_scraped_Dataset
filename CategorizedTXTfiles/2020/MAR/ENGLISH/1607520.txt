      Prime Minister's Office

      Appeal to observe Janta curfew tomorrow from 7 AM to 9 PM
      
      Appeal to thank the selfless service providers of the nation at 5 PM tomorrow

      Posted On: 21 MAR 2020 6:37PM by PIB Delhi

    In an effort to prevent the spread of the virus, appeal of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi is being recalled to observe Janta curfew tomorrow on 22 March from 7 AM to 9 PM.


    To salute the efforts of those providing selfless service to the nation during this trying times, appeal of PM is being recalled urging citizens to stand at the doors and in the balconies of their houses at 5 pm to morrow and express gratitude by clapping hands or ringing bells for five minutes.
