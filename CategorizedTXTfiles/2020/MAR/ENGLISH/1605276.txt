      Bye-election to the Maharashtra Legislative Council from Dhule-cum-Nandurbar Local Authorities' Constituency
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2020 6:48PM by PIB Delhi


    There is one casual vacancy in the Maharashtra Legislative Council from Dhule-cum-Nandurbar Local Authorities' Constituency. The details of vacancy are as under: -

                                                                                       
Name of Member             Cause of vacancy  Date of Vacancy         Date of retirement
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
Amrishbhai Rasiklal Patel  Resignation       01.10.2019 (afternoon)  01.01.2022
