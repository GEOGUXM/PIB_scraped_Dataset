      Prime Minister's Office

      PM appreciates PM of Nepal
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2020 11:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has appreciated PM of Nepal, Shri KP Sharma Oli for his announcement of contribution to the COVID-19 Emergency Fund.


    “Deeply appreciate PM KP Sharma oli ’s announcement of contribution of NPR 10 crores to the COVID-19 Emergency Fund. It reflects Oli Ji’s commitment and support to the collective fight of SAARC countries against the pandemic ”, the Prime Minister said.
