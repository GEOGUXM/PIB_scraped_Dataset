      Prime Minister exhorts ensuring dignity and safety of women is of utmost importance
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2020 12:04PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today said that the nation has to be built with a strong focus on women empowerment, where there is emphasis on equality and opportunity.


    In a tweet, he said “Our Nari Shakti has excelled in every field”.


    While saying Justice has prevailed, he urged it is of utmost importance to ensure dignity and safety of women.


    Justice has prevailed.
    
    It is of utmost importance to ensure dignity and safety of women.
    
    Our Nari Shakti has excelled in every field. Together, we have to build a nation where the focus is on women empowerment, where there is emphasis on equality and opportunity.

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) March 20, 2020
