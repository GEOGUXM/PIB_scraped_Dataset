      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Bureau of Immigration issues a 24×7 helpline in wake of COVID-19 outbreak in India
      Posted On: 17 MAR 2020 6:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The Bureau of Immigration, under Union Ministry for Home Affairs (MHA), has issued a 24×7 helpline to answer queries regarding Visa and travel restrictions, as well as to facilitate foreigners in India in availing consular services.
