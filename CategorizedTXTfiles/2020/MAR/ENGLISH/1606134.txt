      Ministry of Micro,Small & Medium Enterprises

      Promotion of Village Industries
      Posted On: 12 MAR 2020 5:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of India, through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) functions with 6 Zonal offices located all over the country for promotion of Village Industries. The names of the States/UTs included in the Zones are as follows:-

    This information was given by Shri Nitin Gadkari, Union Minister for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
