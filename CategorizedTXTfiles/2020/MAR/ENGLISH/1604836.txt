      Ministry of Defence

      Infiltration by Terrorists
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2020 3:54PM by PIB Delhi


    There have been 138 cases of infiltration by terrorists along Line of Control/International Border during the year 2019. Due to concerted and synergized efforts of security forces, 157 terrorists were neutralized in the year 2019 in Jammu & Kashmir.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Vijay Pal Singh Tomar in Rajya Sabha today.
