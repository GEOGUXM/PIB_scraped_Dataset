      Mughal Gardens to be Closed for Public Viewing from March 7
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2020 7:17PM by PIB Delhi


    Continuing the precautionary measures at Rashtrapati Bhavan in view of COVID-19 Novel Coronavirus, the Mughal Gardens will close for public from Saturday (March 7, 2020) to avoid any large gathering of people.
