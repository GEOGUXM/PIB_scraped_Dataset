      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Curbing Child Labour
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2020 4:38PM by PIB Delhi


    As per 2011 Census, the number of main workers in the age group of 5-14 years in the country is 43.53 lakh which shows a decline from 57.79 lakh as per 2001 Census.

    Since child labour is a result of socio-economic conditions, a definite timeline for elimination of child labour cannot be ascertained.


    This information was given by Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar Union Minister of State (I/C) for Labour and Employment in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
