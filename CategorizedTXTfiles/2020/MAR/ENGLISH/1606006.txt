      Ministry of Railways

      Women Engine Drivers in Railways
      Posted On: 11 MAR 2020 5:43PM by PIB Delhi


    In the year, 2019-2020 (upto 29.02.2020), a total number of 1,280 women candidates have been empanelled for Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) by Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) against Centralized Employment Notification (CEN) No. 01/2018.
