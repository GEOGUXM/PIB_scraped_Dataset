      Ministry of Home Affairs

      MHA writes to States/UTs to prevent harassment of people from North East by linking them to COVID-19 outbreak in India
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2020 6:45PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Ministry for Home Affairs (MHA) has written to all States/UTs to ensure sensitization of law enforcement agencies to take action against harassment of people from North East, including athletes and sportspersons, by linking them to COVID-19 outbreak in India.
