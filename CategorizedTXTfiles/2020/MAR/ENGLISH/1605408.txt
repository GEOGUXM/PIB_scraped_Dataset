      Election Commission

      Bye-election to the Telangana Legislative Council from Nizamabad Local Authorities' Constituency
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2020 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


    There is a casual vacancy in the Telangana Legislative Council from Nizamabad Local Authorities' Constituency. The details of vacancy are as under: -

                                                                                       
Name of Member      Cause of vacancy                Date of Vacancy  Date of retirement
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
R. Bhoopathi Reddy  Disqualification on 16.01.2019  16.01.2019       04.01.2022
