      PM greets the nation on Navroz
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2020 4:16PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted nation on the occasion of Navroz.


    “Navroz Mubarak! I pray that the coming year is filled with happiness and prosperity. May everyone be healthy and their aspirations be fulfilled ”, the Prime Minister said.
