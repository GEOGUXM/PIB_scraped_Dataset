      Prime Minister Greets People of Rajasthan on its State Day
      Posted On: 30 MAR 2020 11:07AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister has greeted people of Rajasthan on its State Day.


    In a message Shri Narendra Modi said, “People of Rajasthan are known for their courage, bravery and valour. I pray that the State continues incessantly on the path of progress and development”


    राजस्थान दिवस की वहां के सभी भाइयों और बहनों को ढेरों शुभकामनाएं। मेरी कामना है कि साहस, शौर्य और पराक्रम के लिए प्रसिद्ध यह प्रदेश प्रगति की राह पर निरंतर आगे बढ़े।

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) March 30, 2020
