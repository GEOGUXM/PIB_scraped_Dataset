      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to participate in the birth centenary celebrations of ‘Jatir Pita’ Bangabandhu, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, tomorrow via video message
      Posted On: 16 MAR 2020 8:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi shall participate in the 100 th Birth Anniversary celebrations of ‘Jatir Pita’ Bangabandhu, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, in Bangladesh tomorrow via video message .


    Due to COVID-19, the events planned for March 17 in Bangladesh will be without any public gatherings.
