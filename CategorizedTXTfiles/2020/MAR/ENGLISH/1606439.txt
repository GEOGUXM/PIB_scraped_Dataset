      PM expresses anguish over the death of people in an accident in Jodhpur, Rajasthan
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2020 9:03PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi expressed anguish over the death of people in a road accident in Jodhpur. “It is very sad to know about the horrific road accident in Jodhpur, Rajasthan,” he said in a tweet. He expressed his condolences to the families of those who died as well as injured in this accident and wished them a speedy recovery.
