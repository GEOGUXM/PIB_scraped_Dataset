      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Jobs provided to the family members of personnel of paramilitary forces killed in the line of duty under scheme of compassionate appointment
      Posted On: 11 MAR 2020 1:57PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Nityanand Rai, in a written reply in Rajya Sabha to a question regarding provision of jobs for the family members of personnel of paramilitary forces killed in the line of duty said that all deserving cases are considered subject to their willingness and fulfilling required eligibility criteria for the job.


    The number of personnel of CAPFs/AR died in the line of duty during the last 5 years and details of family members who have been provided job under scheme of compassionate appointment is given.
