      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Average Length of Development of Roads
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2020 6:17PM by PIB Delhi


    The average rate of highways construction was 11.67 km per day in financial year 2013-14 and the same was about 30 km per day during 2018-19 and average rate of construction during 2019 -20 till December 2019 is 25.24 km per day. In financial year 2019-20, road projects having length about 3434 km have been awarded and 6940 km length of highways has been constructed till December 2019.


    This information was given by the Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways Shri Nitin Gadkari in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
