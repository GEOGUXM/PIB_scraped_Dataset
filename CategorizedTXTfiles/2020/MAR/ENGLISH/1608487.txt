      PM condoles demise of Satish Gujral
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2020 12:52PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Satish Gujral.


    “Satish Gujral Ji was versatile and multifaceted. He was admired for his creativity as well as the determination with which he overcame adversity. His intellectual thirst took him far and wide yet he remained attached with his roots. Saddened by his demise. Om Shanti.”, Prime Minister said.
