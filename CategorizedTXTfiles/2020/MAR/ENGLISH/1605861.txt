      PM greets the nation on the festival of Holi
      Posted On: 10 MAR 2020 8:51AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi greeted the nation on Holi, a festival of color, enthusiasm and joy. He wished “May this festival bring happiness in the lives of all the countrymen.”
