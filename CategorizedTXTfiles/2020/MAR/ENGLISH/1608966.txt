      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Shri Dharmendra Pradhan donates one month's salary to PM National Relief Fund
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2020 12:43PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of Petroleum and Natural Gas & Steel Shri Dharmendra Pradhan has donated his one month's salary to Prime Minister National Relief Fund for relief work following outbreak of COVID-19. In a Tweet, Shri Pradhan said “In these difficult times, let us stand for those who need us the most. In view of #Covid-19 pandemic, I have donated my one month’s salary and released Rs 1 Crore from my MPLADS fund to PM National Relief Fund to help bring a smile to the underprivileged.”
