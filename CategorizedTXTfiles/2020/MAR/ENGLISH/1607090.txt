      Ministry of Tourism

      Meghalaya government closes down all tourist places in the state till 31st March as a precautionary measure in the light of COVID-19 virus
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2020 2:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of Meghalaya has closed down all Tourist places in the State of Meghalaya till 31 st March, 2020 as a precautionary measure in the light of COVID-19 virus. The state government, in a press release, has also said that there is a possibility that the close down may be extended up to 15 th April, 2020. The state has advised that all tourists intending to visit Shillong and other places in Meghalaya are requested to re-schedule their travel plans accordingly.
