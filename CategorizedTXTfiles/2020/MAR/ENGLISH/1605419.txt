      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to address Economic Times Global Business Summit
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2020 5:40PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will address inaugural session of the Economic Times Global Business Summit on 6th March 2020.Prime Minister shall be addressing delegates from across the world.


    Broad theme of Global Business Summit 2020 is ‘Collaborate to Create: Sustainable Growth in a Fractured World’.
