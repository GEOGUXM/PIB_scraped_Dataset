      PM expresses condolences on the passing away of Rajyogini Dadi Janki Ji
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2020 1:10PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressesed condolences on the passing away of Rajyogini Dadi Janki Ji, the Chief of Brahma Kumaris.


    “Rajyogini Dadi Janki Ji, Chief of the Brahma Kumaris, served society with diligence. She toiled to bring a positive difference in the lives of others. Her efforts towards empowering women were noteworthy. My thoughts are with her countless followers in this sad hour. Om Shanti.”, the Prime Minister said.
