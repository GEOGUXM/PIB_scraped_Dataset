      Prime Minister's Office

      Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi had a telephonic conversation with Prime Minister of Israel
      Posted On: 12 MAR 2020 9:20PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi received a telephone call yesterday evening from the Prime Minister of Israel, H.E. Mr. Benjamin Netanyahu.


    The leaders reviewed the excellent progress in bilateral relations. They also exchanged views on the global situation in the context of COVID-19 pandemic.
