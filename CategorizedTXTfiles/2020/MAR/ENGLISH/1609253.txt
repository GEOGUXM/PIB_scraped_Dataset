      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Government closes certain Immigration Land Check Posts for all Incoming Passenger Traffic located at India's borders with Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan and Myanmar
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2020 8:50AM by PIB Delhi


    Government of India has stopped all types of passenger movements through all immigration Land Check Posts located at India's borders with Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan and Myanmar w.e.f. 0000hrs, March 15, 2020 & at India-Pakistan border w.e.f. 0000hrs, March 16, 2020, except at the posts mentioned in the order attached below.


    In the wake of COVID19 India outbreak, as a precautionary measure to contain and control spread of the disease, the travel and registration for Sri Kartarpur Sahib is temporarily suspended from 0000hrs, March 16, 2020, till further orders.
