      Deploy fiscal resources for additional medical facilities, central instruct state government
      Posted On: 24 MAR 2020 3:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central Government has asked all the State Governments to deploy fiscal resources for establishing additional medical facilities such as hospitals, clinical labs, isolation wards, expanding & upgrading existing facilities to combat the challenge posed by COVID19. These facilities need to be well equipped with ventilators, personal protective equipment (PPEs), masks and drugs to treat the patients.
