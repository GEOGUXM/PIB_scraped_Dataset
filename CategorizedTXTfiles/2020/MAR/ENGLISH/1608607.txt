      PM expresses best wishes to PM of UK
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2020 6:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has expressed his best wishes for good health of PM of United Kingdom, Mr Boris Johnson as he tests positive for COVID 19.


    “Dear PM Boris Johnson, You’re a fighter and you will overcome this challenge as well. Prayers for your good health and best wishes in ensuring a healthy UK” the Prime Minister said.
