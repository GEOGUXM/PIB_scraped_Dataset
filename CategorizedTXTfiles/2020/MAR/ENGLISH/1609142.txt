      Ministry of Home Affairs

      MHA to Private Security Agency Associations: Treat Staff//Workers on Duty, Pay accordingly and protect them from Lay-offs during Lockdown to fight COVID-19
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2020 10:25AM by PIB Delhi


    Union Ministry of Home Affairs has written to the private security agency associations to adopt a humane approach & protect its workers and staff members from lay-offs during nationwide lockdown to fight COVID-19.


    These agencies have been urged to treat their workers on duty and paid accordingly during lockdown period.
