      Ministry of Railways

      Earning of Railways on account of Transportation of Goods
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2020 6:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Railways is earning profit on account of transportation of goods during the last three years. The details are as under:


    (Rs. in crore)

                                         
Year     Cost   Earnings  Profit/Loss (-)
                                         
                                         
                                         
2016-17  64367  104339    39972          
                                         
                                         
                                         
2017-18  71155  117055    45900          
                                         
                                         
                                         
2018-19  76621  127433    50812          
                                         
                                         

    The number of claims registered by Railways was 8,533 during the financial year 2016-17, 7,251 during the financial year 2017-18 and 5,777 during the financial year 2018-19.


    Measures initiated by Railways to prevent arising of claims are as under as under:

     1. Escorting of Goods Train.
     2. Insistence on provision of dunnage to protect flap doors wherever required to prevent leakage of sugar, pulses and oil seeds etc.
     3. Proper marking and labeling of packages to avoid their loss/over carriage.
     4. Claims Inspectors are deputed on trains for monitoring and preventing over-carriage of parcels.

    This press release is based on information given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
