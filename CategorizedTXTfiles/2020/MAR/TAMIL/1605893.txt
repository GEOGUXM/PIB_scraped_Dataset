      1.32Kgs of Gold worth Rs. 60 lakhs and cigarettes worth Rs.4 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 10 MAR 2020 6:16PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday one Mohomed Intizan, 45, and two ladies Niluka Sanjeewani, 41, and Deepika Sandamali Jayasekara, 34, all Sri Lankan Nationals who arrived from Colombo by Sri Lankan Airlines Flight UL-125 were intercepted at the exit. On persistent questioning all three admitted to have concealed bundles containing gold paste in their rectum. Total 12 bundles four from each passenger were recovered. On extraction total 1.32 Kgs Gold valued Rs. 59.80 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    In another case one Mohammed Rafeeque Sajepa, 47, of Kasargod who arrived from Kuala lumpur by Air Asia flight AK 011 was intercepted at exit. On examination of his check in bag, 197 Cartons of Cigarettes , 47 Red Marlobro and 150 White Marlboro valued at Rs.3.94 lakhs were found concealed inside. Cigarettes recovered and seized under Customs Act.


    Further investigation is under progress, according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
