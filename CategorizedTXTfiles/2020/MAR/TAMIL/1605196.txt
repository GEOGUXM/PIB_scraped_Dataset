      2 Kg Gold worth Rs. 91 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport. One lady Arrested
      Posted On: 03 MAR 2020 5:16PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday morning one Jenitha,40, of Chennai who arrived from Dubai by Emirates Flight EK 542 was intercepted at the exit. On personal search four plastic pouches containing gold paste were recovered which were concealed in the waist portion of her pants and inner wear by specially stitching them. On extraction 525 grams of gold was recovered. Also two crude gold bracelets, 2 crude bangles, and one semi-finished crude gold chain totally weighing 279 grams worn by her were recovered. Total 803 grams of gold valued at Rs.34.97 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. She was arrested.


    In another three cases based on specific information Mohamed Akeel,23, Mohamed Arshad,28, and Mohamed Afzal,26, all Srilankan nationals who arrived from Colombo by Sri Lankan Airlines flight UL121 were intercepted at exit. On their personalsearch9 bundles (three from each) containing gold in paste form were recovered which were concealed in their rectum. On extraction 1.292 Kg of gold valued at Rs.56.21 lakhs were recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    In four cases 2.095 Kg gold valued at Rs. 91.18 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. One lady passenger was arrested.


    . Further investigation is in progress , according to a press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
