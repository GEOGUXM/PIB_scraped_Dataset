      Vice President condoles the death of former TN Minister, Shri K Anbazhagan
      Posted On: 07 MAR 2020 4:17PM by PIB Chennai


    The Vice President of India, Shri M Venkaiah Naidu today expressed his profound grief over the passing away of the former Tamil Nadu minister, veteran of Dravidian movement and DMK General Secretary, Shri K. Anbazhagan in Chennai.


    In a condolence message, Shri Naidu said Shri Anbazhagan was known for his erudition and had served with distinction as Social Welfare, Education and Finance Minister. He always strove for the uplift of the downtrodden sections.


    The Vice President conveyed his heartfelt condolences to the bereaved family members.
