      Postponement of Army Recruitment Rally Scheduled at Thiruvannamalai
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2020 6:38PM by PIB Chennai


    Owing to Corona Virus outbreak the Recruitment Rally scheduled from 15-25 April 2020 at Arunai Engineering College. Thiruvannamalai has been postponed. The fresh dates of the rally to be announced soon.
