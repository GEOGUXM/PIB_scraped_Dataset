      The launch of GISAT-1 Postponed
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2020 4:40PM by PIB Chennai


    The launch of GISAT-1 onboard GSLV-F10, planned for March 05, 2020, is postponed due to technical reasons. Revised launch date will be informed in due course, according to the press release issued by ISRO.
