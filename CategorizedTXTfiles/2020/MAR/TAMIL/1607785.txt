      Operations at Passport Office at Chennai (including all Passport Seva Kendra & Post Office Passport Service Kendra) suspended till 31 March 2020
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2020 6:46PM by PIB Chennai


    ln the wake of Corona Virus outbreak and as per the instructions of the government, operations at Passport Office at Chennai (including all Passport Seva Kendra and Post Office Passport Service Kendra) are suspended till 31 March 2020. The tatkal counter and enquiries at the Passport Back Office (at Rayala Towers, Anna Salai, Chennai) are also closed.


    ln view of this, measures suggested by this office may be kindly noted :


    1. Those who have availed appointments already (at PSKs, POPSKs and tatkal) are requested to reschedule it to any date after 31 March 2020.


    2. The rescheduling option is available without any extra cost, and can be done any number of times till the situation normalises.


    3. Those applicants who are scheduled to visit the Passport Back Office (at Rayala Towers, Anna Salai, Chennai) are also requested to postpone it to any date after 31 March.


    4. For all enquiries the public may please contact 044-28513639, 044- 28513640, or address it to rpo.chennai@mea.sov.in according to a press release issued by Shri.Ashok Babu, Regional Passport Officer.
