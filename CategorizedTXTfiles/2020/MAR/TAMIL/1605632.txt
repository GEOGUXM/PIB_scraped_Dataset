      Union Minister for Human Resource Development Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal 'Nishank' met the Minister of School Education of Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2020 7:41PM by PIB Chennai


    Today, the Union Minister for Human Resource Development Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal 'Nishank' met the Minister of School Education of Tamil Nadu at his residence. On this occasion, they had a detailed discussion on greater dissemination of technology education in the State of Tamil Nadu.


    The HRD Minister pleased to know that Education Department of Tamil Nadu is using better use of information technology in providing high level education to students. During the meeting discussions were also held on seeking educational support from IIT-Madras for the betterment of education. They also discussed about the effective use of SWAYAM PRABHA platform to provide quality education to students.
