      Urgent Information For Tourists
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2020 1:21PM by PIB Chennai


    All Tourist places in the State of Meghalaya are closed till 31 st March, 2020 and there is a possibility that this may be extended up to 15 th April, 2020. All tourists intending to visit Shillong and other places in Meghalaya are requested to re-schedule their travel plans accordingly.


    It is stated in a press release issued by the Director, Information & Public Relations Govt. of Meghalaya.
