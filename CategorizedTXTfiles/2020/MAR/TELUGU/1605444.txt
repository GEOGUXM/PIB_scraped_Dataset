      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Balakrishna assumes charge as CBFC Regional Officer
      Posted On: 05 MAR 2020 6:47PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Shri. V. Balakrishna assumed charge as Regional Officer, Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC), Hyderabad today. Balkrishna, an Indian Information Service officer earlier worked in various organizations in the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting as Field Publicity Officer, Editor in Telugu monthly magazine, Yojana. News editor in All India Radio, Deputy Director in Press Information Bureau and He worked as Information Officer to the two vice-presidents, prior to his appointment as Regional Officer, CBFC. Speaking on this occasion, he said, without any delay Censor Certificate will be given.
