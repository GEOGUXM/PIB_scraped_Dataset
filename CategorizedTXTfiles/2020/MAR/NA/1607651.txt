      Prime Minister Pays Tributes to Martyrs on Shaheedi Diwas
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2020 9:40AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi paid tributes to Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru on the occasion of the ShaheediDiwas (Martyrs’ Day) today.


    In a tweet Shri Modi said, “A grateful nation shall always remember the great sacrifice made by them. Jai Hind”


    शहीद दिवस पर मां भारती के महान सपूत वीर भगत सिंह, सुखदेव और राजगुरु को कोटि-कोटि नमन। देश के लिए उनका बलिदान कृतज्ञ राष्ट्र सदा याद रखेगा। जय हिंद!

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) March 23, 2020
