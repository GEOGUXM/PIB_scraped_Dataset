      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      IndianOil bags coveted Sportstar ACES Honour
      
      Gopichand Coach of the year; Rohit Sharma Sportsman of the Year (Cricket)

      Posted On: 14 JAN 2020 6:56PM by PIB Kolkata

    Kolkata, January 14, 2020.


    IndianOil Corporation has been awarded the Best PSU/Corporation for the Promotion of Sports at the annual Sportstar ACES Award ceremony in Mumbai.


    IOCian and India opener Rohit Sharma was adjudged best Sportsman of the Year (Cricket) while Padma Bhushan and Dronacharya Awardee Pullela Gopichand was honoured as the Coach of the Year.Mr.Ranjan Kumar Mohapatra, Director (Human Resources), received the honour on behalf of the Corporation.


    With a 33,000-plus work-force, extensive refining, distribution & marketing infrastructure and advanced R&D facilities, lndianOil has in the past six decades provided energy access to millions of people across the length and breadth of the country through its ever-expanding network of customer touch-points, currently numbering over 50,000. Over the years, IndianOil has recruited over 100 sportspersons including cricketers Rohit Sharma, Cheteshwar Pujara, Ajinkya Rahane; shuttlers P Gopichand, K Shrikant, P Kashyap; athlete Hima Das, paddlers Sharath Kamal, Manika Batra; hockey players Devesh Chauhan, Deepak Thakur, Prabhjot Singh; tennis players Rohan Bopanna, Divij Sharan, Sumit Nagal; and many more.
