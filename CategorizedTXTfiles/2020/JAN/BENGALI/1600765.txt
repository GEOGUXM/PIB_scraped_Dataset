      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Dy. Chairman, Tea Board Announces launch of 5th Session of Foodka on Tea & Tourism across North Bengal
      Posted On: 27 JAN 2020 9:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 27, 2020


    Tea Board India along with the Foodka YouTube channel announced grand launch of Fifth Season of Foodka today in presence of Dy. Chairman, Tea Board, Shri A K Ray. It comprises five episodes on tea and tourism across North Bengal. Foodka channel is spearheaded by renowned RJ Mir and reputed food blogger Indrajit Lahiri.


    Tea Board of India and Foodka brought out a series with five episodes dedicated to both the magic of Darjeeling Tea as well as tourism circuit of the region. With the journey through the tea gardens, the episodes will also highlight the food that goes hand in hand with the exquisite beverage which enthralled world inhabitants.


    The videos can be viewed at the following link:
