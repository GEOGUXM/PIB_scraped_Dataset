      Ministry of Railways

      REGULATION OF SER TRAIN SERVICES DUE TO TRAFFIC-CUM-POWER BLOCK
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2020 4:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 1, 2020.


    In view of Traffic-cum-Power Block on Friday, January 3, 2020 for launching of two Limited Height Subways in Chakradharpur-Rourkela section of Chakradharpur Division, the service of the following S E Railway trains will be regulated:-


    SHORT TERMINATION/SHORT ORIGINATION OF TRAINS


      * 58112 Itwari-Tatanagar Passenger leaving Itwari today will be short terminated at Rourkela tomorrow. Therefore, the service between Rourkela-Tatanagar will remain cancelled that day.


      * 58111 Tatanagar-Itwari Passenger leaving Tatanagar on Friday, January 3, 2020 will be short originated from Rourkela on the same day . Therefore, the service between Tatanagar-Rourkela will remain cancelled on Friday, January 3, 2020 .


    RESCHEDULING OF TRAINS


      * 12871 Howrah-Titlagarh Ispat Express will be rescheduled to leave Howrah at 09.25 a.m. instead of 06.55 a.m. on Friday, January 3, 2020 .


      * 12262 Howrah-Mumbai CSMT Duronto Express will be rescheduled to leave Howrah at 09.55 a.m. instead of 08.20 a.m. on Friday, January 3, 2020 .


    DIVERSION OF TRAIN


      * 18477 Puri-Haridwar Utkal Express leaving Puri tomorrow will run on diverted route via Cuttack-Angul-Jharsuguda Road-Bilaspur instead of Cuttack-Jajpur Keonjhar Road-Bhadrak-Kharagpur-Jharsuguda-Bilaspur. The service of this train will remain cancelled between Jajpur Keonjhar Road-Jharsuguda tomorrow .
