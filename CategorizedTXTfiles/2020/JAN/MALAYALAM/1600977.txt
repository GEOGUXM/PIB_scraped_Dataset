      SMS being sent to passport holders
      Posted On: 29 JAN 2020 4:53PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) has commenced sending SMS to passport holders before the expiry of their passport starting September 2019. Two SMS are being sent to the concerned passport holders-one before the nine month and another before seven month of the date of expiry of their passport. As part of the SMS , to help avoid the citizens falling prey to the fake websites in the cyberspace, the citizens are also being informed about the authenticate website of the ministry www.passportindia.gov.in for online submission of application related to passport services.


    The sample SMS being sent to citizens is as follows:-


    “Your passport KXXXXXXX will expire on XX-Feb-20. Apply reissue at www.passportindia.gov.in or mPassport Seva App. “
