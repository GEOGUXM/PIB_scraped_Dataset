      Civilian Athletic Championship at Naval Base
      Posted On: 28 JAN 2020 5:50PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    40 th Southern Naval Command â€œCivilian Athletic Championship 2019-2020 commenced on 27 Jan 20 at Naval Base, Kochi. The chief guest for the opening ceremony was Vice Admiral Anil Kumar Chawla, PVSM, AVSM, NM, VSM, ADC, Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Southern Naval Command.


    300 participants including about 90 women, representing more than 4,000 Civilian employees working in various units of Southern Naval Command are competing for top honours in various track and field events. The participating teams include Naval Ship Repair Yard (NSRY), Naval Armament Depot (NAD) Aluva, INS Venduruthy, Naval Aircraft Yard (NAY) Kochi, Material Organisation, and INS Zamorin, Ezhimala. Athletic events include 100 mtr, 200 mtr and 400 mtr run, Broad jump, High jump, Tug of War, Javelin Throw etc. for both men and women, and 5 Km run, Pole Vault, Triple Jump, 10 Km walk etc. exclusively for male participants.


    The closing ceremony of the meet will be on 29 Jan 2020 with Rear Admiral MD Suresh, AVSM, NM, Chief of Staff, Southern Naval Command as the Chief Guest who will also give away the prizes and trophies.


    The Annual Athletics Championship is yet another of a series of activities regularly undertaken at the Naval Base, Kochi towards improving sportsmanship, enhancing morale and increasing motivation amongst its large civilian personnel.
