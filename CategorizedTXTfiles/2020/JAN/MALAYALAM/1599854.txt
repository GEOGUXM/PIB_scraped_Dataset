      Passport Mela in Thrissur, Aluva and Cochin
      Posted On: 20 JAN 2020 4:37PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Passport Seva Kendras (PSK), in Aluva, Cochin and Thrissur will remain open on 25 th January, 2020 on account of passport mela. Normal applications with prior online appointments will be accepted. Appointments are now available online and can be booked through passportindia.gov.in or mPassport Seva App. For more information, contact 94477 31152
