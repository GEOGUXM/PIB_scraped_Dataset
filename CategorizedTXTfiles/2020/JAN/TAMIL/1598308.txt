      BSNL Presents Christmas and New Year Offer
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2020 6:02PM by PIB Chennai


    BSNL has launched attractive Christmas and New Year offer for its subscribers with 60 days extra validity in Plan 1999 which gives unlimited calls to any network, 3 Gb/day data and 100 SMS/ day now for 425 days instead of 365 days. Plan 1999 comes with additional features like BSNL Tunes an BSNL TV subscription free for 365 days. This offer will be available from 25 th December 2019 to 31 st January 2020.


    BSNL has recently launched another attractive voucher for Rs.97/- which offers unlimited calls to any network, 2 Gb/day and 1000 SMS along with BSNL TV subscription for 18 days.


    It is also informed that BSNL is committed to offer excellent services at affordable price to its subscribers, according to the press release issued by Mr.Sheetla Prasad, DIR (CM), BSNL
