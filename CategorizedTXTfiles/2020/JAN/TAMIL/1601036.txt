      BIS RAIDS A LED LIGHTING COMPANY IN CHENNAI - MISUSING COMPULSORY REGISTRATION MARK
      Posted On: 29 JAN 2020 6:31PM by PIB Chennai


    A team of Officials from Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), Southern Regional Office (SRO), Chennai 600 113 conducted an Enforcement Search and Seizure operation on 28 January 2020 at M/s LUVINA Lighting Company, 1 st floor, Thatha Muthiappa Street, Chennai-600001(Broadway), Tamil Nadu on information of suspected violation of BIS Act, 2016 and misuse of BIS Compulsory Registration Mark and BIS Logo.


    During the operation, about 3,500 Nos. of LED lights of various types such as LED Tubelight, LED Spot Light, LED street light, and LED flood light with BIS Compulsory Registration Mark and BIS Logo were seized which is a violation of Section 17(3) of BIS Act, 2016.


    Action is being initiated by BIS SRO Chennai against the offender under the Bureau of Indian Standards Act, 2016. The offence is punishable with imprisonment up to two years or a fine up to Rs. 2,00,000/- but may extend up to ten times the value of goods or articles produced or sold or offered to be sold or affixed or applied with a Standard Mark, including Hallmark or both as per Section 29 of BIS Act, 2016 for first Contravention.


    General public are, therefore, requested that in case they come across any instances of violation of Quality Control Order, the same may be intimated to the BIS Southern Regional Office, CIT Campus, 4 th Cross Road, Taramani, Chennai-600 113. Such complaints can also be made by E-Mail at the address sro@bis.org.in or by Fax to 044-22541087. The source of such information will be strictly kept confidential.
