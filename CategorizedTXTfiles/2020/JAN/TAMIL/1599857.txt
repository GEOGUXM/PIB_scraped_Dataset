      Shri. S.Venkateswar assumes charge as Director General, (South) PIB
      Posted On: 20 JAN 2020 5:00PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri S. Venkateswar, has assumed charge as Director General (South) PIB, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India in Chennai today. He will head all media units in Southern India, including Press Information Bureau, Regional Outreach Bureau and will also be Additional Press Registrar of Registrar of Newspaper of India (RNI).


    A 1989 batch officer of Indian Information Service (IIS) , he will be in charge of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala , Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Lakshwadeep, Andaman & Nicobar and Puducherry.


    Prior to this, Shri Venkateswar, was Director General PIB (Telangana Region). He had earlier worked in Doordarshan, AIR, Press Information Bureau , and DAVP across India in various capacities.


    Shri Venkateswar had also served as Commisioner Information and Public Relations Department, Government of Andhra Pradesh.
