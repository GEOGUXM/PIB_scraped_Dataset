      1.44 Kg gold worth Rs. 59 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport. One arrested
      Posted On: 09 JAN 2020 12:40PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday night one Lasantha Shanka, 28, a Srilankan national who had arrived from Colombo by flight No. AI 274 was intercepted at the exit. On persistent questioning, he admitted to have kept concealed gold in paste form in rectum. On personal search 4 bundles containing gold in paste form were recovered .On extraction, 431 grams was valued at Rs. 17.63 lakhs was recovered and seized.


    On Wednesday Abdul Kareem,41, of Ramanathapuram , Mohamed Ruzni ,27,of Srilanka arrived from Colombo by Flight UL125 and Sivaguru,33, of Trichy arrived from Dubai by Indigo flight 6E 066 , were intercepted at the exit. On persistent questioning, they admitted to have kept concealed bundles containing gold paste in rectum. On personal search 9 bundles were recovered and on extraction 1013 grams gold valued at Rs. 41.45 lakhs was recovered and seized . One passenger was arrested.


    In four cases 1.44 Kg gold valued at Rs. 59 lakhs was seized under Customs Act 1962. One passenger was arrested


    Further investigation is under process, according to the press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai.
