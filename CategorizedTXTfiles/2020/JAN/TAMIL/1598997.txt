      US Dollars worth Rs. 20.6 lakhs seized by Customs at Airport
      Posted On: 10 JAN 2020 12:18PM by PIB Chennai


    On Wednesday night based on specific information four female passengers Mahalakshmi, 33, of Coimbatore, Sharmila, 33, of Chennai, Saravanaselvi, 45, of Tiruppur who were bound for Kualalumpur by Srilankan Airlines flight No. UL-124 we re intercepted by AIU officers in the departure terminal, when they were proceeding towards security hold after clearing immigration on suspicion of carrying foreign currency. On questioning they gave evasive replies so the ir personal search as well as search of their hand bags was carried out. On examination of their hand bags 29220 US Dollars in denominations of 100, 50 and 20 equivalent to Rs. 20.6 lakhs were found concealed in side. Further, Rs 31000 in Indian currency notes was also recovered from their handbags. The foreign as well as Indian currency was seized under the provisions of Customs Act, 1962 read with FEMA 2015.


    Further investigation is under progress according to the press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai.
