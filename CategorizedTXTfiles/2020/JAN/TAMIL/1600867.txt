      1.5 Kg Gold worth Rs. 63 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 28 JAN 2020 7:06PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday three lady passengers Saburammal,31, Samira,35, and Shakila banu,41,, all hailing from Tiruchirappalli, arrived from Colombo by Srilankan airline flight UL125, were intercepted at the exit . On persistent questioning, they admitted to have concealed gold paste in rectum . On personal search 9 bundles containing gold in paste form were recovered. On extraction , 1.1 Kg of gold valued at Rs. 46.65 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    In one case on Monday one Thajudeen, 28, hailing from Pudukkottai who arrived from Kualalumpur by Malaysian Airlines Flight No. MH-182, was intercepted at the exit.On persistent questioning the passenger admitted to have concealed bundles containing gold paste in his rectum and ejected four bundles through normal course.On extraction 401 grams valued at Rs. 16.34 lakhs was recovered and the same was seized under Customs Act 1962.


    In total 1.5 Kg gold valued at Rs. 63 lakhs seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further Investigation is under progress, according to a press release issue by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport, Chennai.
