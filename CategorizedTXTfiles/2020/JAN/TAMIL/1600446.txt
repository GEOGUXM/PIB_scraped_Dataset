      Destruction of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances
      Posted On: 24 JAN 2020 5:22PM by PIB Chennai


    On 24.01.2020, the officers of the Chennai Customs Zone destroyed approximately 29 Kgs of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic substances. The Narcotic Substances included Ganja, Methaqualone, Cocaine, Heroin and pseudo Ephedrine, the seized drugs are required to be destroyed after completing investigation and other legal formalities, under the provisions of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985.. The estimated market value of all the drugs is Rs. 8 Crores. The total quantity was destroyed in the presence of a High level Drug Disposal committee constituted by senior level IRS officers.


    According to a press release issued by Joint Commissioner of Customs, Chennai-III, Custom House, Chennai, the destruction proceedings were carried out in compliance of the General Statutory Rules 38(E) dated Jan 16 2015 and under supervision & guidance of the Principal Commissioner of Customs, Chennai-III, Shri M.M. Parthiban


    The release further says that this office had destroyed 275.23 Kgs of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic substances on 17.12.2019.
