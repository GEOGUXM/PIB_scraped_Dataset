      Employees Provident Fund Organisation conducts “Nidhi Aapke Nikat”(PF Near You)
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2020 4:13PM by PIB Chennai


    Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation is conducting “Nidhi Aapke Nikat”(PF Near You) at Regional office, Chennai on 10 th of every month for speedy redressal of grievances of Subscribers/Employers covered under the Employees’ Provident Funds & Miscellaneous Provisions Ac, 1952.


    Accordingly, the “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” for the month of January 2020 will be conducted on Friday, the 10th January, 2020 at the premises of Regional Office, Chennai.


    The Subscribers / Pensioners / Employers who are having any grievances can walk in directly on the above said date with relevant documents for getting their grievances redressed, according to a press release issued by Shri. Rituraj Medhi, Regional Provident Fund Commissioner-I, Chennai.
