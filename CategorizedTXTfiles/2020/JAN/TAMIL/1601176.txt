      1.35 Kg gold worth Rs. 57 lakhs seized by Customs at Airport. One Arrested
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2020 6:50PM by PIB Chennai


    On Thursday one Ajmal Tahseen,23, from Chennai arrived from Dubai by Emirates Airlines Flight EK 544, was intercepted the exit. On search of person 3 bundles containing gold paste and one bundle containing crude gold chain weighing 49 grams, were found concealed in his underwear. On extraction 663 grams of gold was recovered. In total 712 grams of gold valued at Rs 30 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. Passenger was arrested.


    Earlier on Wednesday night three passengers, Abdul Majeedh, 36, Ubaidur Rahman, 37, and Sabeer Ali, 37, all from Chennai had arrived from Colombo by Srilankan Airlines Flight No. UL-123 were intercepted at the exit. On persistent questioning all three admitted to have concealed bundles containing gold paste in rectum. Six bundles were recovered and on extraction 555 grams recovered. Also 3 gold rings one each were recovered from their pant pockets.Total 645 grams of gold valued at Rs. 27 lakhs was recovered and seized under customs Act 1962.


    In total 1.35 Kg gold worth Rs.57 lakhs seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further Investigation is under progress, according to a press released by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport, Chennai.
