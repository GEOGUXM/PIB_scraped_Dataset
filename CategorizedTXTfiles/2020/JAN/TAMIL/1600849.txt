      PIB Headquarters

      Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation
      Posted On: 28 JAN 2020 6:23PM by PIB Chennai


    It is informed that Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation is conducting “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” (PF Near You) at Regional office, Chennai on 10th of every month for speedy redressal of grievances of Subscribers/Employers covered under the Employees’ Provident Funds & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.


    Accordingly, the “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” for the month of February 2020 will be conducted on Monday, the 10th February, 2020 at the premises of Regional Office, Chennai.


    The Subscribers / Pensioners / Employers who are having any grievances can walk in directly on the above said date with relevant documents for getting their grievances redressed, according to a press release issued by Mr. Rituraj Medhi, Regional Provident Fund Commissioner-I, Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation, Chennai.
