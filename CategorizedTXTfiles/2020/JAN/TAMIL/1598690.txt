      2.65 Kg Gold worth Rs. 1.10 Crore seized by Customs at Chennai Airport. Three Arrested.
      Posted On: 08 JAN 2020 12:10PM by PIB Chennai


    On Monday night one Ashaff Ali Khan, 51, of Srilanka arrived from Colombo by flight UL123, and Mohamed Azarudeen, 21, and Kalandar Abbas, 36, both of Ramanathapuram arrived from Colombo by Indigo Flight 6E1204 were intercepted at the exit . On persistent questioning, they admitted to have concealed gold paste in rectum. On personal search 10 bundles containing gold in paste form were recovered.


    On Tuesday morning one Mohamed Ramzeen,51, of Srilanka, and Thajilamin, 41, of Ramanathapuram arrived from Colombo by Flight UL125 were intercepted at the exit of Chennai International Airport. On persistent questioning, they admitted to have concealed gold paste in rectum, in rubbery spread form. On personal search 7 bundles containing gold in paste form were recovered.


    In another case on Tuesday one Nagoor Gani ,22, of Chennai, who had arrived from Sharjah by Air India Flight AI968, was intercepted at exit. On personal search two gold cut bits weighing 60 grams were recovered from his pant pocket. On persistent questioning he admitted to have concealed bundles containing gold in paste form in rectum and on personal search 3 bundles were recovered.


    In total six cases, 20 bundles containing gold in paste form recovered and on extraction , 2.65 Kg of gold valued at Rs. 1.10 Crore was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. Three passengers were arrested.


    Further Investigation is under progress according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
