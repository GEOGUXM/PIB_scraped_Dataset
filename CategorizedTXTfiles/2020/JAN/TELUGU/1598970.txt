      Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat – Celebration of Unity in Diversity of our Nation- Abdul Azeem, Director, Youth Services
      Hyderabad is a mini Bharat- S. Venkateswar, DG, PIB
      
      
      
      Telangana –Haryana paired for Inter State Youth Exchange Program

      Posted On: 09 JAN 2020 7:00PM by PIB Hyderabad

    “Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat” is to celebrate the Unity in Diversity of our Nation said Sri Mohammed Abdul Azeem, IAS, Director of Youth Services, Telangana in Inter State Youth Exchange Program by Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan, Telangana State.


    Sri Nazeem said EBSB through this innovative measure, the knowledge of the culture, traditions and practices of different States & UTs will lead to an enhanced understanding and bonding between the States, thereby strengthening the unity and integrity of India.


    Sri S.Venkateswar, Director General, PIB said Hyderabad is a Mini Bharat, where different cultures, different food habits exists. He further said State level pairings, the activity will be very useful to link various States and Districts in annual programmes that will connect people through exchanges in areas of culture, tourism, language, education trade etc. and citizens will be able to experience the cultural diversity of a much larger number of States/UTs while realizing that India is one. Various activities are organized and planned to promote the national integration through Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat by Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, he added.


    Telangana and Haryana states are paired for Inter State Youth Exchange Program, the program started on 9 th January and will continue up to 23 rd January, 2020.
