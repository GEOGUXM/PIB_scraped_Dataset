      AADHAAR SEVA KENDRA OPENS IN WARANGAL, TELANGANA
      Posted On: 16 JAN 2020 6:49PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Aadhaar Seva Kendra operated by Unique ldentification Authority of lndia (UlDAl) has begun operations in Warangal, Telangana. (Address : Kandakatla gateway, Kakatiya University Cross Roads, Nayeem Nagar, Warangal - 506009). This centre has a capacity to handle upto 500 Aadhaar Enrolment or Updates every day. People can avail Aadhaar related services like Enrolment and Updations of Address, Name, Gender, Date of Birth, Mobile number, E-mail !D and Biometric updations including photo, finger print and iris.


    UIDAI has also provided facility to book appointments online. 'The residents can book appointment, by visiting the URL ask.uidai.gov.in , where one can choose their convenient date and time to avail services at the Aadhaar Seva Kendra. The Aadhaar Seva Kendra (ASK) is open from 9:30 am to 5:30 pm, on all 7 days of the week including Sundays. The ASK is a fully air-conditioned centre with adequate seating capacity.


    Aadhaar Enrolment, and Mandatory Biometric update of children of 5 and 15 years, including any other update done along with it, is free of cost. For all other updates/ corrections, a nominal fee of Rs 50 will be charged per transaction.


    Apart from residents of Warangal, people from other areas also can avail Aadhaar related services at this Aadhaar Seva Kendra.
