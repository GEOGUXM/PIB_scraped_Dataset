      "EK BHARAT SHRESTHA BHARAT PROGRAM"
      Posted On: 17 JAN 2020 7:21PM by PIB Hyderabad


    As part of "Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat” cultural exchange program, a program was organized here today. Shri Venkatesh Goud, who has won the National Youth Award, addressed the participants and spoke about the importance of sanitation, and clean surroundings. Swacch Bharat can be achieved only if all the citizens maintain clean surroundings, he said.


    Participants from different districts – showcased various dance forms like Gusadi Tribal dance, lambada, banjara and also Bharatanatyam dances.


    Telangana state is paired with Haryana for the "Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat Program".
