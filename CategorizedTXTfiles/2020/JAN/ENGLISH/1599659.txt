      Nominations for Sardar Patel National Unity Award-2020 Open till 30th April 2020
      Posted On: 17 JAN 2020 1:23PM by PIB Delhi


    The online nominations process for Sardar Patel National Unity Award, the highest civilian award in the field of contribution to the unity and integrity of India, is on and the last date for nominations is 30 th April, 2020. The nominations are being received online in the Ministry of Home Affairs website https://nationalunityawards.mha.gov.in .


    Government of India has instituted the award in the name of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. The Award seeks to recognize notable and inspiring contributions to promote the cause of national unity and integrity and to reinforce the value of a strong and united India.
