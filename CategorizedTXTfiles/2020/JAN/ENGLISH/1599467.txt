      Rashtrapati Bhavan and Mughal Gardens to remain close for Public Viewing on January 23, 25 and 26
      Posted On: 15 JAN 2020 5:16PM by PIB Delhi


    Rashtrapati Bhavan and Mughal Gardens will remain close for public viewing on January 23, 25 and 26, 2020 in view of the Republic Day Parade 2020.
