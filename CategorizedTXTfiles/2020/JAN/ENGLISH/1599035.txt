      No ‘Change of Guard’ Ceremony between Jan 18 and Feb 02, 2020
      Posted On: 10 JAN 2020 4:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Change of Guard Ceremony on the Forecourt of Rashtrapati Bhavan will not to be held between January 18 and February 02, 2020 (January - 18, 19, 25 & 26, 2020 and February 02, 2020) due to the rehearsals of the forthcoming Republic Day Parade/Beating Retreat Ceremony 2020 and the Rashtrapati Bhavan Divas Celebrations.
