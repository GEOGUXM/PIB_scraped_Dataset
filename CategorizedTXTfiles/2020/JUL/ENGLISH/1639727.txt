      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of Professor C. S. Seshadri
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2020 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


        The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Professor C. S. Seshadri.


        In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, "In the passing away of Professor C. S. Seshadri, we have lost an intellectual stalwart who did outstanding work in mathematics. His efforts, especially in algebraic geometry, will be remembered for generations. Condolences to his family and admirers. Om Shanti."
