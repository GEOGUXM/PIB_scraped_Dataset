    Union Home Minister Amit Shah, in his tweet expressed his greetings to all Chartered Accountant (CA) professionals on 'Chartered Accountants Day' today.


    Shri Amit Shah added that “India is proud of its talented CA fraternity which has been playing a pivotal role in nation building.


    Union Home Minister also emphasized that “their immense contribution in India's economic growth and development is exceptional”.


    Greetings to all CA professionals on 'Chartered Accountants Day'.

    
    India is proud of our talented CA fraternity which has been playing a pivotal role in nation building. Their contribution in India's economic growth and development is exceptional.
