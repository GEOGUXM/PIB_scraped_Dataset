      PM condoles the passing away of US Congressman John Lewis
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2020 12:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of US Congressman John Lewis.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, "We mourn the loss of US Congressman John Lewis, a champion of civil rights, non-violence and Gandhian values. His legacy will continue to endure and inspire."
