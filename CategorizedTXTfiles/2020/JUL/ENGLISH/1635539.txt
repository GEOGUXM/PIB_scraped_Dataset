      PM salutes doctors on the occasion of Doctors’ Day
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2020 10:27AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted doctors on the occasion of Doctors’ Day.


    In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, "India salutes our doctors- exceptional care givers who are at the forefront of a spirited fight against COVID-19. doctorsday2020.”


    India salutes our doctors- exceptional care givers who are at the forefront of a spirited fight against COVID-19. #doctorsday2020 pic.twitter.com/WsWroXjVpO
