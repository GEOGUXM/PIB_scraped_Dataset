      Posted On: 21 JUL 2020 12:08PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah expressed his deep grief over the demise of the Governor of Madhya Pradesh, Shri Lalji Tandon. In a tweet the Union Home Minister said Shri Lalji Tandon's entire life was devoted to public service.


    Shri Amit Shah added that as a public servant, Shri Lalji Tandon has left a deep impression on Indian politics and his death is an irreparable loss for the country.


    The Union Home Minister prayed to God for peace of the departed soul and expressed his condolences to his family.
