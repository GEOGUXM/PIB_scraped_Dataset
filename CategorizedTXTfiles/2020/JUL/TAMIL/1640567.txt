      NABARD Sanctions Rs.2485 Cr to Government of Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2020 10:17AM by PIB Chennai


    Nabard has sanctioned Rs.2485 crore for three projects under Rura l Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) and NABARD Infrastructure Development Assistance (NIDA) for two Combined Drinking Water Supply Schemes for Salem and Sivagangai Districts with an amount of Rs.2066 crore to provide drinking water for a population of 17.50 lakh.


    Rs.419 crore sanctioned for Establishment of Advanced Institute for Integrated Research on Livestock and Animal Sciences (AIRLIVAS) at Thalaicasal, Salem District, according to a press release issued by Shri. S. Selvaraj, Chief General Manager, NABARD Tamil Nadu Regional Office.
