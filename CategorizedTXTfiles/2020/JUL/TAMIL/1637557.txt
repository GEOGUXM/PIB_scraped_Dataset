      SHRI M V S D PRASADA RAO, Scientist-G , The New - Deputy Director General For Bureau of Indian Standards (Southern Region), Chennai
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2020 4:50PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri. MVSD Prasada Rao, Sc-G has assumed the Charge today (09 July 2020) as Scientist-G & Deputy Director General (Southern Region), Bureau of Indian Standards, Southern Regional Office, Chennai. The Jurisdiction of Southern Regional Office covers the State(s) of Tamil Nadu, Puducherry, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telengana and Union Territories of Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep.


    In BIS, Shri MVSD Prasada Rao has worked in various capacities in Delhi, Hyderabad and Vizag.


    Prior to assuming charge as Deputy Director General (Southern Region), he had discharged his duty as DDG (Management Systems Certification, FMCD & HaIlmarking ) at New Delhi, according to a press release issued by Shri H.Ajay Khanna, Deputy Director-PR, BIS, Chennai.
