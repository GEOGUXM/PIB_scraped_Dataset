      Issuance of fake Appointment letter to the post of Income Tax Officer
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2020 4:49PM by PIB Chennai


    It is noticed that certain messages are being circulated in Social Media including WhatsApp, regarding issuance of Appointment letter to the post of Income Tax Officer by Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, Delhi. In this regard, it is hereby clarified that the post of Income Tax Officer is filled purely on promotion and there is no direct recruitment for the said post. Further, it is also clarified that the recruitment to the various non-gazetted posts in Income Tax Department is carried out by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) only and in respect of gazetted posts in the Income Tax Department, the recruitment process is carried out by Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) through their respective official websites as per the prescribed procedure. The members of Public are requested to check the official websites of SSC / UPSC, as the case may be, or contact their offices, in case of any doubt about the recruitment process.


    The Public are requested not to fall prey to such spurious messages spread in social media including in WhatsApp , according to a press release issued by Shri. P. DHIVAHAR, Additional Commissioner of Income Tax (HQ), Admin & TPS, (i/c) O/o the Pr. CCIT, TN & P, Chennai.
