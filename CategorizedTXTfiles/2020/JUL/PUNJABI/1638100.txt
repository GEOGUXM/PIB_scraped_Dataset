      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Update with regard to status of Indigenous Vaccine trials in the country
      Posted On: 10 JUL 2020 3:14PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, July 10, 2020


    This is with reference to queries being raised in a certain section of media regarding status of Indigenous Vaccine trials in the country.


    In this regard, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India has clarified that Drugs Controller General of India (DCGI) has permitted two Indigenous Vaccine Candidates for Phase I & Phase II of the clinical trials. The sites where trials will be taking place have been finalised. The trials may be starting soon and the outcomes of trials shall be shared as and when received by the Ministry.


    The update related to the same has been shared on official twitter Handles - @ROBChandigarh & @PIBChandigarh.
