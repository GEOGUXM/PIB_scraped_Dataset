      People in the region welcome the extension of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2020 7:15PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, July 1, 2020


    People in the region have welcomed the decision of the Union Government to extend Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana till the end of November. In his recent address to the nation, Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi said keeping in mind the requirements as well as expenditure increase as several festivals are expected in coming months, the government has decided to extend the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana.


    Anil Kumar from Mahendergarh district in Haryana expressed his gratitude for extending the PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna. Appreciating the decision, he says this would provide a lot of relief to the poor. Ved Prakash, a beneficiary, while showing his gratitude to union government for extending the PM Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana upto Diwali, says he has already been receiving free rations for which he is grateful. Another beneficiary and a daily wager from Haryana exuding relief said that he would continue to get free foodgrains till Diwali and thanked the government for the same. Balbir Singh said the government is providing every help to the poor during these times and this extension for another five months is a welcome step.

    During this five-month period, more than 80 crore people will be provided 5 kg free wheat/rice per month. Along with providing 5kg free rice/wheat to each member of a family, 1 kg free whole chana will also be provided to each family per month. The government will spend more than Rs 90,000 crore towards the extension of the scheme, the Prime Minister also said, adding that if the amount spent towards it in the previous three months is added together, a total of almost Rs 1.5 lakh crore would be spent towards the scheme.
