      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      PIB Chandigarh sets up a Fact Check Unit to counter Fake News
      Posted On: 13 JUL 2020 7:14PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, July 13, 2020


    A Regional Fact Check Unit has been established by Press Information Bureau, Ministry of I&B, Govt. of India at Chandigarh. The objective of the unit is to fact check any news related to central government, which is specific to the states of Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh and UT Chandigarh and may be incorrect or misleading.


    Fact Check Units have been set up by the Press Information Bureau (PIB), Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India across the country to arrest the circulation of factually incorrect/doctored news over different modes of media, which often mislead people on various issues. The mandate of these Fact Check Units is to provide official/authentic version of any such news by making available the factually correct information in public domain.


    An email id pibfactcheckchandigarh@gmail.com has also been created to receive queries/ news stories from the region from public that may need to be fact checked.
