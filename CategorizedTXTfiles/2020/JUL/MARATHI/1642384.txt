      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Proposals from Institutions invited to run coaching programme for SC/ST
      Posted On: 30 JUL 2020 6:07PM by PIB Mumbai


    Nagpur, 30 July 2020


    The office of the National Career Service Centre (NCSC) under Directorate General of Employment & Training, Nagpur working under Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India invited applications from institutions which can train the job seekers from Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribes (SC/ST) in Typewriting, Shorthand (English), General Awareness, General English and Computer operation. The institute has designed a free stipendiary special coaching programme covering above things for SC/ST job seekers. The duration of course organized by National Career Centre will be of 11 months absolutely free of cost likely to start from 1 st Septemebr 2020.


    This training is to be implemented free of cost for SC/ST candidates through Institutions recognized by State Government/Public Welfare Department etc. as per the norms/conditions/criteria.


    The willing institutions are therefore requested to contact the Sub Regional Employment Officer, National Career Service Centre for SC/STs, New Administrative Building No.1, 5th Floor, Civil Lines, Nagpur-440001 on or before 10 August 2020.
