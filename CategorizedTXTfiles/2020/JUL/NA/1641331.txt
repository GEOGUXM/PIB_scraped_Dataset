      PM pays homage to armed forces on Kargil Vijay Diwas
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2020 10:45AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid homage to armed forces on Kargil Vijay Diwas.


    "On Kargil Vijay Diwas, we remember the courage and determination of our armed forces, who steadfastly protected our nation in 1999. Their valour continues to inspire generations", the Prime Minister said.
