      Meghalaya Police registers complaint for fake letter on social media
      Posted On: 10 JUL 2020 7:28AM by PIB Imphal


    Meghalaya Police has registered complaint against unknown people for circulating a fake letter on social media allegedly written by the Editor in Chief of Shillong Times with malicious content addressed to dignitaries of Centre and State Government. The letter is going viral in social media.


    The Meghalaya Police has clarified that the letter dated 9th March, 2020 was not penned by the Editor of Shillong Times and a complaint has been lodged about the same at the Cyber Crime Police Station wherein a criminal case has been registered and under investigation. Meghalaya Police has also stated that the netizens must refrain from circulating the forged letter and any person found to be doing the same will be liable for criminal action as per law.


    Fake and misleading news has become a major menace. It is requested that people should avoid posting any such content as it would invite strict action by law.
