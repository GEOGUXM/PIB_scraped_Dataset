      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S CONTINUES ITS EFFORT TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2020 5:09PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 04 July, 2020


    To transport essential commodities in different parts of the country since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER)’s freight services are running round the clock transporting food grains, fertilizer, coal, minerals and different other consumable inputs.


    ER has run 321 freight trains on Friday with the breakup as given below:-


    Ash – 4 Rakes


    Balt – 4 Rakes


    Cement – 23 Rakes


    Clinker – 9 Rakes


    Container – 19 Rakes


    Coal & Mineral – 74 Rakes


    Fertilizer – 8 Rakes


    Jute – 1 Rake


    Maize – 8 Rakes


    Onion – 1 Rake


    POL – 4 Rakes


    Rice – 5 Rakes


    Salt – 2 Rakes


    Sand – 2 Rakes


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Stone – 20 Rakes


    Wheat – 7 Rakes


    Besides, 126 empty rakes were run at different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
