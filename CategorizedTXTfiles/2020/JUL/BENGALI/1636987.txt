      ER’S FREIGHT SERVICES OPERATE ON ROUND THE CLOCK BASIS TO SUPPLY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 07 JUL 2020 4:42PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 07 July, 2020


    Eastern Railway’s (ER)’s freight services are going on round the clock basis to ensure supply of essential commodities and other items in different parts of the country ever since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown. Freight trains are transporting food-grains, sugar, edible oil, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, steel, coal & minerals.


    The freight loading/unloading performance on July 6, 2020 is as follows:-


    TOTAL LOADING – 58 RAKES


    Coal – 23 Rakes


    Steel – 4 Rakes


    Cement – 4 Rakes


    Ash – 1 Rake


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Stone – 14 Rakes


    Ballast – 4 Rakes


    Sand – 4 Rakes


    Slag – 2 Rakes


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 66 RAKES


    Ballast – 2 Rakes


    Cement – 8 Rakes


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Coal & mineral – 39 Rakes


    Fertilizer – 3 Rakes


    Food-grains – 3 Rakes


    Petroleum oil – 3 Rakes


    Salt – 1 Rake

    Everywhere social distancing & other health & hygiene stipulations were maintained as per Railway protocol.
