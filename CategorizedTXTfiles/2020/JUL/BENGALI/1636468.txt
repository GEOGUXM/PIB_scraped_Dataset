      ER’S ASANSOL DIVISION LAUNCHES “ASAN AROGYA APP” FOR HOSPITAL INFORMATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2020 6:29PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 04 July, 2020


    For better management of hospital records and extending facility of hassle- free appointment with the doctors and access to other medical facility for the ailing staff and their family members, Eastern Railway (ER)’s Asansol Division has launched a Hospital Information Management System App named "ASAN AROGYA" which is available in Google Play Store. It is an Android based mobile app for booking doctor's appointments, obtaining lab reports, cancel / re-schedule of doctor's appointment. Those who will use this App will be able to get appointments with the doctors and other facilities at their fingertips through mobile phone without any need of reaching the hospital for getting the doctor’s appointment or lab reports etc. The App also contains other features like viewing appointment history, see important medical related notices, addition of family members etc.
