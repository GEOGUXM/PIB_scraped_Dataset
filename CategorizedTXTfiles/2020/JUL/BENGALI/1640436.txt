      Ministry of Railways

      ER FREIGHT SERVICES ON TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2020 5:20PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 22 July, 2020


    To maintain the backend supply, Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains on round the clock basis to transport essential commodities in different parts of the country since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown. The freight trains are carrying food-grains, fertilizer, coal & mineral, onion, salt, iron & steel, POL, container, clinker, cement, ballast, ash, sand, stone, maize to cater to the demand of consumables and other inputs.


    ER has run a total of 256 freight trains on July 21, 2020 with breakup given as follows:-


    Ash – 2 Rakes


    Automobile - 1 Rake


    Ballast & Railway Material Consignment - 1 Rake


    Cement - 1 Rake


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container - 15 Rakes


    Coal and mineral - 79 Rakes


    Edible Oil – 2 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 12 Rakes


    Onion – 4 Rakes


    Maize – 7 Rakes


    POL – 3 Rakes


    Food-grains & Rice – 1 Rake


    Jute - 1 Rake


    Slag - 1 Rake


    Sand – 1 Rake


    Steel – 5 Rake


    Stone – 8 Rakes


    Wheat – 3 Rakes


    Besides, 105 empty rakes were operated to the different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
