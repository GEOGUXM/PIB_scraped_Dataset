      Ministry of Railways

      ER DIVERTS ROUTE OF A TRAIN
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2020 5:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 01 July, 2020


    In connection with the pre non-interlocking & non-interlocking programme at the Baruipara station, the 02023 UP Howrah – Patna Special train, leaving Howrah tomorrow will be diverted via Bandel. This has been informed by the CPRO’s office of Eastern Railway today in a press release.
