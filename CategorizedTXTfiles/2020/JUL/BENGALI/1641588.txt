      Ministry of Railways

      SER CANCELS HOWRAH-MUMBAI CSMT SPECIAL BETWEEN HOWRAH AND ROURKELA
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2020 7:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 27 July, 2020


    Due to the complete lockdown in West Bengal on July 29, 2020 (Wednesday) South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided that 02810 Howrah-Mumbai CSMT Special which was earlier rescheduled to leave Howrah on that day will short originate from Rourkela at 02:11 pl on July 30, 2020.


    Hence, the service of the train from Howrah to Rourkela will remain cancelled on July 29, 2020.


    The earlier announcement regarding the departure of this train as per rescheduled timing from Howrah stands cancelled informed the SER authority.
