      Ministry of Railways

      WITHDRAWAL OF STOPPAGES OF HOWRAH-SECUNDERABAD-HOWRAH SPECIAL
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2020 9:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 09 July, 2020


    In view of growing cases of COVID-19 in Ganjam District of Odisha, South Eastern Railway (SER) authority has decided to withdraw the stoppages of 02703/02704 Howrah-Secunderabad-Howrah Special at Balugaon and Brahmapur with immediate effect till further order.
