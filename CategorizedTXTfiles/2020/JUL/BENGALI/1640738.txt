      Ministry of Railways

      ER EXTENDS RUNNING OF PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS FOR COVID-19 TILL DECEMBER 14
      Posted On: 23 JUL 2020 8:02PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 23 July, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) has run time tabled Parcel Express trains in different phases in different routes, to carry essential commodities including the consumables, perishables, medical equipment, medicines etc during the COVID-19 lockdown period to support the need of time for swift transport of those materials to different corners of the country. Presently, the Parcel Express trains originating from ER are continuing between Howrah & Guwahati, Sealdah & Guwahati and Howrah & Amritsar.


    The running of 00303/00304 Howrah-Guwahati-Howrah (Bi-weekly), 00313/00314 Sealdah-Guwahati-Sealdah (Bi-weekly), 00463/00464 Howrah-Amritsar-Howrah (Bi-weekly) and 00467/00468 Howrah-Amritsar-Howrah ( Weekly) Parcel Express trains have been extended till December 14, 2020 on their nominated days of run, to facilitate swift transport of essential commodities.


    ER started operation of Parcel Express trains from April 10, 2020. Till July 22, 2020 the total trips originating from ER counts to 475 and transported about 17183 Tons of consignment.


    These parcel traffic of ER are carrying essential commodities like, the consumables, the garments as well as the medicines, the medical equipment and the PPE materials. The running of Parcel Express trains has helped in maintaining the supply chain to meet the market demand as well as medicines, medical equipment, PPE materials transported by these trains helped in combating the spread of COVID-19.
