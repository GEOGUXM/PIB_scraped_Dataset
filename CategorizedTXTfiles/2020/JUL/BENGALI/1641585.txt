      Ministry of Railways

      SHORT TERMINATION / SHORT ORIGINATION OF 02308/02307 JODHPUR – HOWRAH – JODHPUR SPECIAL TRAIN
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2020 7:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 27 July, 2020


    In view of the lockdown declared on Wednesday, July 29, 2020 in entire West Bengal, 02308/02307 Jodhpur – Howrah – Jodhpur Special train will be short terminated at / short originated from Dhanbad announced Eastern Railway (ER) as follows :


    i) 02308 Jodhpur –Howrah Special train commencing journey from Jodhpur on July 27, 2020 will short terminate at Dhanbad.


    ii) 02307 Howrah – Jodhpur Special train scheduled to commence journey from Howrah on July 29, 2020 will originate from Dhanbad instead of Howrah.


    The ER has requested all the passengers to plan their journey accordingly.
