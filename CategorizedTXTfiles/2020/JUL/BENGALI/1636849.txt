      Ministry of Railways

      ER REDUCES FREQUENCY OF 02303/02304 HOWRAH – NEW DELHI - HOWRAH SPECIAL (VIA PATNA) & 02381/02382 HOWRAH – NEW DELHI - HOWRAH SPECIAL (VIA DHANBAD)
      Posted On: 06 JUL 2020 7:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 July, 2020


    Considering the request of West Bengal Government in view of the emergent COVID-19 situation, the frequency of 02303/02304 Howrah – New Delhi – Howrah Special (via Patna) and 02381/02382 Howrah – New Delhi – Howrah Special (via Dhanbad) which were earlier introduced from June 1, 2020 onwards as per schedule of Poorva Express has been reduced to weekly announced Eastern Railway (ER). The reduced frequency of these trains will be as follows:-

    Both the above pairs of trains will run as per schedule till July 10, 2020 Ex. Howrah and till July 11, 2020 Ex. New Delhi. Thereafter, these trains will run as per above revised schedule till further advice.


    Passengers are requested to plan their journey accordingly.
