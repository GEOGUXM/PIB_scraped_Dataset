      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES EXTENSIVE SANITISATION TO PREVENT SPREAD OF COVID-19
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2020 5:02PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 July, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously carrying on extensive sanitisation at the stations, the compartments of Special Trains with special emphasis to high contact areas, Guard Vans, Driver Cabs, Rest Rooms, compartments of Patrolling Special Trains, as well as all the other working places using, sodium hypochlorite solution and other disinfectants to prevent spread of COVID-19.


    Extensive Sanitisation of the coaches of 02301/ 02302 Howrah-New Delhi-Howrah AC Special Train continuing at Howrah Coaching Yard and at the platform before departure of the train (02301) from the Howrah station.


    As a precautionary measure against spread of COVID-19 virus, all the passenger interface areas like the seating benches in the concourse and the waiting halls, the door handles, the different touch surfaces etc. are cleaned and disinfected on a regular basis at the stations like Howrah, Sealdah, Asansol, Malda, Kolkata terminal, Bandel, Barddhaman, Rampurhat etc. Besides, an Automatic Bag Sanitiser Machine has been installed at the Asansol station and more such machines are under the in-house development for installing at the other stations of ER.


    Sanitisation of the coaches with special emphasis for sanitisation of the high contact areas of 02377/02378 Sealdah-New Alipurduar Special train at the Sealdah Coaching Depot going on to prevent the spread of COVID-19.


    Such extensive sanitisation effort has been taken by ER to make the train ride safer both for the passengers as well as the staff.
