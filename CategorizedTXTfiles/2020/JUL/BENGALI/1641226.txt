      Ministry of Railways

      ER CANCELS SOME SPECIAL TRAINS DUE TO LOCKDOWN IN WB ON JULY 29
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2020 6:01PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 25 July, 2020


    In view of the lockdown declared in entire West Bengal on July 29, 2020 by the State Government, The Eastern Railway (ER) has cancelled the following trains:


    1) 02024 Patna–Howrah Special Train journey commencing on July 29, 2020.


    2) 02023 Howrah–Patna Special Train journey commencing on July 29, 2020.


    3) 02302 New Delhi–Howrah AC Special Train journey commencing on July 28, 2020.


    4) 02301 Howrah–New Delhi AC Special Train journey commencing on July 29, 2020.


    5) 02377 Sealdah-New Alipurduar Special Train journey commencing on July 27, 2020 & July 29, 2020


    6) 02378 New Alipurduar-Sealdah Special Train journey commencing on July 28, 2020 and July 30, 2020


    7) 02201 Sealdah-Bhubaneswar Special Train journey commencing on July 27, 2020


    8) 02202 Bhubaneswar-Sealdah Special Train journey commencing on July 28, 2020


    9) 02213 Shalimar-Patna Special Train journey commencing on July 29, 2020


    10) 02214 Patna-Shalimar Special Train journey commencing on July 28, 2020.


    The ER has hence, requested the passengers, to plan their journey accordingly.
