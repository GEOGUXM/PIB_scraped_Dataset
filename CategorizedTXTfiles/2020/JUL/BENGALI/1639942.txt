      Ministry of Railways

      ER FORMS BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT UNIT TO ENHANCE FREIGHT TRAFFIC
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2020 5:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 July, 2020


    A Business Development Unit (BDU) has been formed at Eastern Railway (ER) Headquarters, to offer prospective customers of Freight Traffic for loading of the miscellaneous non-bulk goods enhancing their rail transportation share of traditional commodities as well as introducing new commodities for transporting through Railways.
