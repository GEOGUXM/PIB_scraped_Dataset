      Ministry of Communications

      Department of Posts Issues Clarification Regarding Appointment in PA or SA Post, Alerts all Regarding Fictitious Letters
      Posted On: 06 JUL 2020 5:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 06 July, 2020


    In connection with the fictitious letters issued to many persons with ill motif, on behalf of Department of Posts intimating their selection in the post of Postal Assistant (PA) or Sorting Assistant (SA) on the basis of Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) examination, the department has issued a clarification stating the actuality.


    The Assistant Director of Postal Services (Rectt.) from the Office of Chief Postmaster General, West Bengal Circle, Shri P Roy stated in a press release that everybody should keep in mind that CHSL Exam is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) and hence all concerned should follow the results published by the SSC in its official website, Employment News and other employment related newspapers and not be misguided by fake letters from unscrupulous persons.


    Shri Roy added that the Department of Posts takes action duly notifying in India Post website after the result is issued by SSC, for information of the concerned candidates. Hence, all concerned have been advised to follow SSC Website as the candidates recommended by SSC are required to consult India Post Website for further instruction.
