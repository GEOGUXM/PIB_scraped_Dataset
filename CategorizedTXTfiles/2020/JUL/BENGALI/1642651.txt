      SER TO CONTINUE RUNNING OF PARCEL EXPRESS TRAIN BETWEEN SHALIMAR AND PORBANDAR
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2020 7:14PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 31 July, 2020


    With a view to ensure uninterrupted supply of essential commodities, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to continue the running of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains between Shalimar-Porbandar-Shalimar (00914/00913).


    This Tri-Weekly Parcel Express train will leave Shalimar every Wednesday, Friday & Sunday from August 5, 2020 to September 2, 2020 and will leave Porbandar every Monday, Wednesday & Friday from August 3, 2020 to August 31, 2020.


    The Shalimar-Porbandar Parcel Express (00914) leaving Shalimar at 9.00 pm will reach Porbandar at 6.25 pm on the third day. In the return direction, the Porbandar-Shalimar Parcel Express (00913) leaving Porbandar at 08.00 am will arrive Shalimar at 06.00 am on the third day. The Parcel Express train will stop at Mecheda (Up direction only), Panskura(Up direction only), Kharagpur, Tatanagar, Chakradharpur, Rourkela and Jharsuguda under SERâ€™s jurisdiction.
