      Ministry of Railways

      CANCELLATION OF TRAINS DUE TO LOCKDOWN
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2020 10:03PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 24 July, 2020


    In view of Complete Lockdown on 25.07.2020 and 29.07.2020 in West Bengal, the following trains will remain cancelled:-


     1. 02021/02022 Howrah-Barbil-Howrah Special leaving Howrah and Barbil on 25.07.2020 and 29.07.2020 will remain cancelled.


     1. 02073/02074 Howrah-Bhubaneswar-Howrah Special leaving Howrah and Bhubaneswar on 25.07.2020 and 29.07.2020 will remain cancelled.
