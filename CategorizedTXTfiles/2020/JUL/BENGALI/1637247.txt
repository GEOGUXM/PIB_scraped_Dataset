      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 08 JUL 2020 5:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 08 July, 2020


    To maintain the backend supply, Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains on round the clock basis to transport essential commodities in different parts of the country since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown. The freight trains are carrying food-grains, fertilizer, coal & mineral, onion, salt, iron & steel, POL, container, clinker, cement, ballast, ash, sand, stone, maize to cater to the demand of the consumables and other inputs.


    Eastern Railway has run a total of 240 freight trains on July7, 2020. The breakup is as follows:-


    Ash – 3 Rakes


    Automobile – 2 rakes


    Ballast & RMC – 4 Rakes


    Cement – 22 Rakes


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container – 14 rakes


    Coal & mineral – 69 Rakes


    BOC – 1 Rake


    Fertilizer – 6 Rakes


    Foodgrain – 3 Rakes


    Iron & Steel – 8 Rakes


    Maize – 11 Rakes


    Onion – 1 Rake


    POL - 5 Rakes


    Sand – 3 Rakes


    Slag – 2 Rakes


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    Stone – 16 Rakes


    Besides, 65 empty rakes were run out to different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
