      Ministry of Railways

      ER REDUCES FREQUENCY OF SEALDAH – BHUBANESWAR SPECIAL
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2020 9:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 09 July, 2020


    The frequency of 02201/02202 Sealdah–Puri–Sealdah special (SHORT-TERMINATED AT BHUBANESWAR) will be reduced from three days a week to two days a week from July 13, 2020 ex. Sealdah and from July 14, 2020 ex. Bhubaneswar.


    The bi-weekly 02201 Sealdah–Bhubaneswar special will leave Sealdah on Monday & Friday instead of existing Monday, Wednesday & Friday. 02202 Bhubaneswar–Sealdah special will leave Bhubaneswar on Thursday & Saturday as against existing Tuesday, Thursday & Saturday.


    Passengers are requested to plan their journey accordingly.
