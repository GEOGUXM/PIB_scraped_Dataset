      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT SERVICES ON TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 02 JUL 2020 6:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 02 July, 2020

    To maintain back end supply, Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains on round the clock basis to transport essential commodities in different parts of the country since the beginning of the nation-wide lockdown. The freight trains are carrying foodgrains, fertilizer, coal & mineral, onion, salt, iron & steel, POL, container, clinker, cement, ballast, ash, sand, stone, maize to cater to the demand of people as well as industry.


    ER has run a total of 280 freight trains on Wednesday with the breakup given as follows:-


    Ash – 2 Rakes
    Automobile – 3 rakes
    Ballast – 4 Rakes
    Cement – 23 Rakes
    Clinker – 7 Rakes
    Container – 15 rakes
    Coal & mineral – 82 Rakes
    Fertilizer – 7 Rakes
    Food grain – 10 Rakes
    POL - 4 Rakes
    Iron & Steel – 14 Rakes
    Maize – 10 Rakes
    Onion – 1 Rake
    Salt – 2 Rakes
    Sand – 2 Rakes
    Slag – 3 Rakes
    Stone – 15 Rakes

    Besides, 76 empty rakes were operated to the different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities. Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were given top priority following the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
