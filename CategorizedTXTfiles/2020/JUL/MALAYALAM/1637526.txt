      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Webinar organized on ‘developing immunity’
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2020 2:26PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    
    
    In view of post COVID-19 scenario, Field Outreach Bureau,under Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Wayanad today (09-07-2020) organized a webinar on ‘ How to develop proper immunity’. Around 60 NSS volunteers from Markaz Arts and Science College, Valanchery participated in the programme. Dr. Sugesh Kumar G.S., Medical Officer, Maruthonkara Govt. Ayurveda Dispensary talked about how we can develop proper immunity to prevent various deceases. The class also focused on basic precautionary measures against COVID-19. Dr. C.P. Muhammad Kutty, Principal, Valanchery Markaz Arts & Science College, Shri Ashkar Ali, NSS programme officer, Shri Prajith Kumar, Field Publicity Officer, Wayanad, Shri C. Udayakumar were also present.
