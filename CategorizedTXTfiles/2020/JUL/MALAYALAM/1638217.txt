      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Webinar on stabilising immunity during COVID times
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2020 8:21PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Field Outreach Bureau, Ernakulam and Mahatma Mathrubhumi Study | Circle, Thiruvankulam, Ernakulam jointly organised a Webinar today on stabilising immunity during COVID times. Shri.M.Swaraj MLA inaugurated the Webinar. Dr.Vishnu Mohan, Medical Officer under AYUSH Wellness Centre lead the class.More than 165 participants from the state and abroad attended the webinar.


    Smt.K.A.Beena, Deputy Director,Regional Outreach Bureau, L.C.Ponnumon, Field Exhibition Officer, M.R.Amal, President, Mahatma Study Circle Unit , M.Renjith Kumar, Coordinator, P.P.Ajimon, advisory member also spoke on the occasion.
