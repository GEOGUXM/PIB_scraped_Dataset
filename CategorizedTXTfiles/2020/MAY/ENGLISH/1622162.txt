      Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh speaks with Japan Defence Minister over phone on mitigating COVID-19
      Posted On: 08 MAY 2020 5:58PM by PIB Delhi


    Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh today held a telephone conversation with Minister of Defence, Japan Mr Taro Kono.


    The two Defence Ministers had discussions on their respective responses against the COVID-19 pandemic.
