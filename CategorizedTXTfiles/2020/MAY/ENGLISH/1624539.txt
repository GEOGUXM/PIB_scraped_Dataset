      Prime Minister's Office

      PM announces ex-gratia for the next of kin of those who lost their lives due to road accident in Auraiya, UP
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2020 9:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has approved an ex- gratia of Rs. 2 lakh each from Prime Minister’s National Relief Fund for the next of kin of those who have lost their lives due to the road accident in Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh.


    The Prime Minister has also approved Rs. 50,000 each for those injured due to the road accident in Auraiya, Uttar Pradesh.


    An ex-gratia of Rs 2 lakh each for the next of kin of those who lost their lives due to the unfortunate accident in Auraiya, UP has been approved from the PM's National Relief Fund. Rs 50,000 each for the injured has also been approved.
