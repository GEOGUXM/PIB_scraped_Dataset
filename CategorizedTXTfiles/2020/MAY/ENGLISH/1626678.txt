      PM greets nation on Eid-ul-Fitr
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2020 8:55AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the occasion of Eid-ul-Fitr.


    "Eid Mubarak!


    Greetings on Eid-ul-Fitr. May this special occasion further the spirit of compassion, brotherhood and harmony. May everyone be healthy and prosperous", the Prime Minister said.
