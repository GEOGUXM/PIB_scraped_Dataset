      Ministry of Finance

      Cessation of 7.75 percent Savings (Taxable) Bonds, 2018
      Posted On: 27 MAY 2020 8:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government of India hereby notifies that the 7.75 percent Savings (Taxable) Bonds, 2018, issued as per Notification F.4(28)-B(W&M)/2017, dated January 03, 2018 , shall cease for subscription with effect from the close of banking business on Thursday, the 28 th of May, 2020.
