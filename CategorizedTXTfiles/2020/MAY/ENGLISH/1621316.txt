      President of India pays floral tributes to Giani Zail Singh on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 05 MAY 2020 7:45PM by PIB Delhi

    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid floral tributes to Giani Zail Singh, former President of India, on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (May 5, 2020). He paid his respects before the portrait of Giani Zail Singh. **** VRRK/KP
