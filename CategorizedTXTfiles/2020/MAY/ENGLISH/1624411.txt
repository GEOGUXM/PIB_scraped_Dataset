      PM greets people of Sikkim on their Statehood Day
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2020 4:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Sikkim on their Statehood Day.


    “Greetings on Sikkim’s Statehood Day. Home to talented and compassionate people, Sikkim has enriched national progress in many sectors. Sikkim’s progress in areas like organic farming have been admired all over. Praying for Sikkim’s progress in the years to come”, the Prime Minister said.
