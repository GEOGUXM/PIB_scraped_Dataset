      PM condoles the demise of Hockey player Balbir Singh Sr
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2020 2:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Hockey player Padma Shri Balbir Singh Sr.


    "Padma Shri Balbir Singh Sr. Ji will be remembered for his memorable sporting performances. He brought home lots of pride and laurels. Undoubtedly a brilliant hockey player, he also made a mark as a great mentor. Pained by his demise. Condolences to his family and well wishers", the Prime Minister said.
