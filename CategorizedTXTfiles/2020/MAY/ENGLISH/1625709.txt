      Ministry of Earth Science

      Super Cyclonic Storm ‘AMPHAN’ (at 1200 Hrs IST) lay as a Cyclonic Storm over Bangladesh
      Posted On: 21 MAY 2020 1:26PM by PIB Delhi


    The Super Cyclonic Storm ‘AMPHAN’ (pronounced as UM-PUN ) moved north-northeastwards with a speed of 10 kmph during past 06 hours as a Cyclonic Storm and lay centered at 0830 hrs IST of today, the 21 st May 2020 over Bangladesh near Lat. 24.7°N and Long. 89.5°Eabout 270 km north-northeast of Kolkata, 150 km south of Dhubri and 110 km south-southeast of Rangpur (Bangladesh).


    It is very likely to continue to move north-northeastwards and weaken further into a Deep Depression during next 03 hours and into a Depression during subsequent 06 hours.
