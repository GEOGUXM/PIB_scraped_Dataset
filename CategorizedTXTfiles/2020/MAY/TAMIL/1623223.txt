      Chennai Customs facilitates #VandeBharatMission flight from Malaysia on Monday
      Posted On: 12 MAY 2020 1:54PM by PIB Chennai


    Chennai Customs has facilitated smooth clearance for the passengers of the fourth repatriation flight as part of the Vande Bharat Mission at Chennai Airport on 11th May. The flight Air India Express IX 687 from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia landed at 11:10 PM on Monday at Chennai International Airport.


    The evacuation flight carried 180 passengers including 58 men and 119 women and 03 Infants. There were three passengers on wheelchair. All passengers were facilitated for smooth customs clearance.


    Due to the Covid-19 pandemic situation, all the international flights have been grounded since 22nd March.
