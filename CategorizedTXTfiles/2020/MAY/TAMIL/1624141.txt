      #VandeBharatMission flights from Manila and London land at Chennai
      
      Chennai Customs facilitates smooth clearance for all passengers

      Posted On: 15 MAY 2020 7:10PM by PIB Chennai

    Chennai Customs has facilitated smooth clearance for the 500 passengers of the two Vande Bharat repatriation flights from Manila and London which landed at Chennai on Thursday night and Friday morning respectively.


    The flight Air India AI 1379 from Manila, Philippines landed at 2205 Hrs on Thursday 14th May at Chennai International Airport carrying 167 passengers of which 110 were male and 57 were female passengers. This was the eighth relief flight under the Vande Bharat Mission landing at Chennai.


    The second flight Air India AI 1570 from London, UK via Mumbai landed at 0755 Hrs on Friday 15th May carrying 333 passengers including 202 males, 126 females and 5 Infants. This was the ninth flight that reached Chennai as part the Vande Bharat evacuation mission.


    All the passengers of both the aircrafts were facilitated smooth customs clearance by Chennai Airport Customs while observing due Covid-19 protection measures.


    Due to the Covid-19 pandemic situation, all the international flights to and from India have been grounded since 22nd March.
