      Ministry of Science & Technology

      Nehru Science Centre, Mumbai organizes lectures for the general public via YouTube on 27th & 28th May 2020
      Posted On: 26 MAY 2020 2:25PM by PIB Mumbai


    Nehru Science Centre is organizing two very important lockdown virtual lectures via YouTube LIVE on 27th and 28th May 2020 on YouTube Channel.


    Shri Rajeev Nair, Former Director, Indian Meteorological Department, Mumbai will deliver a lecture on “The Fury of Cyclone Amphan: on Wednesday 27th May 2020 at 12.30 pm and the second lecture by Dr. Anita Sengupta, Research Associate Professor of Astronautics, University of South California, USA and Former NASA Rocket Scientist will be on “SpaceX Maiden Astronaut Flight” on 28th May at 9.30 am


    Shivaprasad M. Khened, Director, Nehru Science Centre, Mumbai,(Ministry of Culture, Government of India) appeal to the students and general public to kindly join live on YouTube channel (http://www.youtube.com/c/NSCMumbai). The lectures can be attended from your workplace/home as per above-given details.
