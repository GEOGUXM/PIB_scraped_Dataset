      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS ARE CARRYING ESSENTIALS TO DIFFERENT AREAS
      Posted On: 06 MAY 2020 5:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 6 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains as well as Parcel Express trains on round the clock basis to maintain supply of essential commodities at different corners of the country during lock down period. ER freight trains carrying food grains, sugar, fertiliser, cement, stone, petroleum products etc are reaching at different areas to cater to the demand of consumables and other inputs.


    ER has run a total of 170 freight trains on Tuesday. The details are as follows:


    Coal and Mineral – 44 Rakes


    Container – 19 Rakes


    Rice - 6 Rakes


    Wheat - 1 Rake


    Fertiliser – 4 Rakes


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    Steel - 3 Rakes


    POL – 2 Rakes


    Cement – 19 Rakes


    Clinker – 3 Rakes


    Stone - 6 Rakes


    De-oiled Cake – 1 Rake


    Ash – 2 Rakes


    Parcel – 11 Rakes


    Besides, 48 empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation had been done wherever required.
