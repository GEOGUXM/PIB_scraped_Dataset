      Ministry of Railways

      WHEELS OF FREIGHT TRAINS ROLLING ON ROUND THE CLOCK IN ER NETWORK
      Posted On: 04 MAY 2020 4:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 04 May, 2020


    In an endeavour to maintain the supply of essential commodities Eastern Railway (ER) has put all-out effort to run freight trains over its network on day and night basis. Freight trains in ER network carrying essential commodities and other inputs viz. Rice, Wheat, Sugar, Fertiliser, Coal & Minerals, Steel, Cement, Coal etc for maintaining availability of essential items at the market of different areas during the lockdown period.


    THE LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON SUNDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING: 44 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral - 26 Rakes


    Container - 4 Rakes


    Rice - 4 Rakes


    Wheat – 1 Rake


    Cement – 3 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 1 Rake


    Slag - 1 Rake


    Clinker – 2 Rake


    Steel – 1 Rake


    Railway Material Consignment - 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING: 40 Rakes


    Coal – 22 Rakes


    Container- 3 Rakes


    Stone -10 Rakes


    Cement -3 Rakes


    Ballast- 2 Rakes


    Forty one empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Proper sanitization was also done wherever required.
