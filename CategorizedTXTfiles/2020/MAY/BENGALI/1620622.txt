      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN ONE TRIP OF PARCEL EXPRESS TRAIN FROM HOWRAH TO VASCO-DA-GAMA (GOA)
      Posted On: 03 MAY 2020 4:51PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 3 May, 2020


    In view of nationwide lockdown due to the outbreak of COVID-19 and considering the heavy demand for transportation of essential commodities, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to run one trip of Time Tabled Parcel Express Train from Howrah to Vasco-da-Gama (Goa). This Parcel Express Train will carry bulk number of essential commodities, medicines, pharmaceutical equipment and edible items to serve the people of various parts of Southern and Western India.


    The Parcel Express (00638) leaving Howrah at 12 noon hrs on May 5, 2020 will reach Vasco-da-Gama at 12.30 pm. on May 7, 2020. There are some important intermediate stoppages for loading and unloading of essential commodities, vegetables, hard parcel, poly bags, cotton materials, fruits to ferry to different places. The Parcel Express will stop at Mecheda, Panskura, Kharagpur and Balasore under SER’s jurisdiction. This Parcel Express train will also stop at Bhadrak, Khurda Road, Visakhapatnam, Rajahmundry, Vijayawada, Guntur, Nandyal, Dhone, Guntakal, Ballari, Hosapete, Koppal, Gadag, Hubballi and Madgaon stations en route.
