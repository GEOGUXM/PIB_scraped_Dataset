      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS RUNNING ROUND THE CLOCK TO MAINTAIN THE SUPPLY CHAIN
      Posted On: 10 MAY 2020 5:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 10 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is running freight trains 24 hours to maintain the supply chain of essential commodities and other inputs at different areas during the lockdown period. Freight rakes containing rice, maize, onion, sugar, salt, edible oil, fertilizer, POL products, coal, minerals, cement etc are operating to transport these articles to different corners of the country.


    ER has run a total of 184 freight trains on Saturday. The details of which are as follows:


    Cement – 17 Rakes


    Clinker – 6 Rakes


    Container - 12 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 47 Rakes


    Edible Oil - 2 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 3 Rakes


    POL product – 7 Rakes


    Rice - 9 Rakes


    Maize - 1 Rake


    Onion – 2 Rakes


    Railway Material Consignment - 2 Rakes


    Steel – 5 Rakes


    Stone – 12 Rakes


    Salt – 1 Rake


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    TAR – 1 Rake


    Parcel – 9 Rakes


    Forty seven empty rakes were run out to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
