      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO TRANSPORT ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES TO VARIOUS CORNERS OF THE COUNTRY
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2020 4:42PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 17 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously carrying on its freight services in the midst of the lockdown period to maintain supply of essential commodities, consumables as well as other inputs at different areas, informed railway authority in a press release. It also says, ER is operating its freight trains seamlessly round the clock transporting foodgrains, sugar, pharmaceutical, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, stone, steel and coal for power generating sector. The ER has run 205 freight trains on Saturday. The detail is as follows:


    Ash - 2 Rakes


    Cement-12 Rakes


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container- 11 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 50 Rakes


    Fertiliser- 7 Rakes


    Maize- 1 Rake


    Onion- 2 Rakes


    POL product - 3 Rakes


    Rice – 11 Rakes


    Railway Material Consignment- 5 Rakes


    Steel- 6 Rakes


    Stone- 13 Rakes


    Sugar – 4 Rakes


    Parcel – 8 Rake


    Besides, 66 empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Proper sanitisation was done wherever required.
