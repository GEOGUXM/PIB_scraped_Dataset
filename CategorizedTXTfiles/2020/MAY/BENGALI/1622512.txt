      Ministry of Railways

      ER RUNNING ROUND THE CLOCK FREIGHT SERVICES TO MAINTAIN SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 09 MAY 2020 6:08PM by PIB Kolkata


      Kolkata, 9 May, 2020


      To maintain supply of essential commodities and other inputs at different areas during nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) has taken all-out effort to run freight trains round the clock, transporting food grains, sugar, pharmaceuticals, fertilizers, petroleum products, minerals, steel, cement etc and coal for power generating sector.


      TOTAL LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON FRIDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:


      TOTAL UNLOADING: 46 Rakes


      Cement - 6 Rakes


      Clinker – 1 Rake


      Container-1 Rake


      Coal and Mineral – 28 Rakes


      De-Oiled Cake – 1 Rake


      Fertiliser -1 Rake


      POL product -1 Rake


      Railway Material Consignment – 1 Rake


      Rice-3 Rakes


      Steel-1 Rake


      Sugar-2 Rakes


      TOTAL LOADING: 51 Rakes


      Coal – 23 Rakes


      Steel – 3 Rakes


      Container – 1 Rake


      Stone – 14 Rakes


      Cement – 6 Rakes


      Ballast- 3 Rakes


      Ash – 1 Rake


      Forty five empty rakes were run out to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


      Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation is also done wherever required.
