      Ministry of Railways

      SER UNDERTAKES MASSIVE CLEANLINESS & SANITISATION DRIVE TO CONTAIN SPREAD OF CORONAVIRUS
      Posted On: 07 MAY 2020 5:43PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 7 May, 2020


    In view of the tough challenge posed by COVID-19, South Eastern Railway (SER) has undertaken massive cleanliness and sanitization drive on regular measure to prevent the pandemic. Multitude precautionary measures have been taken by SER to support its workforce with disinfected infrastructures and clean environment and this is a continuous process. Daily cleaning, sanitisation & disinfection of all offices, stations, depots, colonies, control offices etc. are being undertaken. This intensive cleanliness and sanitisation drive is being monitored at apex level. The employees who are engaged in this mission are advised to maintain social distancing. Thermal screening of these staff is carried out daily and they are also counseled to adopt health safety measures.


    The frontline line warriors i.e. Loco Pilots, Guards, Station Managers, Yard Masters, Controllers, Traffic Inspectors etc of SER have been working round the clock for maintaining uninterrupted supply of essential commodities to different parts of the country during the nation-wide lockdown in the wake of spread of COVID-19 pandemic. It is very essential & commendable services to the Nation when the country is under complete lockdown. Frontline staff has been given face masks, sanitizers, soaps and gloves to safe guard themselves from the deadly pandemic. Doctors, Nurses and other healthcare personnel have also been provided with Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) manufactured in-house. Employees and stakeholders without face-covers are not allowed to enter railway offices and premises. Besides, SER has been bolstering its preparedness to meet any future challenge in every sector within the ambit.
