      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS WILL RUN FOR MORE DAYS
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2020 6:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) has extended the running of time tabled Parcel Express Trains in different routes to supply essential commodities at different corners of the country during lockdown period. The following Parcel Express Trains have been extended up to May 31, 2020 on nominated days as notified earlier.     ER started operation of Parcel Express trains from April 10, 2020. Total trip originated from ER up to 14.5.2020 is 268 and transported about 3661 tonnes of consignment to different corners of the country. These parcel traffic of ER includes essential commodities & consumables to maintain the supply chain as well as medicine, medical equipment, PPE materials to combat against spread of COVID-19.
