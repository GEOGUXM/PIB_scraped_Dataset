      Ministry of Railways

      ER Cancels Special Trains in View of Amphan
      Posted On: 20 MAY 2020 12:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 May, 2020


    Due to the ensuing severe cyclone ’AMPHAN’-the cyclone which is expected to hit West Bengal in few hours from now onwards, resulting in heavy rain and storm, the Eastern Railway has decided to cancel the scheduled run of 02301 Howrah-New Delhi AC Special Express from Howrah today and 02302 New Delhi - Howrah AC Special Express from New Delhi tomorrow.


    The CPRO Office has informed this today in a press release.
