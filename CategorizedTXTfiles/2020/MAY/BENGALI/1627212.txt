      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO REOPEN ALL COMPUTERISED PASSENGER RESERVATION OFFICES FROM TOMORROW
      Posted On: 27 MAY 2020 6:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 27, 2020


    As a part of restoration of Passenger Services in a phased manner, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to reopen all computerized reservation offices over its jurisdiction from tomorrow.


    Reservation Counters at 26 stations of SER are already functioning from May 22, 2020. Considering the need of the general public and to make the ticket booking easy for all intending travellers, SER authority has decided that all Reserved Ticket Booking Centres will be functioning from tomorrow except the Metiabruz booking office, which is lying within the containment zone.


    However, refund from City Booking Offices viz. Rabindra Sadan, Old Koilaghat will be available from June 1, 2020 onwards.
