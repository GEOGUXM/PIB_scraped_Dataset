      Ministry of Railways

      ER OPERATES 24 X7 FREIGHT SERVICE TO KEEP UP SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 01 MAY 2020 4:02PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata,1st May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is going with its freight service round the clock on all the days over its network to maintain supply of essential commodities and other necessary items at different areas to meet up the demand of consumables and other inputs to maintain the market stability. ER is operating its freight trains on day and night, transporting food grains, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, steel, minerals and coal for power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON THURSDAY:


    TOTAL UNLOADING: 41 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 25 Rakes


    Rice – 9 Rakes


    Sugar – 2 Rakes


    Cement – 3 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 2 Rakes


    TOTAL LOADING: 46 Rakes


    Coal - 26 Rakes


    Steel – 7 Rakes


    Container - 1 Rake


    Stone – 8 Rakes


    Cement – 1 Rake


    Ash – 1 Rake


    Ballast – 2 Rakes


    Forty seven empty were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were maintained keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Proper sanitisation is done wherever required.
