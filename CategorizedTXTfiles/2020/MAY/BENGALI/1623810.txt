      Ministry of Railways

      ER IN 24 HOURS FREIGHT SERVICE TO SUPPLY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 14 MAY 2020 5:03PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 14 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuing its freight services 24 hours in this lockdown period to maintain supply chain of essential commodities, consumables as well as other inputs at different corners of the country. The unabated freight movement in ER network is helping to meet up the demand with the availability of essential commodities and other inputs at different areas.
