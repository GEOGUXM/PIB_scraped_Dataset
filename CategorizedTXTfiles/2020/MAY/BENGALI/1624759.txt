      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S RPF DISTRIBUTES COOKED FOOD AMONG DESTITUTES
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2020 7:57PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 17 May, 2020


    In the midst of COVID-19 lockdown when the poor & starving people are facing utmost difficulties in arranging food for themselves, Eastern Railway (ER) has come forward arranged for lunch as well as dinner for them at different areas over ER network. ER’s Railway Protection Force (RPF) is distributing food prepared by RPF / NGOs and IRCTC among the destitute persons on daily basis. The places & stations where distribution of lunch/dinner have been arranged today include Fairlie Place, Howrah, Shrirampur, Tarakeswar, Nabadwip Dham, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Barrackpore, Ranaghat, Berhampore, Budge Budge, Asansol, Jasidih, Dumka etc.


    Today as many as 2322 persons have been provided with lunch. Arrangement for dinner has also been made for 325 persons, which was distributed by RPF personnel at different places over ER jurisdiction.


    Everywhere social distancing and other hygienic stipulations were maintained.
