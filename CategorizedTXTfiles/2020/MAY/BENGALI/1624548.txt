      INDIAN RAILWAYS IS READY TO RUN SHRAMIK SPECIAL TRAINS FROM ANY DISTRICT IN THE COUNTRY: RAILWAY MINISTER
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2020 9:29PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 16 th May, 2020


    Minister for Railways Shri Piyush Goyal has said that Indian Railways is ready to run Shramik Special trains from any district in the country to provide relief to the migrant labourers. In a tweet today Shri Goyal requested the District Collectors to prepare lists of the stranded laboureres and the destination and apply to Railways through state Nodal Officers.


    It may be mentioned that railway ministry from 1 st May operating 'Shramik' special trains to send stranded migrant labourers back to their home states.
