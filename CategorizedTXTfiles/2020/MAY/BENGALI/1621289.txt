      Ministry of Railways

      ER’s RPF DISTRIBUTE FOOD AMONG THOUSANDS OF NEEDY PEOPLE
      Posted On: 05 MAY 2020 8:14PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 5 May, 2020:


    As a part of Eastern Railway’s (ER)’s endeavour to provide food for the starving people during the lockdown period, Railway Protection Force (RPF) of ER continues to distribute cooked meals to them on a daily basis. Food distribution is done to poor, destitute, beggars, children, coolies, migrant labourers, stranded persons and whoever comes in search of food at and near the Railway stations every day. The food is prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) were distributed among 2065 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like, Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Budge Budge etc. and at far-off places like Ranaghat, Srirampur, Berhampore, Seoraphuli, Asansol, Barakar etc.


    Moreover, dinner for 1185 persons has been prepared today to distribute among the starving people by at various places.


    Proper hygiene and social distancing have been maintained during the distribution of food.
