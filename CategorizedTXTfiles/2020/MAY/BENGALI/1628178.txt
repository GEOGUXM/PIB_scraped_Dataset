      Ministry of Railways

      SER RESCHEDULES HOWRAH-BHUBANESWAR SPECIAL
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2020 6:45PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 31 May, 2020


    02074/02073 Bhubaneswar-Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special Train has been notified to run from tomorrow (June 1, 2020). However, on request of Govt. of West Bengal, it has been decided that 02073 Howrah-Bhubaneswar Special will be rescheduled to leave Howrah at 2:25 pm instead of 1:25 pm for the next seven days starting from tomorrow, informs the CPRO office of South Eastern Railway (SER).


    Consequent upon the change in departure time from Howrah, the departure from Kharagpur will be at 4:30 pm, from Jaleswar at 5:10 pm, from Balasore at 5:47 pm and from Soro at 6:12 pm in SER system.
