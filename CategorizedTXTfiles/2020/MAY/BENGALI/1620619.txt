      ER’S COVID-19 PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS CARRY MORE THAN 935 TONNES OF CONSIGNMENT IN APRIL, 2020
      Posted On: 03 MAY 2020 4:43PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 3 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is running Parcel Express trains in different routes for carrying essential items in the midst of COVID-19 lockdown. ER started operation of Parcel Express trains from 10.4.2020. Total trip originated from ER in the month of April, 2020 is 171 and transported about 935.986 tonnes of consignment. These parcel traffic of ER include essential commodities & consumables as well as medicines & medical equipment.


    ER’s Parcel Express trains have also become a means of speedy transport of medicines & medical equipment and consumable items to different corners of the country during the lockdown period. Till April 30, 2020, Medicine & Medical Equipment weighing a total of 32.56 tonnes, Perishable Items (Betel leaves, Fruits, Vegetables etc ) weighing a total of 551.65 tonnes and Ghee weighing a total of 14.858 tonnes have been dispatched through ER Parcel Express trains. This has helped in augmenting medical infrastructure at different areas to resist spread of COVID-19 as well as meeting up the demand for consumables at different areas.
