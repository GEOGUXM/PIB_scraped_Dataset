      Ministry of Railways

      SER EXTENDS CANCELLATION OF EMU, MEMU, DEMU & CONVENTIONAL PASSENGER TRAINS
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2020 6:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 18 May, 2020


    The South Eastern Railway (SER)’s PRO’s office has informed today that in view of the extension of lockdown due to the outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, all EMU, MEMU, DEMU & conventional passenger trains of (SER) will remain suspended up to May 31, 2020.
