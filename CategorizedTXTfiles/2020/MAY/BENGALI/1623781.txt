      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      ELISA Laboratory of MPDA Shifts to Tea Board of India Building
      Posted On: 14 MAY 2020 3:48PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 14 May, 2020


    The ELISA Screening laboratory of The Marine Products Export Development Authority has shifted its office from Sonarpur. The new office of this statutory body under the Union Ministry of Commerce has started functioning at the fifth floor of Tea Board of India building at BTM Sarani (Brabourne Road), Kolkata. The new contact number is: 033-2234-5908.


    This particular lab tests the presence of antibiotic residues in shrimp. The shrimp or prawn or fish farmers, who are engaged in supplying raw materials to the export value chain, may approach this office at Tea Board of India building for pre-harvest ELISA test along with the farm enrolment details.
