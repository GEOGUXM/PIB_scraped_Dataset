      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S RPF DISTRIBUTES COOKED FOOD AMONG STARVING PEOPLE
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2020 7:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 May, 2020


    As a part of Eastern Railway (ER)’s endeavour to arrange food for needy people during the Nation-wide lock down, Railway Protection Force (RPF) of ER is regularly distributing cooked food and food items among the destitute persons and whoever else comes in search of food at and near the railway stations every day. The food is being prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is distributed daily by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    In this process, today cooked food (lunch) was distributed among 1839 persons at different locations like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus, Ranaghat, Budge Budge, Berhampore Court, Srirampur, Sheoraphuli, Asansol, Dumka etc.


    Moreover, dinner for 325 persons was also arranged and the same distributed among the needy people by RPF personnel, at various places in ER network.


    Everywhere social distancing and all other hygienic stipulations were maintained.
