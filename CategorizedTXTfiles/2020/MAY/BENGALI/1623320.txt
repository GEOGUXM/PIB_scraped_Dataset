      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S RPF DISTRIBUTE FOOD AMONG 1, 53, 438 DESTITUTE PERSONS
      Posted On: 12 MAY 2020 6:23PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 12 May, 2020


    In the midst of COVID-19 lock down, while the poor & needy people are facing difficulties in getting food items, Eastern Railway (ER) stood beside them, arranging for them lunch and dinner regularly. ER’s Railway Protection Force (RPF) is continuously distributing food prepared by RPF / IRCTC /NGOs among the destitute persons on a daily basis at different places in ER jurisdiction.


    During the period, from March 26, 2020 to yesterday, ER’s RPF has distributed food among as many as 1,53,438 needy persons. Out of this, 82,266 meals have been prepared by IRCTC and 71,172 meals by RPF/NGOs.


    Eastern Railway in this way provided cooked food and other food items to the needy and destitute persons to reduce their trauma in search of means of livelihood, during the lockdown period.


    Everywhere social distancing and all other hygienic stipulations are maintained.
