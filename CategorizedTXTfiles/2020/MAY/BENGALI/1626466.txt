      AMPHAN: INDIAN ARMY TO PROVIDE FIVE COLUMNS TO HELP BENGAL GOVERNMENT
      Posted On: 23 MAY 2020 8:36PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 23 rd May, 2020


    Based on request from the Govt of West Bengal, Indian Army has provided five columns to assist the Kolkata City Civil Administration, North and South 24 Parganas district administration in the aftermath of Cyclone Amphan. The army has already started working in the affected areas.


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi after surveying cyclone ravaged areas on Friday with the Chief Minister of the state Smt. Mamata Banerjee assured all assistance to the state to fight the crisis. He has already announced an advance assistance of Rs.1000 crore.


    The army's help is being taken to bring back life to normalcy after the devastating effect of Amphan.
