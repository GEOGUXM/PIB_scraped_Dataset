      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT SERVICES MAINTAIN SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 03 MAY 2020 4:46PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 3 May, 2020


    To ensure supply of essential commodities and necessary inputs at different areas during the nationwide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) has taken all-out effort to run freight trains day and night, transporting food grains, sugar, pharmaceuticals, fertilizers, petroleum products, steel, cement etc and coal for power generating sector.


    TOTAL LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON SATURDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING: 35 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 23 Rakes


    Rice – 5 Rakes


    Sugar - 2 Rakes


    Cement - 3 Rakes


    Fertiliser – 1 Rake


    Tar – 1 Rakes


    TOTAL LOADING: 42 Rakes


    Coal – 21 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Stone – 9 Rakes


    Cement - 6 Rakes


    Ballast – 3 Rakes


    Thirty six empty rakes were run out to different operating points for loading of commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
