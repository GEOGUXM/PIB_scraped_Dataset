      Ministry of Railways

      SER EXTENDS DAYS OF RUN OF TIME TABLED PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS TO SUPPLY ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2020 5:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 16 May, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to extend the days of run of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains till May 30 or June 2, 2020 daily or on different nominated days to supply essential commodities, medicines and medical equipment across the country. Earlier, it was decided to run Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains towards various parts of the country till May 15 or 17, 2020.


    These Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains are running between Shalimar-Ranchi, Howrah-KSR Bengaluru, Shalimar-Mumbai CSMT, Tatanagar-Itwari, Howrah-Secunderabad and Shalimar-Porbander.


    The essential commodities which are transported by these Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains include vegetables, medicines, grocery items, fruits, fish, eggs etc. The above mentioned Parcel Express Trains also cater to the needs of the people residing around Kharagpur, Purulia, Balasore, Rourkela, Chakradharpur and other stations under SER’s jurisdiction in addition to major cities en route.
