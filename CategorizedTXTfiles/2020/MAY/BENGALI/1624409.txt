      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES MAINTAINING REGULAR SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES BY ITS FREIGHT SERVICES
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2020 4:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 16 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously carrying on its freight services in the midst of the lockdown period to maintain supply of essential commodities, consumables as well as other inputs at different areas, informed railway authority in a press release. It also says, ER is operating its freight trains seamlessly round the clock transporting foodgrains, sugar, pharmaceutical, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products, cement, stone, steel and coal for power generating sector.

    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Proper sanitisation was done wherever required.
