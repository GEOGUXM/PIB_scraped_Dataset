      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS CONTINUOUSLY TRANSPORTING ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 13 MAY 2020 5:32PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 13 May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuously running freight trains as well as Parcel Express trains on round the clock basis to maintain supply of essential commodities at different corners of the country since initiation of Nation-wide lock down. Freight trains carrying food grains, sugar, fertilizer, steel, cement, stone, petroleum products, coal etc are reaching at different areas to cater to the demand of consumables and other inputs.


    ER has run a total of 188 freight trains on Tuesday. The detail of which is as follows:


    Ash – 3 Rakes


    Cement – 24 Rakes


    Clinker – 3 Rakes


    Container- 13 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 42 Rakes


    Edible Oil – 1 Rake


    Fertilizer – 2 Rakes


    Maize – 2 Rakes


    Onion – 2 Rakes


    POL product- 1 Rake


    Rice – 8 Rakes


    Steel – 4 Rakes


    Stone – 9 Rakes


    Sugar – 2 Rakes


    Slag – 2 Rakes


    Parcel – 8 Rakes


    Besides, 62 empty rakes were run out to different freight operating point to facilitate loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
