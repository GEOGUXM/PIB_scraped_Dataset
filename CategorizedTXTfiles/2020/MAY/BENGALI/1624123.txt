      Ministry of Railways

      WHEELS OF ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS ROLLING ROUND THE CLOCK
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2020 6:37PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 May, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and other merchandise or consumables for maintaining the supply chain in the midst of lockdown period, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating freight trains round the clock, transporting food grains, sugar, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products etc, cement, steel and coal for the power generating sector.


    ER has run a total of 195 Freight rakes on Thursday. The details are as follows:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 37 RAKES


    Ash - 2 Rakes


    Automobile – 1 Rake


    Cement – 18 Rakes


    Clinker – 2 Rakes


    Container- 14 Rakes


    Coal & Mineral- 60 Rakes


    Edible Oil- 1 Rake


    Dertiliser-3 Rakes


    POL Products- 5 Rakes


    Rice- 9 Rakes


    Railway Material Consignment- 2 Rakes


    Steel-3 Rakes


    Stone- 8 Rakes


    Sugar – 1 Rake


    Slag – 2 Rakes


    Tar- 1 Rake


    Parcel - 10 Rakes


    Besides, 50 empty rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation was done also wherever required.
