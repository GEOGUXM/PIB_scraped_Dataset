      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO MAINTAIN SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES AT DIFFERENT LOCATIONS
      Posted On: 19 MAY 2020 6:04PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 19 May, 2020


    To ensure transport of essential commodities and necessary inputs at different areas in the midst of the nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) has taken all-out effort to run its freight trains on 24x7 basis, transporting food grains, sugar, edible oil, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products, coal and minerals, steel, cement etc.


    ER has run 220 freight trains on Monday. The break-up is as follows:


    Ash - 3 Rakes


    Automobile- 2 Rakes


    Cement-21 Rakes


    Clinker – 4 Rakes


    Container- 13 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 50 Rakes


    Edible Oil- 1 Rake


    Fertiliser- 6 Rakes


    Maize- 2 Rakes


    Onion- 4 Rakes


    POL product - 5 Rakes


    Rice – 9 Rakes


    Steel- 3 Rakes


    Stone- 15 Rakes


    Salt- 1 Rake


    Sugar – 2 Rakes


    Wheat- 1 Rake


    Parcel – 10 Rakes


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
