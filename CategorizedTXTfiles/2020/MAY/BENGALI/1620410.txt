      Ministry of Railways

      SER EXTENDS DAYS OF RUN OF TIME TABLED PARCEL EXPRESS TRAINS
      Posted On: 02 MAY 2020 5:17PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 2nd May, 2020


    South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to extend the run of Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains till May 15 to 17, 2020 daily or on different nominated days for uninterrupted supply of essential commodities, medicines and medical equipment across the country. Earlier, it was decided to run Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains to various parts of the country till May 3 to 4, 2020.


    These Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains are running between Shalimar-Ranchi, Howrah-KSR Bengaluru, Shalimar-Mumbai, Tatanagar-Itwari, Howrah-Secunderabad and Shalimar-Porbander.


    The extended period of running of the above Parcel Express Trains from SER’s jurisdiction is given below:


    Shalimar-Ranchi Parcel Express


    00801/00802 Shalimar-Ranchi-Shalimar Parcel Express has now been extended to run up to May 15, 2020 daily both from Shalimar and Ranchi.


    Howrah-KSR Bengaluru Parcel Express


    00616 Howrah-KSR Bengaluru Parcel Express has now been extended to run up to May 15, 2020 daily from Howrah.


    Shalimar-Mumbai CSMT Parcel Express


    00114 Shalimar-Mumbai CSMT Parcel Express has now been extended to run till May 17, 2020 daily from Shalimar.


    Tatanagar-Itwari Parcel Express


    00882 Tatanagar-Itwari Parcel Express has now been extended to run till May 16, 2020 daily from Tatanagar.


    Howrah-Secunderabad Parcel Express


    00760 Howrah-Secunderabad Parcel Express will have additional services on May 9 and May 16, 2020 from Howrah.


    Shalimar-Porbander Parcel Express


    00914 Shalimar-Porbander Parcel Express will have additional services on May 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 and 16, 2020 from Shalimar.


    The essential commodities which are being transported by these Time Tabled Parcel Express Trains include vegetables, medicines, grocery items, different kind of fruits, fish, eggs etc. The above Parcel Express Trains also cater the needs of the people residing around Kharagpur, Purulia, Balasore, Rourkela, Chakradharpur and other stations under SER’s jurisdiction in addition to major cities en route.
