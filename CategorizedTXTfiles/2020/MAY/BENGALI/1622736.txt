      Ministry of Railways

      ER’S RPF DISTRIBUTE FOOD AMONG DESTITUTE PERSONS
      Posted On: 10 MAY 2020 7:09PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 10 May, 2020


    As a part of Eastern Railway’s (ER)’s initiative for the welfare of the destitute persons, Railway Protection Force (RPF) continues to provide cooked meals and food items to needy people following the Nation-wide lockdown due to COVID-19 pandemic. The food is being prepared by RPF/NGO & IRCTC and the same is being distributed every day by RPF personnel all over the jurisdiction of ER.


    Today, ER’s RPF has distributed cooked food (lunch) among 2200 persons at different locations in and around Kolkata like Fairlie Place, Howrah, Sealdah, Bidhannagar, Park Circus etc. and far-off places like Ranaghat, Kanchrapara, Berhampore, Budge Budge, Sheoraphuli, Nabadwip Dham, Asansol, Barakar, Kalipahari, Dumka etc.


    Moreover, dinner for 315 persons were prepared today for distribution among the starving people by RPF personnel at various places over ER network


    Everywhere social distancing and all other hygienic stipulations were maintained.
