      Ministry of Railways

      ER MAINTAINS SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES BY DAY & NIGHT FREIGHT SERVICES
      Posted On: 02 MAY 2020 5:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 2nd May, 2020


    Eastern Railway (ER) is continuing its freight services round the clock in the midst of lockdown to maintain supply of essential commodities, popular consumables as well as other inputs to different corners of the country. The unabated freight movement in ER network is helping in meeting up the demand with the availability of essential commodities and other inputs at different areas.


    THE TOTAL LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON FRIDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:-


    TOTAL LOADING: 37 Rakes


    Coal - 18 Rakes


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Container – 1 Rake


    Stone – 7 Rakes


    Cement – 1 Rake


    Ash - 2 Rakes


    Ballast- 5 Rakes


    TOTAL UNLOADING: 43 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral - 23 Rakes


    Rice – 10 Rakes


    Sugar- 1 Rake


    Cement - 1 Rake


    Fertilizer – 4 Rakes


    Clinker - 1 Rake


    Slag – 1 Rake


    Container - 1 Rake


    Salt – 1 Rake


    Fifty seven empty rakes were dispatched to different operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of keeping in view the basic guidelines of social distancing and other precautionary measures. Water, soap & sanitizer were available in all working points. Proper sanitisation is also done wherever required.
