      Ministry of Railways

      ER CONTINUES ITS FREIGHT SERVICES TO MAINTAIN SUPPLY OF ESSENTIAL COMMODITIES
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2020 6:58PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 18 May, 2020


    To maintain supply of essential commodities and necessary inputs at different areas in the midst of the nation-wide lockdown, Eastern Railway (ER) has taken all-out effort to run its freight trains on 24x7 basis, transporting food grains, sugar, pharmaceuticals, fertilizers, petroleum products, minerals, steel, cement etc and coal for power generating sector.


    TOTAL LOADING & UNLOADING PERFORMACE OF ER ON SUNDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING: 49 Rakes


    Ballast- 1 Rake


    Cement -3 Rakes


    Clinker- 5 Rakes


    Coal and Mineral – 27 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    POL product – 1 Rake


    Rice – 4 Rakes


    Steel – 2 Rakes


    Sugar – 3 Rakes


    Slag – 1 Rake


    TOTAL LOADING: 57 Rakes


    Coal – 25 Rakes


    Steel- 5 Rakes


    Cement – 7 Rakes


    Container – 3 Rakes


    Stone – 12 Rakes


    Ballast – 2 Rakes


    Jute and other – 3 Rakes


    Everywhere health & hygiene of field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points. Proper sanitisation was also done wherever required.
