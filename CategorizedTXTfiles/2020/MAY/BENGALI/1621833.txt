      Ministry of Railways

      WHEELS OF ER’S FREIGHT TRAINS ROLLING ROUND THE CLOCK
      Posted On: 07 MAY 2020 5:24PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 7 May, 2020


    To maintain the market supply of essential commodities as well as different inputs for agricultural and production sector in the midst of lockdown period, Eastern Railway (ER) is operating freight trains round the clock, transporting food grains, sugar, medical equipment, fertilizers, petroleum products etc and coal for the power generating sector.


    THE TOTAL UNLOADING & LOADING PERFORMANCE OF ER ON WEDNESDAY IS AS FOLLOWS:


    TOTAL UNLOADING – 37 RAKES


    Coal and Mineral - 22 Rakes


    Rice – 5 Rakes


    Cement – 3 Rakes


    Clinker – 2 Rakes


    Sugar – 2 Rakes


    Slag – 1 Rakes


    Ballast - 2 Rakes


    TOTAL LOADING: 41 RAKES


    Coal – 18 Rakes


    Steel – 3 Rakes


    Container – 2 Rakes


    Stone – 11 Rakes


    Cement – 4 Rakes


    Ballast – 3 Rakes


    Forty six empty Rakes were despatched to different freight operating points for loading of essential commodities.


    Everywhere health & hygiene of the field staff were taken care of keeping in view of the basic guidelines of social distancing. Water, soap & sanitizer were available at all working points.
