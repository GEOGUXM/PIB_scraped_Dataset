      VANDE BHARAT MISSION:72 PRAVASIS HAILING FROM THRISSUR RETURNS
      Posted On: 10 MAY 2020 10:53AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Thirty eight out of 72 PRAVASIS, hailing from THRISSUR,who arrived by Abudhabi-Kochi flight early morning have been placed under observation at Guruvayoor Sterling resort, which has been designated by Kerala Government as Covid Care Centre.They were transported to Guruvayoor from Kochi by a special K S.R.T.C bus.The group includes 10 women and a child.

    Others were permitted to leave alongwith their relatives for home quarantine.Pravasis were received at Guruvayoor by Kerala Local Administration Minister A C Moideen, THRISSUR Collector S Shanavas, Municipality Chairperson M Rathi,apart from Police and Revenue officials.
