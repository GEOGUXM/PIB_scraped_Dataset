      89 persons in observation
      Posted On: 10 MAY 2020 12:56PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    In Kozhikode, 89 persons returned from foreign countries recently are kept in observation, by the health department.


    Out of this 31 persons are in Covid care centers arranged by the district administration and 58 persons are in home quarrentin.


    Out of the 58 persons who are in observation at home, 42 persons came in Dubai - Kozhikode flight and 16 persons came in Riyadh- Kozhikode flight.4 persons came to Kozhikode through Beharin-Nedumbassery flight. Out of the 31 persons in the Covid care centers arranged by the district administration , 24 persons came in Dubai - Kozhikode flight and 3 in Riyadh- Kozhikode flight.


    The Remaining 4 persons came to Nedumbassry from Baharin and came to Kozhikode.
