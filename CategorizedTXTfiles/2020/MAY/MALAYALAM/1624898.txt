      Shramik Special Express to leave for Jaipur from Thirur today
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2020 6:20PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Pickup point for migrant labourers who are leaving for Jaipur from Thirur today


    A Shramik Special Express train for migrant labourers is scheduled today from Thirur to Jaipur. The train will leave from Thirur railway station at 9 PM. 1423 migrant labourers are registered in Malappuram district to go to Jaipur. These labourers are from Kondotty, Eranad, Thirurangadi, Perinthalmanna and Nilambur Taluks. Authorities have done all the arrangements including for Medical check-up of the passengers. Special vehicles have been arranged for picking up the passengers. There are special pickup point from where medical check-up will be done and travellers will be carried to railway station.
