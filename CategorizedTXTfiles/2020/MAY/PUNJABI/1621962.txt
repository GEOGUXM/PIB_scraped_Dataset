      Commercial activities come back to life in the region
      Posted On: 07 MAY 2020 6:23PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, May 07, 2020


    Commercial activities are coming back to life in the region in the light of recent relaxations provided by the Government of India. As some shops have started to reopen, shopkeepers in Haryana have welcomed the much needed relief. Kundan, a shopkeeper from the state said that reopening of shops owing to the relaxations in Lockdown has come as a relief not only to the customers but also to them. Umesh Sikri of Kaithal is happy that some of the shops are going to reopen, however he is slightly wary as well fearing that it might escalate the spread of the pandemic. He wished that people keep practicing social distancing.

    As a part of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKY), the Government is also providing free food grains, gas cylinders and cash payment to poor senior citizens, women beneficiaries of Jan Dhan account and farmers. As per latest figures, as on 5th May, 2020 using the digital payment infrastructure, around 39 crore poor people have received financial assistance of Rs 34,800 crore under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP)


    Women beneficiaries in the Haryana have confirmed to receive money in theirs Jan Dhan accounts and expressed satisfaction. Commending the Government for timely crediting money in her Jan Dhan account, Manju Devi of village Masih in the state said that she has received the second installment. Dinesh Devi from the same state said that Rs 500 has been credited in her account under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana. Asha Devi also confirmed receiving money in her account.
