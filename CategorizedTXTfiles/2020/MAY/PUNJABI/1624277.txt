      Industrialists and residents of the region support the economic package for micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs)
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2020 7:04PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, May 15, 2020


    The economic package for micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) has prospects for growth and employment generation. The industrialists and residents in the states of Haryana and Punjab said while supporting the package announced by Finance Minister on Wednesday.


    An industrialist Amit Kumar Sharma while referring to 20 lakh crore relief package under “Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan” said it will provide fillip not only to MSMEs but also to all sectors of the economy. Speaking specifically in respect of package for MSME sector, he said due to the lockdown small businesses were facing hardships and the package will revitalize the sector in future. Anurag Aggarwal, former president, Mohali Industries Association while appreciating the package under “Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan” announced by honourable PM said that it is on par with the developed countries and schemes for MSMEs unveiled by Finance Minister are well thought out. It will facilitate MSMEs’ growth during these times of lockdown. Mr. Aggarwal suggested that bringing more micro industries into the formal credit system will further reinvigorate the sector.

    Dinesh Kumar from Rewari in Haryana said the economic package will give a boost to micro and small scale industries ensuring employment opportunities for the unemployed youth. Deepak Yadav, a resident of village Luhaha, also from Rewari said this will help in resumption of industrial activities which were facing slowdown due to lockdown in the light of COVID-19. Deepak, a resident of Rewari district, shared the same optimism.

    Measures for relief and credit support related to businesses, especially MSMEs under “Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan” to support Indian economy’s fight against COVID-19 were the focus of the announcement by Finance Minister on Wednesday. “Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan” recently announced by Honourable PM Shri Narendra Modi is an economic package of Rs 20 lakh crore with focus on economy, infrastructure, system, vibrant demography and demand.
