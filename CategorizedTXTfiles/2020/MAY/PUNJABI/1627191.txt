      Passengers arriving via domestic flights express satisfaction with the arrangements
      Posted On: 27 MAY 2020 5:32PM by PIB Chandigarh


    Chandigarh, May 27, 2020


    Passengers arriving via domestic flights expressed satisfaction with the arrangements. The domestic flights have resumed after domestic air travel of passenger has been deleted from the list of prohibited activities. Union Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) in an order on 20th May 2020, has amended the guidelines on lockdown measures to fight COVID-19, so as to facilitate domestic air travel for stranded persons in India.


    A passenger who came to Chandigarh from Mumbai by Air India flight said that all the facilities were up to the mark at the airport. She said that every passenger was thoroughly checked and thermal scanned. Another passenger from the same flight said that everyone had downloaded Aarogya Setu app and almost everyone was wearing masks. He expressed satisfaction with the security and hygiene provided by the government. Similarly, a co-passenger expressed full satisfaction at the elaborate safety precautions taken by Air India. He said wearing PPE kit feels very uncomfortable but it is equally important to wear them.
