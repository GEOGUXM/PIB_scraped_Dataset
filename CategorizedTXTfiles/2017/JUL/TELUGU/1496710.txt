      WORKSHOP ON GST HELD AT CGO TOWER
      Posted On: 21 JUL 2017 7:11PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The Regional office of the Textile Commissioner, Bangalore in association with Power loom Service Centre conducted a Workshop on ‘Goods and Services Tax (GST)’ here at CGO Towers, Kavadiguda, Hyderabad on Friday.


    Smt. Shailaja Ramaiyer, Director of Handlooms& Textiles and Apparel Export Parks (FAC), Government of Telangana was the Chief Guest for the workshop. Smt. Vaishali Malhotra, Joint Director, National Academy of Customs, Indirect Taxes & Narcotics, Hyderabad was the Guest of Honor.


    Textiles industry people from different parts of Telangana state participated at the workshop and clarified queries from the experts during the workshop. Shri Gopi, Donthireddy, Dy. Director, National Academy of Customs, Indirect Taxes & Narcotics, Hyderabad Shri Rajesh Kumar, Asst. Director& OIC Power loom Service centre and Shri Guarav Gupta, Asst Director &OIC, regional Office of the Textile Commissioner, Bangalore were also present.
