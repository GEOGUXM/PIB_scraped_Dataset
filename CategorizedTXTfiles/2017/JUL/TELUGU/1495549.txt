      SHRI BANKEY BEHARI AGRAWAL ASSUMED CHARGE AS CHIEF COMMISSIONER, GST & CUSTOMS- HYDERABAD ZONE
      Posted On: 13 JUL 2017 6:50PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Shri Bankey Behari Agrawal, has assumed charge as Chief Commissioner, GST & Customs, Hyderabad Zone, Hyderabad (covering the entire state of Telangana). In addition, he is also in-charge as Chief Commissioner of GST & Customs, Visakhapatnam Zone (covering the state of Andhra Pradesh).


    He is from the 1985 batch of the Indian Revenue Service, and has worked at several places. He has been actively involved with the drafting of GST laws pertaining to Appeals and Revision and was also instrumental in the designing of the 4-Tier GST Training plan which led to training of almost 60,000 (Central/State) officers during 2016.


    On behalf of the department, he has assured full support for resolving grievances/queries of taxpayers and stakeholders. He can be reached at ccehyd@excise.nic.in .
