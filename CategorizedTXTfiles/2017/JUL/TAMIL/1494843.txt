      Recruitment of Direct Agents for PLI
      Posted On: 07 JUL 2017 5:01PM by PIB Chennai


    Office of Chief Postmaster, Chennai GPO is conducting a walk in interview for the post of PLI/RPLI Direct Agents. The interview will take place on 15.07.17 at 10:00 Hrs.


    The applicant should have passed 12 th standard or 10 th standard + (Diploma). The applicant should be between 18 years to 65 years of age on the date of interview.
