      Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers

      2025 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) functional in 31 States/UTs: Shri Mansukh Lal Mandaviya
      Posted On: 21 JUL 2017 7:59PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    Union Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping and Chemicals & Fertilizers, Shri Mansukh Lal Mandaviya, in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today, informed that as on 19 . 07 . 2017, 2025 ‘ Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras ’ ( PMBJKs ) are functional in 31 States / UTs of the country .
