      President of India congratulates Shri Ram Nath Kovind on his election as the 14th President of India
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2017 9:21PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee has congratulated Shri Ram Nath Kovind on his election as the 14th President of the Republic of India.
    
    In his message, the President has said, “I extend to you my heartiest congratulations on your election as the 14th President of the Republic of India.
    
    I also offer my best wishes to you for a successful tenure. May you lead India to the path of democracy, stability and prosperity”.
