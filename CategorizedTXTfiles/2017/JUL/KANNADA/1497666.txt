    SPECIAL COUNTERS TO RECEIVE INCOME TAX RETURNS


    Bangalore, July 28, 2017


    The Income-tax Department is organizing special counters at Gayathri Vihar, Palace Grounds, Bengaluru for three days viz., July 29, 30, 31, 2017 to receive income tax returns from salaried employees as well as pensioners. These special counters are 54 in number to facilitate the employees and pensioners to file their returns. The counters will remain open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. on all the three days. Incomes of those, who exceed Rs.5.00 lakh are required to file their returns on-line electronically. E-filing intermediaries and tax return preparers will also be available at the venue to provide guidance. Facilities like ATMs, PAN Service, help desks for the need of senior citizens and disabled are also organized.


    Special arrangements are also made to receive the returns in bulk. Centralized Processing Centre (CPC), Bangalore is also setting up a counter to receive the acknowledgement of the e-returns filed.


    It may be noted that during this period the returns will not be accepted at the Income-tax Department of HMT Bhavan.
