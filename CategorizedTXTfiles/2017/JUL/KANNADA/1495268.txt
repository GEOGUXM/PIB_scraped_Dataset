    One-year Free & Stipendiary `O' Level Computer
    Software & Hardware Training for SC/ST Job seekers


    Bengaluru, July 12, 2017


    National Career Service Centre for SCs/STs under the Union Ministry of Labour and Employment, Bengaluru is inviting applications from eligible and interested educated unemployed SC/ST youths for one-year free and stipendiary “O” level Computer Software and Hardware Training.


    Minimum educational qualification is 10+2 or above for Software Training and 10+2 or SSLC+ITI, Diploma, Graduation, Post Graduation or Doctorate for Hardware Training programme. The age must be below 30 years as on July 30, 2017. The selected candidates are eligible for a stipend of Rs.1000/- per month during the training period.


    Those who are eligible and interested may contact personally the National Career Service Centre for SC/STs, Ministry of Labour & Employment, Govt. of India, Government ITI Campus, Dairy Circle, Bengaluru – 560 029. Candidates must bring one set of Xerox copies of SSLC Marks Card, II PUC/ITI Marks Card, Caste Certificate and approach between 9.30 a.m. and 6.00 p.m. except Saturdays, Sundays and government holidays. The last date is July 25, 2017.
