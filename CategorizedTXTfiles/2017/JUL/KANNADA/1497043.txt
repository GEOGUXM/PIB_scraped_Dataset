      July 27: Dist level media workshop in Ramanagara


      Bengaluru: Press Information Bureau, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India will be organizing a district level media workshop for the journalists, in Ramanagara on Thursday, July 27, 2017. The workshop is being held in coordination with District the Administration, Zilla Panchayat, District Information and Public Relations Department and District Working Journalists’ Association.


      Dr. Mamatha B.R., Dy Commissioner, will inaugurate the workshop in Zilla Panchayat auditorium at 10.30 AM. Smt. T.C. Saralakumari, Senior Assistant Director, State Information and Public Relations Department and Shri M.H. Prakash, President, District Working Journalists’ Association will also be present.


      Shri Khadri S. Achuthan, Senior media expert and former Indian Information Service officer, Shri Sunil V. and Dr. Harsha R., Assistant Commissioners, GST and Shri Y.A. Vajanthri, Chief Manager, Syndicate Bank (SLBC) will participate as resource persons in the workshop, to be presided over by Shri Abbooru Rajashekhar, senior journalist. Different subjects like recent developments in the field of media, functioning of PIB and RNI, Goods and Services Tax, welfare schemes implemented through banks etc., will be discussed during the workshop.
