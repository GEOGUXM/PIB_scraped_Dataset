      BSNL Open House Session on Saturday
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2017 5:54PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    BSNL Thiruvananthapuram business area will conduct open house session for the customers on 22nd July 2017 (Saturday) from 10:30 am to 01:00 pm at Telephone Exchange, Manacadu. The session will be attended by the senior officers of BSNL, Thiruvananthapuram. Customers are given opportunity to put forth their suggestions and grievances if any, in this session.
