      BSNL landline disconnection date
      Posted On: 17 JUL 2017 12:26PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    BSNL Trivandrum Principal General Manager has informed that the landline telephone bills for the month of June 2017 (bill dtd 05/06/2017 & 06/06/2017) should be paid on or before 19th July 2017 to get continuous BSNL landline service. Customers can pay their bills at nearest BSNL customer service centers, post offices, Janaseva Kendras and through the mobile application My BSNL App and also through the online portal of BSNL.
