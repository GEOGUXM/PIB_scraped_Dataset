      Dak Adalat on August 8
      Posted On: 17 JUL 2017 12:24PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The 91st Dak Adalat of Kerala Postal Circle will be held at the office of the Chief Postmaster General, Kerala Circle, Thiruvananthapuram 695033, on 8th August 2017. The time of the Adalat is 3.30 pm. The complaints/petitions and disputes which will be taken up in the Adalat will cover matters relating to postal services including Counter Services, Savings Bank, Money Orders etc.


    Those who wish to file their cases before the Dak Adalat may send their petition/complaint in a cover super scribed â€œCIRCLE DAK ADALAT- 2017 addressed to Shri. M Salim Kumar, Assistant Director(Customer Service) O/o the Chief Postmaster General, Kerala Circle, Thiruvanathapuram-695033.


    The items for consideration in the Dak Adalat should reach the above address on or before July 28th. Complaints & Petitions taken up in any of the previous Adalats will not be considered in te present Adalat.
