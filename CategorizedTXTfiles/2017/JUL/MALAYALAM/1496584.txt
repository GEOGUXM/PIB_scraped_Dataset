      Temporary disconnection date of BSNL Postpaid Mobile & WIMAX Services
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2017 5:36PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    General Manager for Mobile Services BSNL Kerala Circle has informed that all GSM & WIMAX Postpaid customers have to pay their Mobile Bill dated 28-06-2017, before 21st July 2017 to enjoy continued service.
