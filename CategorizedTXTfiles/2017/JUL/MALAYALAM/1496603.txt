      ‘PF Near You’ on August 10th
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2017 5:56PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The monthly customer oriented interactive programme called "Nidhi Apke Nikat" or "PF near you" started by EPFO is scheduled to be held on 10 th August 2017 at E.P.F. Regional Office Pattom, Thiruvananthapuram for the stakeholders of Trivandrum and Pathanamthitta districts. The programme is intended to address the grievances of not only the employees but employers also for quick and prompt redressal. Trade unions representing the employees can also participate in the programme. Through this programme EPFO intends to invite suggestions from all concerned about the new information technology based initiatives launched for the benefits of subscribers and employers.


    The Regional P.F. Commissioner will personally hear the subscribers between 10.30 AM and 1 PM while employers and trade union representatives can meet the Regional P.F. Commissioner between 3 PM and 4 PM.


    All those who wish to lodge their complaints/ suggestions may give it in writing clearly specifying the EPF Employee/ Employer code / UAN so as to reach Regional P.F. Office, Bhavishyanidhi Bhavan, Pattom, Trivandrum 695004 before 31 st July 2017.
