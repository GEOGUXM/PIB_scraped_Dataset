      TRICHY – TIRUNELVELI – TRICHY INTERCITY EXPRESS WILL BE EXTENDED TO THIRUVANANTHAPURAM FROM 15.7.2017(SATURDAY)
      Posted On: 14 JUL 2017 4:23PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 22627 Trichy – Tirunelveli – Thiruvananthapuram Intercity express will leave Trichy at 07.05 hours and will reach Thiruvananthapuram Central at 15.25 hours on the same day. In the return direction Train No. 22628 Thiruvananthapuram – Tirunelveli – Trichy Intercity express will leave Thiruvananthapuram Central at 11.55 hours and will reach Trichy at 20.15 hours on the same day with the stoppages at Manaparai, Dindigual Jn., Madurai Jn., Virudunagar Jn., Satur, Kovilpatti, Vanchi Maniyachchi Jn. Tirunelveli J., Valliyur, Nagercoil Town & Kulitturai.
