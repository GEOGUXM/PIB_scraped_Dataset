      All India Entrance Test for Craft Instructors Training Courses on August 12th
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2017 5:49PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The all India Entrance Test for craft instructors training courses of Director General of Training under Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship will be held on 12 August 2017. This was informed by regional vocational Institute of women, Kazhakkoottam. Online applications are invited from eligible candidates on www.nimi.gov.in to appear in the entrance test which will be held in 29 centers across the country. Admissions in Private and Govt. ITOTs will also made through this entrance test only. The last date of submission of online application is 2017 July 26 th . More details will available in the website www.nimi.gov.in or www.ahibangalore.in or http://citsadmission.atichnnai.org.in
