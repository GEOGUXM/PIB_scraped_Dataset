      Seminar on Expanding Global Business
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2017 5:23PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Federation of Indian Export Organizations, set up under Ministry of Commerce, Government of India is organizing a Special Seminar - INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS FORUM - Expanding Business Globally, jointly with Sharjah Airport International Free Zone Authority at 6 pm on 11th July 2017 at Hotel Taj Vivanta, Thiruvananthapuram.


    Sharjah Airport International Free Zone, well known as the SAIF-Zone, is the premier business destination situated in the Emirate of Sharjah - the 'industrial powerhouseâ€™ of the UAE with a modern infrastructure and an emphasis on cost effectiveness. The SAIF-Zone today has various companies operating from India carrying out varied business activities like Exports, manufacturing, trading and providing services such as IT etc. Senior delegates from SAIF Zone Authority would interact with the participants on various business opportunities available in UAE. Exporters, those who wish to start Export Business, Business people, and Bank representatives can participate in the highly useful session.
