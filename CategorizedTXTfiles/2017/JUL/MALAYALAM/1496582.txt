      CHANGE IN COMPOSITION OF DIBRUGARH – KANNIYAKUMARI – DIBRUGARH VIVEK EXPRESS TRAINS
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2017 6:32PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The composition of Train No.15906 / 15905 Dibrugarh – Kanniyakumari – Dibrugarh Vivek weekly express trains has been revised. One General Second Class coach has been removed and one AC 3-tier coach has been added.


    The revised composition of the train: AC 2-tier – 1, AC 3-tier – 3, Sleeper Class – 11, General Second Class – 5, Pantry Car – 1 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.


    The revision in composition will come into effect on 22.07.2017 ex Dibrugarh and on 27.07.2017 ex Kanniyakumari.
