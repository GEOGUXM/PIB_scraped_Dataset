      Ministry of Civil Aviation

      Performance of Domestic Airlines for the year 2017
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2017 4:04PM by PIB Delhi


    Traffic data submitted by various domestic airlines has been analysed for the month of June, 2017. Following are the salient features:


    Passengers carried by domestic airlines during January-June, 2017 were 561.55 lakhs as against 475.79 lakhs during the corresponding period of previous year thereby registering a growth of 18.03%.
