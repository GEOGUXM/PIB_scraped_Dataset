      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Condition of NH Connecting Imphal to Jiribam
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2017 2:26PM by PIB Delhi


    In a written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today, the Minster of State for Road Transport & Highways and Shipping Shri Mansukh L. Mandaviya has informed that the National Highway connecting Imphal and Jiribam is in traffic worthy condition. The National Highways & Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) is currently responsible for maintenance of this road.


    Total Expenditure of Rs. 117.29 Crore has been incurred for repair, widening and maintenance of this road during the last three years. An amount of Rs. 161.40 Crore has been sanctioned during 2016-17 for maintenance of this road as per phasing given below:

                                
Financial Year  Amount          
                                
                                
                                
2016-17         Rs. 16.12 Crore 
                                
                                
                                
2017-18         Rs. 113.00 Crore
                                
                                
                                
2018-19         Rs. 32.28 Crore
