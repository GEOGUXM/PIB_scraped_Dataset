      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Supply of Subsidized Foodgrains through Ration Shops
      Posted On: 28 JUL 2017 5:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The highly subsidized prices of foodgrains under the National Food Security Act,2013 (NFSA) i.e. Rs.1/2/3 per kg for coarsegrains/ wheat/ rice have been continued upto June, 2018.
    
    Currently, about 80.55 crore persons are covered under NFSA for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains. The budget allocation for food subsidy in 2017-18 is Rs. 1,45,138.60 crore.
    
    This information was given by Shri C. R. Chaudhary, Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha, today.
