      Ministry of Shipping

      Ferry Fleets and Ghats under Inland Water Transport of India
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2017 6:07PM by PIB Delhi

      

      Eighteen ferry terminals are to be constructed in six cities of Varanasi, Patna, Munger, Bhagalpur, Kolkata and Haldia on the National Waterways-I under the Jal Marg Vikas Project (JMVP) . The Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) has engaged a reputed consultant for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for the purpose. The consultants have been tasked to identify suitable locations for construction of the ferry terminals.
