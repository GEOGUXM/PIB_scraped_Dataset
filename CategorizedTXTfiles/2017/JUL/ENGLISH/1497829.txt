      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Development of Paper Industry
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2017 4:41PM by PIB Delhi


    The paper industry is de-licensed. The production of pulp, paper and newsprint during last three years is as under:-


    (In million tons)

                   
Year     Production
                   
                   
                   
2014-15  17.03     
                   
                   
                   
2015-16  17.33     
                   
                   
                   
2016-17  17.4*     
                   
                   

    Source: Production and Capacity figures : CPPRI.


    *Estimated production.


    The following steps have been taken by the Government for promotion and development of paper industry:-


    i. Development Council for Pulp, Paper & Allied Industries (DCPPAI) has been re-constituted.


    ii. Central Pulp & Paper Research Institute (CPPRI) is an autonomous body under administrative control of Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion to promote R&D in the field of Pulp & Paper.


    This information was given by the Commerce and Industry Minister Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman in a written reply in Lok Sabha today
