      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Shri Radha Mohan Singh today interacts with the public through Facebook Live
      Lakhs of people watched this program and asked questions pertaining to the agrarian matters
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2017 6:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Minister Shri Radha Mohan Singh today interacted with the public through Facebook Live. This interaction was organised in coordination with the Ministry of Information Bureau and Press Information Bureau. The three departments of the Agriculture Ministry participated in the event. Lakhs of people watched this program and asked questions from the Minister who furnished the audience with instant replies. Several comments were put forward even prior to the event using #AskRadhaMohanSingh.
    
    During the event, the Minister discussed various schemes related to the Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Ministry such as Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, Soil Health Card, and e-NAM and also replied to the queries related to the matters pertaining to the agriculture sector. The audience asked a volley of questions and the Minister tried to respond to as many as possible even after the LIVE interaction.
    
    Post the interaction, the Minister praised the participation of the people saying that it proves that people are serious about agriculture and schemes related to it.
