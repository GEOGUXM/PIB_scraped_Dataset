      Ministry of Shipping

      MoU Signed for Development of Port Road Connectivity
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2017 6:08PM by PIB Delhi


      The Ministry of Shipping, Ministry of Road Transport and Highways and National Highways Authority of India had signed an MoU for the development of seventy-nine port road connectivity projects which includes coastal roads identified under Sagarmala Programme during the Integrated Transport and Logistic Summit held from 3 rd to 5 th May, 2017 in New Delhi. The details of 79 port connectivity projects are as follows:-
