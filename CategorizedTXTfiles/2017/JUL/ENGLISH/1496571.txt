      Change of Guard Ceremony not to be held on July 22, 2017
      Posted On: 21 JUL 2017 12:23PM by PIB Delhi


    The Change of Guard Ceremony at Rashtrapati Bhavan will not be held on Saturday (July 22, 2017) due to full dress rehearsal for assumption of office of the President of India by the President elect on July 25, 2017.
