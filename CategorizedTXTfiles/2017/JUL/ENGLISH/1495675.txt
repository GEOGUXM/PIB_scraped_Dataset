      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      CIC to organise seminar on ‘Land Records and Right to Information Act 2005’ tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 JUL 2017 7:04PM by PIB Delhi


    

    The Central Information Commission (CIC) will organise a seminar on Land Records and Right to Information Act 2005, here tomorrow.

    
    The seminar will begin with an address by the Chief Information Commissioner Shri Radha Krishna Mathur, followed by a speech by Shri Dinesh Singh, Secretary, Department of Land Resources. Speakers include Prof M. Sridhar Acharyulu, Information Commissioner, CIC, Shri Himangshu Sekhar Das, Chief Information Commissioner, Assam and Shri K. Ramanujam, Chief Information Commissioner, Gujarat.
    
    The seminar will be attended by Principal Secretaries/ Secretaries of State Government’s Land Resources/ Revenue Departments, Chief Information Commissioners and Information Commissioners from State Information Commissions and Central Information Commission. In addition, some NGOs working in the area of land records will also participate.
