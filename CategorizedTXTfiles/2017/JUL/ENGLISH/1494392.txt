      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Under-recoveries for the month of July 2017 will be Rs. 6.82 per litre for PDS Kerosene
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2017 5:47PM by PIB Delhi

      

        The Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas has reviewed international prices of crude oil and petroleum products for the month of June 2017. In the case of PDS Kerosene, the under-recoveries with effect from July 1, 2017 will be Rs 6.82 per litre. The under-recovery was Rs. 8.86 per litre in the first fortnight of June 2017 and Rs. 8.61 in the last fortnight of the month. The cash transfer to customer under DBTL will be Rs. 86.54, out of which Rs. 58.35 will be Cash Compensation on Domestic LPG by Govt. to consumers & Rs 28.19 will be the Cash compensation on Domestic LPG by OMCs towards ‘Uncompensated Costs’ to consumers.
