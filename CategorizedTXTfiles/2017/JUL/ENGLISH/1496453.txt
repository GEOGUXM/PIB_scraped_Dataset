      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      190 more working women hostels proposed to be set up for all over the country to benefit about 19000 working women
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2017 5:08PM by PIB Delhi


    190 more hostels have been proposed for all over the country during year 2017-18 to 2019-20 for benefit of about 19000 working women.


    Since its inception in 1972-73, 163 hostels have been sanctioned under the scheme of Working Women Hostel in Maharashtra and National Capital for benefit of more than 13,800 working women.


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Women & Child Development, Smt Krishna Raj in reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
