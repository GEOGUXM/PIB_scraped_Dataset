      Ministry of Shipping

      Privatisation of Dredging Corporation of India Limited
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2017 4:13PM by PIB Delhi


    A proposal for disinvestment of Dredging Corporation of India Limited is under consideration of the Government of India. The proposal is aimed at upgrading technology, improving capability, infusing capital to buy necessary dredgers and equipments and instilling experienced professional management to optimize the operations of DCI.


    This information was given by the Minister of State for Shipping and Road Transport and Highways Shri Pon. Radhakrishnan in a written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today.
