      President of India to visit West Bengal from July 14 to 15, 2017
      Posted On: 13 JUL 2017 4:13PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit West Bengal from July 14 to 15, 2017.


    On July 14, 2017, the President will inaugurate the Satya Bharti School at Kanidighi. On the same day, he will also inaugurate the 7th Edition of KKM Memorial Gold Cup- Rural Football Tournament -2017 at Mackenzie Park Football Ground.


    On July 15, 2017, the President will distribute LPG connections at Jangipur Bhawan. He will also address an Ex-Servicemen rally at Nabagram Military Station before returning to Delhi.
