      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Immunization Under UIP
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2017 4:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The Universal Immunization Programme which started in 1985 has not been renamed as Mission Indradhanush; the latter is a strategy under Universal Immunization Programme to cover unvaccinated and partially vaccinated children.


    As on 19 th July 2017, more than 247 lakh children have been vaccinated. Around 67 lakh pregnant women have been vaccinated with Tetanus toxoid. The state/UT/month/year-wise details are given below:
