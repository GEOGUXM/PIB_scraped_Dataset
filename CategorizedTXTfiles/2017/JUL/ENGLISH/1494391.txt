      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 46.72 per bbl on 30.06.2017
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2017 5:46PM by PIB Delhi

      

        The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 46.72 per barrel (bbl) on 30.06.2017. This was higher than the price of US$ 46.62 per bbl on previous publishing day of 29.06.2017.


        In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3024.47 per bbl on 30.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 3005.46 per bbl on 29.06.2017. Rupee closed weaker at Rs . 64.74 p er US$ on 30.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.47 per US$ on 29.06.2017 .
