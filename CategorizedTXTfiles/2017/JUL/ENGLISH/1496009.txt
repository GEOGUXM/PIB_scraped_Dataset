      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Cyber wing police forces
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2017 4:56PM by PIB Delhi

      

        Under Modernization of State Police Forces (MPF) Scheme, with a view to strengthen State Police Forces, assistance is provided to the State Governments in the form of grant-in-aid towards purchase of equipment,including equipment for handing Cyber Crimes, as per strategic priorities of the States incorporated in the respective State Action Plans. Several states have included cyber related equipment in their State Action Plans as per their requirements for which funds have been released. Law & order and crime investigation, being state subjects, are addressed by State specific mechanisms including for cyber crimes.
