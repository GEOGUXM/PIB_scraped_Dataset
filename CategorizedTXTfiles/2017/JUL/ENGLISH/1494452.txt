      President of India to address the annual convocation of Indian Institute of Science tomorrow
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2017 2:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Bengaluru (Karnataka) tomorrow (July 5, 2017) to address the annual convocation of the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru.
