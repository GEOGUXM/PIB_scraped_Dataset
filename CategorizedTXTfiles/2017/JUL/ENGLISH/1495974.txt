      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Smt. Smriti Zubin Irani assumes additional charge of Ministry of I&B
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2017 3:46PM by PIB Delhi


    Smt. Smriti Zubin Irani, Minister for Textiles assumed additional charge as Minister for Information & Broadcasting here today. Minister of State for Information and Broadcasting Col. Rajyavardhan Rathore, Secretary (I&B) Shri N.K. Sinha, CEO Prasar Bharati Shri Shashi Shekhar Vempati and senior officers of the Ministry were also present on the occasion.
