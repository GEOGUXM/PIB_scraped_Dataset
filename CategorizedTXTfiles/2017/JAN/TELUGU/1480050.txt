      Ministry of Defence

      Union Minister of State for Defence Dr Subhash R Bhamre Inaugurates the Seminar on 'Leveraging Defence Expenditure as a Tool for Nation Building' at CDM, Secunderabad, today
      Posted On: 05 JAN 2017 6:36PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Union Minister of State for Defence Dr. Subhash R Bhamre has inaugurated the Seminar on "Leveraging Defence Expenditure as a tool for Nation building" at the College of Defence Management (CDM) in Sainik puri, Secunderabad today.

    While delivering the inaugural address the Minister of State for Defence Dr Subhash R Bhamre said that this type of seminars essentially generate new ideas and invoke thought process amongst practitioners, decision makers and various stakeholders. He pointed that the aspiration of India as it marches ahead towards global prominence and may be even dominance in the near future.

    Dr. Subhash R Bhamre opined that the increased investment in the Defence manufacturing, capability and capacity to export Defence equipment, streamlined procurement and offset policy, boost to Research & Development efforts will help the country to develop a strong military at an affordable cost. It also helps to create employment, increase government revenues, reduce fiscal deficit, provide diplomatic leverage and thus contribute to nation building. Speaking on the occassion Rear Admiral Dushyant Singh Chouhan, Commandant, CDM said that the Seminar would lead to two days of intense and invigorating discussions with tangible recommendations to assist the policy makers.

    Eminent speakers such Lieutenant General Subrata Saha, Deputy Chief of Army Staff, Air Chief Marshal NAK Browne (Retd), Lieutenant General SL Narasimhan (Retd), Adviser NSAB, Former Ambassador RS Kalha, Smt Shobhana Joshi, IDAS (Retd), Shri R Tandon, Joint Secretary (UNP), Several senior officers of Armed forces have participated in the seminar.
