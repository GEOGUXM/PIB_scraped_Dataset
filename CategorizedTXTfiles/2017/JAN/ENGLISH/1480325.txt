      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condemns the terror attack in Kabul; condoles loss of innocent lives in the attack
      Posted On: 10 JAN 2017 11:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condemned the terror attack in Kabul. Prime Minister condoled the loss of innocent lives in the attack.

    "Strongly condemn the terror attack in Kabul and condole loss of innocent lives. India stands with Afghanistan in its fight against terrorism", the Prime Minsiter said.
