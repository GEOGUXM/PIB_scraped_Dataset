      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 54.53 per bbl on 26.01.2017
      Posted On: 27 JAN 2017 10:18AM by PIB Delhi

      

      The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 54.53 per barrel (bbl) on 26.01.2017. This was higher than the price of US$ 54.34 per bbl on previous publishing day of 24.01.2017.


      In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3716.41 per bbl on 26.01.2017 as compared to Rs. 3703.43 per bbl on 24.01.2017. Rupee closed at Rs . 68.16 p er US$ on 26.01.2017 .
