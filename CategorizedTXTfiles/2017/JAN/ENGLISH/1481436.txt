      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointments
      Posted On: 31 JAN 2017 5:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the following:


    (i) Extension in service of Shri P.P.Pandey, IPS (GJ:1980) as Director General of Police (DGP) and Director, Anti Corruption Bureau, Gujarat for three months beyond 31.01.2017.


    (ii) Premature repatriation of Shri Rupak Kumar Dutta,IPS (KTK/81) Special Secretary (CFT) in MHA to his parent cadre with immediate effect.
