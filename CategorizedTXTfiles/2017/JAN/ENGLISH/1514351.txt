      Prime Minister's Office

      PM wishes people of Meghalaya on Statehood Day.
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2017 8:45AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has wished the people of Meghalaya on their Statehood Day.
    
    "Statehood Day greetings to the people of Meghalaya. My best wishes for the development journey of the state," the Prime Minister said.
