      PM expresses sadness over death of Jawans in Kashmir avalanche
      Posted On: 26 JAN 2017 3:04PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has expressed sadness over the death of Jawans in an avalanche in Kashmir.


    "Deeply saddened at the death of our Veer jawans in an avalanche in Kashmir. Have directed the authorities for speedy search and rescue ops." the Prime Minister said.
