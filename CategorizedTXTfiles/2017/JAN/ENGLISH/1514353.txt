      Prime Minister's Office

      PM wishes people of Manipur on Statehood Day.
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2017 9:41AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has wished the people of Manipur on their Statehood Day.


    "Greetings to the people of Manipur on their Statehood Day.


    I hope Manipur will remain blessed with joy and prosperity," the Prime Minister said.
