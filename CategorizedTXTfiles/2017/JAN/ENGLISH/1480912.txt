      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Rabi Crops Sowing Crosess 628 Lakh Hactare
      Posted On: 20 JAN 2017 6:54PM by PIB Delhi

      

      As per preliminary reports received from the States, the total area sown under Rabi crops as on 20 th January 2017 stands at 628.34 lakh hectares as compared to 592.36 lakh hectare this time in 2016.

      Wheat has been sown/transplanted in 313.14 lakh hectares , rice in 19.36 lakh hectares, pulses in 157.68 lakh hectares, coarse cereals in 56.25 lakh hectares and area sown under oilseeds is 81.91 lakh hectares.

      The area sown so far and that sown during last year this time is as follows:
