      Change of Guard Ceremony to be held at 1130 hrs on January 7
      Posted On: 04 JAN 2017 12:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The Change of Guard Ceremony at the Forecourt of Rashtrapati Bhavan (entrance from Gate No. 2 or 37) will held at 1130 hrs instead of its regular timing 1000 hrs this Saturday (January 7, 2017).
