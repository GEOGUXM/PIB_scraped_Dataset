      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the nation on New Year
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2017 8:00AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has extended his New Year greetings to the nation.


    "Greetings on the start of 2017. May this year bring good health, happiness and prosperity in everyone's lives", the Prime Minister said.
