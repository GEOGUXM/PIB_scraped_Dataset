      President to deliver New Year message to Governors and Lt. Governors through video conferencing
      Posted On: 04 JAN 2017 5:12PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will deliver through Video Conferencing a New Year Message to the Governors and Lt. Governors of States/UTs using the National Knowledge Network from Rashtrapati Bhavan tomorrow (January 5, 2017).
