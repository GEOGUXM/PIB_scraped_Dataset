      Prime Minister's Office

      PM wishes people of Tripura on Statehood Day.
      Posted On: 21 JAN 2017 9:54AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has wished the people of Tripura on their Statehood Day.
    
    "On their Statehood Day, I convey my best wishes to the people of Tripura and pray for the state's all-round growth", " the Prime Minister said.
