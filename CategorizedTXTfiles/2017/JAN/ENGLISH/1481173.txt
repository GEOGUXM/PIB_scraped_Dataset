      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      National Essay Competition on Water related issues
      Posted On: 25 JAN 2017 11:22AM by PIB Delhi


    Central Ground Water Board under Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation will be conducting a National Essay Competition on Water related issues. The topic for the Essay Competition will be “Ground Water- Life line of the Nation”. Each essay should not exceed 1500 words. The Essay Competition aims to make the youth aware of the need for conservation and prevention of pollution of water and also to involve their parents and society in this noble cause. The competition is open to all in the age group up to 18 years. The Essay Competition will be held across the country in two phases. The winners will be awarded 60 cash prizes ranging from Rs. 5000 to 50000. Last date for receipt of entries by would be February 20, 2017. Please visit www.cgwb.gov.in for details of the contest.
