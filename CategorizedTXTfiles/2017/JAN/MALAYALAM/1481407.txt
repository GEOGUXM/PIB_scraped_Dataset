      Ashique Karattil assumes charge Passport Officer Thiruvananthapuram
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2017 5:33PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Shri. Ashique Karattil, an Indian Economic Service Officer of 2010 Batch has taken over charge of the post of Passport Officer, Thiruvananthapuram today (30.01.2017 ). Formerly he worked as Assistant Director Ministry of Finance and Deputy Director, Ministry of Civil Aviation.
