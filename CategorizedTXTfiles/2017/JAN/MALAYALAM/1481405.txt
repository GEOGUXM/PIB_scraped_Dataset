      CIVIL MILITARY LIAISON CONFERENCE
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2017 5:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Civil Military Liaison Conference between senior officers of Government of Kerala and Armed Forces will be held at 3pm tomorrow (31 Jan 2017) at Chief Minister’s Conference Hall, Government Secretariat, Thiruvananthapuram. The conference will be presided over by Shri.Pinarayi Vijayan, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Kerala.
    
    Civil Military Liaison Conference is a forum wherein cooperation between State Govt officials and officers of Armed Forces is strengthened and problems of Ex-Servicemen/Widows and serving soldiers are assuaged. Last CMLC was held on 4 March 2015.
