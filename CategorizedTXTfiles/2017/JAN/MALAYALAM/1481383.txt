      PM pays tributes to Mahatma Gandhi, on his Punya Tithi
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2017 5:39PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Mahatma Gandhi, on his Punya Tithi.
    
    "पूज्य बापू की पुण्यतिथि पर उनको शत-शत नमन...Tributes to beloved Bapu on his Punya Tithi", the Prime Minister said.
