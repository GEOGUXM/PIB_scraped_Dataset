      President of India pays floral tributes to former President V.V. Giri on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 4:24PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind paid floral tributes to Shri V.V. Giri, former President of India on occasion of his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (August 10, 2017).


    The President, officers and staff paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Shri V.V. Giri in the Durbar Hall of Rashtrapati Bhavan.
