      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 28 AUG 2017 12:55PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Shri Justice Dipak Misra was sworn in as the 45th Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of India at a ceremony held today (August 28, 2017) in the Durbar Hall of Rashtrapati Bhavan. He made and subscribed to the oath of office before the President.
