      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointment
      Posted On: 04 AUG 2017 5:09PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Competent Authority has approved the assignment of the additional charge of the post of Secretary, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan) to Smt. G. Latha Krishna Rao (KN:1982) Secretary, Department of Social Justice & Empowerment, with immediate effect and until further orders.
