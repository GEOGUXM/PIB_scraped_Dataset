      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Minister of WATER RESOURCES, RIVER DEVELOPMENT AND GANGA REJUVENATION be pleased to state the latest status of proposal for SAUNI Project, Phase-II
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 4:28PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    SAUNI PROJECT
    4128. SHRIMATI DARSHANA VIKRAM JARDOSH


    SHRI D.S. RATHOD


    Will the Minister of WATER RESOURCES, RIVER DEVELOPMENT AND GANGA
    REJUVENATION be pleased to state:
    (a) the latest status of proposal for SAUNI Project, Phase-II;
    (b) the time by which it is likely to be cleared; and
    (c) the details of sources of internal as well as external funding for the same along with the
    quantum of financial aid/ loan to be granted by each of the funding agencies?


    ANSWER


    THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR WATER RESOURCES, RIVER DEVELOPMENT AND
    GANGA REJUVENATION
    (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)


    (a) to (c) Government of Gujarat submitted the proposal of Saurashtra Narmada Avataran
    Irrigation (SAUNI) Project Phase II in October, 2016 for availing loan of Rs.3200 crores from
    Asian Development Bank (ADB)/World Bank or other relevant financial institutions through
    Finance Department of Gujarat. The Department of Economic Affairs (DEA) has posed the
    SAUNI-II project to ADB on 02.06.2017 for financial assistance of Rs.3200 crores.
