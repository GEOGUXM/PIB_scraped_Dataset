      PM greets people on the occasion of Janmashtami
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2017 6:37AM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM greets people on the occasion of Janmashtami


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Janmashtami.
    
    “श्रीकृष्ण जन्माष्टमी के पावन पर्व की सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं। Greetings on Janmashtami,” the Prime Minister said.
