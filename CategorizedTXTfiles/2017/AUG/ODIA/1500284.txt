      PM remembers former Prime Minister Shri Rajiv Gandhi, on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2017 9:10PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has remembered former Prime Minister Shri Rajiv Gandhi, on his birth anniversary.
    
    "On his birth anniversary, we remember former PM Shri Rajiv Gandhi and recall his contribution to the nation", the Prime Minister said.
