      Prime Minister's Office

      PM constantly monitoring the situation in Gorakhpur
      Posted On: 12 AUG 2017 9:03PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, is constantly monitoring the situation in Gorakhpur.
    
    “PM is constantly monitoring the situation in Gorakhpur. He is in constant touch with authorities from the Central and UP Governments.
    
    MoS Health Anupriya Patel and the Union Health Secretary will take stock of the situation from Gorakhpur”, the Prime Minister said.
