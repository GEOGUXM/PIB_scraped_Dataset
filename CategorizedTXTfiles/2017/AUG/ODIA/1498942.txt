      Vice President congratulates the Vice President-elect, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu
      Posted On: 05 AUG 2017 2:39PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Hamid Ansari congratulated the Vice President-elect, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu on his election as the next Vice President of India.
    
    In a telephonic conversation after the results were declared, Shri Ansari wished Shri Venkaiah Naidu the very best for his future role as the Vice President of India and the Chairman, Rajya Sabha.
