      75th anniversary of Quit India movement: Pledge for New India taken
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2017 6:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    New India Pledge was taken today in various Central Government Offices in in the State as part of 75 th anniversary of Quit India movement.


    Shri. E. Marappan Additional Director General led the pledge taking in Press Information Bureau, Thiruvananthapuram under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Thiruvananthapuram. Shri Issac Eappen, Assistant Minister led the pledge taking in Press Information Bureau, Kochi office.
