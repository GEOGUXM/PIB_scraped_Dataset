      ‘PF NEAR YOU’ COMPLAINT REDRESSAL CAMP
      Posted On: 23 AUG 2017 2:32PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) Organisation, Cochin is organizing a Complaint Redressal Camp ‘PF Near You’ at its Office in Kaloor and Thrissur, Alappuzha district offices on 11 th September, 2017 at 11.00 a.m. EPF Members/ Pensioners/Employers/ Trade Unions from Ernakulam, Alappuzha and Thrissur districts who have complaints regarding E.P.F. or E.P.F pension may send their complaints mentioning the Universal account number, Pension Payment Order Number and Telephone Number to the Public Relations Officer, Bhavishya Nidhi Bhavan, EPF Organisation, Kaloor, Cochin-682017 before 26 th August 2017. Complaints should be sent in an envelope superscribed as “Complaint Redressal Camp”. Complaints can also be registered in EPFO website www.epfindia.gov.in.
