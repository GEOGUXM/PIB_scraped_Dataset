      A Conclave on education for Social inclusion
      Posted On: 16 AUG 2017 12:24PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Indira Gandhi National Open University Regional Centre Trivandrum as part of its educational initiative is organizing a programme ‘Education for Social Inclusion: A Conclave’ in association with ATETA Life Transforming Solutions, Trivandrum and Saraswathi Vidyalaya, Vattiyoorkavu on 17th August 2017. Interactive sessions for empowering the transgender are the highlight of the conclave. Celebrity Coach Ms. Lissy Shajahan will be leading the session ‘Avesha – empowering you.’ The programmes will commence at 9.30 am in the Seminar Hall of IGNOU Regional Centre, Killipalam, Thiruvananthapuram.


    The programme will be concluding with a merit evening which will honour the students of IGNOU Regional Centre who topped the Civil Service examinations 2016.


    Shri. James Varghese IAS, Additional Chief Secretary, and Ms. Vidhu Vincent, Film Director will be the Chief Guest and Guest of Honour. Dr. B. Sukumar, Regional Director, IGNOU Regional Centre Thiruvananthapuram will preside over the function.
