      GRAMIN DAK SEVAK APPOINTMENT
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 5:30PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The online selection process of Gramin Dak Sevaks in respect of Kerala circle which was kept on hold will be opened for applicants from 21 st August to 20 th September 2017 in the websites https://indiapost.gov.in, https://appost.in/gds online, informed Departments of Posts Ernakulam Postal Division
