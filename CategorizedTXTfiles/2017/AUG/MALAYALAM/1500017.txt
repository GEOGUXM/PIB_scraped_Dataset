      HAJ PILGRIMAGE: MORE TRAINS TO STOP AT ALUVA
      Posted On: 17 AUG 2017 11:57AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 12081/12082 Janshatabdi Express Via Kottayam running between Kannur and Thiruvananthapuram central and Train No. 12695/12696 Super fast Express running between Thiruvananthapuram Central and Chennai central is provided with additional stoppage at Aluva station from 17th August to 24th August 2017 for the benefit of Haj Pilgrims.
