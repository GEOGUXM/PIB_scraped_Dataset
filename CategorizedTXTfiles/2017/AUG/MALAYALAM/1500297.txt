      CANCELLATION OF SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 21 AUG 2017 12:28PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The following special trains between Kochuveli – Karaikkal are cancelled due to poor patronage.


    1. Train No.06044 Kochuveli – Karaikkal special train, scheduled to leave Kochuveli on 23.08.2017 and 30.08.2017.


    2. Train No.06043 Karaikkal – Kochuveli special train, scheduled to leave Karaikkal on 24.08.2017 and 31.08.2017.
