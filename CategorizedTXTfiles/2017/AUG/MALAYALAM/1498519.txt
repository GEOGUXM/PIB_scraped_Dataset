      NIOS FEE RELAXATION FOR HANDLOOM WEAVERS AND THEIR CHILDREN
      Posted On: 04 AUG 2017 1:47PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    National School of Open Schooling(NIOS) will extend 75 % relaxation on fee to handloom weavers and their children belonging to SC/ST, Below Povery Line and women handloom weavers. NIOS has entered into a Memorandum of Understanding with Ministry of Textiles in this regard. Accordingly such eligible learners on joining NIOS courses needs to remit only 25 % of the prescribed fee. For more details regarding admission and courses contact: www.nios.ac.in , rdkochi@nios.ac.in, 0484-2310033/2310032.
