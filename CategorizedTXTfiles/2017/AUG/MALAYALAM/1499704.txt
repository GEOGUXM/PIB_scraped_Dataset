      Unregistered and Sped Post articles to be delivered on September 3
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2017 7:10PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    In view of the three consecutive Postal holidays from 2 nd September to 4 th September 2017, on account of Bakrid, weekly holiday and Thiruvonam respectively, Chief Postmaster Gereral has informed that unregistered postal articles and Speed Post articles will be delivered on 3-09-2017 (Sunday) from all Departmental Post Offices and Speed Posts Centres across the state.


    The policy of the Postal Department is that there should not be three or more consecutive holidays without delivery of postal articles.
