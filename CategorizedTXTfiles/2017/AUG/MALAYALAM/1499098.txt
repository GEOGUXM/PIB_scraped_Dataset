      IGNOU extends last date for submission of application for admission
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2017 5:55PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has announced its admissions to the July 2017 admission cycle. The last date for submission of applications for all programmes other than certificate programmes is extended till 18th August 2017.


    The University is offering Masters and Bachelors Degree programmes in various subjects including Rural Development, Computer Applications, Tourism Studies, English, Hindi, Philosophy, Gandhi and Peace Studies, Education, Public Administration, Economics, History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Adult Education, Extension and Development Studies, Gender and Development Studies, Distance Education, Anthropology, Commerce, Social Work, Dietetics and Food Services Management, Counselling and Family Therapy, Library and Information Science etc. IGNOU is also offering Post Graduate Diploma, Diploma, Post Graduate Certificate and Certificate programmes including Appreciation Courses on a wide range subjects.


    The University has switched to online mode of admissions except for certain merit based programmes from July 2017 admission cycle onwards. The online admission facility can be accessed through the link www.onlineadmission.ignou.ac.in


    For further details contact IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum, Rajadhani Building, Killippalam, Karamana. P.O, Thiruvananthapuram 695002, Ph: 0471-2344113/2344120 Email: rctrivandrum@ignou.ac.in
