      Independence Day celebrations by Railway Division
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2017 7:56PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Independence Day Celebrations of Thiruvananthapuram Railway Division will be held at Railway Divisional Office, Thycaud, at 09.05 hrs on 15th August 2017.


    Shri Prakash Butani, Divisional Railway Manager will unfurl the tricolour and will receive the salute in parade by Railway Protection Force. Parade will be followed by demonstration of Bomb detection and diffusal by RPF Bomb Detection Squad (BDS) and dog show by RPF dog squad.
