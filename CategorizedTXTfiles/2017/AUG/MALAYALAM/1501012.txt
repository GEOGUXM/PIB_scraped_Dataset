      ONAM VACATION FOR C.A.T
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2017 5:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT), Ernakulam Bench will remain closed from 05 th September to 08 th September 2017 on account of Onam vacation. During the period, the vacation Bench of Judicial Member Shri. U. Sarathchandran will hold sitting on 06 th September at 11.00 a.m. to hear urgent and such other matters as the bench may direct. The Registry of the Tribunal will function as usual but only applications of urgent nature will be received from 10.30 a.m. to 1.00 p.m during the vacation period.
