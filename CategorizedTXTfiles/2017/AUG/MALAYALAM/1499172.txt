      CHANGE IN TRAIN COMPOSITION
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 4:25PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    One unreserved coach has been reduced from the composition in the service of Train No.15906/ 15905 Dibrugarh-Kanyakumari-Dibrugarh Vivek Express due to technical reasons. The revised composition of this train will become effective form the service which left Dibrugarh from 05/08/2017 and that which leaves Kanyakumari from 10/08/2017.


    The new composition of the train will be as under-


    2-tier AC coach- one ; 3-tier AC coaches-three; Sleeper class coaches-Eleven; Unreserved coaches-four and Luggage cum brake van coaches-2
