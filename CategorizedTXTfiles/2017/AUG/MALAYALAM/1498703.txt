      VISIT OF DEPUTY CHIEF OF AIR STAFF TO INDIAN NAVAL ACADEMY
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2017 4:25PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Air Marshal R Nambiar, AVSM, VM & BAR, Deputy Chief of Air Staff (DCAS), arrived at the Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala today, Monday, 07 August 2017.


    On his arrival, he was received by Vice Admiral SV Bhokare, AVSM, YSM, NM, Commandant, INA. The DCAS, during his stay at INA, was briefed on training facilities and processes at INA. He was also taken on a guided tour of all training and station facilities.


    The visiting dignitary expressed happiness at seeing the outstanding facilities and clean station environment. He is on scheduled to depart INA on PM 07 Aug 17.
