      Envoys of four Nations Present Credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 23 AUG 2017 6:37PM by PIB Guwahati

      Envoys of Malawi, Uzbekistan, Austria and Indonesia presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (August 23, 2017).
      The envoys who presented their credentials were: -
      1. His Excellency Mr. George C. Mkondiwa, High Commissioner of Malawi
      2. His Excellency Mr. Farhod Arziev, Ambassador of Uzbekistan
      3. Her Excellency Mrs. Brugutte Oppinger Walchshofer, Ambassador of Austria
      4. His Excellency Mr. Sidharto Reza Suryodipuro, Ambassador of Indonesia
