      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Kharif Crop Sowing Crosses 1013 Lakh Hectare Area
      Posted On: 25 AUG 2017 5:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The total sown area as on 25 th August 2017, as per reports received from States, stands at 1013.83 lakh hectare as compared to 1019.60 lakh hectare at this time last year.


    It is reported that rice has been sown/transplanted in 358.28 lakh ha, pulses in 135.96 lakh ha, coarse cereals in 178.85 lakh ha, sugarcane in 49.78 lakh hectare and cotton in 119.67 lakh ha.
