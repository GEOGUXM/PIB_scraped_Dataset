      Ministry of Earth Science

      Severe Weather Warning 10th to 14th August
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 4:51PM by PIB Delhi


    India Meteorological Department has issued severe weather warning for the next four days as below:


    10 August :♦ Heavy to very heavy rain at a few places with extremely heavy falls at isolated places very likely over Arunachal Pradesh and Sub­Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim; heavy to very heavy with extremely heavy falls at isolated places over Assam & Meghalaya; heavy to very heavy at isolated places over Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura and heavy at isolated places over Himachal Pradesh, West Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Konkan & Goa, Coastal Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Rayalaseema, Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu & Pudducherry.


    11 August:♦ Heavy to very heavy rain at a few places with extremely heavy falls at isolated places very likely over Sub­Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim; heavy to very heavy with extremely heavy falls at isolated places over Bihar, East Uttar Pradesh and Assam & Meghalaya; heavy to very heavy at isolated places over Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Arunachal Pradesh and Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura and heavy at isolated places over West Uttar Pradesh and Jharkhand.


    12 August : ♦ Heavy to very heavy rain at a few places with extremely heavy falls at isolated places very likely over Sub­Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim and Bihar; heavy to very heavy with extremely heavy falls at isolated places over Assam & Meghalaya; heavy to very heavy at isolated places over Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand and East Uttar Pradesh; heavy at isolated places over West Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Gangetic West Bengal, Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura and Tamilnadu & Puducherry.


    13 August : ♦ Heavy to very heavy rainfall at a few places over Sub­Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim; heavy to very heavy at isolated places over East Uttar Pradesh and Bihar; heavy at isolated places over Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Jharkhand, Gangetic West Bengal, Odisha, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Assam & Meghalaya, Coastal Andhra Pradesh, South Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu & Puducherry.


    14 August : ♦ Heavy to very heavy rainfall at isolated at places over Sub­Himalayan West Bengal & Sikkim and Jharkhand; heavy at isolated places over Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Punjab, East Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, Assam & Meghalaya, Coastal Andhra Pradesh, South Interior Karnataka, and Tamilnadu & Pudducherry
