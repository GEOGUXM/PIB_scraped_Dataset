      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 51.59 per bbl on 09.08.2017
      Posted On: 10 AUG 2017 3:23PM by PIB Delhi

      

        The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 51.59* per barrel (bbl) on 09.08.2017.


        In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs. 3288.67 per bbl on 09.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 3288.77 per bbl on 08.08.2017. Rupee closed weaker at Rs . 63.75 per US$ on 09.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 63.74 per US$ on 08.08.2017 .
