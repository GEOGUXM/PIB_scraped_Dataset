      Election Commission

      Bye-election to the Karnataka Legislative Council by the Members of Legislative Assembly.
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2017 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


    There is one casual vacancy in the State Legislative Council of Karnataka occurred due to death of following sitting member elected by the Members of Legislative Assembly.

                                                                     
Sl. No.  Name of Member     Date of retirement  Remarks              
                                                                     
                                                                     
                                                                     
1.       Smt. Vimala Gowda  17.06.2018          Demised on 17.04.2017
