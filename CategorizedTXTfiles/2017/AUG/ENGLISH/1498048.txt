      Ministry of Finance

      Disposal of Complaints received by bank Ombudsman
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2017 7:10PM by PIB Delhi


        The. Bank-wise details regarding number of complaints received by Banking Ombudsmen (BO) and disposed of in the last three years i.e. 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17 are given in Annexure.


        Majority of the complaints received under the Banking Ombudsman Scheme are resolved through conciliation and mediation of BO. In very few cases, where conciliation and mediation fails to achieve mutually agreeable settlement, BO passes an Award or rejects the complaint.
