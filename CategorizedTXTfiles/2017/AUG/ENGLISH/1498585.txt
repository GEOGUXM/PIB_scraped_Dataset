      Ministry of Finance

      39 Debt Recovery Tribunals (DRTs) functioning in the country; 6 new DRTs started functioning in the financial year 2016-17
      Posted On: 04 AUG 2017 5:17PM by PIB Delhi


    At present 39 Debt Recovery Tribunals (DRTs) are functioning in the country.


    Government had decided to open six new DRTs at Ernakulam (Kerala), Bengaluru (Karnataka), Hyderabad (Telangana), Dehradun (Uttarakhand), Chandigarh (UT) and Siliguri (West Bengal). All these DRTs have already been established and started functioning in the financial year 2016-17.


    This was stated by Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar, Minister of State for Finance in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
