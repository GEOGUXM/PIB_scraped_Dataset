      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Minimum daily wage for unskilled non-agricultural workers
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2017 4:15PM by PIB Delhi


    In the Central Sphere, the Central Government has issued a notification on 19.01.2017 to revise the minimum rates of wages from Rs.250 per day to Rs.350 per day for unskilled non-agricultural workers in ‘C’ category regions based on the recommendations of 15th Indian Labour Conference and Jugdement of Hon’ble Supreme Court in the Reptakos & Co. Vs its Workers.


    This information was given by Shri Bandaru Dattatreya, the Minister of State (IC) for Labour & Employment, in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha, today.
