      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 51.04* per bbl on 28.08.2017
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2017 11:10AM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 51.04* per barrel (bbl) on 28.08.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs. 3259.93 per bbl on 28.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 3270.01 per bbl on 25.08.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 63.87 per US$ on 28.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.07 per US$ on 25.08.2017 .
