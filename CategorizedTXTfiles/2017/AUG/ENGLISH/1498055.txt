      Ministry of Civil Aviation

      Domestic Market Share of Air India
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2017 7:57PM by PIB Delhi

      

        The share of Air India, in terms of domestic passengers, for the year 2016-17 is about 14.2 percent of the total scheduled operations for domestic passengers. The growth of domestic air passengers in scheduled operations in the last three years in the country is as follows:-

        This information was given by Minister of State for Civil Aviation Shri Jayant Sinha in a written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
