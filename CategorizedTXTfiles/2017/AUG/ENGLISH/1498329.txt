      Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation

      Gross Domestic Product
      Posted On: 03 AUG 2017 5:25PM by PIB Delhi


    Annual estimates of Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) and Per Capita Income (measured as per capita net state domestic product) at constant and current prices are prepared by all the States/UTs except UTs of Daman & Diu, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Lakshadweep. As per latest reports, data for the 33 States/UTs is available till the year 2014-15. The information is given in the statement at Annexure.


    Estimates of Gross Domestic Product (GDP), Net Domestic Product (NDP) and National Income, measured as Net National Income (NNI) at current prices and GDP growth rate at constant prices (at base year 2011-12) during last three years are as under:
