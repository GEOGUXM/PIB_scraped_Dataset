      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointments
      Posted On: 05 AUG 2017 12:00PM by PIB Delhi

        (1) Shri Atul Kumar Tiwari, IAS (KN:1990), as Joint Secretary, Department of Rural Development, from the date of assumption of the charge of the post, for a tenure of five years or until further orders, whichever is earlier vice Shri Santosh A. Mathew, IAS (BH:1985);
