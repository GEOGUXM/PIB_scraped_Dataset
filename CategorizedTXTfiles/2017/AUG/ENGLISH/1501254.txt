      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 50.52 per bbl on 30.08.2017
      Posted On: 31 AUG 2017 10:33AM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 50.52 per barrel (bbl) on 30.08.2017. This was lower than the price of US$ 50.56 per bbl on previous publishing day of 29.08.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs. 3230.09 per bbl on 30.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 3236.45 per bbl on 29.08.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 63.94 per US$ on 30.08.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.02 per US$ on 29.08.2017 .
