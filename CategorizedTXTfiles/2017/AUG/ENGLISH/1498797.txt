      Ministry of Defence

      Military Ties With Foreign Countries
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2017 3:11PM by PIB Delhi


      India has signed agreements / Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) on defence cooperation with several countries, which cover a range of activities including training, exchange of personnel and financial aspects of cooperation. A list of such agreements / MOUs signed over the past three years is as under:-
