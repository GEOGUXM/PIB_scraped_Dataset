      Ministry of Railways

      Ministry of Railways is launching various new train services
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2017 6:39PM by PIB Delhi


    Indian Railways is launching several new train services on different routes during this weekend (12th &13th August,2017). Thousand of passengers from the states of UP, Odisha, Bihar, Punjab, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Gujarat & Jammu & Kashmir will benefit from these services. Minister of Railways, Shri Suresh Prabhakar Prabhu is inaugurating some of these train services on 12th August 2017 through video conferencing from New Delhi & some trains services from Mumbai on 13th August 2017.


    Again on 13th August, 2017, Minister of State for Railways & Minister of State for Communications (I/c) Shri Manoj Sinha is inaugurating some train services from Ghazipur.
