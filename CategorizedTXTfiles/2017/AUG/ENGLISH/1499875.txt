      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointment
      Posted On: 16 AUG 2017 3:52PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the appointment of Dr. I.V. Subba Rao, IAS (Retd) as Secretary to the Vice President of India, on contract basis, in the rank and pay of Secretary, for a period of 02 years initially.
