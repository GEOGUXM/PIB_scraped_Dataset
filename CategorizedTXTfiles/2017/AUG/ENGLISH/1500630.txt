      Election Commission

      Bye-elections to the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Council by the Members of Legislative Assembly.
      Posted On: 24 AUG 2017 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


      Four (4) casual vacancies in the State Legislative Council of Uttar Pradesh have occurred due to the resignation of sitting members of Uttar Pradesh Legislative Council elected by the Members of State Legislative Assembly. The details are given as under: -

                                                                 
Sl.No.  Name of Member        Date of Vacancy  Date of retirement
