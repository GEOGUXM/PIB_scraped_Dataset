      Ministry of Civil Aviation

      Funding From States for Operating in Financially Non-Viable Routes
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2017 4:32PM by PIB Delhi

      
      The Alliance Air, a wholly owned subsidiary of Air India, has been operating flights under Viability Gap Funding (VGF) arrangements with the following State Governments on Cost minus Revenue equals to VGF arrangements:
      
      1. With North Eastern Council (NEC) in the North East, Alliance Air operates flights with ATR42 aircraft on the following sectors:
      
      Kolkata/ Guwahati/ Lilabari & v.v. - 4 flights per week
      
      Kolkata/ Guwahati/ Tezpur & v.v. - 3 flights per week
      
      Kolkata/ Shillong/ Kolkata - 6 flights per week
      
      2. With Diu Administration, Alliance Air operates flights on Mumbai/ Diu/ Mumbai sectors - 4 flights per week with ATR72 aircraft.
      
      3. With Lakshadweep Administration, Alliance Air operates flights on Kochi/ Agatti/ Kochi sectors and Bangalore/ Kochi/ Agatti & v.v. sectors with ATR72 aircraft
      
      This information was given by the Minister of State in the Ministry of Civil Aviation Shri Jayant Sinha in written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
