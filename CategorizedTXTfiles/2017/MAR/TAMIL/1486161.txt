      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates countrymen on the passage of GST Bill
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2017 4:00PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has congratulated the countrymen on the passage of GST Bill.


    “GST बिल पास होने पर सभी देशवासियों को बधाई | नया साल , नया कानून , नया भारत! ”, the Prime Minister said.
