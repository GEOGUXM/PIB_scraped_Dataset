      PM congratulates Capt. Amarinder Singh on taking oath as Punjab CM
      Posted On: 16 MAR 2017 1:27PM by PIB Chennai

      Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Capt. Amarinder Singh on taking oath as Chief Minister of Punjab.
      

      “Congratulations Capt. Amarinder Singh on taking oath as CM. Wishing you the very best in working for Punjab’s development. ,” the Prime Minister said.
