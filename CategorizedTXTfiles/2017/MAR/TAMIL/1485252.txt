      PM greets Parsi community on Navroz
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2017 6:39PM by PIB Chennai


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the Parsi community on Navroz, the Parsi New Year.
    
    “Navroz Mubarak to all Parsi friends! Praying for a year that is full of happiness, prosperity and good health”, the Prime Minister said.
