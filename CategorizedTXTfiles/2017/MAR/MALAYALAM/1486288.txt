      NETRAVATI EXPRESS RESCHEDULED
      Posted On: 30 MAR 2017 3:05PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 16346, Thiruvananthapuram Central- Lokmanya Thilak, Netravathi Express scheduled to leave Thiruvananthapuram Central at 09.50 hrs on 31st March 2017(Friday) is rescheduled to start at 13.50 hrs on same day, as traffic regulation is imposed in Central Railway in connection with commissioning of double line between Roha and Nagathane stations.
