      ADDITIONAL STOPS FOR SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2017 5:36PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 06547/06548, Special trains between Ernakulam and Yeswantpur is now allotted with stoppages at Ottappalam and Thiruppathur stations. Similarly, Train No. 06035/06036, Special train between Ernakulam and Rameswaram is now allotted with additional stoppage at Udumalapettai station.
