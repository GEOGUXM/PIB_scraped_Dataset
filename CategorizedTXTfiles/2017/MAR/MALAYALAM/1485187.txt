      Observatory to be opened for public on March 23
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2017 5:12PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      	 Every year March 23rd is celebrated as World Meteorological Day with a pertinent topic as a theme of celebration. The theme for this year is "Understanding Clouds".
      	 The Meteorological Centre at the Observatory in Thiruvananthapuram is celebrating World Meteorological Day by keeping it open to public on 23rd March 2017.
