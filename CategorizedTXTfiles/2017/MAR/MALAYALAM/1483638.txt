      VELANKANNI SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2017 5:33PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      Southern railway will run special fare special trains in Kochuveli - Karaikkal sector via Nagercoil to clear extra rush of passengers during summer season.
      Train No. 06044, Kochuveli- Karaikkal weekly special fare Train will start at 15.15 hrs from Kochuveli on Wednesdays starting from 08th March to 28th June 2017, to reach Karaikkal at 03.45 hrs on next day. In the return direction, Train No.06043, Karaikkal- Kochuveli special Fare weekly trains will start at 22.45 hrs on Thursdays , starting from 09th March to 29th June 2017 and will reach Kochuveli at 12.25 hrs, on Fridays.
      	 With a composition of Two 2AC, Three 3AC, 11 sleeperclass and 2 General Coaches these trains will have stoppages at Trivandrum, Nagercoil Town Tirunelveli, Kovilpatti, Satur, Virudunagar, Madurai, Dindugal, Thiruchirappally, Thanjavur, Thiruvarur, Nagapattinam and Nagore stations.
