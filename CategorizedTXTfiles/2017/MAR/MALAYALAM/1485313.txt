      Interactive session on taxation of deposits with co-operative societies
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2017 5:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      	 Income Tax Department is in the possession of large volume of information on deposits in co-operative societies and is receiving queries from public in this regard.
      		
      	 Income tax department is organizing an interactive session on 24.03.2017 (Friday) from 10.00 am to 1.00pm at Income Tax Office, Kowdiar, Thiruvananthapuram to clear the doubts of the public on the taxation of deposits with co-operative societies. Senior officers of the Income Tax department will address the queries of the public.
