      Postal exam postponed
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2017 5:23PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      The Postman / Mail Guard Examination of Kerala Postal Circle which was scheduled to be held on 2017 April 9 (9-4-2017 Sunday) is postponed to 2017 May 7 (7-5-2017 Sunday). This was informed by Chief Postmaster General, Kerala Circle.
