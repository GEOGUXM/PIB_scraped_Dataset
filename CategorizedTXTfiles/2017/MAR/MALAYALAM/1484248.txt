      TRAIN REGULATION FOR SUB WAY WORK
      Posted On: 13 MAR 2017 5:45PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


      Southern Railway will take up left over construction work on a subway between Iraniyel and Kuliturai stations on 19th and 20th March 2017. Train No. 16605, Mangaluru-Nagercoil Ernad express on 19th March will be short terminated at Tribvandrum central in connection with the work. Subsequently Train No. 16606, Nqagercoil-Mangaluru Ernad Express will originate from Thiruvananthapuram central on 20th March 2017.
