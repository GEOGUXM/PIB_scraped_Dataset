      TRAINS AUGMENTED
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2017 5:38PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      To clear extra rush of passengers during summer vacation Southern railways will attach one additional 3 AC coach on select trains for a period of one month with immediate effect.
      1. Train No. 19260/19259- express train between Bhavnagar & Kochuveli
      2. Train No.19578/19577 Hapa- Tirunelveli express
      3. Train No. 19262/19261 Porbunder-kochuveli express and
      4. Tr. No. 17229/17230, Thiruvananthapuram Hydrabad Express will be attached with additional coach till 04th June 2017...
