      REGISTRATION OPEN TILL 27 MARCH FOR ARMY RECRUITMENT RALLY AT KOLLAM
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2017 4:33PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      The website for registrations for recruitment into the army, www.joinindianarmy.nic.in will not be available from 20th March to 24th March 2017. However, the candidates can register from 25th till 27th March. The last date for the online registration is 27th March. The rally is scheduled to be held from April 11 to 21 at Lal Bahadur Shastri Stadium, Kollam.
      	 Any candidate having problems in registration is advised to contact ARO, Trivandrum on 0471-2351762, send a mail to trivandrumaro@gmail.com or visit ARO, Trivandrum during visiting hours. Candidates without online registration will not be permitted to attend the rally.
      	 The rally is open for all the male candidates of seven southern districts of Kerala (i.e,Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha, Idukki & Ernakulam) for the category of Soldier General Duty, Soldier Technical, Soldier Clerk/Store Keeper Technical, Soldier Nursing Assistant & Soldier Tradesmen.
