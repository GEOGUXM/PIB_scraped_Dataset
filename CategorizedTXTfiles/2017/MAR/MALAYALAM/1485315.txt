      TRACK DOUBLING WORK IN KOTTAYAM SECTION MORE TRAINS TO BE REGULATED
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2017 5:07PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


      In connection with the ongoing track doubling work between Thiruvalla and Changanasseri stations and yard remodeling at Changanasseri station, Trains in Kottayam section will be regulated as under.

      • 	 Tr. No. 16347, Thiruvananthapuram- Mangaluru Express scheduled to leave Thiruvananthapuram Central at 20.40 hrs is likely to be detained for 45 minutes in Kottayam section on 24th to 26th March 2017(Friday, Saturday & Sunday)
      • 	 Train No. 16333, Veravel- Thiruvananthapuram Express on 24th March and Tr. No. 16335, Gandhidham-Nagercoil express on 25th March 2017 will also be detained for 45 minutes in Kottayam section.
      Main work of connecting new line to existing lines will be taken up on 28th March and more regulation will be effective.
      Regulation on 28th March 2017 (Tuesday only)
      Trains Diverted Via Alappuzha
       	 Tr. No. 16526, Bengaluru-Kanyakumari Express
       	 Tr. No. 12081, Kannur-Thiruvananthapuram Janshatabdi Express
       	 Train No.17229, Thiruvananthapuram-Hydrabad Sabari Express
       	 Train No. 16382,Kanyakumari-Mumbai CST express
       	 Train No.12626/12625, Kerala Express between NewDelhi -Thiruvananthapuram in both directions. All the diverted trains will be provided stoppages at Ernakulam Junction, Cherthala, Alappuzha and Harippad stations.
      Trains fully cancelled
       	 Train No. 56394/56393 Kollam –Kottayam Passenger service at 08.35 hrs and its return trip at 17.45 hrs.
       	 Train No. 66307/66308, Ernakulam-Kollam MEMU service at 05.25 hrs from Ernakulam and its return trip at 11.10 hrs from Kollam.
       	 Train No. 66300/66301, Kollam- Ernakulam MEMU service at 07.40 hrs and its return trip at 14.40 hrs from Ernakulam Jn.
       	 Train No. 66302/66303, Kollam-Ernakulam MEMU via Alappuzha at 08.50 hrs and its return trip from Ernakulam at 12.20 hrs.
       	 Train No. 56381/56382,Ernakulam-Kayamkulam passenger via Alappuzha at 10.00 hrs from Ernakulam and its return journey at 13.00 hrs from Kayamkulam.
      Trains partially cancelled
       	 Train No. 56365/56366, Passenger trains between Punalur and Guruvayoor will remain partially cancelled between Kottayam and Punalur.
       	 Train No. 56387/56388,Ernakulam-Kayamkulam Passenger at 11.30 hrs from Ernakulam and its return trip at 17.00 hrs from Kayamkulam will remain partially cancelled between Kayamkulam and Kottayam.
