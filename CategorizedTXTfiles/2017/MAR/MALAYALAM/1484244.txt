      SAINIK SCHOOL ENTRANCE EXAMINATION FINAL RANK LIST PUBLISHED
      Posted On: 13 MAR 2017 5:40PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      The final merit list of All India Sainik Schools Entrance Exam along with the joining instruction for the admission into Class VI & IX for the academic year 2017-18 is published in the school website www.sainikschooltvm.nic.in.
      The qualified candidates will be intimated by post. All medically unfit candidates may apply for review medical by submitting an application through parents/guardians, addressed to the Principal, Sainik School, Kazhakootam on or before 21 Mar 2017 along with opinion of a Govt. Medical Practitioner.
