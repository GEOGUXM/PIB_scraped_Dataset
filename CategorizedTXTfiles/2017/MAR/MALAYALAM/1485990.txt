      National Award for Ananthapuri FM
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2017 3:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Ente Bhoomi (My Earth) a twenty minute radio documentary written and produced by Biju Mathew, Programme Executive, Ananthapuri FM, Trivandrum won the Akashvani Annual Award 2016 under the category radio documentary.


    Using the innumerable scope of sound mixing ‘Ente Bhoomi’ tries to convey the message of environmental awareness. It fosters a sense of connection to the natural world. Promotes sustainable development and encourages conservation of irreplaceable natural resources and vulnerable plant and animal species. The documentary will be rebroadcast on April 22, Earth Day.
