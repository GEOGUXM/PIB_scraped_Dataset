      BSNL fest in connection with Women's day
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2017 5:48PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      In connection with International Women’s Day Celebrations, women employees of BSNL Chief General Manager Office will hold a MEGA MELA on 7th & 8th March 2017 from 9 AM to 7 PM at Chief General Manager Office, PMG Junction, Thiruvananthapuram.
      	 All arrangements have been made to clear doubts about BSNL Plans, Tariffs, etc. in this fest. There is also a MEGA OFFER OF “FREE 3G SIM”. Along with this, women employees will also conduct an Exhibition Cum Sale of various Home made delicious food products and Mural Paintings on Canvas and Fabric for the benefit of Cancer patients .
      Lucky Draw & Quiz Competitions have also organized as part of the event.
