      Ministry of Finance

      ALL INCOME-TAX OFFICES IN STATE OF GUJARAT SHALL REMAIN OPEN ON 25 AND 26 MARCH, 2017
      Posted On: 24 MAR 2017 1:44PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    AHMEDABAD, 24-03-2017

      In order to facilitate the general public to take advantage of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojna, 2016 (PMKGY-2016) which is open upto 31 st March, 2017, for declaration of undisclosed income, all Income-tax Offices in state of Gujarat shall remain open on 25 th and 26 th March, 2017 (Saturday & Sunday) on normal office hours.
