      Ministry of External Affairs

      India Condemns Twin Terror Attack in Kabul
      Posted On: 02 MAR 2017 12:50PM by PIB Delhi


    India condemns twin terror attack in Kabul.

      We strongly condemn the twin terror attacks in Kabul on 1 March 2017, which have resulted in death of more than a dozen people.
      We convey our heartfelt condolences to the people and Government of Afghanistan for the loss of lives and property. It is our sincere hope that the injured will make early recovery.
      India has been a victim of terrorist violence and understands the pain and suffering of the people of Afghanistan. We reiterate our resolve to work with Afghanistan to bring perpetrators of terrorist violence to the justice they deserve wherever they may be.
