      Change of Guard Ceremony not to be held on April 1
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2017 2:19PM by PIB Delhi


      The Change of Guard Ceremony at Rashtrapati Bhavan will not be held on Saturday (April 1, 2017) due to functions associated with the visit of Prime Minister of Malaysia to India.
