      PM urges people to take the pledge to save every drop of water, on World Water Day
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2017 2:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has urged people to take the pledge to save every drop of water, on World Water Day.
    
    "On World Water Day lets pledge to save every drop of water. When Jan Shakti has made up their mind, we can successfully preserve Jal Shakti.
    
    This year, UN has chosen a valid theme- wastewater. It will help further awareness on water recycling and why it is essential for our planet", the Prime Minister said.
