      President of India’s message on the occasion of National Day of Ghana
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2017 6:00PM by PIB Delhi

      
      The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee has extended his greetings and felicitations to the Government and people of the Republic of Ghana on the occasion of their National Day (March 6, 2017).
      
      In a message to His Excellency Mr. Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo, the President of the Republic of Ghana, the President has said, “On behalf of the Government, the people of India and on my own behalf, it gives me great pleasure to extend warm greetings and felicitations to you and to the people of the Republic of Ghana on your National Day.
      
      India and Ghana have a time-tested relationship of friendship and robust co-operation. I am confident that our multi-faceted relations will grow from strength to strength in the coming years.
      
      I take this opportunity to convey our good wishes for Your Excellency’s good health and well-being as well as for the progress and prosperity of the friendly people of the Republic of Ghana”.
