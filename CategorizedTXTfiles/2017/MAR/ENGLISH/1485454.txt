      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      63% of operational Anganwadi Centres have sanitation facilities as on December, 2016
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2017 5:05PM by PIB Delhi


    There are 14 lakhs sanctioned Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in the country out of which 13.52 lakhs AWCs are operational as on 31.12.2016. Toilet facilities are available in 8.52 lakh AWCs as on 31.12.2016.


    The Ministry of Women and Child Development in convergence with Ministry of Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Institutions has formulated action plan to construct 4 lakhs AWC buildings with child friendly toilets in the next 4 years i.e. up to 2019 under MGNREGS. During 2015-16 and 2016-17, 89536 AWC buildings (including toilets) were approved for construction.
