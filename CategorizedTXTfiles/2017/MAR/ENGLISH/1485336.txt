      PM expresses grief over terror attack in London
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2017 11:09AM by PIB Delhi

      Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has expressed grief over the terror attack in London.
      “Deeply saddened by the terror attack in London. Our thoughts and prayers are with the victims and their families.
      At this difficult moment, India stands with UK in the fight against terrorism”, the Prime Minister said.
