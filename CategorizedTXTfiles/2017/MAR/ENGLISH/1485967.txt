      Credit Sale and advances by CPSUs
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2017 3:04PM by PIB Delhi

      

        As per information available from the Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs), the CPSE-wise details of long term trade receivables for the last three years is given at Annex.


        As per Comptroller & Auditor General of India (C & AG) Reports laid in the Parliament in the respective years, the following instances of deviation from norms for credit sale / advances were noticed and reported in the Reports of C & AG of India for the last three years.
