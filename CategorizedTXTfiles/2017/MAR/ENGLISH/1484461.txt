      Ministry of Culture

      Installation of Point of Sale Machines at Monuments
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2017 6:59PM by PIB Delhi

      

      Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has started installing Point of Sale (POS) machines at the ticketed monuments to encourage cashless purchase of entry tickets. The detail of monuments is given below.


      Out of 3686 centrally protected monuments, only 116 monuments are ticketed monuments.


      No specific target has been fixed. However, service provider Banks have been requested to install the POS machine at the ticketed monuments on priority.
