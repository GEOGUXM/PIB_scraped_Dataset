      PM congratulates Manohar Parrikar on being sworn in as Goa CM
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2017 6:31PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Shri Manohar Parrikar on being sworn in as Chief Minister of Goa.
    
    “Congratulations to Manohar Parrikar and his team on being sworn in. My best wishes in taking Goa to new heights of progress,” the Prime Minister said.
