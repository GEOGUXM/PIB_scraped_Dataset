      President of India Presents Dr. B. C. Roy National Awards for the years 2014, 2015 and 2016 and Hari Om Ashram Alembic research awards for the years 2008, 2009, 2010, 2014, 2015, 2016
      Posted On: 28 MAR 2017 5:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee presented Dr. B. C. Roy National Awards for the years 2014, 2015 and 2016 and Hari Om Ashram Alembic Research Awards for the years 2008, 2009, 2010, 2014, 2015, 2016 at a function held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (March 28, 2017).
