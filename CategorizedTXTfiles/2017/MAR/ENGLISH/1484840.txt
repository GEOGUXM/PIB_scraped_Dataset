    So far, an amount of Rs. 735.99 crore has been released to implementing agencies under ISDS.


    As per the Management Information System of ISDS, a total of 54787 persons were trained during last three years in Karnataka, of which 42,6 91 persons have been provided placement.


    The above information was given by the Minister of State, Textiles, Shri Ajay Tamta today, in a written reply to a Rajya Sabha question.
