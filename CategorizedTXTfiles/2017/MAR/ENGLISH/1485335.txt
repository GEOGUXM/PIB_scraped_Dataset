      President of India to inaugurate the third Adri Silver Jubilee International Conference on “Bihar and Jharkhand: shared history to shared vision” tomorrow
      Posted On: 23 MAR 2017 1:02PM by PIB Delhi

      The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Bihar (Patna) tomorrow (March 24, 2017) where he will inaugurate the third Asian Development Research Institute (ADRI) Silver Jubilee International Conference on “Bihar and Jharkhand: Shared History to Shared Vision”.
