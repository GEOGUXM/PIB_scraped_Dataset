      The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee presented Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri awards today (March 30, 2017) at a Civil Investiture Ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan.


      Among the dignitaries present on the occasion were the Vice President of India, Prime Minister, Speaker of Lok Sabha and Union Minister of Home Affairs.
