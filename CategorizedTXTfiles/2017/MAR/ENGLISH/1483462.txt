      Vice President condoles the passing away of Shri Abdul Salam
      Posted On: 01 MAR 2017 2:01PM by PIB Delhi


      The Vice President of India, Shri M. Hamid Ansari has condoled the passing away of the Member of Parliament (RS), Shri Abdul Salam. In a message, he said that Shri Salam will be remembered for his service to the people of the Manipur.

      Following is the Vice President’s message :

      “I am deeply grieved to hear about the demise of Shri Abdul Salam, Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha.

      A senior Parliamentarian from Manipur, Shri Salam will be remembered for his service to the people of the State.

      I convey my deepest condolences to the bereaved members of the family and join the nation in praying for eternal peace for the departed soul.”
