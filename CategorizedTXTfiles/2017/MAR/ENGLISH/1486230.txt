      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      More than two lakh beneficiaries covered under Maternity Benefit Programme during 2016-17 till date
      Posted On: 30 MAR 2017 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


      Maternity Benefit Programme is a centrally sponsored scheme, under which the maternity benefits of Rs.6,000/- per beneficiary is provided to Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM). As per the existing norms maternity benefit is provided to PW&LM who are of 19 years of age and above, for first two live births. As reported by State Governments/Union Territory Administrations, 2,07,720 beneficiaries were covered under the Maternity Benefit Programme during 2016-17, as on date .
