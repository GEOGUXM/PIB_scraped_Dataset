      Prime Minister's Office

      Mr. Bimalendra Nidhi, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Home Affairs of Nepal calls on PM
      Posted On: 17 MAR 2017 6:08PM by PIB Delhi


    Mr. Bimalendra Nidhi, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Home Affairs of Nepal called on Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today.
    
    Mr. Bimalendra Nidhi briefed the Prime Minister about recent developments in Nepal.
    
    The Prime Minister said that India is fully committed to strengthening the age-old ties of friendship and kinship between the people of both countries, and to support the Government of Nepal in all its endeavours for socio-economic development of Nepal.
