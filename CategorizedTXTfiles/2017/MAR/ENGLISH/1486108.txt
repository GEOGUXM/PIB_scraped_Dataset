      President of India to Inaugurate ‘Namami Brahmaputra’ Festival tomorrow
      Posted On: 30 MAR 2017 11:45AM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Assam (Guwahati)tomorrow (March 31, 2017) where he will inaugurate the ‘Namami Brahmaputra’ festival.


    The five day long ‘Namami Brahmaputra’ festival is being organized by the Government of Assam with aim to strengthen the bonds among various communities of Assam.
