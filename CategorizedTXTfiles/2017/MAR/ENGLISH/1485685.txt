      President of India’s message on the eve of National Day of Bangladesh
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2017 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee has extended his greetings and felicitations to the Government and people of the People's Republic of Bangladesh on the eve of their National Day (March 26, 2017).
    
    In a message to His Excellency Mr. Md. Abdul Hamid, the President of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, the President has said, “On behalf of the Government, the people of India and on my own behalf, it is with immense pleasure that I extend warm greetings and felicitations to Your Excellency and the people of Bangladesh on the occasion of your National Day.
    
    It is a matter of satisfaction that the close relations between India and Bangladesh have substantially expanded in recent years. We have succeeded in enhancing and intensifying our co-operation in areas of our shared interest. I am confident that our joint efforts will contribute to the realisation of the aspirations of our respective peoples.
    
    Please accept, Excellency, my best wishes for the continued progress and prosperity of the people of Bangladesh”.
