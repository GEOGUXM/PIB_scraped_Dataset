      Ministry of Railways

      Bio-Toilets In Trains and at Railway Stations
      Posted On: 31 MAR 2017 3:49PM by PIB Delhi

      

        As on date, about 67,000 bio-toilets have been installed in passenger coaches and 45 bio-toilets have been set up at railway stations. Prior to the launch of first Green Corridor scheme between Rameswaram and Manamadurai in July 2016, about 43,000 bio-toilets were installed in coaches till 31.07.2016.


        Year-wise tentative details of bio-toilets installed in coaches and set up at railway stations are as under:
