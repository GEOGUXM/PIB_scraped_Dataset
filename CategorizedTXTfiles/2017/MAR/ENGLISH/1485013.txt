      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 50.39 per bbl on 20.03.2017
      Posted On: 21 MAR 2017 10:40AM by PIB Delhi

      

    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 50.39 per barrel (bbl) on 20.03.2017. This was lower than the price of US$ 50.44 per bbl on previous publishing day of 17.03.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs. 3294.95 per bbl on 20.03.2017 as compared to Rs. 3305.67 per bbl on 17.03.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 65.38 p er US$ on 20.03.2017 as compared to Rs. 65.54 per US$ on 17.03.2017 .
