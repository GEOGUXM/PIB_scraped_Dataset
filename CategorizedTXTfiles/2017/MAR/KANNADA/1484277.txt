      PM greets people on the occasion of Holi
      Posted On: 13 MAR 2017 9:13AM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM greets people on the occasion of Holi

      
      
      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Holi.
      
      “होली के पावन पर्व की सभी को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। Greetings on the festival of colours, Holi. May the festival spread joy and warmth everywhere,” the Prime Minister said.
