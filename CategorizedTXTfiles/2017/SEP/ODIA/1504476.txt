      PM wishes former PM, Dr. Manmohan Singh on his birthday
      Posted On: 26 SEP 2017 8:15PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has wished former Prime Minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh, on his birthday.
    
    “Warm birthday wishes to our former Prime Minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh. May he lead a long life filled with good health”, the Prime Minister said.
