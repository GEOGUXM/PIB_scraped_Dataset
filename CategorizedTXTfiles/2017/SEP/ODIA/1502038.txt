      Ministry of Coal

      Shri Haribhai Parthibhai Chaudhary, MoS Coal & Mines, assumes charge today (Sept 5, 2017) in Ministry of Coal
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2017 2:48PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    In the august presence of Minister of Railways and Coal, Shri Piyush Goyal, newly appointed Minister of State in the Ministry of Coal and Ministry of Mines, Shri Haribhai Parthibhai Chaudhary assumed the charge of his office in Ministry of Coal here today (Sept 5, 2017) at Shastri Bhawan.
    
    Prior to this, Shri Chaudhary was Minister of State in Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). Earlier, he also served as Minister of State in Ministry of Home Affairs.
    
    After taking charge, Shri Chaudhary held meeting with the senior officers of the Ministry of Coal.
