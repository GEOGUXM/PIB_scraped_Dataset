      PM salutes Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya, on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 25 SEP 2017 6:36PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya, on his birth anniversary.


    " पंडित दीनदयाल उपाध्याय जी की जयंती पर उन्हें शत् शत् नमन। Remembering our inspiration, Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya ji on his Jayanti.", the Prime Minister said.
