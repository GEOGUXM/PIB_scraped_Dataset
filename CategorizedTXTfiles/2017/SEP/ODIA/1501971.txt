      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar takes over as the Minister of State (IC) for Labour & Employment
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 9:06PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar took over as the Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Labour and Employment here today. Interacting with the media on the occasion, Shri Gangwar said that we will work as per the need of the nation. We will take along all and consult all stakeholders on policy issues.
    
    He is 7th time Lok Sabha member from Bareilly constituency since 1989 till date (except 2009-14). Shri Gangwar has vast experience in governance in various ministries like Finance, Petroleum and Natural Gas, Parliamentary Affairs, Science & Technology, Labour & Employment, Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises, Textiles and Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation. He has been Member of various Parliamentary Committees.
    
    Shri Gangwar was born on 1st November 1948 in Bareilly. He completed his B.Sc from Agra University and LLB Degree from Rohilkhand University.
