      PM greets the people on Hindi Diwas
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2017 4:42PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on Hindi Diwas.
    
    “हिन्दी दिवस के अवसर पर बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं।”, the Prime Minister said.
