      PM bows to Shaheed Bhagat Singh, on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 28 SEP 2017 7:17PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has bowed to Shaheed Bhagat Singh, on his birth anniversary.


    “I bow to the brave Shaheed Bhagat Singh on his Jayanti. His greatness and exemplary courage inspires generations of Indians”, the Prime Minister said.
