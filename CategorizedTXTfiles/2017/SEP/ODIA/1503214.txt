      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the demise of Lok Sabha MP Mahant Chand Nath
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2017 5:24PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Lok Sabha MP Mahant Chand Nath.
    
    The Prime Minister said,"Saddened by the demise of LS MP from Alwar, Mahant Chand Nath ji. He will be remembered for his rich social work. My deepest condolences."
