      DIVISIONAL dak adalat
      Posted On: 21 SEP 2017 5:01PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Divisional level ''DAK ADALAT'' of Ernakulam Postal Division will be held at the office of the Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Ernakulam Division, Ernakulam Head Post Office building, Kochi on 28 th September, 2017 at 11 am. Complaints relating to Postal services in Ernakulam postal division may be taken up in the Dak Adalat. Cases already taken up in previous Dak Adalats and settled will not be entertained. Complaints can be sent in envelopes super scribed “Dak Adalat” and addressed to “ Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Ernakulam Division, Kochi-682011,” so as to reach there on or before 26 th September , 2017.
