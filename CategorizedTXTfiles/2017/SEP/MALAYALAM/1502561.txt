      CHANGES IN PATTERN OF TRAIN SERVICES
      Posted On: 11 SEP 2017 5:37PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    

    Due to the ongoing track and signal modification work in connection with the Commissioning of doubling work between Srirangam and Ponmalai (GOC) stations there is a change in pattern of services for the following trains from 10.09.2017 to 30.09.2017.


    Trains Rescheduled


    Train No.16352 Nagercoil Jn – Mumbai CST Bi-weekly Express scheduled to leave Nagercoil at 05.00 hrs. is rescheduled to leave at 06.30 hrs. (90 minutes late) on dates 14.09.2017, 17.09.2017, 21.09.2017, 24.09.2017, 28.09.2017.


    Trains Diverted


    Train No.16128 Guruvayur - Chennai Egmore Express leaving Guruvayoor on 29.09.2017 and Train No 16127 Chennai Egmore – Guruvayur Express leaving Chennai Egmore on 30.09.2017 will be diverted via Tiruchirapalli, Karur Jn, Namkkal, Salem, Vriddhachalam.
