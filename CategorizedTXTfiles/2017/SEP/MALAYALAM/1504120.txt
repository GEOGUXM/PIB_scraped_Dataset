      Unregistered rent agreements will be accepted as proof of address for obtaining passport
      Posted On: 26 SEP 2017 7:14PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Ministry of External Affairs has decided to accept unregistered rent agreements as a proof of address for obtaining Passport. The decision was taken considering the difficulties faced by applicants to produce registered rent agreements. From now onwards, applicants can submit unregistered rent agreement as a proof of address while applying for Passport.
