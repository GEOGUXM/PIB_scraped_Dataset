      Passport mela for media fraternity
      Posted On: 11 SEP 2017 5:34PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Thiruvananthapuram Regional Passport Office will hold a Passport mela at Passport Seva Kendra, Vazhuthacaud on 23-9-2017 (Saturday) exclusively for Print and Electronic media personnel, employees and their family members residing under its jurisdiction to submit application for Fresh / Reissue of Passport. Applicants may apply online, remit fees and visit the passport Seva Kendra, Vazhuthacaud on 23-9-2017. The Application Reference Number (ARN) and requisite documents in original along with self-attested photocopies should be brought by the applicants for submission.
