      MINI ARMY RECRUITMENT RALLY FOR NURSING ASSISTANTS
      Posted On: 07 SEP 2017 7:27PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A mini Army Recruitment Rally for the posts of Soldier Nursing Assistant will be conducted at Kozhikode from 23 Oct to 04 Nov, 2017. The rally is open for all the male candidates of seven southern districts of Kerala (i.e,Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Kottayam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha, Idukki&Ernakulam). Candidates passed 10+2 with English, Physics, Chemistry and Biology and born between 01 Oct 1994 and 01 Apr 2000 are eligible to apply through www.joinindianarmy.nic.in. Online applications are now open and will remain open till 07 Oct 2017. The candidates should have minimum 165 cms of height, 50 kg weight and a chest of 77 cms.
