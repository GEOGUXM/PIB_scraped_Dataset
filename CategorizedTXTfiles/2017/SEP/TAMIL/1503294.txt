      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people on the occasion of Mahalaya
      Posted On: 19 SEP 2017 2:51PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Mahalaya.
    
    "Shubho Mahalaya! I pray that the festive season furthers the atmosphere of peace, prosperity and happiness in our society", the Prime Minister said.
