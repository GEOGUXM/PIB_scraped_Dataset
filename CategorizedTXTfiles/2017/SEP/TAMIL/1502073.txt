      55854 candidates from Southern Region to take up Stenographers Examination
      Candidates can download e-admission certificate from SSCSR website

      Posted On: 07 SEP 2017 5:08PM by PIB Chennai

    Staff Selection Commission will be conducting Stenographers (Grade ‘C’ & ‘D’) Examination, 2017 from 11.09.2017 to 14.09.2017. The exam will be Computer based and 55854 candidates from Southern Region will take up the examination.


    The examination will be held in 26 venues located at 6 Centres (Cities/towns) viz. Chennai, Madurai in Tamil Nadu and Union Territory of Puducherry ,Guntur, Vijayawada and Vishakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh; Hyderabad in Telangana. Examination will be held in two sessions. Forenoon session 10.00 AM to 12.00 Noon and Afternoon Session: 02.45PM to 04.45 PM.


    Southern Regional Office has already posted basic details like date, time and City, of the examination in its Website ( www.sscsr.gov.in ) so that candidates can know these details well in advance. Facility to download e-admission certificate is provided in SSCSR website in such a way that candidates can download it only 7 days before their actual date of examination. These details are also intimated to the candidates by SMS to the phone numbers and e-mail to the mail IDs provided by the candidates in their Online application.


    Candidates are requested to note that they shall not be permitted to take up the examination without e-Admission Certificate and Original valid Identity Proof enumerated in the Instructions. All the candidates are, therefore, requested to download their e-Admission Certificate 7 days before their actual date of examination without fail. For further queries/clarifications, candidates may contact Southern Regional office helpline numbers (Landline-044-28251139& Mobile: 9445195946).
