      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Arjun Ram Meghwal, Assumes Charge as Union Minister of State for Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 1:30PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal has assumed the charge as Union Minister of State for Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation in New Delhi today. Earlier on his arrival at Shram Shakti Bhawan he was received by Secretary and other senior officials of the Ministry. Shri Meghwal was MoS Finance and Corporate Affairs before the recent cabinet reshuffle.
