      Ministry of Coal

      Status of Coal Despatch by Coal India Limited (CIL) to Power Sector as on 18th September 2017
      Posted On: 20 SEP 2017 3:05PM by PIB Delhi

      * On 18 th September 2017, the total daily coal despatch of Coal India Limited was 1.31 MT
      * Out of the total daily coal despatch, despatch to power sector was about 1.05MT & rest 0.26 MT was despatched to non regulated sector.
      * On 18th September 2017, a total of 201 railway rakes of coal were despatched to different consumers out of which 185 railway rakes were despatched to power sector alone.
      * The despatch to power sector through rail is around 13.8% more as compared to 18 th September 2016.
      * The pit head stock at CIL coal mines was 32.876 MT as on 18th September2017 .
      * On 18.09.2017, the number of power plants in critical and super critical category was 6 & 12 respectively .
