      Ministry of Coal

      Status of Coal Despatch by Coal India Limited (CIL) to Power Sector as on 20th September 2017.
      Posted On: 22 SEP 2017 12:59PM by PIB Delhi


      On 20 th September 2017, the total daily coal despatch of Coal India Limited was 1.46 MT


      · Out of the total daily coal despatch, despatch to power sector was about 1.17MT & rest 0.29MT was despatched to non regulated sector.


      · On 20 th September 2017, a total of 216.5 railway rakes of coal were despatched to different consumers out of which 198.5 railway rakes were despatched to power sector alone.


      · The despatch to power sector through rail is around 15.3% more as compared to 20 th September 2016.


      · The pit head stock at CIL coal mines was 32.466 MT as on 20th September 2017.


      · On 20 th September 2017 , the number of power plants in critical and super critical category was 7 & 9 respectively .
