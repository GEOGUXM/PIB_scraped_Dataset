      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism today
      Posted On: 26 SEP 2017 5:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The Eleventh day’s work of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism was planned to undertake Cleanliness campaign at the Sacred Heart Cathedral near GPO and surrounding area by Officers and staff members of the Ministry of Tourism. Accordingly the designated officials and officers visited the Sacred Heart Cathedral near GPO, New Delhi. Shri P. Venkatesan, Director, PMU (SBM) met the Rev. Priest of the Sacred Heart Cathedral. The Rev. Priest extended a warm welcome and granted permission for under taking cleanliness in Sacred Heart Cathedral area. The Officers and staff members undertook an intensive Cleanliness work in the approach road, surrounding area of entrance gate, parking area, inside the Cathedral etc. The Rev. Priest also joined the team members in the cleaning work and picked-up the garbage. Thus the eleventh day’s work ended yielding the desired result.
