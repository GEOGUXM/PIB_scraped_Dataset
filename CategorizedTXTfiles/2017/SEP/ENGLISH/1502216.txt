      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Kharif Crop Sowing Crosses 1041 Lakh Hectare Area
      Posted On: 08 SEP 2017 5:23PM by PIB Delhi


    The total sown area as on 8 th September 2017, as per reports received from States, stands at 1041.17 lakh hectare as compared to 1049.87 lakh hectare at this time last year.


    It is reported that rice has been sown/transplanted in 371.46 lakh ha, pulses in 139.17 lakh ha, coarse cereals in 183.43 lakh ha, sugarcane in 49.88 lakh hectare and cotton in 120.98 lakh ha.
