      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Shri Suresh Prabhu takes the charge of Ministry of Commerce and Industry
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 2:36PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Suresh Prabhu took the charge of Ministry of Commerce and Industry in New Delhi today. The Minister of State (Commerce and Industry) Shri C R Choudhary , Commerce Secretary Mrs Rita Teaotia and DIPP Secretary Mr Ramesh Abhishek welcomed the Minister. After taking over the charge Shri Prabhu took a detailed presentation from both the departments and interacted with the officials.
