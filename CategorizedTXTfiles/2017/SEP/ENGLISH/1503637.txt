      Ministry of Coal

      Status of Coal Despatch by Coal India Limited (CIL) to Power Sector as on 19th September, 2017
      Posted On: 21 SEP 2017 4:58PM by PIB Delhi


    · On 19 th September 2017, the total daily coal despatch of Coal India Limited was 1.45 MT


    · Out of the total daily coal despatch, despatch to power sector was about 1.17 MT & rest 0.28MT was despatched to non regulated sector.


    · On 19 th September 2017, a total of 208 railway rakes of coal were despatched to different consumers out of which 196 railway rakes were despatched to power sector alone.


    · The despatch to power sector through rail is around 10.9% more as compared to 19 th September 2016.


    · The pit head stock at CIL coal mines was 32.66 MT as on 19th September 2017.


    · On 19 th September 2017, the number of power plants in critical and super critical category was 8 & 11 respectively .
