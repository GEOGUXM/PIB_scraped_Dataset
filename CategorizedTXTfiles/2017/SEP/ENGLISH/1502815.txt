      President of India presents Rajbhasha Awards on the occasion of Hindi Diwas
      Posted On: 14 SEP 2017 2:31PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind presented the Rajbhasha Awards on the occasion of Hindi Diwas at a function held in New Delhi today (September 14, 2017). He also launched the ‘LILA Mobile App’ in the presence of Shri Rajnath Singh, Union Minister of Home Affairs and Shri Hansraj Gagaram Ahir and Shri Kiran Rijiju, Ministers of State for Home Affairs.
