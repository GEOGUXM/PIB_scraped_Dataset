      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada by Ministry of Tourism
      Posted On: 19 SEP 2017 10:57AM by PIB Delhi


    The Fourth day’s work of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism was planned to undertake Cleanliness campaign at the Jama Masjid and surrounding area by officers and staff member of the Ministry of Tourism. Accordingly the designated officials and officers visited the Jama Masjid monument, Delhi. Shri P. Venkatesan, Director Ministry of Tourism, met the Shahi Imam of the Mosque. The Imam welcomed the team and permitted the cleaning work inside the Mosque. After this, Shri P. Venkatesan explained the historicity and importance of the monument. After this the officers and staff member undertook an intensive cleanliness work. The cleaning work done in the parking area, service road, surrounding area of ticket counter and drinking water area inside the Mosque complex. Thus the fourth day’s work ended yielding the desired result.
