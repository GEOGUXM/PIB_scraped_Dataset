      PM invites people to discover the beauty of Incredible India on World Tourism Day
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2017 11:34AM by PIB Delhi


    PM Shri Narendra Modi has invited people from all over the world to come and discover the beauty of India. He also urged youngsters of the country to travel across the country and explore its diversity.
    
    The Prime Ministr said, "On World Tourism Day, I invite people from all over the world to come & discover the beauty of Incredible India & hospitality of our people.
    
    I specially urge my young friends to travel across India & see first hand the diversity of our vibrant nation. "
