      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism today
      Posted On: 22 SEP 2017 6:14PM by PIB Delhi


    The Seventh day’s work of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism was planned to undertake Cleanliness campaign at the Sai Mandir and surrounding area by Officers and staff members of the Ministry of Tourism. Accordingly the designated officials and officers visited the Sai Mandir, Lodhi road, New Delhi.


    The Director, PMU (SBM) met the Secretary of the Temple. He welcomed the team from the Ministry in his office and deputed an official to guide the team in the area to be cleaned. The Officers and staff members undertook an intensive Cleanliness work. Heavy rain disrupted the cleaning work done in the parking area, service road, surrounding area of main entrance gate. Cleaning was done near the cafeteria area, flower, Prasad and pooja articles selling area. Thus the Seventh day’s work ended yielding the desired result.
