      President of India pays floral tributes to Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2017 7:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind paid floral tributes to Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, former President of India on occasion of his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (September 5, 2017).


    The President, officers and staff paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan in the Durbar Hall of Rashtrapati Bhavan.
