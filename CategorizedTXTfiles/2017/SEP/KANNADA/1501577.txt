      PM greets the people on Onam
      Posted On: 03 SEP 2017 12:03PM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM greets the people on Onam


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people, on the auspicious festival of Onam.
    
    “Onam greetings to everyone. May this auspicious festival enrich our society with happiness, harmony and wellbeing”, the Prime Minister said.
