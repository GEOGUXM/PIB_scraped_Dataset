      PM condoles the demise of LS MP and former Union Minister Shri Sultan Ahmed
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2017 3:34PM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM condoles the demise of LS MP and former Union Minister Shri Sultan Ahmed


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Lok Sabha MP and former Union Minister Shri Sultan Ahmed.
    
    “Saddened on the demise of LS MP and former Union Minister Shri Sultan Ahmed. My thoughts are with his family and supporters in this sad hour”, the Prime Minister said.
