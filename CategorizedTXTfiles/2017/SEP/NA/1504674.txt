      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada by M/O Tourism
      Posted On: 25 SEP 2017 5:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The Tenth day’s work of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism was planned to undertake Cleanliness campaign at the Gurudwara Bangla Sahib and surrounding area by Officers and staff members of the Ministry of Tourism. Accordingly the designated officials and officers visited the Gurudwara Bangla Sahib, New Delhi. Shri P. Venkatesan, Director, PMU (SBM) met the General Manager of the Gurudwara. The team was extended a warm welcome and permission was granted for under taking cleanliness in Gurudwara area. The Officers and staff members undertook an intensive Cleanliness work in the approach road, surrounding area of main entrance gate, service road, parking area etc. Thus the tenth day’s work ended yielding the desired result. The cleaning work was documented by the DD News and AIR News services Divisions.
