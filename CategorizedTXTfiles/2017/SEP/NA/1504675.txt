      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada by M/O Tourism
      Posted On: 24 SEP 2017 6:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ninth day’s work of Swachhta Pakhwada of Ministry of Tourism was planned to undertake cleaning of Water Coolers, toilets/washrooms/roof tops and Balcony of Buildings of the Ministry’s offices at Transport Bhawan and C-1 Hutments. Accordingly, an intensive cleanliness work was undertaken in all the Water Coolers, toilets/washrooms/roof tops and Balcony of Buildings etc. After the work, office Water Coolers, toilets, washrooms, roof tops and Balcony of Buildings premises looked net and clean. Thus the ninth day’s work ended yielding the desired result.
