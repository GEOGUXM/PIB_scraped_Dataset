      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates P.V. Sindhu on winning Korea Open Super Series title
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2017 2:09PM by PIB Guwahati


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Badminton Player P.V. Sindhu on winning Korea Open Super Series title
    
    The Prime Minister said, "Congratulations to P V Sindhu on emerging victorious in the Korea Open Super Series. India is immensely proud of her accomplishment."
