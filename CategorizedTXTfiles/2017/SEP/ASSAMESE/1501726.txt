      Dr. Satya Pal Singh takes charge as the Minister of State for Human Resource Development
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 4:54PM by PIB Guwahati


      Dr. Satya Pal Singh took over as the Minister of State for Human Resource Development in New Delhi today. He represents Baghpat (Uttar Pradesh) Parliamentary constituency in Lok Sabha.


      Shri Satya Pal Singh is the member of 16th Lok Sabha (2014-2019). He is also a Member, Joint Parliamentary Committee on Security of Parliament House Complex; Member, Standing Committee on Home Affairs; Member, Joint Committee on Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament and Member, Consultative Committee, Ministry of Rural Development, Panchayati Raj and Drinking Water and Sanitation.


      Shri Singh an IPS officer, 1980 batch has handled various responsible positions very efficiently. He was Police Commissioner Mumbai, Pune and Nagpur and Additional D.G.P., Law & Order and Establishment, Maharashtra State. He took VRS from government job and joined the mainstream politics in 2014.
