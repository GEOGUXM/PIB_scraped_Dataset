      Red Fort, Delhi to be Closed for Tourists and Visitors Tomorrow on Account of Visits by VVIPs to Dussehra Celebration Outside Red Fort
      Posted On: 29 SEP 2017 4:24PM by PIB Guwahati


    Red Fort (Lal Quila), Delhi will be closed for tourists and visitors tomorrow i.e. on Saturday September 30, 2017 on account of the visits by VVIPs to the Dussehra celebration outside the Red Fort. Light and sound show inside the Red Fort will also not be held.
