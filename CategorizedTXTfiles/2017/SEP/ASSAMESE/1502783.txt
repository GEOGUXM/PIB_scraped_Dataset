      Counselling date extends upto 18th September, 2017 for 15% seats on All India Quota in recognized Veterinary Colleges during 2017-18
      Posted On: 13 SEP 2017 6:30PM by PIB Guwahati


    The Veterinary Council of India through its Public Notice dated 11.09.2017 published schedule for counselling for allocation of seats to the recognized Veterinary Colleges for admission to B.V.Sc & A.H. course under 15% All India Quota during 2017-18. . In addition to this, the candidate has to satisfy the eligibility criteria as per VCI â€“ MSVE Regulations, 2016, details of which are available in the Information Bulletin on website www.vci.nic.in , www.aipvt.vci.nic.in and www.dahd.nic.in .


    According to previous schedule last date for registration by candidate was 13.09.2017 (upto 14.00 hrs) and date of reporting as immediate. However, in order to provide sufficient time period for students to register themselves for counselling and for reporting to veterinary colleges on allocation of seats, Central Government has permitted for extending the date of registration and also reporting by candidates to veterinary colleges after allocation of seats. Accordingly, candidates who have successfully qualified NEET (UG) 2017 and fulfilling the eligibility criteria as per Minimum Standards of Veterinary Education Regulations, 2016 and desirous to take admission to B.V.Sc & A.H. course under 15% All India Quota in recognized Veterinary Colleges can now register for counselling upto 18 th September, 2017 (11.00 hrs).


    The processing and allocation of seats will be done upto 19 th September, 2017 (16.00 hrs). The issuance of allocation letter to the candidate and the allotted Veterinary Colleges will be made available on 19 th September, 2017 (17.00 hrs) and reporting to the allotted Colleges by the candidates will be upto 25 th September, 2017 (16.00 hrs). The candidates are requested to visit the Website www.vci.nic.in , www.aipvt.vci.nic.in and www.dahd.nic.in
