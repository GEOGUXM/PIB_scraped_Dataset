      Prime Minister's Office

      PM expresses grief over the demise of Shri Jomde Kena
      Posted On: 05 SEP 2017 11:35AM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister Shri narendra Modi has expressed grief on the demise of Shri Jomde Kena, Health Minister of Arunachal Pradesh and extended condolences to his family and supporters.
