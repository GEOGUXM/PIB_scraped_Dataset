      Ministry of Minority Affairs

      Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi takes over as Union Minister of Minority Affairs
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 5:39PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi took over as Union Minister of Minority Affairs here today. Speaking on the occasion, Shri Naqvi said that “3E”- Education, Employment and Empowerment is our focus during last three year and Ministry of Minority Affairs will ensure socio-economic-educational progress of all the Minorities. The Minister emphasized that the Government is committed to empowerment of Minorities with dignity.


    Shri Naqvi said that all the schemes of Minority Affairs have been implemented effectively on ground level. He said that our commitment is “inclusive growth and atmosphere of trust”.
