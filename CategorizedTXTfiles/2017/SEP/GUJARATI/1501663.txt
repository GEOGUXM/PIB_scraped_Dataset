      Ministry of Drinking Water & Sanitation

      Uma Bharti Takes over as New Union Minister for Drinking Water and Sanitation
      Posted On: 04 SEP 2017 5:37PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Sushri Uma Bharti today took over as new Union Minister for Drinking Water and Sanitation. Earlier, on her arrival at the Ministry, the Minister was received by Shri Parameswaran Iyer, Secretary and other senior officials of the Ministry.
