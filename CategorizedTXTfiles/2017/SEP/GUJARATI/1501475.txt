      President of India’s greetings on the eve of Idu’l Zuha
      Posted On: 01 SEP 2017 5:01PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind has greeted fellow citizens on the eve of Idu’l Zuha.


    In his message the President has said, “On the occasion of Idu’l Zuha, I extend greetings to all my fellow citizens, especially to my Muslim brothers and sisters in India and abroad.


    On this day, let all of us rededicate ourselves to the spirit of trust, sacrifice and service, the essence of this festival, and share our happiness with people who are in need. May this unique festival enrich our composite culture, strengthen our unity and harmony and inspire all of us to work towards the well being of mankind”.
