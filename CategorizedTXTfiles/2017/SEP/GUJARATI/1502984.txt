      CMD HUDCO receives Rajbhasha Kirti Puruskar 2017
      Posted On: 15 SEP 2017 3:08PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Dr. M Ravi Kanth, CMD HUDCO, received ‘Rajbhasha Kirti Puraskar’ 2017’, from the Hon’ble President of India, on Hindi Diwas. HUDCO received the award under the CPSE category, for its contribution in promot ing the us e of Hindi as official language .
