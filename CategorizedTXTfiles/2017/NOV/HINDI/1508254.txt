      Ministry of Minority Affairs

      Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi participates in “Guruparb” in Hyderabad
      Posted On: 04 NOV 2017 7:38PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister for Minority Affairs Shri Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi today said here that despite different religions, culture and various form of worship, India is an example of "unity in diversity" and the culture of tolerance, harmony, peace and brotherhood is at the core of all the religions.


    Addressing on the occasion of “Guruparv” of Guru Nanak Devji Maharaj, organized by Gurdwara Sri Guru Singh Sabha in Hyderabad, Shri Naqvi said that Guru Nanak Devji Maharaj’s message of tolerance, harmony, peace and brotherhood has become even more relevant today.


    Shri Naqvi said that Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi-led Government is working firmly with commitment to "Ek Bharat, Shrestha Bharat”. We should not allow any conspiracy to succeed in disturbing the country’s unity.
