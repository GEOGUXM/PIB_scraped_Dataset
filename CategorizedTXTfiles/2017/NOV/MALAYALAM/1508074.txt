      IGNOU Admission cycle for January 2018
      Posted On: 02 NOV 2017 5:55PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has announced its January 2018 admission cycle.


    The University is offering Masters and Bachelors Degree programmes in various subjects including Rural Development, Computer Applications, Tourism Studies, English, Hindi, Philosophy, Gandhi and Peace Studies, Education, Public Administration, Economics, History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Adult Education, Extension and Development Studies, Gender and Development Studies, Distance Education, Anthropology, Commerce, Social Work, Dietetics and Food Services Management, Counselling and Family Therapy, Library and Information Science etc. IGNOU is also offering Post Graduate Diploma, Diploma, Post Graduate Certificate and Certificate programmes including Appreciation Courses on a wide range of subjects.


    Bachelor Preparatory Programme (BPP), a course of 6 months duration is offered by IGNOU in English, Hindi and in various Regional Languages including Malayalam. Educational aspirants who missed formal schooling of 10+2, can seek admission to the various Bachelor Degree Programmes offered by IGNOU on successful completion of BPP.


    The applications for admissions are to be submitted online through the following link: www.onlineadmission.ignou.ac.in


    For further details contact IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum, Rajadhani Building, Killippalam, Karamana. P.O, Thiruvananthapuram 695002, Ph: 0471-2344113/2344120 Email: rctrivandrum@ignou.ac.in
