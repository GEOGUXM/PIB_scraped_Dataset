      Meeting of IGNOU Co-coordinators
      Posted On: 23 NOV 2017 7:23PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The 8th Meeting of the Coordinators and Programme-in-charges of Learner Support Centres of Indira Gandhi National Open University, Regional Centre Trivandrum will be held tomorrow(24th November 2017).


    The meeting is being organized to discuss various matters regarding the recent changesimplemented by the University in various aspects of its functioning.


    Coordinators and Programme-in-charges of the 48 learner support centres spread across Trivandrum, Kollam and Pathanamthitta Districts of Kerala and Kanyakumari, Tirunelveli and Tuticorin Districts of Tamil Nadu will attend the programme.
