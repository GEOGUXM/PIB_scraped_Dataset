      ​​​​​​​SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 10 NOV 2017 9:58AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The following special trains will be run to clear the extra rush of traffic.


    1. Special fare special train from Kochuveli to Mangalore Jn.


    Train No.06055 Kochuveli – Mangalore Jn. special fare special train will leave Kochuveli at 20.00 hrs. on 11th November, 2017 and reach Mangalore Jn. at 11.40 hrs. the next day.


    Composition: AC 3-tier – 2, Sleeper Class – 10, General Second Class – 2 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.


    Stoppages: Chirayinkeezh, Varkalasivagiri, Kollam, Kayankulam, Kottayam, Ernakulam Town, Aluva, Thrisur, Shoranur, Tirur, Kozhikkode, Vadakara, Kannur, Payyanur and Kasargod.


    2. Special fare special train from Mangalore Jn to Chennai Central.


    Train No.06060 Mangalore Jn. – Chennai Central special fare special train will leave Mangalore Jn. at 20.15 hrs. on 12th November, 2017 and reach Chennai Central at 11.45 hrs. the next day.


    Composition: AC 3-tier – 2, Sleeper Class – 10, General Second Class – 2 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.


    Stoppages: Kasaragod, Payyanur, Kannur, Vadakara, Kozhikode, Tirur, Shoranur Jn., Palakkad, Coimbatore, Tiruppur, Erode, Salem, Jolarpettai, Katpadi, Arakkonam and Perambur.
