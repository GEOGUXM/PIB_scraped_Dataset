      BSNL landline disconnection date
      Posted On: 15 NOV 2017 5:43PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    BSNL Trivandrum Principal General Manager has informed that the landline telephone bills for the month of October 2017 (bill dtd. 05/10/2017 & 06/10/2017) should be paid on or before 17 th November 2017 to get continuous BSNL landline service. Customers can pay their bills at nearest BSNL customer service centers, post offices, JanasevaKendras, through the mobile application ‘My BSNL App’ and online portal of BSNL also.
