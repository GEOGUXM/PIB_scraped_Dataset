      Air Force Association: AGM & Family Get Together
      Posted On: 08 NOV 2017 5:44PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Annual General Body Meeting and family get together of Air Force Association Kerala Branch for the current year will be held at Sheron Cuture Centre, Thodupuzha, Idukki on 11th Nov 2017. Air Marshal RKS Bhadauria, Air Officer Commanding-in-Chief, HQ Southern Air Command, IAF will be the Chief Guest on the occasion.


    Awards will be presented to top ten children of AFA Kerala Branch members who have scored high marks in the last SSLC or equivalent examination and honouring of octogenarian members will also be held at the function.
