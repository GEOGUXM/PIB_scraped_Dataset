      IGNOU Term End Exams from December 1 to 23
      Posted On: 29 NOV 2017 8:11PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Term End Examinations for December, 2017 of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) will be held from 1st December 2017 to 23rd December 2017 across the country.


    A total of 5,500 students will appear for the examinations at nine examination centres in Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Pathanamthitta, Kanyakumari, Tirunelveli and Tuticorin districts under the IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum. The hall ticket is available on IGNOU website www.ignou.ac.in. Students must possess valid identity card issued by the University during the examination.


    For more details contact IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum Rajadhani Complex, Killippalam, Karamana P.O., Thiruvanathapuram- 695002
