      Small savings account holders should link their Aadhar / Mobile number
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2017 5:32PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Postal department has informed that all post office small savings account holders should link their Aadhar/Mobile number to respective accounts before 31st December 2017. Customers can link their Aadhar/Mobile numbers across the Post office savings bank counters.
