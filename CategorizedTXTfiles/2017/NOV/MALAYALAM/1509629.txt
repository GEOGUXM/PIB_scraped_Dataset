      ‘HACKATHON’ for School students
      Posted On: 15 NOV 2017 5:41PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Liquid Propulsion System Centre (LPSC) at Valiyamala in Thiruvananthapuram, one of the lead centers of ISRO, on its pearl jubilee is organising a ‘HACKATHON’ for school students across Kerala. Four day residential programme consisting of three day training and one day problem solving session based on Raspberry Pi and Arduino will be held as part of this. The idea is to give them a feel of control applications related to robotics and automation. The programme will be held at Thiruvananthapuram from 26 th to 29 th December 2017. This is a fully residential programme for 8 th and 9 th standard students. It will be led by eminent Academicians & scientists of LPSC.


    Interested schools can apply on or before 20 th November 2017.


    For more details, visit www.lpsc.gov.in
