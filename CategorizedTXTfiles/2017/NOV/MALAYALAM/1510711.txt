      Temporary disconnection date of BSNL Postpaid Mobile & WIMAX Services
      Posted On: 23 NOV 2017 7:25PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    General Manager for Mobile Services BSNL Kerala Circle has informed that all GSM & WIMAX Postpaid customers have to pay their Mobile Bill dated 03-11-2017, before 27 th November 2017 to enjoy continued service.
