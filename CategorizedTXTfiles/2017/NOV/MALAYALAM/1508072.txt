      Vigilance Awareness week closing ceremony
      Posted On: 02 NOV 2017 5:51PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The closing ceremony of “Vigilance Awareness Week-2017” being conducted by the Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, Thiruvananthapuram will be held tomorrow (3 rd November 2017, Friday) . State Chief Information Commissioner Shri. Vinson M. Paul will be the Chief Guest of the programme which will be held at 3.30 pm at Income Tax Recreation Club Hall, 6 th floor, AyakarBhawan ,Kowdiyar.
