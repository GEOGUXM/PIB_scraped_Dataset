      Admission to Sainik School Kazhakootam Last Date December 5
      Posted On: 21 NOV 2017 5:26PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Sainik School, Kazhakootam has invited application for admission to Class VI & IX (boys only) for the academic year 2018-19. Total number of vacancies for Class VI is – 60 nos. and for Class IX – 10 nos. Age limits for Class VI : Born between 02 Jul 2007 and 01 Jul 2008, for Class IX : Born between 02 Jul 2004 and 01 Jul 2005 and should be studying in Class VIII in a recognised school. The last date of issue of application form and prospectus and filling up of online application is 30th November 2017. The last date for receipt of filled in application at school is 05th December 2017.


    The date of Entrance Exam is on 07 Jan 2018 (Sunday) and the centres are Kozhikode, Ernakulam, Kottayam, Palakkad, Sainik School Kazhakootam. 15% of seats are reserved for SC, 7.5% for ST, Out of remaining seats 67% are for Kerala Domicile- General and Defence (75 : 25). Different types of scholarships are given by Central/State Governments depending upon the parents’ monthly income.


    The Prospectus, Application form and previous year question papers can be obtained from the Principal, Sainik School, Kazhakootam, Sainik School PO, Trivandrum – 695585 on the basis of request letter along with Demand Draft payable at Trivandrum drawn in favour of “Principal, Sainik School Kazhakootam”. Registration fee (including cost of Prospectus, Application form and previous year question papers) is Rs.450/- (Rs.300/- for SC/ST candidates). If the candidates purchased directly from the school, the registration fee is Rs.400/- (Rs.250 for SC/ST candidates). Address with telephone No., category and the class applied for and Date of Birth should be clearly mentioned in the request letter. On receipt of request letter and demand draft, the prospectus, application form and model question papers will be sent by Registered Post. The application form may also be downloaded from the school website `www.sainikschooltvm.nic.in’. The candidates should apply through online also. The online application form can be filled up on the link which would be available in the school website. Application forms will be available from 17 Oct to 30 Nov 2017.


    For further details visit www.sainikschooltvm.nic.in. The admissions will be made strictly in accordance with the merit in the Entrance Examination, Interview and Medical fitness of candidates. The school has not deputed any individual/organisation/institution for admission related coaching/training.
