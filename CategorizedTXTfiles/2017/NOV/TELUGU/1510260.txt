      THIRD REGIONAL POSTAL PENSION ADALAT ON JANUARY 8
      Posted On: 20 NOV 2017 6:31PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The third Regional Postal Pension Adalat of Telangana Circle for the half- year ending December 31, 2017 will be held on January 8, 2018 (Monday) at 11.00 A.M. at Committee Hall, 2 nd floor, Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Telangana Circle, Dak Sadan, Abids, Hyderabad-500001.


    The grievances of Postal Pensioners relating to Pension Gratuity and allied matters may be sent to Shri N. Padmanabharao, Accounts Officer (IFA), Office of the Postmaster General, Hyderabad Region, 5 th Floor, Dak Sadan, Abids, Hyderabad - 500001 on or before December 15, 2017. Grievances received thereafter will not be entertained, according to a press note issued by Postmaster General, Hyderabad Region, Hyderabad-1.
