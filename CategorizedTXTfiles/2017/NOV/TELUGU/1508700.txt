      The 20th International Children s film festival started on a colourful note
      Posted On: 08 NOV 2017 8:44PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The 20th International Children s film festival started on a colourful note at Hyderabad today. Shri T Srinivas Yadav, Minister of Cinematography,Govt of Telangana inaugurated the festival. He said Hyderabad, the most happening city is an apt place to organise the festival. He said more films for children with good content must be made. Speaking on the occasion Shri Mukesh Khanna, Chairman of CFSI requested the government of Telangana to announce Hyderabad as the Southern Centre of CFSI. Shri Shravan Kumar, CEO of ICFFI said that the festival is growing in stature with every edition as can be seen from the increasing number of entries. A total of 1402 entries have been received of which 300 will be screened in this weeklong festival Nov 8 to 14th. Colourful cultural programmes by children were staged at the inauguration.
