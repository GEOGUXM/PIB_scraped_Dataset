      Various Programmes conducted at 20th ICFFI on 6th day of the fest
      Posted On: 13 NOV 2017 6:13PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Various programmes were conducted in the city in connection with 20th International Children’s Film Festival here on Monday.
    
    Little Director’s Workshop was held at the main venue of the ICFFI prasads IMAX. Children in the age group of 10-15 years participated in the workshops.
    
    children from different schools of Hyderabad and various parts of Telangana visited the IMAX Venue today.


    
    An Open forum on ‘Humor & Fantasy in children’s films’ was also conducted on this Venue.


    Half Ticket, a Marathi film about desires and the yearning for the unattainable, It is an Indian Marathi language film directed by Samit Kakkad and produced by Video Palace. The film is the story of two slum boy brothers and their absolute focus to get what they cannot dare dream of.


    
    Shri Mukhesh Khanna, Chairperson, CFSI visited the main venue of the festival and interacted with the children. film makers from different parts and the delegates expressed their happiness over the arrangements being made for the festival.
