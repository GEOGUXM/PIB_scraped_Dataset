      PM’s message on World Toilet Day
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2017 10:27AM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Following is the text of Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi ’s message on World Toilet Day.


    “On World Toilet Day we reaffirm our commitment towards improving sanitation facilities across our nation.


    I compliment all those individuals and organisations working towards building more toilets in various parts of India. Their invaluable contribution adds solid momentum to Swachh Bharat Mission.”
