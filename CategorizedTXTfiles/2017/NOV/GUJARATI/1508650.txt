      HUDCO OBSERVED VIGILANCE AWARENESS WEEK
      Posted On: 08 NOV 2017 5:23PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Housing & Urban Development Corporation Ltd. (HUDCO) has observed Vigilance Awareness Week 2017 from 30 th October to 4 th November 2017 at their Regional Office, Ahmedabad. During the week, HUDCO has organized an Elocution Competition on the topic “My Vision – Corruption Free India” in Navkar Public School, Ambawadi, Ahmedabad and also conducted Debate, Quiz, Essay writing and Slogan writing Competitions to the staff of HUDCO Regional office.


    Sh. S Gurudatta, Regional Chief, HUDCO administered the Integrity Pledge and highlighted the role of citizens and students in creation and need of awareness to fight against the corruption as well as refraining from indulging in unethical acts.
