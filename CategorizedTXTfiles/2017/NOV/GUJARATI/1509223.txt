      PM condoles loss of lives in the earth quake in Iran and Iraq
      Posted On: 13 NOV 2017 1:54PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives in the earth quake that has affected parts of Iran and Iraq.


    “My thoughts are with all those who have lost their loved ones in the tragic earthquake that has affected parts of Iran and Iraq. I pray that those injured recover at the earliest”, the Prime Minister said.
