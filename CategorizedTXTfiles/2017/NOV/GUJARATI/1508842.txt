      HUDCO organises International Training Programme under MEA, ITEC and SCAAP.
      Posted On: 09 NOV 2017 5:56PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Dr M. Ravi Kanth, CMD, HUDCO welcomed twenty two international delegates from twelve countries to HUDCO’s HSMI for a six week training programme under the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Indian Technical, Economic Cooperation (ITEC) and Special Commonwealth Assistance for Africa Programme (SCAAP) scheduled from 30.10.2017 to 08.12.2017. The theme of the training programme is ‘Formal Solutions to Informal Settlements’. The participants were from Chile, Cambodia, Ethiopia, Guatemala, Nigeria, Tajikistan, Tanzania, Lesotho, Malawi, Uganda, Zimbabwe and Afghanistan.
