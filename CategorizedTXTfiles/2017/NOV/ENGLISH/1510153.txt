      PM pays tributes to former PM Indira Gandhi on her birth anniversary
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2017 11:24AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister Mrs. Indira Gandhi on her birth anniversary.


    “Tributes to former PM Mrs. Indira Gandhi on her birth anniversary.”, the Prime Minister said.
