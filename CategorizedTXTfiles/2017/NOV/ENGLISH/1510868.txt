      President of India to inaugurate International Gita Mahotsava-2017 tomorrow
      Posted On: 24 NOV 2017 8:34PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will inaugurate the International Gita Mahotsava-2017 tomorrow (November 25, 2017) in Kurukshetra, Haryana.


    Earlier in the day, the President will visit Brahma Sarovar. He will also lay the foundation stone of the Gita Research Centre at Gita Gyan Sansthanam.
