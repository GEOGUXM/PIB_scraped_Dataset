      Election Commission

      Fact Sheet on Himachal Pradesh Assembly Elections 2017
      
      
      In Himachal Pradesh, as per information provided by the Chief Electoral Officer, there are NO Recognized State Parties.

      Posted On: 01 NOV 2017 12:12PM by PIB Delhi

        Smallest & Largest Constituency by Area & Electorate :


        Among the Assembly Constituencies ( ACs ) , by area, the smallest is Shimla and the largest one is Lahual & Spiti . By size of the Electorate, the smallest is Lahual & Spiti AC with 22,995 electors and the largest being Sullah AC with 95,064 electors .         The electors who have received the Electoral Photo ID Card ( EPIC ) are further categorized by sex which, as the charts show, are fairly evenly constituted by Males and Females . Further, there are 14 electors reported from the ‘ Third Gender ’ in the electorate and there are a total of 5 NRI voters and 37,574 voters from the Services .
