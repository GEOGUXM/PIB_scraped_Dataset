      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 59.61 per bbl on 03.11.2017
      Posted On: 06 NOV 2017 12:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 59.61 per barrel (bbl) on 03.11.2017. This was lower than the price of US$ 59.07 per bbl on previous publishing day of 02.11.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3849.43 per bbl on 03.11.2017 as compared to Rs. 3815.80 per bbl on 02.11.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 64.58 per US$ on 03.11.2017 as compared to 64.59 per US$ on 02.11.2017.
