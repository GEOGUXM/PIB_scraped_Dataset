      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Enhancement of Merchandise Exports from India Scheme (MEIS) for two subsectors of Textiles Industry
      Posted On: 24 NOV 2017 8:44PM by PIB Delhi


    The Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) has issued Public Notice no. 42 today by which rates for incentives under the Merchandise Exports from India Scheme (MEIS) for two subsectors of Textiles Industry, that is readymade garments and made ups have been enhanced from two percent to four percent of value of exports with effect from 01.11.2017 till 30.06.2018.
    
    The estimated annual incentives will be Rs. 1,143.15 crore for 2017-18 and Rs. 685.89 crore for 2018-19. This measure will incentivise the exports of labour intensive sectors of readymade garments and made ups and contribute to employment generation.
