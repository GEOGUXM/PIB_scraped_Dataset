      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      UP Singh to be the New Union Water Resources Secretary
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2017 4:23PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Upendra Prasad Singh, IAS (OR:85) presently holding the charge of Director General, National Mission for Clean Ganga has been appointed as Secretary, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation. He will succeed Dr. Amarjit Singh, IAS (GJ:82) who will superannuate on November 30, 2017.
