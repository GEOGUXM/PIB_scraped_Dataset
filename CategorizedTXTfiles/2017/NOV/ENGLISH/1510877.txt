      Ministry of Finance

      GOI floats Series-III of Sovereign Gold Bonds 2017-18
      Issue price shall be Rs2,961 per gram with Settlement on December 4, 2017.

      Posted On: 24 NOV 2017 8:51PM by PIB Delhi

    Government of India, in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), had floated Series III of Sovereign Gold Bonds 2017-18, for the period from October 09, 2017 to December 27, 2017 (with subscription period Monday to Wednesday every week). The Bonds will be issued on the succeeding Monday after each subscription period.

    The Government of India in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI),has decided to allow discount of Rs50 (Rupees Fifty) per gram from the issue price to those investors who apply online and the payment is made through digital mode.
