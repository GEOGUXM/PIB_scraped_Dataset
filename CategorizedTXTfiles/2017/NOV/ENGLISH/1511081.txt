      NMCG pavilion gets Appreciation Certificate
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2017 11:42AM by PIB Delhi


      The pavilion of Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation bagged gold medal in the in the Ministries/ Departments category for excellence in display in the just concluded India International Trade Fair- 2017 New Delhi. Large number of visitors including students and youths visited the pavilion and showed keen interest in the exhibits. Several quiz competitions were organised for the visitors to make them aware about various water resources related issues including water conservation, drip irrigation, inter linking of rivers etc.


      Shri Akhil Kumar, Joint Secretary, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation received the Gold Medal award from Union Minister of State for Commerce and Industry Shri CR Chaudhary at a ceremony in New Delhi yesterday.


      The pavilion of National Mission for Clean Ganga received appreciation certificate in the PSUs/ EPCs/ commodity boards, banks and other govt. departments/organisations category for excellence in display in India International Trade Fair- 2017 New Delhi. Shri DP Mathuria, Executive Director (Technical) in National Mission for Clean Ganga received the appreciation certificate from Union Minister of State for Commerce and Industry Shri CR Chaudhary at a ceremony in New Delhi yesterday.
