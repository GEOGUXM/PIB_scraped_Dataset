      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 60.36 per bbl on 15.11.2017
      Posted On: 16 NOV 2017 1:26PM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 60.36 per barrel (bbl) on 15.11.2017. This was lower than the price of US$ 61.17 per bbl on previous publishing day of 14.11.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs 3945.36 per bbl on 15.11.2017 as compared to Rs. 4007.56 per bbl on 14.11.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 65.37 per US$ on 15.11.2017 as compared to 65.52 per US$ on 14.11.2017.
