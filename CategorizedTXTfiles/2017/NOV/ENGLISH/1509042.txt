      PM pays tributes to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad and Acharya JB Kripalani on their birth anniversaries
      Posted On: 11 NOV 2017 10:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid tributes to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad and Acharya JB Kripalani on their birth anniversaries.


    “Tributes to two stalwarts of Indian history, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad and Acharya JB Kripalani on their birth anniversaries. Their contribution towards India’s freedom movement and after was extremely beneficial in the building of our nation.”, the Prime Minister said.
