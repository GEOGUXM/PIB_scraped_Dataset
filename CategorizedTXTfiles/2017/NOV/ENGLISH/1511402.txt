      PM congratulates Mirabai Chanu on winning gold at World Weightlifting Championship
      Posted On: 30 NOV 2017 3:43PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Ms. Mirabai Chanu on winning gold at the World Weightlifting Championship.


    “India is proud of Mirabai Chanu, who has won a gold at the World Weightlifting Championship. Congratulations and best wishes for her future endeavours.”, the Prime Minister said.
