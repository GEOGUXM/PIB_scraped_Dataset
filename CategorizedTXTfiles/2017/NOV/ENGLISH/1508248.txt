      President of India to Visit Chhattisgarh on November 5 and 6, 2017
      Posted On: 04 NOV 2017 7:15PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Chhattisgarh on November 5 and 6, 2017. This will be his first visit to the State after assuming office as President.


    On November 5, 2017, the President will commence his engagements by paying tributes at Shahidi Smarak Sthal in Raipur. Later in the day, he will grace and address the Chhattisgarh Rajyotsava in Naya Raipur.


    On November 6, 2017, the President will visit Guru Ghasidas Jaitkham and attend the Bhoomi Pujan for a Saamudayik Bhavan at Girodhpuri before returning to New Delhi.
