      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Pankaj Advani on winning IBSF World Snooker Championship
      Posted On: 30 NOV 2017 3:35PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Shri Pankaj Advani on winning his 18th world title in the recently concluded IBSF World Snooker Championship.


    “Congratulations Pankaj Advani for winning your 18th world title in the recently concluded IBSF World Snooker Championship. Your accomplishments make us very proud.”, the Prime Minister said.
