      PM greets people of Uttarakhand on Uttarakhand Foundation Day
      Posted On: 09 NOV 2017 10:10AM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM greets people of Uttarakhand on Uttarakhand Foundation Day


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people of Uttarakhand on Uttarakhand Foundation Day.
    
    “विकास के पथ पर अग्रसर उत्तराखंड के निवासियों को राज्य के स्थापना दिवस की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। On Uttarakhand Foundation Day, my greetings to the people of the state”, the Prime Minister said.
