      PIB Headquarters

      Dak Adalat on 13.12.2017
      Posted On: 21 NOV 2017 3:00PM by PIB Chennai


    The Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, Chennai City North Division will conduct by “Dak Adalat” at its office premises at No. 5 (Old No.3) IV Floor, Ethiraj Salai, Egmore, Chennai – 600 008 at 11:00 hours on 13.12.2017 (Wednesday).


    The members of the public are requested to send their unsettled grievances / pending complaints relating to postal services in respect of post offices under the administrative jurisdiction of Chennai City North Division on or before 06.12.2017.
