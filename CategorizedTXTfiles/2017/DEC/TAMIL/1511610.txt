      Prime Minister's Office

      PM extends greetings on Id-E-Milad
      Posted On: 02 DEC 2017 9:55PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has extended his greetings on the occasion of Id-E-Milad .


    “Greetings on Id-E-Milad. May the teachings of Prophet Mohammad further the spirit of harmony in our society”, the Prime Minister said.
