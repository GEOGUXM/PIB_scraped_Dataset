      Prime Minister's Office

      PM wishes Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his birthday
      Posted On: 25 DEC 2017 11:28AM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has wished Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his birthday.


    “Birthday greetings to our beloved Atal Ji. His phenomenal as well as visionary leadership made India more developed and further raised our prestige at the world stage. I pray for his good health”, the Prime Minister said.
