      Vice President Greets People on the eve of Christmas
      Posted On: 24 DEC 2017 5:39PM by PIB Chennai


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted the people of our country on the joyous occasion of Christmas. In a message, he said that Christmas reaffirms our faith in the values of compassion and forgiveness taught by Lord Jesus Christ for the enlightenment of humanity.
    
    Following is the text of Vice President’s message:
    
    “I extend my warm greetings and best wishes to all our citizens on the auspicious occasion of Christmas.
    
    Christmas is a celebration of the birth of Lord Jesus Christ. The festival reaffirms our faith in the values of compassion and forgiveness taught by Lord Jesus Christ for the enlightenment of humanity.
    
    May this festival bring peace, harmony and happiness in our lives.”
