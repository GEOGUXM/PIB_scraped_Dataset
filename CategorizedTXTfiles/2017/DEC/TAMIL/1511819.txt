      President of India to grace the 83rd convocation of Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra,Tomorrow
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2017 2:06PM by PIB Chennai


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Agra (Uttar Pradesh) tomorrow (December 5, 2017) to grace the 83rd Convocation of Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra.
