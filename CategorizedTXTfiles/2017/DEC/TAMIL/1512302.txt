      e-Support Centre inaugurated in IGNOU Madurai
      Posted On: 08 DEC 2017 4:22PM by PIB Chennai


    Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU), Regional Centre, Madurai has established e-Support Centre, as part of the Digital India Movement. The e-support Centre was inaugurated for the benefit of the distance learners of IGNOU to ease support services.


    As part of the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, the IGNOU RC Madurai and DHAN Foundation, Madurai has also organized one day workshop on the theme “Women Empowerment and their Rights for Social Development” at Madurai for the benefit of NGO functionaries.


    Speaking on the occasion, Shri. K. Veera Raghava Rao, IAS, District Collector, Madurai highlighted the importance of Open & Distance Learning programmes for empowerment of the society and also highlighted the need of lifelong learning, livelihood education and life skill education at his speech and requested the women participants to work at the gross root level for the upliftment of women education with cultural knowledge for social transformation.


    Dr. M. Shanmugam, senior Regional Director, IGNOU RC Madurai, Mr. M.P. Vasimalai, Executive Director, DHAN Foundation, Madurai and other dignitaries were also present during the occasion.
