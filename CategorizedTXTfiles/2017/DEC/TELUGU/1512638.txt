      DAVP EXHIBITION IN SHADNAGAR
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2017 6:31PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The Revenue Divisional officer (RDO) of Shadnagar, Shri M. Krishna inaugurated a five – day Directorate of Advertising and Visual Publicity (DAVP) exhibition at the Mandal Panchayat Development office today. The theme of the exhibition is “Sath Hai Vishwas Hai, Ho Raha Vikas Hai”. Speaking on the occasion, Shri Krishna urged people to utilise the government schemes. He said that exhibitions such as this helped in increasing the awareness of the people about the government’s activities.


    The exhibition showcases the programmes and achievements of the government – GST, surgical strikes, schemes for the farmers, economically backward, women and children etc. MPP Smt Bujji Babu Naik, Shadnagar, MPDO Smt Rajeshwari, ERDO Smt Sumitra and MEO Mr Shankar Rathod were also present. Field Exhibition Officer Shri Suresh Dharmapuri welcomed the gathering.
