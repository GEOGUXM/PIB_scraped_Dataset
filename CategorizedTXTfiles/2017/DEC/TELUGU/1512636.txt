      Dr. R. HEMALATHA APPOINTED DIRECTOR OF NIN
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2017 6:29PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Dr. R. HEMALATHA, Sr. Grade Deputy Director, has been appointed as Director of National Institute of Nutrition (NIN) under the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR).


    According to a press release issued by Dr. M. Maheshwar, Media coordinator, Dr. Hemalatha is recipient of several ICMR awards, including Fellow of National Academy of Medical Sciences in 2016 and Fellow of Union of Nutrition Sciences (FIUNS) in 2017.


    Dr. Hemalath has vast experience in the field of Maternal and Child Health, clinical and population based studies.
