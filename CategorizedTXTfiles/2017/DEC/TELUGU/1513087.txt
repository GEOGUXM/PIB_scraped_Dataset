      NTPC-RAMAGUNDAMORGANISED LIVESTOCK CAMP AT LINGAPURAM
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 6:36PM by PIB Hyderabad


    NTPC-Ramagundam under its CSR activities in association with State Animal Husbandry Deptt has organized a Livestock Camp at Lingapuram on December 18, 2017. SmtA Malleswari, Sarpanch, Lingapuram;MPTC Smt. E Padma and ZPTCSmt K Sandhyaraniinaugurated the camp. This is the first animal checkup camp of the planned 15 camps in nearby villages for FY 2017-18. The remaining 14 camps to be organised in nearby villages in next few days.


    Doctors from State Government Veterinary Department, Dr. Sravan Kumar and DrSreepriya and their staff coordinated the camp wherein 1320 animals of 49 farmers were treated and provided medicines free of cost.


    Lingapuram villagers, NTPC CSR team and others were present at the camp.
