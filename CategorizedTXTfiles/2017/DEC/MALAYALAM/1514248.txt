      Due dates for BSNL landline Bill Payment
      Posted On: 26 DEC 2017 5:56PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The due dates without fine for monthly and bimonthly bill for Landline customers of Thiruvananthapuram BSNL for the month of December 2017 is 27 th and 28 th December respectively. Customers can pay their bills at nearest BSNL customer service centers, Post offices and Janaseva Kendras, Customers can also pay their bills through the BSNL online portal or through the mobile application ‘My BSNL App’. Customers are requested to pay their bills on or before the due date.
