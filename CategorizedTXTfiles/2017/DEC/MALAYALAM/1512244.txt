      Last date for Philately Scholarship-December 10
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2017 7:40PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    In connection with the promotion of aptitude and research in Philately among students Govt of India has launched the ‘DEEN DAYAL SPARSH YOJANA’,- a philately scholarship scheme. As per the scheme students of class VI to IX having good academic record and pursuing philately as a hobby will be awarded with a scholarship of Rs.6000/- per annum @ Rs.500 per month, on the basis of two level competitions.


    Level 1-Regional level Philately Quiz


    Level 2-Philately project submission by the level 1 winners


    The eligible candidate may please forward their nomination duly attested by the head of the institution to the following address:


    Senior Superintendent of Post offices


    Trivandrum North Division


    Tvm-695001


    The last date for receipt of nomination is 10.12.17
