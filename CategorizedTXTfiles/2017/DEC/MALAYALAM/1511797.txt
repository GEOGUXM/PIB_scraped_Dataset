      Kazhak Expo: Science Exhibition At Sainik School Kazhakootam
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2017 10:59AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Sainik School Kazhakootam organized its Annual Science Exhibition on 03 Dec 2017. Various departments such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths, Social Studies, Computer, Art, Craft, NCC and clubs such as Nature Club, Bird Watching Club, Horticulture Club, etc had installed their independent stalls with a variety of innovative exhibits that captured the imagination of the inquisitive visitors. Non-conventional ways of energy production, energy conservation, waste disposal, nature care, organic farming, mathemagic, etc. were some of the highlights that left the visitors awestruck. The expo was inaugurated by Smt. Asha Rajiv, First lady of the campus.


    The visitors included not only parents but also the alumni, children from nearby schools and the local public.
