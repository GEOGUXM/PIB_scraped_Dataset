      Augmentation Of Trains
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2017 7:28PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    For the benefit of unreserved passengers, Trivandrum- Hazrat. Nizamuddin- Trivandrum weekly express will be permanently augmented with two additional second class (general) coaches.


    1. Train No 22655 Trivandrum - Hazrat.Nizamuddin weekly express train will be augmented with two additional second class coaches from 06.12.2017.


    2. Train No.22656 Hazrat.Nizamuddin - Trivandrum weekly express train will be augmented with two additional second class coaches from 08.12.2017.
