      Special Book Exhibition of Publications Division
      Posted On: 22 DEC 2017 5:14PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Special Book Exhibition organised by Publications Division under, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India, at the Sales Emporium, Press Road, Thiruvananthapuram will be opened till 05th January, 2018.


    A discount of 10% To 45% on all books (depending on the purchase value) and a special discount of 50% on selected books will be given during the exhibition.


    Several new titles, including Rashtrapati Bhawan Series, Gandhian Literature, Builders of Modern India, Art & Paintings, Journalism and Indian History etc. are available in English and Hindi for sale during this period.
