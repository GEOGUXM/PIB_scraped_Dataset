      Temporary disconnection date of BSNL Postpaid Mobile Services
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2017 3:16PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    General Manager for Mobile Services BSNL Kerala Circle has informed that all GSM Postpaid customers have to pay their Mobile Bill dated 03-12-2017, before 26 th December 2017 to enjoy continued service.
