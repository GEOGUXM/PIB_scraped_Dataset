      All India Sainik Schools Entrance Exam On 07th Jan Admit Cards Available At School Website
      Posted On: 22 DEC 2017 5:16PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Admit Cards for All India Sainik School Entrance Exam 2018 scheduled to be held on 07th January 2018 (Sunday) are being disbursed through the website www.sainikschooladmission.in. Candidates are requested to use their User ID and Password for downloading Admission tickets. Candidates registered through offline are to use the User ID and Password received in their mob number/E-mail address given in the application form. For any further clarification please contact school on Phone No. 0471-2167590 or 2418247 during working hours between 0900 hrs to 1400 hrs.
