      GST: Division of taxpayers finalized
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2017 3:14PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The allocation of Goods and Services Tax taxpayers in Kerala between the Central GST and State GST Departments has been finalized in accordance with the guidelines prescribed by the GST Council. As per the above allocation, the taxpayers will be administered by the concerned Central GST or State GST Department to which they are allocated.


    The lists of the Tax payers allocated to central GST Department and State GST Department are published in the websites www.cenexcisekochi.gov.in and www.keralataxes.gov.in as Annexure A and Annexure B respectively.


    The allocation of the remaining few Taxpayers will be finalized shortly. Chief Commissioner, Central Tax, Central Excise and Customs, Thiruvananthapuram Zone, Shri. Pullela Nageswara Rao said that the allocation of the remaining few Taxpayers will be finalized shortly.
