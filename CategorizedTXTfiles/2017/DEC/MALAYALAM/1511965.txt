      RNI Registration: Applications with inadequate documents will be rejected
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2017 5:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Deputy Press Registrar of Registrar of Newspapers for India (RNI) informed that the applications for both fresh and revised registration with inadequate documents will no longer be accepted by RNI. Such applications received directly at RNI headquarters from 11 th December 2017 and received through posts from 15 th December 2017 will be either rejected at the Dak counter or send back to the applicant through posts. Publishers sending application by post are requested to enclose a self-addressed/ stamped envelope along with their application.
