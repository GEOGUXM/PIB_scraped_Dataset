      Philatelic quiz postponed to January 8
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2017 6:46PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The date of Philatelic Written Quiz in connection with the Scholarship scheme of Department of Posts “Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana” scheduled for 19 th December 2017 is postponed to 8 th January 2018. Kerala circle Chief Postmaster General has informed that the last date of receipt of application is also extended to 29 th December 2017.
