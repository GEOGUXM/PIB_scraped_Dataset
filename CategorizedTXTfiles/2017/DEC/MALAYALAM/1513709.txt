      Christmas Vacation for C.A.T
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2017 5:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    
    The Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT), Ernakulam Bench will remain closed from 26th December to 29th December 2017 on account of Christmas vacation. During the period, the vacation Bench will hold sitting on 27th December at 11.00 a.m. to hear urgent matters. The Registry of the Tribunal will function as usual during the vacation period and will receive only applications of urgent nature between 10. 30 a.m. and 1.00 p.m.
    IE-AB (21.12.2017)
