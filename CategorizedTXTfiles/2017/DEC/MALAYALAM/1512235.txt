      Four Malayalam books of Publications Division to be released in Kochi
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2017 7:25PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Four Malayalam books brought out by Publications Division under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting will be released at the International book festival in Ernakulam on Friday, December 8, 2017 by eminent archeologist Dr. K.K. Mohammad. Director General Publications Division Smt. Sadhana Rout will preside over the function. Rani LekshmiBai, SardarVallabhabai Patel, Razia Sultana, SaralPanchathantra part two are the books which will be released.


    Fifty percent discount will be given for books purchased from publications divisions stall at the International book festival. Apart from this 35% discount will be given to all books for purchase above Rs. 10,000. Facilities are also available in the stall for subscription and purchase of Central Government magazines like Yojana and Kurukshetra from the stall.
