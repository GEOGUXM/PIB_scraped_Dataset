      Thiruvananthapuram Division’s Dak Adalat on December 22nd
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2017 7:41PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Thiruvananthapuram South Postal division’s Dak Adalat will be held at Thiruvananthapuram South Divisional Office on December 22nd at 3 PM. Complaints if any against the services rendered by the offices in the Thiruvananthapuram South Division may be forwarded to the address “Shri. B Padmakumar, Superintendent of Post Offices, Thiruvananthapuram South Division, Thiruvananthapuram-695036” on or before 15-12-2017. The cover containing complaints should be super scribed as ‘DAK ADALAT’.


    Earlier complaints received at customer care centers or Division level, which are not settled till the date only will be considered at DAK ADALAT.
