      President of India to grace the valedictory function of 32ND Indian Engineering Congresstomorrow
      Posted On: 22 DEC 2017 5:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram NathKovind, will grace the valedictory function of the 32 nd Indian Engineering Congress organised by the Institution of Engineers (India) in Chennai tomorrow (December 23, 2017). This will be his first visit to the State of Tamil Nadu after assuming office as President. Earlier in the day, he will visit the Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Memorial in Rameswaram to pay his respects.


    On December 24, 2017, the President will reach RashtrapatiNilayam, Secunderbad, for his southern sojourn. On the same day, the President will attend a dinner hosted by Shri ESL Narasimhan, Governor of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.


    On December 25, 2017, the President will meet Chief Justice and Judges of the High Court of Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. On the same day, he will visit Kanha Shanti Vanam and plant a sapling there.


    On December 26, 2017, the President will host a reception for senior dignitaries of the State, Ministers, officials, leading citizens, academics, etc. at the RashtrapatiNilayam, Secunderabad.


    On December 27, 2017, the President will visit Andhra Pradesh and inaugurate the centenary conference of the Indian Economic Association at Acharya Nagarjuna University, Guntur. On the same day, he will also dedicate various projects of the Andhra Pradesh Government at the Interim Government Complex, Secretariat, Velagapudi, before returning to Delhi.
