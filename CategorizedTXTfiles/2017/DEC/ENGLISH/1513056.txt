      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Status of work on National Highways in Hyderabad and Karnataka region
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 5:50PM by PIB Delhi


    The contracts for 14 projects amounting to Rs. 6086.16 Crore having length of 843.93 km are ongoing which are likely to be completed by Mar 2020 and 4 projects are in tendering stage and in addition 18 projects have been proposed for development of National Highways in Hyderabad, Karnataka (Article 371(J) region in Karnataka.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Road Transport & Highways, Shipping and C&F Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya in a written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today
