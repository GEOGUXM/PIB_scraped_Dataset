      PM congratulates Viswanathan Anand on winning World Rapid Chess Championship
      Posted On: 29 DEC 2017 6:04PM by PIB Delhi

      Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Viswanathan Anand on winning World Rapid Chess Championship.
      PM said, " Congratulations Viswanathan Anand . You have proven your mettle time and again. Your tenacity inspires us. India is proud of your exemplary success at the World Rapid Chess Championship."
