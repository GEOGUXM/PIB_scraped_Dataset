      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Increase in Trade Deficit
      Posted On: 20 DEC 2017 5:36PM by PIB Delhi


    India’s merchandise exports registered a positive growth of 5.17% during 2016-17 over 2015-16 and 12.01% during 2017-18 (Apr-Nov) over 2016-17 (Apr-Nov). Further, India’s merchandise trade deficit has been declining for last two years.
