      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Rehabilitation of old bridges on NHs
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 5:46PM by PIB Delhi


    There are 23 bridges on National Highways which are over 100 years old, out of which 2 bridges at Km 143.800 on NH-19 in the State of Bihar and at Km 266.370 on NH-228 in the State of Gujarat require rehabilitation and major maintenance and other bridges require only minor and routine maintenance.


    As per the condition survey conducted till date various defects and distress has been noticed in several bridges. The State wise number of 6716 such bridges requiring minor, major repair and rehabilitation are as follows:-
