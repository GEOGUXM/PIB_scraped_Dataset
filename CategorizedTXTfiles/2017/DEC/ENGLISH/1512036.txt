      Ministry of Shipping

      V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust creates New Record in Single day Cargo Handling
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2017 5:44PM by PIB Delhi


    V O Chidambaranar Port Trust in Chennai created a new record for single day handling of cargo yesterday, when it handled 36,526 Metric Tonnes of thermal coal at the berth NCB-I from the vessel MV. Asian Champion. The previous single day record was 35,656 Metric Tonnes of thermal coal at the same berth from the vessel MV. Tentenc on 30.10.2016.


    Shri I. Jeyakumar, Chairman of V.O. Chidambaranar Port Trust said that this Port is continuously striving to achieve improvement in performance and productivity in order to attract more volume of traffic.
