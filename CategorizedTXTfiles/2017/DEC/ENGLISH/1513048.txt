      President of India to address the valedictory function of the World Telugu Conference tomorrow
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 5:37PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Telangana (Hyderabad) on December 19 and 20, 2017.


    On December 19, 2017, the President will address the valedictory function of the World Telugu Conference in Hyderabad.


    On December 20, 2017, the President will pay his respects at the statue of Lord Buddha at Hussain Sagar Lake, Hyderabad before returning to Delhi.
