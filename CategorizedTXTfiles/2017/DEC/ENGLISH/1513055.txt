      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Four lane roads in Himachal Pradesh
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 5:47PM by PIB Delhi


    This Ministry of Road Transport and Highways is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways network in the country. During the last three years and the current year, five National Highway projects have been sanctioned / awarded in Himachal Pradesh for four lane construction as per details below:-

                                                                                                                                                                                    
Sl. No.  Name of the Project                                                                                      Status of Work                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
1        Four-laning of NH-21 from Nerchowk to Pandoh including Pandoh bypass Section (Km 190.000 to Km 221.305)  Work awarded. Targeted for completion in 30 months from appointed.
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
2        Four-laning of NH-21 from Pandoh bypass to Takoli Section (Km 221.305 to Km 242.000)                     Work awarded. Targeted for completion in 42 months from appointed.
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
3        Four-laning of NH-21 from Takoli to Kullu Section (Km 242.000 to Km 272.000))                            Work awarded. Targeted date for completion is December, 2019.     
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
4        Four-laning of NH-22 from Parwanoo to Solan Section (Km. 67.000 to Km 106.139)                           Work awarded. Targeted date for completion is March, 2018.        
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    
5        Four-laning of NH-22 from Solan to Kaithlighat Section (Km 106.139 to Km 131.00)                         Work sanctioned and bids are invited.                             
                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                    

    This information was given by Minister of State for Road Transport & Highways, Shipping and C&F Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya in a written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
