      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      More than 24 lakh Anganwadi Workers /Helpers receiving benefit of insurance cover
      Posted On: 22 DEC 2017 5:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The Govt. of India has provided insurance cover to Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) and Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) in association with the Life Insurance Corporation of India. The AWWs and AWHs in the age group of 18-50 years are covered under Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) and Rs.2 lakhs is payable on member’s death due to any reason; those in the age group of 18-70 years are covered under Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) for which following benefits are being given: -

Total and irrecoverable loss of sight of one eye or loss of use of one hand or foot                                                         Rs.1 lakh  
                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                           

    AWWs and AWHs in the age group of 51-59 years are covered under modified Anganwadi Karyakarti Bima Yojana (AKBY) as long as they are engaged. The Scheme provides the following compensations:


     1. Natural Death – Rs. 30,000/- and


     1. Accident – (a) Death/Total permanent disability – Rs.75,000/-


    (b) Partial Permanent disability – Rs.37,500/.
