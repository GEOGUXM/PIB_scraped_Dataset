      Decongesting Cities

    The Ministry is implementing a scheme namely, ‘Strengthening of Public Transport System’ to improve public transport system. The Scheme helps in reducing dependence on private vehicles. This scheme provides for financial assistance to states for strengthening of public transport system. The assistance to States is for introduction of information technology solutions in public transport system.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh L. Mandaviya in a written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today
