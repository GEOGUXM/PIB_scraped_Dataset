      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Status of New AIIMS
      Posted On: 19 DEC 2017 3:24PM by PIB Delhi


      In Budget 2014-15, four new AIIMS at Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal, Vidarbha in Maharashtra and Poorvanchal in UP have been announced.


      In Budget 2015-16, six new AIIMS at Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Assam, Tamil Nadu, Punjab and Bihar have been announced.


      In Budget 2017-18, two new AIIMS at Jharkhand and Gujarat have been announced.


      Detailed status of new AIIMS is attached as follow:
