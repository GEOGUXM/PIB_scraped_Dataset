      PM condoles the demise of former CJI Justice AS Anand
      Posted On: 01 DEC 2017 7:23PM by PIB Delhi

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of former Chief Justice of India, Justice AS Anand.

      “Saddened by the demise of former Chief Justice of India, Justice AS Anand. My thoughts are with his family in this hour of grief. May his soul rest in peace.”, the Prime Minister said.
