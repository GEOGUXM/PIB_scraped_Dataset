      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Housing Facility to beedi workers
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 4:45PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Labour & Employment has formulated Revised Integrated Housing Scheme (RIHS), 2007, which has now been revamped to RIHS, 2016, which is applicable to the workers engaged in Beedi/Iron Ore Mines, Manganese Ore & Chrome Ore Mines (IOMC)/Limestone Ore Mines, Dolomite Ore Mines (LSDM) /Mica Mines and Cine Industries, registered with the Labour Welfare Organisation (LWO).


    In the current scheme, a housing subsidy of Rs. 1,50,000/- is released in three installments in 25:60:15 ratio. First installment (25%) of the subsidy i.e. Rs. 37,500/- per tenement is released as advance, the second installment (60%) of the subsidy i.e. Rs. 90,000/- is released on reaching the lintel level and the third installment (15%) i.e. Rs. 22,500/- per tenement is released after the construction of the house has been completed.
