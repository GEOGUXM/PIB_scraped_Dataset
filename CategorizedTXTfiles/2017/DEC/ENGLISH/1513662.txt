      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Projects/proposals amounting to more than Rs.2600 Crores appraised/recommended under Nirbhaya Fund
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2017 4:52PM by PIB Delhi


      Government of India has set up a dedicated fund called “Nirbhaya Fund” in 2013, for implementation of initiatives aimed at enhancing the safety and security for women in the country. It is a non-lapsable corpus fund. The details of funds allocated under Nirbhaya Fund is as under:

                                                                          
Years              2013-14   2014-15   2015-16  2016-17  2017-18  Total   
                                                                          
                                                                          
                                                                          
Funds Allocated    1000 Cr.  1000 Cr.  Nil      550 Cr.  550 Cr.  3100 Cr.
                                                                          
                                                                          
(Rupees in Crore)                                                         
                                                                          
                                                                          

      The Empowered Committee set up under “Nirbhaya Fund” by the Government of India appraises, recommends and reviews from time to time various proposals/projects proposed by different Ministries/Departments/ States/UTs. So far twelve meetings of Empowered Committee have been held which appraised and recommended p roposals/projects amounting to Rs.2644 Crores approximately.
