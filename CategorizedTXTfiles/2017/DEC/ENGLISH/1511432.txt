      PM greets people of Nagaland on Statehood Day
      Posted On: 01 DEC 2017 11:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Nagaland on their Statehood Day.


    “On the occasion of their Statehood Day, I convey my greetings to the people of Nagaland. This state is blessed with immense natural beauty and industrious citizens. I pray that the state scales new heights of development in the times to come.”, the Prime Minister said.
