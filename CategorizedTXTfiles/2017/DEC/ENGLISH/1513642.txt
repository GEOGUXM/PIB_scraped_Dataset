      Ministry of New and Renewable Energy

      Generation of Electricity from Municipal Solid Wastes
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2017 4:22PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of State (IC) for Power and New & Renewable Energy, Shri Raj Kumar Singh, in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today, informed that only one Waste to Energy project from South Delhi Municipal Corporation was received and approved for funding under the Swachh Bharat Mission ( SBM ) , being implemented by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs .


    Shri Singh added that at present, six waste - to - energy plants using Municipal Solid Waste ( MSW ) , with a cumulative installed capacity of 65 . 75 MW are in operation in the country .
