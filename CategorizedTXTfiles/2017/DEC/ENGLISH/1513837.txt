      Ministry of Finance

      Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) by Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI)
      Posted On: 22 DEC 2017 4:38PM by PIB Delhi


    Rs. 103.45 crores has been disbursed to Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) under the Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS) scheme by SIDBI as on December 15, 2017.


    Commitment of funds under FFS has been made to 18 AIFs so far. As on December 15, 2017, 13 AIFs have commenced drawdowns. These 13 AIFs have invested in 77 Startups so far.
