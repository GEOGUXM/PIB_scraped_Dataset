      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Rabi Crops sowing crosses 514 lakh hectare
      Posted On: 15 DEC 2017 5:46PM by PIB Delhi


    As per preliminary reports received from the States, the total area sown under Rabi crops as on 15 th December, 2017 stands at 514.22 lakh hectares as compared to 509.12 lakh hectare this time in 2016.


    Wheat has been sown/transplanted in 245.50 lakh hectares, Rice in 12.88 lakh hectares, pulses in 138.19 lakh hectares, coarse cereals in 46.93 lakh hectares and area sown under oilseeds is 70.73 lakh hectares.
