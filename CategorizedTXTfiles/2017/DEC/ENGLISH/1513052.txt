      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      National Highway Projects in Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 18 DEC 2017 5:42PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has approved Annual Plan consisting of 34 nos. of projects amounting to Rs. 915.71 Crore for development of 408.52 kms of National Highways under NH (O) for 2017-18. Besides, National Highway Authority of India has also targeted to award 14 nos. of projects amounting to Rs.12774.67 crore for development of 601.52 kms of National Highways in Tamil Nadu for the year 2017-18. Detail of projects to be sanctioned/awarded is as follows:-
