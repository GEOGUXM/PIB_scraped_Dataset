      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Rabi Crops Sowing Crosses 565 Lakh Hactare
      Posted On: 29 DEC 2017 5:22PM by PIB Delhi


    As per preliminary reports received from the States, the total area sown under Rabi crops as on 29 th December, 2017 stands at 565.79 lakh hectares as compared to 571.47 lakh hectare this time in 2016.


    Wheat has been sown/transplanted in 273.85 lakh hectares, rice in 16.33 lakh hectares, pulses in 150.63 lakh hectares, coarse cereals in 50.71 lakh hectares and area sown under oilseeds is 74.27 lakh hectares.
