      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Survey on Tb Prevalency
      Posted On: 19 DEC 2017 3:20PM by PIB Delhi


    39 percent of India’s population is not suffering from Tuberculosis. The programme is conducting a TB prevalence survey which will provide information specific for the country in the year 2018.


    The National Strategic Plan (NSP) for Tuberculosis (2017-25) has been formulated by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.In addition to the existing strategies under RNTCP, the NSP focusses on:


    · early diagnosis of all the TB patients, prompt treatment with quality assured drugs and treatment regimens


    · suitable patient support systems to promote adherence.


    · engaging with the patients seeking care in the private sector.


    · prevention strategies including active case finding and contact tracing in high risk / vulnerable population and


    · Use of ICT tools for effective monitoring of Patients.


    · Multi-sectoral response for addressing social determinants
