      Ministry of Food Processing Industries

      Expansion of Food Processing Industries in the Country
      Posted On: 27 DEC 2017 4:13PM by PIB Delhi


      With a view to promote Food Processing Industries in the country, the Government has approved the umbrella Central Sector Scheme “PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA” with and allocation of Rs 6,000 Crore for the period coterminous with the 14 th Finance Commission cycle.


      The status of progress of the projects under various schemes of Ministry of Food Processing Industries, during the last three years is placed below.
