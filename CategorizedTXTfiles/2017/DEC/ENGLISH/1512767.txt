      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      Rescheduling of Winter Session of Parliament is not unprecedented: Shri Vijay Goel
      Posted On: 15 DEC 2017 4:25PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of State for Parliamentary Affairs and Statistics & Programme Implementation Shri Vijay Goel, in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today informed that, in the past also the Winter Session of Parliament had been held for lesser period or rescheduled as given below:


    Lok Sabha

                                                                       
Lok Sabha/Session      From        To          Duration  Actual sitting
                                                                       
                                                                       
                                                                       
5 th /18 th            25.10.1976  05.11.1976  12        11            
                                                                       
                                                                       
                                                                       
9 th /6 th             27.12.1990  11.01.1991  16        10            
                                                                       
                                                                       
                                                                       
10 th /12 th           07.12.1994  23.12.1994  17        13            
                                                                       
                                                                       
                                                                       
15 th /15 th (Part-I)  05.12.2013  18.12.2013  14        10

    The Winter Session, 2017 has 14 sittings spreading over 22 days, Shri Goel added.
