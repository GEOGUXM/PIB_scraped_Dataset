      PM pays tributes to former PM Chaudhary Charan Singh on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 23 DEC 2017 3:38PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister, Shri Chaudhary Charan Singh, on his birth anniversary.


    “Remembering Chaudhary Charan Singh ji on his birth anniversary. Chaudhary Sahab served the nation with utmost dedication. He worked assiduously for the development of our villages and always championed the rights of our farmers.”, the Prime Minister said.
