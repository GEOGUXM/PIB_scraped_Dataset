      Ministry of Electronics & IT

      PENSION ADALAT FOR SETTLEMENT OF COMPLAINTS AND PROBLEMS
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2017 2:22PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Pension Adalat for settlement of Complaints and problems of Postal Pensioners will be held at 11:00 hrs onwards on 19.12.2017 at the office of Sr. Supdt. Of Post Offices, City Division, Ahmedabad, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad-380009. The Sr. Supdt. Of Post Offices, City, Division, Ahmedabad will hear each complaint individually.


    The Concept of the Pension Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settlement of problems of Pensioners on the spot. The complaints received after due date and related to policy matter will not be entertained.
