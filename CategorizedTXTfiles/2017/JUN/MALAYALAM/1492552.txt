      Quiz Competition for Employees
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2017 4:23PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The staff recreation club of C-DAC, Thiruvananthapuram, SWARAM is organizing the 11th edition of the Inter-Institutional Quiz Competition, iIQ 2017 at 2.00 pm on Saturday, 10th June 2017, at C-DAC, Vellayambalam. The topic is General Awareness and Current Affairs. The quiz is open for the employees of Government/Private/Public Sector Organizations and recognized educational institutions in Kerala. Teams are requested to register online @ http://tinyurl.com/nnulu2l


    The last date for Registration is 08th June 2017.


    For further details, contact via e-mail jayan@cdac.in or call Phone (+91 9895054580).
