      Dak Adalat on June 20th
      Posted On: 01 JUN 2017 3:54PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Thiruvananthapuram South Postal Division’s Divisional level ‘Dak Adalat’ will be held on 20 th June 3 PM. Complaints if any against the services rendered by the offices in Thiruvananthapuram South Division may be forwarded to Shri. Padmakumar, Superintendent of PO’s, Thiruvananthapuram South Division, Thiruvanathapuram-695036 on or before 12 th June. The cover containing complaints should be super scribed as ‘DAK ADALT’.


    Earlier complaints received at customer care center or Divisional levels which are not settled till date will be considered at DAK ADALAT. New complaints are considered as usual.
