      Sabka Saath Sabka Vikas Sammelan at Pathanamthitta tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 JUN 2017 2:46PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    As part of the third anniversary celebrations of the NDA government at the Centre, Sabka Saath Sabka Vikas Sammelan will be held at St.Stephens Auditorium, Makkamkunnu, Pathanamthitta tomorrow (15 June 2017). BJP State president Shri. Kummanam Rajasekharan will be the chief Guest. The aim of the Sammelan is to increase awareness on the schemes of the government that have made difference and positively impacted the lives of the common man. There will be video presentation of 3 years of achievements of government. Various entertainment programmes will also be held. The Sammelan is organised by Dredging Corporation of India Limited, Visakhapatnam.
