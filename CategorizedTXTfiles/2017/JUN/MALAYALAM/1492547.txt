      TRAIN DETENTION FOR MAINTENANCE WORK
      Posted On: 02 JUN 2017 4:03PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Ongoing track maintenance in Mararikkulam-Alappuzha section is now shifted to Alappuzha-Ambalappuzha section. As such Train No. 16128, Guruvayur-Chennai Egmore Express and train No. 16603, Mangaluru-Thiruvananthapuram Maveli Express is likely to be detained for 15 minutes to 30 minutes at Alappuzha station till 20th June 2017. Train No. 22207, Chennai Central- Trivandrum express reaching Alappuzha station on Saturdays alone will also get detained for 20 minutes at Alappuzha Station during the maintenance period.
