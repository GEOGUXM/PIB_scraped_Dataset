      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Shri Sher Bahadur Deuba on being elected the Prime Minister of Nepal
      Posted On: 06 JUN 2017 2:45PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Shri Sher Bahadur Deuba on being elected the Prime Minister of Nepal.

    
    "Called Shri Sher Bahadur Deuba to congratulate him on being elected the Prime Minister of Nepal
    
    I conveyed my best wishes to Shri Deuba for peace, prosperity and progress in Nepal under his leadership", the Prime Minister said.
