      MODI Fest to mark the completion of three years of NDA Government to be inaugurated in Chennai tomorrow
      Public can give their suggestions on government schemes during the fest

      Posted On: 01 JUN 2017 5:42PM by PIB Chennai

    Three Day festival MODI Fest, (Making of Developed India Festival), organized by Information & Broadcasting Ministry, to mark the completion of three years of NDA government will begin in Chennai tomorrow(02.06.2017). The festival gets underway in Tamil Nadu with the first exhibition being inaugurated by Union Minister of State for Road Transport, Highways and Shipping, Shri Pon. Radhakrishnan at YMCA grounds, Nandanam by 11 A.M tomorrow.


    Public can give their suggestion and feedback on government schemes in the ‘Jan Ki Baat’ stall, put up in the MODI Fest. And the fest also provide the public an opportunity to get detailed information about the various schemes of the Central government like Skill development, Jan Dhan Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, Fasal Beema Yojana and Ujjwala Yojana.


    Various contests like Quiz, Selfie with PM, Guidance session by experts on various subjects and Lucky draw are also planned to make the experience interactive and lively.


    MODI fest is being conducted throughout the country and will be held at 12 places in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry. Achievements and initiatives of the central government in the last three years will be highlighted. MODI Fest will be conducted till June 18, 2017 at various places.
