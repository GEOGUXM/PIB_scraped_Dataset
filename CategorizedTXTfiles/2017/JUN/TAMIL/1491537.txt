      Last date for nominations of Padma Awards is Sept 15th, 2017
      Posted On: 01 JUN 2017 4:38PM by PIB Chennai


      The process of nominations for the Padma Awards, 2018 is on from 1 st May 2017. The last date for receipt of the nominations for the Padma Awards 2018 is September 15, 2017.


      The nominations/ recommendations for the Padma Awards will be received online only on the Padma portal designed by MHA, which is available on the address www.padmaawards.gov.in . The Padma Portal gives details of all previous awardees.


      Nomination/recommendation will be accepted only online. Nominations received after September 15, 2017 will also not be considered.


      With the online portal, public at large have been enabled to participate in the nomination process.
