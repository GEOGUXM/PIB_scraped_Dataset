      Prime Minister's Office

      Ustad Amjad Ali Khan calls on PM
      Posted On: 13 JUN 2017 6:18PM by PIB Chennai


    Ustad Amjad Ali Khan, Sarod Player called on the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, today.


    The veteran musician and Sarod Maestro has presented his book ‘ Master on Masters ’ on the lives and times of 20 greatest icons of Indian Classical Music, to the Prime Minister.
