      Recruitment of Direct Agents for PLI
      Posted On: 13 JUN 2017 3:33PM by PIB Chennai


    Office of Senior superintendent of Post Offices, Tambaram Division is conducting an walk in interview for the post of PLI/RPLI Direct Agents. The interview will take place on 23.06.17 at 11:00 Hrs.


    The applicant should have passed 12 th standard or equivalent examination conducted by any recognized Board/institution (In areas, where population is less than 5000, 10 th pass or equivalent is eligible). The applicant should be between 18 years to 65 years of age on the date of interview.
