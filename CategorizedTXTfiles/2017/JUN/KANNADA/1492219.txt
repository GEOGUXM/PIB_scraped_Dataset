      PM greets BIMSTEC nations on 20th anniversary of BIMSTEC

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted BIMSTEC nations on the 20 th anniversary of BIMSTEC ( Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation).


      “Today is a special day- we mark the 20th anniversary of BIMSTEC. My greetings to all BIMSTEC nations.


      BIMSTEC is a natural platform for regional cooperation. The commitment to peace and development connects the BIMSTEC family”, the Prime Minister said.
