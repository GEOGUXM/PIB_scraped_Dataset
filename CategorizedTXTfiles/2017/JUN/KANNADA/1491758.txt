      PM congratulates Sai Praneeth on winning Thailand Open badminton tournament

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has congratulated Sh. B Sai Praneeth for winning the Thailand Open badminton tournament.


      “Congratulations to B Sai Praneeth for winning the Thailand Open badminton tournament. India is elated on the accomplishment”, the Prime Minister said.
