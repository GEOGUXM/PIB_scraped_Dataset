      PROMOTIONAL OFFERS: BSNL
      Posted On: 07 JUN 2017 6:50PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited ( BSNL ) Telangana Telecom Circle is happy to announce promotinal offer of a new FTTH (Fiber to the Home) plan "Fibro Combo ULD 550" for the benefit of the customers in Adilabad, Khammam, Mahabubnagar, Nalgonda, Nizamabad, & Warangal Distircts for a period of 90 days w.e.f. 01-06-2017. Customers can enjoy the following facilites under this plan with a fixed monthly charges of Rs 550/- . Unlimited Data with a speed upto 10 Mbps till 60 GB and 1 Mbps thereafter. Free calls between 9 PM to 7 AM every day & full day on all Sundays to any net work. For further details please visit nearest BSNL customer service centre or call toll free number 1800 345 1500 or visit BSNL website www.bsnl.co.in .
