      BSNL TO ORGANISE MEGA MELA BETWEEN JUNE 21 to 24 JUNE 2017
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2017 8:15PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited ( BSNL ) is conducting Mega Mela on 21-06-2017 to 24-06-2017 in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. During the Mela, Customers can get Free 3G SMART SIMs in all Customer Service Centers, Franchaisees/Retail outlets and in Road shows. Customers can get 350MB Data free with all new connections. They can book Land Line, Broadbabd and FTTH connections also at melas conducted by BSNL Staff. BSNL CGM Shri Anantharam requested the customers to avail this opportunity. Further details can be had from 1503 or 18001801503.
