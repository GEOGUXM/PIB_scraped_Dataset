      GOODS AND SERVICES TAX LAUNCH FUNCTION New Delhi, June 28
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2017 6:49PM by PIB Hyderabad


    A special function for launch of the Goods and Services Tax will be held in the Central Hall of Parliament on June 30, 2017 from 11PM onwards. The event would be live webcasted by DD on PIB site www.pib.nic.in .


    Press Information Bureau has made elaborative arrangements for coverage of the event and instant translation of speeches of President, Prime Minister and Finance Minister in English, Hindi and Urdu. The event would be live webcasted by DD on PIB site. All Regional/Brach Offices are requested to intimate the regional media about this. Social media Unit will also be live tweeting the event which may also be intimated to local media.
