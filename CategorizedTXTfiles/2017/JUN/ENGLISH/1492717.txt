      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 47.23 per bbl on 13.06.2017
      Posted On: 14 JUN 2017 10:52AM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was maintained at the same level at US$ 47.23 per barrel (bbl) on 13.06.2017, as on 12.06.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3043.64 per bbl on 13.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 3038.56 per bbl on 12.06.2017. Rupee closed weaker at Rs . 64.45 per US$ on 13.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.34 per US$ on 12.06.2017 .
