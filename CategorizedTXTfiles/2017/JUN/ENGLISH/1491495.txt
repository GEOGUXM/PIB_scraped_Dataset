      President of India to lay foundation stone of driver’s training institute and secondary school at village Dhaula under SMARTGRAM initiative tomorrow
      Posted On: 01 JUN 2017 1:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit village Dhaula, Gurugram (Haryana) tomorrow (June 2, 2017) to lay the foundation stone of a Driver’s Training Institute and a Secondary School under the SMARTGRAM Initiative.
