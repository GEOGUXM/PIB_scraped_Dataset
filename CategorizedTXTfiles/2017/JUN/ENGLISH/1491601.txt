      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 50.17 per bbl on 01.06.2017
      Posted On: 02 JUN 2017 11:24AM by PIB Delhi

      

        The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 50.17 per barrel (bbl) on 01.06.2017. This was higher than the price of US$ 49.86 per bbl on previous publishing day of 31.05.2017.


        In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3234.72 per bbl on 01.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 3218.04 per bbl on 31.05.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 64.47 p er US$ on 01.06.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.55 per US$ on 31.05.2017 .
