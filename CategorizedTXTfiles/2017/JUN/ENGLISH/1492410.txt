      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Preliminary Report of Crop Coverage in the Kharif Season
      
      
      Kharif Crop Sown in 81.33 Lakh Hectare so far

      Posted On: 09 JUN 2017 5:33PM by PIB Delhi
      

        Preliminary reports of crop coverage in the Kharif season have started coming in. The total sown area as on 9 th June 2017, as per reports received from States, stands at 81.33 lakh hectare as compared to 72.31 lakh hectare at this time last year.


        It is reported that rice has been sown/transplanted in 5.51 lakh ha, pulses in 1.64 lakh ha, sugarcane in 47.39 lakh hectare and cotton in 14.06 lakh ha.
