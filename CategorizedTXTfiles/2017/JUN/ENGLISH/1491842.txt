      Ministry of Finance

      24 States pass the State GST (SGST) Act While 7 States viz. Meghalaya,Punjab,Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Jammu & Kashmir and West Bengal have yet to pass the SGST Act.
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2017 7:19PM by PIB Delhi


        Twenty Four (24) States have passed the State GST (SGST) Act till today i.e. 5 th June, 2017 while 7 States viz. Meghalaya, Punjab,Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Jammu & Kashmir and West Beng al have yet to pass the State GST (SGST) Act .
