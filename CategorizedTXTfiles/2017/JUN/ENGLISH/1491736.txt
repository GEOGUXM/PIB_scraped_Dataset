      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      Sharad Jain new DG NWDA
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2017 3:06PM by PIB Delhi


    Dr. Sharad Kumar Jain has assumed the additional charge of Director General, National Water Development Agency (NWDA) under Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation. Shri Jain is presently working as Scientist G with National Institute of Hydrology (NIH), Roorkee. Shri Jain succeeds Shri S. Masood Husain who has moved over as Member (WP&P), Central Water Commission.
