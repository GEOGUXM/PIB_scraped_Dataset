      Ministry of Coal Establishes GST Facilitation Cells for All Stakeholders
      Posted On: 09 JUN 2017 5:32PM by PIB Delhi


        With a view to facilitate the implementation of GST for the stakeholders concerned with Ministry of Coal and its organizations, Secretary ( Coal ) held a meeting to review the status of preparedness for GST and the steps to be taken by the organizations under it for smooth implementation within the organizations and also to facilitate and guide the stakeholders particularly, their consumers and suppliers . A facilitation cell has been opened in the Ministry and GST Cell has been made operational in each of the PSUs under it .
