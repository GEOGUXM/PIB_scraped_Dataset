      JNPT observes World Environment Day: organizes training on Sustainable Port Management
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2017 5:30PM by PIB Delhi


      On the occasion of World Environment Day, the JNPT Antwerp Port Training and Consultancy Foundation has arranged a 5 day training program on “ Ports and Environment-Sustainable and Responsible Management” from 5 th to 9 th June 2017, at its training centre for officials of other major ports.


      Shri Anil Diggikar, Chairman, JNPT today administered Environmental Pledge to 200 port employees. A sapling plantation drive was also carried out near the Administration Building of JNPT. World Environment Day is celebrated worldwide to create awareness about protection of the Environment. This year’s theme declared by United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) is “Connecting People to Nature”.
