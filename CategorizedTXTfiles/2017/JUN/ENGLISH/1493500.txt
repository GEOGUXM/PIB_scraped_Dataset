      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointments
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2017 9:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The ACC has approved in-situ upgradation of the following officers to the level of Special Secretary, as a measure personal to them, by temporarily upgrading the posts held by them, with effect from the date of assumption of charge of the post of Secretary to Government of India by the immediate inter-se junior of each officer.


    (1) Smt Reena Ray, IAS (AGMUT:1984), Additional Secretary, Ministry of Human Resource Development


    (2) Smt Malini Shankar, IAS (MH:1984), Director General, Shipping


    (3) Shri Alok Srivastava, IAS (MP:1984), Additional Secretary, Ministry of Shipping


    (4) Shri Rajiv Kumar, IAS (JH:1984), Establishment Officer & Additional Secretary, DoPT


    (5) Shri A.B.P. Pandey, IAS (MH:1984), CEO, Unique Identification Authority of India
