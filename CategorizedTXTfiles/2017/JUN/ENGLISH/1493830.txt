      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 44.28* per bbl on 26.06.2017
      Posted On: 27 JUN 2017 11:20AM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 44.28* per barrel (bbl) on 26.06.2017.
