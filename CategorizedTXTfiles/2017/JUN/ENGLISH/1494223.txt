      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Kharif Crop Sown in 222.30 Lakh Hectare so far
      Posted On: 30 JUN 2017 5:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The total sown area as on 30 th June 2017, as per reports received from States, stands at 222.30 lakh hectare as compared to 187.03 lakh hectare at this time last year.


    It is reported that rice has been sown/transplanted in 38.93 lakh ha, pulses in 18.80 lakh ha, coarse cereals in 38.12 lakh ha, sugarcane in 47.52 lakh hectare and cotton in 46.10 lakh ha.
