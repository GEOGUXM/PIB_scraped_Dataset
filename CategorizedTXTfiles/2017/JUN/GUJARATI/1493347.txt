      President's Secretariat

      PRESS COMMUNIQUE
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2017 4:04PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The President has accepted the resignation of Shri Ram Nath Kovind, as Governor of Bihar.
    
    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri Keshari Nath Tripathi, Governor of West Bengal to discharge the functions of the Governor of Bihar, in addition to his own duties, until regular arrangements for the office of the Governor of Bihar is made.
