      DAK ADALAT FOR SETTLEMENT OF COMPLAINTS REGARDING POSTAL SERVICES
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2017 4:17PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 20-06-2017


    Dak Adalat for settlement of complaints and problems of Ahmedabad General Post Office Jurisdiction only on postal services will be held at 11:30 hours on 30.06.2017 at the office of chief postmaster, Ahmedabad GPO-380001. The Manager will hear each complaint individually. The idea of above Divisional Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settle problems of member of public on the spot. The complaints relating to postal service should be sent to Dy. Manager, Customer Care Centre, Ahmedabad GPO, Ahmedabad-380001. The complaints should reach to above officer latest by 24.06.2017 complaints of general nature and pertaining to policy matter will not be entertained. The complaints should be in brief and related to Ahmedabad GPO only.
