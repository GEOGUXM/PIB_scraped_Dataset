      Prime Minister's Office

      PM strongly condemns attacks in London
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2017 5:36PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condemned the attacks in London and has said that the attacks are shocking and anguishing.


    “Attacks in London are shocking and anguishing. We condemn them. My thoughts are with families of the deceased and prayers with the injured.” the Prime Minister said.
