      PM expresses sadness over the loss of lives in the forest fire in Portugal
      Posted On: 18 JUN 2017 8:32PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressed sadness over the loss of lives in the forest fire in Portugal.

    
    "Sad to learn of tragic loss of lives in the forest fire in Portugal. Deepest condolences to the Portugese people on this tragedy", the Prime Minister said.
