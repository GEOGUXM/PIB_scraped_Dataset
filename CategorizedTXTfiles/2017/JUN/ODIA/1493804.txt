      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets on Eid-ul-Fitr.
      Posted On: 26 JUN 2017 9:16PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has conveyed his greetings on the festival of Eid-ul-Fitr.

    
    "Eid-ul-Fitr greetings! May this auspicious day further the spirit of peace and brotherhood in our society", the Prime Minister said.
