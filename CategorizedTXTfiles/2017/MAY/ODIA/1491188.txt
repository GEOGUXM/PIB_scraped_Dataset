      PM addresses 8th HOM Conference
      Posted On: 06 MAY 2017 5:57PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today addressed the 8th Heads of Mission Conference in New Delhi.


    The theme of this year's Conference is "Effective Diplomacy, Excellent Delivery."
