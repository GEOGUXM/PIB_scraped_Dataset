      Register Aadhar Number for continued receipt of Pensionary Benefits
      Posted On: 08 MAY 2017 3:54PM by PIB Chennai


    As members and pensioners of Employees’ Pension Scheme (EPS-1995), run by the EPFO, receive subsidy from the Government of India, the Government through notification dated 4th January, 2017 has made the Aadhar Number mandatory for continued receipt of Pensionary Benefits for the members and pensioners of the EPS. In order to continue the benefit of Government’s subsidy under the scheme, the members and pensioners are required to undergo Aadhar enrollment process and submit their Aadhar details to EPFO and also submit their Digital Life Certificate (Jeevan Pramaan Patra).


    Pensioners who are yet to submit their Digital Life Certificate or those who have submitted their Life Certificates in prescribed form in paper to the Bank are also required to enroll themselves for Aadhar immediately and submit their Digital Life Certificates to any EPFO


    office or the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) with copy of Aadhar Card, Bank Pass Book, PPO number and mobile phone for enrollment.


    Pensioners who have not yet submitted Digital Life Certificate ( Jeevan Pramaan Patra) are advised to submit their Digital Life Certificate immediately or otherwise the pension will not be credited in their bank account with effect from 1st June 2017.
