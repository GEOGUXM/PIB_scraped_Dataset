      Deputy Chief Labour Commissioner takes charge
      Posted On: 05 MAY 2017 4:30PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri V. Srinivas, CLS has taken charge as the Deputy Chief Labour Commissioner (Central), Government of India, Ministry of Labour & Employment, at Chennai today. He will be in charge of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.
