      President of India to present Malti Gyan Peeth Puraskars tomorrow
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2017 6:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will present the Malti Gyan Peeth Puraskars for the year 2017 tomorrow (May 29, 2017) at a function to be held at Rashtrapati Bhavan.


    The Malti Gyan Peeth Puraskar was instituted by Mohinder Singh Syngle Education & Research Society in honour of its founder Smt. Malti Mohinder Singh Syngle, eminent educationist and social worker.
