      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 48.82 per bbl on 04.05.2017
      Posted On: 05 MAY 2017 2:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 48.82 per barrel (bbl) on 04.05.2017. This was lower than the price of US$ 49.56 per bbl on previous publishing day of 03.05.2017.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket decreased to Rs. 3135.01 per bbl on 04.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 3179.27 per bbl on 03.05.2017. Rupee closed weaker at Rs . 64.21 p er US$ on 04.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.14 per US$ on 03.05.2017 .
