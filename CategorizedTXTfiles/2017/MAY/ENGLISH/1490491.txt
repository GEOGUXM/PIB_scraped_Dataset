      Ministry of Finance

      Finance Secretary Shri Ashok Lavasa holds investor meetings at the India Financial Conference in New York City
      Forty-four representatives from 35 different Institutional investor firms participated in 148 meetings at the Conference
      Posted On: 23 MAY 2017 3:16PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Ashok Lavasa, Finance Secretary, Government of India, interacted with renowned US based funds and global institutional investors with interests in the BFSI sector at the day long India Financial Conference held recently in New York City.


    Forty-four representatives from 35 different Institutional investor firms participated in 148 meetings organized with the corporates and industry experts. The investor meetings were done on a one-on-one basis and in small group settings.


    The India Financial Conference was hosted by ICICI Securities Limited on May 12, 2017. Ms. Chanda Kochhar, Managing Director, ICICI also attended the Conference along with senior officials of the finance industry of India.
