      Ministry of Earth Science

      Severe Weather Warning
      Posted On: 29 MAY 2017 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


    India Meteorological Department has issued severe weather warning for the three days as below:


    29 th May: Heavy to very heavy rain very likely at few places with extremely heavy fall at isolated places over Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura


    30 th May: Heavy to very heavy rain very likely at a few places with extremely heavy fall at isolated places and squally wind speed 45-55 kmph gusting to 65 kmph would prevail over South Assam and Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram and Tripura.


    31 st May: Heavy to very heavy rain very likely at a few places with extremely heavy fall at isolated places over Assam and Meghalaya.
