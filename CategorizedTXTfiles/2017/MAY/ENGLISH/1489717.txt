      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 49.71 per bbl on 11.05.2017
      Posted On: 12 MAY 2017 2:37PM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 49.71 per barrel (bbl) on 11.05.2017. This was higher than the price of US$ 48.18 per bbl on previous publishing day of 10.05.2017*.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3203.24 per bbl on 11.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 3108.10 per bbl on 10.05.2017. Rupee closed stronger at Rs . 64.44 p er US$ on 11.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.51 per US$ on 10.05.2017** .
