      President of India to address the Annual convocation of IGNFA tomorrow
      Posted On: 04 MAY 2017 1:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Uttarakhand on May 5 and 6, 2017.


    
    On May 5, 2017, the President will address the annual convocation of the Indira Gandhi National Forest Academy (IGNFA) in Dehradun. He will return to Delhi on May 6, 2017.
