      President of India to visit West Bengal on May 18 and 19, 2017
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2017 8:28PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit West Bengal on May 18 and 19, 2017.
    
    On May 18, 2017, the President will inaugurate the Indian Institute of Liver and Digestive Sciences at Sonarpur, South 24 Parganas. On the same day, he will receive the first copy of the book 'Metaphysics, Morals and Politics’ from Prof. Amal Kumar Mukhopadhyay in Kolkata.
    
    On May 19, 2017, the President will pay floral tributes to Shri Neelam Sanjiva Reddy, former President of India on his Birth Anniversary at Raj Bhavan, Kolkata. Later in the day, he will present Dr. Malati Allen Noble Award, Dr. Sarkar Allen Mahatma Hahnemann Award and Dr. Sankar Allen Swamiji Award instituted by the Dr. Malati Allen Charitable Trust in Kolkata and inaugurate the Integrated Renewable Energy Smart Microgrid Centre and Centre for Water and Environmental Research at the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology at Shibpur, Howrah. Before returning to Delhi, he will also receive the books ‘Jnan, A-Jnan O Bijnan’ and ‘Samudra Banijjer Prekshite Sthala Banijjya, Bharat Mahasagar Anchal, 1500-1800’ from Dr. Sushil Chaudhury and Dr. (Smt.) Mahasweta Chaudhury in Kolkata.
