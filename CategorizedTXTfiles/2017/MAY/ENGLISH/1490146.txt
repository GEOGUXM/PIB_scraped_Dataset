      Vice President & Chairman Rajya Sabha condoles the passing away of Shri Anil Madhav Dave
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2017 12:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President and the Chairman Rajya Sabha, Shri M. Hamid Ansari has condoled the passing away of the Minister of State (I/C) of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Shri Anil Madhav Dave. In a message, he said that Shri Dave was an active and respected member of Rajya Sabha since 2009, and his presence would be missed by all who worked with him.


    Following is the text of Vice President’s message :


    “I am deeply grieved to learn of the sad demise of Shri Anil Madhav Dave, Minister of State (I/C) of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.


    His departure is a personal loss. Shri Dave was an active and respected member of Rajya Sabha since 2009, and his presence would be missed by all who worked with him.


    I convey my sincere condolences to his family, friends and supporters and join the nation in praying for the eternal rest of the departed soul.”
