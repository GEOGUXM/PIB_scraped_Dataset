      President of India strongly condemns terror attack in Kabul
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2017 5:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee has strongly condemned the terror attack in Kabul today (May 31, 2017).
    
    In his message to H. E. Dr. Mohammad Ashraf Ghani, the President of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan, the President said, “I write to convey heartfelt condolences on behalf of the Government and people of India for the loss of innocent lives in the terror attack that took place in Kabul this morning.
    
    This is yet another instance of senseless violence that cannot be justified under any circumstances. India strongly condemns this cowardly act and stands in solidarity with the people of Afghanistan during these difficult hours.
    
    Please accept, Excellency, the deepest sympathies of the people of India and our sincerest wishes for the full and speedy recovery of those injured in the attack.
    
    We pray for peace in Afghanistan and the progress of its people”.
