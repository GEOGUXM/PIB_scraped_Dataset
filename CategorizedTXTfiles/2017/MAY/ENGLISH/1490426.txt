      President of India to address the 159th Founder’s Day Celebrations of the Lawrence School Tomorrow
      Posted On: 22 MAY 2017 1:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Tamil Nadu on May 23 and 24, 2017.
    
    On May 23, 2017, the President will address the 159th Founder’s Day Celebrations of the Lawrence School at Ootacamund, The Nigiris. He will return to Delhi on May 24, 2017.
