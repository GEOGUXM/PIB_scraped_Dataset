      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Dr Jitendra Singh briefs media on key initiatives taken by Union Government during last three years
      Posted On: 23 MAY 2017 7:00PM by PIB Delhi

      

        The Union Minister of State (I/C) for Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr. Jitendra Singh briefed the media on the key initiatives taken by the Union Government and progress achieved during the last three years, here today. The Minister also released two booklets during the event.


        During the conference, presentation was made to highlight the key initiatives of Ministry of DoNER, Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT), Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG) and Department of Pension & Pensioners' Welfare (DoP&PW) under Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Department of Atomic Energy and Department of Space in the last 3 years.
