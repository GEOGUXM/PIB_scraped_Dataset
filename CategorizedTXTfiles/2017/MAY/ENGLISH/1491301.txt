      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people of Goa on Statehood Day
      Posted On: 30 MAY 2017 11:42AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Goa on Goa Statehood Day.


    “Greetings to the people of Goa on Goa Statehood Day. I pray for Goa's progress and prosperity in the years to come”, the Prime Minister said.
