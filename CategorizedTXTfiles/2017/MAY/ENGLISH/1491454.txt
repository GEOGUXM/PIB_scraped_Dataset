      Vice President condemns the terror attack in Kabul
      
      
      Says perpetrators and those who support and shelter them should be punished

      Posted On: 31 MAY 2017 6:43PM by PIB Delhi
      
      The Vice President of India, Shri M. Hamid Ansari has condemned the terror attack in Kabul. In a message, he said that such wanton acts of violence can have no justification and the perpetrators and those who support and shelter them should be punished for this crime against humanity. The people of India stand as one with their sisters and brothers in Afghanistan in this difficult hour, he added.
      
      Following is the text of Vice President’s message:
      
      “I am shocked to learn of the terrorist attack in Kabul which has resulted in loss of lives and injuries to innocent civilians.
      
      Such wanton acts of violence can have no justification. The perpetrators and those who support and shelter them should be punished for this crime against humanity.
      
      India remains committed to building a peaceful, secure and prosperous Afghanistan. The people of India stand as one with their sisters and brothers in Afghanistan in this difficult hour.
      
      I offer my sincere condolences to the families of the bereaved. I join the nation in praying for their eternal rest and for the early recovery of the injured.”
