      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Shri Rajnath Singh condoles the passing away of Shri Anil Madhav Dave
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2017 10:55AM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Home Minister Shri Rajnath Singh has condoled the passing away of the Union Environment Minister, Shri Anil Madhav Dave.


    Shri Rajnath Singh said that it is extremely shocking and saddening to learn of the demise of our colleague and Union Environment Minister Shri Anil Madhav Dave. He said that Shri Dave was very active and sensitive to issues pertaining to environment. His dedication to work was highly appreciated.


    “I offer my heartfelt condolences to the members of Shri Anil Dave's family. May God give them strength to bear this huge loss”, the Union Home Minister said.
