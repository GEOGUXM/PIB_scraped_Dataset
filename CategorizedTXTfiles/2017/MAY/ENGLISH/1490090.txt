      Envoys of three nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2017 6:55PM by PIB Delhi


    

    Envoys of Costa Rica, Thailand and Spain presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee at a ceremony held in Rashtrapati Bhavan today (May 17, 2017).

    
    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -
    
    1. Her Excellency Ms. Mariela Cruz Alvarez, Ambassador of the Republic of Costa Rica
    
    2. His Excellency Mr. Chutintorn Gongsakdi, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Thailand
    
    3. His Excellency Mr. Jose Baranano, Ambassador of Spain
