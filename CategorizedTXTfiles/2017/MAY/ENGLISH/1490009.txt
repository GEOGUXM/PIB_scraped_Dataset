      Ministry of Urban Development

      Punjab CM meets UD Minister Shri Venkaiah Naidu
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2017 11:53AM by PIB Delhi


      Punjab Chief Minister Shri Amarinder Singh today met Minister for Urban Development, Housing & Urban Poverty Alleviation and Information & Broadcasting Shri M Venkaiah Naidu and discussed various aspects of implementation of new urban sector schemes in the State.


      Stressing on the need for timely execution of new schemes launched for development of basic urban infrastructure, affordable housing, ensuring cleanliness, public transportation etc., Shri Venkaiah Naidu informed Punjab CM about the recent initiative of conducting review meetings in state capitals across the country for first hand report on implementation issues and progress under new missions.


      Senior officials from the two Urban Ministries of Government of India and State Government participated in the discussions.


      Shri Naidu mentioned that, under the leadership of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, project appraisal and decision making is being delegated to the States in the true spirit of cooperative federalism so that project approvals and execution is expedited.


      Shri Naidu informed Punjab Chief Minister that a spirit of healthy competition is being promoted among cities to improve urban planning and execution for better service delivery.
