      President of India presents Presidential Awards for Classical Tamil
      Posted On: 09 MAY 2017 3:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee presented the ‘Presidential Awards for Classical Tamil’ for the year 2013-14, 2014-15 and 2015-16 today (May 9, 2017) at Rashtrapati Bhavan. The Presidential Awards for Classical Tamil were instituted to give recognition and honour to distinguished scholars who have made outstanding contribution to Classical Tamil language and literature.
