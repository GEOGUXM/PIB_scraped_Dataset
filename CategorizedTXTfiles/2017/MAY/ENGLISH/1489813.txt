      President of India to Deliver First Bhairon Singh Shekhawat Memorial Lecture Tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 MAY 2017 8:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Rajasthan (Jaipur) on May 15, 2017 to deliver the 1st Bhairon Singh Shekhawat Memorial Lecture and present the 1st Bhairon Singh Shekhawat Life Time Achievement Honour in Public Service to Shri Pawan Kumar Chamling, Chief Minister of Sikkim.
