      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people on the start of holy month of Ramzan
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2017 12:32PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the start of holy month of Ramzan.
    
    “Greetings on the start of Ramzan. May this holy month enhance the spirit of togetherness, peace and harmony across the world”, the Prime Minister said.
