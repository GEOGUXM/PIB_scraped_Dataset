      President of India pays homage to Neelam Sanjiva Reddy on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 19 MAY 2017 1:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee paid homage to Shri Neelam Sanjiva Reddy, former President of India on the occasion of his Birth Anniversary at Raj Bhavan, Kolkata today (May 19, 2017). He paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Shri Neelam Sanjiva Reddy at Raj Bhavan.
