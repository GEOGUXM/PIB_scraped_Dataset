      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Release of monthly Wholesale Price Index (WPI) for April 2017 under new series of WPI (Base 2011-12)
      Posted On: 08 MAY 2017 3:44PM by PIB Delhi


    The monthly wholesale Price Index for April 2017 under new series of WPI (Base 2011-12) will be released on 12th May 2017 instead of 15th May 2017 after Joint Press briefing scheduled for 12th May 2017 at 5.00 P.M at National Media Centre for the revision of base year of all-India Index of Industrial Production (IIP) and Wholesale Price Index (WPI) from 2004-05 to 2011-12.
