      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 48.18* per bbl on 10.05.2017
      Posted On: 11 MAY 2017 10:38AM by PIB Delhi


    The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 48.18* per barrel (bbl) on 10.05.2017. This was higher than the price of US$ 48.14 per bbl on previous publishing day of 08.05.2017*.


    In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs. 3108.10 per bbl on 10.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 3091.17 per bbl on 08.05.2017. Rupee closed weaker at Rs . 64.51 p er US$ on 10.05.2017 as compared to Rs. 64.22 per US$ on 08.05.2017** .
