      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of Justice Leila Seth
      Posted On: 06 MAY 2017 6:12PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Justice Leila Seth.
    
    "Saddened by the passing away of Justice Leila Seth. Her remarkable contribution to the legal field will be remembered. My condolences", the Prime Minister said.
