      TRAINS DIVERTED
      Posted On: 29 MAY 2017 3:00PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    In connection with commissioning of double line between Manapparai-Kalpattichatram stations of Thiruchirappally-Dindugal section under Madurai division , Train No. 16128, Guruvayur- Chennai Egmore leaving Guruvayur on 30th May, to 06th June 2017, Train No. 16127, Chennai Egmore-Guruvayur Express on 07th June and Train No. 16352, Nagercoil- Mumbai CST express on 01st and 04th June will be diverted via Dindugal- Karur- Thiruchirappally route.
