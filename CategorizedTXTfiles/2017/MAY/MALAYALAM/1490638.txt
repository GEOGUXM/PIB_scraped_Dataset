      Sree Chitra Medical Institute Annual Convocation: Union Health Minister to be Chief Guest.
      Posted On: 12 MAY 2017 4:46PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Science and Technology (SCTISMT will hold the annual convocation of its 33 rd batch on 15 th May, 2017 at the Achutha Menon Centre auditorium at 11 am. 122 senior residents and students who graduate during the year 2016, after completing PDF, DM, MCh, PDCC, PhD, MPH, Mphil and MS programs, will receive their degrees on this occasion.


    Shri. Jagat Prakash Nadda, Union Minister of health and Family Welfare who is the Chief Guest will deliver the convocation address and confer the degrees. The President of the Institute and former Cabinet Secretary, Shri. K.M. Chandrasekhar, will preside over the function. Dr. Anil Kakodkar, former Chairman, Atomic Energy Commission is the guest of honor. Prof. Asha Kishore, Director of the Institute will present the report of the academic activities of the Institute.
