    The Term End Examinations for June 2017 of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) will be held from 1st June to 21st June 2017 across the country. A total of 6441 students are appearing the examinations at nine examination centres in Thiruvananthapuram, Kollam, Pathanamthitta, Kanyakumari, Thirunelveli and Tuticorin districts under the IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum .The hall ticket is available on IGNOU website www.ignou.ac.in . Students must possess valid identity card issued by the university during the examination.


    For more details contact IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum Rajadhani Complex, Killippalam, Karaman.P.O., Thiruvananthapuram- 695002.
