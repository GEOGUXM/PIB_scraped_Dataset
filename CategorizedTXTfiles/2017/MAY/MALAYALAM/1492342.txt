      National Homeopathic Institute to be inaugurated tomorrow by Minister of Ayush
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2017 1:57PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Union Minister of State for Ayush (Independent) Shri. Shripad Naik will inaugurate the National Homeopathic Research Institute in Mental Health at Kurichi, Kottayam tomorrow. State Health Minister Smt. K.K. Shylaja will be the Chief Guest of the function to be presided over by Sri. Kodikunnil Suresh MP.


    With attaining national status, MD, Phd programmes will commence in the Institute, after getting University recognition. Scientific treatment is also available here for learning disabilities, autism, and other mental illness.


    The inauguration of Upgraded National Ayurveda Institute for Panchakarma will be done by Union Minister of State for Ayush Shri. Shripad Naik at Cheruthuruthy near Shoranur tomorrow morning.
