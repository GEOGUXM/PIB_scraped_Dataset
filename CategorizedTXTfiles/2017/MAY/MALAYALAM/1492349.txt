      CANCELLATION OF PASSENGER SPECIALS
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2017 2:16PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 06733, Angamali-Ernakulam passenger special train at 09.25 hrs and Train No. 06736, Ernakulam Jn-Angamali passenger special train at 15.10 hrs are cancelled temporarily with effect from 26th May 2017 due to poor patronage and to provide more time gap for safety related work.
