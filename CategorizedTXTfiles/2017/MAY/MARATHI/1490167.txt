      Ministry of Home Affairs

      National Flag will fly half-mast today
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2017 3:47PM by PIB Mumbai

      The Government of India today announced with profound sorrow the death of Shri Anil Madhav Dave, Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Environment, Forest and Climate Change.
      As a mark of respect to the departed dignitary, the Centre has decided that the National Flag will fly half-mast today in Delhi and all State/UT Capitals on all the buildings where it is flown regularly and on the day of the funeral where the funeral takes place.
