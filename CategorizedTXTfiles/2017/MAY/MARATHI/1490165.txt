      Ministry of Civil Aviation

      Performance of Domestic Airlines for the year 2017
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2017 3:46PM by PIB Mumbai


    Traffic data submitted by various domestic airlines has been analysed for the month of April, 2017. Following are the salient features:


    Passengers carried by domestic airlines during January-April, 2017 were 364.13 lakhs as against 309.35 lakhs during the corresponding period of previous year thereby registering a growth of 17.71%.
