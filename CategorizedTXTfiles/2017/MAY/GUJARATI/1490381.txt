      DAK ADALAT FOR SETTLEMENT OF COMPLAINTS AND PROBLEMS ON POSTAL SERVICES
      Posted On: 22 MAY 2017 5:28PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 22-05-2017


    Dak Adalat for settlement of complaints and problems on Postal Services will be held at 11.00 hours and onwards on 07-06-52017 at the office of Senior Superintendent of Post Offices, City DIvision, Ahmedabad, Nr. All India Radio, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad – 380 009. The SSPOs, Ahmedabad City Division will hear each complaint individually,


    The concept of the Dak Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settlement of problems of public members on the spot. The complaints related to Postal Services in brief should be sent to Shri Shahikumar Uttam, Senior Superintendent of Post Offices City Division, Ahmedabad, Nr. All India Radio, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad – 380 009. The complaint should reach to the above office latest by 05-06-2017. The complaint received after the due date and related to policy matter will not be entertained. The complaints should be concrete and in brief & should not be general natured.


    AP/J.Khunt/GP
