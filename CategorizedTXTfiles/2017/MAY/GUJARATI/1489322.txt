      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the accident in Etah,UP
      Announces ex gratia of Rs. 2 lakhs from PMNRF
      Posted On: 05 MAY 2017 4:58PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the death of persons who died in an accident in Etah district of Uttar Pradesh.
    
    The Prime Minister has also announced ex gratia of Rs. 2 Lakhs from PMNRF for the next of kin of those killed in the accident and Rs. 50,000 for those seriously injured.
    
    "My thoughts are with all those who lost their loved ones in the accident in Etah, UP. May those injured in the accident recover quickly", the Prime Minister said.
