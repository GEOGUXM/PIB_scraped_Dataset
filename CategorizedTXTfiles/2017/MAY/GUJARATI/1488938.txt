      President of India to attend the 8TH convocation of the Lovely Professional University tomorrow
      Posted On: 01 MAY 2017 5:51PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will visit Punjab (Phagwara) tomorrow (May 2, 2017) where he will attend the 8th Convocation of the Lovely Professional University.
