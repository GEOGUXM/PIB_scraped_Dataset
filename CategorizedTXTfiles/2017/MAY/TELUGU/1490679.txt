      BSNL ORGANISES MEGA MELA ON 25th to 27th MAY 2017
      Posted On: 24 MAY 2017 6:56PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited ( BSNL ) is conducting Mega Mela on 25-05-2017 to 27-05-2017 in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. During the Mela Customers can get Free 3G SMART SIMs in all Customer Service Centers, Franchaisees/Retail outlets and in Road shows. Customers can get 310MB Data free with all new connections. They can book Land Line, Broadbabd and FTTH connections also at melas conducted by BSNL Staff. Customers are requested to avail this opportunity. For further details dial 1503 or 18001801503
