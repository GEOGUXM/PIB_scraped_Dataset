      PRICE REDUCTION ON COST OF SELLING/REPLACEMENT SIMS IN BSNL
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2017 7:02PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited ( BSNL ) is happy to announce the reduction of selling/replacement price of all types of SIMs (Normal+Micro+Nano) from existing ₹20/- to ₹15/- under Pre-paid and Post-paid Mobile Services w.e.f. 26-05-2017 both in AP and Telangana Circles. Customers are requested to avail this opportunity. For further details dial 1503 or 18001801503
