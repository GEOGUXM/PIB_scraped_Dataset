      PRIME MINISTER MANN KI BAAT BROADCAST ON ALL INDIA RADIO ON MAY 28TH, 2017
      Posted On: 26 MAY 2017 7:07PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The Hon’ble Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi will address the nation through “Mann ki Baat” on May 28 th , 2017 (Sunday) at 11.00 a.m. through the entire All India Radio network encompassing medium wave, shortwave, FM, Vividhbharati and LRS and will be relayed by all AIR stations of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.


    Telugu version of the Mann Ki Baat ( మనసులో మాట ) will be broadcast immediately after the PM’s speech and repeat broadcast of Telugu version will also be made at 8.00 p.m. from the stations of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh states on May 28 th , 2017 informed V. Udayashankar, Programme Head- All India Radio, Hyderabad.
