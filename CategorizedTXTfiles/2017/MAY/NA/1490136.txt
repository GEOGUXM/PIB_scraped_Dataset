      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      Shri Ananthkumar condoles the passing away of Shri Anil Madhav Dave
      Posted On: 18 MAY 2017 11:16AM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister for Chemicals & Fertilizers and Parliamentary Affairs, Shri Anathkumar has condoled the passing away of Environment Minister, Shri Anil Madhav Dave.


    “Deeply saddened by the demise of my friend & colleague Shri Anil Madhav Dave ji. My deep condolences to his family members.”


    Shri Kumar said that Shri Dave was very focussed and deeply concerned towards issues pertaining to environment. His dedication to work is a source of inspiration for all of us.
