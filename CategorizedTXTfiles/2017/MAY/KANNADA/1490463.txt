      PM congratulates President Hassan Rouhani on his re-election

      The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated the President of Iran Hassan Rouhani on his re-election.


      "Heartiest congratulations to my friend, President Hassan Rouhani on his re-election.


      Iran will continue to achieve new heights under the dynamic leadership of President Hassan Rouhani .


      India remains committed to strengthening our special relationship with Iran." , the Prime Minister said.
