      PM condoles the demise of Shri KPS Gill
      Posted On: 25 MAY 2017 6:20PM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM condoles the demise of Shri KPS Gill


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Shri KPS Gill.
    
    "KPS Gill will be remembered for his service to our nation in the fields of policing and security. Pained by his demise. My condolences", the Prime Minister said.
