      PM condemns the terrorist blast in Kabul
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2017 12:56PM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM condemns the terrorist blast in Kabul


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has strongly condemned the terrorist blast in Kabul.
    
    “We strongly condemn the terrorist blast in Kabul. Our thoughts are with the families of the deceased and prayers with the injured.
    
    India stands with Afghanistan in fighting all types of terrorism. Forces supporting terrorism need to be defeated,” the Prime Minister said.
