      PM salutes Sardar Patel on his Jayanti
      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted Sardar Patel on his Jayanti.

      Posted On: 31 OCT 2017 10:47AM by PIB Kolkata

    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted Sardar Patel on his Jayanti.
    
    "We salute Sardar Patel on his Jayanti. His momentous service and monumental contribution to India can never be forgotten", the Prime Minister said.
