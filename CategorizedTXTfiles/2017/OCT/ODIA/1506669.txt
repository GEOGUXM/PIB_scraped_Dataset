      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people on the occasion of 'Bhai Dooj'
      Posted On: 21 OCT 2017 5:40PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of 'Bhai Dooj'.
    
    "भाई दूज के पावन अवसर पर सभी देशवासियों को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं।", the Prime Minister said.
