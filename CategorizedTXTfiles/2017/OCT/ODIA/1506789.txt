      PM congratulates Indian Hockey Team for winning the Asia Cup 2017
      Posted On: 22 OCT 2017 8:52PM by PIB Bhubaneshwar


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Indian Hockey Team for winning the Asia Cup 2017.
    
    "Great game, great win! Congrats to our hockey team for winning the Asia Cup 2017. India rejoices at this stupendous victory", the Prime Minister said.
