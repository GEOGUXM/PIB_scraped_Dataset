      BSNL Open House on Saturdayat Varkala
      Posted On: 19 OCT 2017 7:55PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    BSNL Thiruvananthapuram business area will conduct open house session for the customers on Saturday 21 st October 2017from 11.00 am to 01.00 pm at Telephone Exchange, Varkala.
