      Paryatan Parv : Walkathon Held at Kovalam
      Posted On: 06 OCT 2017 1:50PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    As part of the “PARYATAN PARV” celebrations launched by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, the Institute of Hotel Management And Catering Technology,(IHMCT) at Kovalam, Thiruvananthapuram oraganised a Walkathon from IHMCT campus to Kovalam Junction on Thursday (05.10.2017). Mr.L.V.Kumar, Principal, IHMCT inaugurated the Walkathon. Sri. S.Ramakrishnan, Assistant Director, India Tourism, Chennai, Ministry of Tourism, Head Of Departments, Faculty / Staff Members and students of the Institute actively participated in the “Walkathon”.


    The “PARYATAN PARV” celebrations will run from 5th to 25th October, 2017 across several cities and iconic tourism destinations in the country. Throughout these 20 days, the ‘Paryatan Parv’ will give an opportunity for the citizens of the country to explore the cultural heritage and enjoy the diverse cuisine, cultural programmes, music, theatre, etc.


    As part of the celebrations, there will be a Manipuri Dance performance by Sanskriti Sangam in Bekal Fort, Kasargod on the 25th of this month. Illumination and Guided walks around the fort, quiz, essay, painting competitions for students will also be held on the occasion.
