      Run for Unity conducted in SAI Lakshmibai National College of Physical Education
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2017 7:43PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    SAI Lakshmibai National College of Physical Education (LNCPE) organized Run for Unity at Karyavattam as part of the celebration in connection with National Unity Day (RastriyaEktaDiwas). The run was flagged off by Shri. A Srinivas, DIG, CRPF, Pallipuram. The Unity Run started from the Greenfield stadium, Kariyavattom and terminated in the Athletic Stadium, SAI LNCPE. More than thousand persons comprising cyclists, trainees, athletes, employees, coaches and students from neighboring schools, CRPF & Army personals from Pallipuram and Pangode Military camp participated in the mini marathon.
