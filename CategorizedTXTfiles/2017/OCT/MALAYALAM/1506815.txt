      TELECOM ADALAT
      Posted On: 23 OCT 2017 11:42AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The next sitting of the Telecom Adalat of Thiruvananthapuram Business Area is scheduled to be held during October - 2017.


    Customer complaints/grievances for which reply was not received or the reply received was unsatisfactory after exhausting all stages of redressals can only be represented to the Adalath. The exact date and time of Adalat will be intimated later.


    Complaints with full particulars and relevant documentary evidences may be sent to Assistant General Manager (OP), Olo PGMT, Uppalam Road, Thiruvananthapuram 695001 on or before 27/10/2017. The cover must be super scribed "Complaint for Telecom Adalath".
