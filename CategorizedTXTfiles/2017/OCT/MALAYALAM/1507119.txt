      Air India flights rescheduled on Aaratu day
      Posted On: 25 OCT 2017 7:38PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Aaratu procession of 'SreePadmanabhaSwamy Temple" is scheduled to be held on 28.10.2017 and the procession would pass through the operational area of Trivandrum Airport between 1600 and 2100 Hr(IST). Accordingly the following Air India flights have been rescheduled:


    On 28th October 2017, Saturday


    1. Flight AI 967 Thiruvananthapuram/Sharjah will depart from Thiruvananthapuram at 22.00 Hrs instead of 20.35 Hrs.


    2. Flight AI 264 from Thiruvananthapuram to Delhi will depart from Thiruvananthapuram at 22.00 Hrs instead of 19.15 Hrs.


    3. Flight Al 668 from Thiruvananthapuram to Mumbai will depart from Thiruvananthapuram at 22.00 Hrs instead of 20.15 Hrs


    Passengers may contact Air India Toll Free number 1800 180 1407 or 0471 2310310/2316020/2316023/2500008 for any enquiries.
