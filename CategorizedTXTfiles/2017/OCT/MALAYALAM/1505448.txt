      SAINIK SCHOOL KAZHAKOOTAM BAGS AWARD FOR BEST SCHOOL MAGAZINE (LAYOUT AND DESIGN)
      Posted On: 03 OCT 2017 12:07PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    
    The school journal of Sainik School Kazhakootam, ‘The Cadet’, has bagged one of the most coveted awards - Trophy for the BEST SCHOOL MAGAZINE in layout and design. Principal Col. A Rajiv received the award from Shri Subhash Bhamre, Hon’ble Minister of State for Defence at the All India Principals’ Conference held at Sainik School Nalanda, on behalf of the school. Every year, journals of all the Sainik Schools are scrutinized by an expert panel prior to the Principals’ Conference. The school journal achieved similar accolades in the past years too.
