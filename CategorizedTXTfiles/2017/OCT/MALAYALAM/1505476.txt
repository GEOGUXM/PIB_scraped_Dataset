      Sree Chitra’s National blood donation week celebrations to conclude with ‘Blood march’
      Posted On: 06 OCT 2017 1:39PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) Blood Bank , Thiruvananthapuram is celebrating one year of successful 100 % voluntary blood donation. On the concluding day of the National Blood Donation week celebrations of the Institute, a "Blood March" and a formal function will be held at Museum Compound in Thiruvananthapuram tomorrow (October 7th) evening.


    Sree Chitra Institute Director Professor Asha Kishore will inaugurate the blood march at 4.30 PM. 200 repeat blood donors and donor organizers will participate in the march. The march will be followed by facilitation of blood donors and organizers. The function will be presided over by Dr. C Sarada, Medical Superintendent, SCTIMST. Dr Divya S Iyer IAS, Sub Collector, Thiruvananthapuram will deliver the key note address.
