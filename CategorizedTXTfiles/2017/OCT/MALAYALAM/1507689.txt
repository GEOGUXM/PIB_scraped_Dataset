      GraminDak Sevaks: online application restarted
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2017 7:39PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Chief Post Master General, Kerala circle has informed that the online submission of application for all categories of GraminDakSevaks in Kerala Postal Circle has started on 30-10-2017. The last date of online application is 29-11-2017. For submission of application and other details please visit the site as under. https://indiapost.gov.in, https://appost.in/gdsonline
