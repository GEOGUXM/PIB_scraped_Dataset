      Ministry of Earth Science

      Severe Weather Warning – 09th to 13th October, 2017
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2017 5:43PM by PIB Delhi


    India Meteorological Department has issued severe weather warning for 09-13 October, 2017 as below:


    09 October (Day 1):♦ Heavy to very heavy rain at a few places with isolated extremely heavy falls very likely over Gangetic West Bengal and heavy to very heavy rain at isolated places over Jharkhand and Odisha; heavyat isolated places over Assam & Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura, Madhya Maharashtra, Marathawada, Konkan & Goa, Telangana, Rayalaseema, North Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu & Puducherry.

      * Squally wind speed reaching upto 45­55 kmph gusting to 65 kmph very likely along & off West Bengal and north Odisha Strong wind 30­40 kmph gusting to 50 kmph also very likely over Gangetic West Bengal, Jharkhand and north Odisha.
      * Fishermen are advised not to venture along & off West Bengal and north Odisha coasts.
      * Thunderstorm accompanied with lightning very likely at isolated places over Madhya Pradesh, Vidarbha, Chhattisgarh, Bihar, Jharkhand and Odisha, Madhya Maharashtra, Marathawada, Konkan & Goa, Coastal Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Rayalaseema, Coastal Karnataka, South Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu & Puducherry. Thunderstorm accompanied with Gusty winds and lightning very likely at isolated places over Assam & Meghalaya and Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura

    10 October (Day 2):♦ Heavy to very heavy rain very likely at isolated places over Gangetic West Bengal andheavy over Chhattisgarh, Bihar, Assam & Meghalaya, Madhya Maharashtra, Konkan & Goa, Telangana, Rayalaseema and North Interior Karnataka.

      * Strong wind speed reaching 30­40 kmph gusting to 50 kmph very likely over Gangetic West Bengal, east Jharkhand and north Odisha.
      * Thunderstorm accompanied with lightning very likely at isolated places over Madhya Pradesh, Vidarbha, Chhattisgarh, Bihar, Madhya Maharashtra, Marathawada, Coastal Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Rayalaseema, Coastal Karnataka, Interior Karnataka and Tamilnadu & Puducherry.
      * Fishermen are advised not to venture along & off West Bengal and north Odisha coasts.

    11 October (Day 3):♦ Heavy rain very likely at isolated places over Bihar, Assam & Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura and Konkan & Goa.


    12 October (Day 4):♦ Heavy rain very likely at isolated places over Assam & Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura and Konkan & Goa..


    13 October(Day 5): Heavy rain very likely at isolated places over Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Meghalaya and Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura.
