      PM pays tributes to former Prime Minister Mrs. Indira Gandhi, on her death anniversary
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2017 7:08AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister Mrs. Indira Gandhi, on her death anniversary.
    
    "Tributes to former PM Mrs. Indira Gandhi on her death anniversary", the Prime Minister said.
