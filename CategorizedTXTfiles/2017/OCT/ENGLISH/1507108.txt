      Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation

      National projects construction corporation limited pays dividend of Rs. 2.05 Crore
      Posted On: 25 OCT 2017 5:30PM by PIB Delhi


      National Projects Construction Corporation Limited (NPCC),a PSU of the Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation has paid a dividend of Rs.2.05 cr. to the Union Government. Shri Nitin Gadkari, Union Minister for Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation received the dividend cheque for the financial year 2016-17 from Shri Sanjay Kundu, CMD, NPCC in New Delhi yesterday.


      The CMD, NPCC also presented cheque of Rs. 42.33 Lakh on account of dividend tax and cheque of Rs. one cr. towards payment of interest on a loan taken from the Ministry.


      Union Minister of State for Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal and Secretary of the Ministry Dr. Amarjit Singh were also present on the occasion.
