      President of India To Visit Kerala Tomorrow
      Posted On: 07 OCT 2017 6:35PM by PIB Delhi


    

    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Kerala (Amritapuri, Kollam) tomorrow (October 8, 2017) to attend the launch of welfare progammes organised by the Mata Amritanandamayi Math which include unveiling of Amrita clean water initiatives for rural India and distribution of certificates of official ODF status to 10 villages adopted by Mata Amritanandamayi Math.
