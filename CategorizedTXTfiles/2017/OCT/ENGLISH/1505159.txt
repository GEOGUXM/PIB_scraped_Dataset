      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles the passing away of Shri Kundan Shah
      Posted On: 07 OCT 2017 3:06PM by PIB Delhi


    
    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Shri Kundan Shah.
    
    “Anguished by the passing away of Shri Kundan Shah. He will be remembered for his wonderful usage of humour and satire to showcase the life and struggles of common citizens. My thoughts are with his family and admirers. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
