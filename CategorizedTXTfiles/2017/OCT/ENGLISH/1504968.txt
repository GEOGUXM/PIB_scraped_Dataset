      Public display of Ceremonial Change of Guard will also take place on Sundays in the evening from October 8, 2017
      Posted On: 05 OCT 2017 5:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The public display of the Ceremonial Change of Guard at Rashtrapati Bhavan will now also take place on Sundays w.e.f. October 8, 2017. While the ceremony will continue to be held at its scheduled time in the morning on Saturdays, it will take place on Sundays in the evening at 16:30 hrs. In case Saturday or Sunday is a Gazetted holiday and Rashtrapati Bhavan is closed for visitors, the Change of Guard Ceremony will not be carried out on those days.
