      Election Commission

      Bye Election to the Council of States from Rajasthan.
      Posted On: 24 OCT 2017 7:18PM by PIB Delhi

      

        There is casual vacancy in the Council of States from Rajasthan due to resignation of sitting member. The details are given as below:

                                                                                       
S.No  State      Name of Member         Cause of vacancy           Term of office up to
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
                                                                                       
1.    Rajasthan  Sh. M. Venkaiah Naidu  Resignation on 10.08.2017  04.07.2022          
                                                                                       
                                                                                       

        The Commission has decided to hold a bye-election to the Council of States from Rajasthan to fill up the above said vacancy in accordance with the following programmme: -
