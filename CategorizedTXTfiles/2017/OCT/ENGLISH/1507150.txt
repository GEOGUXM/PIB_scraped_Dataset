      President of India to visit Kerala on October 27 & 28, 2017
      Posted On: 26 OCT 2017 5:21PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Kerala on October 27 and 28, 2017.


    On October 27, 2017, the President will launch the Technocity project and lay the foundation stone for the first government building in Technocity in Thiruvananthapuram. Later in the day, he will pay tributes to social reformer Ayyankali by garlanding his statue in Thiruvananthapuram and also attend a civic reception hosted in his honour.


    On October 28, 2017, the President will travel to Kochi to grace the valedictory function of the diamond jubilee celebrations of High Court of Kerala.
