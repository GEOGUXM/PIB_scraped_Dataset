      Ministry of Tourism

      Shri K.J. Apphons Visits Injured Swiss Tourist Couple Quentin Jeremy Clerc and Marie Droz at Apollo Hospital, New Delhi
      Posted On: 28 OCT 2017 6:11PM by PIB Delhi

      Shri K. J. Alphons, Minister of State (Independent charge) for Tourism visited the injured Swiss tourist couple Quentin Jeremy Clerc and Marie Droz at Apollo Hospital, New Delhi today.

        The Minister also had discussion with the management and doctors regarding the treatment.


        Both the patients are fast recovering and are very happy about the treatment that they are getting at the hospital.
