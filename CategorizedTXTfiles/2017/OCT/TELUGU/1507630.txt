      VIGILANCE AWARENESS WEEK 2017 IS BEING OBSERVEDFROM 30TH OCTOBER – 4TH NOVEMBER 2017 AT NMDC
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2017 6:34PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Vigilance Awareness Week 2017 is being observed from 30 th October – 4 th November 2017 at NMDC Limited as per the guidelines of Central Vigilance Commission (CVC), Government of India.


    At NMDC Corporate Office, on the Inaugural Day today Dr. Narendra K. Nanda, Director (Technical) administered the Integrity Pledge to all the employees wherein all the Directors and the employees have taken the Pledge.


    An Elocution Competition for the employees was organized on the topic “My Vision – Corruption Free India”, wherein participants in large number participated.
