      SPECIAL PASSPORT ADALAT ON 01 – 02 NOVEMBER, 2017
      Posted On: 27 OCT 2017 6:27PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Regional Passport Office, Hyderabad will be conducting a special Passport Adalat on 01 – 02 November, 2017. All Passport applicants with grievances and who have applied before 31 st July 2017 are requested to avail the opportunity and get their pending applications cleared. Applicants should visit RPO Hyderabad main office (near clock tower, Kummarguda, Secunderabad) from 9.30 AM to 12.30 PM on 01-02 November 2017, with all supporting/original documents.


    The Passport Adalat is being organised as part of the vigilance awareness week (30 th October to 4 th November) to facilitate grievance redressal and promote transperancy in Passport service delivery, said that Dr. E. Vishnu Vardhan Reddy, IFS, Regional Passport Officer.
