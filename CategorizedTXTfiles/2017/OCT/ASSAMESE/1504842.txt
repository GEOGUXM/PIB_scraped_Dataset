      PM to address the golden jubilee celebrations of Institute of Company Secretaries of India(ICSI)
      Posted On: 04 OCT 2017 11:13PM by PIB Guwahati


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will address the company secretaries from all over India at a programme to mark start of the golden jubilee year of Institute of Company Secretaries of India(ICSI) at 6PM, today.
