      President of India pays floral tributes to Shri KR Narayanan on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 27 OCT 2017 11:46AM by PIB Guwahati


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid floral tributes to Shri K.R. Narayanan, former President of India, on the occasion of his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (October 27, 2017).


    The President, officers and staff paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Shri K.R. Narayanan in the Durbar Hall of Rashtrapati Bhavan.
