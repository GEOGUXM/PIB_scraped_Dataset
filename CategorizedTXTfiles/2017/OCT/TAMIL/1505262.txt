      DDK invites application for the post of Assistant News Editors and reporters
      Posted On: 09 OCT 2017 11:59AM by PIB Chennai


    Regional News Unit, Doordarshan Kendra, Chennai has initiated the process of empanelment of Assistant News Editors and News Reporters on assignment basis.


    Any candidate between 25 to 50 years, possessing a degree in Journalism or a degree with diploma in Journalism from a recognised university can apply. Candidate must have minimum 3 years of experience relating to news broadcasting /news organisation. The last date for receipt of the filled in applications is 20.10.2017. Applications can be downloaded from www.ddpodhigai.org.in under the page titled ‘INFORMATION’.
