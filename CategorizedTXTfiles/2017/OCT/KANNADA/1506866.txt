      PM congratulates his Japanese counterpart Shinzo Abe on re-election
      Posted On: 23 OCT 2017 10:48AM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM congratulates his Japanese counterpart Shinzo Abe on re-election


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated his Japanese counterpart Shinzo Abe, on victory in the snap polls held on Sunday.
    
    "Heartiest greetings to my dear friend Shinzo Abe on his big election win. Look forward to further strengthen India-Japan relations with him", the Prime Minister said.
