      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people on Baisakhi
      Posted On: 13 APR 2017 11:46AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on Baisakhi.
    
    “बैसाखी की सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं।," the Prime Minister said.
