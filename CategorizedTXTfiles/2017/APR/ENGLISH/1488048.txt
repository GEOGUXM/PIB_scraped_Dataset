      PM pays tributes to Dr. B. R. Ambedkar on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 14 APR 2017 9:41AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on Ambedkar Jayanti.


    “Tributes to venerable Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on Ambedkar Jayanti. Jai Bhim. अम्बेडकर जयंती के पावन अवसर पर पूज्य बाबासाहेब को नमन।”, the Prime Minister said.
