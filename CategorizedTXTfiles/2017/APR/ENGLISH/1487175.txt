      Ministry of Finance

      Capital Investments in Bank by GOI
      Posted On: 07 APR 2017 5:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government infused a sum of Rs. 25000 crore in 19 PSBs during financial year 2015-16 and Rs. 24997.182 crore into 16 PSBs during the FY 2016-17.The capital is calculated/ provided keeping in view overall macroeconomic scenario, credit estimation, basel requirement and Reserve Bank of India recommendations if any. The Bank-wise details of capital infused into 16 PSBs during FY 2016-17 by Government of India are as under:
