      Ministry of Corporate Affairs

      Use of CSR Fund on Health and Education
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 6:02PM by PIB Delhi


        The CSR expenditure of companies is compiled development sector-wise as listed in Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013. The development sector-wise CSR spend of the companies on education and health for the years 2014-15 and 2015-16 is given in Table below. The Companies Act, 2013 already provides an institutional framework for the use of CSR funds by companies.
