      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Books released on Civil Services Day function
      Posted On: 21 APR 2017 5:56PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi released two books namely: “New Beginning” on Innovations for the Prime Minister’s Award for Excellence and “Fostering Excellence” for initiatives under identified Priority Programmes here today.


    Soft copies of the books are available on the DARPG website with the following link:
