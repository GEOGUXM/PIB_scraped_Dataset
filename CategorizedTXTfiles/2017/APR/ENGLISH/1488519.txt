      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC approves one year service extension to Cabinet Secretary Shri PK Sinha
      Posted On: 24 APR 2017 8:29PM by PIB Delhi


    

    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved extension in service to Shri Pradeep Kumar Sinha, IAS (UP:1977) as Cabinet Secretary for a further period of one year beyond 12.06.2017.
