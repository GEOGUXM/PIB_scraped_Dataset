      PM expresses grief over loss of lives in fire at Chhindwara in MP
      Posted On: 21 APR 2017 9:32PM by PIB Delhi

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressed grief and shock over the loss of lives in a fire breakout at Chhindwara district of Madhya Pradesh.

      “My thoughts are with those who lost their loved ones in the fire at Chhindwara. The incident is shocking beyond words.


      I pray that those who are injured due to the fire in Chhindwara recover quickly. State Government is closely monitoring the situation”, the Prime Minister said.
