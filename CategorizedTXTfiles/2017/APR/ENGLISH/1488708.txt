      Ministry of Tourism

      Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada of M/O Tourism Today
      Posted On: 26 APR 2017 8:04PM by PIB Delhi


        The Eleventh day’s Swachhta Pakhwada work of Ministry of Tourism was undertaken at Tees January Marg Gandhi Memorial, New Delhi. As per schedule, Cleaning of Gandhi Smriti and Darshan Samiti area, officers and staff from Ministry of Tourism involved in intensive cleaning work, from 03:00 P.M to 05:00 P.M., which resulted in achieving the desired level of cleanliness. A team from Ministry of I&B’s Song & Drama Division, also created awareness near exit gate of Gandhi Smriti on cleanliness in the form of “Nukkad Natak”.
