      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Sale of Cars
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 6:05PM by PIB Delhi


        Society of Automobiles Manufacturers (SIAM) has shared the detail on domestic sales of passenger vehicles during the last two years and the current years as under:

                                                                         
Category                         2014-15    2015-16    2016-17(April-Feb)
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
Passenger Cars                   18,77,706  20,25,479  19,12,931         
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
Utility Vehicles (MUVs) / SUVs)  5,52,135   5,86,664   6,84,306          
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
Vans                             1,71,395   1,77,535   1,66,969          
                                                                         
                                                                         
                                                                         
Total Passenger Vehicles         26,01,236  27,89,678  27,64,206         
                                                                         
                                                                         

        SIAM has reported that the inflation rates have come down in the last two years and fuel prices have also not changed substantially in the recent past. Hence, no major impact on sales is seen due to these factors.


        This information was given by Minister of State in the Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Shri Babul Supriyo in reply to a written question in the Lok Sabha today.
