      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      FAME India Scheme
      Posted On: 12 APR 2017 5:10PM by PIB Delhi


    The Phase-1 of the FAME India scheme, limited to major cities, has been extended further for a period of 6 months beyond 31st March 2017 till 30th September 2017, with slight modification in the scheme to the extent of exclusion of benefits available for Mild Hybrid Technology under the scheme.


    Based on the outcome and experience gained in Phase-1, the scheme shall be reviewed appropriately with inputs from stakeholders for implementation post Phase-1 period.


    This information was given by Minister of State in the Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Shri Babul Supriyo in reply to a written question in the Rajya Sabha today.
