      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Progressive implementation of Hindi in the Ministry
      Posted On: 12 APR 2017 5:11PM by PIB Delhi


      Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises consists of two Departments namely Department of Heavy Industry (DHI) and Department of Public Enterprises (DPE). There are no CPSEs under the administrative control of DPE. So far as DHI is concerned, as per the Information available the status of progressive implementation of Hindi in the Ministry and its major enterprises/organisations during the last three years i.e. 2014, 2015 and 2016 and the current year is given in Annexure.


      This Ministry and its attached Enterprises are moving towards achieving the goal enumerated in Annual Programme issued by the Department of Official Language, Ministry of Home Affairs and directions issued by the Advisory Committee and Implementation Committee.


      Annexure


      T he status of progressive implementation of Hindi in the Ministry and its attached enterprises during last three years i.e. 2014, 2015 and 2016 and the current year 2017.
