      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Film screened on Civil Services Day function
      Posted On: 21 APR 2017 8:18PM by PIB Delhi

      

        ster's Office 21-April, 2017 13:52 IST


        The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi presented a total of 12 awards on the occasion of 11 th Civil Services Day here today. These awards included 10 awards under the five Priority Programmes and 2 awards under Innovation category.


        During the function, “Making New India-2017”, a film by Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG), Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions was screened. The film shows the initiatives of the PM award winning districts/organisations in the excellence in implementation of Priority Programmes and innovations.


        The film ‘Making New India-2017’ is available on the DARPG website with the following link:


        http://darpg.gov.in/sites/default/files/films/MAKING-NEW-INDIA-2017.html
