      PM greets the people of Odisha, on Utkala Dibasa
      Posted On: 01 APR 2017 8:02PM by PIB Delhi

      
      
      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Odisha, on Utkala Dibasa.
      
      "Utkala Dibasa greetings to the people of Odisha", the Prime Minister said.
