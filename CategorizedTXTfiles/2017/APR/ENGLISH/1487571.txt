      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Functioning of PSUS Without Head
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 6:07PM by PIB Delhi

      

        As per available information, there are 42 Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) which presently do not have a regular Managing Director/Chairman & Managing Director (CMD)/Chairman. The list of these 42 CPSEs is annexed.


        The filling up of Board level posts in CPSEs is a continuous process and these 42 vacant posts would get filled in due course after completion of necessary formalities.


        The Government has laid down guidelines for upgradation of Board level posts of CPSEs on a personal basis which provides for grant of higher schedule of pay to the concerned Board level incumbent.


        There are no cases which have been approved by the Appointments Committee of the Cabinet where Chairman/CMD/Directors of Schedule ‘C’ CPSEs are getting pay scales of Schedule ‘B’ CPSEs.
