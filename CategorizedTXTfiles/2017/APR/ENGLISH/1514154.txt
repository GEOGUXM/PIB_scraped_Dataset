      Prime Minister's Office

      PM announces ex gratia for the next of kin of those killed and seriously injured, in the bus accident in HP, from PMNRF
      Posted On: 19 APR 2017 6:28PM by PIB Delhi

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has announced ex gratia of Rs. 2 lakhs for the next of kin of those killed, and Rs. 50,000 for those seriously injured, in the bus accident near Shimla in Himachal Pradesh, from PMNRF.
