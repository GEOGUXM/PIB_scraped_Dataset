      President’s Greetings on The Eve of Mahavir Jayanti
      Posted On: 08 APR 2017 6:45PM by PIB Delhi


    

    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee in his message on the eve of Mahavir Jayanti has said: -

    
    “On the auspicious occasion of Mahavir Jayanti, I convey best wishes and greetings to all my fellow countrymen in India and abroad.
    
    Bhagwan Mahavira’s philosophy of ahimsa, truth, compassion and non-violence are of increasing relevance in today’s world.
    
    On this day, let us imbibe his teaching of right faith, right knowledge and right conduct. Let us unite and build a society free from violence, terrorism and exploitation.”
