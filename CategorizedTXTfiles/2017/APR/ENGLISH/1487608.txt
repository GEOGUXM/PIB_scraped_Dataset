      Ministry of Urban Development

      French MoS for Transport, Fisheries and Maritime Affairs Mr. Alain Vidalies calls on Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 8:08PM by PIB Delhi


    

    The French Minister of State for Transport, Fisheries and Maritime Affairs, Mr. Alain Vidalies called on the Union Minister for Urban Development, Housing & Urban Poverty Alleviation and Information & Broadcasting, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu here today.

    
    During the meeting, both sides discussed various issues including existing French financial assistance, technical collaboration and possible areas of further cooperation. The two sides discussed the status of Metro rail projects in India, such as already operational Metro rail/Mono rail, under construction Metro/MRTS Projects and metro projects under consideration. Smart Cities Mission and Swachh Bharat Mission were also part of the discussions.
    
    The Ministers also considered the possible areas of further cooperation between the two countries such as technical and investment assistance to Puducherry, Chandigarh & Nagpur for project development and implementation, support to Smart Cities in Maharashtra, assistance to National Mission programme and sending experts and professions from French Cities to build capacity of Smart Cities.
