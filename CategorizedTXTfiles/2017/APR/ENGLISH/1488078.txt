      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Global Crude oil price of Indian Basket was US$ 54.59* per bbl on 17.04.2017
      Posted On: 18 APR 2017 11:00AM by PIB Delhi

      

        The international crude oil price of Indian Basket as computed/published today by Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas was US$ 54.59* per barrel (bbl) on 17.04.2017.


        In rupee terms, the price of Indian Basket increased to Rs.3515.92 per bbl on 17.04.2017 as compared to Rs. 3511.07 per bbl on 14.04.2017. Rupee closed at Rs . 64.41 p er US$ on 17.04.2017 .
