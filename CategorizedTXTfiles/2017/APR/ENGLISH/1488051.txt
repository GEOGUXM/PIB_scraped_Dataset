      PM greets people on Easter
      Posted On: 16 APR 2017 9:19AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Easter.
    
    “Easter greetings to everyone! May the teachings of Jesus Christ further the spirit of harmony, compassion and togetherness in our society, “the Prime Minister said.
