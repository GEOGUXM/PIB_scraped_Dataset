      SCHEDULE OF PRESIDENT’S VISIT TO HYDERABAD
      Posted On: 25 APR 2017 6:46PM by PIB Hyderabad


    The President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will arrive in Hyderabad tomorrow at Begumept Airport by 12.05 PM. The President will address the inaugural function of Centenary Celebrations of Osmania University. He will have lunch at Rajbhavan. Later Shri Pranab Mukherjee will also address the first convocation of the English and Foreign Languages University at Gachibowli. After that Shri Pranab Mukherjee will reach Begumpet Airport from there he will depart to Rashtrapati Bhavan by 6.20 PM.
