      PM greets people on Hanuman Jayanti
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 11:25AM by PIB Bengaluru


    PM greets people on Hanuman Jayanti


    
    
    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on Hanuman Jayanti.
    
    “हनुमान जयंती की सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं," the Prime Minister said.
