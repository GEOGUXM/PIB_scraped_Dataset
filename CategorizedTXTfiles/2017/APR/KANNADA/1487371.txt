      PM condemns attacks in Egypt

      Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condemned the attacks in Egypt.


      “Deeply pained by the attacks in Egypt. We condemn these attacks. My thoughts are with families of the deceased and prayers with the injured”, the Prime Minister said.
