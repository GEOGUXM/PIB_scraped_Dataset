      PM strongly condemns the terror attack in Afghanistan

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has strongly condemned the terror attack in Mazar-i-sharif, Afghanistan .


      “Strongly condemn the cowardly terror attack in Mazar-i-sharif. Our prayers and condolences to the familes who lost loved ones”, the Prime Minister said.
