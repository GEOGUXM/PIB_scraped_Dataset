      PM remembers Jesus Christ on Good Friday

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, on Good Friday, has remembered the service and sacrifice of Jesus Christ.


      “Today, on Good Friday, we remember the service and sacrifice of Jesus Christ. His was a life devoted to mitigating human suffering.”, the Prime Minister said.
