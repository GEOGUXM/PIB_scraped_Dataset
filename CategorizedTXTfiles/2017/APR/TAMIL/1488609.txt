      Career Coaching for SC/ST Job Seekers
      Posted On: 25 APR 2017 6:12PM by PIB Chennai


    National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, (Erstwhile coaching-Cum-Guidance Centre for SC/ST) Chennai, a subordinate office of Directorate General of Employment, Ministry of Labour & Employment Government of India, is imparting fee coaching to educated Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe job seekers in General English, General Awareness, Typing & shorthand under the Special Coaching Scheme (24 th Phase) and for imparting One Year O Level Computer Software Training (10 th Phase). Interested candidates may send their applications.


    Under Free and stipendiary special coaching Scheme for SC/STs, with a coaching duration of 11 months, candidates will be paid a stipend of Rs 500 per month. Candidates should have completed 10+2 or equivalent. They should be less than 27 years of age and there is no course fee for the candidates.


    Under the ‘O’ level one year computer training (Software) the training is of one year and the candidates will be paid a stipend of Rs 500 per month. The candidates should have a minimum educational qualification of 10+2 or its equivalent. They should be from 18-30 years. There is no course fee for the candidates.


    Applications for the course will be issued from 2nd May 2017. Last date of receiving the application will be 12-06-2017.
