      New Zonal Office for Employees Provident Fund Organisation at Coimbatore
      Posted On: 10 APR 2017 5:43PM by PIB Chennai


    The Employees’ Provident fund Organisation under Ministry of Labour and employment has carved out a Zonal Office at Coimbatore from Tamil Nadu Region under the restructuring scheme of the organization for better administration of the provision of the act and scheme.


    The Zonal office at Coimbatore consists of Regional Office Coimbatore, Madurai, Salem, Trichy, Tirunelveli and Nagercoil. It also covers the District Offices at Tirupur, Pollachi, Ooty, Coonoor, Trichy, Tanjavur, Kimbakonam, Cuddalore, Karur, Salem, Krishnagiri, Erode, Dindigul, Sivakasi and Tuticorin.


    Shri. Varghese M P. Additional Central provident Fund Commissioner has taken charge of the Zonal Office, Coimbatore at Zonal Office, Bhavishya Nidhi Bhavan, Dr. Balasundaram Road, Coimbatore – 641018.


    Shri. Varghese M P. Additional Central Provident Fund commissioner, before joining at Zonal Office, worked in different functional areas such as pension, Legal custom Services, Publicity, vigilance at Head Quarters and the last spell as Director, National Training Academy at New Delhi.


    He was also associated with drafting of social Security Code and visited Takyo, Turin (Italy) in connection with bilateral agreement.


    While taking the charge of Zonal Commissioner, he has reiterated the need for the total digitalization and making ease of doing business.


    He was requested the Employer’s and Employees for full co-operation in the Digital Life Certificate, Registering of Establishment under OLRE linking of Aadhar number seeded with UAN Account already allotted and also to make use of new amnesty scheme which has been introduced and extended up to 30 th June 2017.
