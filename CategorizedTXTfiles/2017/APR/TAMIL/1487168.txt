      Prime Minister's Office

      PM speaks to Russian President Vladimir Putin
      Posted On: 06 APR 2017 5:06PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has spoken to Russian President Mr. Vladimir Putin, today.

    
    Prime Minister expressed deepest condolences to the Government and people of Russia on the tragic loss of lives in the blast in St. Petersburg metro.
