      Prime Minister's Office

      PM extends greetings on National Maritime Day
      Posted On: 05 APR 2017 4:26PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has extended his greetings on National Maritime Day.
    
    “Greetings on National Maritime Day. We cherish India’s glorious maritime heritage and vitality of the maritime sector in India’s growth.”, the Prime Minister said.
