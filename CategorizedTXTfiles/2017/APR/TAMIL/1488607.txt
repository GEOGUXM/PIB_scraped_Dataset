      Recruitment to the post of Multi-Tasking Staff to be held on 30.04.2017, 14.05.2017, 28.05.2017 & 04.06.2017
      Posted On: 25 APR 2017 6:10PM by PIB Chennai


    Staff Selection Commission will conduct recruitment to the post of Multi-Tasking (Non-technical) Staff in Different States and Union Territories 2016 on 30.04.2017, 14.05.2017, 28.05.2017 & 04.06.2017.


    In Southern Region, examination will be held at 14 Centres (cities/towns) viz. Guntur, Kurnool, Rajahmundry, Tirupati and Vishakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh; Hyderabad, Nizamabadand Warangal in Telangana; Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tiruchirappalli, Tirunelveliin Tamil Nadu; Puducherry in Union Territory of Puducherry.


    The examination will be held from 10.00 AM to 12.00 Noon in the Morning Session and from 02.00 PM to 04.00 PM in the Afternoon Session.


    Southern Regional Office has already posted e-Admit Cards authorising the candidates to appear for the said examination in its website (www.sscsr.gov.in), with facility to download it for candidates having examination on 30.04.2017 and 14.05.2017. These details are being intimated to the candidates by SMS to the phone numbers and e-mail to the mail IDs provided by the candidates while applying for the posts. For candidates having examination on 28.05.2017 and 04.06.2017, e-Admit cards will be posted in its website from 08.05.2017 and 15.05.2017 respectively.


    Candidates are requested to note that they shall not be permitted to take up the examination without e-Admit Card and Original Identity Proof. All the candidates are, therefore, requested to download their e-Admit Card without fail.


    For further queries/clarifications, candidates may contact Southern Regional office helpline numbers: Landline - 044-28251139 & Mobile: 9445195946.
