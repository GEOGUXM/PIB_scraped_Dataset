      PM greets people on the occasion of Ram Navami
      Posted On: 04 APR 2017 1:31PM by PIB Ahmedabad

      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people, on the occasion of
      
      Ram Navami.
      "रामनवमी के पावन अवसर पर देशवासियों को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। Greetings on Ram
      
      Navami. " , the Prime Minister said.
