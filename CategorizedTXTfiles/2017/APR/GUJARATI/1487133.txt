      Ministry of Railways

      World Health Day Observed by Vadodara Division of WR
      Posted On: 07 APR 2017 3:24PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    7 th April, World Health Day has been observed by Divisional Railway Hospital – Vadodara and in all the Health units of this division. On this occasion a health awareness rally was organized in the morning. CMS chief medical Superintendent alongwith officers, paramedical staff and the nursing students of Nupur Nursing College participated in the rally. In afternoon a seminar was also organized at Auditorium of the Divisional Railway Hospital in which Dr. Anopsinh Chhastia delivered his lecture on the theme of World Health Day of this year” Depression – let’s talk “.
