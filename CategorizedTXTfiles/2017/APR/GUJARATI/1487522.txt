      SHRI SUNIL CHAWLA HAS TAKEN OVER A CHIEF GENERAL MANAGER OF NABARD, GUJARAT REGIONAL OFFICE, AHMEDABAD
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 4:18PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 11-04-2017


    Shri Sunil Chawla has taken over a Chief General Manager of NABARD, Gujarat Regional Office, Ahmedabad. Prior to taking charge as the head of Gujarat Regional Office, Shri Chawla as the Joint Director of BIRD (Bankers Insitute of Rural Development), Lucknow .
