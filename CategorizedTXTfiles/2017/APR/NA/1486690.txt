      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

    A homage function on the occasion of the 110th birth anniversary of Babu Jagjivan Ram was organised at 0730 hrs to 0800 hrs on 5thapril 2017 at Samta Sthal, opposite Rajghat, New Delhi by the Babu Jagjivan Ram National Foundation.
