      30th Convocation of IGNOU on 13 April
      Posted On: 10 APR 2017 4:16PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is conducting its 30th Convocation at the Headquarters at New Delhi and simultaneously at the 53 Regional Centres located all over the country on Thursday, 13th April 2017.
    Accordingly, the convocation ceremony of IGNOU Regional Centre Trivandrum is being organized at 4 pm at Tagore Theatre, Vazhuthacaud. 1244 students in person and 754 students in-absentia are eligible to receive their Degree / Diploma certificates in the convocation.
