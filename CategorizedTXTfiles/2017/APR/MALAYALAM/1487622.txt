      ECHS MEDICAL COLLEGE POLYCLINIC RELOCATED
      Posted On: 11 APR 2017 5:38PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) Medical College Polyclinic working under Ministry of Defence and Station Headquarters Trivandrum has been relocated from Prasanth Nagar to a new building near the post office at Kottamukku. Brigadier Micheal A.J Fernandez, Station Commander Pangode Military Station inaugurated the Polyclinic, today (11 Apr 2017) by lighting the lamp. Mrs Pearl Fiona Fernandez, Chairperson, FWO, 91 Inf Bde, Lt Gen Thomas Mathew, PVSM (Retd), Serving Officers of Pangode Military camp and Veterans from all three Services attended the function.
    The station Commander addressed the veterans and talked about the Welfare schemes for Ex-Servicemen run by Ministry of Defence and Station Headquarters Trivandrum. A Patient Care Book giving all information required for veterans was also released during the occasion. Col Chandra Mohanan R (Retd), Officer-in-charge Polyclinic and staff members explain about the servicer and new facilities provided at the Polyclinic. Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme has started since 2003 and provides medical treatment and insurance to the Veterans of the Armed Forces, widows and their dependents through the Polyclinics.
