      IGNOU Trivandrum regional convocation tomorrow
      Posted On: 12 APR 2017 11:43AM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The convocation ceremony of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) Regional Centre Trivandrum will be held at Tagore Theatre, Vazhuthacaud, Thiruvananthapuram at 4 PM tomorrow (April 13, 2017) . This was informed by IGNOU Regional Director Dr. B. Sukumar at Press Conference in Thiruvananthapuram today. Kerala Governor, Shri. Justice (Retd.) P. Sathasivam will deliver the convocation address and award degrees to meritorious students under the Regional Centre.
    Under Trivandrum regional Centre 429 students secured eligibility for Masters Degree, 349 students secured Post Graduate Diploma, 1109 students qualified for Bachelor Degree and 111 students got diploma eligibility.
    Ms.Sumi P (Master of Public Administration) and Ms. Richard Juno Raj D.L (Master of sociology) from IGNOU Regional Centre, Trivandrum will be awarded Gold Medals for securing highest marks in their respective programmes across the country.
    Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is simultaneously conducting its 30th Convocation at its headquarters at New Delhi and at the 53 Regional Centres located all over the country. At the main function at IGNOU headquarters, Minister of State for Human Resources Development, Dr. Mahendra Nath Pandey will deliver the convocation address.
