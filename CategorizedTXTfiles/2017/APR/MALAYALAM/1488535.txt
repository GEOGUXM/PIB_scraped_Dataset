      Hall Ticket for Postal exam
      Posted On: 24 APR 2017 5:54PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The examination for Postman / Mail Guard of Kerala Postal Circle will be held on 7th May 2017 (7-5-2017 Sunday). Candidates can download their hall tickets from Kerala Post official website: www.keralapost.gov.in.
