      EXTENSION OF VALIDITY OF RAILWAY TIME TABLES
      Posted On: 07 APR 2017 5:43PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The validity of ‘Trains at a Glance, October, 2016’ and ‘South Zone Public Time Table, October, 2016’ has been extended up to 30th September, 2017. Accordingly, the new Time Table will be effective from 01st October, 2017 instead of 01st July, 2017
    Hence, all trains will run as per the schedule of the existing Time Table up to 30th September, 2017.
