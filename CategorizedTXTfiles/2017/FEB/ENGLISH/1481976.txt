      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Make in India Campaign
      Posted On: 07 FEB 2017 2:41PM by PIB Delhi

      

    Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion have informed that the ‘Make in India’ initiative launched on 25 th September, 2014 aims at promoting India as a important investment destination and a global bub in manufacturing, design and innovation. It is not a specific scheme but a broader nation building initiative to create a conducive environment for investment, development of modern and efficient infrastructure, opening up new sectors for foreign investment and forging a partnership between Government and industry through a positive mind set. At present, India ranks 130 th in the world bank’s annual Doing Business Report (DBR), 2017 as against 131 st rank (revised) in the Doing Business Report, 2016.


    This information was given by Minister of State in the Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Shri Babul Supriyo in reply to a written question in the Lok Sabha today.
