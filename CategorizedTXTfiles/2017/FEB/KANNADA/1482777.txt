    Aero India 2017 in Special Counter of Posts


    Bengaluru February 15, 2017


    Department of Posts has set up a Special Counter at Aero India 2017, Yelahanka, Bengaluru from 14.02.2017 to 18.02.2017.


    Varieties of Philatelic Stamps, Miniature Sheets, Philatelic Ancillary items like Picture Post Cards, Greeting Cards, Mugs, book marks etc., are put on sale. My Stamp Counter is also available.
