      SUMMER SPECIAL TRAINS
      Posted On: 28 FEB 2017 5:07PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      The following special trains will be run to clear the extra rush of passengers during the ensuing summer season.
      Special Fare special trains between Chennai Central and Ernakulam Jn.
      Train No.06005 Chennai Central – Ernakulam Jn. Special fare special train will leave Chennai Central at 22.30 hrs. on 3rd, 10th, 17th & 24th April 2017 (Mondays) and reach Ernakulam Jn. at 10.45 hrs. the next day.
      Train No.06006 Ernakulam Jn. – Chennai Central Special fare special train will leave Ernakulam Jn. at 19.00 hrs. on 6th, 13th, 20th & 27th April 2017 (Thursdays) and reach Chennai Central at 07.20 hrs. the next day.
      Composition: AC 2-tier – 1, AC 3-tier – 2, Sleeper Class – 12 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.
      Stoppages: Katpadi, Jolarpettai, Salem, Erode, Tiruppur, Coimbatore, Palakkad, Thrissur and Aluva.
      Train No.06005 Chennai Central – Ernakulam Jn. Special fare special train will stop at Ernakulam Town also.
      Train No.06006 Ernakulam Jn. – Chennai Central Special fare special train will stop at Perambur also.
      Special Fare special train from Ernakulam Jn. to Mysore
      Train No.06041 Ernakulam Jn. – Mysore Special fare special train will leave Ernakulam Jn. at 17.00 hrs on 4th, 11th, 18th & 25th April 2017 (Tuesdays) and reach Mysore at 07.30 hrs the next day.
      Composition: AC 2-tier – 1, AC 3-tier – 2, Sleeper Class – 12 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.
      Stoppages: Ernakulam Town, Aluva, Thrissur, Ottapalam, Palakkad, Coimbatore, Tiruppur, Erode, Salem, Whitefield, Krishnarajapuram, Bengaluru Cantonment, KSR Bengaluru, Kengeri and Mandya.
