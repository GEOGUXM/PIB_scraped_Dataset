      BSNL Landline disconnection date
      Posted On: 16 FEB 2017 5:39PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      BSNL Trivandrum General Manager requests that the landline telephone bills for the month of January 2017 (bill dtd. 05/01/2017 & 06/01/2017) be paid on 17th February 2017 to get continuous BSNL landline service. Customers can pay their bills at nearest BSNL customer service centers, Post offices, Janaseva kendras, through the mobile application `My BSNL App'. and online portal of BSNL also.
