      India 2017 available at Publications Division Sales Emporium
      Posted On: 23 FEB 2017 12:20PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      The prestigious reference annual India-2017 published by Publications Division, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India is now available at Sales Emporium, Press Road, Trivandrum-695001 (Tel. No.0471 - 2330650).
      The annual priced @ Rs.350/- is a comprehensive digest of country's progress in different fields. It also deals with all aspects of development - from rural to urban, Industry to Infrastructure, Science & Technology to Art & Culture, Economy, Health, Defence to Education & Mass Communication.
      India-2017 is a must read for comprehensive understanding of current affairs, sports and important events. With its authenticity of facts & data, the book is a treasure for civil service aspirants, students, researchers and academicians.
