      Passport Melas in Thiruvananthapuram, Neyyattinkara and Kollam
      Posted On: 23 FEB 2017 12:16PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      Passport Office, Thiruvananthapuram will hold a Passport Mela at Passport Seva Kendras in Kollam, Vazhuthacaud and Neyyattinkara on 04.03.2017(Saturday) to enable the applicants to submit their applications for passport. Appointments for this will be released specially. Applicants may apply online and take appointments and visit the Passport Seva Kendras at Kollam, Vazhuthacaud and Neyyattinkara at the scheduled time. Walk-in will be allowed between 9 AM to 11.30 AM for Govt./PSU employees with No Objection Certificate, students, minors and senior citizens. The Application Reference Number (ARN) and requisite documents in original along with self-attested photocopies should be brought by the applicants for submission. Payments can be made online or through cash payment at the Passport Seva Kendras for walk-in categories.
