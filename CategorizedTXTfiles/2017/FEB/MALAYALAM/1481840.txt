      Dak Adalat on February 27th
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2017 2:47PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A Divisional level Dak Adalat will be held on February 27th at 3 PM at Postal Divisional Office, Thiruvananthapuram South Division, Thiruvananthapuram - 695036. Complaints if any against the services rendered by the offices in Thiruvananthapuram South Division may be forwarded to Shri. B. Padmakumar Superintendent of POs, Thiruvananthapuram South Division, Thiruvananthapuram -695036 on or before 17.02.2017. The cover containing complaints should be super scribed as "DAK ADALAT''.
    
    Earlier complaints received at customer are centre or Divisional level which are not settled till date only will be considered at the DAK ADALAT.
