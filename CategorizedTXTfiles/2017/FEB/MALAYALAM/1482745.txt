      SPECIAL ARRANGEMENTS FOR SHIVARATRI
      Posted On: 15 FEB 2017 1:09PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    Train No. 56605, Coimbatore- Thrissur Passenger reaching Thrissur at 20.45 hrs on 24th February 2017 will be extended to Aluva so as to reach Aluva at 22.40 hrs. In the return direction Train No. 56603, Thrissur- Kannur Passenger will originate from Aluva at 04.10 hrs on 25th February 2015 to clear extra rush of devotees in connection with Maha Shivaratri festival at Aluva.
