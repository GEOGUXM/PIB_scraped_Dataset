      Staff Selection Commission - Junior Engineers (Civil, Electrical & Quantity Surveying And Contract) Examination, 2016 (Paper-1)to be held from 01.03.2017 to 04.03.2017
      Posted On: 21 FEB 2017 6:34PM by PIB Chennai

    Staff Selection Commission will conduct Junior Engine e rs (Civil, Electrical & Quantity Surveying and Contract) Examination, 2016 (Paper-1) in Computer based mode from 01.03.2017 to 04.03.2017. In Southern Region Examination will be held in 46 Venues (Colleges/ Institutions) located at 12 Centres (cities/towns) viz. Vijayawada, Rajahmundry and Vishakhapatnam in Andra Pradesh; Hyderabad, Karimnagar and Warangal in Telangana; Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Tirchirappali and Tirnelveli in Tamil Nadu; and Puducherry in Union Territory of Puducherry.
    The Examination will be held from 10.00 AM to 12.00 Noon in the Morning Session and from 02.45 PM to 04.45 PM in the Afternoon Session.



    Southern Regional Office has already posted e-Admit Cards, authorising the candidates to appear for the said examination in its Website (www.sscsr.gov.in) , with the facility to download it. These details are also intimated to the candidates by SMS to the phone numbers and e-mail to the mail Ids provided by the candidates while applying for the posts.



    Candidates are requested to note that No candidate shall be permitted to appear in the examination without e-Admit Card. All the candidates are, therefore, requested to download their e-Admit Card without fail.
