      Mr. P.M.Pantulu, Scientist-F, The New Deputy Director General of Bureau of Indian Standards - BIS (Southern Region), Chennai
      Posted On: 13 FEB 2017 6:35PM by PIB Chennai


    Mr. P.M.Pantulu has assumed duty as Scientist-F & Deputy Director General (Southern Region), Bureau of Indian Standards, Southern Regional office, Chennai on 13 Feb 2017. Mr.P. M. Pantulu is a Civil Engineer and having more than three decades of experience in Bureau of Indian Standards had worked under various capacities all over India. Before assuming charge as Deputy Director General (Southern Region), he was holding the post of Deputy Director General (Certification) at BIS, Head Quarters, New Delhi.


    Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) is body corporate functioning under Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, Government of India. Its primary functions, as per BIS Act, 1986, are Standardization, Product Certification and Management Systems Certification.


    The Jurisdiction of Southern Regional Office covers Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telengana and Union Territories of Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep and Yanam.
