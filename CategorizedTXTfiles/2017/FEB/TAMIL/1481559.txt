      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people, on the occasion of Basant Panchami
      Posted On: 01 FEB 2017 1:05PM by PIB Chennai


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people, on the occasion of Basant Panchami.


    “ बसंत पंचमी के शुभ अवसर पर सभी देशवासियों को शुभकामनाएं I Greetings on the special occasion of Basant Panchami”, the Prime Minister said.
