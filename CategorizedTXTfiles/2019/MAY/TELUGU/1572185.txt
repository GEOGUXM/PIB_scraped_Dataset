      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      T V K REDDY TO ATTEND AS INDIAN DELEGATE IN SECOND MASS MEDIA FORUM OF THE SCO COUNTRIES IN KYRGYZSTAN FROM MAY 23 TO 26
      Posted On: 15 MAY 2019 5:43PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Shri T V K Reddy, Additional Director General, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh Region has been nominated by Government of India as a delegate to the Second Mass Media forum of SCO (Shanghai Cooperation Organization) countries. The event is organised by Kyrgyz Republic’s Ministry of culture, Information & Tourism from May 23 to 26, 2019 in Bishkek.


    The topic of the Mass Media forum is the “Role of mass media in the development of the Shanghai Cooperation Organization”. This event will become a unique platform for active work through the mass media to create an objective vision of the organization and strengthen its positive image in the global information space.


    The first SCO Media Summit was held in Beijing on 1 st June 2018 under the motto “Development of the Shanghai spirit and opening of a new era in the mass media cooperation”, where over 110 media outlets from 16 countries participated, including SCO member states, observer states and Dialogue partners.
