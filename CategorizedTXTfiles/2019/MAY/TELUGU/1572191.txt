      DOT UNEARTHS ILLEGAL VIoP EXCHANGE
      Posted On: 13 MAY 2019 5:52PM by PIB Hyderabad


    An illegal voice over internet protocol (VoIP) exchange, has been unearthed by Cuddapah Police. In a pr√®ss release issued here today, Shri P. Krishna Kumar, Director (Security) Andhra Pradesh Licence Service Area (APLSA) said that police have arrested Shaik Anwar Basha and recovered from SIM boxes, 345 illegal SIM cards, a Lap-top and some other electronic equipment. An FIR has been filed by DOT on Basha.


    The accused has been converting VoIP calls to local calls by means of VoIP Gateway. Shri Krishna Kumar has cautioned the public to be aware of international calls with an Indian number or no number displayed on their phone. He advised the public to report to DOT call centre on Toll Free number - 1800 110 420 or 1963.
