      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Ms. Debasree Chaudhuri takes charge as the Minister of State of the Ministry of Women and Child Development
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 7:35PM by PIB Delhi


    Ms. Debasree Chaudhuri took charge as the Minister of State of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, in New Delhi today. The 48 year Old Minister representing Raiganj Lok Sabha Constituency in West Bengal has worked in the field of youth and women development.


    Addressing media persons, soon after taking charge, the Minister said that she will b uild on the positive measures already taken up by the Ministry and will continue to pursue the course of holistic development and gender equality of women.
