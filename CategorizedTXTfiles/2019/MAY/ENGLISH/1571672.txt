      Election Commission

      Bye-elections to the Telangana Legislative Council from three Local Authorities' Constituencies.
      Posted On: 07 MAY 2019 12:02PM by PIB Delhi


    There are three casual vacancies in the Telangana Legislative Council from Ranga Reddy, Nalgonda and Warangal Local Authorities' Constituencies. The details of vacancies are as under: -

                                                                                                                              
Name of Local Authorities’ Constituency      Name of Member              Cause of vacancy  Date of Vacancy  Date of retirement
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
Ranga Reddy Local Authorities’ Constituency  Patnam Narender Reddy       Resignation       11.12.2018       04.01.2022        
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
Nalgonda Local Authorities’ Constituency     Komatireddy Rajgopal Reddy  Resignation       17.12.2018       04.01.2022        
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              
Warangal Local Authorities’ Constituency     Konda Muralidhar Rao        Resignation       22.12.2018       04.01.2022        
                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                              

    2. The Commission has decided to hold bye-elections to the Telangana Legislative Council to fill the above mentioned vacancies in accordance with the following schedule: -

1.                                                                             
     Issue of Notifications                         7 th May, 2019 (Tuesday)   
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Last date of making nominations                14 th May, 2019 (Tuesday)  
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Scrutiny of nominations                        15 th May, 2019 (Wednesday)
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Last date for withdrawal of candidatures       17 th May, 2019 (Friday)   
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Date of poll                                   31st May, 2019 (Friday)    
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Hours of poll                                  8.00 a.m. – 4.00 p.m.      
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Counting of Votes                              3rd June, 2019 (Monday)    
                                                                               
                                                                               
1.                                                                             
     Date before which election shall be completed  7 th June, 2019 (Friday)   
                                                                               
                                                                               

    3. The Model Code of Conduct concerning these elections will come into force with immediate effect in the concerned Constituencies. Please see the details in Commission’s website under https://eci.gov.in/files/file/4070-biennial-bye-elections-to-the-legislative-councils-from-council-constituencies-by-graduates%E2%80%99-and-teachers%E2%80%99-and-local-authorities%E2%80%99-constituencies-%E2%80%93-mcc-instructions-%E2%80%93-regarding/
