      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Santosh Kumar Gangwar assumes Charge as Minister Of State (I/C) for Labour and Employment
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 5:05PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar has assumed office as Minister of State (I/C) for Labour and Employment in New Delhi today. Secretary, Shri Heeralal Samariya and senior officials of the Ministry welcomed the new Minister.


    Briefly interacting with media persons, Shri Gangwar thanked the Prime Minister for showing faith in him and giving the charge of the Ministry once again. He said that in my last tenure we took many decisions for Labour welfare and social security of work force of the country. I would like to continue the process in a time bound manner. The Ministry launched Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Man Dhan for the benefit of 40 crore unorganized sector workers in the last tenure and we will take the process further.


    He is 8 th time Lok Sabha member from Bareilly constituency since 1989 till date (except 2009-14). Shri Gangwar has vast experience in governance in various ministries like Finance, Petroleum and Natural Gas, Parliamentary Affairs, Science & Technology, Labour & Employment, Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises, Textiles and Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation. He has been Member of various Parliamentary Committees.


    Shri Gangwar was born on 1st November 1948 in Bareilly. He completed his B.Sc from Agra University and LLB Degree from Rohilkhand University.
