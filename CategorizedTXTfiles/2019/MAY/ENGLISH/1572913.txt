      Piyush Goyal takes charge as Commerce & Industry Minister
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 3:43PM by PIB Delhi


    Piyush Goyal taking charge as Minister of Commerce & Industry today


    Union Minister of Commerce & Industry and Railways, Piyush Goyal, took charge today afternoon as Commerce & Industry Minister. He was accompanied by Former Commerce & Industry Minister, Suresh Prabhu.


    Speaking on this occasion, Piyush Goyal said that he is extremely humbled by stepping into the shoes of a visionary like Suresh Prabhu and will try to do his best to take the work of the Ministry forward.
