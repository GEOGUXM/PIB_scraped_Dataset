      Envoys of three Nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 21 MAY 2019 1:18PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassadors of Germany, Colombia and Peru presented credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (May 21, 2019).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Mr Walter Johannes Lindner, Ambassador of Germany


    2. Mr Alvaro Sandoval Bernal, Ambassador of Colombia


    3. Mr Carlos Rafael Polo Castaneda, Ambassador of Peru
