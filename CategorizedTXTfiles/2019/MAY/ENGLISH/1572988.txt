      AYUSH

      Shri Shripad Yesso Naik takes over as Minister of State with Independent Charge for Ayush
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 6:47PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Shripad Yesso Naik took charge of the office of the Union Minister of State for AYUSH (I/C) in New Delhi today. He also holds the portfolio of the Union Minister of State for Defence. Shri Naik was Union Minister of State for AYUSH in the previous government.
