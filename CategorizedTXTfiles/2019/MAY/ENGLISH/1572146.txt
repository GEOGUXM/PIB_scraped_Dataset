      Election Commission

      8 Parliamentary Constituencies in Bihar to go to Polls in Phase-7 of General Elections 2019
      
      Over 1 Crore 52 lakh 52 thousand electors to cast vote for 8 Lok Sabha seats
      
      157 candidates including 20 female candidates in fray
      
      15,811 Polling stations in place for smooth conduct of Polls
      
      Posted On: 17 MAY 2019 11:26AM by PIB Delhi


      8 Parliamentary Constituencies (PCs) of Nalanda, Patna Sahib, Patliputra, Ara, Buxar, Sasaram, Karakat and Jahanabad in Bihar will go to Polls in Phase-7 of General Elections on 19th May.


      157 candidates, including 20 female candidates are in fray for these 8 Lok Sabha seats. Nalanda, Patna Sahib, Patliputra, Ara, Buxar, Karakat and Jahanabad PCs are General Seats while Sasaram PC is reserved for SC.


      As per the data issued by CEO Bihar, there are total 15252608 voters in these 8 PCs, out of which 8095447 are males, 7156660 females and 501 third gender voters. 15,811 Polling stations have been set up in this phase of elections in Bihar.
