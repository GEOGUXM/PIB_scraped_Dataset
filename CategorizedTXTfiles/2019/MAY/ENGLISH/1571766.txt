      Election Commission

      Giridih, Dhanbad, Jamshedpur and Singhbum Parliamentary Constituencies in Jharkhand to go to Polls in Phase VI of General Elections 2019
      
      8.62% increase in Electors in these four PCs since last General Elections
      
      84,338 electors in 18-19 years age group in Phase VI in Jharkhand
      
      8,581 Polling Booths set up for Phase VI Polls In Jharkhand
      
      
      15 candidates contesting from Giridih PC, 20 from Dhanbad PC, 23 from Jamshedpur PC and 9 from Singhbum PC

      Posted On: 09 MAY 2019 10:18AM by PIB Delhi

    In the Sixth phase of General Elections 2019, Giridih, Dhanbad, Jamshedpur and Singhbum Parliamentary Constituencies (PC) in Jharkhand would be going to polls on 12th May. Giridih (PC No. 6), Dhanbad (PC No. 7) and Jamshedpur (PC No. 9) are General seats, whereas Singhbum (PC No. 10) is an ST seat.


    As per the data sourced from CEO (Jharkhand) website, there are a total of 66,64,929 electors in the four PCs, out of which 35,05,565 are male electors, 31,79,720 female electors and 116 third gender electors. There are 84,338 electors in the age group of 18-19 years in Phase VI in Jharkhand.     The data sourced from ECI website shows that in General Election 2014, there were a total of 61,35,962 electors in these four PCs. This number has grown to 66,64,929 during General Elections 2019, which is an increase of 5,28,967 (8.62%) electors since last General Elections.


    An interesting fact for Phase VI of General Elections is that in Jharkhand, the Dhanbad PC is the smallest PC in terms of area (2177.55 Sq.Km.), yet the largest PC in terms of size of electorate (20,44,802).


    In Phase VI, from different Political Parties, 15 candidates are contesting from Giridih PC, 20 candidates from Dhanbad PC, 23 candidates from Jamshedpur PC and 9 candidates from Singhbum PC.
