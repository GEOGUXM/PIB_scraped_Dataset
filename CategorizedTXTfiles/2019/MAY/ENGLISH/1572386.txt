      Ministry of Home Affairs

      MHA asks States/UTs to take adequate security measures in connection with counting of votes tomorrow
      Posted On: 22 MAY 2019 5:17PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Home Affairs has alerted the State Chief Secretaries and Directors General of Police regarding possibility of eruption of violence in different parts of the country in connection with the counting of votes tomorrow.


    MHA has asked the States and UTs to maintain law & order, peace and public tranquility. States/ UTs have been further asked to take adequate measures for the security of strong rooms and venues of counting of votes. This is in the wake of calls given and statements made in various quarters for inciting violence and causing disruption on the day of counting of votes.
