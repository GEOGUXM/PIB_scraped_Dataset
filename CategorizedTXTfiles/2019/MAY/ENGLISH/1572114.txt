      Rajmahal, Dumka and Godda Parliamentary Constituencies in Jharkhand to go to Polls in Phase VII of General Elections 2019
      
      8.24% increase in Electors in these three PCs since last General Elections
      
      62,762 electors in 18-19 years age group in Phase VII in Jharkhand
      
      4,315 Polling Booths set up for Phase VII Polls in Jharkhand
      
      14 candidates contesting from Rajmahal PC, 15 candidates from Dumka PC and 13 candidates from Godda PC
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2019 5:01PM by PIB Delhi


    In the Seventh phase of General Elections 2019, Rajmahal, Dumka and Godda Parliamentary Constituencies (PC) in Jharkhand would be going to polls on 19 th May. Rajmahal (PC No. 1)and Dumka (PC No. 2) are ST seats, whereas Godda (PC No. 3) is a General seat. A total of 4,315 polling booths have been set up for Phase VII polls in Jharkhand.


    As per the data sourced from CEO (Jharkhand) website, there are a total of 45,64,681 electors in the three PCs, out of which 23,64,541 are male electors, 22,00,119 female electors and 21 third gender electors. There are 62,762 electors in the age group of 18-19 years in Phase VII in Jharkhand.     The data sourced from ECI website shows that in General Election 2014, there were a total of 42,17,287 electors in these four PCs. This number has grown to 45,64,681during General Elections 2019, which is an increase of 3,47,394 (8.24%) electors since last General Elections.


    In Phase VII, from different Political Parties, 14 candidates are contesting from Rajmahal PC , 15 candidates from Dumka PC and 13 candidates from Godda PC .
