      Ministry of Coal

      Shri Pralhad Joshi takes charge as new Minister of Coal
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 8:04PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Pralhad Joshi took over as the new Minister of Coal here today i.e. 31 st May, 2019 . In addition to Coal, he has also been assigned the portfolio of Minister of Parliamentary Affairs and Mines. Shri Sumanta Chaudhury, Secretary, Ministry of Coal and other Senior Officers were also present on this occasion.


    Brief Profile of Shri Pralhad Joshi


    Shri Pralhad Joshi (born 27 November 1962) is a member of the 17th Lok Sabha (2019) of India . He represents the Dharwad constituency of Karnataka and is the Ex-President of the Karnataka state unit of the Bharatiya Janata Party . Shri Pralhad Joshi was elected as a Member of the Lok Sabha for the first time in 2004.


    He has been a Member, Business Advisory Committee, Member, Consultative Committee, Ministry of Urban Development, Housing and Urban Poverty Alleviation, Member, Committee on Ethics, Chairperson, Standing Committee on Petroleum and Natural Gas, Member, General Purposes Committee, Member, Committee on Privileges in the Parliament among others during his long and distinguished tenure as a Parliamentarian.
