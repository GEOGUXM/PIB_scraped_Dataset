      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Audition for Cultural Troupes from May 27
      Posted On: 22 MAY 2019 4:43PM by PIB Guwahati


    The audition and screening for the empanelment of cultural troupesin Regional Outreach Bureau (Songs and Drama Division), Guwahati has been scheduled to be held from 27 th May, 2019. The audition will be held at the auditorium of Shilpagram, NEZCC, Government of India, Panjabari, Guwahati. The audition will start at 10 AM every day on the interview dates.


    Call letters to the eligible candidates have already been sent by post. Those who have not received the call letter may contact ROB, Guwahati, SoochnaBhawan, Chandmari or may call at 9706407255.
