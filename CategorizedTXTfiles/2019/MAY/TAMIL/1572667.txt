      Last date for TANCET 2019 extended
      Posted On: 27 MAY 2019 3:17PM by PIB Chennai


    The last date for registration and submission of application through online for Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) 2019 for admission to (i) M.B.A., (ii) M.C.A., (iii) M.E./M.Tech/M.Arch./M.Plan. degree programmes for the academic year 2019-2020 is extended upto 31.05.2019, as per the press release issued by the secretary (TANCET), Anna University, Chennai.
