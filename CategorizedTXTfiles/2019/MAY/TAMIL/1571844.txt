      State Finance Audit Report submitted to the Governor of Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 10 MAY 2019 3:42PM by PIB Chennai


    In accordance with Article 152 (2) of the Constitution of India, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India is required to forward his Audit Report on the accounts of the Government of Tamil Nadu to the Governor, who causes it to be laid on the table of the State Legislature.


    Accordingly, the State Finance Audit Report of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India – Government of Tamil Nadu for the year ended 31 March 2018, was handed over to the Hon’ble Governor of Tamil Nadu on 03 May 2019 for laying before the State Legislative Assembly.
