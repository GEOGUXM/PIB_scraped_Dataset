      Special Coaching Scheme under Shorthand for SC/ST: applications invited
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2019 3:25PM by PIB Chennai


    The National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, 3 rd Floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Building, 56, Santhome High Road, Chennai-600 004, (Tel 044-24615112) under M/o Labour & Employment, DGE, Government of India, inviting proposals from reputed firms/Institutions of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, for imparting the DGE sponsored scheme of 26 th Phase Special Coaching Scheme under Shorthand to 60 SC/ST candidates. Interested Institutes/Firms need to meet the following criteria such as well-trained faculties, training infrasture, location etc.


    The Selected institute will impart training in (1) General Awareness, (2) General English (3) Numerical Ability, (4) Shorthand and Typing, (5) Basic Knowledge in Computer in order to make the eligible SC/ST educated Job Seeker of Tamil Nadu viable for facing the different Competitive Examinations. The selected Institues/ Firms will be paid professional Charges of Rs.800/- per candidate per month.


    The interested institutions/Firms may apply to the Sub-Regional Employment Officer, NATIONAL CAREER SERVICE CENTRE FOR SC/ST, CHENNAI (FORMERLY CGC) 3 rd Floor, Tamil Nadu Slum Clearance Board Building, 56, Santhome High Road, Chennai-600 004 (Tel 044-24615112) on or before 20th June 2019.


    The details are informed by the Sub-Regional Employment Officer, NCSC for SC/ST, Chennai in a press release.
