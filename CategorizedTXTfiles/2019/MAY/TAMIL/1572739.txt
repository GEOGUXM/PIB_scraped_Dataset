      Nidhi Aapke Nikat (PF Near You) Programme in Chennai
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2019 4:26PM by PIB Chennai


    The Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) is conducting “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” (PF Near You) in the Regional office of the EPFO, Chennai on 10 th of every month for speedy redressal of long pending grievances of the Subscribers/Employers covered under the Employees’ Provident Funds & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.


    Accordingly, the “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” for the month of June 2019 will be conducted on Monday the 10th June, 2019 in the Regional Office of the EPFO, No.37, Royapettah High Road, Chennai 600 014. The following timeslots are allotted for “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” Programme:


     1. Subscribers/Members/Pensioners : 10.30AM to 1.00 PM

     2. Employers : 3.00 PM to 4.00 PM
     3. Exempted establishments located

    In jurisdiction : 4.00 PM to 5.00 PM


    The Subscribers / Employers who desire to get their long pending unresolved grievances redressed or have any other suggestions on improvement of service/organization, they may register their names with the Public Relations Officers duly furnishing their PF code Number / Account Number, nature of grievance etc on or before 03rd of June 2019. The aforesaid timings are only for guidance and the employers may also present their grievances, if any, earlier during the day.


    The details are informed by the Regional Provident Fund Commissioner, Regional Office, Chennai in a press release.
