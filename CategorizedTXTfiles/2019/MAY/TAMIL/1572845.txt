      NABARD has sanctioned Rs.159 crore to Tamil Nadu
      Posted On: 30 MAY 2019 7:00PM by PIB Chennai


    National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has sanctioned financial assistance of Rs. 159 crore to Government of Tamil Nadu for improvements to 147 roads and reconstruction of 115 rural bridges damaged in the Gaja cyclone ine the four districts of Pudukottai, Thanjavur, Dindigul and Nagapattinam. On completion of the projects by 31 March 2021, connectivity of 701 villages to 247 marketing centres in these four districts is expected to be improved.

RIDF funds are being extensively availed by State Governments to improve the critical infrastructure projects in rural areas. Tamil Nadu is one of the leading States in the country in availing RIDF assistance and has successfully completed many rural infrastructure projects in the previous years.
