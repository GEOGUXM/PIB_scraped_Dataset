      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      "JANAMAT 2019"
      Posted On: 21 MAY 2019 1:35PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 21, 2019


    The Regional News Unit of All India Radio, Kolkata is mounting Special Programmes on the day of Counting of Lok Sabha Elections votes on 23-05-2019. The Programmes titled "Janamat-2019" will start from 10.00 a.m. in the morning and will continue till 4.00 p.m. on that day linking Primary Channel Gitanjali and FM Rainbow. The programme will carry additional news bulletins besides existing bulletins to cater election results and elections related discussions. The second part of the programme will be aired from 8.05 p.rn. to 9.00 p.m. in both the Channels. Experts, Analysts and Representatives of Political Parties will be present at the Studio of Akashvani Kolkata to express their views on out come of poll results. AIR Stations of Siliguri, Murshidabad and Santiniketan will also relay the same. The programme will also be available on Social Media Platform. This include Facebook page: Akashvani Sang bad Kolkata and Youtube Channel: RNU Kolkata Social Media.
