      Posted On: 21 MAY 2019 11:19AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 21, 2019


    Election Commission of India (ECI) has directed repolling in 200-Sanskrit Collegiate Schoolâ€™s Room Number 1 of Jorasanko Assembly segment of Kolkata Uttar Parliamentary Constituency (PC). The poll for the General Election, 2019 was held in this constituency in the seventh phase of poll on May 19, 2019. Repolling will be held in this polling station tomorrow (Wednesday) from 7 am to 6 pm.


    After taking into account of all material circumstances, on the basis of reports submitted by the Returning Officer and the General Observer of this PC, the Secretariat of the Election Commission of India, has given instructions to Chief Electoral Officer (CEO), West Bengal for holding fresh election in this polling station tomorrow under Sub-Section (2) of Section 58 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951.
