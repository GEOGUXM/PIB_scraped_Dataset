      Ministry of Shipping

      KoPT takes Measures to cope up with Very Severe Cyclonic Storm ‘Fani’
      Posted On: 02 MAY 2019 3:55PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 2, 2019


    Kolkata Port Trust (KoPT) has taken several precautionary measures to deal with the severe cyclonic storm ‘Fani’. The KoPT authority has informed that the organisation is closely monitoring the movement of the cyclone. Besides issuing general alert, all ships in the docks both at Kolkata Dock System and Haldia Dock Complex have been issued specific advice to take precautionary measures. Five Coast Guard vessels and all operational crafts of the port have been provided safe berthing inside the docks.


    This apart, ships from riverine jetties at Haldia and Budge Budge and also from Sagar and Diamond Harbour anchorages have either been put out to sea or shifted to docks. Two control stations have been opened with required facilities-one at Kolkata and other at Haldia. Liaisons have been kept with Director General, Shipping, Coast Guard and State disaster management stations.


    It is also informed that shipping to KoPT will remain suspended from tonight till the storm is over. Inland Waterways Authority of India has also been advised to secure Bangladeshi vessels in safe area.
