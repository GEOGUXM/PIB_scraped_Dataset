      Election Commission

      ECI Prohibits Poll Campaigning and Sell of Liquor after 10 PM Tonight Till Conclusion of Poll in 9PCs in Seventh Phase
      Posted On: 16 MAY 2019 7:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 16, 2019


    The Election Commission has prohibited poll campaigning for the General Election, 2019 from 10 PM tonight. In a written order from the Capital, the Commission has strictly directed that no person shall convene, hold, attend, join or address any public meeting or procession in connection with election or display to the public any election matter by means of cinematograph, television or other similar apparatus or propagate any election matter to the public by holding, or by arranging the holding of any musical concert or any theatrical performance from tonight. ECI has also strictly directed that no spirituous, fermented or intoxicating liquors or other substances of similar nature can be sold or distributed at hotels, eating houses, taverns, shops or any other place, public or private within polling areas in the nine constituencies to be going for poll in the seventh phase of General Election. The constituencies are DumDum, Barasat, Basirhat, Jaynagar (SC), Mathurapur (SC), Diamond Harbour, Jadavpur, Kolkata Dakshin and Kolkata Uttar from 10 PM tonight till the conclusion of poll on May 19, 2019 (Sunday).


    Besides, exit poll or publicising by print or electronic media on the result of the poll is also prohibited from 7.00 am till 6.30 pm on May 19.
