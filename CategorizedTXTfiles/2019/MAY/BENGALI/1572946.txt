      PIB OFFICIALS TAKE PLEDGE TO MAKE SURROUNDINGS FREE FROM TOBACCO ON WORLD NO TOBACCO DAY
      Posted On: 31 MAY 2019 5:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 31 May 2019


    The officials and staff members of Press Information Bureau (PIB) under the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting observed the World No Tobacco Day with a pledge to make the office premises free from smoke and tobacco, here today. Officials of Department of Publications Division and Central Board of Film Certification also participated and took pledge for the same cause. The pledge was administered by Shri Chinmay Chakravarty, Director (M&C), PIB, Kolkata. A round of discussion was also organized on the subject of ‘Harm caused to environment and human beings by smoking and use of tobacco products’.


    PIB has put up boards carrying a message for prevention of smoking and use of tobacco products in various corners and floors of its office building located at 8, Esplanade East, Kolkata.
