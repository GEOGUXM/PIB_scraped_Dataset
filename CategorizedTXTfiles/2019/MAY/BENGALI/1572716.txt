      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      ARJUNA AWARDEE AND INDIANOIL EMPLOYEE SPRINTER HIMA DAS QUALIFIES H.S. EXAM WITH FIRST DIVISION
      Posted On: 28 MAY 2019 12:57PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 28, 2019


    National icon, Arjuna awardee athlete and IndianOil Employee at IndianOil AOD State Office, Hima Das has passed the Higher Secondary Examination with First Division Marks. Hima has recently won an Asian Games Silver (2018) and is known as the “Dhing Express”, a resident of the Dhing Village in Nagaon District of Assam.


    Sri Amit Kr Basu, Executive Director IC (RS), IndianOil has applauded Hima’s passion for academic performance despite her challenging schedule as one of the leading athletes in the country.


    Hima secured a total of 349 marks out of 500 marks from Dhing College which is affiliated with the Guwahati University (GU). She is a student of Arts and her subjects included English, Assamese, Political Science and Geography. She is among 11 students who secured First Division in Arts Stream from her college.


    This achievement is significant in view of the rigorous L.C. Training schedule Hima has to manage and even as of now She is busy with her regimented training schedule at Poland.
