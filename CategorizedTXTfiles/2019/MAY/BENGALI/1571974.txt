      CEO, WB Inaugurates Tram in City Decorated with Pictures and Messages to Encourage Voters to Cast Their Votes
      Posted On: 14 MAY 2019 2:38PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 14, 2019


    Nine Lok Sabha constituencies of West Bengal including Dum Dum, Barasat, Basirhat, Jaynagar (SC), Mathurapur, Diamond Harbour, Jadavpur, Kolkata Dakshin and Kolkata Uttar will go to poll in the seventh and the last phase of Lok Sabha poll in West Bengal on May 19, 2019 (Sunday).


    To encourage voters to exercise their franchise for building up a strong democracy, the Chief Electoral Officer, Dr Aariz Aftab inaugurated a tram decorated with tricolour balloon, pictures and messages all writen in Bengali today at Ballugunge Tram Depot of the city. ‘Chalo Desh Gori’ or let us build the country and Voter Help Line number 1950 are mentioned too in the outer body of the tram.


    Inside the tram, EVM machines with VVPATs were displayed, to make people aware of the technique of casting vote.


    In the seventh phase of poll 59 constituencies across seven States and one Union Territory, including Bihar, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal and Chandigarh will go to poll.
