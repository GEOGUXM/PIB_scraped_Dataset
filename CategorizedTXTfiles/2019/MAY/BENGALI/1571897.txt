      ECI Directs Repolling in 116 & 110 Nos. Polling Stations of Barrackpore and Arambag (SC) PCs
      Posted On: 11 MAY 2019 12:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 11, 2019


    Election Commission of India (ECI) has directed repolling in 116-Kanchrapara Udbodhoni Madhyamik Vidyalayaâ€™s Room Number 1 and 110-Laskarpur Netaji Prathamik Vidyalaya of Bizpur and Tarakeswar Assembly segment of Barrackpur and Arambag (SC) Parliamentary constituencies. The poll for the General Election, 2019 was held in these two constituencies in the fifth phase of poll on May 6, 2019. Repolling will be held in these two polling stations on May 12, 2019 (Sunday) from 7 am to 6 pm.


    After taking into account of all material circumstances, the Secretariat of the Election Commission of India, has given instructions to Chief Electoral Officer (CEO), West Bengal for holding fresh election in these two polling stations on May 12 under Sub-Section (2) of Section 58 of the Representation of the People Act, 1951.


    The repolling will take place simultaneously with the sixth phase of poll in eight Parliamentary constituencies of West Bengal including Tamluk, Kanthi, Ghatal, Jhargram (ST), Medinipur, Purulia, Bankura and Bishnupur (SC) constituencies on May 12.
