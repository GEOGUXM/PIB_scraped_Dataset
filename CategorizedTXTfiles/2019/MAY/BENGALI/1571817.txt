      NOMINATIONS INVITED FROM MINISTRIES/DEPARTMENTS FOR CONFERMENT OF VAYOSHRESHTHA SAMMAN – 2019
      Posted On: 09 MAY 2019 7:08PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 09 May 2019


    The President of India confers National Award – Vayoshreshtha Samman to eminent senior citizens and institutions involved in rendering distinguished services for the cause of elderly persons on the International Day of Older Persons (IDOP) which falls on 1 st October every year. In this connection, the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment has invited nominations for Vayoshreshtha Samman – National Awards for Senior Citizens – 2019 from individuals / institutions vide an advertisement dated 17 th April 2019 in leading newspapers across the country. Additionally, as per Para 6 (i) of the notification dated 18/01/2013, ministries/departments of the Government of India and autonomous organizations / State Governments or U T Administrations have also been asked to nominate suitable individuals/institutions for the awards.


    In this connection, all the ministries / departments have been asked to forward nominations of eligible individuals / institutions for conferment of the award to the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment latest by 31 st May 2019.
