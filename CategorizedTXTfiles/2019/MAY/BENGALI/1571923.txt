      Election Commission

      Deputy Election Commissioner Sudeep Jain in the city to review preparations for last phase polling and counting
      Posted On: 13 MAY 2019 3:43PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 13, 2019


    In order to review preparations for the seventh phase of polling to be held May 19 and counting to be held on May 23, Deputy Election Commissioner Shri Sudeep Jain held a series of meetings in Kolkata today. In a review meeting held in the morning at the building of Bengal Chamber of Commerce in the city, re-assessment of security arrangements and law & order situation of the state took place. All the general observers along with the District Electoral Officers and SPs remained present in the review meeting. Shri Sudeep Jain is to hold another meeting with Chief Electoral Officer of West Bengal- Dr. Aariz Aftab, Special Police Observer Shri Vivek Dubey and Special Observer Shri Ajay Nayek later, among other programmes.
