      Election Commission seeks Review Report on Contingency Plan for Combating Cyclone ‘Fani’ from DEOs by 12 noon tomorrow
      Posted On: 02 MAY 2019 6:50PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, May 2, 2019


    The Election Commission has taken adequate contingency measures to combat the ensuing cyclone ‘Fani’ in regards to fifth phase of poll on May 6, 2019 (Monday) as well as the EVMs kept in strong-rooms. All the District Election Officers or DEOs have been instructed to take care of the Dispersal-cum-Reception Centre (DCRC) infrastructure, EVM warehouses, polling personnel and vehicular movement. They have been given special instruction to review the plans regarding this, so as to take the right step at the right moment and send a report on the same to Chief Electoral Officer’s office by 12 pm tomorrow.


    This apart, all the District Election Officer’s offices are equipped with disaster management system to deal with natural calamities.
