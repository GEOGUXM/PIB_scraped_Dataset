      Dak Adalat to solve postal service issues
      Posted On: 14 MAY 2019 3:28PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 14.05.2019


    Dak Adalat for settlement of complaints and problems on Postal Services will be held at 11:00 hours onwards on 22.05.2019 at the office of Sr. Supdt. of Post Offices, City Division, Ahmedabad, Nr. All India Radio, Ashram Road, Ahmedabad-380009. The Sr. Supdt. of Post Offices, City Division, Ahmedabad will hear each complaint individually.


    The concept of the Dak Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settlement of problems of public members on the spot. The complaints related to Postal Services (in brief) should be sent to Shri B.B. Prajapati, Sr. Supdt. of POS, City Division, Ahmedabad-380009. The complaint should reach to the above office latest by 20.05.2019. The complaint received after due date and related to policy matter will not be entertained.
