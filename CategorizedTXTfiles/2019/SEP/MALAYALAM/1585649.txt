      Union Minister of State for Shipping (independent charge) and Chemicals & Fertilizers visits FACT, Kochi
      Posted On: 20 SEP 2019 1:00PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

     1. Hon’ble Union Minister of State for Shipping (Independent Charge) and Chemicals & Fertilisers, Shri Mansukh Mandaviya, visited The Fertilisers And Chemicals Travancore Limited (FACT) today. He was received by FACT Chairman & Managing Director, Shri Kishor Rungta.

    Hon’ble Minister actively participated in Swatch Bharat (cleanliness drive) with FACT officials, employees and CISF personnel at Udyogamandal Complex. He had a brief meeting with the top officials of FACT in which CMD, FACT, explained the issues faced by the organization and action taken to improve performance.
