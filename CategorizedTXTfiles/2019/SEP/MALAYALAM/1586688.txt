      RMS dak adalat
      Posted On: 30 SEP 2019 3:04PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Divisional level ''DAK ADALAT'' of Ernakulam RMS EK Division will be held at the office of the SRM, RMS, Ernakulam Division, fifth floor of Ernakulam HPO complex , Kochi on 18 th October, 2019 at 11 am. Complaints relating to Postal services in Ernakulam RMS EK division may be taken up in the Dak Adalat.


    Complaints can be sent in envelopes super scribed “Dak Adalat” and addressed to “ The Superintendent, RMS Ernakulam Division, Kochi-682011,” so as to reach there on or before 14 th October, 2019.
