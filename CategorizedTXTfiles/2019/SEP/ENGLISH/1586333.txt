      Posted On: 25 SEP 2019 11:30PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi met Prime Minister of Belgium Mr. Charles Michel on the sidelines of the High Level Segment of the 74th Session of the UNGA in New York on 25th September 2019.


    PM Modi congratulated PM Charles Michel on his election as the President of the European Council.


    Both leaders reviewed the excellent relations between India and Belgium and the follow up decisions taken during the visit of His Majesty the King of Belgium to India in 2017.


    They discussed intensification of strategic and security relations between India and the EU. They agreed to intensify their efforts on early conclusion of negotiations on the Bilateral Investment and Trade Agreement, connectivity, closer cooperation on counter-terrorism, multilateral institutions, and migration and mobility.


    The two leaders also agreed that the 15th India-EU Summit should be held at an early date.
