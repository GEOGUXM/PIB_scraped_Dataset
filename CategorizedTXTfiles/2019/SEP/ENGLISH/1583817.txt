      PM greets the people on the occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi
      Posted On: 02 SEP 2019 9:17AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi.


    “सभी देशवासियों को पावन पर्व गणेश चतुर्थी की ढेर सारी शुभकामनाएं। गणपति बाप्पा मोरया! Wishing everyone a blessed Ganesh Chaturthi”, the Prime Minister said.


    सभी देशवासियों को पावन पर्व गणेश चतुर्थी की ढेर सारी शुभकामनाएं। गणपति बाप्पा मोरया!

    
    Wishing everyone a blessed Ganesh Chaturthi.

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) September 2, 2019
