      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Press Note No. 3 (2019 Series)
      
      Withdrawal of Press Note No. 17 (1984 Series)
      Posted On: 12 SEP 2019 4:34PM by PIB Delhi


    The Press Note No. 17 (1984) series regarding Environmental clearance of Industrial Licence- conditions of Letter of Intent/Industrial Licence, relates to pre-1991 period. After the Industrial Policy Resolution 1991, and considering various amendments made to Notification No. 477(E) dated 25 th July, 1991 only the following four Industries are covered under compulsory licensing:


    i. Cigars and Cigarettes of tobacco and manufactured tobacco substitutes


    ii. Electronic aerospace and defence equipment


    iii. Industrial Explosives


    iv. Hazardous chemicals


    As Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade is not issuing licence in any other case, this Press Note 17 (1984 Series) has become irrelevant. Accordingly, this Press Note is withdrawn.


    Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, New Delhi, 12 th September 2019
