      PM greets the people on Onam
      Posted On: 11 SEP 2019 10:24AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the auspicious occasion of Onam.


    “Greetings on the auspicious occasion of Onam! May this festival further the spirit of happiness, well-being and prosperity in our society”, the Prime Minister said.
