      President of India presents National Service Scheme Awards
      Posted On: 24 SEP 2019 3:29PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, presented the National Service Scheme (NSS) Awards for 2017-18 to universities, NSS units and NSS volunteers at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (September 24, 2019) New Delhi.
