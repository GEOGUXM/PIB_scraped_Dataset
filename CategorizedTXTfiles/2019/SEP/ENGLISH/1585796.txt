      Prime Minister's Office

      PM interacts with Kashmiri Pandits in Houston
      Posted On: 22 SEP 2019 7:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today met a delegation of Kashmiri Pandits in Houston, Texas.


    During the interaction, the community members strongly supported the steps taken by the Prime Minister for the progress of India and the empowerment of every Indian.


    “ I had a special interaction with Kashmiri Pandits in Houston.”, Prime Minister tweeted after the interaction.
