      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC appointments
      Posted On: 11 SEP 2019 1:24PM by PIB Delhi

     1. The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the appointment of Dr. P.K. Mishra, currently functioning as Additional Principal Secretary to Prime Minister, as Principal Secretary to Prime Minister with effect from 11 th September, 2019. His appointment will be co-terminus with the term of the Prime Minister or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
     2. The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the appointment of Shri P.K. Sinha, currently functioning as OSD in the Prime Minister's Office, as Principal Advisor to Prime Minister with effect from 11 th September, 2019. His appointment will be coterminus with the term of the Prime Minister or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
