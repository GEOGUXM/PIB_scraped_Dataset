      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Government imposes Minimum Export Price (MEP) for Onions at US $ 850 per Metric Ton
      Posted On: 13 SEP 2019 8:28PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Government has decided that the export of all varieties of onions ( as described under Chapter 7 of Schedule 2 of ITC (HS)) shall be permitted only on Letter of Credit (LC) subject to minimum export price (MEP) of US $ 850 F.O.B. per Metric Ton until further orders. A Notification to this effect has been issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade, Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce & Industries, Government of India dated 13 th September, 2019.
