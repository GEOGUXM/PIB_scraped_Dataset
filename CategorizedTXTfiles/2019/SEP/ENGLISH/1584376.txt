      Ministry of Education

      16 KV Students to witness Chandrayaan-2 landing with the Prime Minister at ISRO, Bengaluru
      Posted On: 06 SEP 2019 6:34PM by PIB Delhi


    Students from various Kendriya Vidyalayas across the country have been selected through an Online Space Quiz conducted by ISRO, to witness the historic landing of Chandrayaan-2 with the Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi on September 7 at ISRO, Bengaluru.


    A total number of 150279 students from various Kendriya Vidyalayas from all over the country participated in the online Space Quiz Conducted by ISRO.
