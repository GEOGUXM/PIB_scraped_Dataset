      Ministry of Culture

      New Facility of ‘High Voltage Demonstration’ by Nehru Science Center on 20th September
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2019 2:25PM by PIB Mumbai


    Nehru Science Centre, National Council of Science Museums, Ministry of Culture, GOI, Mumbai is organized inauguration of a new facility innovated by the Nehru Science Center, a “Sparking High Voltage Demonstration “ program. The program will be held on Friday, 20th September 2019 at 2.30 pm at Nehru Science Center, NCSM, Dr.E. Moses Road, Worli, Mumbai-18.
    Dr. Vasant Shinde, Director General, National Maritime Heritage Complex, Gandhinagar (Gujrat) and Chairman, Executive Committee, Nehru Science Center and Chairman, Executive Committee, Nehru Science Centre will be the Chief Guest of the program.
    The program is open for all. Therefore; a large number of people should attend and gracious function said organizer in press invitation.
