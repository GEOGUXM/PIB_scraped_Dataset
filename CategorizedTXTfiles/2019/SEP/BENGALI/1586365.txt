      Ministry of Railways

      REGULATION OF TRAIN SERVICES
      Posted On: 26 SEP 2019 10:46PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 th September, 2019


    In view of commissioning of new Electronic Interlocking system and modification of yard at Tundla Station over Allahabad Division of North Central Railway, the following trains will be regulated by way of cancellation and diversion as under:-


    CANCELLATION OF TRAINS


    · 12815 Puri-Anand Vihar Nandan Kanan Express leaving Puri on 19.10.2019.


    · 12816 Anand Vihar-Puri Nandan Kanan Express leaving Anand Vihar on 21.10.2019.


    · 12876 Anand Vihar-Puri Neelachal Express leaving Anand Vihar on 20.10.2019.


    · 12875 Puri-Anand Vihar Neelachal Express leaving Puri on 18.10.2019.


    DIVERSION OF TRAINS


    · 22857 Santragachi-Anand Vihar Express leaving Santragachi on 30.09.19, 07.10.19 & 14.10.19 will run via Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya -Varanasi-Lucknow-Moradabad-Ghaziabad instead of Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya- Kanpur Central-Chipyana Buzurg-Ghaziabad.


    · 12443 Haldia-Anand Vihar Express leaving Haldia on 03.10.19,
    10.10.19 & 17.10.19 will run via Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya-Varanasi- Lucknow-Moradabad-Ghaziabad instead of Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya-Kanpur Central-Chipyana Buzurg-Ghaziabad.


    · 12825 Ranchi-Anand Vihar Jharkhand Sampark Kranti Express leaving Ranchi on 03.10.19, 07.10.19, 10.10.19, 14.10.19 & 17.10.19 will run via Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya-Varanasi-Lucknow-Ghaziabad instead of Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya-Kanpur Central-Chipyana Buzurg-Ghaziabad.


    · 12826 Anand Vihar-Ranchi Jharkhand Sampark Kranti Express leaving Anand Vihar on 02.10.19, 05.10.19, 09.10.19, 12.10.19, 16.10.19 & 19.10.19 will run via Ghaziabad - Moradabad-Lucknow- Varanasi-Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya instead of Ghaziabad-Chipyana Buzurg-Kanpur Central-Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya.
