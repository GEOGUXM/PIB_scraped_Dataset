      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      ITPO Organises a Two-day Fair on Leather and Accessories
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2019 5:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 27, 2019


    India Trade promotion Organisation (ITPO) is organising a two-day fair on leather and accessories termed India Leather & Accessories Fair (ILAF) in association with Central Leather Research Institute and other associations connected with leather industry, which started today in the city.


    As many as 61 leading Indian companies are participating in this fair, where leather goods, finished leather, lining leather, industrial gloves, industrial leather goods, chemicals for manufacturing of leather, adhesive machinery components, sewing machines, leather goods accessories and the like have been displayed. Business meetings will be arranged in the midst of the fair between Indian and overseas buyers or importers.


    In total 35 confirmed buyers from USA, Korea, Germany, Spain, Italy, Tajikistan, Bangladesh, Kenya, Senegal, Portugal, Uzbekistan, UAE, Poland, Australia, Chile and South Korea are participating in the fair.


    A Reverse Buyer Seller Meet and a fashion show will be organised too during the fair.


    The Executive Director ITPO, Shri Rajesh Agrawal as well as its General Manager Smt Hema Maity were present among others on the occasion.


    The two-day event is going to have visitorship from across the globe apart from India. The fair timing is between 10 am and 6 pm.
