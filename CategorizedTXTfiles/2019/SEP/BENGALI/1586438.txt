      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN FIVE PAIRS OF PUJA SPECIAL TRAINS BETWEEN SANTRAGACHI AND HAPA
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2019 4:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, September 27, 2019


    With a view to clear extra rush of passengers during ensuing Puja, South Eastern Railway has decided to run five pairs of Special trains between Santragachi-Hapa-Santragachi during the period from 04.10.19 to 04.11.19.


    82034/82033 Santragachi-Hapa-Santragachi Suvidha Special


    Santragachi-Hapa (82034) Suvidha Special leaving Santragachi at 21.05 hrs on 04.10.19, 11.10.19 and 01.11.19 (Friday) will reach Hapa at 16.35 hrs, on Sunday. In the opposite direction, Hapa-Santragachi Suvidha Special (82033) leaving Hapa at 10.40 hrs on 07.10.19, 14.10.19 and 04.11.19 (Monday) will arrive Santragachi at 05.45 hrs, on Wednesday.


    02834/02833 Santragachi-Hapa-Santragachi Superfast Special


    Santragachi-Hapa (02834) Superfast Special leaving Santragachi at 21.05 hrs on 18.10.19 and 25.10.19 (Friday) will reach Hapa at 16.35 hrs, on Sunday. In the opposite direction, Hapa-Santragachi Superfast Special (02833) leaving Hapa at 10.40 hrs on 21.10.19 and 28.10.19 (Monday) will arrive Santragachi at 05.45 hrs, on Wednesday.


    Both Special trains consisting of twelve AC 3-Tier and four Sleeper Class coaches will have stoppages at Kharagpur, Tatanagar, Chakradharpur, Rourkela, Jharsuguda, Bilaspur, Raipur, Durg, Gondia, Nagpur, Wardha, Badnera, Akola, Malkapur, Bhusaval, Jalgaon, Amalner, Nandurbar, Surat, Bharuch, Vadodara, Anand, Nandiad, Ahmedabad, Viramgam, Sunar, Wakaner and Rajkot between Santragachi and Hapa.
