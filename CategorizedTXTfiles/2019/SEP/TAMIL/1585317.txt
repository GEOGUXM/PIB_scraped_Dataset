      COAST GUARD COMMANDER REGION (EAST) CALLS ON DEPUTY CM OF TAMIL NADU
      Posted On: 17 SEP 2019 5:14PM by PIB Chennai


    Inspector General S Paramesh, PTM, TM Commander Coast Guard Region (East) paid a courtesy call to the Hon’ble Deputy Chief Minister O. Panneerselvam of Tamilnadu on 17 Sep 2019.


    IG Coast Guard shared several facets of the service and apprised the Hon’ble Deputy Chief Minister with respect to various developments in the Area of Responsibility. Discussions were focused on strengthening the maritime domain of this coastal state. During interaction, the Hon’ble Deputy Chief Minister was assured that Indian Coast Guard would continue providing rescue and relief to the fishermen whenever they are in distress at sea.
