      DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 16 SEP 2019 4:27PM by PIB Chennai


    It is proposed to conduct DAK ADALAT on 25.09.2019 at 1500 hours at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002. The members of the public are requested to send their grievances, if any regarding Postal services (Money order / Registered Articles / Savings Bank / Savings Certificate etc.,) availed at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002, either in person or by post or through email at doannaroadhpo.tn@indiapost.gov.in with the caption DAK ADALAT, on or before 22.09.2019. They may also attend the Dak Adalat in person on 25.09.2019 at 1500 hours.
