      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Abrogation of Article 370 is historical;Our government is committed to the development of Minorities and SC/ST's : Ramadas Athawale
      Reviewed the progress of social welfare schemes in Telangana

      Posted On: 20 SEP 2019 7:11PM by PIB Hyderabad

    Union Minister of State for Social Justice & Empowerment Shri Ramdas Athawale said Dr. B.R. Ambedkar was against the article 370 and believed in One Nation and One Constitution.


    Minister praised Prime Minister in taking historical decisions to scrape Article 370. He acclaimed about various Welfare Programmes launched by Union Government. In the last 5 years Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) 88,19,184 houses were approved and 2,11,939 houses are sanctioned and under PMJDY near about 37 cr bank accounts were opened and near about 79 lakh bank accounts were opened in Telangana. 10, 75,166 ujwala yojana beneficiaries are in Telangana, the Minister added.


    The Minister requested the Telangana Government to control the SC/ST Atrocities in the state, which are alarmingly rising in the state.


    The Minister also spoke about the steps to Make India free of manual scavenging.There are nearly 53,000 people involved in manual scavenging throughout the country. We are giving Rs. 40,000 to each individual so that they can start some work on their own, so that no one is left doing this work down in next 5 years he stated.


    Minister also asserted that there is no need for debate on reservation in the country and said that Union Government should not be perceived as anti Minority. He urged the youth to promote inter-caste marriages to end casteism.


    Earlier, the Union Minister Ramdas Athawale reviewed the progress of social welfare schemes in Telangana with the senior officials of Telangana State.
