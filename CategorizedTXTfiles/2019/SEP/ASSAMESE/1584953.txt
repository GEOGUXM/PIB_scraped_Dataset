      Ministry of Micro,Small & Medium Enterprises

      NSIC employees donates Rs. 1.19 lakh to CM’s Relief Fund
      Posted On: 12 SEP 2019 12:57PM by PIB Guwahati


    


    NSIC employee’s contribution to the Chief Minister’s Relief Fund Assam


    NSIC employees of Guwahati in support from other employees across the country donated a sum of Rs. 1.19 lakh to the Chief Minister’s Relief Fund Assam recently.


    Sri P. Udayakumar, Director (Planning & Marketing), NSIC handed over the cheque to Sri Sarbananda Sonowal, Chief Minister of Assam in presence of Dr. Avinash Choudhary, Zonal General Manager, NSIC (North East). Chief Minister of Assam thanked for the generous contribution by the employees of NSIC and requested for continued support for the development of the state.


    The National Small Industries Corporation Limited, is a Government of India Enterprises under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises for the development of MSME sector of the country. Sri P. Udayakumar, Director informed the Chief Minister of Assam about NSIC’s role to serve the MSME sector of the North East through various schemes like – Raw Material Assistance Scheme, Raw Material Distribution Scheme, Skill Development Training etc.
