      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Regional Outreach Bureau celebrates 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi
      Posted On: 27 SEP 2019 11:48AM by PIB Guwahati


    Regional Outreach Bureau, Guwahati under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting celebrates the 150 th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi from 24 th September. The Director General, North East Zone Shri. L R Vishwanath has decided that the mobile exhibition will move in and around Guwahati till 2nd October 2019. As part of this mobile exhibition vans on the theme of Gandhi put up in different parts of the city. Simultaneously ten troupes of ROB Guwahati are performing skits and other cultural performances on the theme of SwachhtaAbhiyan, Plastic Free India and Fit India in and around Guwahati city.


    One Mega Exhibition on the 150 th Birth Anniversary of Gandhiji is also planned during 14 th -20 th October in the city.
