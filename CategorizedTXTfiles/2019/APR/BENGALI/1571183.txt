      Ministry of Civil Aviation

      FREE HEALTH CHECKUP CAMP ORGANIZED AT NSCBI AIRPORT KOLKATA
      Posted On: 26 APR 2019 1:29PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, Friday, April 26, 2019


    A one-day medical health check-up camp was held at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport on 24th April 2019 in association with Dafodil Hospitals Pvt. Ltd. at the meeting hall of New Operational Building of Airports Authority of India, Kolkata. Health check-ups at the medical camp included Blood Sugar, ECG and P.F.T test etc. for the employees, dependents and workers engaged by contractors of AAI. The team of Dr. Raktimavo Sarkar (MD) and Dr. Bipul Roy (MD) led the camp and motivated employees to keep periodic check on health to ensure healthy life. Total 184 members participated in the camp and were benefited by the various tests and consultation provided by the senior doctors.
