      Ministry of Defence

      NATIONWIDE AIR FORCE CENTENARY MARATHON 2019
      Posted On: 10 APR 2019 4:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: 10 April 2019


    A Centenary Half Marathon is planned to be organized on 14 Apr 2019 at all Air Force stations nationwide, including Air Force Station Barrackpore, to propagate the accomplishments of the legendary Marshal of the Indian Air Force among the Air Force Personnel and also to inculcate camaraderie and sportsmanship.


    The mega event is aimed at participation of over 100 air warriors each at 100 Air Force stations running 21 kms simultaneously, making it one of the largest participated events involving more than 10,000 Indian Air Force personnel running at the same time across the length and breadth of the nation. Air Force station Barrackpore is a major Air base in the Eastern Air Command, where the event is being organized under the command of Group Captain AS Parandekar.


    Marshal of the IAF Arjan Singh DFC is an icon of India’s military history who had assiduously led the IAF during 1965 war. He was awarded ‘Padma Vibhushan’’ for his astute leadership of the IAF during the war. A fearless and exceptional pilot who had flown over 60 different types of aircraft and kept his flying currency till his CAS rank. Subsequently, he also served as a diplomat & advisor to the Indian Govt. He has been a source of inspiration to all personnel of Armed Forces.
