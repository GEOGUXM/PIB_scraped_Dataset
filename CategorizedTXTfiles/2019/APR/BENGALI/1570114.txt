      Every polling booth to display important contact and helpline numbers on polling day
      Posted On: 05 APR 2019 6:44PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 5, 2019


    Every polling booth will be provided with signages containing necessary information for voters in the upcoming Lok Sabha 2019 election, informed Shri Sanjay Basu, Additional CEO, West Bengal. Keeping up with the spirit of providing assured minimum facilities to the voters, contact numbers of District Electoral Officer, Returning Officer, BLO, sectoral and mobile teamâ€™s contact number as well voter helpline number 1950 will be displayed in the booths. This function of sharing important numbers with voters in all the polling booths of that parliamentary constituency is being done in an elaborate manner this time, stated the Addl. CEO. These important numbers pertaining to that particular parliamentary constituency will be pasted in all the booths on the day before polls are to be conducted. The signages will include permanent information which stands for Assembly Constituency Number and Polling Station Number.


    Further, information related to necessary and alternate documents which can be carried as ID proof for casting vote will also be displayed in every booth. Also, guidelines on how to vote using EVM and VVPAT will be displayed in every booth.
