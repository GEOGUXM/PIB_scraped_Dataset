      Ministry of Science & Technology

      TREE PLANTATION CEREMONY OF CSIR-CMERI
      Posted On: 11 APR 2019 4:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 11, 2019


    Council of Scientific and Industrial Research & Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute (CSRI-CMERI), Durgapur organized a plantation ceremony on 10 th April 2019 for the development of an Organic Fruit Orchard/Plantation in the premises of CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur as a part of the ‘Clean and Green India Initiative’. The Plantation Ceremony was inaugurated by Shri Mrinal Kanti Mandal, Divisional Forest Officer, WBFS, the Chief Guest and Prof. (Dr.) Harish Hirani, Director, CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur. Around 150 trees were planted on this occasion of various species such as Mango, Papaya, Jackfruit, Amla, Coconut etc.


    Prof. (Dr.) Harish Hirani, stated that CSIR-CMERI will be using the High Density Plantation System (HDPS) for the inaugurated Organic Fruit Orchard which will strictly adhere to the National Standards for Organic Production (NSOP).. Dr. Hirani also charted out his vision of planting 500 trees by December 2019, which will be in proportion to the total number of employees by that time.


    Shri Mrinal Kanti Mandal, Divisional Forest Officer, WBFS, the Chief Guest, appreciated the wonderful initiative by CSIR-CMERI. This activity of CSIR-CMERI is in perfect tandem with the National Forest Policy which intends to increase the National Forest Cover to 33%, he mentioned.
