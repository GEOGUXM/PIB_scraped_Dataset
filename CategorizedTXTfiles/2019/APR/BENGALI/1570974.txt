      Election Commission

      5 Lok Sabha Constituencies of West Bengal to go to Polls in Third Phase Tomorrow
      Posted On: 22 APR 2019 4:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 22, 2019


    In the third phase of General election, 116 Lok Sabha constituencies across 13 States and two Union Territories, including five constituencies of West Bengal-Balurghat, Maldaha Uttar, Maldaha Dakshin, Jangipur and Murshidabad will have poll tomorrow (April 23, 2019). The polling in West Bengal will start at 7 am and continue till 6 pm.


    A total 61 candidates from West Bengal are contesting in this phase from 11 parties including Independent other registered parties. Out of these 61 candidates, six are women. There are 8528 polling booths in West Bengal in this phase of election. The total electoral number in West Bengal in this phase of poll is 8,023,846. The number of male voters is 4,106,010, the number of females, 3,917,624 and third gender 212.


    The Additional Chief Electoral Officer, West Bengal, Shri Sanjoy Basu recently stated that more than 92 per cent of the booths in West Bengal will be provided with Central Armed Police Force in the third phase of election.


    The Special Observer, Shri Ajay V Nayak too stated that West Bengal is progressing in a positive manner and things are improving in terms of election management and overall conduct of election process.


    The Commission has already impressed upon all the observers to remain on constant vigil and ensure that the polls are conducted in an absolutely free, fair and credible manner.
