      Election Commission Directs Repoll in 181 No. Polling Station of Coochbehar (SC) PC
      
      Average Poll Percentage in West Bengal for Third Phase of Lok Sabha Poll, 2019, is 81.97

      Posted On: 25 APR 2019 10:40AM by PIB Kolkata

    Kolkata, April 24, 2019


    Five Parliamentary constituencies among 116 covering 13 States and two Union Territories had poll in the third phase of General Election to Lok Sabha, 2019 on Tuesday (April 24, 2019). The average percentage of polling till 6 pm was 81.97. The poll percentage of Balurghat was 83.61, whereas, the percentages in Maldaha Uttar, Maldaha Dakshin, Jangipur and Murshidabad was 80.28, 81.01, 80.69 and 84.33 respectively.


    The Additional Chief Electoral Officer Shri Sanjoy Basu today stated that after scrutiny and validation, the Election Commission has directed repolling in 181 no. polling Station of Sitalkuchi Assembly segment of Coochbehar (SC) constituency. Repolling will be done on April 29, 2019 from 7 am to 6 pm. It is to be mentioned that this particular polling station had poll in the first phase of the General Election on April 11, 2019.
