      ECI Comes With Key Rings, Badges with Voter Awareness Messages
      Posted On: 04 APR 2019 5:05PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, April 4, 2019


    The Election Commission of India has come with some token merchandise like key rings, badges with voter awareness messages on them. Some of them have messages like ‘Desh ka Maha Tauhar’, ‘Veni, Vidi, Vote’, ‘Every Vote Counts, #GoVote’ and the like. These token articles will be sent to different locations where polling booths are nearby, so that they can be distributed among the citizens. Citizens, particularly, the new voters will be sensitised by this initiative, so that they get interested in exercising their franchise.


    The Additional Chief Electoral Officer, Shri Sanjoy Basu informed that the messages in Bengali too will be distributed among the electors residing near the booths before the poll. He also said that audio-visual presentation of sign languages prepared for helping voters, who suffer from hearing impairedness to cast their votes. Besides, wheel chairs will be there in booths for helping the people with locomotive disorder.


    The CEO’s Office in West Bengal has already appointed Jeeja Ghosh, the disability activist as State icon. Besides, mascots like Fajlibabu in Malda and Gutipishi’s Sahajpath in Murshidabad district or Bhottu, the owl to inspire electors to cast their votes so that no voter is left behind have already earned popularity.
