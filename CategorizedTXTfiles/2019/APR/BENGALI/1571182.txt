      Ministry of Railways

      CHAIRMAN, RAILWAY BOARD HOLDS REVIEW MEETING WITH GENERAL MANAGER, S E RAILWAY
      Posted On: 26 APR 2019 1:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 April, 2019


    Sri Vinod Kumar Yadav, Chairman, Railway Board & Ex-Officio Principal Secretary, Govt. of India, Ministry of Railways visited S E Railway Headquarters, Garden Reach on April 25, 2019 and held a high level meeting to review the performance of South Eastern Railway (SER). Sri P. S. Mishra, General Manager, SER, all Principal Head of the Departments and other senior officers were present at the meeting.


    Sri Yadav reviewed all the aspects of railway working and laid special emphasis on safety, punctuality, freight loading and earnings. Chairman, Railway Board also stressed on infrastructure development works viz. new lines, doubling, electrification etc.


    Sri Mishra, apprised the Chairman, Railway Board about the achievements and key activities of SER in the year 2018-19 and target for the year 2019-20. He also mentioned about the planning and progress of SER on originating freight & passenger earnings, infrastructure developmental projects, track renovation and railway electrification works.


    Shri Yadav expressed satisfaction about the all round performance of SER and stressed upon improvement of passenger amenities. Chairman, Railway Board also mentioned about the measures taken by railway on staff welfare.
