      IGCAR FOUNDATION DAY, April 30, 2019
      Posted On: 30 APR 2019 5:52PM by PIB Chennai


    Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research (IGCAR), the second largest R&D unit of the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), located at Kalpakkam celebrated its foundation day on April 30, 2019. IGCAR has been formed on April 30, 1971 by an executive order by Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, the then Chairman, Atomic Energy Commission. The event also coincides with the birth centenary of Dr. Vikram Sarabhai.


    Dr. R. Chidambaram, DAE- Homi Bhabha Chair Professor & Former Principal Scientific Advisor, Government of India was the chief guest for the event. Dr A K Bhaduri Director IGCAR welcomed the gathering. On this occasion, Shri K. N. Vyas Secretary, DAE presided over the function and inaugurated the 50th campaign of CORAL facility. A memoir cataloguing some of the major milestones of the centre over the last forty eight years was released by Shri S.K. Sharma, Chairman & Managing Director NPCIL. Former Directors of IGCAR Dr. P. R. Vasudeva Rao, Vice Chancellor, HBNI and Dr. S. A.V. Satya Murty also participated in the function.
