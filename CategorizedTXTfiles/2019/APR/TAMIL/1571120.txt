      Shri P. Mahesh assumes charge as new Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division
      Posted On: 24 APR 2019 5:18PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri.P.Mahesh, IRSME has taken over charge as the new Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division of Southern Railway today, 24 th of April, 2019 .


    An officer belonging to Indian Railway Service of Mechanical Engineers (IRSME), Shri P.Mahesh has vast experience in different facets of Railway working. He had served Southern Railway, North Frontier Railway and Integral Coach Factory in various capacities earlier.


    Shri P.Mahesh has the unique distinction of having held the post of Chief Workshop Manager in three prestigious workshops of Indian Railways, namely, Golden Rock Workshop, Carriage & Wagon Workshops at Perambur and New Bongaigon (Assam). Besides, he had also worked as Consultant with RITES while on deputation in Sri Lanka.


    Shri P.Mahesh, IRSME succeeds Shri Naveen Gulati as the Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division of Southern Railway.
