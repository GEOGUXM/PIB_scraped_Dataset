      Shri B Ilangovan assumes charge as Additional Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division
      Posted On: 26 APR 2019 2:09PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri.B.Ilangovan IRAS has taken over charge as the new Additional Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division of Southern Railway today, the 26 th April, 2019.


    As an officer belongs to Indian Railway Accounts Service (IRAS), 1999 batch, Shri.Ilangovan has vast experience in different facets of Railway working. He had served in various capacities including Sr. Divisional Finance Manager for Madurai Railway Division, Dy. General Manager of Southern Railway, Dy. Financial Advisor & Chief Accounts Officer of construction, Workshop, Stores of Southern Railways. Before assumes charge as Addl. Divisional Railway Manager he worked as Senior Divisional Finance Manager of Chennai Division.


    Shri.B.Ilangovan IRAS succeeds Shri.Sunil.P.Sorte as the Addl.Divisional Railway Manager of Chennai Division of Southern Railway.
