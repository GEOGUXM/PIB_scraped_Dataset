      Maharashtra registers 57% voting in 4th and final phase of Loksabha poll
      Posted On: 29 APR 2019 7:54PM by PIB Mumbai


    Mumbai- 29 April 2019


    In Maharashtra the voting for 4 th and final phase for LokSabha was held peacefully. The voting for all constituencies was over in Maharashtra. In the last phase in 17 LokSabha constituencies average 57% voting was registered and the overall voting percentage in state was 60=68%.

      * In urban areas voters including celebrities enthusiastically exercise their voting right
      * The voting percentage was good in cities
      * In Maharashtra Thane Loksabha constituency was the largest and Mumbai South-Central was the smallest in terms of number of voters.
      * In Gadchiroli-Chimur the largest i.e. 71.98% voter turnout was registered and in Kalyan the voters turnout was lowest i.e. 44.27%
