      President’s greetings on the eve of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi, Puthandu Pirappu
      Posted On: 13 APR 2019 4:52PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi, Puthandu Pirappu, which are being celebrated on 14 th and 15 th April, 2019, has said:-


    “On the auspicious occasion of Vaisakhi, Vishu, Rongali Bihu, Naba Barsha, Vaisakhadi and Puthandu Pirappu, I extend my warm greetings and good wishes to all fellow citizens in India and abroad.


    These harvest festivals are a celebration of the hard work and efforts of our farmers. Celebrated in different parts of the country, these festivals present a glimpse of India’s rich and diverse culture.


    May this joyous occasion, inspire us to do our best for the nation’s progress and to strengthen fraternity among people across various regions of the country”.
