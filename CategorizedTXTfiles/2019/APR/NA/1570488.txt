      President’s Ram Navami Greetings
      Posted On: 12 APR 2019 4:36PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of Ram Navami has said: -


    “On the occasion of Ram Navami, I extend my greetings and best wishes to all fellow citizens.


    The life of Maryada Purushottam Ram inspires us to follow his footsteps and strive for nobility in thought, words and deeds. Let us imbibe these values in the service of a prosperous nation and a harmonious world.”
