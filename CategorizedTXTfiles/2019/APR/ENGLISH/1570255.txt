      Election Commission

      India votes in the First Phase of Lok Sabha Elections-2019 on 11th April, 2019
      
      91 parliamentary constituencies in 20 states and UTs to go to polls
      
      Voting for All Loksabha seats in Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Uttarakhand, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Lakshadweep, Andaman and Nicobar Islands and 17 seats in Telangana to take place on Thursday
      
      Assembly Elections for Andhra Pradesh, Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh & first phase of Odisha Assembly polls to be held on 11th April

      Posted On: 09 APR 2019 2:49PM by PIB Delhi

    India votes in the first phase of General elections-2019 on Thursday , 11 th April. 91 out of 543 Lok Sabha constituencies are in the fray in this round.


    Voting will take place for all the parliamentary constituencies in Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Uttarakhand, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Lakshadweep, Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Telangana on Thursday.


    The other states going to polls in the first phase include - Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jammu and Kashmir, Maharashtra, Manipur, Odisha, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal. In these states, votes will be cast for some of the constituencies in the first phase of general elections.
