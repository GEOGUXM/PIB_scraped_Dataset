      Lakshadweep to go to polls on April 11, 2019
      Posted On: 09 APR 2019 4:51PM by PIB Delhi


    The picturesque islands of Lakshadweep will go to polls in the first phase of the General Elections on April 11, 2019. The only Parliamentary constituency in the Islands has a total electorate of 54266, out of which 27,475 are male electors and 26,791 are female electors. The number of first time electors in the age group 18-19 years is 2337, which is about 4.3% of the total number of electors in the union territory. There are 49 polling stations, set up to facilitate the voting process.


    The Lakshadweep parliamentary constituency is reserved for Scheduled Tribes.
