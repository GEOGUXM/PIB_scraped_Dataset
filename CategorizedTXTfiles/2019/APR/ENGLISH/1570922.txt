      PM greets people on Easter
      Posted On: 21 APR 2019 10:18AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Easter.
    
    “Greetings on Easter! The sacred thoughts of Lord Jesus Christ inspire millions. He emphasised on making our society free from evils and being compassionate towards the poor and downtrodden. May this special day enhance the spirit of peace and togetherness,” the Prime Minister said.
