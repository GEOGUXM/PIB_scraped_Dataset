      Election Commission

      14 out of 28 Lok Sabha seats in Karnataka will go for voting tomorrow in the Second phase on the 18th April 2019
      Posted On: 17 APR 2019 1:24PM by PIB Delhi


    The State of Karnataka will go to the polls tomorrow on 18 th April 2019 in Phase 02 of the General Elections. Out of total 28, only14 Parliamentary constituencies in the state are scheduled to go to polls in a second phase. The state has two-phase polling. The State has a total electorate of 2,63,38,277 in this phase out of which 1,33,52,234 are male electors, 1,29,83,284 are female electors and 2,759 belong to third gender category. There are 30,410 polling stations set up in the state in this phase.


    In this phase there are a total of 241 candidates ( out of which 17 are women) in the fray.
