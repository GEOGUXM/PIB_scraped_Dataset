      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people on the occasion of Ram Navami
      Posted On: 13 APR 2019 10:14AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Ram Navami.


    Prime Minister said, “ रामनवमी के पावन पर्व पर सभी देशवासियों को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। जय श्रीराम!”
