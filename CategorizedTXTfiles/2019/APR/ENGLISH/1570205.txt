      Election Commission

      Meghalaya goes to polls in Phase 1 on 11th April
      
      9 Candidates in the fray for Shillong & Tura Lok Sabha seats
      
      3,167 polling stations set up for smooth conduct of elections
      Posted On: 08 APR 2019 7:08PM by PIB Delhi


    Meghalaya goes to polls in the 1 st Phase of General Elections-2019. Voting to take place for two Parliamentary Constituencies of Shillong & Tura on 11 th April.


    Total number of voters in the state is 1892716. These include 936579 males and 956137 females.


    Nine candidates, including one woman, will be contesting for 2 Lok Sabha seats.


    There will be 3,167 Polling Stations spread over the entire State.
