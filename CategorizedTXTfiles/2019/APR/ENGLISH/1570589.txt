      Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA)

      Cabinet approves extension of duration of New Urea Policy-2015 for existing gas based urea unit
      Posted On: 15 APR 2019 12:31PM by PIB Delhi


    The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs, chaired by Hon'ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved the proposal of the Department of fertilizers to extend the duration of New Urea Policy-2015 from April 1, 2019 till further orders, except for the provisions which stand already amended vide notification dated March 28, 2018.


    It will facilitate to continue operations and regular supply of urea to the farmers.
