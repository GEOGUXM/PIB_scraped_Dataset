      General Elections 2019 for Tamilnadu will be held in the Second phase on April 18th, 2019
      Posted On: 10 APR 2019 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


    The State of Tamilnadu will go to polls in the Phase 2 of the General Elections on April 18 th 2019. All 39 Parliamentary constituencies in the state are scheduled to go to polls for the seats to Lok Sabha in a single phase and Bye-elections to legislative assemblies for 18 Assembly constituencies are also held on the same day. The State has a total electorate of 5,91,23,197 out of which 2,92,56,960 are male electors, 2,98,60,765 are female electors and 5,472 belong to others category. There are 67,664 polling stations set up in the state.
