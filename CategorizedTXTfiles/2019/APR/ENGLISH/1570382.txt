      Election Commission

      Puducherry to go to polls in the Second phase on April 18th, 2019
      Posted On: 10 APR 2019 4:58PM by PIB Delhi


    Puducherry will go to polls for General Elections 2019 in a single Phase. The single parliamentary constituency is to vote on 18 th April 2019. There are 9 , 73,161 total electors in the union territory with 4,59,266 male electors, 5,13,799 female electors and 96 in others category as on 26.03.2019. Number of polling stations in the union territory is 970.
