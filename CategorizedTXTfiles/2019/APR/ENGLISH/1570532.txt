      Vice President greets people on Ram Navami
      Posted On: 12 APR 2019 10:38AM by PIB Delhi


    The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted people on Ram Navami. In a message, he said that Lord Rama was the personification of virtue, righteousness, courage and compassion. He stood for the values of truth, respect for parents, kindness and love for all living beings, he added.


    Following is the text of Vice President's message:


    "I convey my warm greetings and good wishes to the people of our country on the auspicious occasion of ‘Ram Navami’.


    Lord Rama was the personification of virtue, righteousness, courage and compassion. He stood for the values of truth, respect for parents, kindness and love for all living beings. The ‘ Maryada purushottama ’ is revered as the perfect king, the ideal hero and a role model in the truest sense of the word. His resolute commitment to justice, good governance and welfare of his people will continue to inspire us.


    May this festival lead us to walk the path he illumined and create a world permeated by the ideals he lived."
