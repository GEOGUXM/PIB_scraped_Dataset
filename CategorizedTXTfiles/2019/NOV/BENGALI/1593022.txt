      EASTERN ARMY COMMANDER VISITS MIZORAM
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 1:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, November 22, 2019


    

    

    Lt Gen Anil Chauhan, General Officer Commanding-in-Chief Eastern Command, visited Mizoram on Wednesday and Thursday (November 20 & 21, 2019).


    

    During the visit, the Army Commander held meetings with Shri PS Sreedharan Pillai, Hon'ble Governor at Raj Bhawan and Shri Zoramthanga, Chief Minister of Mizoram. He apprised them of the security related issues and assured them of all possible assistance by the Armed Forces to the state Government.
    
    
    The Army Commander accompanied by senior military officers also visited forward posts along the Indo - Myanmar Border and interacted with troops deployed there. The Army Commander commended them for their professionalism & dedication to duty and exhorted them to continue the good work and live up to the motto of 'Friends of the North East'. He commended the troops of Assam Rifles for their dedication to duty and praised their role in ensuring peace and tranquility in the region.
