      Aadhaar Services Easier Now
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2019 4:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, November 19, 2019.


    
    
    
    
    "Aadhaar Seva Kendra" at Sector-5, Bidhan Nagar, has started working. This Aadhaar Seva Kendra has capacity to facilitate 1000 residents per day.
    
    People may visit this Kendra for Aadhaar Enrollment, Update or correction in Name, Address, Gender, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, E-mail, Photo and Biometric update i.e. update in finger prints and iris.
    
    UIDAI has facilitated people to book an online appointment through its website from ask.uidai.gov.in . This Aadhaar Seva Kendra will remain open on all week days from morning 9.30 to evening 5.30 hours.


    
    The new enrolment and mandatory biometric update for the children at the age of 05 and 1 5 is free and for any other correction, a nominal fee of Rs. 50/- has been fixed. Any resident may avail new enrolment, update/mandatory biometric update or correction facilities available at this Aadhaar Seva Kendra.
