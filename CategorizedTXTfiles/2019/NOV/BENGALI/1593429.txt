      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Director General (Eastern Zone), Ministry of I & B Assumes Charge
      Posted On: 25 NOV 2019 5:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, November 25, 2019


    
    
    
    Shri Rabindra Nath Mishra, an Indian Information Service (IIS) officer of Batch of 1987, has assumed the charge of Director General (Eastern Zone), Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, at the office of PIB Kolkata today. Prior to this, Shri Mishra was the Director General (Western Zone), Ministry of Information & Broadcasting (I&B).

    

    During his three decades service in IIS, Shri Mishra has served in News Services Division, All India Radio and DD News, New Delhi, PIB New Delhi, PIB Srinagar, PIB Raipur and several other organizations under the Ministry of I&B. He has also served as Special Correspondent of Prasar Bharati in Kathmandu also. As DG (Eastern Zone), Shri Mishra will be in charge of the functioning of the offices of both Press Information Bureau and Regional Outreach Bureau in the Eastern Zone, i.e., Jharkhand, Bihar and Orissa apart from West Bengal.
