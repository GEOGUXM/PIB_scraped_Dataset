      Ministry of Science & Technology

      IISF: PROGRAMME ON TRADITIONAL INDIAN CRAFT – STOREHOUSE OF SCIENTIFIC KNOWLEDGE
      Posted On: 07 NOV 2019 5:49PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 07 November, 2019


    As a part of the 5th India International Science Festival a day long conference, was held today at Science City on the topic of Traditional Indian Craft – Storehouse of Scientific Knowledge.


    In the inaugural session of this programme Dr. Saroj Ghose, Padma Bhushan, Former President ICOM, Former Director General, NCSM said, Traditional craft is a storehouse of scientific knowledge. He stressed on the fact - science and craft are interdependent. Addressing on this programme Shri A. B. Chowdhury, Director General, NCSM, Kolkata said a science city will be built at Guwahati in near future. Prof. Vashant Shinde, eminent Archaeologist in his keynote address on the topic ‘Origin and Development of Basic Science and Technology: An Archaeological Approach’. He said that our modern civilization is based largely on the Harappan Civilization. Classical flutist Dr. Pt. Keshav Ginde also addressed the gathering on the topic ‘Keshav Venue: Innovating towards an ideal flute’.
