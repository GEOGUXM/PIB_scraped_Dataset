      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Governor pays tribute to Loknayak Birsa Munda
      Posted On: 15 NOV 2019 6:00PM by PIB Mumbai


    Maharashtra Governor Bhagat Singh Koshyari garlanded the photograph of Loknayak Birsa Munda on the occasion of 144 th birth anniversary of the great revolutionary freedom fighter at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai on Friday (15 th Nov).


    Principal Secretary of Tribal Development Department Manisha Varma, Principal Secretary Rural Development Aseem Kumar Gupta and Principal Secretary to the Governor Santosh Kumar were present. A presentation on Tribal Development Department and Rural Development department was made on the occasion.
