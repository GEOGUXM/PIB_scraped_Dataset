      Films Divisions celebrating Constitution Day on 26th November
      
      Films Division organized a screening of three documentaries on the constitution Day
      Posted On: 25 NOV 2019 6:37PM by PIB Mumbai


    Films Division, GOI, Mumbai is celebrating as part of “ Constitution Day “ on 26 th November 2019. Three documentaries viz. ‘Our Constitution’, ‘India’s Struggle for Freedom: We the People of India’ and ‘Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar’ will be the screening on tomorrow. The screening will be widely publicized and will be free and open to all. The screening will be held in J.B. Hall, Films Division, Mumbai on 26 th November at @2.30 pm.
