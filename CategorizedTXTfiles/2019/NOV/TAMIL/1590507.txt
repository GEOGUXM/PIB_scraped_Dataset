      5.6 kg Gold worth Rs. 2.24 Crores seized from Air India aircraft by Customs at Chennai Airport.
      Posted On: 05 NOV 2019 6:32PM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday based on information that gold was likely to be smuggled by way of concealment in aircraft arriving from Dubai , a team of AIU officers carried out rummaging of Air India flight AI-906 which had arrived from Dubai. This International flight was converted into flight AI 440 and was to fly to Delhi on its domestic run. During rummaging four bundles wrapped in black adhesive tape were found concealed in rear toilet of the ai,rcraft. On cut opening the bundles, 48 gold bars of 10 Tola each with foreign markings totally weighing 5.6 Kg valued at Rs. 2.24 Crore were recovered and the same were seized as unclaimed under the provision of Customs Act, 1962. Further Investigation is under progress according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
