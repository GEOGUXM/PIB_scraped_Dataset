      11 Selected: Job Fair in Chennai
      Posted On: 04 NOV 2019 6:04PM by PIB Chennai


    The National Career Service Centre for SC/ST, Directorate General of Employment (DGE) Government of India, Ministry of Labour and Employment, Santhome High Road, Chennai-4 had organized a “JOB FAIR/ROJGAR MELA” in collaboration with M/s. Workfreaks Corporate Services Private Ltd., Chennai on dated 30-10-2019 at office premises.


    Around 150 candidates belonging to different categories and Employers from the areas of BPO/Banking/Insurance/E-Commerce/Education/IT/Advertising and FMCG/HR/Marketing and Sales etc. participated in the Job Fair.


    Out of 150 candidates, 11 candidates were directly selected and 60 were shortlisted for next stage for various posts available in companies. Sri Sujit Kumar Sahoo, Sub Regional Employment Officer, Smt. Bhavani Rajendran, Assistant Employment Officer of NCSC for SC/ST, and Shri Syed Nasrullah, Director, Workfreaks Corporate Service Pvt. Limited has successfully organized the programme, according to a press release issued by Shri Sujit Kumar Sahoo, Sub Regional Employment Officer, Chennai.
