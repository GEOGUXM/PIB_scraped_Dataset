      CMFRI to Host three day Food and Aqua-Agri Fest
      Posted On: 13 NOV 2019 4:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute (CMFRI) is hosting a food and aqua-agri festival from tomorrow (14.11.2019). Farmed live fish, Lakshadweep varieties, tilapia live kitchen and facility for on the spot easy bank loan will be some of the attractions of the festival. The three-day event will also offer a range of quality products produced by organic farmers, farmer producer companies.


    The fest will have a special section for organic products from Lakshadweep, including sweets, value addition from tuna, virgin coconut oil, etc. In addition to the display of a wide array of organic products, the fest will have pure and high-quality spices, value added products, different types of oils, natural cosmetic products, curry powders and so on produced by farmer groups under the guidance of central research institutions. Organic vegetables, pokkali rice, pokkali rice powder, value addition from fish, seeds of turmeric and ginger and plants of pepper will also be available at the fest.


    Exhibition of farming technologies and machineries will provide a ringside view of inputs as well as material for those interested in taking up farming activities. A special pavilion at the fest is designed for easy bank loan that will extend support those interested in taking up enterprises in agriculture and allied areas. Besides, a buyer seller meet will be arranged under the guidance of the NABARD. Panel discussions on relevant topics in agriculture, fisheries, animal husbandry and safety of food will also be held during the event. The time of the festival is from 10 am to 9 pm, and entry to the fest is free of cost.
