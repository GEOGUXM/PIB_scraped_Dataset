      Fundamentals of Indian economy are strong – Vice President
      
      India provides very attractive investment avenues– VP
      
      Interacts with the delegates of YPO Europe One
      
      Posted On: 09 NOV 2019 6:42PM by PIB Delhi


    he Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu said today that the fundamentals of Indian economy are strong and India offers very good investment opportunities.


    Shri Naidu made these remarks while interacting with the delegates of YPO, a global leadership community of Chief Executives, in New Delhi today.


    Forty-five members from 22 countries of YPO Europe One are visiting India. The Vice President invited these delegates to invest in India.
