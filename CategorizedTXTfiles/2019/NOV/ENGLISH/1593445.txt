      Ministry of Defence

      Indigenous Production by DRDO
      Posted On: 25 NOV 2019 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    There are 56 ongoing Mission Mode (MM) Projects with a cost of Rs 49424.54 cr. and 218 ongoing Technology Demonstration (TD) mode projects with a cost of Rs 13427.45 cr.


    Above projects are directed for 100 percent indigenous productions, however a few projects have collaboration with foreign countries during development phase.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Tiruchi Siva in Rajya Sabha today.
