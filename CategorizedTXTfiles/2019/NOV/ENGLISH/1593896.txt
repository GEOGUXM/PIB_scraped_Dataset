      Ministry of Railways

      Wi-Fi at Railway Stations
      Posted On: 27 NOV 2019 6:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Indian Railways have decided to provide Wi-Fi services at all Railway stations (except F category halt stations) over Indian Railways.


    Wi-Fi services have been provided at 5444 stations upto 20th November, 2019. Zone-wise details are as under:-
