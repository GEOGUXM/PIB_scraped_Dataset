      PM pays tributes to former PM Indira Gandhi on her birth anniversary
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2019 9:09AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister Smt. Indira Gandhi on her birth anniversary.


    "Tributes to our former PM Smt. Indira Gandhi Ji on her birth anniversary”, the Prime Minister said.
