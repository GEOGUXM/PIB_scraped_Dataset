      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Training to Women Athletes
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2019 4:36PM by PIB Delhi


    Sports Infrastructure facilities, including stadia, are constructed and developed for all athletes. Female athletes have equal and easy access to sports facilities, including stadia.


    At present, 4994 female athletes are being assisted for training under various schemes of Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. The information of women sportspersons undergoing training under various SAI schemes is at Annexure-I.
