      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Electric Buses
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2019 4:48PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has sanctioned 5095 electric buses to 64 Cities / State Transport Corporations for intra-city operation, 400 electric buses for intercity operation and 100 electric buses for last mile connectivity to Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) under Phase-II of FAME India Scheme.
