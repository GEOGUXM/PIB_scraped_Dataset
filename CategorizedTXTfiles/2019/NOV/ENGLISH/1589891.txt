      Prime Minister Greets Kerala on its Formation Day
      Posted On: 01 NOV 2019 10:34AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi today greeted people of Kerala on its formation day.


    The state was formed on 1 st November 1956, under the States Reorganisation Act.


    “ On Kerala Piravi day, greetings to my sisters and brothers of Kerala. People from this state have made remarkable contributions to the nation. May the people of the state be happy and prosperous” he said.
