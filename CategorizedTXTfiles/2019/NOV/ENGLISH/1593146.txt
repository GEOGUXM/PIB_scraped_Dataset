      Ministry of Railways

      Expediting Electrification of Railways
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 5:35PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Railways have prepared an Action Plan to electrify balance Broad Gauge (BG) Routes of Indian Railways. As on 01.11.2019, 37,237 Route Kilometers of rail lines have been commissioned on electric traction, which is 57.91% of total Indian Railwaysâ€™ BG network.     To expedite electrification of railway lines in the country, steps taken includes award of Engineering Procurement and Construction (EPC) contracts, better project monitoring mechanism, delegating more power to field units for award of contracts/sanction of estimates and close monitoring at highest level.


    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
