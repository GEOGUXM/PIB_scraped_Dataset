      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Research on Developing Newmedicines
      Posted On: 19 NOV 2019 3:15PM by PIB Delhi


    Research work on diseases like tuberculosis, malaria and dengue is being supported by Indian Council of Medical Research, D/o BioTechnology& D/o Science & Technology through various initiatives under National and International R&D programmes as indicated hereunder:-


     1. Launch of Malaria Elimination Research Alliance


     1. Special Call for research proposals on Dengue


      * III. Mission AMR,


      * IV. Major projects for development of vaccines for Malaria, Tuberculosis and Dengue,


     1. Malaria Vaccine Development Programmes,


      * VI. Dengue subunit VLP based vaccine candidate,

     1. Regional Prospective observational Research for Tuberculosis India under Indo-US vaccine Programme.

     1. Technology developed by ICGEB, New Delhi has been transferred to Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd. for development of phytopharmaceutical drug for dengue,


      * IX. Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council, a Public Sector undertaking of D/o Biotechnology also supports in R&D activities in these areas.


     1. Funding by D/o Science & Technology to Collaborative Projects under Public Private Partnership, creation of 57 state of the art infrastructure for Pharmaceutical R&D, Grants-in-aid to Indian Pharma Industries for clinical trials in neglected diseases including malaria, kala-azar etc.


    The Minister of State (Health and Family Welfare), ShAshwini Kumar Choubey stated this in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha here today.
