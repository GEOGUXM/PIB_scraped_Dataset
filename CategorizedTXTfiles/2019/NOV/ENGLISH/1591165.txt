      PM greets the nation on Milad-Un-Nabi
      Posted On: 10 NOV 2019 10:07AM by PIB Delhi

      Prime Minister Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the occasion of Milad-Un-Nabi today.
      Prime Minister said, " Greetings on Milad-Un-Nabi. Inspired by the thoughts of Prophet Muhammad, may this day further the spirit of harmony and compassion in society. May there be peace all around. "
