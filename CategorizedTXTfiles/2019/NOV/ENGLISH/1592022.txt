      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Targets fixed under PMUY Scheme already achieved
      Posted On: 18 NOV 2019 5:43PM by PIB Delhi


    The State/UT-wise annual LPG connections released under Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) including 11.05 lakh LPG customers with 5 Kg connections as on September 7th 2019, are at Annexure I .


    No State/District-wise targets have been fixed under PMUY. State/UTs-wise LPG connections released during the year 2019-20 across the country are at Annexure-II .


    The targets set under PMUY have already been achieved by the Government.
