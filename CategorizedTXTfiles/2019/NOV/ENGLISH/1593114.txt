      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      FRP dues to Sugarcane Growers
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 5:00PM by PIB Delhi


    Out of sugarcane dues payable of Rs.55247.24 crore on all India basis for sugar season 2016-17, sugarcane dues of Rs. 55188.54 crore on FRP basis have been paid to Sugarcane farmers. State-wise details of cane price payable and cane dues paid is at Annexure.


    More than 99% of sugarcane dues payable for sugar season 2016-17 on FRP basis have been credited into the bank accounts of sugarcane farmers; and only Rs.58.70 crore cane dues are outstanding.


    This information was given in a written reply by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri. Danve Raosaheb Dadarao in Rajya Sabha today.
