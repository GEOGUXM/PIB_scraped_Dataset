      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      Parliamentary Affairs Shri Pralhad Joshi holds A Meeting to discuss government legislations for the forthcoming winter session of Parliament
      Posted On: 05 NOV 2019 7:41PM by PIB Delhi


    A meeting was chaired by the Minister for Parliamentary Affairs, Coal and Mines Shri Pralhad Joshi today to discuss government legislations for the forthcoming winter session of Parliament. The Minister of State for Parliamentary Affairs and Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises, Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal and Minister of State for Parliamentary Affairs and External Affairs, Shri V Muraleedharan and Secretaries from various Ministries / Departments attended the meeting.


    Shri Pralhad Joshi urged all offi cials to complete all formalities and bring in the legislative business before the Parliament well within the stipulated time for their proper listing in the agenda. He also reviewed various pending bills before both the Houses of Parliament.


    Secretaries and senior officers from various ministries assured the Minister that they would come up before the Parliament with all the required documents for the forthcoming legislative business.
