      Ministry of Railways

      Security Cameras in Trains
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 5:38PM by PIB Delhi


    It has been decided to fit Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) cameras in trains / coaches of Indian Railways. Till date, CCTV cameras have been provided in more than 2,000 coaches in around 114 Mainline trains, 88 Electric Multiple Unit (EMU) rakes and 4 Mainline Electric Multiple Unit (MEMU) rakes.


    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
