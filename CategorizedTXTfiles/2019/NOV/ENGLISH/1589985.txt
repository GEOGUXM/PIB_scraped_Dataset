      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC approval
      Posted On: 01 NOV 2019 5:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The Competent Authority has approved assignment of additional charge of the post of Secretary (Security), Cabinet Secretariat to Shri Rajesh Bhushan, IAS (BH: 1987), Secretary (Coordination), Cabinet Secretariat with immediate effect and till the appointment of a regular incumbent or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
