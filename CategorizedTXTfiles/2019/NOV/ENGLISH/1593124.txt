      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      E- Commerce Guidelines for Consumers
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 5:08PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has drafted new rules to regulate e-commerce. The draft rules have been uploaded on the Department’s website for stakeholders’ consultation. The salient features of the draft e-commerce rules are focussed towards protecting the interests of the consumers before, during and after purchase from e-commerce companies.


    This information was given in a written reply by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution , Shri. Danve Raosaheb Dadarao in Rajya Sabha today.
