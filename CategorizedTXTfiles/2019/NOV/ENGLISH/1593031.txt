      Ministry of Textiles

      Integrated Skill Development Scheme in the Textile Sector
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 1:52PM by PIB Delhi


    A stakeholder meeting was organised under Samarth-Scheme for Capacity Building in Textiles sector (SCBTS) on 14.05.2018 to familiarize the stakeholders about the scheme and its guidelines. In addition, regular interactive meetings are taking place at regular intervals with stakeholders in the Ministry on Samarth Scheme Implementation. State Governments are also associated in the implementation of various other schemes/projects of Ministry of Textiles.


    Under Samarth Scheme for Capacity Building in Textiles sector(SCBTS), so far a total of 21 State Government agencies from 18 States have agreed to partner with the Ministry under the scheme. The Ministry has accorded in-principle approval for allocation of over 3.5 lakh targets to the agencies nominated by State Governments to undertake training programme under the schem e .
