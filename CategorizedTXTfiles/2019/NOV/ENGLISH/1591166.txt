      PM reviews situation arising due to Cyclone Bulbul
      Posted On: 10 NOV 2019 10:14AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi today reviewed the situation in the wake of cyclone conditions and heavy rain in parts of Eastern India. He also spoke to Chief Minister of West Bengal Ms. Mamata Banerjee regarding the situation arising due to Cyclone Bulbul and assured all possible assistance from the Centre.
