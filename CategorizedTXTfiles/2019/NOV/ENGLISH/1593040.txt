      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Anganwadi Centres in Tribal Sub-Plan
      Posted On: 22 NOV 2019 2:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has sanctioned 14 lakhs AWCs across the country. These include AWCs in tribal areas.


    The total number of Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in Tribal Sub-Plan areas reporting on ICDS-RRS portal is 1,34,806 with 69,14,204 registered beneficiaries.
