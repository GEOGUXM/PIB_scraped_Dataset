      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Foreign Minister of Bhutan calls on Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah in New Delhi
      
      Both leaders discuss bilateral ties and agree to strengthen it further

      Posted On: 18 NOV 2019 4:47PM by PIB Delhi

    The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bhutan, Shri T. Dorji, met the Union Minister for Home Affairs, Shri Amit Shah in a cordial atmosphere, in New Delhi today.


    Both the leaders discussed the historically close ties between the two nations and agreed to further strengthen the bilateral relationship in future.
