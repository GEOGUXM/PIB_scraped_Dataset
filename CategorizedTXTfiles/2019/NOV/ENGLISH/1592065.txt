      Ministry of Defence

      Recruitment of Women in the Army
      Posted On: 18 NOV 2019 7:10PM by PIB Delhi


    Indian Army has a plan for phased induction of women in below Officer Rank in the Corps of Military Police.Presently, there is no proposal for additional recruitment of women in Army.


    This information was given by Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh in a written reply to ShriS. Muthukaruppanin Rajya Sabha today.
