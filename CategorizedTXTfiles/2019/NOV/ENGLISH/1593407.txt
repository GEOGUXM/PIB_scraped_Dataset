      Ministry of Micro,Small & Medium Enterprises

      Marketing Competitiveness of MSMEs
      Posted On: 25 NOV 2019 4:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of MSME supports Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to enhance their marketing competitiveness through Procurement and Marketing Support Scheme, Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY) and scheme for International Cooperation.
