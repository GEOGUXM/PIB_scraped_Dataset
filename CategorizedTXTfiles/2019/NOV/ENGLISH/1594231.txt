      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Agarbatti Industry
      Posted On: 29 NOV 2019 4:16PM by PIB Delhi


    Imports of agarbatti are largely coming from Vietnam. Out of total 1,04,097 metric tonne (MT) of imports of agarbatti, imports from Vietnam was around 98,000 MT in 2018-19.


    By Notification dated 31.08.2019, import of agarbatti has been amended from free to restricted with immediate effect.


    This information was given by the Minister of Commerce and Industry, Piyush Goyal, in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today.
