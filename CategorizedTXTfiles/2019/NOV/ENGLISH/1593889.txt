      Ministry of Railways

      Revenue from Advertisements and Platform Tickets in Railways
      Posted On: 27 NOV 2019 6:47PM by PIB Delhi


    Rs. 139.20 crore were earned from the sale of platform tickets in the year 2018-19. Further, Rs.78.50 crore were earned from sale of platform tickets, up to September, 2019 during 2019-20.


    Rs. 230.47 crore were earned from advertisements and shops including those on platforms in the year 2018-19. Rs.128.40 crore were earned from advertisement and shops including those on platforms up to September, 2019 during 2019-20.


    As per the extant policy, contracts for shops and advertisements are awarded after open competitive bidding. The rates are outcome of the competition during the bid. However, in order to ensure minimum revenue, the bidding is done after finalizing minimum licence fee and the quotes are to be submitted above the same.


    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
