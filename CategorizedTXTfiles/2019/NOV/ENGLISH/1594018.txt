      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Ashram Schools in Tribal Sub-Plan Areas
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2019 4:17PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides financial support to the State Government for construction of Ashram schools under Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Plan (SCA to TSS). There responsibility for running and overall maintenance of schools constructed with the financial support provided to states under SCA to TSS, including provision of teachers rests with the respective state governments.
