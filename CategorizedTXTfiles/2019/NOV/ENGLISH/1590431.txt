      PM chairs a meeting on pollution and cyclone
      Posted On: 05 NOV 2019 3:02PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi chaired a meeting today to discuss the situation arising due to pollution in various part of Northern India.


    He also reviewed the situation arising due to cyclone conditions in parts of western India.
