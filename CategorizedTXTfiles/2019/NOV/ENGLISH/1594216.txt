      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Common Application Software
      Posted On: 29 NOV 2019 3:56PM by PIB Delhi


    ICDS-Common Application Software has been developed under POSHAN Abhiyaan which inter-alia captures data on weight and height of children, opening of AnganwadiCentres, distribution of Take Home Ration and attendance of children for pre-school education at Anganwadi.


    The Application also captures the Pre-School Education attendance provided to the Children (3-6 years) at therespective AnganwadiCentres. There is no plan for integration of data from the AWCs level to the school level, presently.


    As on 28.11.2019, the ICDS Common Application Software (ICDS-CAS) has been rolled out across 5,51,270 Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) across 324 districts in 27 States/UTs. The details are as under:
