      Ministry of Railways

      Occupancy of Vande Bharat Trains
      Posted On: 20 NOV 2019 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    At present, two pairs of Vande Bharat Trains (Train18) are operational on Delhi-Varanasi-Delhi route and Delhi- Sri Mata Vaishno Devi -Delhi route. The average occupancy of these two pairs of trains since its introduction upto 08.11.2019 are as under:-

                                                                  
Train No.  Route                                      % Occupancy*
                                                                  
                                                                  
                                                                  
22435/36   Delhi- Varanasi- Delhi                     115.71      
                                                                  
                                                                  
                                                                  
22439/40   Delhi - Sri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra-Delhi  99.27       
                                                                  
                                                                  

    *inclusive of bookings done for en-route/intermediate stations


    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
