      Election Commission announces Schedule for General Election to the Legislative Assembly of Jharkhand, 2019
      Posted On: 01 NOV 2019 6:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The Election Commission of India today announced Schedule for General Election to the Legislative Assembly of Jharkhand, 2019.


    Polling will be held in five phases i.e. on 30 th November, 2019, 7 th December 2019, 12 th December 2019, 16 th December 2019 , and 20 th December, 2019. Counting will be held on 23 rd December, 2019 The detailed schedule is attached herewith.
