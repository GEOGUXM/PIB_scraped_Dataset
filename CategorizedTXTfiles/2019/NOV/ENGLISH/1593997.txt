      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Stipend to Persons Engaged Under Various Schemes
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2019 3:40PM by PIB Delhi


    Only the student volunteers under Mahila Shakti Kendra scheme are paid stipend of Rs.50 per hour towards community service as per guidelines of the scheme. Stipend is not paid in schemes like Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS), Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS).


    Only one proposal has been received from State Government of Mizoram for pay upgradation for the staff of State Resource Centre for Women (SRCW) under Mahila Shakti Kendra. However, currently there is no plan for revision.


    Under ICDSthe scheme of, payment of salaries are made only to District Programme Officers(DPOs) and 1 Statistical Assistant each at District Level, CDPOs & 1 Statistical Assistant each at Project Level and Supervisors, at the following cost-sharing ratio between Centre and States / UTs :

                                           
States & UTs (with Legislature)      25:75 
                                           
                                           
                                           
8 North East and 3 Himalayan States  90:10 
                                           
                                           
                                           
UTs (without Legislature)            100:00
                                           
                                           

    However, no proposal for increasing salaries for the aforesaid officials under ICDS is under consideration presently.
