      Ministry of Textiles

      Weavers and Handlooms
      Posted On: 29 NOV 2019 3:40PM by PIB Delhi


    Under the scheme of Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS) handloom weavers are provided looms and accessories. Under HSS 12,983 number of weavers have been benefitted with 13,119 number of HSS items distributed during the last two years.


    Government of India is implementing converged Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana (MGBBY) across the country including Solapur district for enrolment of handloom weavers and workers. Five hundred number of handloom weavers and workers have been enrolled in Maharashtra State during the last three years under MGBBY.
