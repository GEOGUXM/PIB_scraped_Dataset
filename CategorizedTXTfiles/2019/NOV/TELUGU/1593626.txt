      CONSTITUTION DAY AT EFLU
      Posted On: 26 NOV 2019 7:21PM by PIB Hyderabad


    English and Foreign Languages University (EFLU), Hyderabad, celebrated the Constitution Day that commemorates the seventieth year of the adoption of the Constitution of India. Live telecast of Samvidhan Divas from Parliament Central Hall was watched by the Vice Chancellor, students, and members of teaching and non-teaching staff of the University.


    The audience watched attentively the addresses of the Hon’ble President of India,the Hon’ble Vice President of India, and the Hon’ble Prime Minster.


    The Preamble of the Constitution of India was administered by Prof. E. Suresh Kumar, Vice Chancellor of the University, wherein a large number of students and the members of teaching and non-teaching staff participated.
