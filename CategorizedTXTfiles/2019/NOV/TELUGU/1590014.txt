      EFFORTSTO REDUCE LITIGATION BY IT DEPARTMENT
      Posted On: 01 NOV 2019 6:32PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Income tax Department of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana region has withdrawn 969 appeals before Honâ€™ble Supreme Court, Andhra Pradesh High Court, High Court of Telangana and Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, as part of its drive to reduce litigation.


    A press note issued by the office of the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, informed that the cases were withdrawn in pursuance of Circular no 17/2019 issued by CBDT on August 8, 2019. The department will not contest cases with tax effect below Rs. 50 lakhs before ITAT, below Rs. 1 crore before High Court and below Rs. 2 crore before Supreme Court.
