      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Disbursement of Funds Under Rashtriya Mahila Kosh
      Posted On: 28 NOV 2019 3:41PM by PIB Delhi


    The total disbursement of funds under the Rashtriya Mahila Kosh from 2018, till date aggregated to Rs.1173 lakh.An amount of Rs. 50 lakh was disbursed to the North-eastern States from 2016 till date.
