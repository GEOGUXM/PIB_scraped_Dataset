      Ministry of Home Affairs

      OBSERVING “JAL SHAKTI ABHIYAN” BY BSF PERS
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2019 5:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 06, 2019


    On the sidelines of the "Jal Shakti Abhiyan" 2019 and to generate public awareness on water conservation, M&C Frontier HQ, BSF organized oath taking ceremony and tree plantation drive at HQ campuses and its far flung Border out-posts and villages with full enthusiasm.


    Besides above, motivational lectures on promotion of rain water harvesting, Govt. Schemes on water conservation etc were delivered at different border places. In addition, under the guidance of President, BSF Wives Welfare Association (BWWA), HQ BSF, Mizoram &Cachar Frontier, Silchar, organized a 'Drawing & Painting Competition' for the wards of BSF personnel at Masimpur campus.
