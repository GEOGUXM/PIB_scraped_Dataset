      Ministry of Railways

      “OPERATION NUMBER PLATE” – SPECIAL DRIVE IN RAILWAY PREMISES BEFORE INDEPENDENCE DAY
      Posted On: 13 AUG 2019 5:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 13, 2019


    Massive intensive Drives called "Operation Number Plate” were made all over South Eastern Railway during the period from 09.08.2019 to 11.08.2019 to identify and remove unclaimed/illegally parked vehicle close to Railway stations. All the four Divisions i.eKharagpur, Adra, Ranchi and Chakradharpur conducted raid on vehicles parked for more than five days in parking area and found 83 vehicleswere parked.


    Similarly, raids were also conducted on vehicles parked for more than one day in "no parking "area and found 69 vehicles were parked. As a result, 54 persons were arrested under section159 of Rly Act.andan amount of Rs. 2700/- was also realised as fine from 3 persons and other 51 persons were released on bail.


    Besides, in 25 cases, the vehicles were handed over to owners.Another 17 cases were registered against unknown.In 56 cases verification is being done by GRP.


    These kind of Operations were undertaken as security measures in view of upcoming Independence Day and the drives will be continuingtill 15.08.2019.
