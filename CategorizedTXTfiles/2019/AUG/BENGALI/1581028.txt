      ONE-DAY SEMINAR ON 125TH BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF EMINENT NATURALIST GOPAL CHANDRA BHATTACHARYYA
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2019 3:52PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 1, 2019


    A seminar was held on the occasion of 125 th birth anniversary of eminent naturalist and science writer, Shri Gopal Chandra Bhattacharyya, here today. The programme was jointly organized by Vigyan Prasar, under Deptt. of Science and Technology, Bangiya Bijnan Parishad and Gopal Chandra Bhattacharyya Bijnan Prasar Smritiin collaboration with Bose Institute. Dr. Nakul Parasar, Director, Vigyan Prasar was the chief guest on the occasion. The dignitaries also included Prof. Dr. Uday Bandyopadhyay, Director, Bose Institute; Shri Tapan Saha, Executive Secretary of Bangiya Bijnan Parishad among others. The discussions in the seminar centred round Life and Works of Gopal Chandra Bhattacharyya, Bio-diversity â€“ Inspection & Conservation, Science Literature, Journals and Gopal Chandra Bhattacharyya.


    Gopal Chandra Bhattacharyya is well known for his fundamental research in bio-science. A favourite of Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose, his research on the life cycle of insects and unknown facts on plant life, which was expressed through his science-based books, had awed all. His research works were published in 22 Indian and foreign research journals. He is credited for popularizing science through his attractive style of writing literary books in Bengali language.
