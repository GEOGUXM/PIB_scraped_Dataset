      Ministry of Science & Technology

      SEMINAR ON INDUSTRIAL LUBRICATION-2019
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2019 4:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 8, 2019


    A Hindi Seminar on ‘Industrial Lubrication’ was inaugurated by Prof. (Dr.) Harish Hirani, Director, CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur, Chief Guest Shri A.V. Kamlakar, CEO, SAIL-IISCO & DSP and other dignitaries today. The seminar was attended by delegates from IOCL, HPCL, RDCIS, SAIL-DSP, SAIL- ASP, PG and other renowned organizations.


    On the occasion Prof. (Dr.) Harish Hirani, Director, CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur stated that the paradigm Industrial Lubrication has mammoth significance in today’s Industrial Scenario. In such a scenario it is impending upon the practitioners of Science & Technology to apply the latest R&D development to further boost the efficacy of Lubrication Technologies. He also stated that all should strive towards attainment of self-reliance on Lubrication Technologies so that we are able to minimize dependence on foreign technologies. As a testimony to the latest developments in Lubrication Technologies Dr. Hirani cited the example of ‘Floating Ball’ and ‘Carbon-Graphite Solid Lubricant’. Dr. Hirani while stating the importance of Hindi as medium of communication, elaborated that usage of Hindi will help in fostering a sense of brotherhood and Unity.


    Chief Guest Shri A.V. Kamlakar expressed his appreciation for Prof.(Dr.) Harish Hirani, Director, CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur for taking the initiative to organize such a wonderful technical seminar for the first time in Hindi in this area. Such seminars will help in the promotion of Rajbhasha in the field of Science and Technology. Shri Kamlakar urged all to espouse the Rajbhasa which will help in effectively communicating latest trends in Science & Technology pan-nation.
