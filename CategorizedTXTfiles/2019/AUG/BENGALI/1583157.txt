      Ministry of Railways

      CONVERSION OF KHARAGPUR-JAJPUR KEONJHAR ROAD PASSENGER TO MEMU
      Posted On: 27 AUG 2019 5:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: August 27, 2019


    It has been decided that the existing conventional rake of 58009/58010 Kharagpur-Jajpur Keonjhar Road Passenger will be replaced by MEMU Rake w.e.f. 28/8/2019. With the conversion of rake, the above trains will be re-numbered and will run as 68109/68110 Kharagpur-Jajpur Keonjhar Road MEMU Passenger w.e.f 28/8/2019.


    68109 Kharagpur-Jajpur Keonjhar Road MEMU Passenger leaving Kharagpur at 16:50 hours will arrive Jajpur Keonjhar Road at 22:15 hours. In the return direction, 68110 Jajpur Keonjhar Road-Kharagpur MEMU Passenger leaving Jajpur Keonjhar Road at 05:40 hours will arrive Kharagpur at 10:55 hours.
