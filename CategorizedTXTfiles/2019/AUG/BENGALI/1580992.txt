      ADVANCE HQ EAC CELEBRATES KARGIL VIJAY DIWAS
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2019 1:16PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 1, 2019


    To commemorate the 20th anniversary of Kargil Vijay Diwas, a ‘Victory Run” was organized by Advance Head Quarters Eastern Air Command, on 31 Jul 19. Smt Lakshmi Narmada, President AFWWA(L) flagged off the run. Air Vice Marshal Amar Prasad Babu, Air Officer Commanding, Adv HQ EAC along with Air warriors, families, children, veterans and civilians participated with great zeal and enthusiasm.
