      PIB Kolkata Observes 73rd Independence Day with Honour
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2019 1:11PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, August 15, 2019


    Press Information Bureau (PIB), Kolkata observed the 73 rd Independence Day with dignity and honour today in the office premises. The Additional Director General of PIB Kolkata, Smt Jane Namchu unfurled the tricolour and greeted every staff member of the office and even paid deep respect to the revolutionaries who brought us freedom from British reins.


    All the staff members of the office were present on the occasion.
