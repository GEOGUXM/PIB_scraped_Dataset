      INFORMATION KIOSKS FOR E- FILING FOR IT- RETURNS INAUGURATED
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2019 7:15PM by PIB Hyderabad


    To Facilitate E-filing of Income Tax Returns, as a part of KARDATA E-SAHYOG PROGRAMME, information kiosks and help desks were inaugurated here today at Income Tax Offices at IT Towers in Masab Tank; Ayakar Bhawan in Basheer Bagh and at Signature Towers in Kondapur. It is compulsory to file Return of Income for the AY 2019-20 (FY 2018-19) in electronic form only, barring certain cases of Senior Citizens. The staff at the information kiosks would help public to file their income tax returns.
