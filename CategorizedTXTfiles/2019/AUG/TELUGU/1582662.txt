      CONDUCTING OF PENSION ADALAT BY ATOMIC MINERALS DIRECTORATE FOR EXPLORATION AND RESEARCH
      Posted On: 22 AUG 2019 6:23PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Based on the instructions received from Nodal Ministry, Atomic Minerals Directorate for Exploration and Research, a Constituent Unit of Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India is conducting Pension Adalat in their Head Office at Begumpet, Hyderabad on 23.08.2019 between 10.30 hours and 16.00 hours. All the pensioners/Family Pensioners of AMD who are having any sort of grievances with regard to Pension may attend the Adalat for instant redressal of their issues. Further, they may send their grievances in advance on e-mail Id: ao.iii.amd@gov.in .
