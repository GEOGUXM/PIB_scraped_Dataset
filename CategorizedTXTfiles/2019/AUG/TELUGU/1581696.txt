      ‘OUR MAHATMA’ PHOTO EXHIBITION AT SECUNDERABAD RAILWAY STATION
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2019 6:52PM by PIB Hyderabad


    On the occasion of 73 rd celebrations of Mahatma Gandhi, the Regional Outreach Bureau (ROB) under the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is organising a 4 day photo exhibition, from August 10 th to August 13 th , 2019 titled “Mana Mahatmudu – Our Mahatma”, on the first platform of Secunderabad Railway Station.


    The exhibition will be inaugurated by Shri G. Kishan Reddy, Minister of State for Home Affairs on August 10 th at 12 noon.


    The exhibition showcases rare photos of Gandhiji’s childhood, the Alfred High School in which he studied, Champaran Satyagraha, Gandhiji in Yerawada Jail, Salt Satyagraha, Quit India Movement among others. Also on display will be photos from 1942-1947 when Gandhiji was at the helm of our Freedom Struggle. Photos of Gandhiji and Kasturba returning from South Africa are also part or the exhibition.


    Song & Drama Division artists will stage cultural programmes on the occasion.


    Shri Rakesh, Chief Public Relations Officer, South Central Railway (SCR), Sri Srinivas Joint General Manager, SCR, Ms. Archana Rachel, Senior DCM, Sri Vijay Kumar, ACM, Dr. Manas Krishnakant, Assistant Director, ROB, Shri. Haribabu, Assistant Director, ROB, Shri Koteshwar Rao, FPO, ROB and other officials and staff will be present at the inauguration.
