      Ministry of Finance

      Aayakar Bhawan Guwahati holds E-mela at its premises from today
      Posted On: 20 AUG 2019 3:05PM by PIB Guwahati


    As part of the e-mela organized by the office of the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, North Eastern Region, Guwahati, one additional ‘e-mela for Salaried assessees’ has been made operational from 19 th August till 31 st August, 2019, barring Saturday and Sunday (24 th & 25 th August, 2019) at Aayakar Bhawan, G.S. Road, Guwahati during office hours.


    The ‘e-mela for Salaried assessees’ has been made operational from 13 th August till 31 st August, 2019, barring Saturdays and Sundays (17 th , 18 th , 24 th & 25 th August, 2019), at Old Aayakar Bhawan, MSTU, Uzan Bazar, Guwahati during office hours.


    On the occasion of its 159 th anniversary of existence, the Income Tax Department has launched Kardata e – Sahayog Abhiyaan on 24 th July, 2019 to facilitate the tax payers in filling their Return of Income for the Assessment Year 2019 – 20 electronically within the due dates. In continuation to this, office of the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, North Eastern Region, Guwahati is organizing “e– mela” – a taxpayer’s assistance programme for the salaried employees of the North Eastern Region in e-filing of their Income Tax Returns for the Assessment Year 2019 – 20.
