      Income Tax Department to Organize “e – mela” in Guwahati
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2019 12:19PM by PIB Guwahati


    Office of the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, North Eastern Region, Guwahati is organizing an “e – mela” – a taxpayer’s assistance programme for the salaried employees of the North Eastern Region in e-filing of their Income Tax Returns for the Assessment Year 2019 – 2020.


    The ‘e-mela’ will be operational from 13 th August till 31 st August, 2019, barring Saturdays and Sundays (17 th , 18 th , 24 th & 25 th August, 2019), at Old Aayakar Bhawan, MSTU, Uzan Bazar, Guwahati during office hours. The mela will be officially launched at Assam Secretariat on 8 th August, 2019 at 10.00 AM wherein assistance will be provided to the employees of Assam Secretariat on 8 th and 9 th August, 2019 in filing their Return of Income for the Assessment Year 2019 – 2020.
