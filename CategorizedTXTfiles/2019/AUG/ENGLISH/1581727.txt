      President of India hosts 'At Home' for Freedom Fighters on the 77th Anniversary of Quit India Day
      Posted On: 09 AUG 2019 9:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, hosted an ‘At Home’ reception for freedom fighters on the 77 th anniversary of Quit India Day today ( August 9, 2019 ) at Rashtrapati Bhavan.


    On the occasion, the President honoured and felicitated 78 freedom fighters from across the country in the presence of the Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu; Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi; Speaker, Lok Sabha, Shri Om Birla; Union Minister for Home Affairs, Shri Amit Shah and other dignitaries.
