      Election Commission

      Bye Elections to the Legislative Councils of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana by the members of Legislative Assembly (MLAs)
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2019 5:13PM by PIB Delhi


    There are three casual vacancies in the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Council and one casual vacancy in the Telangana Legislative Council by the Members of Legislative Assembly. The details of the vacancies are as follow:

                               
ANDHRA PRADESH                 
                               
                               
                                                                           
Name of Member                   Cause of vacancy                Term Upto 
                                                                           
                                                                           
                                                                           
Karanam Balarama Krishna Murthy  Resignation on 06.06.2019       29.03.2023
                                                                           
                                                                           
                                                               
Alla Kali Krishna Srinivas       29.03.2023                    
                                                               
                                                               
                                                               
Kolagatla Veerabhadra Swamy      29.03.2021                    
                                                               
                                                               
                               
TELANGANA                      
                               
                               
                                                                           
K Yadava Reddy                   Disqualification on 16.01.2019  03.06.2021
