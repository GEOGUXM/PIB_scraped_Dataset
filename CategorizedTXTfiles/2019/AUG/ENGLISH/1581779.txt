      President of India’s Greetings on The Eve Of Idu’l Zuha
      Posted On: 11 AUG 2019 5:37PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind has greeted fellow citizens on the eve of Idu’l Zuha.


    In his message the President has said, “On the occasion of Idu’l Zuha, I offer my greetings and good wishes to all fellow citizens,especially our Muslim brothers and sisters in India and abroad.


    Idu’l Zuha symbolizes love, fraternity and service to humanity. Let us commit ourselves to these universal values that represent our composite culture”.
