      Youth Affairs and Sports Minister Kiren Rijiju condoles the passing away of Shri Arun Jaitley
      Posted On: 24 AUG 2019 4:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of State (I/C) for Youth Affairs and Sports, Shri Kiren Rijiju has condoled the demise of former finance minister and Senior BJP leader Shri Arun Jaitley. In a tweet message he said," I am extremely saddened to learn the passing away of Arun Jaitley ji. His death is a personal loss for me. Arun ji was a brilliant lawyer and a great parliamentarian.
