      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2019 6:45AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan.


    “ रक्षाबंधन की आप सभी को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। Greetings on the auspicious occasion of Raksha Bandhan,” the Prime Minister said.
