      Prime Minister's Office

      Prime Minister to address Manorama News Conclave 2019 tomorrow
      Posted On: 29 AUG 2019 5:38PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will address Manorama News Conclave 2019, tomorrow i.e. 30 August 2019 at 10 AM, through video conferencing. The conclave is being held in Kochi and organized by Malayala Manorama Company Limited.
