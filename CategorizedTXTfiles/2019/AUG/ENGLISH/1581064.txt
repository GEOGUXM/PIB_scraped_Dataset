      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Entries invited for Design of Postage Stamp and First Day Cover for Golden Jubilee Edition of IFFI
      Posted On: 01 AUG 2019 6:37PM by PIB Delhi


    In order to commemorate the Golden Jubilee Edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI), set to take place from the 20th to 28th November, 2019 in Goa, it has been decided that a special Postage Stamp and First Day Cover will be released. A competition for designing the Postage Stamp and First Day Cover has been launched by the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting on MyGov platform. Those interested may log in on www.mygov.i n to participate in the competition.


    The contest is open from 1st August, 2019 to 15th August, 2019. The winner will receive a Certificate of Appreciation along with travel and hospitality for 2 nights and 3 days to Goa to attend the Festival.
