      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the Nation on Independence Day
      Posted On: 15 AUG 2019 6:40AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the Nation on Independence Day.


    “सभी देशवासियों को स्वतंत्रतादिवस की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं। जय हिंद!


    Happy Independence Day to all my fellow Indians. Jai Hind,” the Prime Minister said.
