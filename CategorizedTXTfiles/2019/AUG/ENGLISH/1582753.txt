      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister’s Medal for Excellence in Investigation for the year 2019 declared
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2019 5:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Home Minister’s Medal for Excellence in Investigation for the year 2019 have been awarded to 96 Police personnel (List attached). This medal, constituted in 2018, with the objective to promote high professional standards of investigation of crime and to recognize such Excellence in Investigation by investigating officers.


    Among the personnel receiving these awards, 15 are from CBI, 11 are from Maharashtra Police, 10 are from Uttar Pradesh Police, 9 are from Kerala Police, 8 are from Madhya Pradesh Police, 6 are from Delhi Police and Karnataka Police and the remaining from the other States/UT and Central Investigating Agencies. These include thirteen (13) women police officers.
