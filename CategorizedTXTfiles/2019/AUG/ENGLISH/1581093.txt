      Ministry of Finance

      GST Revenue collection for July, 2019
      
      ₹ 1,02,083 crore total gross GST revenue collected

      Posted On: 01 AUG 2019 8:36PM by PIB Delhi

    The total gross GST revenue collected in the month of July, 2019 is ₹ 1,02,083 crore of which CGST is ₹ 17,912 crore , SGST is ₹ 25,008 crore , IGST is ₹ 50,612 crore (including ₹ 24,246 crore collected on imports) and Cess is ₹ 8,551 crore (including ₹797 crore collected on imports). The total number of GSTR 3B Returns filed for the month of June up to 31 st July, 2019 is 75.79 lakh .


    The revenue in July, 2018 was ₹ 96,483 crore and the revenue during July, 2019 is a growth of 5.80% over the revenue in the same month last year. During April-July 2019 vis-à-vis 2018, the domestic component has grown by 9.2% while the GST on imports has come down by 0.2% and the total collection has grown by 6.83% . Rs. 17,789 crore has been released to the states as GST compensation for the months of April-May, 2019.
