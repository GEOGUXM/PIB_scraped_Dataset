      PM greets nation on the occasion of Eid al-Adha
      Posted On: 12 AUG 2019 9:21AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted fellow citizens on Eid al-Adha. “My best wishes on the occasion of Eid al-Adha. I hope it furthers the spirit of peace and happiness in our society. Eid Mubarak!”, the PM said.
