      Ministry of Railways

      Hon’ble President of India awarded President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service and Police Medal for Meritorious Service to Officers and Staff of RPF/ RPSF
      Posted On: 14 AUG 2019 6:00PM by PIB Delhi


    On the occasion of Independence Day 2019, the Hon’ble President of India has awarded President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service and Police Medal for Meritorious Service to the following Railway Protection Force (RPF)/Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) officers and staff.


    President’s Police Medal (PPM) for Distinguished Service


     1. Shri T. S. Gopakumar, Assistant Security Commissioner, Southern Railway


    Police Medal (PM) for Meritorious Service


     1. Shri Jyoti Kumar Satija, DIG/ JJR RPF Academy, Lucknow

     2. Shri Sanjay Kumar Gupta, Assistant Security Commissioner, North Western Railway
     3. Shri Anil Kumar Singh, Assistant Commandant, 2 BN/ RPSF
     4. Shri N. Ashwini Kumar, Vigilance Officer (Inspector), IRCTC
     5. Shri Indu Prakash Singh, Assistant Security Commissioner, RDSO
     6. Shri Devi Singh Meena, Assistant Sub-Inspector, North Western Railway
     7. Shri Basappa Lamani, Inspector, 11 BN/ RPSF
     8. Shri Vikas Bajaj, Sub-Inspector, 6 BN/ RPSF
     9. Shri Chandra Prakash Dubey, Assistant Sub-Inspector, Northern Railway
    10. Shri Rambabu Sharma, Sub-Inspector, KRCL
    11. Shri Parbat K. Madam, Sub-Inspector, KRCL
    12. Shri Narayan Prasad Pandey, Sub-Inspector, South-East Central Railway
    13. Shri Ajoy Bandyopadhyay, Sub-Inspector, South-East Central Railway
    14. Shri Biswadip Bose, Sub-Inspector, South-East Central Railway
    15. Shri Ramkumar Yadav, Sub-Inspector, Central Railway
