      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Enhanced Penalties Under Amended Motor Vehicles Act to Become Applicable from Next Month
      
      Centre has Notified Some Provisions of the Act Today

      Posted On: 28 AUG 2019 8:14PM by PIB Delhi

    The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has notified today, through S.O. No 3110(E), the provisions of the Motor Vehicles Amendment Act 2019 that will be applicable with effect from 1st of September 2019. These are provisions which require no further amendments in the Central Motor Vehicles Rules 1989. Important among the provisions notified today are the provisions for enhanced penalties.


    For the remaining provisions the Ministry has initiated the process of formulating draft rules. As and when the process is completed, the relevant provisions would be notified for implementation.
