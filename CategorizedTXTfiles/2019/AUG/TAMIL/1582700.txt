      Indian Overseas Bank – CENTRAL OFFICE – CHENNAI
      Posted On: 23 AUG 2019 11:36AM by PIB Chennai


    “Indian Overseas Bank in coordination with the Rotary Club of Madras (East) conducted the blood donation camp on 21.08.2019 at IOB – Central Office – Chennai commemorating 73 rd Independence day. The blood donation camp was inaugurated by Shri KarnamSekar, MD &CEO of Indian Overseas Bank. The Executive Director Shri Ajay Kumar Srivastava and all General Managers of IOB graced the occasion.


    The Rotary Club President Shri T. R. Gopala Krishnan and Shri M. Srinivasan ex-President were also present. More than 200 donors have donated blood during the camp”


    IOB Staff donating blood at blood donation camp in presence of MD & CEO Shri. KarnamSekar along with ED –Ajay Kumar Srivastava and other staff members


    While inaugurating the blood donation camp Mr. KarnamSekar – MD & CEO stated that for any social cause, IOB has always been a forerunner and in limelight championing such events as part of its Corporate Social Responsibility and will continue to be so forever.
