      Pension Adalat of Income Tax Department
      Posted On: 06 AUG 2019 4:43PM by PIB Chennai


    As per directions of the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, it has been decided to conduct Pension Adalat to redress the grievances of pensioners of Income Tax Department, Tamilnadu & Puducherry. The same will be held at “Vaigai Hall” (Old Auditorium), Aayakar Bhawan, 121, M G Road, Nungambakkam, Chennai - 600 034 at 11 a.m. on 23.08.2019 (Friday) . The pensioners of Income Tax Department, Tamilnadu & Puducherry are requested to furnish their grievances, if any on or before 14.08.2019 to Shri. R Sundar , Administrative Officer at Aayakar Bhawan (Aayakar Seva Kendra) appointed as Nodal Officer for this purpose, according to the press release issued by Smt. R ILAVARASI, Addl.Commissioner of Income Tax, Chennai.
