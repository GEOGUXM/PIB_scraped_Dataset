      Pension Adalat
      Posted On: 07 AUG 2019 2:49PM by PIB Chennai


    Government of India has decided to conduct PENSION ADALAT to look into the grievances of the Pensioners, so as to redress the same by taking on the spot-decisions there by reducing the delays, if any, in settling their legitimate duesrelating to pension and other retirement benefits for Central Government employees, covered under CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972. Problems / difficulties that are being faced by the pensioners will be identified besides, it also seeks to promote pensioners welfare and serves as a forum for redressal of their grievances. The Pension Adalat is scheduled to be convened on 23.08.2019 at 3.00 pm at the Office of the Commissioner of GST & Central Excise, Chennai Outer Commissionerate, 2054-1, Newry Towers, II Avenue, Anna Nagar, Chennai 600 040.
