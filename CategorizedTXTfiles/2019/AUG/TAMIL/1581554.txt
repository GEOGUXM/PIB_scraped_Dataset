      Security Stepped up at Chennai Airport ahead of Independence Day
      Posted On: 08 AUG 2019 5:58PM by PIB Chennai


    Security measures at Chennai Airport is stepped up in view of 73 rd Independence Day. Security has beefed up in all areas of airport including parking lot. Passengers flying out of Chennai Airport are requested to reach airport well in advance to avoid any inconvenience.


    A special meeting was convened in this regard with all the stake holders, in the airport. The heightened security measures will be in place till August 31 st 2019 , according to the press release issued by AAI Chennai Airport.
