      New logo and certificate identity of CBFC to be unveiled tomorrow
      Posted On: 30 AUG 2019 9:43PM by PIB Mumbai


    The Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) is getting a new logo and certificate design. These are being unveiled at a first-of-its-kind film industry interaction between eminent industry leaders and CBFC board members, being held in Mumbai on August 31, 2019. The Union Minister for Information & Broadcasting, Shri Prakash Javdekar will be the Chief Guest. Secretary, Ministry of I&B, Shri Amit Khare will be the Guest of Honour. The new logo and certificate identity will be revealed by the Chairman, CBFC.


    “The new is design is futuristic in its approach and in sync with the new digital world; it is also interactive in its design sensibility” said the Chairman, Shri Prasoon Joshi, who has himself conceptualised the new look.The design has been done by designer Shri Rohit Devgun, with technical support from NSDL.
