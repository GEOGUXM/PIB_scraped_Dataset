      Gold worth Rs. 37 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 11 OCT 2019 7:02PM by PIB Chennai


    On Friday Sivakumar Palaniyandi ,42, and Sagay Mary,35, who had arrived from Colombo by Indigo Flight 6E 1204 and Srilankan Airline flight UL 125 were intercepted at the exit on suspicion of carrying gold . The personal search of Sivakumar resulted in the recovery of assorted gold jewellery of 94 % purity, weighing 525 grams valued at Rs.19.5 lakhs and that of Sagay Mary resulted in recovery of assorted gold jewellery of 22 K purity weighing 128 gms valued at Rs 4.7 lakhs and 320 grams of gold valued at Rs 12.6 lakhs was recovered from gold paste which was concealed in rectum.


    In total gold weighing 973 gms valued at 37 lakhs was recovered and same seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further Investigation is under progress.
