      Gold worth Rs. 37.6 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 21 OCT 2019 1:08PM by PIB Chennai


    On Sunday based on information one Mohamed Nalif, 24, from Ramanathapuram and Syed Abdul Rahim, 35, from Chennai, who had arrived from Dubai to Chennai by Air India Flight No.AI 906 were intercepted at the exit on suspicion of carrying gold. They appeared to be nervous and on questioning if they had anything to declare they gave evasive reply. On their personal search, six bundles of gold in rubbery spread from (three bundles each) were recovered from their rectum. On extraction 949 grams of 24 k purity gold, valued at Rs. 37.6 lakhs was recovered and the same seized under Customs Act 1962. Further investigation is in progress, according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport, Chennai.
