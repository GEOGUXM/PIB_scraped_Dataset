      2.3 Kg gold Gold worth Rs. 91.5 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 24 OCT 2019 6:00PM by PIB Chennai


    On Thursday based on information one Farjana Abubakkar Siddiqu,27, from Pulianthope and Sathakathulla ,47, from Ramanathapuram, had arrived from Malaysia by Air Asia Flight No.AK-13 and Dubai by Indigo Flight 6E 66 respectively, were intercepted exit. On personal search six bundles of rubbery paste containing gold was found concealed in Farjanas inner garments and stiched inside of her skirt and three bundles were found concealed in rectum of Sathakathulla. Further, two gold bits weighing 40 grams were also recovered from his trouser pockets. On extraction 927 grams was recovered from paste. Total 967 grams of gold valued at Rs. 38.37 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962. Both arrested.


    On Wednesday four passengers, Mohamed Sarjoon,30, Adhil Hasim Abdul Nazir,26, Anbalagan Karuppaiah ,61, and Shahul Hamid Zackiriya, 37, all native of Ramanathapuram had arrived from Dubai by Air India Flight AI 906 were intercepted at the exit . On their personal search gold in the form of rubbery paste was found concealed in rectum.On extraction 1343 grams of gold valued at Rs. 53 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further Investigation is under progress, according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai.
