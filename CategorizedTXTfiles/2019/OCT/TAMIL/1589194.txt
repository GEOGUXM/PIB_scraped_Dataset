      FRAUDSTERS CONVICTED IN A CUSTOMS CASE OF CBI
      Posted On: 25 OCT 2019 2:46PM by PIB Chennai


    Shri. A.M. Ravi, BA., BL., the Learned Additional Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Egmore, Chennai, on 24.10.2019 convicted one Company and three Private persons in a Customs fraud case.


    The allegation of the case registered by CBI, EOW, Chennai on 02.09.2009 was that A-1 M/s.General Imports Company (India) Pvt. Ltd., A-2 Srichand Talreja, the Managing Director of A-1 Company, A-3 Ritesh Talreja, Director of A-1 Company and A-4 K.Kuppuswami, Customs House Agent entered into criminal conspriacy to import spurious and sub-standard drugs into India by using forged documents without proper licence and thereby cheated the Customs Department and the public in general.


    After completion of investigation, charge sheet was filed before the Ld. Addl. Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Egmore, Chennai, in CC 5271/2010 U/s.120-B r/w 420, 471, 482 IPC Sec.13(1)(a)(b) r/w 9-B(e), 10(a)(b)(c) of Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940, Sec.132 & 135 of Customs Act, 1962 and substantive offences thereof.


    After trial, the Court has convicted the following accused persons for the said offences committed by them, as indicated below.
