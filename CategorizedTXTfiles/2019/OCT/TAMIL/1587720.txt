      PIB Headquarters

      Indian Overseas Bank reduces lending rates for Housing loans, Vehicle loans, MSE loans etc. effective from 01.11.2019
      Posted On: 10 OCT 2019 4:44PM by PIB Chennai


    In line with the decision of RBI to reduce Repo rate by 25 bps, Chennai based Public Sector Bank Indian Overseas Bank has reduced the Interest rate applicable for Retail segment (Housing, Vehicle, Education and Clean loans) and Micro & Small Enterprises by 25 bps and the revised Repo Linked Lending Rate will be 8% reduced from 8.25% with effect from 01.11.2019.


    Earlier Indian Overseas Bank has linked Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR) to its loans to be sanctioned under Retail segment (Housing, Vehicle, Education and Clean loans) and Micro & Small Enterprises with effect from 1 st October 2019.
