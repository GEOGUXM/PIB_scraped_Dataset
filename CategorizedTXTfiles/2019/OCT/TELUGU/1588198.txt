      OPEN DAY AT NIN
      Posted On: 15 OCT 2019 6:52PM by PIB Hyderabad


    NIN observes OPEN DAY on Friday, the 18th October, 2019. During the OPEN DAY, NIN would showcase its scientific achievements and research facilities to students, the general public and local media.Visitors can also interact with scientists.


    The OPEN DAY is being organized under the banner of India International Science Festival (IISF)-2019 and joint patronage of Ministry of Science & Technology, Ministry of Earth Sciences and the Dept. of Health Research together with Vijnana Bharati (VIBHA).
