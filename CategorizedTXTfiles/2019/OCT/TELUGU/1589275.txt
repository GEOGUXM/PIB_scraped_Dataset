      MODI GOVT IS COMMITTED TO THE HOLISTIC WELFARE OF POLICE PERSONNEL - G .KISHAN REDDY
      MOS HOME AFFAIRS KISHAN REDDY PRESIDES AS CHIEF GUEST FOR FRIENDLY CRICKET MATCH ORGANISED BY CRPF

      Posted On: 25 OCT 2019 7:26PM by PIB Hyderabad

    The government under leadership of Shri .Narendra Modi, honourable prime Minster is making all efforts for welfare of the families of the forces said Shri G.Kishan Reddy, Union Minister of state for home affairs. The minister presided as chief guest for friendly cricket match of Celebrities & EX-Cricketers and police officials organised by Central Reserve Police Forces (CRPF)


    This match was conducted today at Vijay Anand Gardens, Shivarampally Hyderabad as a part of series of event on the occasion of 31 st October Rashtriya Ekta Diwas (National Unity Day) commemorating Shri Sardar Vallabhbhai Patelâ€™s 144 th birth anniversary.


    Speaking on the occasion Shri G.Kishan Reddy applauded the Central Reserve Police Force CRPF for their role in internal security maintenance of the country and expressed his satisfaction about the role played by central and state police in maintaining the law and order. Minister appealed the public to visit National Police Memorial at Delhi pay homage and remember the brave police man who made the supreme sacrifice and laid down their lives.


    On this occasion, patriotic songs presented by CRPF band enthralled the audience with national fervour and enthusiasm.


    Honorary counsel General of Republic of Korea Shri Suresh Chukkapalli, Tollywood celebrities actors Padmashri Dr.Bhramanandham,Dr.Rajshekar, Smt jeevitha,Shivaji Raja participated in the event. Cyberbad police commissioner Shri V.C .Sajjanar IPS Commissioner of Police and other officials and CRPF family members were present at the event.
