      PROF. MUHTOR NASIROV OF SAMARKAND UNIVERSITY, UZBEKISTAN CALLS ON EFLU VICE CHANCELLOR
      Posted On: 11 OCT 2019 6:50PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Prof. Muhtor Nasirov, Vice-Rector, International Programmes, Samarkand State University, Uzbekistan, called upon Prof. E. Suresh Kumar, Vice Chancellor, the English and Foreign Languages University, Hyderabad today.


    Prof. E. Suresh Kumar and Prof. Muhtor Nasirov discussed the possibility of joint research ventures, teacher training,and blending academic and scholarly ties with the students and faculty of the University. They also discussed the possibility of student and faculty exchange between the EFL University and Samarkand State University.


    Prof. E. Suresh Kumar presented a brief account of the programmes and courses offered by the EFL University. In his interaction with the Vice-Rector, the ViceChancellor of the EFLU deliberated the possibility of signing an MoU with Samarkand State University.
