      SSC invites application for Combined Graduate Level Examination -2019
      Posted On: 28 OCT 2019 2:45PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Staff Selection Commission has invited applications for Combined Graduate Level Examination-2019 (CGLE) for filling up Group-B, Group-C posts in various ministries/departments and organisations. The examination will be held all over the country from 2 to 11 March, 2020 through a open computer based Online Competitive Examination (Tier-1). Only online applications will be entertained and application should be made through the website https://ssc.nic.in . The last date for receiving online application is November 25, 2019 till 5 .00 pm. Fee is exempted for all women candidates and candidates belonging to SC/ST/PwD/Ex-serviceman categories are eligible for reservations in the recruitment. More details are uploaded in the websites https://ssc.nic.in and www.ssckkr.kar.nic.in . For assistance call in the following numbers 080-25502520, 9483862020 on Monday to Friday from 10.00 am to 5.00 pm.
