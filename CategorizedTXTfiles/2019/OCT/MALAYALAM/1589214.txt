      EPFO to observe Vigilance Awareness Week
      Posted On: 25 OCT 2019 4:30PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    The Employees Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) Kochi regional office will observe Vigilance Awareness Week from October 28 to November 2, 2019. This year the theme of the awareness week is “Integrity-A way of Life”. The Vigilance Awareness Week will commence with integrity pledge by office staff on October 28 at 11.00 am. As a part of this, a customer grievance redressal camp will be organised at the office on October 30 at 11 .00 am. During Vigilance Awareness Week, a drop box for complaints would be kept in the reception of the centre. Employers, employees, pensioners and union representatives can avail this facility.
