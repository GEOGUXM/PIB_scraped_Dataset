      Mega Adalat For The Defence Pensioners In Kerala On 31st Oct And 01st Nov 19 At Alappuzha
      Posted On: 24 OCT 2019 6:37PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A State Level Defence Pension Adalat will be held at Prince Convention Centre, A S Road (NH 47) Near Bye-pass Road, Alappuzha on 31 st October & 01 st November 2019 under the aegis of the Controller of Defence Accounts, Chennai to redress pension related grievances of defence pensioners/defence family pensioners (including Commissioned Officers, Defence Civilians and their families) drawing defence pension in Kerala. The Adalat will be inaugurated by Shri.Sanjeev Mittal, IDAS, Controller General of Defence Accounts, New Delhi.


    The prescribed format for application is available in the website – www.cdachennai.nic.in . Any pensioner having grievance relating to grant/disbursement of defence pension may send their application along with copy of discharge book, first page of bank pass book with updated entries from 1.1.2016 to till date, PPO/Corr.PPO (if available) to the Adalat Officer, Office of the C.D.A, 618, Anna Salai, Teynampet, Chennai – 600 018. Alternatively, the pensioners can directly come to the venue on 31 st Oct or 01 st Nov between 10 a.m to 5 p.m and present their grievances before the Adalat Bench. For further details please contact Ph.044-24349980 Extn.135, Fax : 044-24348142.
