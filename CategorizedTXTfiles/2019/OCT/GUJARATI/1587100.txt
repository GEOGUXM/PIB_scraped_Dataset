      DAK ADALAT TO SOLVE PROBLEMS RELATING TO POSTAL SERVICES
      Posted On: 03 OCT 2019 5:43PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Department of Post proposes to hold Dak Adalat for Settlement of grievances relating to Postal services. Dak Adalat for 3 rd Quarter ending on 30.09.2019 will be held at 11:00 hours onwards on 22.10.2019 (Tuesday) in the O/o the Postmaster General, Head Quarter Region, Speed Post Bhavan, Shahibaug, Ahmedabad-380004.


    The Postmaster General will hear each complainant individually and settle the problem of public on the spot. The idea of the above stated Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settlement of problems of the Public on the spot.


    The complaints of Postal services related to Head Quarter Region, Ahmedabad should be sent to Shri V K Darji, Asstt. Director Postal Services (Mails, Estt. & Rectt.), Complaint section, O/o the Postmaster General, Ahmedabad Head Quarter Region, Speed Post Bhavan, Shahibaug, Ahmedabad-380004, so as to reach latest by 11.10.2019 (Friday) . The complaints received after the due date will not be entertained.


    The complaints should be concrete and in brief . The complaints of a general nature and of policy matters will not be entertained. Moreover, the complaints should not contain more than one point or subject in one application .
