      Ministry of Home Affairs

      A Day Film Festival on Sardar Vallabh Bhai Patel held at Films Division today
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2019 12:40PM by PIB Mumbai


        Films Division, Government of India as part of the celebration of “National Unity Day” is organizing one day film festival on Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel on 31st October, 2019 at 2.30 pm commemorating his 144 th Birth Annivarsary. The festival will take place in J.B. Hall, Films Division Complex, Pedder Road, Mumbai. Screening is open for all.


        A documentary “ Sardar Patel”(21 min./ B&W/ Hindi/ Dir. Omprakash Sharma) made by film division, will be screened in the festival in addition to feature film “Sardar” (2hr.39 min./ Col./ Hindi/ Dir. Etan Mehata
