      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Governor to flag off ‘Run for Unity’ on Sardar Patel Jayanti
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2019 5:51PM by PIB Mumbai


    The Governor of Maharashtra Bhagat Singh Koshyari will flag off the ‘ Run for Unity ’ on the occasion of the Birth Anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel in Mumbai at 8.00 A.M . on Thursday (31st Oct).


    The Run will start from NCPA, Gate No 3, Marine Drive, Mumbai.
