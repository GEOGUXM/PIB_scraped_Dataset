      Ministry of Culture

      Panel Discussion on “Integrity – A Way of Life” jointly organized by Nehru Science Centre, NTPC and IDF
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2019 3:23PM by PIB Mumbai


    Nehru Science Centre, Mumbai has joined hands with National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC) and Indian Development Foundation (IDF) to organise a panel discussion on the theme “Integrity – A Way of Life” on Thursday, 31st October, 2019 at 3:00 p.m. at the Nehru Science Center, (Ministry of Culture, Government of India), Dr. E. Moses Road, Worli


    This panel discussion is under the Vigilance Awareness Week, which is celebrated from 28th October to 2nd November, 2019..


    In order to bring greater transparency, accountability and to promote integrity in the governmental transactions, the Government of India has introduced several schemes and policies for making the system as transparent as possible. However, it is necessary to create a large public understanding of the system and also to create understanding about the existing standards of integrity. To follow up the said thoughts, Nehru Science Centre in Mumbai is celebrating Vigilance Awareness Week from 28th October to 2nd November, 2019.
