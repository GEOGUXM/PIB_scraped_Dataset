      PM greets nation on the occasion of Vijayadashmi
      Posted On: 08 OCT 2019 9:30AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashmi.


    “ विजयादशमी के पावन पर्व पर आप सभी को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं।


    Greetings on the auspicious occasion of Vijaya Dashami”, the Prime Minister said.
