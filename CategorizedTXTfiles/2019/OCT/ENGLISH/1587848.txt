      Ministry of Education

      Union HRD Minister inaugurates 13 Newly Constructed Buildings of KVs and lays foundation stone of 06 KVS through Video Conferencing
      Posted On: 11 OCT 2019 5:57PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Human Resource Development Minister Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ inaugurated 13 newly constructed buildings and laid down the foundation stones of 6 Kendriya Vidyalayas located in 12 states through Video Conferencing in New Delhi today. More than 20 thousand students will be benefitted with this huge completion of work having a cost investment of more than Rs. 350 crores. Secretary (SE&L), MHRD Mrs. Rina Ray, Joint Secretary, MHRD & Vice Chairman of KVS Sh. RC Meena and Joint Secretary, MHRD Sh. Sanjay Kumar were also present during the programme along with senior officers of KVS.


    Addressing the 19 locations through video conferencing, the union HRD Minister Sh. Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ said that KVS has become an institution of excellence within the government system and has now been recognized on international platforms. He extended his warm greetings to the concerned Principals, Staff, Parents and Students and local MPs, MLAs and other people’s representatives and stressed that keeping in view this pace KVS will soon become a world class institution.


    Commissioner, KVS Sh. Santosh Kumar Mall gave detailed information about the new KV buildings in his welcome address.
