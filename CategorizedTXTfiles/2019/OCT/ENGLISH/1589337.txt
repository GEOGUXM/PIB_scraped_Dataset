      President of India pays homage to Shri K.R. Narayanan on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 27 OCT 2019 5:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid homage to Shri K.R. Narayanan, former President of India, on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (October 27, 2019).


    The President, officers and staff of Rashtrapati Bhavan as well as family members of Shri Narayanan paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Shri K.R. Narayanan.
