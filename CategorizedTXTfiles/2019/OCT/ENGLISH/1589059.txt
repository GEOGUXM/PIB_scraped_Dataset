      President of India to Grace The Commemoration of Golden Jubilee of Vishwa Shanti Stupa in Rajgir Tomorrow
      Posted On: 24 OCT 2019 4:21PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Bihar tomorrow (October 25, 2019) to grace and address the commemoration of Golden Jubilee of Vishwa Shanti Stupa in Rajgir.
