      PM pays tributes to Sardar Patel on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2019 9:26AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Sardar Patel on his Jayanti.


    “ देश की एकता के सूत्रधार लौह पुरुष सरदार वल्लभभाई पटेल को उनकी जन्म-जयंती पर शत-शत नमन। Tributes to the great Sardar Patel on his Jayanti. His contribution to our nation is monumental”, the Prime Minister said.


    देश की एकता के सूत्रधार लौह पुरुष सरदार वल्लभभाई पटेल को उनकी जन्म-जयंती पर शत-शत नमन।
