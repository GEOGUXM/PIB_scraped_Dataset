      PM congratulates Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau on winning election
      Posted On: 22 OCT 2019 8:08PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi today congratulated Justin Trudeau on winning elections in Canada.


    “Congratulations Justin Trudeau! India and Canada are connected by shared values and a strong commitment to democracy as well as plurality. Looking forward to working together to further strengthen bilateral relations”, he said.
