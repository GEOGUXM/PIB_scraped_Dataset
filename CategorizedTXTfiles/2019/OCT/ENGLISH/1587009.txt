      PM pays tributes to Mahatma Gandhi and Lal Bahadur Shastri on their birth anniversary
      Posted On: 02 OCT 2019 11:00AM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi paid tributes to Mahatma Gandhi and Lal Bahadur Shastri on their birth anniversary.


    PM said that Gandhi Ji’s commitment to peace, harmony and brotherhood remained unwavering. He envisioned a world where the poorest of the poor are empowered. His ideals are our guiding light. #Gandhi150


    On Lal Bahadur Shastri, he said that India will never forget the valuable contribution of Shastri Ji. He was a stalwart who never deviated from his ideals and principles, come what may.


    At Rajghat, paid tributes to Bapu.
