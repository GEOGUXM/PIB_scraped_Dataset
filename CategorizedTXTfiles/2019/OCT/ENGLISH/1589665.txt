      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Alberto Fernández on his victory in the Presidential elections in Argentina
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2019 10:10PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Narendra Modi has congratulated Alberto Fernández on his victory in the Presidential elections in Argentina.


    “Heartiest congratulations Alberto Fernández on your impressive victory in the Presidential elections. Looking forward to working with you to further expand and deepen the strategic partnership between India and Argentina”, the Prime Minister said.


    Heartiest congratulations @alferdez on your impressive victory in the Presidential elections. Looking forward to working with you to further expand and deepen the strategic partnership between India and Argentina.

    — Narendra Modi (@narendramodi) October 30, 2019
