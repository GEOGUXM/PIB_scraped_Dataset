      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles passing away of CPI veteran Gurudas Dasgupta
      Posted On: 31 OCT 2019 2:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of CPI veteran Gurudas Dasgupta.


    “Shri Gurudas Dasgupta Ji was one of the most committed and articulate proponents of his ideology. He was a strong voice in Parliament, whose interventions were keenly heard across the political spectrum. Saddened by his passing away. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
