      Prime Minister mourns the passing away of Kadri Gopalnath
      Posted On: 11 OCT 2019 8:27PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today condoled the passing away of veteran Carnatic musician Shri Kadri Gopalnath. He said “The remarkable Kadri Gopalnath epitomised excellence. Blessed with exceptional diligence and talent, he made a valuable contribution to Carnatic music. His works were popular across continents. Pained by his demise. My thoughts are with his family and admirers.
