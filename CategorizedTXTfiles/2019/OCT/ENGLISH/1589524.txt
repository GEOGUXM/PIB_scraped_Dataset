      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Shri Atanu Chakraborty, Economic Affairs Secretary,gets additional charge of Deptt. of Expenditure
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2019 12:36PM by PIB Delhi


    The Competent Authority has approved assignment of additional charge of the post of Secretary, Department of Expenditure to Shri Atanu Chakraborty, IAS (GJ:1985), Secretary, Department of Economic Affairs with immediate effect and till the appointment of a regular incumbent or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
