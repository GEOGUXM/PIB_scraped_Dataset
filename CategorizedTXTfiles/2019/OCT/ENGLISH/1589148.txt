      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      President of India summons both the houses of Parliament for sessions from 18th of November
      Posted On: 24 OCT 2019 8:05PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India has summoned both the houses of parliament for sessions from Monday, the 18th of November 2019.


    Both the Houses have issued bulletins on dates for the session, which is to conclude on Friday, the 13th of December 2019.
