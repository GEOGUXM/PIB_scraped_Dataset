      PM salutes air warriors and their families on Air Force Day
      Posted On: 08 OCT 2019 9:45AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted air warriors and their families on Air Force Day.


    "Today, on Air Force Day, a proud nation expresses gratitude to our air warriors and their families. The Indian Air Force continues to serve India with utmost dedication and excellence." the Prime Minister said.
