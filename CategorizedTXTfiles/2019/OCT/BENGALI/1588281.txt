      EAC PLANS FIGHTER OPS FROM KOLKATA & ANDAL AIRFIELDS
      Posted On: 16 OCT 2019 5:26PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, October 16, 2019


    Eastern Air Command has planned fighter operations from six airfields in its area of operations namely Dimapur, Imphal, Guwahati, Kolkata, Pasighat and Andal.


    The exercise will be in two phases from 16-19 Oct 2019 and 29 Oct to 01 Nov 2019 and will be part of capability build up and operational training from dispersed locations in case of hostilities.


    The exercise will familiarize the IAF crew with the procedure of flying at these busy airfields and coordinate with civilian counterparts.


    It will help familiarising the civilian functionaries at these airfields in the conduct of military ops.


    The SU 30 MKi and Hawk 132 will be taking part in this exercise.
