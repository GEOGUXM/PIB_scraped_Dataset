      EASTERN ARMY COMMANDER VISITS TAWANG
      Posted On: 28 OCT 2019 3:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, October 26, 2019


    

    
    General Officer Commanding-in-Chief Eastern Command, Lt Gen Anil Chauhan, reviewed the security situation and operational preparedness in the border areas of Arunachal Pradesh in Tawang Sector on 24 and 25 Oct 2019.
    
    The Army Commander was accompanied by GOC, Gajraj Corps, Lt Gen Manoj Pande. The General Officer was briefed in detail on the prevailing security situation and operational preparedness along the LAC by the respective commanders on ground.
