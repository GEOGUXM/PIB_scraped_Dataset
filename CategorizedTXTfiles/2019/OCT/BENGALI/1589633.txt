      Ministry of Finance

      UNITED BANK OF INDIA TODAY RELEASED FINANCIAL RESULTS QUARTER 2 FOR THE FINANCIAL YEAR 2019-20
      Posted On: 30 OCT 2019 6:26PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, October 30, 2019.


    United Bank of India today released Financial Results Q2, F.Y. 2019-20 at Kolkata. Shri Ashok Kr. Pradhan, Managing Director & CEO, UBI today said UBI total bsiness stood at Rs.208725 crore with Deposits at Rs.134304 crore and Advances at Rs.74441 crore. He said, Deposits grew by 3.33%, Advances increased by 11.36% and Total Business improved by 6.06% on Year over Year basis.


    Shri Pradhan said Current Account & Savings Account (CASA) deposits at Rs.68387 crore further improved to 50.92% of total deposits with a growth of 169 bps Year over Year. He said the Bank registered a quarterly Net Profit of Rs.123.88 crore for the Qr ended Septernber 2019 against a Net Loss of Rs.883.17 crore in the corresponding quarter last fiscal. On sequential basis, Net Profit for the quarter increased by approx 18% from Rs.104.99 crore (Qr FY-20).


    He said in this financial year the Bank earned Operating Profit of Rs.598.57 crore for Q2 ended September 2019 against an Operating Profit of Rs.206.37 crore in September 2018 quarter, registering a growth of 190.78% on Year over Year basis. Net interest margin (NIM) increased to 2.91 % in Q2 FY-20 thereby recording an improvement of 75 bps over 2.16% in Q2 FY-19. Net Interest Income (NII) for quarter ended September 2019 increased to Rs.772.78 crore against Rs.442.60 crore in the same quarter last fiscal.


    He said Cost to Income Ratio improved to 55.55% against 77.56% over the corresponding period previous year.
