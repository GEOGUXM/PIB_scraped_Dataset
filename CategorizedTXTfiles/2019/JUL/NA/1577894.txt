      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah honours the outgoing Director, IB Shri Rajiv Jain and Secretary, R&AW Shri Anil Dhasmana.
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2019 1:39PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah hosted a dinner in honour of retired Director, Intelligence Bureau (IB) Shri Rajiv Jain and Secretary, R&AW Shri Anil Dhasmana here on Monday (08-07-2019) evening.


    Speaking on the occasion, Shri Amit Shah lauded their contribution in strengthening National Security during their illustrious career spanning 40 years.


    Shri Jain and Shri Dhasmana thanked the Home Minister for the gesture of hosting a dinner in their honour.


    Ministers of State (MoSs) for Home Affairs, Shri Nityanand Rai and Shri G Kishan Reddy, National Security Advisor, Shri Ajit Doval, Union Home Secretary Shri Rajiv Gauba and other senior officers were present on the occasion.
