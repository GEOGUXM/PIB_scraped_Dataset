      Ministry of Education

      Union HRD Minister inaugurates 8th Deputy Commissioners' Conference of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2019 6:40PM by PIB Delhi


    Union HRD Minister Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank today inaugurated the 8 th Deputy Commissioners' Conference of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathana in New Delhi. The three-day long annual conference of KVS is being attended by all Deputy Commissioners of its 25 Regions and 05 Directors of Zonal Institutes of Education and Training (ZIETs).


    Addressing the inaugural session Dr. Nishank said that top leadership shall adopt highly positive approach for achieving positive results. He said that small initiatives can also bring big changes in the society and we shall motivate the children to adopt habits which can bring changes in long run. He emphasised on adopting the habit of water conservation and cleanliness by children. For a strong and prosperous India, we have to prepare a dedicated entrepreneurial generation. Our schools have the responsibility of preparing the country's future leadership, he said.


    Union HRD Minister, who also holds the post of Chairman, KVS, announced that the pending results for the examinations held on various posts of KVS will be declared within a week. The kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan will announce these results on 8 th July 2019.


    Sh. Ram Chandra Meena, Joint Secretary, MHRD who is also the Vice Chairman of KVS congratulated the senior leadership of KVS for the outstanding performance in various fields, be it Academics, Sports, or Art & Culture.


    Commissioner, KVS Sh. Santosh Kumar Mall welcomed the Chief Guest and other dignitaries on this occasion and outlined the agenda of this three day conference. Sh. Saurabh Jain, Additional Commissioner (Admin), KVS presented the vote of thanks.
