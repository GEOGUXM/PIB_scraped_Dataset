      PM greets people on Ashadhi Bij, the Kutchi New Year
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2019 3:01PM by PIB Delhi


      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the auspicious occasion of Ashadhi Bij, the Kutchi New Year.


      “Greetings on the auspicious occasion of Ashadhi Bij, the Kutchi New Year. May the coming year bring joy and prosperity. May everyone’s aspirations be fulfilled”, the Prime Minister said.
