      Ministry of Defence

      20th Anniversary of Kargil Vijay Diwas celebrated at Eastern Command
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2019 12:03PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July.26, 2019


    The 20th Anniversary of Kargil Vijay Diwas was celebrated by Eastern Command in Kolkata today. Lt. General MM Naravane, General Officer Commanding in Chief, Eastern Command laid a wreath at Vijay Smarak, Fort William Kolkata and paid homage to the martyrs, He urged all to carry forward the legacy established by the immortal soldiers and continue serving the Nation with renewed vigor and dedication.


    The day is named after the successful Operation Vijay in 1999, when India regained the control of the high outposts which had been stealthily taken over by Pakistan. The theme of this year’s celebration is “Remember, Rejoice and Renew”to remember the martyrs, rejoice their sacrifices and strengthen the resolve of the armed forces to the cause of the nation. The objective of celebrating Kargil Vijay Diwas was to evoke feelings of nationalism and patriotism through nationwide campaigns amongst the youth and to pay homage to the soldiers whose sacrifices made this victory possible.


    A number of events including “Run for Fun”, “Ek Daud Shaheedon ke Naam” along with motivational lectures at schools and colleges were part of the day-long celebration.
