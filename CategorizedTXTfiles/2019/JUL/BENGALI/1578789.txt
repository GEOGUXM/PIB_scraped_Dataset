      Ministry of External Affairs

      Passport Mela to be organised at Passport Seva Kendras and Post Office Passport Seva Kendras in West Bengal
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2019 5:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 July 2019


    Passport Melawill be organized at Passport Seva Kendras (PSK) in Kolkata, Agartala and Siliguri as well as Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in Bangaon, Ranaghat,Beadon Street and Krishnanagaron July 27 (Saturday). Appointments for the same will be released in the website www.passportindia.gov.in from July 17 (Wednesday) at 3.30 P.M. onwards. The number of appointments for fresh/re-issue passports issued from various centres are: PSK Kolkata – 1400 (Normal Category) , PSK Agartala – 100 (Tatkaal Category), PSK Siliguri – 150 (Normal Category), POPSK Bangaon – 50 (Normal Category), POPSK Ranaghat – 50 (Normal Category), POPSK Beadon Street -50 (Normal Category) and POPSK Krishnanagar – 50 (Normal Category).


    Applicants must carry their Application Reference Number (ARN) along with necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (both in original and self attested duplicate copies) at the time of appointment. The detailed information of required documents can also be obtained from the above-stated website. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system. Only those applicants who have obtained appointments for 27.07.2019 through online mode (www.passportindia.gov.in) will be entertained. On-hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed on that day.
