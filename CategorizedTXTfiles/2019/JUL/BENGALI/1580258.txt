      INDIANOIL RANKS THIRD STRONGEST BRAND IN THE COUNTRY
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2019 3:53PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 25, 2019


    Indian Oil Corporation Limited has ranked third strongest brand in the country in a recent brand survey by Brand Finance.


    Based on its performance on parameters such as Marketing Investment, Stakeholder Equity, and Business Performance, the company has been assigned a Brand Strength Index (BSI) score of 84.6 out of 100, making it the strongest oil & gas brand in the country. IndianOil registered a huge improvement as compared to its previous score of 77.2 out of 100 in the survey last year.


    With a 33,000-plus work force, extensive refining, distribution & marketing infrastructure and advanced R&D facilities, lndianOil plays a significant role in fuelling the socio-economic development of the country. With a mandate to ensure India's energy security and self-sufficiency in refining & marketing of petroleum products, lndianOil in the past six decades has provided energy access to millions of people across the length and breadth of the country through its ever-expanding network of over 50,000 customer touch-points.


    With a turnover of Rs. 6, 05,924 crores and a net profit of Rs. 16,894 crores in 2018-19, IndianOil is one the largest Corporates in the country.


    The annual report on Indian brands is published by a London-based independent business valuation and strategy consultant – Brand Finance. Every year, Brand Finance values 5,000 of the world’s biggest brands. The 100 most valuable Indian brands have been included in the ‘Brand Finance India 100 2019’ ranking.
