      Ministry of Railways

      SER BAGS GOVIND VALLABH PANT SHIELD FOR BEST PERFORMANCE AMONG ALL ZONAL RAILWAYS
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 2:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 1, 2019


    South Eastern Railway has bagged the prestigious “Govind Vallabh Pant Shield” for overall efficiency during the financial year 2018-19 as announced by the Ministry of Railways. The Govind Vallabh Pant Shield is awarded by Ministry of Railways to the best performing Zonal Railway. This apart, South Eastern Railway has also bagged the Signal & Telecommunication Engineering Shield and Civil Engineering Construction Shield. The National Railway Week Award Ceremony will be held at Mumbai on 7 th July, 2019. The prestigious Govind Vallabh Pant Shield and other Shields will be handed over to the General Manager, South Eastern Railway by the Hon’ble Minister of Railways, Sri Piyush Goyal.


    Shri P.S.Mishra, General Manager, South Eastern Railway has congratulated all the staff and officers of this Railway for their outstanding performance.
