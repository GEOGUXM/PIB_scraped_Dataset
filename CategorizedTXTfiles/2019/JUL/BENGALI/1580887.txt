      MULAKAT WITH MULTILINGUAL WRITERS AT KOLKATA
      Posted On: 31 JUL 2019 5:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 31, 2019


    Sahitya Akademi organized a Mulakat programme with multilingual young poets at its auditorium at Kolkata on 30 July 2019. In the programme, Dr Devendra Kumar Devesh delivered the welcome address and he introduced the poets to the audience. In the programme, Bengali poet Sri Falguni Ghosh, English poet Ms Nikita Parik, Hindi poet Smt Ghungru Parmar, Santali poet Sri Sutalal Saren and Urdu poet Sri Fahed Pasha read out from their selected writings. Their poems were based on the contemporary social situations and the surroundings as well as their personal feelings and experiences. Santali poet Sri Saren read out his poems in original with Bengali and Hindi translations. Dr Subodh Sarkar, eminent Bengali poet and Convener, Bengali Advisory Board, Sahitya Akademi, expressed that this kind of experience to be with multilingual poetry in Kolkata was rare. The programme is well attended local writers, poets and poetry lovers.
