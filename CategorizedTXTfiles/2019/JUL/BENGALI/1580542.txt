      Ministry of Defence

      KARGIL VIJAY DIWAS AT PANAGARH MILITARY STATION
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2019 3:06PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 27 July 2019


    Lt Gen S S Mishra, General Officer Commanding, 17 Corps laid a wreath at the War Memorial in Panagarh Military Station, to commemorate the 20th anniversary of nation's victory in Kargil Operations.


    Tributes were paid to the martyrs through a series of events including motivational talks, runs for fun at various educational institutions in Durgapur, Masimpur, Bankura, Burdwan, Nabagram, Berhampore and Purulia were organized to promote the spirit of national integration.


    The events were designed to evoke a feeling of nationalism and patriotism, especially among the youth. The students were further made aware about career prospects in the Army.
