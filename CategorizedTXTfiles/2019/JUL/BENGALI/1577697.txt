      Ministry of Home Affairs

      BSF and DRI Aizawl rescued Exotic Birds/Wildlife worth Rs 28 Lakhs
      Posted On: 08 JUL 2019 2:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 8, 2019


    A team of BSF officials, Aizawl and DRI Aizawl, acting on a tip off, conducted a joint Ops in general area of Khamrang, between Sairang and Kawnpui road Kolasib, Mizoram on Saturday (06 July 2019). In the ensuing operation, they intercepted two suspected vehicles. On checking the vehicles, Ops party found 26 suspicious boxes and on further checking these boxes the party recovered 26 exotic and endangered birds which were being smuggled from Myanmar and destined for Kerala. In this connection 4 suspects from Kerala who came to receive the consignment were detained. Two vehicle drivers along with co-drivers were also apprehended for illegally transporting these birds. The birds were found encaged in 23 small and 03 big boxes. A total of 26 birds which included 01 EMU, 02 Crown headed Pigeon and 23 Cockatoo (yellow crested) white in colour. Total worth of these birds were estimated to be appx 28 lakh rupees. These illegal businesses are clandestinely carried out randomly through Indian-Myanmar borders. It is pertinent to mention that this is the second such recovery made in Aizawl this year.
