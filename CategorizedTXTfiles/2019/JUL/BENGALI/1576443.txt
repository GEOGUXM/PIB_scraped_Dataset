      Durgapur Steel Plant Observes 137th Birth Anniversary of Dr B C Roy
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 3:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 1, 2019


    Durgapur Steel Plant (DSP) observed the 137 th birth anniversary of the legendary doctor and former Chief Minister of West Bengal Dr Bidhan Chandra Roy today in the plant premises in a befitting manner.


    Shri P K Pradhan, Executive Director (P&A), DSP garlanded the bust of Dr Roy at Ispat Bhawan. A large number of senior officials and employees of DSP were also present on the occasion and paid floral tribute Dr Roy.


    The medical fraternity of DSP Main Hospital observed the day and organised a special programme to mark the occasion.


    Several socio-cultural organisations in and around the steel township also observed the day with honour.
