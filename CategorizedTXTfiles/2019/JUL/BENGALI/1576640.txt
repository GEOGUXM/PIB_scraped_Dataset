      Ministry of Textiles

      SCHEME FOR DEVELOPMENT OF KNITTING AND KNITWEAR SECTOR
      Posted On: 02 JUL 2019 2:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 02 July 2019


    The Ministry of Textiles has issued Resolution of the Scheme for Development of Knitting & Knitwear Sector under Power Tex India, which is effective from 1 st July 2019 to 31 st March 2020. The Knitting & Knitwear Sector is predominantly MSME in size. It is one of the major employment generators and contributes about 27% of the total fabric production in India & about 15% of Knitted fabrics is being exported.
