      PRESS COUNCIL INVITES ENTRIES FOR NATIONAL AWARDS FOR EXCELLENCE IN JOURNALISM 2019
      Posted On: 08 JUL 2019 2:59PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 8, 2019


    The Press Council of India has invited Entries by August 30, 2019 from journalists/freelancers in order to recognize outstanding contribution of journalists and photo journalists of Indian nationality in print journalism.


    The Press Council of India, mandated by an Act of Parliament to improve standards of journalism and maintain press freedom has instituted “National Awards” to honour the journalists on the occasion of National Press Day which falls on November 16 for excelling in the fields of print journalism in the categories mentioned below.
