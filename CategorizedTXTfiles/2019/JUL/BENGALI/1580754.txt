      AIR FORCE STATION SINGARSI CELEBRATES KARGIL VIJAY DIWAS
      Posted On: 30 JUL 2019 5:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, July 30, 2019


    Air Force Station Singarsi, Jhadkhand celebrated the 20th anniversary of Kargil Vijay Diwas from 20-28 Jul 2019.


    The Station Commander, Group Captain NS Roy on this occasion flagged off a Run for Fun which saw participation not only from the Air Warriors but their Sanginis as well as children.


    Motivational posters and banners were placed at prominent locations and video displaying the bravery of the armed forces during the Kargil War were also played for the station personnel.


    The station had an opportunity to remember and pay tribute to the Veterans who made the Indian Air Force and the nation proud by their valour and sacrifice.
