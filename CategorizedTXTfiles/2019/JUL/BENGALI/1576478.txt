      Ministry of External Affairs

      Passport Mela to be organised at Passport Seva Kendras and Post Office Passport Seva Kendras in West Bengal
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 4:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 1 July, 2019


    Passport Mela will be organized at Passport Seva Kendras (PSK) in Kolkata, Agartala and Siliguri as well as Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in Bangaon, Ranaghat and Beadon Street on July 13 (Saturday). Appointments for the same will be released in the website www.passportindia.gov.in from July 3 (Wednesday) at 3.30 P.M. onwards. The number of appointments for normal category (fresh/re-issue) passports issued from various centres are: PSK Kolkata – 1290, PSK Agartala – 80, PSK Siliguri – 150, POPSK Bangaon – 40, POPSK Ranaghat – 40, POPSK Beadon Street -40. In addition to these, appointments will be given also for 50 number of tatkaal (fresh/re-issue) at PSK Agartala.


    Applicants must carry their Application Reference Number (ARN) along with necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (both in original and self attested duplicate copies) at the time of appointment. The detailed information of required documents can also be obtained from the above-stated website. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system. Only those applicants who have obtained appointments for 13.07.2019 through online mode (www.passportindia.gov.in) will be entertained. On-hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed on that day.
