      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Shri Kiren Rijiju gave Arjuna Awards to Smriti Mandhana and Rohan Bopanna
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2019 5:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Kiren Rijiju, Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Youth Affairs & Sports today gave Arjuna Awards to Ms. Smriti Mandhana (Cricket) and Shri Rohan Bopanna (Tennis) in a ceremony held in New Delhi.


    Ms. Smriti Mandhana and Shri Rohan Bopanna were selected for Arjuna Awards 2018 for their outstanding achievements in the sports disciplines of Cricket and Tennis respectively. They could not receive the Arjuna Awards from President of India during the Award Ceremony held on 25 th September 2018, as they were out of the country at that time taking part in international tournaments.


    Ms. Smriti Mandhana is o ne of the finest women cricketers to have represented India in all formats in recent times. She is first Indian woman to achieve a double-hundred in a one-day game. She is the only Indian player to be named in the ICC Women's Team of the Year 2016. She was given Best International Cricketer (Women) Award by BCCI for 2017-18.
