      EFLU VC MEETS DIRECTOR OF SOUTH INDIA BRITISH COUNCIL
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2019 7:02PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Prof. E. Suresh Kumar, Vice Chancellor, The English and Foreign Languages University met Ms. Janaka Pushpanathan, Director, British Council, South India and Ms. Anu Thampi, Head, Schools of English and Skills, British Council, South India, today at the EFL University. They discussed the possibility of collaborating on new projects in English Language Training. Prof. Suresh spoke about the Universityâ€™s significant achievements in the field of English Language Teaching, Training and Research as a great resource to initiate collaboration with the British Council.


    Ms. Janaka Pushpanathan, while apprising the Vice Chancellor about the on-going collaborative projects between the two institutions, expressed interest for student exchange programmes in the future. Referring to the Action Research Mentor Scheme (ARMS) of the British Council, she suggested that the School of English Language Education at the University could participate in the programme. She also discussed the British Council Scholarships and the collaborative Multi-lingual Project in which both the institutions are currently engaged.


    Prof. Surabhi Bharati, Dean, International Relations and Prof. Meera Srinivas, Director, Centre for Consultancy and Language Training (CCLT) were present at the meeting.
