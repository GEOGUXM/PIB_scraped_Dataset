      BSNL GOES GREEN, BILLS NOW TO GO ONLY ON DIGITAL MODE
      Posted On: 29 JUL 2019 2:24PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 29 th July 2019


    Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL), Ahmedabad has decided to “Go GREEN’ under the ‘Digital India’ concept of Government of India. Hence, it is hereby informed to all our valuable mobile and landline customers that from 01.08.2019, all the postpaid bills will be sent on your registered mobile number/e-mail address. Therefore, all the subscribers are requested to register their mobile number/e-mail address at the nearest CSC/Cash Counter/Accounts office. Customers can also send complete details of their Landline/Mobile/Ftth postpaid connection through e-mail on “BSNLGOGREENATD@GMAIL.COM’ at the earliest to enable this office to timely incorporate your details in the system, if not yet registered. We hope to serve you better at all times.
