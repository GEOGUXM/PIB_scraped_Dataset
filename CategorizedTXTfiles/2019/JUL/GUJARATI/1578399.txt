      Office Of The Official Liquidator Shifted To S.G. Highway, Thaltej
      Posted On: 11 JUL 2019 5:12PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 11.07.2019


    The Office of the official Liquidator, Ministry of Corporate Affairs attached with the High Court of Gujarat has been recently shifted from its old premises i.e. Jivabhai Chambers, Ashram Road to the newly constructed Corporate Bhawan, behind Zydus Hospital, S.G. Highway, Thaltej. All concerned workers of closed mills, corporate, bankers and stakeholders are requested to please take note of the aforesaid shifting of and change of the address of the Office of Official Liquidator, High Court of Gujarat, Ahmedabad.
