      Shri. Vishwanath Singh Jadon takes over as Accountant General
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2019 11:21AM by PIB Chennai


    Shri. Vishwanath Singh Jadon, IA&AS has taken over charge as Accountant General (Economic & Revenue Sector Audit), Tamil Nadu, Chennai on 01-07-2019. Earlier, he served as Senior Deputy Accountant General in the office of the Accountant General, Odisha, Bhubaneswar. Prior to that he served as Director (Audit) in the Office of Principal Director of Audit, London.


    He belongs to Indian Audit and Accounts Service of 2003 batch. He has done M.Tech in Thermal Engineering and Certified Information System Auditor (CISA) by ISACA, USA.
