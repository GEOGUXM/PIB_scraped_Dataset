      NABARD awards IOB for excellence in performance under SHG Bank Linkage Programme
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2019 5:32PM by PIB Chennai


    Indian Overseas Bank has been continuously exhibiting an outstanding performance under SHG- Bank Linkage in the state of Tamil Nadu with special focus on rural women to make them Bankable through SHG Linkage. As a mark of recognition, NABARD has awarded Indian Overseas Bank, the Second prize among the Public Sector Commercial Banks for excellence in performance under SHG Bank Linkage Programme in Tamil Nadu during the year 2018-19.


    Shri Ajay Kumar Srivastava, Executive Director of Indian Overseas Bank received the award and citation from Thiru. D.Jayakumar, Minister of Fisheries, Personnel and Administrative Reforms, Tamil Nadu on the eve of NABARDâ€™s 38 th Foundation Day Celebration held at Chennai on 13.07.2019.
