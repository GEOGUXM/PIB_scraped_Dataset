      Prasar Bharati invites applications for Marketing Executive
      Posted On: 17 JUL 2019 3:08PM by PIB Chennai


    Prasar Bharati is inviting applications for Marketing Executive and Marketing Executive Gr. I for Doordarshan Kendra, Chennai (1 post each) and All India Radio, Chennai (1 post each) on contractual basis.


    Filled in applications for the posts can be sent by Registered / Speed Post to The Head of Programmes, Doordarshan Kendra, No.5, Swami Sivananda Salai, Chepauk, Chennai 600 005 or can be sent through E-mail to dtcddkchennai@gmail.com . Last Date for receipt of applications is 06/08/2019.


    For more details log on to www.prasarbharati.gov.in/allvacancies.php
