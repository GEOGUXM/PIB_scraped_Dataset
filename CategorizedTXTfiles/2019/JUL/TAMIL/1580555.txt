      CELEBRATION OF 20th ANNIVERSARY OF KARGIL VIJAY DIWAS
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2019 5:43PM by PIB Chennai


    To commemorate the 20 th anniversary of Kargil war “Kargil Vijay Diwas” was celebrated at Air Force Station, Tambaram. Towards this various events were organised which showcased the courage, valor and sacrifices of the armed forces. In order to spread the message of Kargil victory a “Victory Run” was conducted which was attended by all personnel of the station. Inter School Painting Competition was organised at Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 2 on the theme of “Kargil Victory” and large number of students participated in the event with enthusiasm.


    Static display of aircraft, various aircraft weapon systems, Security and safety equipment were also set up to give the public an insight of the systems used by the Indian Air Force. The event was attended by enthusiastic students of KCG Engineering College, Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Kendriya Vidyalayas, Zion Metriculation and Higher Secondary School, Alwin Memorial Public School, Baal Vidya mandir and Sri Narayana Mission Senior Secondary School. The crowd was attended by Indian Air Force representatives, who motivated and guided them to various displays and briefed them about the functioning. Students expressed their inquisitiveness by asking various queries regarding the aircraft and equipments displayed.


    All events were inaugurated / flagged off by Air Commodore Man Singh Awana Ati Vishisht Seva Medal Vishisht Seva Medal, Air Officer Commanding Air Force Station, Tambaram. The Air Officer interacted with students during the static display event and encouraged students to join Air Force.
