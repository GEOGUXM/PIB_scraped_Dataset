      Ministry of Communications

      Postal Pension Adalat to be held in Mumbai on August 23, 2019
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2019 2:17PM by PIB Mumbai


    The Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai will be holding the Postal Pension Adalat for pensioners / family pensioners of Postal Department on Friday, 23-08-2019 from 11.00 AM onwards at the Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai - 400 001.


    Pensioners can apply in the pro forma given here to Sr. Accounts Officer, O/o Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai GPO Bldg., 2nd Floor, Mumbai - 400 001 on or before 23.07.2019. The applications received after 23.07.2019 will not be considered in the Pension Adalat.


    It is further intimated that the cases earlier scheduled to be taken up in the Pension Adalat dated 25.07.2019 stand extended and will now be taken up on 23.08.2019.
