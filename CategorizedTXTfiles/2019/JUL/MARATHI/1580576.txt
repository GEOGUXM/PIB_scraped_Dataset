      Mann Ki Baat programme has now become programme of Dil Ki Baat, Ghar Ki Baat- Prakash Javadekar
      Posted On: 28 JUL 2019 1:02PM by PIB Mumbai

      Pune residents listened to Prime Minister's Mann Ki Baat today, in the company of Union Minister for Environment, Forest & Climate Change and Information & Broadcasting, Shri Prakash Javadekar. While talking to the audience after the programme, the Union Minister said,
      "This is second edition of Mann ki Baat Programme of Prime Minister. This programme has become a household name and very popular programme. Because it comes from the heart and it touches everybody.Therefore, today he laid emphasis on Water conservation, water retention, efficient utilisation of water, water saving. Prime Minister also talked about Book corner, reading culture, he gave example of students who have conquered cancer and are playing at world stage. Prime Minister also talked about Chandrayaan and inspired students to do study in Space technology. He promised to take winners from quiz contest to Sriharikota. Prime Minister talked about cleanliness because Swachh Bharat has become national mission now. He talked about dialogue and development taking place in Kashmir and how it has defeated nefarious designs of separatists . Prime Minister finally appealed that 15th August should become a public movement and a household ceremony."
