      Ministry of Communications

      Pension Adalat on 23 August by Government of Maharashtra in coordination with The Accountant General (A&E)-II, Maharashtra , Nagpur
      Posted On: 27 JUL 2019 12:40PM by PIB Mumbai


    A Pension Adalat for the redressal of grievances of retired employees of Government of Maharashtra will be held at Sai SAbhagruh, Gandhi Nagar, Nagpur on 23 Auguest 2019 Representatives of the State Government and Accountant General office will be present at the Adalat for hearing and resolving the grievances.


    The Adalat will be held in two sessions. In the first session from 9 AM to 1 PM, grievances of retired officials from the districts of Nagpur, Gondia, Bhandara, Wardha, Chandrapur, Gadchiroli, Amravati, Yavatmal, Akola, will be heard/addressed. The grievances of the retired officials from the districts of Aurangabad, Beed, Jalna, Latur, Nanded, Osmanabad, Parbhani, Hingoli Washim and Buldhana will be heard/addressed during the second session from 1 PM to 4 PM.


    In order to facilitate prompt and efficient redressal of the grievances, the pensioners are requested to register their grievances with the Accountant General in advance. Grievances can be registered by submitting the same at the Office of the Accountant General(A&E)-II, Maharashtra, Opposite Ravi Bhavan, Civil Lines, Nagpur - 440001 on or before 14 August. Grievances may also be sent by post to the above address well in advance so as to reach on or before 14 August. Grievances can also be sent by email on or before 14 August to the following e-mail id: pensiontakrarngp@gmail.com. The complaints can also be sent by SMS/WhatsApp to contact number 9423441755 .


    Grievances relating to State Government departments related ,matters may be addressed to ‘Joint Director- Accounts & Treasury, Collectorate Premises, Civil Lines, Nagpur”, email jd.nagpur@mahakosh.in
