      Ministry of Textiles

      Cotton Production in Maharashtra
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2019 4:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The Cotton Advisory Board (CAB) has estimated the cotton production in Maharashtra for the current cotton season 2018-19 as 77 lakh bales. The details of cotton production in Maharashtra during the last three seasons vis-à-vis current season is as under:-


    (Quantity in lakh bales of 170 kgs each)

                                                                    
Cotton season                     2015-16  2016-17  2017-18  2018-19
                                                                    
                                                                    
(October – September)                                               
                                                                    
                                                                    
                                                                    
                                                                    
                                                                    
Cotton production in Maharashtra  76.00    88.50    83.35    77.00  
                                                                    
                                                                    

    This information was given by the Union Minister of Textiles, Smriti Zubin Irani , in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
