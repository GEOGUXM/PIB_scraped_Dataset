      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointment
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2019 11:32AM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the appointment of Shri Vikas Swarup, IFS (1986), High Commissioner in Ottawa as Secretary (CPV & OIA) in the Ministry of External Affairs with effect from 01.08.2019.
