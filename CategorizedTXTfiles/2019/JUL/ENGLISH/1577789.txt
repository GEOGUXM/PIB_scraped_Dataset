      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Construction of New NHs in Rajasthan
      Posted On: 08 JUL 2019 5:23PM by PIB Delhi


    A total of 19 projects costing Rs. 14,411 crore have been approved for construction of new National Highways in the State of Rajasthan. Out of these, 12 projects costing Rs. 8,885 crore are in construction stage and remaining 7 projects amounting to Rs. 5,526 crore are ready for commencement.


    This information was given by the Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways Shri Nitin J Gadkari in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
