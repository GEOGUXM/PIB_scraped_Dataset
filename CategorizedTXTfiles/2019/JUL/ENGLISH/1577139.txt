      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Toy Kits for Children in Anganwadis
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2019 2:40PM by PIB Delhi


    Anganwadi Services is a centrally sponsored scheme under the Umbrella Integrated Child Development Service (ICDS) Scheme, Pre-school non-formal education is one of the six free services provided to the Children in the age group of 3-6 years through Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) located across the country. Under ICDS(General), funds for procurement of Pre-school Education/ Early Child Care Education (ECCE) Kits (including Training) at each AWCs/Mini AWCs @ Rs. 5000/- per AWC per annum are provided to all the States/UTs. States /UTs utilize this fund for procurement of educational items, black board, audio-video visuals and activity books in the AWCs.
