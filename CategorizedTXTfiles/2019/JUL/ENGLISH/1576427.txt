      Ministry of Defence

      Waiver in Landing charges at Defence Airports
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 3:02PM by PIB Delhi


    Exemption from payment of landing and parking charges for civil aircraft operating from IAF airfields under Regional Connectivity Scheme has already been published in the Gazette of India for 32 airfields and 5 Advanced Landing Grounds.


    The details of exemption given with respect to the airfields/ALG is given below.

                                      
1. Adampur        20. Jammu           
                                      
                                      
                                      
2. Agra           21. Jorhat          
                                      
                                      
                                      
3. Ambala         22. Kalaikunda
