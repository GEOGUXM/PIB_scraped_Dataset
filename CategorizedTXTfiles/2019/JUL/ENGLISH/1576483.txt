      Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

      Training to Men, Women and Persons with Disability
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 4:42PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship through National Skill Development and Corporation promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training through Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20. State Governments are expected to facilitate the establishment of PMKKs, by provision of land etc. Targets under Centrally Sponsored State Managed â€“ PMKVY 2016-20 may be allocated to PMKKs by State Governments.


    Under the skill ecosystem, the concerned Sector Skill Council (SSC) develop sector specific QPs/NOS for various job roles. Some courses/job roles developed by concerned SSCs which are in high demand are kept under PMKVY for imparting skill development training.


    As on 12.06.2019, the number of women, men and persons with disability trained in the PMKKs under the PMKVY are as under:
