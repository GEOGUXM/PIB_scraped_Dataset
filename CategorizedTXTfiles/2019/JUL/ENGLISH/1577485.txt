      A path breaking budget towards making India 5 Trillion Dollar Economy: VC NITI
      Posted On: 05 JUL 2019 4:35PM by PIB Delhi

      Vice Chairman NITI Aayog Dr. Rajiv Kumar has hailed Union Budget 2019-20 as a path breaking budget which sets out a clear path for accelerating private investment driven economic growth for India to emerge as a Five trillion dollar economy. At the same time it has effectively addressed the major challenges facing the economy at present.
      NITI Aayog congratulates the Finance Minister Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman on her maiden budget and looks forward very much to contributing to the country's march forward.
