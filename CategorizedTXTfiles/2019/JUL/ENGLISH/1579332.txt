      Over 22,000 MSMEs registered under ZED scheme
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2019 3:23PM by PIB Delhi


    More than 22,288 Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) have registered themselves to take up Zero Defect, Zero Effect (ZED) manufacturing under ZED scheme.
