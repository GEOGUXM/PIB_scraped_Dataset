      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Prize Money for Medal Winners
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2019 3:32PM by PIB Delhi


    Cash Awards to the medal winners in international sports events including those in Olympics Games, Commonwealth Games and Asian Games are given under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports’ scheme ‘Special Awards to Medal Winners in International Sports events and their Coaches’. Amount of award money to medal winners of Olympic Games, Commonwealth Games and Asian Games is as follows:


    (In Rs.)

                                                          
Name of event       Gold Medal  Silver Medal  Bronze Medal
                                                          
                                                          
                                                          
Olympics Games      75 lakh     50 lakh       30 lakh     
                                                          
                                                          
                                                          
Asian Games         30 lakh     20 lakh       10 lakh     
                                                          
                                                          
                                                          
Commonwealth Games  30 lakh     20 lakh       10 lakh     
                                                          
                                                          

    The details of award money given to individual sportspersons on winning medal in the World Cup/ World Championship under the scheme are as follows:


    (In Rs.)

                                                                                                    
Name of event                                                 Gold Medal  Silver Medal  Bronze Medal
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
World Championship/ World Cup (conducted in four year cycle)  40 lakh     25 lakh       15 lakh     
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
World Championship/ World Cup                                 20 lakh     14 lakh       8 lakh      
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
(held once in two years)                                                                            
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
World Championship/ World Cup                                 10 lakh     7 lakh        4 lakh      
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    
(held annually)                                                                                     
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                    

    For team events, the amount of cash award is determined as under :

                                                                                                                                             
Strength of ‘Team’ on the ground     Number of times the award amount for the corresponding event, medal, level and category of participation
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
(i) A team of two persons            1 ½ times                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
(ii) A team of 3 or 4 persons        2 times                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
(iii) A team of 5 to 10 persons      3 times                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             
(iv) A team of more than 10 persons  5 times                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                             

    The actual prize money determined as above is shared equally among the team members. However, no member of team gets less than 50% of the award amount for the corresponding individual event and medal. Cash award is given to the individual/team on winning medal in international sports events as per the criteria prescribed in the scheme. The scheme is equally applicable to the sportspersons of Kabaddi discipline.


    This information was given by the Minister of State (I/C) Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today.
