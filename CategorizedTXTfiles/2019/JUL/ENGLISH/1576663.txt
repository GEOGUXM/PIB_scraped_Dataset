      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Monitoring of ISI Marked Productions
      Posted On: 02 JUL 2019 3:44PM by PIB Delhi


    The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) license holder is required to operate Scheme of Inspection and Testing (SIT), which prescribes the specific tests as per relevant Indian standard and the frequency for conducting them. BIS carries out surveillance on the manufacturers holding BIS license through visit to the factory premises at periodic intervals. During the surveillance, verification of the implementation of SIT and compliance of the product to relevant standard is verified. Product samples are also drawn and sent to BIS laboratory or BIS recognized laboratory for checking conformance to relevant standard.


    In addition, samples of the product bearing BIS standard mark are also purchased from the market and got tested at laboratory for checking conformance to relevant standard. Based on the non-conformance if any, observed from the test report, actions is initiated against the manufacturer as per BIS (Conformity Assessment) Regulations, 2018.

    Cases are registered on the basis of evidence found during the raids at the premises of non-licensees. All cases of raids where evidence of misuse of BIS Standard Mark is observed are filed in the Court of Law under Section 29 of BIS Act. As on date, 1078 criminal cases are pending in various courts all over India. However, the number of cases registered during the last 3 year is as under:
