      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Rs 3000 crore allocated for Ministry of DoNER in General Budget 2019-20
      Posted On: 05 JUL 2019 1:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Minister of Finance and Corporate Affairs Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman presented the Union Budget 2019-20 in Parliament here today.


    The Budget Estimates for Ministry of Development of North Easter Region for FY 2019-20 is Rs 3,000 crore as compared to the RE of Rs 2629 crore in FY 2018-19.
