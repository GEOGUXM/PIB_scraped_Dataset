      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Use of Plastic Waste in Roads/Highways Construction
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2019 3:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has issued guidelines for use of plastic waste in wearing course of National Highways on pilot basis. The plastic waste has been used in the state of Tamil Nadu for about 11 Km length and about 1 Km length in the state of Kerala.


    This information was given by the Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways Shri Nitin Gadkari in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
