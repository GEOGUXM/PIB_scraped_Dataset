      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Financial performance of BHEL
      Posted On: 02 JUL 2019 12:03PM by PIB Delhi


    Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) has reported profit in each of the last 3 years (i.e. 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19) as follows:


    Amount in Rs. Crore

                                                   
BHEL: Standalone Results  2016-17  2017-18  2018-19
                                                   
                                                   
                                                   
Profit Before Tax (PBT)   628      1,585    2,058  
                                                   
                                                   
                                                   
Profit After Tax (PAT)    496      807      1,215  
                                                   
                                                   

    The Government has appointed 2 Government Nominee Directors and 4 Independent Directors who are in place on the Board of BHEL (as on 19.06.2019) who actively contribute in overseeing the Companyâ€™s strategic direction, reviewing and monitoring corporate performance, ensuring regulatory compliance and Corporate Governance, and safeguarding the interests of the shareholders.


    Further, the working and performance of BHEL is reviewed by the Department of Heavy Industry (DHI) on a regular basis. DHI also assists the Company in the achievements of its plan through advisories/ suggestions, suitable inter-ministerial interventions and taking up its issues with other Govt. Agencies/ Departments/ Ministries and Customers on case to case basis.


    This information was given by the Minister of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises, Arvind Ganpat Sawant, in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha yesterday.
