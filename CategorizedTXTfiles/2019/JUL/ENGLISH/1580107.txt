      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC approval
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2019 5:13PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the extension of the term of appointment of Shri Sanjay Kothari, lAS (HY:78) (Retired) as Secretary to the President, on co-terminus basis with the President or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
