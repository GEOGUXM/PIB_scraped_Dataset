    Border Roads Organisation is a Central Government Department under Ministry of Defence. All the facilities granted by Central Government to its employees governed under Central Civil Services Rules (CCS Rules) are also admissible to GREF Personnel. Since BRO is working in difficult and inhospitable areas with adverse climatic conditions, therefore, following additional facilities are also extended to the employees:

      * Free ration at par with Army
      * Free Uniform
      * CSD canteen facilities at par with Army
      * Free single accommodation to subordinates. Free single accommodation to Officers in non-static locations only.
      * 60 days Earned Leave in field units having six working days in a week etc.

    The following facilities have been provided to casual paid labourers working in BRO:

      * Compensation under Employees Compensation Act, 1923(ECA).
      * Financial assistance in the event of death from Border Roads Special Relief Fund (BRSRF).
      * Non-productivity bonus.
      * Ex-gratia amount is paid to the casual paid labourers or to their NOK @ Rs. 5 Lacs, 4 Lacs & 3 Lacs in case of death, permanent disability and partial disability respectively.
      * Warming facilities for casual paid labourers working above 8000 Ft height.
      * Supply of safe drinking water, free electricity in casual paid labourers Camp and issue of ration on Govt. rates.
      * Nursery/Primary School facilities and crèches for children of CPLs, vegetable shops.
      * Transport facilities to worksite is also being provided.
      * Medical facilities are being provided to casual paid labourers through Medical Inspection Room.
      * Shelter for accommodation etc.

    The facilities provided to casual paid labourers have not been withdrawn/discontinued.


    All benefits as applicable to women employees of Central Government are automatically extended to women employees in BRO.


    BRO engages labourers on requirement basis preferably from local areas. BRO is a defence organization and formation of union is not allowed.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Naba Kumar Sarania in Lok Sabha today.
