      Ministry of Tribal Affairs

      Upliftment of tribal people
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 4:30PM by PIB Delhi


    Major part of infrastructure development in tribal dominated areas and provision of basic amenities to tribal people in the country is carried out through various schemes / programmes of concerned Central Ministries and the State Governments. Central Ministries have been mandated to earmark 4.3% to 17.5% of their total Scheme allocation every year for projects and schemes for the welfare and development of tribal population of the country.


    Details of Tribal Sub Scheme (TSS) / Scheduled Tribe Component (STC)/ Tribal Sub Plan (TSP) funds allocated by Central Ministries / Departments, State Governments and Ministry of Tribal Affairs under various schemes catering to Education, Health, Agriculture, Livelihood, Housing, Drinking water & Sanitation, Employment Generation, Skill Development, Women and Child Development etc. during 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as below:
