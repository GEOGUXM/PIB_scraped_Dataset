      Widening of Trade Deficit
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2019 4:03PM by PIB Delhi

    Trade deficit marginally widened to USD 15.36 billion in May 2019 from USD 14.97 billion in May 2018, with exports growing faster than imports. Exports increased to USD 29.99 billion in May 2019 from USD 28.94 billion in May 2018, registering a positive growth of 3.63% and Import increased to USD 45.35 billion in May 2019 from USD 43.92 billion in May 2018, registering a positive growth of 3.27%.
