      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Income limit of Parents of OBC Students
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2019 4:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has fixed the income limit of parents of OBC students for availing scholarships from Government under the welfare schemes of OBCs as under:

                                                                                                                                     
Name of the schemes                                                                                          Income limit (per annum)
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students                                                                      2.50 lakh               
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
Post-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students                                                                     1.50 lakh               
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
National Fellowship for OBC Students                                                                         No income limit         
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
Dr. Ambedkar Pre and Post Matric scholarship Scheme for Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-nomadic Tribes (DNTs).  2.00 lakh               
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     
Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for EBC Students                                                        1.00 lakh               
                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                     

    The Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment after careful consideration and in consultation with various Ministries and Stake-holders has fixed the income limit of parents of OBC Students


    The schemes for welfare of OBC, EBC & DNT's are up to financial year 2019-20. Any change in income limit of the parents of OBC and other students is considered at the time of continuation & revision of the scheme.


    This information was given by Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Krishan Pal Gurjar in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.
