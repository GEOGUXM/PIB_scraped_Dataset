      Ministry of Textiles

      Export of Cotton
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2019 5:43PM by PIB Delhi


    The export of cotton for the current cotton season i.e. 2018-19 is estimated at 50 lakh bales out of which 44.69 lakh bales have already been exported up to 30.04.2019.


    This information was given by the Union Minister of Textiles, Smriti Zubin Irani, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
