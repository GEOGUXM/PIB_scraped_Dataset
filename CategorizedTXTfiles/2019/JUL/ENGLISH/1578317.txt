      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Portal for Searching Sports Talent
      Posted On: 11 JUL 2019 12:18PM by PIB Delhi


    With an aim to attract talented sports persons from all over the country, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports (MYAS) through Sports Authority of India (SAI) launched the National Talent Search Portal (NTSP) on 28.08.2017. It has been so designed that any sports persons from across the country can submit online application for admission in any of the SAI Training Centres (STC)/ Special Area Games (SAG)/SAI Extension Centre by uploading his/her profile on the inbuilt format of the portal. A total of 4, 62,083 sportspersons accessed NTSP and 41 trainees have been selected. The details are placed at Annexure .


    The portal allows access on 24x7 basis by sports persons who intend to seek admission in SAI Centres. The received applications are scrutinized by the NTSP Cell and eligible applicants are sent to the SAI training centres preferred by the applicants.


    This information was given by the Minister of State (I/C) Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
