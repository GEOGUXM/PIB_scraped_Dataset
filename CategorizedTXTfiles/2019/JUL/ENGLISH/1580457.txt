      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Funds Released for WCD Schemes
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2019 5:31PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Women and Child Development releases funds under different Schemes to the States, including Andhra Pradesh. Funds released in 2019-2020 under its schemes to Andhra Pradesh are at Annexure .


    Complaints received regarding corruption in utilization of funds are referred to State Governments to take action as appropriate .
