      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      NGOs Implementing Ujjawala Scheme
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2019 4:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The state-wise and year-wise details of Non-Government Organisations (NGOs) implementing the Ujjawala Scheme for rehabilitating victims of trafficking during the last three years is at Annexure-I.


    As per information received from Government of Uttar Pradesh, 9 NGOs have withdrawn from Ujjawala Scheme during the last 3 years.


    The details of funds sanctioned, released and utilized under the scheme during the last three years, year and State-wise is at Annexure-II .


    The number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme during the said period, year-wise and State-wise is at Annexure-III .
