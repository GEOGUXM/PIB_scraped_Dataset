      Ministry of Textiles

      Textile Training Centre
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2019 5:45PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Textilesis implementing various Textiles sector schemes/programmes and conducts textiles training activities in partnership with training institutes, industry partners, State Governments by providing them necessary support for operational technical requirements. Opening of training centres under various schemes is need based and demand driven. The various Centres in the state of Gujarat where training is provided to textile workers are furnished below:
