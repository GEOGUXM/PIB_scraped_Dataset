      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Call Centre for Grievances Redressal Of Pensioners
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2019 3:10PM by PIB Delhi


    A call centre for central government pensioner inaugurated on 20.6.2019. Total number of Grievances received through Call Centre is 762 as on 12.07.2019. A statement showing category wise number of total grievances received through call center and forwarded to concern Ministry/Department through online CPENGRAMS Portal to resolve the grievances.
