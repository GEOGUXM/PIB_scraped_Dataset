      President of India in Srinagar; pays tributes at Chinar Corps (15 corps) War Memorial on 20th Kargil Vijay Diwas
      Posted On: 26 JUL 2019 2:08PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid his respects to soldiers and officers of the Indian Army who were martyred in the Kargil conflict of 1999 at the Chinar Corps (15 Corps) War Memorial in Srinagar today (July 26, 2019), on the 20 th Kargil Vijay Diwas. This is the first visit of the President to Srinagar and to the Valley. On earlier visits to Jammu and Kashmir, he had visited the Ladakh and Jammu regions.


    The President was scheduled to visit the Kargil War Memorial in Dras this morning, but the visit was called off due to inclement weather. Conditions were not suitable for air travel from Srinagar to Dras.
