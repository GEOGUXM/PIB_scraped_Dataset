      Ministry of Shipping

      Funds for CEZ Thoothukudi, T.N.
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2019 5:39PM by PIB Delhi


      In a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today, the Minister of Statefor Shipping (I/C) and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh Mandaviya informed that Thoothukudior Tuticorin has been identified as a potential district for development of a Coastal Economic Zone as per the National Perspective Plan of Sagarmala. He further informed that the Government of India has identified 16 projects for modernization of VO Chidambarnar Port at Tuticorin. While 10 of these projects have been completed, the remaining are under various stages of implementation/ tendering/ DPR preparation.       The Minister also informed that the Government of India has funded 3 port modernization projects at VOChidambarnar Port under Sagarmala. These include development and operation of a truck parking terminal, a 150 m coastal berth with dredged depth of 10 m to handle vessels upto 15000 DWT and dredging the dock basin for the coastal cargo berth. The Ministry released Rs 60.88 Crore for the three projects, all of which stand completed.
