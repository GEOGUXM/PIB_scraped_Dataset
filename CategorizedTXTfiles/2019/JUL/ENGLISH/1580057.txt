      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      North Eastern States Road Investment Programme (NESRIP) Project
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2019 3:50PM by PIB Delhi


    T he work of “Barpeta to Kalitakuchi” (58.5 Km) road is going on in the State of Assam under ADB assisted North Eastern States Road Investment Programme (NESRIP) project.


    Physical progress of the said work is 90.73% as on 31st May, 2019.


    The work is likely to be completed by December, 2019.


    The work is expected to be completed within the stipulated timeframe.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in LokSabha today.
