      Ministry of Shipping

      Ongoing Projects to enhance traffic handling capacity of Mumbai Port and JNPT
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2019 5:48PM by PIB Delhi


    In a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today, the Minister of State forShipping (I/C) and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh Mandaviya informed that t he present traffic handling capacity of Mumbai Port and Jawaharlal Nehru Port are 78.4 and 118.3 Million Tonnes Per Annum respectively. He informed thatthe ports have taken up several projects to further enhance their capacity.
