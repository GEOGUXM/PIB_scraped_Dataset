      Prime Minister's Office

      PM condoles demise of Smt. Sheila Dikshit
      Posted On: 20 JUL 2019 4:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of former Delhi Chief Minister, Smt. Sheila Dikshit.


    “Deeply saddened by the demise of Sheila Dikshit Ji. Blessed with a warm and affable personality, she made a noteworthy contribution to Delhi’s development. Condolences to her family and supporters. Om Shanti", the Prime Minister said.
