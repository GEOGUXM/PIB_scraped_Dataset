      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Status of NESIDS projects
      Posted On: 18 JUL 2019 5:33PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region has till date sanctioned 39 projects with approved cost of Rs.1163.84 crores under North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS), along with releasing of Rs.10 lakhs to enable the State Government to initiate and finalize the tender formalities. As per scheme guidelines, balance of first instalment of 40% of admissible cost is released on submission of copy of work order by the State Government. Accordingly this Ministry has so far released 40 per cent of the cost in 6 projects.     Second instalment of balance 60% is released once the State Government mandatorily submits Utilization Certificates for 75% of first instalment released and commensurate physical progress duly signed by the Head of designated agency and countersigned by Planning Secretary of the State Government alongwith other requisite documents. Till date, second instalment of 60% has not been asked for by the State Governments in any of the projects sanctioned under NESIDS.


    The time frame, in which the sanctioned projects are usually completed, varies from 1 year to 3 years.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
