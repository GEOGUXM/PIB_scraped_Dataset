    The revenue generated from c ustoms duty has an increasing trend from 2009-10 to 2016-17. As the customs (CVD & SAD) has been subsumed under GST from 1 st July 2017, the customs duty collection has declined in 2017-18 and 2018-19 while the revenue generated fromIGST+ Compensation Cess (Net) from imports has substantially increased in past two years.


    This information was given by the Union Minister of Commerce and Industry, PiyushGoyal, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
