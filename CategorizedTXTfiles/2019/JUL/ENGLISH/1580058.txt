      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Funds utilised for schemes of M/DoNER
      Posted On: 24 JUL 2019 3:51PM by PIB Delhi


    There are 243 projects which were sanctioned during last 10 years for the state of Assam under various schemes of the Ministry of DoNER, out of these 195 projects are ongoing and at different stages of implementation. In addition 51 projects of NEC are ongoing at different stages.


    The amount of unutilized funds lying with state government against these ongoing project are to the tune of Rs.638.25 crore. The state government is in the process of utilizing this unspent amount.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in LokSabha today.
