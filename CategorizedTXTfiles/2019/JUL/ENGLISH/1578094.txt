      Ministry of Development of North-East Region

      Promotion of Handicrafts and Handlooms from North Eastern Region
      Posted On: 10 JUL 2019 3:26PM by PIB Delhi


    The North Eastern Handicrafts and Handlooms Development Corporation Limited (NEHHDC), a Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSE) of Ministry of DoNER, Govt. of India is a nodal agency for production and marketing of Handloom and Handicraft products of North Eastern Region, within India as well as abroad by organizing and participating in various exhibitions, trade fairs and expos. NEHHDC participated in Thai International Travel Fair held at Queen Sirikit Convention Centre, Bangkok from 7 th to 11 th February, 2018 to showcase a wide range of Handloom and Handicraft products from the North Eastern Region.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
