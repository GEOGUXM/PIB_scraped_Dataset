      Ministry of Tourism

      Government has taken various initiatives for development and promotion of tourism in Madhya Pradesh: Shri Prahlad Singh Patel
      Posted On: 22 JUL 2019 5:21PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Tourism, under its different Schemes, has taken various initiatives for the development and promotion of tourism in the State of Madhya Pradesh .


    Under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, the Ministry provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of thematic tourist circuits in the country, for holistic development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations and for the development of infrastructure. The projects under these schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments / UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. The Ministry of Tourism also provides CFA to States / UTs for organizing Fairs, Festivals and Tourism related Events which have the potential for promoting Tourism.

    Khajuraho in Madhya Pradesh is also one of the sites identified by the Ministry of Tourism for development as an Iconic Tourist Site.


    To ensure better connectivity to the tourist sites, Ministry provides financial assistance for developing Last Mile Connectivity, Helipads, Jetties, Wayside Amenities, Cruises, Parking, Battery operated vehicles etc.


    As part of its on - going activities, the Ministry annually releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country, including those in the State of Madhya Pradesh. Promotions are also undertaken through the Social Media accounts and website of the Ministry. In addition, the Indiatourism Offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country.


    The information was given by Minister of State (I/C) for Culture and Tourism, Shri Prahlad Singh Patel in reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
