      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Trade Fair Complexes
      Posted On: 03 JUL 2019 6:21PM by PIB Delhi


    The Department of Commerce provides financial assistance for setting up export infrastructure including International Trade Promotion Centres, under the ‘Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES)’, as and when proposals are received from eligible agencies.


    The Department of Commerce has approved financial assistance for three trade promotion centres under the ‘Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES)’.
