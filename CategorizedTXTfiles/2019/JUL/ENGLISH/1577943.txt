      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Modernisation of Police Forces
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2019 4:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has completely revamped the earlier Modernisation of Police Forces Scheme and approved implementation of the umbrella scheme of “Modernisation of Police Forces” on 27.09.2017. The scheme not only has a focused approach to specific theatres like Left Wing Extremism affected districts, Jammu & Kashmir and the North Eastern States but also has a vastly enhanced outlay of Rs. 25,061.90 crores.


    The umbrella scheme has two verticals – Security Related Expenditure (SRE) of specific theatres &Modernisation of Police Forces. Salient features of the Modernisation of Police Forces vertical are given below:

      * Central assistance is provided to the State Governments for weapons, equipment for Forensics, Information Technology, Communication, Training, etc. The scheme is focused on facilitating targeted interventions and ‘mobility’ and ‘construction of police infrastructure including housing’ are now permitted in case of Jammu & Kashmir, insurgency affected North Eastern (NE) States and Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected Districts.
      * Three Himalayan States, namely, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh and 8 North Eastern States including Sikkim are eligible to receive financial assistance on 90:10 Centre:State sharing basis. The remaining States are eligible for financial assistance on 60:40 Centre:State sharing basis.
      * Specific projects in the North-East region.

    Approved total outlay of the umbrella scheme is Rs. 25,061.90 crores with Central share of Rs.18,636.30crores and State contribution at Rs.6,425.60 crores


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri G. Kishan Reddy in a written reply to question in the Lok Sabha today.
