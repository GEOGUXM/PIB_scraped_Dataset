      Ministry of Textiles

      Urban Haats in Bihar
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2019 5:30PM by PIB Delhi


    One proposal has been received to establish Urban Haat for Handicrafts and Craftsman at Madhubani, Bihar from District Rural Development Authority (DRDA)Madhubani, Bihar and was approved byProject Approval Monitoring Committee (PAMC) held on 31 st July 2018. There is no proposal received for “Urban Haat” at Kishanganj.


    This information was given by the Union Minister of Textiles, Smriti Zubin Irani, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
