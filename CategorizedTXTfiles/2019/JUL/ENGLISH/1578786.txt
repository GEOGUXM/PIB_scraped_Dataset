      Ministry of Shipping

      About 10,000 Jobs Created Under Sagarmala in Last Three Years
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2019 5:16PM by PIB Delhi


    The Minister of State for Shipping (I/C) and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri MansukhMandaviya in a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today informed that approximately 10,000 jobs have been created through projects initiated under Sagarmala by Ministry of Shipping during the last three years.


    The Minister also provided information about thestate-wise number of projects which have been initiated till May 2019, by the Ministry under Sagarmala. The details are as follows-
