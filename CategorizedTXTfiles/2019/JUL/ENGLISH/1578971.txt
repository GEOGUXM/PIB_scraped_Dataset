      PM condoles loss of lives due to collapse of building in Mumbai
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2019 5:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives due to the collapse of building in Mumbai's Dongri.


    “Collapse of a building in Mumbai’s Dongri is anguishing. My condolences to the families of those who lost their lives. I hope the injured recover soon. Maharashtra Government, NDRF and local authorities are working on rescue operations & assisting those in need”, the Prime Minister said.
