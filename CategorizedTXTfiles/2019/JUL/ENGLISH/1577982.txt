      Ministry of Tourism

      Tourism contributed nearly 12.38% to employment during 2017-18: Tourism Minister
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2019 5:41PM by PIB Delhi


    Assessment of number of tourists at different destinations in States/UTs is made in terms of visits in the States/UTs during a calendar year. Ministry of Tourism, Government of India compiles the State/UT wise figures of Domestic Tourist Visits (DTVs) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTVs) on the basis of information provided by State Governments/UT Administrations. The DTVs and FTVs during 2016, 2017 and 2018 are given in the table below:


    (Figures in million)

                                  
Year                DTVs     FTVs 
                                  
                                  
                                  
2016                1615.39  24.71
                                  
                                  
                                  
2017 (Provisional)  1652.49  26.89
                                  
                                  
                                  
2018 (Estimated)    1852.38  27.39
                                  
                                  

    The estimated share (direct + indirect) of jobs due to tourism was 12.38% during 2017-18.


    This information was given by the Minister of State (I/C) of Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Tourism, Shri Prahlad Singh Patel in reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
