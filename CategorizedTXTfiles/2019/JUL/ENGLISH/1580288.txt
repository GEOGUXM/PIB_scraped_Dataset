      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Drivers Training for Safe Driving
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2019 4:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The Central . Motor .Vehicles . Rules 1989 provides for driving training as it directly as well as indirectly helps in ensuring good driving skills and knowledge of rules of road regulation among drivers. The Government of India implements scheme for setting of model Institutes of Driving Training and Research (IDTR), Regional Driving Training Centre (RDTC) and Driving Training Centre (DTC) at district level.


    The financial assistance under these schemes are as under:-

                                                                                                                                  
Sl. No.  Scheme                                              Financial assistance                                                 
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
1        Institutes of Driving Training and Research (IDTR)  Maximum Rs.18.50 crore per IDTR                                      
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
2        Regional Driving Training Centre (RDTC)             Maximum Rs.5.00 crore per RDTC                                       
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                  
3        Driving Training Centre (DTC)                       50% of the project cost subject to a maximum of Rs 1.00 crore per DTC
