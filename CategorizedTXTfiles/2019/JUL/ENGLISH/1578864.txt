      PM greets people on Guru Purnima
      Posted On: 16 JUL 2019 1:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the occasion of Guru Purnima.


    “सभी देशवासियों को गुरु पूर्णिमा की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं।


    On the auspicious day of Guru Purnima, we bow in reverence to all our Gurus who have played an important role in inspiring, moulding and shaping our society”, the Prime Minister said.


    सभी देशवासियों को गुरु पूर्णिमा की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं।
