      Ministry of Culture

      Various development projects have been sanctioned to mark 100th anniversary of massacre of Jallianwala Bagh-Shri Prahlad Singh Patel
      Posted On: 09 JUL 2019 4:22PM by PIB Delhi


    National Implementation Committee (NIC) headed by the Home Minister has approved the following development project at Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial:-

     1. To renovate, upgrade and provide basic amenities at the memorial.
     2. Museum curation and museum interiors.
     3. To develop Light & Sound show/projection mapping.

    Work has started from 1 st June, 2019


    Cultural programmes planned for the commemoration period are kavi sammelan, plays, exhibitions and seminars across the country.


    This information was given by the Minister of State (I/c) of Culture and Tourism, Shri Prahlad Singh Patel in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today.
