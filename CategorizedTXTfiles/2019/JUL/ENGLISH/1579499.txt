      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Assistance to Sugar Mills to Offset the Cost of Sugarcane
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2019 4:41PM by PIB Delhi


    With a view to offset the cost of cane and facilitate timely payment of cane price dues of farmers for sugar season 2017-18, Government has notified a scheme on 9 th May, 2018 envisaging assistance to sugar mills @ Rs. 5.50 per qtl of cane crushed. Under a similar scheme notified on 5 th October, 2018 for sugar season 2018-19, assistance is to be extended to sugar mills @ Rs. 13.88 per quintal of cane crushed.


    The Central Government has so far extended assistance amounting to Rs. 392.76 crore to sugar mills under the scheme notified on 9 th May, 2018. No funds have so far been released under the scheme notified on 5 th October, 2018. As per provisions of both the schemes, banks shall credit the amount of assistance to the farmers’ accounts on behalf of the mills against cane dues payable and subsequent balance, if any, shall be credited into mill’s account. Data regarding number of farmers benefitted under the scheme is not maintained by the Government.


    This information was given in a written reply by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri Danve Raosaheb Dadarao in the Rajya Sabha today.
