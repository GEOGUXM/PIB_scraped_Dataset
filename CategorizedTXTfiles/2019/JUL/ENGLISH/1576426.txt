      Ministry of Defence

      Purchase of Army’s Disposal Vehicles for The Members
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 3:02PM by PIB Delhi


    During the last two years (2017 and 2018) total number of 36 Members of Parliament had applied to purchase Army’s disposal vehicle. The vehicles were made available to all the Members who had applied. 11 MPs were allotted vehicles from Vehicle Sub Depot Meerut, 15 MPs were allotted from Ordnance Depot Avadi, 03 MPs were allotted from Central Ordnance Depot Chheoki, 02 MPs were allotted from Central Ordnance Depot Mumbai, 02 MPs were allotted from Central Armoured Fighting Vehicle Depot Kirkee, 01 MP each was allotted vehicles from 224 ABOD Jodhpur, Northern Command Vehicle Depot Udhampur and Vehicle Depot Panagarh.


    All the Members of Parliament got the vehicle from the same depot from where they had asked for.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Ajay Pratap Singh in Rajya Sabha today.
