      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      ACC Appointment
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 7:15PM by PIB Delhi


    The competent authority has approved assignment of additional charge of the post of Secretary, Ministry of Textiles to Shri Rabindra Panwar, IAS (BH:1985) Secretary, Ministry of Women and Child Development with immediate effect and till the appointment of a regular incumbent or until further orders whichever is earlier.
