      Ministry of Textiles

      Textile Training Centres
      Posted On: 12 JUL 2019 4:12PM by PIB Delhi


    This Ministry is implementing various Textiles sector schemes/programmesand conducts textiles training activities in partnership with training institutes, industry partners, State Governments by providing them necessary support for operational technical requirements. The details of the various Textiles Training Centres under various Textile Sector Schemes is at Annexure. Opening of training centres under various schemes is need based and demand driven.
