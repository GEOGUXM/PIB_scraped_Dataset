      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      Uzbekistan Ambassador to India Shri. FarhodArziev called on the Minister for Parliamentary Affairs, Coal and Mines Shri Pralhad Joshi today
      Posted On: 15 JUL 2019 5:50PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassador of Uzbekistan to India Shri. FarhodArziev called on the Minister for Parliamentary Affairs, Coal and Mines Shri PralhadJoshi in the Parliament Housetodayand expressed his countries desire for further strengthening of bilateral ties. Shri Joshi said that all diplomatic avenues can be explored for the purpose. Details of friendly relations between the two countries were elucidated by the ambassador.
