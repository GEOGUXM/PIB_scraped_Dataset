      Prime Minister's Office

      PM congratulates Dutee Chand on winning Gold in Women's 100m Sprint
      Posted On: 10 JUL 2019 12:30PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Dutee Chand o n winning Gold in Women's 100m Sprint.


    “Exceptional achievement of an exceptional athlete! Congratulations Dutee Chand for winning a hard earned and well deserved Gold in the Women’s 100 m finals. You make India proud”, the Prime Minister said.
