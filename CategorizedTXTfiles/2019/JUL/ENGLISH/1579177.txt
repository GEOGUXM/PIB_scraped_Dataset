      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Export of Marine Products
      Posted On: 17 JUL 2019 5:34PM by PIB Delhi


    European Union (12.96% of export value in USD) continued to be the third largest export destination for marine products after USA (34%) and South East Asia (21.7%).
