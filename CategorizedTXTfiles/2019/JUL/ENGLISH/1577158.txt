      PM greets people on the occasion of Rath Yatra
      Posted On: 04 JUL 2019 3:00PM by PIB Delhi


      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the occasion of the Rath Yatra.


      “Best wishes to everyone on the special occasion of the Rath Yatra. We pray to Lord Jagannath and seek his blessings for the good health, happiness and prosperity of everyone. Jai Jagannath”, the Prime Minister said.
