      Ministry of Textiles

      Indian Institute of Fashion Technology
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2019 5:25PM by PIB Delhi


    ANational Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Campus is operational at Hyderabad, which is joint Capital of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.


    Asper policy for establishment of a new campus of NIFT,the State Government is required to submit a proposal alongwith following commitments:


    20-30 acres of land;


    Rs.150-200 cr. for construction of campus at the price rates of CPWD for 2011-12;


    Rs.30 cr. for provision of equipments, machines and furniture at the price rates of 2011-12;


    Annual revenue grants to adjust deficit revenue, till the campus becomes financially viable.


    The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has so far not submitted a complete proposal along with requisite commitments,in accordance with the policy guidelines, for setting up of a NIFT campus in their proposed new capital.


    This information was given by the Union Minister of Textiles, Smriti Zubin Irani, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
