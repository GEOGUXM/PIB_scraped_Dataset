      Ministry of Education

      Government of India has taken several measures to give special focus to higher education-HRD Minister
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2019 5:03PM by PIB Delhi


    Following measures have been taken by Government to give special focus/attention to Higher Education:

     1. Issuing of new University Grants Commission (UGC) regulation for Open and Distance Learning that allows entry of reputed institutions to offer education on the distance mode.

    (ii) Using of ICT technology- Study Webs of Active-Learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM) portal to reach out to people and allow them to secure good quality education.

     1. Opening of more centrally funded institutions.
     1. Encouraging opening of institutions by State Governments through the Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) which aims to achieve equity, access and excellence in higher education. The scheme supports components such as upgrading autonomous colleges to universities, clustering colleges to establish a university, setting up of new professional colleges in un-served and underserved areas as well as providing infrastructure grants to universities and colleges to scale up capacity.
     2. More scholarship programmes to supplement the cost of education.

    This information was given by the Union Minister for Human Resource Development, Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today.
