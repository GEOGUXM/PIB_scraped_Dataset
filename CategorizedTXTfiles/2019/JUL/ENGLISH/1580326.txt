      President of India to Pay Homage at the Kargil War Memorial Tomorrow
      Posted On: 25 JUL 2019 7:06PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit the Kargil War Memorial in Dras, Jammu and Kashmir, tomorrow (July 26, 2019) on the 20 th anniversary of the Kargil Vijay Diwas. He will pay homage to soldiers and officers of the Indian Army who were martyred in the Kargil conflict of 1999.
