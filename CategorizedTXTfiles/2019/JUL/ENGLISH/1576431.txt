      Ministry of Defence

      Proposal to set up Military Recruitment Centres in Gujarat
      Posted On: 01 JUL 2019 3:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The Indian Army conducts Recruitment Rallies across the country covering all districts at least once a year. Applications are invited online and candidates called in batches for selection with equal opportunity to all irrespective of region. Two Army Recruitment Offices are located in Gujarat at Ahmedabad and Jamnagar for the entire State.


    Air Force Selection Board in Gandhinagar covers Gujarat conducts online examination twice a year for Officers and Airmen. The Indian Navy also conducts online recruitment examination twice a year at Rajkot, Ahmedabad and Surat and physical and medical tests at Jamnagar.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Naranbhai J. Rathwa in Rajya Sabha today.
