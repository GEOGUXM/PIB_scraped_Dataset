      Ministry of Food Processing Industries

      Over 10 lakh people engaged in food processing units of value addition of cereals in 2016-17
      Posted On: 19 JUL 2019 3:26PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of State for Food Processing Industries Shri Rameswar Teli responding to a written question on quantity of cereals produced in the country and persons employed in its value addition in Rajya Sabha today informed that 10,56,890 persons were engaged in registered food processing units of value addition of cereals in 2016-17, as per the latest available data under the Annual Survey of Industries, Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation.
