      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Creation of separate IAS / IPS Cadre
      Posted On: 17 JUL 2019 3:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The State Government of Arunachal Pradesh, among other State Government, has submitted proposal for creation of a separate cadre for IAS / IPS / IFS officers for the State. Such proposals are examined on the basis consensus amongst the constituents of the Joint Cadre Authority by the Ministry of Home Affairs.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
