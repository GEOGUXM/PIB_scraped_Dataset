      VENAD EXPRESS TO SKIP ERNAKULAM JUNCTION
      Posted On: 12 MAR 2019 4:46PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      Due to line block in connection with engineering work in Ernakulam Junction yard, the service of train No.16301 /16302 Shoranur Junction - Thiruvananthapuram Central - Shoranur Venad Express will run via Ernakulam Town Station, skipping Ernakulam Junction from 13.03.2019 to 16.03.2019.
