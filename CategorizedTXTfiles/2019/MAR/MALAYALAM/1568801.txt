      Preponement In The Departure Timings Of Train Services At Thiruvananthapuram Central
      Posted On: 13 MAR 2019 5:56PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram

      For facilitating the engineering work between Ernakulam Town and Angamali section, the service of Train No. 16343 Thiruvananthapuram-Madurai Amritha Express and the service of Train No.16349 Thiruvananthapuram-Nilambur Rajyarani Express will leave Thiruvananthapuram central at 21.00 hrs instead of 22.00 hrs from 18/03/2019 to 25/04/2019.( 60 minutes early departure from Thiruvananthapuram Central)
        * The train will reach Thrissur at 03.20 hrs.
        * The train will run as per the normal schedule from Thrissur station.
