      Extension Of Special Train Services Between Coimbatore And Jabalpur
      Posted On: 16 MAR 2019 5:54PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


      To clear the extra rush of passengers , the service of Super fast special trains between Coimbatore and Jabalpur is extended as detailed below:


      1. The service of Train No.02197 Coimbatore – Jabalpur Weekly Superfast Special will leave Coimbatore at 19.05 hrs. on Mondays


      · On 8 th , 15 th , 22 nd , 29 th April


      · On 6 th , 13 th , 20 th , 27 th May


      · On 3 rd , 10 th , 17 th , 24 th June


      · On 1 st July 2019 and reach Jabalpur at 10.20 hrs. o­n Wednesdays (13 services).


      2. The service of Train No.02198 Jabalpur- Coimbatore Weekly Superfast Special will leave Jabalpur at 11.00 hours on Saturdays


      · on 6 th , 13 th , 20 th and 27 th April ,


      · on 4 th , 11 th , 18 th and 25 th May ,


      · on 1 st , 8 th , 15 th , 22 nd and 29 th June 2019 and reach Coimbatore at 03.50 hours on Mondays (13 services).


      Composition:


      AC 2-tier-cum-AC 3-tier Coach– 1, AC 3-tier Coaches– 3, Sleeper Class Coaches– 10, Unreserved Coaches – 4 & Luggage-cum-brake van – 2 coaches.


      Stoppages:


      Palakkad Junction, Shoranur, Tirur, Kozhikode, Vadakara, Thalassery, Kannur, Payyanur, Kanhangad, Kasaragod, Mangaluru Jn., Mulki, Udupi, Kundapura, Mookambika Road Byndoor, Kumta, Karwar, Madgaon, Thivim, Kudal, Kankavali, Ratnagiri, Chiplun, Khed, Roha, Panvel, Igatpuri, Manmad, Bhusaval, Khandwa, Harda, Itarsi, Pipariya, Gadarwara and Narsinghpur.
