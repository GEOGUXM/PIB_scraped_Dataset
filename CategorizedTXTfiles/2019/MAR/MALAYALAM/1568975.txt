      Rescheduling Of Passenger Train
      Posted On: 15 MAR 2019 5:53PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    To facilitate the track maintenance using Ballast Cleaning machine between Ottapalam and Lakkiti station, the service of Train No.56604 Shoranur- Coimbatore Passenger scheduled to leave Shoranur Junction at 08.00 hours will be rescheduled to leave Shoranur at 09.30 hours with effect from 18.03.2019 to 24.03.2019 (Except Friday).
