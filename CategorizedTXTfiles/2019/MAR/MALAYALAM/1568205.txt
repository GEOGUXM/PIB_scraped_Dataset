      PIB Headquarters

      Regional Dak Adalat
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 2:31PM by PIB Thiruvananthpuram


    A Regional ‘‘DAK ADALAT'' of Central Postal Region will be held at the Office of the Post Master General, Central Region, Gandhi Nagar(Maveli Road), Kochi on 27 th March, 2019 at 11.00 am. Complaints relating to Postal and Counter Services, Savings Bank, Money Order etc. under Central region will be taken up in the Adalat.


    Cases already taken up and settled in previous Dak Adalats will not be entertained. Complaints can be sent in envelopes super scribed “Regional Dak Adalat: March 2019” and addressed to “ Smt K. Kalaivani, Assistant Director(PG), Office of the Postmaster General, Central Region, Kochi-682020,” so as to reach there on or before 20 th March, 2019.
