      General Elections 2019 Factsheet on Electorate and Polling Stations for Maharashtra - Phase I
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2019 5:45PM by PIB Mumbai


    Seven constitutencies in Maharashtra are going to polls in the 1st Phase of Lok Sabha Elections 2019. As per the latest electoral data, there are more than 1.3 crore electors, comprising 66.7 lakh males, 63.6 lakh females and 181 electors in "Others" Category. 14,731 polling stations are being set up by the Election Commission for the first phase.
