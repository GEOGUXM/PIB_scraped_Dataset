      Ministry of Finance

      Departments of Mumbai Customs Zone – I to be functional during March 30 – 31, 2019
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2019 5:19PM by PIB Mumbai


    Mumbai Customs Zone-I, in its endeavour to discharge its economic responsibility, informs importers, exporters, customs Brokers and other stakeholders that all concerned departments of Mumbai Customs Zone-I will be working during the last weekend of current financial year i.e. 30.03.2019 and 31.03.2019 to facilitate the trade.


    All importers, exporters and other stakeholders are advised to avail the facility.
