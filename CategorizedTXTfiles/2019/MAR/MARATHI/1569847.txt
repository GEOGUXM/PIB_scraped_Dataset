      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Swachhata Pakhwada by All India Institute of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2019 5:46PM by PIB Mumbai


    All India Institute of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, under Directorate General of Health Services, Ministry of Health & Family welfare is observing Swachhata Pakhwada during April 1 – 15, 2019.


    The programme is being inaugurated on 1 st April. On 3 rd April, there will be a talk on menstrual cycle, by a medical doctor. This will be followed by a poster and slogan competition on 4 th April. On 5 th April, street plays will be held. On 8 th April, there will be a competition on the theme “Best out of Waste”.


    9 th April is earmarked for Rangoli and drawing competitions. Various events like elocution competition, tree-guard painting, Q & A session and demonstration on how to make manure, will be arranged from 10 th to 15 th April.


    The event will end on 15 th April, 2019 with prize distribution ceremony.
