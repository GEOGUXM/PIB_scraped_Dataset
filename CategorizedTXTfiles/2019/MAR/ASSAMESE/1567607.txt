      Ministry of Electronics & IT

      CM to inaugurate new Assam NIC Complex
      Posted On: 06 MAR 2019 2:05PM by PIB Guwahati


    
    The Chief Minister of Assam, Shri Sarbananda Sonowal will be inaugurating the new office complex of the Assam State Centre of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) at Last Gate, Guwahati on March 08, 2019 at 9:30 am. Its state-of-the-art, fully-equipped new office will enable NIC to provide added impetus in terms of its frontline services for the Digital Assam initiative as an important part of the Prime Minister’s Digital India vision.
    The Assam State Centre of NIC not only runs all the Central Government ICT-enabled e-Governance projects and services in the State, it is also the core ICT support and consultancy organization for the State Government in a majority of its Information and Communications Technology (ICT) initiatives. All of the State Government’s connectivity requirements are also wholly dependent on the NIC network backbone NICNET.
