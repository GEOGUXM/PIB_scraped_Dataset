      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      ‘New India Chaupal’ Programme to be held in Guwahati tomorrow
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 5:23PM by PIB Guwahati


    The Government of India has always believed in the idea of “Sabka Saath Sabka Vikas”. It has consistently made efforts into identifying issues plaguing India and has come up with solutions for these issues. “New India Chaupal” is one such initiative by Government of India. This initiative has a multi-pronged approach; Dissemination of information of various schemes implemented by Govt. of India, Approach beneficiaries of these schemes, collect their testimonials and encourage them to share their story with their fellow villagers and Provide an opportunity for public enroll & benefit in these schemes.


    The Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is organising these awareness programmes Jagran, an agency hired through Broadcast Engineering Consultants India Limited (BECIL). In Assam, more than 400 programmes are being organised in different districts of the State during the last 2 weeks. The campaign to reach out to villages in 26 districts throughout Assam targeting to reach maximum citizens. The Jagran Group is organising a programme on “New India Chaupal” at Rukmini Nagar Bihutoli, Rangmanch Path, Housing Colony, Guwahati tomorrow.


    The programmes give information on Central Government schemes and include nukkad natak s, exhibition panel displays, video/film show through mobile LED van and other display items.


    Through this campaign the Government aim to achieve an increase in top of the mind recall and increase public participations in various schemes started by the Government of India like Atal Pension Yojana, Skill india, Sukanya Samriddhi account, Make in india etc.
