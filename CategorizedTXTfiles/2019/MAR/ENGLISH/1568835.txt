      President of India to inaugurate festival of Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Gandhinagar tomorrow
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2019 4:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Gujarat tomorrow (March 15, 2019) to inaugurate the Festival of Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Gandhinagar. He will also present the 10 th Biennial National Grassroots Innovation Awards on the occasion.
