      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Chairperson, Lokpal administers oath of office to the Members, Lokpal
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2019 12:58PM by PIB Delhi


    The Chairperson, Lokpal, Sh. Justice P. C. Ghose administered the oath of office to the following Members of Lokpal at a function organised here today:


    1. Sh. Justice Dilip Babasaheb Bhosale


    2. Sh. Justice Pradeep Kumar Mohanty


    3. Smt. Justice Abhilasha Kumari


    4. Sh. Justice Ajay Kumar Tripathi


    5. Sh. Dinesh Kumar Jain


    6. Smt. Archana Ramasundaram


    7. Sh. Mahender Singh


    8. Dr. Indrajeet Prasad Gautam


    The Secretary DoPT, Dr C. Chandramouli, the Central Vigilance Commissioner (CVC) Sh. K V Chowdary, Director, Intelligence Bureau (IB) Shri Rajiv Jain and other dignitaries were also present.
