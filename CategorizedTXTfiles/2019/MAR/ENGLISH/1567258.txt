      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the people on Maha Shivratri
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2019 9:48AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has extended his greetings on the occasion of Maha Shivratri.


    “Maha Shivratri greetings to everyone. महाशिवरात्रि के पावन अवसर पर सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं। ऊँ नम: शिवाय”, the Prime Minister said.
