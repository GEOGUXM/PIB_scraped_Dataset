      President of India Presents Padma Awards
      Posted On: 11 MAR 2019 12:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind presented one Padma Vibhushan, eight Padma Bhushan and forty-six Padma Shri Awards today (March 11, 2019) at a Civil Investiture Ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan.
