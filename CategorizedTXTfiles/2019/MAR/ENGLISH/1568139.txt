      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 12:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The President has accepted the resignation of Shri KummanamRajasekharan as Governor of Mizoram.


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Prof. JagdishMukhi, Governor of Assam to discharge the functions of the Governor of Mizoram, in addition to his own duties, until regular arrangements for the office of the Governor of Mizoram is made.
