      President of India attends Mahashivratri celebrations at Isha Yoga centre in Coimbatore
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2019 9:20PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram NathKovind, attended the Mahashivratri celebrations at Isha Yoga Centre in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, as the Chief Guest today (March 4, 2019). Speaking on the occasion, the President said this celebration is an opportunity to experience the grace of Mahashivaratri. He expressed happiness at SadhguruJaggiVasudev and the Isha Foundation making India’s age-old spiritualism and values accessible to today’s youth.
