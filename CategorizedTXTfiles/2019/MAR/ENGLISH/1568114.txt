      PM Salutes Nari Shakti on International Women's Day
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 9:46AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted the spirit of 'Nari Shakti' on International Women's Day.


    “On International Women's Day, we salute our indomitable Nari Shakti. We are proud to have taken numerous decisions that have furthered women empowerment. Every Indian is proud of the stupendous accomplishments of women in various spheres”, the Prime Minister said.
