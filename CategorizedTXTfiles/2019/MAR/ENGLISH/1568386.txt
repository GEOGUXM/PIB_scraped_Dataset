      Ministry of Railways

      Ministry of Railways appoints Shri S.S.Srinivas as Officer on Special Duty, South Coast Railway to facilitate the process of formation of the new Zone
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 7:43PM by PIB Delhi


    Ministry of Railways has appointed Shri S.S.Srinivas as Officer on Special Duty (OSD), South Coast Railway. Prior to this assignment, Shri Srinivas, an Indian Railways Personnel Service (IRPS) officer, was working as Chief Personnel Officer (Administration), South Central Railway.


    Officer on Special Duty/South Coast Railway will undertake planning and preparatory work for formation of the New Zone at Visakhapatnam, such as survey of existing infrastructural facilities, requirement of land acquisition, preparation of DPR for additional requirements and the blue print for smooth transition of staff into the New Zone.
