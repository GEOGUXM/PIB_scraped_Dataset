      President of India condoles the passing away of Shri Manohar Parrikar
      Posted On: 17 MAR 2019 10:18PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind has condoled the passing away of the Chief Minister of Goa, Shri Manohar Parrikar.


    The President said, “Extremely sorry to hear of the passing of Shri Manohar Parrikar, Chief Minister of Goa, after an illness borne with fortitude and dignity. An epitome of integrity and dedication in public life, his service to the people of Goa and of India will not be forgotten.”
