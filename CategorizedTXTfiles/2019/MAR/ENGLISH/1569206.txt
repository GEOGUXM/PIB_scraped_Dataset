      President’s Holi Greetings
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2019 6:44PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of Holi has said:-


    “On the occasion of the joyous festival of Holi, I convey my greetings to all fellow citizens in India and abroad. This festival is a celebration of spring and of our sense of fraternity and mutual goodwill. May the confluence of colours bring positive energy to all our families and our shared society”.
