      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN ONE PAIR OF SUPERFAST AC SPECIAL BETWEEN SANTRAGACHI AND PURI
      Posted On: 18 MAR 2019 5:26PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 18 th March, 2019


    In order to clear extra rush of passengers on the eve of Holi Festival, South Eastern Railway has decided to run 1 pair of Superfast AC Special Train between Santragachi-Puri-Santragachi leaving Santragachi on 20.03.19 and leaving Puri on 21.03.19.


    02837 Santragachi -Puri Special leaving Santragachi at 19.00 hrs will reach Puri at 03.30 hrs, the next day. In the opposite direction, 02838 Puri-Santragachi Special leaving Puri at 23.35 hrs will arrive Santragachi at 08.30 hrs, the next day.


    The Special Train consisting of fourteen AC 3-Tier coaches will have stoppages at Kharagpur, Balasore, Bhadrak, Jajpur Keonjhar Road, Cuttack, Bhubaneswar and Khurda Road between Santragachi and Puri.
