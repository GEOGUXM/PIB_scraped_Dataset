      Election Commission

      ECI Directs Usage of Eco-friendly Substances for preparing Election Publicity Material
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2019 2:41PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 22, 2019


    Considering the long-term deleterious impact of materials like plastics, polythene etc., on the life-giving and life-sustaining environment, the Election Commission of India (ECI) has directed that all political parties, contesting candidates and their authorised agents shall desist from utilising environmentally hazardous materials like plastics, polythene etc for the preparation and usage election-related publicity materials during the election.


    The District Election Officers (DEO) and Returning Officers (RO) have been directed to emphasise the importance of environment protection and preservation during the meetings with the political parties and contesting candidates and ensure that the instructions of the Commission with regard to the restrictions on the usage of non-eco-friendly materials like plastics, polythene etc during electioneering shall be adhered to by all concerned.


    The Chief Electoral Officers (CEO)s have been entrusted upon the task of impressing upon the importance of using eco-friendly and bio-degradable materials for campaign material to various political parties and candidates in the States and Union Territories and issue necessary instructions in this regard.
