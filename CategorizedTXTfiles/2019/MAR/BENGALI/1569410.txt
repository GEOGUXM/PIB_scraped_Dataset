      Election Commission

      Today is Last Date for Filing of Nominations for the First phase of General Election, 2019 in 20 States including West Bengal
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2019 4:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 25, 2019


    Today is the last date of filing nominations for the first phase of General Election, 2019 in 20 States including West Bengal. The States include Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jammu & Kashmir, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Sikkim, Telengana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep, besides, West Bengal. Coochbehar (SC) and Alipurduars Parliamentary constituencies in West Bengal will be experiencing the poll in the first phase of General Election. Total 1809598 and 1642285 electors respectively from Coochbehar (SC) and Alipurduars (ST) will be exercising their franchises in the first phase of election from West Bengal.


    It is to be noted that the notification for the first phase of General Election 2019 was issued on March 18, 2019. Tomorrow (March 26, 2019) is the date for scrutiny of nominations. March 28, 2019 is the last date for withdrawal of the candidatures.


    The date of poll for the first phase of election is April 11, 2019.
