      Election Commission issues Assured Minimum Facilities at Polling Stations
      Posted On: 22 MAR 2019 9:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 22, 2019

    The Election Commission has prescribed that there will be some minimum facilities assured at polling stations. These facilities include provision of ramps, provision of drinking water, adequate medical kit, proper lighting and electricity arrangements, help desk, proper signage, toilets, shade in polling stations. Till date the number of complaints received through C-VIGIL is 1426, out of which 882 have been found correct and action taken.
