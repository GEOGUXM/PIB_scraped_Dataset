      Four-day Long Celebration of International Women’s Day Comes to a Befitting End Today
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2019 4:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 14, 2019


    The four-day long celebration of International Women’s Day by the IndianOil’s Eastern Region Office ended today with organisation of blood donation camp at the office premises. The celebration started on March 8, with the unveiling of an e-magazine ‘Expression’ by women employees of the office.


    The Chief Guest of the inaugural programme was Smt Damayanti Sen, IPS. Shri Pradip Kundu of State Crime Bureau and Smt Indrani Maji, Regional Level Coordinator, WIPs, ERO and Shri Rabindranath Ghosh, ED(Regional Service), ER were also present on the occasion.


    A skit competition along with food, arts and crafts carnival was organised in all these four days. It is to be noted that all proceeds from the sales of carnival will be donated to organisations involved in empowering girl child.
