      Election Commission

      Notification for Third Phase of General Election to Lok Sabha, 2019 issued today
      Posted On: 28 MAR 2019 6:07PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: 28 March 2019


    The Election Commission has issued notification for the third phase of General Election to Lok Sabha today (March 28, 2019). In this phase a total of 115 Parliamentary Constituencies spread over 14 states will go to poll. The states including West Bengal that will go for poll in the third phase include Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, Odisha, Uttar Pradesh, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu. Five Parliamentary Constituencies of West Bengal will be experiencing poll in this phase of General Election. These Constituencies are- Balurghat, Maldaha Uttar, Maldaha Dakshin, Jangipur and Murshidabad.


    This apart, four Parliamentary Constituencies in Assam, five in Bihar, seven in Chhattisgarh, two in Goa, 26 in Gujarat, one in Jammu & Kashmir, 14 in Karnataka, 20 in Kerala, 14 in Maharashtra, six in Orissa, 10 in Uttar Pradesh, one each in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu will also experience poll in the third phase of General Election.


    The last date of filing nomination for this phase poll is April 4, 2019. The date of scrutiny of the nominations is April 5, 2019. The last date of withdrawal of candidature is April 8, 2019. Except Jammu & Kashmir, the date of poll of other States including West Bengal is April 23, 2019.
