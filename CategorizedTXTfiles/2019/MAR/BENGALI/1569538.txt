      ECI Designs Mobile App for Developing Culture of Electoral Engagement
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2019 1:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 27, 2019


    The Election Commission of India (ECI) has taken an initiative by designing a Mobile Application for developing a culture of electoral engagement. This App has been developed under continuous efforts of the Commission to create better awareness.


    The app provides a number of facil ities to voters like Electoral Search (#GoVerify your name in the electoral roll), submission of online forms for new voter registration, shifting to a different constituency, for overseas electors, objection in the electoral roll, correction of entries and shifting within Assembly constituency. The voters can also register complaints related to electoral services and track its disposal status, acquire FAQ on voters or elections, and results.
