      Ministry of Railways

      WORKING HOURS OF COMPUTERISED RESERVATION OFFICES ON 21.03.2019 (DOLYATRA)
      Posted On: 18 MAR 2019 5:30PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 18 th March, 2019


    Computerised Reservation Offices over Kolkata and Howrah area will function from 14.00 hrs to 18.00 hrs on 21.03.19 (Thursday) instead of 08.00 hrs. to 14.00 hrs. on the occasion of “DOLYATRA”.


    However, current booking counters at Howrah, Sealdah and Kolkata Terminal will function as usual.
