      Election Commission

      Maximum New Electors in Age-group of 18-19 Years in Murshidabad District, West Bengal
      Posted On: 20 MAR 2019 5:59PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 20, 2019


    In West Bengal the total number of new electors in the age-group of 18-19 years in 2019 is 1168704. Out of this figure, the maximum number of electors of the said age-group is from Murshidabad district. Here, the number is 131768; out of which 67171 are male, 64571 are female and 26 are third gender category.


    In this sphere, South 24 Parganas is in the second position, where 60022 are male, 45348 are female and 13 belong to third gender category.


    North 24 Parganas in West Bengal Bengal is in the third position so far as the electors of the age-group of 18-19 years are concerned. Here, the total number is 94700, out of which 52578 are male, 42110 are female and 12 belong to third gender category.
