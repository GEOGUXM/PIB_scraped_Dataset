      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN THIRTEEEN PAIRS OF WEEKLY SUMMER SPECIAL TRAINS BETWEEN SANTRAGACHI AND PURI
      Posted On: 26 MAR 2019 4:10PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 th March, 2019


    In order to clear extra rush of passengers during summer, it has been decided to run 13 pairs of Weekly Summer Special Trains between Santragachi-Puri-Santragachi during the period from 05.4.19 to 29.06.19.


    08404 Santragachi -Puri Special leaving Santragachi at 19.10 hrs every Saturday will reach Puri at 04.00 hrs, the next day. In the opposite direction, 08403 Puri-Santragachi Special leaving Puri every Friday at 21.25 hrs will arrive Santragachi at 06.45 hrs, the next day.


    The Special Train consisting of one AC 2-Tier, three AC 3-Tier, ten Sleeper Class and four General Second Class coaches will have stoppages at Kharagpur, Balasore, Bhadrak, Jajpur Keonjhar Road, Cuttack, Bhubaneswar and Khurda Road between Santragachi and Puri.


    Booking will be available from PRS & Internet with immediate effect.
