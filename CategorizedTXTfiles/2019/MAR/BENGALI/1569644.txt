      Ministry of External Affairs

      One-day Passport Mela in Kolkata and Agartala
      Posted On: 27 MAR 2019 5:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 27 March, 2019


    A passport Mela is being organised at Aakash Tower, Eastern Bye Pass, Ruby, Kasba 781, Anandpur, Kolkata-7000107 on March, 30, 2019. A similar mela will be held at Agartala also, in the Passport Seva Kendra office, located at 1 st floor, Jackson Gate Building, Lenin Sarani, Agartala-799001 on the same day. 1300 and 150 appointments of Normal category (Fresh/Re-issue) will be processed respectively from 10 a.m. onwards on that day.


    The appointments will be released on website www.passportindia.gov.in from 28 th March at 1530 hours onwards. It has been stated that tatkaal, on-hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed.


    Passport applicants need to carry their online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, proof of address and proof of date of birth, in original as well as self-attested photocopy for the purpose. The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from the above-stated website. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system.
