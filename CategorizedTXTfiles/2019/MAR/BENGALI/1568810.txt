      Ministry of Defence

      SECURITY FORCES RESCUE KIDNAPPED WOMAN FROM CADRES IN MANIPUR
      Posted On: 14 MAR 2019 2:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: March 14, 2019


    On March 13, 2019 afternoon a Quick Reaction Team of Assam Rifles rescued a woman from New Wangparal Village in Chandel District of Manipur, who was kidnapped by NSCN(IM) cadres on Tuesday, March 12, 2019. The operation also resulted in apprehension of two hardcore NSCN(IM) cadres involved in the kidnapping.


    On 13 March 2019, At around 2.30 PM based on specific information regarding presence of two armed cadres of NSCN(IM) in a school building of New Wangparal along with a woman in blindfolds, Assam Rifles Battalion launched a search and rescue operation. At around 3.30 PM, the Quick Reaction team reached the spot. Taken by surprise, the armed NSCN(IM) cadres fired on the troops and fled from the site. In a daring move the team led by the young company commander chased them down and showing extraordinary courage managed to corner them thereby forcing them to surrender.


    The two cadres have been handed over to the Chandel Police. FIR has also been lodged against the cadres for kidnapping, attempt to murder and under Unlawful Activities Prevention Act for being in possession of restricted-calibre weapon and ammunition.


    The cadres were identified as self styled Lt Colonel Justin Khapai and self-styled Sergeant Korungthang of NSCN(IM).
