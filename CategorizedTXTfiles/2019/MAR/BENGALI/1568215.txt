      Ministry of Culture

      A digital interactive exhibition on Nationalism from the soil of Bengal inaugurated at National Library.
      Posted On: 08 MAR 2019 2:48PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 08, 2019


    An exhibition on Nationalism from the soil of Bengal was inaugurated by Shri Keshari Nath Tripathi, Governor of West Bengal at the Belvedere House, National Library in Kolkata today.


    Established by the National Library, Union Ministry of Culture, and designed and developed by the team of Expression Ad Agency Pvt Ltd and Tagbin Services Pvt Ltd, this digital, interactive exhibition aims to enlighten the youth of the inspiring lifetimes of Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay, Rabindranath Tagore, Subhas Chandra Bose and Shyama Prasad Mookerjee, and their unforgettable contribution to the freedom struggle against British Imperialism.


    The Indian Museum, Kolkata also curated an exhibition linking the past titled “Story of India through Images and Symbols” at the Belvedere House, National Library. The Exhibition is a collection of archeological artifacts of stone images depicting Gautama Buddha, Lord Mahaveera, Bhagvan Vishnu, Bhagvan Shiva Shakti and symbols like Yantra, Lotus Feet of Buddha, Ashoka Chakra, etc.
