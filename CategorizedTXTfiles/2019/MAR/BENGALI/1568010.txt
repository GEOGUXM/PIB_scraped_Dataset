      Ministry of Railways

      South Eastern Railway (SER) signs MOU with IRFC
      Posted On: 07 MAR 2019 6:24PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 7, 2019


    Indian Railway Finance Corporation Limited (IRFC), New Delhi and South Eastern Railway (SER), Garden Reach, Kolkata have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) on March 6, 2019. For South Eastern Railway, Principal Chief Medical Director, Dr. Rupa Mitra and for IRFC, MD, Sri Vijay Kumar signed the MOU.


    Under this MOU, IRFC will provide assistance to ongoing Cochlear Implant Programme at Central Hospital, SER, under their CSR scheme in the tune of approximately Rs 18 million.


    Under this scheme, IRFC will donate 25 nos. of Cochlear Implants for use in severe to profound deaf people specially children, turning their life into a useful and productive one. Unlike hearing aids, which make sounds louder, cochlear implants bypass the damaged hair cells of the inner ear (cochlea) to provide sound signals to the brain.


    Central Hospital, SER, Garden Reach, Kolkata is running Cochlear Implant Programme since May 2012 and till date, 60 successful implants have been carried out at Central Hospital, SER. SER has also signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for cochlear implant project and cases are being referred to Central Hospital for this highly technical and delicate surgery and the first cochlear implant under this scheme in Eastern India was done at Central Hospital, SER.
