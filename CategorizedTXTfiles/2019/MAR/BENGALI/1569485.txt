      Ministry of Railways

      CANCELLATION OF TATANAGAR-LTT ANTYODAYA EXPRESS & HOWRAH-CSMT (MUMBAI) EXPRESS
      Posted On: 26 MAR 2019 4:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 th March, 2019


    In view of ongoing various modernization and safety related works in various sections of South Eastern Railway, the following trains will remain cancelled:


      * 22886 Tatanagar-LTT Antyodaya Express leaving Tatanagar on 28.03.19 and 31.03.19 will remain cancelled.

      * 22885 LTT-Tatanagar Antyodaya Express leaving LTT on 30.03.19 and 02.04.19 will remain cancelled.
      * 12870 Howrah-CSMT (Mumbai) Express leaving Howrah on 29.03.19 will remain cancelled.
      * 12869 CSMT(Mumbai)-Howrah Express leaving CSMT(Mumbai) on 31.03.19 will remain cancelled.
