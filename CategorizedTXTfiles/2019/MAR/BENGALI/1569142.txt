      Election Commission Notifies for Second Phase of General Election
      Posted On: 19 MAR 2019 10:01PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 19, 2019


    The Election Commission of India has notified for the second phase of General Election to the Parliament. According to the notification, March 26, 2019 is the last date of making nominations. March 27, 2019 is the date of scrutiny of nomination and March 29, 2019 is the last date of for the withdrawal of the candidature. The date of poll for the second phase of election is April 18, 2019.


    In the second phase of the General Election, Jalpaiguri (NC), Darjeeling and Raigunj will undergo poll on the above mentioned date.
