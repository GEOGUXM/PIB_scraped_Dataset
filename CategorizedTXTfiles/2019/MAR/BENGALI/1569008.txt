      Ministry of Finance

      New Principal Accountant General (General & Social Sector Audit) for West Bengal
      Posted On: 18 MAR 2019 5:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 18, 2019


    Smt Sayantani Jafa, belonging to the 1991 batch of Indian Audit & Accounts Service has assumed the charge of the Principal Accountant General (General & Social Audit), West Bengal with effect from January 29, 2019. Before this assignment, she was Principal Accountant (Accounts & Entitlement), Maharashtra. She has also worked in various senior managerial positions of the Institution of C&AG of India including Director General of Audit, Eastern Railway, Kolkata and Accountant General (Audit), Maharashtra.


    From now onwards, she will be handling Constitutional responsibility of audit of expenditure incurred by the Government of West Bengal and its Public Sector Undertakings and autonomous bodies under the General and Social Sector and also audit of Annual Accounts (Finance Accounts and Appropriation Accounts) of the State Government compiled by the office of the Pr AG (A & E), West Bengal and certification thereof with other assignments.
