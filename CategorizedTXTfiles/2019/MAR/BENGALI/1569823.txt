      Election Commission

      Expenditure Observers for Eight Districts of West Bengal
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2019 2:48PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: 29 March 2019


    Twelve (12) Expenditure Observers have been appointed by Election Commission of India (ECI) till date for 11 Parliamentary Constituencies in eight districts of West Bengal.


    They are - Shri Nasir Khan for Coochbehar (SC) Constituency, Smt Shreekala D Pardeshi (IRS) for Alipurduar (ST), Shri Srinivasa Rao Vana (IRS) for Jalpaiguri (SC) Constituency, Dr Sudhanshu Rai and Shri Shiva Sewak for Darjeeling Contituency, Shri P Sridharan for Raigunj Constituency of Uttar Dinajpur, Shri S Devarajan for Balurghat Constitueny of Dakshin Dinajpur. For Maldaha Uttar and Dakshin Constituencies, Shri Sandeep Kumar and Shri Himanshu P Joshi respectively have been appointed as Expenditure Observers. For the three Constituencies of Murshidabad district, Shri Gurbinder Singh (Jangipur), Shri Ravi Kumar (Baharampur) and Shri Appu Joseph Jose (Murshidabad) have been appointed as Expenditure Observers by ECI.
