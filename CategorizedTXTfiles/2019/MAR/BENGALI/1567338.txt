      Eighth Edition of Indo-Bangla Joint Exercise Sampriti-2019 Commences
      Posted On: 04 MAR 2019 6:00PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, March 4, 2019


    As a part of the ongoing India-Bangladesh defence cooperation, the eighth edition of joint exercise Sampriti-2019 commenced at Bangabondhu Cantonment, Tangail, Bangladesh on March 2, 2019. The opening ceremony for the same was done on March 3, 2019 (Sunday). The exercise will continue till March 15, 2019.


    The Indian contingent was represented by the ninth battalion of the Rajputana Rifles, while the Bangladesh counterpart was represented by 36 East Bengal Battalion.


    Major General Mizanur Rahman Shamim, General Officer Commanding, 19 Infantry Division, Bangladesh Army welcomed the Indian contingent and in his speech, he highlighted the common shared beliefs of democracy, freedom, equality and justice that are nurtured by both the countries.


    In this edition of exercise, as a platoon level exercise and graduated to company level exercise-2015 onwards, both sides will jointly train, plan and execute a series of well-developed tactical drills for neutralisation of likely threats that may be encountered during simulated UN peace-keeping operations.
