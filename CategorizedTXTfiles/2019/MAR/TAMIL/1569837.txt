      Income Tax Offices in Tamil Nadu & Puducherry Region to remain open on 30, 31 March
      Posted On: 29 MAR 2019 4:59PM by PIB Chennai


    T he press release issued by Ms. R Ilavarasi, Addl. Commissioner of Income Tax, Office of the Pr. Chief Commissioner of Income Tax, TN & Puducherry Region Chennai, states that the last date for filing belated/revised tax returns for the Assessment Year 2018-19 falls on 31 st March 2019 and the financial Year also closes on 31 st March 2019. Therefore all the Income Tax Offices in Tamilnadu & Puducherry Region shall remain open on 30 th March 2019 and 31 st March 2019 during normal office hours to facilitate filing of tax returns by the taxpayers.
