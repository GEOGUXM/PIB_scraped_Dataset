      Ministry of Railways

      DAK ADALAT TO SOLVE PROBLEMS RELATING TO POSTAL SERVICES
      Posted On: 25 MAR 2019 5:17PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    Ahmedabad, 25-03-2019


    Department of Post proposes to hold Dak Adalat for Settlement of grievances relating to Postal services. Dak Adalat for 1 st Quarter will be held at 10:30 hours onwards on 28.03.2019 (Thursday) at O/o the Postmaster General, Head Quarter Region, Speed Post Bhavan, Shahibaug, Ahmedabad-380004.


    The Postmaster General will hear each complainant individually and settle the problem of public on the spot. The idea of the above stated Adalat is to give a personal hearing and settlement of problems of the Public on the spot.
