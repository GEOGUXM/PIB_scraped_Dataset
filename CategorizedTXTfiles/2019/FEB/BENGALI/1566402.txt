      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      3-Day Training Event on Youth Leadership & Community Development Programme of NYKS Starts Today
      Posted On: 26 FEB 2019 6:23PM by PIB Kolkata


    Barrackpore, February 26, 2019


    The Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS), South Kolkata under Union Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is organising a three-day training workshop on Youth Leadership & Community Development programme. The workshop started today at Monirampur Fishery Research Institute under Barrackpore sub-division of North 24 Parganas. The workshop was inaugurated by Shri Nabin Kumar Nayek, State Director, NYKS. The workshop will end on February 28, 2019.


    In his inaugural speech, Shri Nayek said, the youths coming for training in this workshop will be made aware of the various flagship programmes of the Union Government as well as the schemes of the State Government. The youths will also be sensitised on how to enhance capacities of young people to take leadership in respect of community development and welfare activities. The workshop will also deal with the ways the young people may achieve employment avenues.


    Total 40 youths from the different parts of West Bengal as well as several youth organisations are participating in this training-based workshop.
