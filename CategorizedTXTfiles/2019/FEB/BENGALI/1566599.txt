      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      Pritish Bharat takes over as Chief of West Bengal State Office of Indian Oil Corporation Limited
      Posted On: 28 FEB 2019 1:29PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, February 28, 2019


    Shri Pritish Bharat, Executive Director, IndianOil has taken as Chief of West Bengal State Office from February18 , 2019. Prior to taking over the as the Chief of West Bengal State office of IndianOil , he was the State Head of Odisha State Office of IndianOil.


    A Mechanical Engineer from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela with Post Graduation in Business Administration & Management from Slovenia University, Shri Bharat has over three decades of experience in Petroleum Industry and has vast knowledge in spearheading retail function of IndianOil.


    Serving this energy behemoth of India, Shri Bharat has worked in major leadership roles for IndianOil in various locations across the country.
