      Sahitya Akademi held condolence meeting to pay tribute to Atin Bandopadhyay
      Posted On: 14 FEB 2019 11:51AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 14 February, 2019.


    Sahitya Akademi organized a condolence meeting to pay tribute to Atin Bandopadhyay on Wednesday (February 13, 2019) in Kolkata. Dr. Devendra Kumar Devesh, Regional Secretary, Sahitya Aakdemi, presented a brief life sketch of Atin Bandyopadhyay before the audience. The dignitaries present on the occasion included eminent poet and Convenor of Bengali Advisory Board of Sahitya Aakdemi Dr. Subodh Sarkar, Ms Krishna Basu, Sri Samik Ghosh, and Shri Subrata Mukhopadhyay.They spoke about the life and works of Late Atin Bandopadhyay and the influence of his writings on contemporary writers.
