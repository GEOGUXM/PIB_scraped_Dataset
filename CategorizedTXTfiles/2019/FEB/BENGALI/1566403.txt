      SER EMERGES CHAMPIONS IN 33rd ALL INDIA RAILWAY BODY BUILDING CHAMPIONSHIP
      Posted On: 26 FEB 2019 6:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 26 th February, 2019


    South Eastern Railway emerged Champions in the 33 rd All India Railway Body Building Championship held at Kharagpur from 22 nd to 24 th February, 2019 organised by South Eastern Railway Sports Association (SERSA). The 2 nd and 3 rd positions were bagged by Central Railway and Southern Railway respectively.


    S E Railway has consecutively bagged 1 st Position in the last 6 years in the All India Railway Body Building Championship.
