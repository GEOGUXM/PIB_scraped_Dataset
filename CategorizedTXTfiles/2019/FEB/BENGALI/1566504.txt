      Ministry of Defence

      GOC-in-C, Eastern Command Visits Panagarh Military Station
      Posted On: 27 FEB 2019 4:56PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, February 27, 2019


    The General Officer Commanding-in-Chief of Eastern Command, Lieutenant General Manoj Mukund Naravane has emphasised on the necessity to remain ready for meeting the emergency security challenges of the country. He visited Panagarh Military Station today, where he was received by Lieutenant General P N Rao, General Officer Commanding, Brahmastra Corps.


    The Brahmastra Corps was raised at Ranchi and will shortly be shifting to Panagarh.


    The Army Commander reviewed the security situation and operational preparedness of the formation. He also interacted with the troops and appreciated their continued efforts in developing the Military Station.
