      Ministry of External Affairs

      POST OFFICE PASSPORT SEVA KENDRAS TO START AT SAMSI, KAKDWIP, JIAGUNJ AND GHATAL
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2019 6:19PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 February 2019


    Post Office Passport SevaKendra (POPSK), Samsi and Kakdwip are going to be inaugurated on 27.02.2019 and on 01.03.2019 respectively. Also Post Office Passport SevaKendra (POPSK),Ghatal and Jiaganj are going to be operationalize from 27.02.2019. Addresses of the POPSKs aregiven below. Applicants are requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheetsalong with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original &self-attested Xerox copy). The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from thewebsite www.passportindia.gov.in . 40 appointments will be attended to from the date ofinauguration/operationalization onwards at all the POPSKs. Appointments for the same will bereleased on the website www.passportindia.gov.in as per the details given below. The applicantshave to pay the passport fees through online payment system.
