      Ministry of Finance

      Swachhta Pakhwada observed in the office of Central Tax (Audit), Kolkata Commissionerate
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2019 5:27PM by PIB Kolkata


    - Kolkata, 6 February, 2019


    Swachhta Pakhwada was observed in the office of Central Tax (Audit), Kolkata Commissionerate from January 16-31, 2019. The officers and staffs of the Commissionerate participated and performed in the cleanliness drive which included activities like weeding out old files, reviewing divyang access in toilets and publicity programmes like essay writing and cartoon drawing competition on the topic of cleanliness.
