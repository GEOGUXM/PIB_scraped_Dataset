      Ministry of External Affairs

      POST OFFICE PASSPORT SEVA KENDRA AT JHARGRAM
      Posted On: 15 FEB 2019 3:33PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15 February 2019


    Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Jhrgram is going to be inaugurated on February 22, 2019 at Jhargram Head Post Office, Jhargram. Applicants are requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original & self-attested Xerox copy).


    The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in.Forty appointments will be entertained from February 22, 2019 onwards.


    Appointments for the same will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.inonFebruary 20, 2019 from 3.30 pm onwards. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system.


    Only those applicants who have obtained appointments through the online mode (www.passportindia.gov.in) will be entertained. Tatkaal, On-Hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed.
