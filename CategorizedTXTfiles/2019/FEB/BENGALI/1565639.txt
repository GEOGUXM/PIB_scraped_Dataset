      SAHITYA AKADEMI PAYS HOMAGE TO LATE PROF. NAMVAR SINGH
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2019 6:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 February 2019


    SahityaAkademi paid homage to the eminent Hindi critic and scholar Prof.Namvar Singh, the fellow of SahityaAmademi today at the Akademi’s Kolkata office. Prof. Singh passed away on February 19, 2019.


    Prof.Namvar Singh was born on July 28, 1926 in village Jiyanpur in Varanasi, district of Uttar Pradesh. He completed his masters with gold medal from Benaras Hindu University and even completed PhD.


    Prof. Singh taught in Benaras Hindu University as well as in Sagar University and Jodhpur University. But it was in Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), created a new pattern of teaching and prepared a curriculum for that. He retired from JNU in the year 1992 after which he served as a Professor Emeritus at the Centre of Indian Languages at JNU.


    Apart from his academic engagement Dr. Singh worked as the editor of ‘Janyuga’ (weekly news magazine) and ‘Alochana’ (Hindi literary magazine).


    After retirement from JNU Dr. Singh was appointed as Chancellor of Mahatma Gandhi Antarrashtriya Hindi Vishwavidlaya, Wardah.


    Recipient of many prestigious awards including SahityaBhusanSamman of Uttar Pradesh Hindi Sansthan and SahityaAkademi Award, Prof. Singh wrote more than a dozen of books including KavitaKeNayePratiman and Chhayavaad.
