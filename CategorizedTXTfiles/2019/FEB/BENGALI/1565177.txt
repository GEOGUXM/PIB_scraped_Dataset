      SAHITYA AKADEMI ORGANISES PRAVASI MANCHA WITH GAUTAM DUTTA
      Posted On: 19 FEB 2019 2:18PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 19 February 2019.


    Sahitya Akademi organized Pravasi Mancha with Shri Gautam Dutta of USA who is famous for publications like Yoddha Ghumiea Ache, Borofe Holud Ful, Dui Kobi Mukhomukhi, Griha Yuddha and the like on February 18, 2019 at the Akademi’s office auditorium. Dr. Subodh Sarkar, an eminent poet and convenor of Bengali Advisory Board of Sahitya Akademi introduced Shri Dutta to the audience. It is to be noted that Dr. Sarkar being an avid follower of Shri Dutt’s poetry, reminisced the days when he met Shri Dutta to eagerly hear him.


    In the beginning of the function, Dr. Devendra Kumar Devesh, Regional Secretary, Sahitya Akademi gave a brief introduction of the programme besides welcoming the guests.


    Shri Dutta read out 16 poems, like Pratidinee Ekushe February, Anjelika Movie Hall, Debdut, Ektu Pa Chaliea, etc. The themes of his poems are based on relationships, family and contemporary ups and downs of the society. The poet even recited a poem on Sunil Gangopadhyay and Subhas Mukhopadhyay after which followed the interaction of the poet with the audience.


    It is to be noted that renowned litterateur Late Sunil Gangopadhyay and Shri Dutta co-edited African-Americal poetry collection Ami Amar Mrityur Por Sadhinata Chai Na .
