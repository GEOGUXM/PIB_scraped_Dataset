      Ministry of Defence

      DGR EX-SERVICEMEN EMPLOYMENT SEMINAR IN CITY
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2019 6:30PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 20 February 2019


    Directorate General Resettlement (DGR), Ministry of Defence, will bring together job seekers and employers on a single platform at the DGR Ex-Servicemen Employment Seminar to be held on 27 February 2019 at Army Cantonment, opposite Nicco Park, Sector V, Salt Lake, Kolkata-700091.


    Registration for all ESM/Retiring Service personnel will be carried out at the venue. ESM need to bring ESM ID Card and five copies of latest CV/Bio data with photograph. Shortly Retiring Service personnel need to bring Armed Forces ID Card and five copies of CV/Bio data with photograph. Entry will be on first come first serve basis.


    For further queries and assistance, candidates may contact Joint Director (SE & CI), DGR through e-mail seopadgr@desw.gov.in and telephone: +91 1126174531 / 9911112476. Website: http://www/dgrindia.com , http://www/triviz.com .
