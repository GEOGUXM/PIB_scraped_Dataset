      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Shri Nitin Gadkari to lay foundation stone for a Flyover in Jabalpur today
      Posted On: 22 FEB 2019 11:12AM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister for Road Transport & Highways, Shipping and Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation Shri Nitin Gadkari will lay the foundation stone for a flyover project at Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh today. The 5.9 km long flyover from Damoh Toll Plaza to Ranital Madan Mahal Chowk, Medical College Road is estimated to cost Rs 758.54 crore.
