      President of India in Kanpur; inaugurates centenary celebrations of DAV College and addresses annual day of BNSD College
      Posted On: 25 FEB 2019 6:39PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, inaugurated the centenary celebrations of the DAV College, Kanpur today (February 25, 2019) in Kanpur. In a separate event, he also graced and addressed the alumni association conference and annual day function of the BNSD Inter College and Shiksha Niketan, Kanpur. In his younger days, the President studied in both the institutions.


    Earlier in the day the President visited the Balaji Mandir, Kanpur and the Dhamma Kalyana Kanpur International Vipassana Meditation Centre.
