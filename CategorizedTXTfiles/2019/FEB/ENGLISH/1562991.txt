      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Cabinet approves the revised Office Memorandum pertaining to the Cabinet Note on “Amendment of Constitution to provide for reservation for Economically Weaker Sections”
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2019 9:49PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi has given ex post facto approval to the revised Office Memorandum (OM) pertaining to the Cabinet Note on “Amendment of Constitution to provide for reservation for Economically Weaker Sections”. The OM was approved by the Cabinet on 8 th January 2019.


    Benefits:


    The approval will promote social equity by providing opportunities in higher education and employment to those who have been excluded by virtue of their economic status.
