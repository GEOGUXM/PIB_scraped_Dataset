      UPSC

      Lateral Recruitment to Senior Positions in Government of India
      Posted On: 19 FEB 2019 1:52PM by PIB Delhi


    Department of Personnel & Training (DOPT) had invited applications on 10 th June 2018 from talented and motivated Indian nationals willing to contribute towards nation building to join the Government at the level of Joint Secretary in ten different Ministries/Departments of the Government of India on contract basis.


    The task of selection of suitable candidates from those who applied was entrusted to the Union Public Service Commission on 29 th October, 2018. UPSC launched Detailed Application Form (DAF) on 11.12.2018 with closing date as 01.01.2019 and detailed information was collected from all those applicants who had applied in response to DoPT advertisement.


    On the basis of the Detailed Application Form (DAF) submitted by the candidates, the Union Public Service Commission has shortlisted the candidates to be called for interview to the posts under reference. The modalities adopted for shortlisting and the list of shortlisted/provisionally shortlisted candidates have been made available in the public domain on the official website of the Commission ( www.upsc.gov.in ) on 15.02.2019.
