      Ministry of Steel

      16.7% increase in steel export in 2017-18
      Posted On: 04 FEB 2019 1:47PM by PIB Delhi


    The total exports of finished steel have grown by 16.7% in 2017-18. The Minister of State for Steel, Vishnu Deo Sai, informed this in Lok Sabha today. The details of exports of finished steel are as under: -


    (Figures in MTs)

                                                     
                           2016-17  2017-18  Increase
                                                     
                                                     
                                                     
Exports of Finished Steel  8.245    9.620    16.68%  
                                                     
                                                     

    The contribution of non-alloy steel segment in total finished steel exports stood at 8.727 MT. The breakup of finished steel exports in 2017-18 is given below: -


    (Figures in MTs)

                                                             
                                             2016-17  2017-18
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
Exports of Finished Steel - Non-Alloy        7.587    8.727  
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
Exports of Finished Steel - Alloy/Stainless  0.658    0.893  
                                                             
                                                             
                                                             
Total Exports of finished Steel              8.245    9.620
