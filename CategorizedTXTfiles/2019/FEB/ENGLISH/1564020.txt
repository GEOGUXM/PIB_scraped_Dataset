      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Cinematograph (Amendment) Bill, 2019 introduced in Rajya Sabha
      Posted On: 12 FEB 2019 3:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The Cinematograph (Amendment) Bill 2019 was introduced in Rajya Sabha today. The Bill seeks to amend provisions of Cinematograph Act, 1952 in order to tackle film piracy by including penal provisions for unauthorized camcording and duplication of films.
