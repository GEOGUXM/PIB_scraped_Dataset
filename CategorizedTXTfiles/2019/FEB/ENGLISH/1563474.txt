      President of India pays floral tributes to Dr Zakir Hussain on his birth anniversary
      Posted On: 08 FEB 2019 12:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid floral tributes to Dr Zakir Hussain, former President of India, on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (February 8, 2019).
