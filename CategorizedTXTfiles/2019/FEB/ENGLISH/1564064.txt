      Ministry of Culture

      Digitisation undertaken by National Archives of India
      Posted On: 12 FEB 2019 5:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Digitization in National Archives of India is a core activity, undertaken by it in a phased manner. The digitized records are uploaded on Abhilekh Patal, online search portal of NAI. Information available online helps scholars, historians, academicians and other users of archives in the country sitting anywhere at their desk minimizing in-person visit in NAI.


    The expenditure incurred by NAI during the last two financial years and in the current financial year is given below :

     1. In the financial year 2016-17 – Rs. 61,67,817.00
     2. In the financial year 2017-18 – Rs. 1,08,92,691.00
     3. In current financial year 2018-19 – Rs. 78,10,429.00

    The above information was given by Minister of State (independent charge) for Culture and Minister of State for Environment, Forest and Climate change Dr. Mahesh Sharma, in reply to an Unstarred Question in the Rajya Sabha, today.
