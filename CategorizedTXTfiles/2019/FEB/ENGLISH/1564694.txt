      Ministry of Finance

      Additional Offering Period (TAP) of Bharat 22 ETF opened for a day;Issue got over-subscribed by over 10 times of Base Issue size
      Posted On: 14 FEB 2019 12:53PM by PIB Delhi


    Additional offering of Bharat 22 ETF was opened for a day today. The Base Issue size was fixed at Rs.3500 crore. The issue received overwhelming response from all categories of investors including the retail investors. The issue was over-subscribed over 10 times of base issue size till 7.00 pm and received about Rs.40,000 crore. The Government has decided to retain approximately Rs.10,000 crore as its divestment proceeds.
