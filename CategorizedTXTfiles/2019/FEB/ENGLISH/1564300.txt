      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Second Meeting of Search Committee for recommending panel of names for Chairperson and Members of Lokpal held today
      
      Committee takes note of the applications/nominations received in response to its advertisement

      Posted On: 13 FEB 2019 6:45PM by PIB Delhi

    The Second Meeting of the Search Committee for recommending the panel of names for Chairperson and Members of Lokpal was held here today (13 th February 2019). All the Members of the Search Committee were present during the meeting.


    The Committee took note of the applications/nominations received in response to its Advertisement dated 6 th February 2019. The Committee also took note of earlier applications received during the term of previous Search Committee and decided that as the term of the earlier Committee had ended, these applications are not valid. Interested persons may apply afresh if they are eligible.


    The Committee decided to hold its next meeting on 25 th February 2019.
