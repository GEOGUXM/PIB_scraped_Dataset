      Department of Space

      Manned Mission to Space
      Posted On: 07 FEB 2019 7:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has set time-line for sending manned mission to space. It is proposed to demonstrate human spaceflight before 75 th anniversary of Indian Independence (Year 2022).


    The amount expected to be spent on this mission is within Rs. 10,000 crores.


    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
