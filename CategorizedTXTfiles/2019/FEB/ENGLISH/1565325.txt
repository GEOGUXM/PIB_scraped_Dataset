      Ministry of External Affairs

      Cabinet approves MoU between India and Morocco on the Reciprocal facilitation of procedures for issue of Business Visa
      Posted On: 19 FEB 2019 9:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi has given ex-post facto approval to the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between India and Morocco on the Reciprocal facilitation of procedures for issue of Business Visa.


    Benefits:


    The MoU aims to facilitate issuance of multiple entry business visa to nationals of the two countries who wish to travel to the territory of the other country to make a business deal, establish an industrial / business project, buy/sell industrial products, or set up other business / investments related to these activities. Such visas are normally required to be issued for a period of up to 12 months within a specified time limit of 7 days in regular cases. In cases whether further scrutiny is required, both sides may inform the applicant accordingly.
