      Former President, Shri Pranab Mukherjee to release a compilation of the Vice President’s speeches on February 15
      
      Several eminent personalities to grace the occasion

      Posted On: 13 FEB 2019 5:26PM by PIB Delhi

    The former President, Shri Pranab Mukherjee will release a compilation of speeches of the Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu, “Selected Speeches: Volume-1” at Vigyan Bhavan, New Delhi at 5 PM on February 15.


    The book comprises selected speeches delivered by Shri Venkaiah Naidu at more than 400 meetings since his assumption of office on August 11, 2017. They provide an insight into his vision and perspective on a wide range of issues confronting the nation. During his interactions, the Vice President has been focusing on agriculture, youth and education, science and technology and culture.


    The book contains 92 speeches and is categorized into six broad sections - ‘Functioning of Legislatures’, ‘Nation and Nationalism’, ‘Polity and Governance’, ‘Economic Development’, ‘Media’ and ‘India and the World’.


    The Leader of Opposition, Rajya Sabha, Shri Ghulam Nabi Azad, the Minister of State for Youth Affairs & Sports and Information & Broadcasting (I/C), Col. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore, the Deputy Chairman, Rajya Sabha, Shri Harivansh and other dignitaries will be present on the occasion.


    The Publications Division of the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting has brought out the book.
