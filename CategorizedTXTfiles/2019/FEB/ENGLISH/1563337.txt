      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Release of Funds from NSDF
      Posted On: 07 FEB 2019 5:32PM by PIB Delhi


    Assistance from National Sports Development Fund (NSDF) has been given during last four years to sportspersons for their customized training belonging to 22 sports disciplines viz., athletics, archery, badminton, boxing, chess, cycling, fencing, golf, gymnastics, hockey, judo, sailing, shooting, squash, swimming, table tennis, tennis, weightlifting, wrestling, wushu, para-sports and winter sports. The selected athletes are provided financial assistance for their customised training at Institutes having world class facilities and other necessary support. The above list of sports disciplines is inclusive of the sports disciplines, sportspersons of which have been assisted under Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS), which is being implemented by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports within overall ambit of NSDF.


    Funds from NSDF are released to the sportspersons directly or to the coaches / academies associated with their customized training. State-wise details of sportspersons given assistance from NSDF are not maintained. Details of funds released sports-wise from NSDF during 2015-16 to 2018-19 are given in Annexure .


    This information was given by Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Youth Affairs and Sports Col. Rajyavardhan Rathore (Retd.) in a written reply to the Lok Sabha today.
