      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Appointment
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2019 5:49PM by PIB Delhi


    The Competent Authority has approved assignment of additional charge of the post of Secretary of the newly created Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare to Shri Tarun Shridhar, lAS (HP:1984), Secretary, Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying with immediate effect and till the appointment of a regular incumbent or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
