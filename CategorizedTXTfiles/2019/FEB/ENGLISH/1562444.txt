      Prime Minister's Office

      PM Condoles Loss of Lives due to Derailment of Seemanchal Express
      Posted On: 03 FEB 2019 1:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi was deeply anguished by the loss of lives due to the derailment of coaches of the Seemanchal Express. He said “My thoughts are with the bereaved families. May the injured recover quickly. Railways, NDRF, and local authorities are providing all possible assistance in the wake of the accident.”
