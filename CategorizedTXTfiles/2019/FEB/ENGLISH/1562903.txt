      Ministry of Culture

      President of India Presents Sangeet Natak Akademi Awards 2017
      Posted On: 06 FEB 2019 6:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, presented the Sangeet Natak Akademi Awards for the year 2017 at a function held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (February 06, 2019)


    Union Minister of state(I/c) for Culture, Dr. Mahesh Sharma along with senior officers of the Ministry along with many dignitaries from the field of Art & Culture attended the event.
