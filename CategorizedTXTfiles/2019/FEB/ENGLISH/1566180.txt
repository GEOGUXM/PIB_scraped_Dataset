      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister to inaugurate and lay foundation stone of various residential and office buildings of CAPFs, CPOs and Delhi Police tomorrow
      Posted On: 25 FEB 2019 1:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Home Minister Shri Rajnath Singh will inaugurate 29 infrastructure projects (both residential and non-residential buildings) of Central Armed Police Forces (BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, NDRF & SSB), Delhi Police and Central Police Organizations (Land Ports Authority of India and Central Forensic Science Laboratories) at a function here tomorrow. These projects have been constructed at various locations across the Country at a total cost of Rs.1,714.97 crore. The projects involve 53 non-residential buildings and 4,723 residential quarters.
