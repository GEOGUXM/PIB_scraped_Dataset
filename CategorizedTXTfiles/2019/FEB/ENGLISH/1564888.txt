      President of India to Grace the Closing Ceremony of Fourth Agri Leadership Summit in Ganaur, Sonipat, Tomorrow
      Posted On: 16 FEB 2019 7:33PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Haryana (Ganaur, Sonipat) tomorrow (February 17, 2019) to grace the closing ceremony of the Fourth Agri Leadership Summit, organised by the Government of Haryana. He will also present the Haryana Kisaan Ratna Puraskaar and the Haryana Krishi Ratna Puraskaar on the occasion.
