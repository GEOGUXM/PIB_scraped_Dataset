      President of India to visit Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh on February 21 and 22
      Posted On: 20 FEB 2019 5:24PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Tamil Nadu (Chennai) and Andhra Pradesh (Venkatachalam, Nellore) on February 21 and 22, 2019.


    On February 21, 2019, the President will unveil the statue of Mahatma Gandhi at the Dakshina Bharat Hindi Prachar Sabha in Chennai.


    On February 22, 2019, the President will grace the 18 th anniversary celebrations of the Swarna Bharat Trust in Venkatachalam. He will also visit the Akshara Vidyalaya in Venkatachalam.
