      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to attend the Gita Aradhana Mahotsav at ISKCON, New Delhi tomorrow
      Posted On: 25 FEB 2019 6:47PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will attend the Gita Aradhana Mahotsav at ISKCON-Glory of India Cultural Centre, East of Kailash, New Delhi, on 26 February 2019.


    At the event, Prime Minister will unveil the Bhagwad Gita, prepared by ISKCON devotees to the world. Measuring over 2.8 meters and over 800 kg, the Bhagwad Gita is one of the kind in the world. It will have the original verses of Bhagwad Gita along with commentary. Prime Minister will turn a page of the Bhagwad Gita to mark its official opening.


    Prime Minister will also address the gathering.
