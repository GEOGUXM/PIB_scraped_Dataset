      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to visit Durgapur, West Bengal on 2 February 2019
      Posted On: 01 FEB 2019 6:01PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri. Narendra Modi, will visit Durgapur in West Bengal on February 2, 2019.


    During the visit, he will dedicate to the nation, the Railway electrification of 294 kilometre long Andal- Sainthia-Pakur-Malda and Khana- Sainthia Sections. Electrification of this section will enable ease of transportation of coal, stone chips and ballast to North and North East India in a seamless manner.


    He will also dedicate the twenty kilometre long Hijli- Narayangarh third line to the nation.
