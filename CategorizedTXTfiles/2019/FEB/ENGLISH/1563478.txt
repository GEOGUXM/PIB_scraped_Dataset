      Envoys of four nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 08 FEB 2019 12:44PM by PIB Delhi


    High Commissioner of Namibia and Ambassadors of Maldives, Turkmenistan and Sudan presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (February 08, 2019).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Mr Gabriel P Sinimbo, High Commissioner of the Republic of Namibia


    2. Ms Aishath Mohamed Didi, Ambassador of the Republic of Maldives


    3. Mr Shalar Geldynazarov, Ambassador of Turkmenistan


    4. Mr Ahmed Yousif Mohamed Elsiddig, Ambassador of the Republic of Sudan
