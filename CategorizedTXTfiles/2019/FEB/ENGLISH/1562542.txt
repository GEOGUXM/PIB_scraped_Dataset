      Ministry of Labour & Employment

      Sectoral Job Creation
      Posted On: 04 FEB 2019 4:16PM by PIB Delhi


    Government has decided to formulate a new Industrial Policy which would be a roadmap for all business enterprises in the country for which consultations have been held with stakeholders to get their ideas and feedback.


    This information was given by Shri Santosh Kumar Gangwar Union Minister of State (I/C) for Labour and Employment in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
