      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Shabir Shah, all other inmates from Kashmir & Pakistan fit & fine in Tihar, prison cautions against fake news in media
      Posted On: 24 FEB 2019 6:53PM by PIB Delhi


    Tihar prison authorities in Delhi have denied occurrence of any untoward incident or mishap involving inmate Shabir Shah from Jammu and Kashmir.


    Refuting fake news circulating in a section of media, DIG Tihar Prison, Shri Shailendra Parihar said Shabir Shah is absolutely "fit and fine". He said all other inmates from J&K and Pakistan are also fine. The Prison authorities appealed to all to be cautious against fake news.
