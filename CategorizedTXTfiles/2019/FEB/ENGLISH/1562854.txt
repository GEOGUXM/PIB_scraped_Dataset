      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Search Committee for Lokpal invites applications for Chairperson and Members of Lokpal
      
      Last date for receipt of applications/nominations is 22nd February, 2019

      Posted On: 06 FEB 2019 4:49PM by PIB Delhi

    The Search Committee for Lokpal headed by Justice Ranjana Prakash Desai has invited applications for the purpose appointment of Chairperson and Members of Lokpal.


    The eligibility criteria and application/nomination form can be accessed through the website of the Department of Personnel and Training viz. http://dopt.gov.in under the link “Lokpal”:


    Link: https://dopt.gov.in/sites/default/files/Lokpal-Advertisement.pdf


    The last date for receipt of applications/nominations is 22 nd February, 2019 – 05.00 PM. The same may be addressed to the Chairperson, Search Committee for appointment of Chairperson and Members of the Lokpal at Post Box No. 12, GPO, New Delhi – 110001 or may be forwarded by e-mail at the following id lokpal.searchcomm@gov.in latest by 22 nd February at 05.00 PM. The date/time stamp of the application/nomination at the receiving end will be treated as final for the purpose.


    The applications/nominations received after the last date and stipulated time will not be entertained.
