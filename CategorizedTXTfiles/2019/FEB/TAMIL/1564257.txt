      CAG Submits finance accounts and appropriation accounts
      Posted On: 13 FEB 2019 5:15PM by PIB Chennai


    Under the Comptroller and Auditor General’s (duties, powers and conditions of services) Act, 1971, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India prepares the finance accounts of the government of tamil Nadu showing that year’s receipts and disbursements for the state annually. Along with this, the Comptroller and Auditor General of India also compiles the appropriation accounts for the state government showing the excess or saving under each grant or appropriation as passed by the legislature. Both these accounts duly signed by Comptroller and Auditor General of India for the year 2017-18 were forwarded to the state government for being laid before the state legislature. Another publication ‘Accounts at a Glance’ reflecting the concised version of these accounts also prepared by the accountant general, is also sent to state government for circulation among the state legislators and senior officers of the state government.
