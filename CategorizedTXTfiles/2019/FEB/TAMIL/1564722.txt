      PENSION ADALAT
      Posted On: 15 FEB 2019 3:01PM by PIB Chennai


    The Postal Department proposes to conduct “PENSION ADALAT” AT Regional Level at the Office of the POSTMASTER-GENERAL, SOUTHERN REGION, TN., MADURAI 625 002 at 11 A.M. on 12.03.2019 in order to provide an opportunity to Postal Service/Family Pensioners to represent their grievances or complaint, if any, related to the unsettled Pension claims.


    As such, Postal Pensioners/Family Pensioners are requested to forward two copies of their representations along with the PPO No, if available, to the Postmaster-General, Southern Regiona, Tamilnadu, Madurai-625 002 on or before 25.02.2019.
