      CIPET creates Guinness World Record; forms “Largest Human Recycling Logo” with 3373 students
      Posted On: 04 FEB 2019 4:49PM by PIB Chennai


    CIPET organized an event for attempting the Guinness World Record on “Largest Human Recycling Logo” on 23 rd January 2019 @ 8.30 Hrs. at YMCA Ground, Nandanam, Chennai wherein 3373 students had participated with an aim to promote awareness for using Biodegradable Plastics and recycled plastics in general. This event was held as a part of the Golden Jubilee Celebrations of CIPET.


    CIPET has successfully broken the previous Guinness World Record achieved at an event organised by The New Indian Express, Nippon Paint India Pvt Ltd and B.S Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science & Technology (all India) in Chennai, India, on 19 March 2018 with 1,726 people.


    The Asia Books of Record issued the Certificate on the spot by recognizing the CIPET’s initiatives and now the Guinness World Record assessment team has successfully awarded the Certificate to CIPET for its achievement.


    Director General, CIPET - Prof.(Dr.). S. K .Nayak congratulated the entire Team CIPET who put sincere efforts to attain this prestigious award. Further, he said that with the support of Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers, Govt. of India, CIPET is always leading in front to create awareness on Positive attributes of Plastics and Recycling of plastics as well as Biodegradable plastics in the country.
