      Ministry of Commerce & Industry

      Applications Invited for National Intellectual Property Awards
      Posted On: 04 FEB 2019 5:52PM by PIB Mumbai


    National Intellectual Property (IP) Awards are conferred to recognize and reward:


     1. contribution of individual, company, R&D institutions, academic institutions and organizations for their creations and commercialization of IP which have contributed to harnessing the countryâ€™s intellectual capital and creating IP eco-system that boosts creativity and innovation, and


     2. contribution of law enforcement agency in ensuring effective implementation of IP laws and creating a healthy IP ecosystem.


    These awards are being given since 2009 onwards. Each award will carry a cash prize of Rs. 1,00,000 and a citation. The awards will be conferred by the Union Minister for Commerce and Industry, at an event organized by Office of Controller General of Patents, Designs and Trademarks in collaboration with Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), on World IP Day (26th April).
