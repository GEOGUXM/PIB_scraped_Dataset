      Maharashtra keep winning run intact in kho kho at Khelo India Youth Games
      Posted On: 14 JAN 2019 9:25PM by PIB Mumbai


    Hosts Maharashtra continued their run winning in three age group categories in the kho kho event at the Khelo India Youth Games 2019.


    In the Under-17 boys section the hosts downed Delhi 14-9, with Dilip Khandvi, Hrishikesh Joshi and Rohan Kore playing a stellar role. In the corresponding girls event, Maharashtra downed Tamil Nadu 13-9. The architect of the win was Srushti Shinde, Kiran Shinde and Ritika Magdum.
