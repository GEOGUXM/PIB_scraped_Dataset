      President of India Presents Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Bal Puraskar
      Posted On: 22 JAN 2019 6:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, presented the Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Bal Puraskar 2019 today (January 22, 2019) at Rashtrapati Bhavan in presence of Smt Maneka Sanjay Gandhi, Union Minister for Women and Child Development and other dignitaries.
