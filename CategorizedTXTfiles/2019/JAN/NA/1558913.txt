      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Flyover at Golagamudi Junction in Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh
      Posted On: 07 JAN 2019 4:57PM by PIB Delhi


    The issue of safety of road users at Golagamudi Junction in Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh was discussed in a meeting of the Road Safety Committee of Nellore district. The National Highways Authority of India has accordingly taken up the proposal for construction of two Vehicular Under Passes (VUPs), two L- VUPs and two flyovers -one at Golagamudi Junction- along with a service road on Nellore Bypass of NH-16. The estimate for the said structures has been prepared.


    This information was given by Shri Nitin Gadkari, Minister of Road Transport & Highways in response to a question in Rajya Sabha today.
