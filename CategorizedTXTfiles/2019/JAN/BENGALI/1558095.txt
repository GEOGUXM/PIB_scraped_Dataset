      Ministry of Defence

      DIG, BSF, Mizoram and Cachar Frontier, K.N.Suyal Retires
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2019 10:58AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 01, 2019.


    Mizoram and Cachar Frontier BSF Masimpur bade farewell to Shri K N Suyal, Deputy Inspector General of BSF on his superannuation on December 31, 2018 at BSF Campus Masimpur.


    Shri Suyal joined BSF in 1980 and served for glorious 38 years in various Borders, Training Institutions, Headquarters and UN-MISSION on deputation to Bosnia. Shri Suyal, DIG is a native of Dehradun (Uttarakhand).He commanded BSF Battalions and Sectors in Western and Eastern borders of India. In recognition of his dedicated & unblemished service to the nation, he had been decorated with President’s Police Medal for Meritorious Service and President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service in the year 2007 and 2017 respectively.


    Dr K C Mahali, IG BSF Mizoram and Cachar frontier, DIG/PSO Shri R P Sanwal, DIG (G) Shri J C Nayak, DIG (OPS) Shri Sanjay Shiva, BSF Officers, subordinate officers and other ranks gave an emotional farewell to him.


    Dr. K.C. Mahali, IG and Shri R.P. Sanwal, DIG/PSO presented a memento to Shri K. N. Suyal on behalf of the Staff during the farewell ceremony.
