      Ministry of Culture

      National level quiz on “Mahatma Gandhi; his life and works” held at BITM.
      Posted On: 29 JAN 2019 3:40PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 29, 2019.


    In commemoration of the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, a National level quiz competition on “Mahatma Gandhi; his life and works” was held at Birla Industrial and Technological Museum (BITM) here today. The National level quiz competition is being held by the National Council of Science Museum (NCSM) in all the 22 Science Centers across the country. Started on October, 2, 2018, the competition will conclude on January, 30, 2019. The final round will be held at Science City, Kolkata tomorrow on January, 30, 2019.


    381 teams comprising of 2 students from class VIII to class X participated at block level. The winning 22 teams from each center participated in today’s semi final competition which was held at BITM. The 5 winner teams from today’s competition will participate in final round which would be held at Science City tomorrow.


    Congratulating the participating students, Shri Jawhar Sirkar, former Secretary, Ministry of Culture, Government of India and former CEO Prasar Bharati and Shri Pratik Ghosh, Director and Secretary, Gandhi Smarok Sangrahaloy, Barrackpore shed light on the life and philosophy of Mahatma Gandhi and urged the students to revive the philosophy of Gandhi and apply in their individual lives and make the society a better place to live in.


    Shri V.S. Ramachandran, Director, BITM; Shri Samarendra Kumar, Director, NCSM (Hqrs) and Shri Subhabrata Chaudhuri, Director, Science City, Kolkata among others were also present on the occasion.
