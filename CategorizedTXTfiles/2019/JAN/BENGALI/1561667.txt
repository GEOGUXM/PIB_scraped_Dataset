      Ministry of External Affairs

      Post Office Passport Seva Kendra to be inaugurated at Ashoknagar, Barasat
      Posted On: 28 JAN 2019 4:17PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 28, 2019.


    A Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) will be inaugurated at Barasat at Ashoknagar Sub Post Office on 01.02.2019 (Friday). Applicants are requested to bring Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original and self attested Xerox copy). The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in from 01.02.2019 onwards. Appointments for the same will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.in from 30.01.2019 at 15.30 PM onwards. The applicants have to pay passport fee through online payment system.


    Further, applicants who have obtained appointments through the online mode www.passportindia.gov.in will be entertained. Tatkaal, On-hold files, wall-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed.
