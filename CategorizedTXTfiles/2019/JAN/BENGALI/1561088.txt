      Ministry of Defence

      OFB CONTRIBUTES RS 10 LAKHS TO ARMY BATTLE CASUALTIES FUND
      Posted On: 23 JAN 2019 4:05PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata: 03 Magha 1940


    Wednesday, 23 January 2019


    As a part of social responsibility, the Ordnance Factory Board (OFB) has contributed Rs 10 lakhs to the Army Battle Casualties Fund. Shri S P Mishra, Member of OFB alongwith Shri Rajvir Singh, Deputy Director General, handed over a cheque of Rs 10 lakhs to Maj Gen R P Kalita, Addl Director General (Ceremonial & Welfare) on 21 January 2019.


    The Indian Army has opened an account named ‘Army Welfare Fund Battle Casualties”, which accepts donations for the families of the deceased. The donations are utilized to render financial assistance to the Veer Naaris, next of kin and dependents. OFB has pledged continued support in this direction.
