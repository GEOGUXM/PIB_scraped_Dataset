      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN SIX PAIRS OF WEEKLY SUPERFAST SPECIAL TRAINS BETWEEN SANTRAGACHI AND CHENNAI
      Posted On: 14 JAN 2019 5:39PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 14, 2019.


    With a view to clear extra rush of passengers, South Eastern Railway (SER) has decided to run six pairs of Weekly Superfast Special trains between Santragachi-Chennai-Santragachi from January 18 to February 23, 2019.


    02841 Santragachi-Chennai Special leaving Santragachi at 12.40 PM every Friday will reach Chennai at 4.40 PM, the next day. In the opposite direction, 02842 Chennai-Santragachi Special leaving Chennai at 6.20 PM every Saturday will arrive Santragachi at 11.30 PM, night the next day.


    The special train consisting of four AC 2-Tier, five AC 3-Tier and four Sleeper Class coaches will have stoppages at Kharagpur, Bhadrak, Jajpur Keonjhar Road, Bhubaneswar, Khurda Road, Brahmapur, Vizianagaram, Duvvda, Rajahmundry, Vijayawada and Nellore between Santragachi and Chennai.
