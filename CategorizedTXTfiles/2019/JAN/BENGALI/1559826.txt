      Ministry of Defence

      Cleanliness Drive at Babughat
      Posted On: 14 JAN 2019 2:21PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 14, 2019.


    NCC Cadets of 1 Bengal Artillery Battery Fort William carried out cleanliness drive under the leadership of Major Dr. B. B. Singh inside Ganga Sagar Mela Camp at BabuGhat. The cadets also helped in distribution of food and water to the pilgrims. They also helped the organisers in the security awareness among the pilgrims and dispersal of the pilgrims to Ganga Sagar Mela.


    The Girl cadets helped the woman pilgrims, helping them in cleanliness and cautioning them of pickpockets and thieves.


    This programme was conducted by Col. Jainveer Singh Commanding Officer of 1 Bengal Artillery Battery Fort William.
