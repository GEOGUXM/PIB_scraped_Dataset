      PIB Kolkata celebrated 70th Republic Day
      Posted On: 26 JAN 2019 2:14PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 26, 2019


    The nation today celebrates the 70th Republic Day with pride, honour, vigour and enthusiasm. PIB Kolkata celebrated this special day along with the whole country. Smt Jane Namchu, ADG,(M&C) unfurled and hoisted the national flag while Sri Chinmay Chakraborty, Director (M&C) and all other staff members honoured the occasion. The programme was concluded with the national anthem being sung by all staff members of PIB Kolkata.
