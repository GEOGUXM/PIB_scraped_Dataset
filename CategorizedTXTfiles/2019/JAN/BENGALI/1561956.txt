      Ministry of External Affairs

      Post Office Passport Seva Kendra to be inaugurated at Darjeeling on February 4
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2019 5:12PM by PIB Kolkata


    -Kolkata, 30 th January, 2019


    A Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) will be inaugurated at Darjeeling in Ladenla Road, opposite Rink Mall on February 4, 2019. 40 appointments will be processed from February 4 onwards. Appointments will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.in from February 1, 2019 at 1530 hours onwards. The applicants have to pay passport fee through online payment system.


    Further, applicants are to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (in original as well as self attested photocopy) while reporting for the appointment. Detailed information of the documents required and other details can be obtained from the website www.passportindia.gov.in .
