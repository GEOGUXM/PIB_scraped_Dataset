      Ministry of Defence

      Eastern Command Celebrates Army day
      Posted On: 15 JAN 2019 1:37PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 15, 2019


    The Eastern Command today celebrated Army Day with dignity and honour to commemorate Lt Gen (later Field Marshal) K M Cariappa taking over as the first Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Army on this very day in 1949.


    Lt Gen M M Naravane, General Officer Commanding-in-Chief (GOC-in-C), Eastern Command laid a wreath at Vijay Smarak, Fort William and paid homage to martyrs and courageous soldiers who laid down their lives while protecting the country. Similar ceremonies were organised in all military stations in the Eastern Theatre.


    To mark the occasion, five units of Eastern Command were conferred with the Chief of Army Staff citation for exemplary professional performance. Similarly, 18 units were awarded the Unit Appreciation by GOC-in-C, Eastern Command, which includes 10 Infantry Battalions, two Artillery Regiments, two Engineer Regiments, one Aviation Squadron, one ASC Battalion and two Assam Rifles Battalions.


    It is to be mentioned that on this very day, the Army reaffirms its commitments towards the nation and assures her peace and security.
