      SAKSHAM-2019 starts in Kolkata
      Posted On: 16 JAN 2019 6:48PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 16, 2019.


    The Saksham - 2019 i.e. Oil and Gas Conservation awareness programme in West Bengal was formally inaugurated in Kolkata today by Dr. Ajoy Kumar Ray, Professor â€“ IIT Kharagpur, in presence of Shri Amit Kumar Basu, ED I/C IOCL, Eastern Region, Shri Amitava Majumdar, Chief GM, IOCL, WB in presence of senior officials and dealers from Oil Marketing Companies.


    The large gathering included more than 400 school students & officials from various NGOs.


    The activities of SAKSHAM-2019 include (i) bringing awareness about health hazards due to use of Bio Mass and appeal to stop use of bio mass and encourage use of greener fuels like LPG in rural areas (ii) drive to make villages and towns smoke free (iii) training of drivers of commercial vehicles (auto, taxi & trucks) for conserving diesel/CNG which will also help in reduction of the green house gases and (iv) motivation of students and youth for promoting oil and gas conservation and their contribution towards clean and green environment.
