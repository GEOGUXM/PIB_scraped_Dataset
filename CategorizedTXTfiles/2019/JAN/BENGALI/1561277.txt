      PIB holds a quiz competition on Swachhata at BITM
      Posted On: 24 JAN 2019 4:54PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 24, 2019.


    As part of Swachhta Pakhwada, a quiz competition-cum interactive session on Swachhata was held by Press Information Bureau (PIB), Kolkata at Birla Industrial and Technological Museum (BITM) in Kolkata today. Over 350 girl students from class V to X of Dasnagar Chapala Devi Balika Vidyalaya, Howrah participated in the competition.


    Welcoming the students, Smt. Jane Namchu, Addl. Director General, PIB, Kolkata urged them to bring about behavioral and attitudinal change through innovative activities so that the ultimate goals of the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan could be achieved and sustained. Shri Samrat Bandopadhyay, Dy. Director, PIB, Kolkata , conducted the quiz. Prize was also distributed among the participating students.
