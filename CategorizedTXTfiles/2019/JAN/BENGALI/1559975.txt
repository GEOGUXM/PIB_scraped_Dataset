      Ministry of External Affairs

      Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Arambagh to start
      Posted On: 15 JAN 2019 3:23PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 15, 2019.


    Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK), Arambagh is going to be inaugurated on January 21, 2019 at Arambagh Head Post Office, Hooghly.


    Applicants are requested to bring online Application Reference Number (ARN) sheets along with other necessary documents of identity proof, address and proof of date of birth (original & self attested xerox copy).


    The detail information of the documents required can be obtained from the website of the Regional Passport Office - www.passportindia.gov.in .


    Five appointments will be entertained on January 18, 2019 from 10 AM onwards and 40 appointments on daily basis from the next working day i.e. January 21, 2019. Appointments for the same will be released on the website www.passportindia.gov.in on January 17, 2019 from 3.30 PM onwards. The applicants have to pay the passport fees through online payment system.


    Only those applicants who have obtained appointments through the online mode ( www.passportindia.gov.in ) will be entertained. Tatkaal, On-hold files, walk-in and PCC applications will not be accepted and processed.
