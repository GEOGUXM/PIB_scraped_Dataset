      PM remembers Swami Vivekananda on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 12 JAN 2019 3:28PM by PIB Kolkata


    New Delhi: 12-January-2019


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has remembered Swami Vivekananda, on his Jayanti .


    “Arise, awake and stop not till the goal is reached. Remembering these powerful words, and the rich thoughts of the venerable Swami Vivekananda on his Jayanti. He emphasised on the ideals of service and renunciation. His belief in Yuva Shakti was unwavering.


    The thoughts and ideals of SwamiVivekananda inspire and energise crores of Indians, particularly our youth. It is from him that we draw the motivation of building an India that is strong, vibrant, inclusive and an India that takes global leadership in several areas”, the Prime Minister said.
