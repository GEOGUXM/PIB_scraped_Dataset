      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      PNGRB to Hold Roadshow for 10th City Gas Distribution (CGD) Bidding Round at Kolkata
      Posted On: 07 JAN 2019 2:25PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, January 07, 2019.


    Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB), the nodal agency for facilitating economic activities in natural gas distribution, is organizing a road show on January 08, 2019 for promoting 10 th City Gas Distribution (CGD) bidding round in Kolkata.


    After the success of the recently concluded ninth CGD bidding round for 86 Geographical Areas (GAs) covering 174 districts in 21 states/ UTs which attracted great interest from investors across the sector, wherein total 406 bids were received from 38 organizations (including 8 new and 2 overseas investors, this round ensures remarkable minimum work commitment of 2.2 crore domestic PNG connections, 4600 CNG stations and 1.16 lakh inch-km of steel pipeline. The 10th CGD Bidding Round offering 50 (GAs) covering 124 districts in 14 states was recently launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi on November 22, 2018 at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi.


    This road show at Kolkata on January 08, 2019 is part of the series of roadshows planned by PNGRB across India over the next few weeks before the deadline for bid submissions end on February 05, 2019. Prior to this, a road show was held at Chandigarh, Patna and Jaipur which entailed great response from all the stakeholders.


    In the ninth CGD bidding round almost all the GA’s offered were awarded by PNGRB and similar expectations can be attributed to 10 th CGD bidding round. At present, CGD authorization has been given by PNGRB in two GAs in West Bengal covering Kolkata, Howrah (part), Hoogly (part), Nadia (part), North 24 Parganas (part), South 24 Parganas (part) and Burdwan. Now, in the 10th CGD bidding round, four Gas covering eight districts- Darjeeling, Jalpaiguri, Uttar Dinajpur, Howrah (except area already authorized), Hoogly (except area already authorized), Nadia (except area already authorized), North 24 Parganas (except area already authorized) and South 24 Parganas (except areas already authorized) are being offered by PNGRB.
