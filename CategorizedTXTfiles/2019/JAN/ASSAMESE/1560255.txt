      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      2nd North East Zonal Conference Starts Today in Guwahati
      Posted On: 16 JAN 2019 4:45PM by PIB Guwahati


    The two day Second North East Zonal Conference of the media units under the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting starts today at Bapuji Bhawan of Assam Khadi and Village Industries Board Complex, Guwahati. Representatives from all the media units functioning in the 8 states of North East participated in today’s conference. The Director General of NE Zone Shri Kuldeep Singh Dhatwalia took the review of Publicity programmes and administrative matters with the media heads of the North East Zone offices.


    The publicity activities taken in the North East zone by different media units were discussed in the conference. Indian Information Service officers and representatives from Press Information Bureau and Regional Outreach Bureau of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura presented the issues and activities of their respective units. Smt. Ranjana Dev sharma, Additional Director General, Bureau of Outreach communication, New Delhi and Ms. Jane Namchu, Additional Director General, Press Information Bureau, Imphal were also present in today’s conference.


    Earlier, during the inauguration ceremony of the conference the “Swachhta Pledge” was administered by Shri Kuldeep Singh Dhatwalia,Director General to all Officers and Staff of NE Zone present in the conference to mark the inauguration of Swachhta Pakhwada from 16 th to 31 st January. “We all should join hands in this cleanliness drive and give our contribution towards making our country clean”, Shri Dhtwalia said after taking the pledge.
