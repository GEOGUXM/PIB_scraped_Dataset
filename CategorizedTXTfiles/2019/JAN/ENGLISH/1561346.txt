      Ministry of Defence

      Successful Flight Test of LRSAM
      Posted On: 24 JAN 2019 9:01PM by PIB Delhi


    Ship launched Long Range Surface-to-Air Missile (LRSAM) has been successfully test fired from INS Chennai against an incoming aerial target flying at low altitude,today. The missile destroyed the target with a direct hit. All the mission objectives have been met. LRSAM has been jointly developed by Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), India and M/s Israel Aerospace Industries (IAI), Israel for the Indian Navy.


    Raksha Mantri Smt Nirmala Sitharaman congratulated DRDO, Indian Navy and associated team members.
