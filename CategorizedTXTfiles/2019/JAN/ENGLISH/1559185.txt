      Stephen Harper, Former Prime Minister of Canada, calls on the Prime Minister
      Posted On: 08 JAN 2019 7:34PM by PIB Delhi


    Former Prime Minister of Canada, Stephen Harper, who is on a visit to India to attend the Raisina Dialogue, met Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi earlier today.


    The two leaders exchanged views on developments in India-Canada relations, main global trends and cooperation among democracies.
