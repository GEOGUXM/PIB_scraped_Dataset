      Ministry of Defence

      Gallantry Awards to Army on Republic Day 2019
      Posted On: 25 JAN 2019 4:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind has approved the award of one Ashok Chakra, two Kirti Chakras and nine Shaurya Chakras to the Indian Army personnel on the occasion of Republic Day 2019 which has been included in the list of citations.


    Following documents are attached to the Press Release :-

      * List of Complete Honours and Awards to Army.
      * Summary of Complete Honours and Awards to Army.
      * Photographs of Awardees.
      * Citations of Awardees.
