      President of India to grace the closing function of Pravasi Bharatiya Divas in Varanasi tomorrow
      Posted On: 22 JAN 2019 5:53PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Uttar Pradesh (Varanasi) tomorrow (January 23, 2019) to grace the closing function of the PravasiBharatiya Divas and to present the PravasiBharatiyaSamman Award.
