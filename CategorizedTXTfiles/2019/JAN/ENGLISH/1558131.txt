      Ministry of Defence

      Mazagaon Dock Shipbuilders
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2019 3:11PM by PIB Delhi


    Vacancy position of permanent posts in Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL) is as follows:-

      * Executive posts-173
      * Non-Executive posts- NIL

    There are some skilled workers working in Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL) for last 10 years. However, there are no permanent vacancies to be filled up as indicated at above.


    Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL) had floated an open tender on 03.01.2018 for engaging an agency to supply hands on outsourced basis. After following due commercial procedure, order was placed on the agency on 04.09.2018. In addition, Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL) has also advertised for recruiting 798 persons on fixed term contract basis.


    This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Dr. Subhash Bhamre in a written reply to Shri Arvind Sawant in Lok Sabha today.
