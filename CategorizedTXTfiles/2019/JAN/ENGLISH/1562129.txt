      Ministry of Finance

      Necessary Preventive Measures taken by National Academy of Indirect Taxes and Customs (NACIN) against Swine Flu
      Posted On: 31 JAN 2019 7:43PM by PIB Delhi


    It has been reported that several cases of Swine Flu have been detected in general in National Capital Region (NCR) with more concentration in Faridabad. Some cases of Swine Flu have also been reported in National Academy of Customs, Indirect Taxes and Narcotics (NACIN), Faridabad. Out of nine positive cases among the officer trainees, four OTs were hospitalized and all have been discharged after proper treatment. There is a declining trend in the cases of Swine Flu and no new case has been reported among the OTs after Tuesday.


    The Academy administration has taken all necessary preventive measures including treatment and fumigation of entire campus in collaboration with District Health Department and other empanelled doctors and hospitals. The OTs and staff of NACIN have been given the vaccination and medication required. All necessary logistical support is being extended for proper care.
