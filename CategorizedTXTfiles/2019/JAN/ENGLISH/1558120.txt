      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Security Personnel Martyred Combating with Terrorists
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2019 3:08PM by PIB Delhi


    The number of security personnel martyred while combating with terrorists in Kashmir and other parts of the country guarding borders or serving their duty during last three years is given below:-

                                                        
Year                  No. of security personnel martyred
                                                        
                                                        
                                                        
2016                  171                               
                                                        
                                                        
                                                        
2017                  167                               
                                                        
                                                        
                                                        
2018 (upto November)  163                               
                                                        
                                                        

    Ex-gratia/compensation is paid to families of security personnel martyred as per extant guidelines of CAPFs/ Army/State Government. There is a provision of compassionate appointment to the Next of Kin/ dependent of family member of martyred personnel. Financial assistance/ scholarship is also given to the children of martyrs for their education. Further, there is a provision for installation of Smarak (Memorial) of a martyr in school/college premises where the martyr studied.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
