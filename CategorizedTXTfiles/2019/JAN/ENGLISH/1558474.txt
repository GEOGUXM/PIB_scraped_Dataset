      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Ropeways and Cable-Cars in Hilly Areas
      Posted On: 03 JAN 2019 5:03PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government is working on probability for use of ropeways and cable-cars in hilly areas and hybrid airboats on rivers. An MoU has been signed between WAPCOS Limited and Doppelmayr, a World Leader in Ropeways.Initial discussions have been held with Government of Uttarakhand and Govt. of Himachal Pradesh. WAPCOS Limited and Ropeways & Rapid transport System Development Corporation, Govt. of Himachal Pradesh have finalised MoU for preparation for DPR & PMC for implementation of Ropeways System for Shimla &Manali Cities.


    Shri Mansukh L Mandaviya, MoS, Road Transport & Highways gave this information in reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
