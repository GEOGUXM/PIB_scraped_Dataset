      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets the nation on New Year
      Posted On: 01 JAN 2019 10:00AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has extended his New Year greetings to the nation.


    “Wishing everyone a joyous 2019! May everyone be happy and healthy. I pray that all your wishes are fulfilled in 2019”, the Prime Minister said.
