      Envoys of five nations present credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 10 JAN 2019 1:14PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassadors of Israel, Mali, Belarus, Lao PDR and Niger presented their credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (January 10, 2019).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Dr Ron Malka, Ambassador of Israel


    2. Mr Sekou Kasse, Ambassador of the Republic of Mali


    3. Mr Andrei Rzheussky, Ambassador of the Republic of Belarus
