      Ministry of Defence

      Visit of Nepal Army Chief to India
      Posted On: 11 JAN 2019 4:00PM by PIB Delhi


    General Purna Chandra Thapa, Chief of the Army Staff, Nepal Army will be visiting India from 12 to 15 January 2019 on his first official bilateral visit after taking over the command of the Nepal Army. Based on an age old tradition existing between the armies of both countries, the Nepal Army Chief is being conferred with the Honorary Rank of ‘General of Indian Army’ by the Hon’ble President of India on 12 January 2019 at Darbar Hall of Rashtrapati Bhawan.


    During the visit General Purna Chandra Thapa shall be interacting with senior Indian Army hierarchy and shall be visiting formations based at Jaipur and Lucknow. The visit symbolies the deep-rooted military to military ties and shall be a platform to further strengthen the defence cooperation between the two armies.
