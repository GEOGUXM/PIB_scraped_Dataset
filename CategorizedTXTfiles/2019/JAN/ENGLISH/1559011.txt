      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Appointment of Shri Kumar Rajesh Chandra, IPS (BH:85), DG, BCAS as DG, SSB.
      Posted On: 08 JAN 2019 10:23AM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the appointment of Shri Kumar Rajesh Chandra, IPS (BH:85), presently working as DG, BCAS to the post of DG, SSB from the date of joining of the post and upto 31.12.2021 i.e. date of his retirement on superannuation or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
