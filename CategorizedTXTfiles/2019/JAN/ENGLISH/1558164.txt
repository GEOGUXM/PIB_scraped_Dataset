      Prime Minister's Office

      PM to visit Punjab on January 3, 2019
      
      To inaugurate 106th edition of Indian Science Congress in Jalandhar

      Posted On: 02 JAN 2019 3:51PM by PIB Delhi

    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi will visit Punjab on January 3, 2019.


    He will inaugurate the 106 th edition of Indian Science Congress (ISC)-2019 on January 3 in Jalandhar, Punjab. He will also deliver the inaugural address on this occasion.Prime Minister will then proceed to Gurdaspur in Punjab, where he will address a Public Rally.
