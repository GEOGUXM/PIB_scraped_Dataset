      Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers

      Shri Chhabilendra Roul takes charge as Secretary, Department of Fertilizers
      Posted On: 03 JAN 2019 5:36PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Chhabilendra Roul has assumed the charge of Secretary, Department of Fertilizers, Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers, Government of India, with effect from 31.12.2018 (AN) vice Smt. Bharathi S. Sihag, IAS (HP:83) consequent upon her superannuation on 31.12.18 (AN).


    Shri Roul is an Indian Administrative Services Officer of 1985 batch, from Punjab Cadre.
