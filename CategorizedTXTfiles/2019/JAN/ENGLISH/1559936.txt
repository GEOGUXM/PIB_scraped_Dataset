      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Central Government Appoints 14th January, 2019 as The Date on Which Provisions of Constitution (One Hundred and Third Amendment) Act, 2019 shall come in to force
      Posted On: 14 JAN 2019 8:20PM by PIB Delhi


    In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Constitution (One Hundred and Third Amendment) Act, 2019, the Central Government hereby appoints the 14 th January, 2019 as the date on which the provisions of the said Act shall come into force.


    The Gazette Notification is enclosed herewith.
