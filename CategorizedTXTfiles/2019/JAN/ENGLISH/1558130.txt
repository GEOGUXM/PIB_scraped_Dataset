      Ministry of Home Affairs

      National Database on Sexual Offenders
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2019 3:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The Ministry of Home Affairs has launched the National Database on Sexual Offenders (NDSO) on 20 th September 2018. This online facility is exclusively for the use of law enforcement agencies having access to Inter-operable Criminal Justice System (ICJS). NDSO is a central database of sexual offenders in the country which is being maintained by the NCRB.


    As on 28 th December 2018, the NDSO has 4,93,778 records of offenders involved in sexual offences like rape, gang rape, eve teasing, stalking, child abuse etc.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
