      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people of Himachal Pradesh on their Statehood Day
      Posted On: 25 JAN 2019 12:27PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted people of Himachal Pradesh on their Statehood Day.


    "Best wishes to the people of Himachal Pradesh on their Statehood Day. May this state, the land of natural beauty and bravery, attain even more prosperity in the years to come. I pray that the people of this state are blessed with joy and good health," the Prime Minister said.
