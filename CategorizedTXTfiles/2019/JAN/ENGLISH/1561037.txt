      President of India pays homage to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose
      Posted On: 23 JAN 2019 1:15PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid homage to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (January 23, 2019). He paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Netaji at Rashtrapati Bhavan.
