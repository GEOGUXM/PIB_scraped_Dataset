      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Neutralistion of Khalistani Terror Module
      Posted On: 02 JAN 2019 3:14PM by PIB Delhi


    A total of 18 Khalistani terror modules have been neutralized in Punjab during the last two years, wherein a total of 95 accused persons have been arrested.


    As per available information, the involvement of Khalistan Liberation Force (KLF) and International Sikh Youth Federation (ISYF) have come to notice in this incident. The Punjab Police has arrested 02 persons involved in the grenade attack and has also identified 03 other persons, residing in other countries as accused, in respect to whom open dated warrants of arrest have been procured.


    This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir in written reply to a question in the Rajya Sabha today.
