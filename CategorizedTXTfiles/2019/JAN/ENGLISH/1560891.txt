      Ministry of Culture

      Red Fort, Delhi to remain closed for public and general visitors from 22nd January to 31st January, 2019
      Posted On: 22 JAN 2019 12:56PM by PIB Delhi


    As per directions of Archaeological Survey of India under the aegis of Ministry of Culture, Government of India, Red Fort, Delhi will remain closed for the Public and general visitors from the morning of 22 nd January, 2019 to 31 st January, 2019, in view of the Republic Day function and Bharat Parv.
