      Ministry of Railways

      One RPF Personnel Awarded President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service
      
      15 RPF Personnel Awarded Indian Police Medal for Meritorious Services

      Posted On: 25 JAN 2019 1:16PM by PIB Delhi

    On the occasion of Republic Day-2019, the Hon’ble President of India has awarded President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Services and Police Medal for Meritorious Services to the following Railway Protection Force (RPF)/Railway Protection Special (RPSF) officers and staff.


    President’s Police Medal (PPM) for Distinguished Service


    1. Shri P. Ponraj, Security Commissioner/Southern Railway


    Police Medal (PM) for Meritorious Service


    1. Shri T. A. Ramchandran, Assistant Security Commissioner/KRCL


    2. Shri T. Murali Krishna, Assistant Security Commissioner /South Central Railway


    3. Shri Sanjaya Kumar Swain, Assistant Security Commissioner /South East Central Railway


    4. Shri Omprakash Yadav, Head Constable /Western Railway


    5. Shri Basavraj Damodar Garag, Sub-Inspector/ Central Railway


    6. Shri Ashok Kumar Kahar , Sub-Inspector / Central Railway


    7. Shri Bhagwan Bhausingh Rane, Assistant Sub-Inspector /Central Railway


    8. Shri Rajesh Kumar Tripathi, Head Constable /Central Railway


    9. Shri Chandan Singh, Asistant Sub-Inspector /West Central Railway


    10. Shri Binod Kumar Shukla, Sub-Inspector /TC-MLY


    11. Shri Sukhdev Ghosh, Sub-Inspector / South East Central Railway


    12. Shri A. Veeranna, Inspector/South Central Railway


    13. Shri Rajesh Kumar Vishwakarma, Assistant Sub-Inspector / South East Central Railway


    14. Shri Ramesh Kumar Borkar, Head Constable / South East Central Railway


    15. Shri Dattatray V. Godlolu, Head Constable/Central Railway
