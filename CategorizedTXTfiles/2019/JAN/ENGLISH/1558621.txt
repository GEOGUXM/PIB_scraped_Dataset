      Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

      Implementation of Rashtriya Gokul Mission
      Posted On: 04 JAN 2019 4:22PM by PIB Delhi


    Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries has been implementing Rashtriya Gokul Mission with the aim of development and conservation of indigenous bovine breeds. The details of the projects approved/implemented in various States since inception of the mission is given at Annexure-I.
