      National Academy of Indian Railways (NAIR) organised its 68thFoundation Day on 31st January 2019
      Posted On: 30 JAN 2019 4:54PM by PIB Ahmedabad


    National Academy of Indian Railways, Lalbaug, Vadodara is celebrating its 68 th Foundation Day on 31 st January 2019. Prof.G. Raghuram, Director, I.I.M. Bangalore will be the Chief Guest of the function being organized under the leadership of Shri Pradeep Kumar, Director General of the academy. Function will be Presided by Shri Vinod Kumar Yadav, Chairman Railway Board, New Delhi. During the Foundation Day function, ‘Distinguished Alumnus Award’ for the year 2018 will be presented to Shri R. K. Singh, an officer of Indian Railway Service of Engineers (IRSE). Various medals and trophies will be also presented to the approx. 33 Railway officersand many other staff, who displayed their outstanding performances in various training programmes and other activities during the year 2018.
