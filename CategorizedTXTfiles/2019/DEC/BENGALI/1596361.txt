      Ministry of Railways

      DISRUPTION OF TRAIN SERVICES ON HOWRAH-KHARAGPUR SECTION
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2019 5:22PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 13, 2019


    Train services in Howrah-Kharagpur section have been disrupted due to public agitation staged at Uluberia on non-railway issue. Nearly 200 to 250 people obstructed UP & DN line at Uluberia from 15.22 hrs. today.


    As a result, the following Up & DN trains suffer ed detention:-


      * 12841 Howrah-Chennai Coromondal Express


      * 22897 Howrah-Digha Kandari Express


      * 18030 Shalimar-LTT Kurla Express


      * 18646 Hyderabad-Howrah East Coast Express


      * 12246 Yesvantpur-Howrah Duronto Express


      * 22862 Shalimar-Adra Rajya Rani Express


      * 20827 Jabalpur-Santragachi Humsafar Express


    This apart, 11 EMU local and Passenger trains were detained and controlled at various stations in the section.
