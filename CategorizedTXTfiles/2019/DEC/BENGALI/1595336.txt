      Ministry of Finance

      Stakeholder Feedback Divas on New GST Return on December, 7.
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2019 6:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 06, 2019.


    The new GST returns (Form GST ANX 1/Form GST ANX 2) will be rolled out from April, 1st, 2020. to create awareness amongst the stakeholders, Stakeholder Feedback Divas on New GST Return will be organized on December, 7, 2019 from 09: 30 am to 06:00 pm at GST Bhavan, 180, Rajdanga Main Road, Shantipally, Kolkata-700107. The offline tools of the said returns are available on trial basis which can be downloaded from www.gst.gov.in under download section. Stakeholders may actively use the trial feature and offer valuable feedback to the respective jurisdictional offices. They should participate actively in the Feedback Divas so that the feedbacks could be considered before the rollout of the new feature.
