      INDIAN STATISTICAL INSTITUTE FELICITATES SHRI PRANAB MUKHERJEE, HON’BLE FORMER PRESIDENT OF INDIA AND THE FORMER CHAIRMAN OF THE INSTITUTE
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2019 11:33AM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 11, 2019.


    Indian Statistical Institute felicitated Shri Pranab Mukherjee, Hon’ble Former President of India and the former Chairman of the Institute on 11th December, 2019 in Platinum Jubilee Auditorium of the Institute at 6 PM for being conferred with Bharat Ratna, the highest civilan award of the nation. The presence of Shri Jagdeep Dhankar, Hon’ble Governor of West Bengal and Shri Tapas Kumar Sanyal, Director General (ES), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, Government of India in this occasion has made the programme more auspicious. Padmashree Shri Bibek Debroy, Chairman, Economic Advisory Council to Prime Minister and President, Indian Statistical Institute delivered welcome address to the guests and felicitated Shri Pranab Mukherjee with a shawl, and Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay, Director, ISI presented him a memento on behalf of the Institute. In his speech Shri Mukherjee passionately memorised the contribution of Prof. Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis in Indian Statistics especially in the Second Five Year Plan of our country. He expressed his gratitude for his attachment to the Institute as a Chairman. Shri Dhankar called Shri Mukherjee a real statesman as well as a successful finance minister of world’s largest democracy. He also praised Shri Mukherjee for being deserved Bharat Ratna. Shri Tapas K. Sanyal applauded Shri Mukherjee’s contribution to our parliamentary administration and also in economic reforms of India. In this occasion, a pictorial album has also been released in the memories of his long association with the Institute. Prof. Bimal Roy, Chairman, National Statistical Commission and Prof. Goutam Mukherjee, Dean of Studies, ISI were also the eminent guests of the occasion. However, Prof. Sanghamitra Bandyopadhyay, Director of the Institute concluded the programme by delivering her vote of thanks.
