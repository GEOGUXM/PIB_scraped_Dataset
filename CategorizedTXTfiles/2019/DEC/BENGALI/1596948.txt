      Ministry of Railways

      SER TO RUN 9 PAIRS OF SUVIDHA SPECIAL TRAINS BETWEEN SANTRAGACHI AND COIMBATORE VIA KATPADI
      Posted On: 19 DEC 2019 4:38PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 19, 2019.


    With a view to clear extra rush of passengers, South Eastern Railway has decided to run nine pairs of Weekly Suvidha Special trains between Santragachi-Coimbatore-Santragachi leaving Santragachi every Wednesday from 01.01.20 to 26.02.20 and leaving Coimbatore every Friday from 03.01.20 to 28.02.20.


    80823 Santragachi-Coimbatore Special leaving Santragachi at 21.50 hrs every Wedneday will reach Coimbatore at 14.25 hrs every Friday. In the opposite direction, 80824 Coimbatore-Santragachi Weekly Special leaving Coimbatore at 21.45 hrs every Friday will arrive Santragachi at 08.45 hrs every Sunday.


    The Suvidha Special train consisting of two AC-2 Tier, four AC-3 Tier, twelve Sleeper Class and two General Second Class Coaches will have stoppages at Kharagpur, Balasore, Bhadrak, Naraj Marthapur, Bhubaneswar, Khurda Road, Brahmapur, Sompeta, Palasa, Srikakulam Road, Vizianagaram, Kottavalasa, Duvvada, Tuni, Samalkot, Rajahmundry, Eluru, Vijayawada, Tenali, Ongole, Nellore, Gudur, Sullurupeta, Perambur, Katpadi, Salem, Erode and Tiruppur stations enroute.
