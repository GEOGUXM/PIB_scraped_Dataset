      Ministry of Finance

      Taxpayers Lounge inaugurated at Kolkata for creating awareness on services of Income Tax and for ensuring better compliance
      Posted On: 14 DEC 2019 12:15PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 14, 2019


    With a objective to create awareness about various services provided by Income Tax Department for Taxpayers and also to educate about ensuring better compliance of Direct Tax laws, a Taxpayers Lounge was inaugurated by Ms Asha Agrawal, Pr. DGIT (Admin and TPS), Central Board of Direct Taxes here today at India International Mega Trade Fair. The Taxpayer’s Lounge is to also educate and create awareness among school-going children and college students who are future taxpayers. Taxpayer Lounges have been set up in the past at New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bhubaneshwar, Ahmedabad,Varanasi, Bengaluru and Patna.


    The various facilities/activities provided at the Taxpayers’ Lounge is as follows:

     1. Apply for PAN, Aadhar-PAN linking and PAN related queries.
     2. e-filing and Form 26AS related queries.
     3. Availability of services of Tax Return preparers.
     4. Taxpayer Information Series brochures on various topics, available both in e-format and paper format.
     5. Virtual Reality Game and car game to convey the importance of paying Income Tax to younger age-group visitors, in an engaging manner.
     6. Nukkad Natak, Quiz, magician and caricature artist etc. to make the public aware about the need to pay taxes and their contribution to nation building.
     7. Drawing competitions for young children accompany visiting adults.

    The Lounge will also be utilized to have feedback about the issues experienced by the taxpayers.
