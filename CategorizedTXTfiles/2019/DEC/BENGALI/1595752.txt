      TWO SER BODY BUILDERS BAG GOLD & SILVER MEDALS IN WORLD BODY BUILDING CHAMPIONSHIP AS WELL AS ASIAN BODY BUILDING CHAMPIONSHIP 2019-20
      Posted On: 10 DEC 2019 5:27PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, December 10th, 2019.


    
    
    
    Sri N Sarbo Singh and Shri Kundan Kumar Gope of South Eastern Railway won the Silver Medal in 80 Kg category and secured 4th position in 55 Kg category in the World Body Building Championship, 2019-20, held at South Korea from November 5 to 11, 2019.


    

    They have also participated and emerged champions in the Asian Body Building Championship 2019-20 held at Indonesia from September 27 to October 3, 2019. Sri N Sarbo Singh won Gold Medal in the 80 Kg category while Sri Kundan Kumar Gope won Gold Medal in the 55 Kg category.


    
    
    Sri Sanjay Kumar Mohanty, General Manager, SER felicitated Sri N Sarbo Singh and Sri Kundan Kumar Gope for their victorious achievement and encouraged them to do better in the forthcoming National & International Events. Sri J K Saha, Principal Chief Mechanical Engineer, SER & President, SER Sports Association was also present on the occasion.
