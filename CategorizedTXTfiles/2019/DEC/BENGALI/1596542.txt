      Ministry of Defence

      An "interactive session of Mukti joddhas " program was held at Fort William.
      Posted On: 15 DEC 2019 8:13PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, 15th December, 2019


    

    
    Every year, December 16th is celebrated as Vijay Diwas to commemorate the victory of the Indian Armed Forces along with Mukti-Bahini, over Pakistan. This historic military victory resulted in Pakistan ceding control of then East-Pakistan , thus paving the way for the birth of sovereign nation of Bangladesh. This day marks the culmination of an unparalleled victory in history, when India stood against the tide of times and fought valiantly for the principles of freedom and equality that helped millions of Bangldeshi people to realise their cherished dream of an independent nation. This day is celebrated as a day of remembrance of those who sacrificed their lives during the 1971 liberation war of Bangladesh. Eastern Command of army has traditionally celebrated this day as a mark of respect to the fallen heroes of the Indian Armed Forces and Mukti-Jodhas who made his victory possible.

    As part of the Vijay Diwas celebrations, today an “interactive session of Mukti joddhas "program was held at Fort William.


    
    A 70 member delegation, led by Mr Shajahan Khan Independence Award, MP, Bangladesh attended solemn occasion and shared his experience. He said Bangladesh would’nt forget the Indian contribution in the liberation of Bangladesh in 1971.


    
    Let.Gen Anil Chauhan UYSM,AVSM,SM,VSM,GOC-in-C,Eastern Comand was also present in this program. He emphasized on documantion of the Indian Army’s role in Bangladesh war history for the coming generations . Gen Shankar Roy choudhary PVSM,AVSM(retd) was also present in program and shared his experience.
