      PM salutes soldiers of Armed Forces, on Armed Forces Flag Day
      Posted On: 07 DEC 2019 10:45AM by PIB Delhi


      The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has saluted the soldiers of Armed Forces and their families on Armed Forces Flag Day.


      “On Armed Forces Flag Day we salute the indomitable courage of our forces and their families. I also urge you to contribute towards the welfare of our forces”, the Prime Minister said.
