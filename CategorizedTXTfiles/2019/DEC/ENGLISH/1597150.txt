      Ministry of Railways

      Integral Coach Factory produces 3000 Coaches in record 215 days
      Posted On: 21 DEC 2019 2:49PM by PIB Delhi


    Displaying dedication and efficiency, Integral Coach Factory (ICF) of Indian Railways has produced its 3000 th coach of the year in less than 9 months. This would help in meeting the growing demand for coaches. The number of working days to achieve the above figure has been reduced from 289 days in the last year to 215 days in the current year, a reduction of 25.6%. Till the year 2014, the same amount of time was being taken for the production of only 1000 coaches.
