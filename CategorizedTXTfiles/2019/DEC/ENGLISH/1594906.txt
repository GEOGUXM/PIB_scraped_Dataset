      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      Senior Citizens Welfare Fund
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2019 4:44PM by PIB Delhi


    A "Senior Citizens’ Welfare Fund (SCWF)” has been established under the Finance Act, 2015, to be utilized for such schemes for the promotion of the welfare of senior citizens, in line with the National Policy on Older Persons.


    Available amount with Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment under Senior Citizens Welfare Fund is Rupees 410.23 crore. The unclaimed money lying under Small Savings Schemes, Employees Provident Fund, Public Provident Fund schemes, Life and non-life insurance schemes or polices maintained by insurance companies and accounts of Coal Mines Provident Fund is transferred into Senior Citizens’ Welfare Fund.


    Annual Report for 2017-18 for Senior Citizens Welfare Fund is available. Annual Report for 2018-19 will be laid before the Inter- ministerial Committee for approval. The recommendation of financial proposals, till date, is given below .
