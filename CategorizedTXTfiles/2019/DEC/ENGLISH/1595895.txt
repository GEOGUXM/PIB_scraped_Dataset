      Ministry of Railways

      Strengthening of Railway Bridges
      Posted On: 11 DEC 2019 4:25PM by PIB Delhi


    As on 01.04.2019, there are 1,50,746 Railway Bridges on Indian Railwaysâ€™ network. There is a well-established system of inspection of railway bridges in Indian Railways. All the bridges are inspected twice a year, one before the onset of monsoon and one detailed inspection after the monsoon by the designated officials. After inspection, every bridge is assigned an Overall Rating Number (ORN) and based on the ORN of bridge Rebuilding/Strengthening is undertaken. The data are maintained Zonal Railway wise and not area-wise.
