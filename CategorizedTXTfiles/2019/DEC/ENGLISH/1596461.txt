      PM pays homage to martyrs of 2001 Parliament attack
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2019 3:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid homage to the martyrs of 2001 Parliament attack.


    “Today we pay homage to the brave personnel who sacrificed their lives while protecting our Parliament. Their martyrdom will never be forgotten ”, the Prime Minister said.
