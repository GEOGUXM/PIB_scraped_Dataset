      Ministry of Communications

      Revised RFP for selection of Agency for conducting e-Auction of Spectrum
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2019 2:07PM by PIB Delhi


    The Request for Proposal (RFP) No.1000/03/2018-WF/Auction for selection of Agency for the conduct of e-Auction, published on 26.08.2019 is cancelled due to administrative reasons.


    Notice for revised Request for Proposal (RFP) for selection of Agency for conducting e-Auction of Spectrum in various Bands has been issued by the Department of Telecommunication today.


    Further details and documents can be seen on the DoT website www.dot.gov.in . and on www.eprocure.gov.in.
