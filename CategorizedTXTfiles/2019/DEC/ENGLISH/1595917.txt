      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      Vacant Posts in CIC
      Posted On: 11 DEC 2019 4:55PM by PIB Delhi


    Applications for appointment to the post of four Information Commissioners in the Central Information Commission were called for vide advertisement dated 04-01-2019 and atotal no. of 256 applications were received by the last date of receipt of application. The process of selection of Information Commissioners involves following subsequent steps:-

     1. Tabulation of particulars of applicants and to sent to the Search Committee (constituted by Hon’ble PM) chaired by Cabinet Secretary.
     2. Shortlisted names by the Search Committee alongwith particulars of all the applicants are sent to the Committee constituted under Section 12(3) headed by the Hon’ble Prime Minister.
     3. Persons recommended by the Committee under Section 12(3) are appointed by the Hon’ble President.

    This information was provided by the Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Development of North-Eastern Region (DoNER), MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, DrJitendra Singh in written reply to a question in LokSabha today.
