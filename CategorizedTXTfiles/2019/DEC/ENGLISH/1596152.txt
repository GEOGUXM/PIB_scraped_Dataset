      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      National Programme for Youth and Adolescent Development Scheme
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2019 3:04PM by PIB Delhi


    The total funds allocated and utilized under the National Programme for Youth and Adolescent Development Scheme (NPYADS) during the last two financial years are as under:

                                                        
S. No.  Financial Year  Funds Allocated  Funds Utilized 
                                                        
                                                        
                                                        
1.      2017-18         Rs. 18.00 Crore  Rs. 17.78 Crore
                                                        
                                                        
                                                        
2.      2018-19         Rs. 25.00 Crore  Rs. 21.90 Crore
                                                        
                                                        

    Under the scheme, there is no provision for state-wise allocation of funds.


    In the last two financial years, following achievements had been made under the NPYADS:


    (i) Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Awards were conferred upon four individuals by the Hon’ble President of India on August 29, 2017.


    (ii) 5 th North East Youth Festival was organized at Gangtok, Sikkim from 28 th to 30 th October, 2017.


    (iii) 22 nd National Youth Festival was organized at Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh from 12 th to 16 th January, 2018.


    (iv) National Youth Award 2015-16 was conferred upon 23 individuals and 7 organizations during the opening ceremony of 22 nd National Youth Festival.


    (v) Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Awards were conferred upon ten individuals by the Hon’ble President of India on 25th September, 2018.


    (vi) 6 th North East Youth Festival was organized at Agartala, Tripura from 15 th to 18 th November, 2018.


    (vii) Grants had been released to various NGOs for carrying out youth related activities.


    This Information was given by the Minister of State (I/C) for Youth Affairs and Sports, Shri Kiren Rijiju in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
