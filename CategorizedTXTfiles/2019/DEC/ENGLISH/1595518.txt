      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Construction of stadiums by SAI
      Posted On: 09 DEC 2019 2:39PM by PIB Delhi


    Sports Promotional Schemes of the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports that are being implemented through Sports Authority of India (SAI) to scout and nurture talent are Khelo India Scheme, Scheme for Assistance to National Sports Federations, and releasing block grants to Sports Authority of India (SAI) for running its various training facilities across India.


    Sports Authority of India (SAI) has 10 Regional Centers, two Academic Institutions and 5 stadiums across the country as mentioned in Annexure-I.


    The details of stadiums/ hostels and regional centers under SAI where construction is under progress is at Annexure – II.
