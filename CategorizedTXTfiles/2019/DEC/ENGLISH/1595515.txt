      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Setting up new sports academies
      Posted On: 09 DEC 2019 2:38PM by PIB Delhi


    The sports academies setup by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports through Sports Authority of India (SAI) is at Annexure I . However, with effect from 20 th September, 2019, the academies at Regional Centers have been merged and now called National Center of Excellence (NCOE). Details of these NCOEs are at Annexure II .


    NCOE are fully funded by the Government. The Government provides funding for boarding/lodging, sports kits, competition exposure, medical expenses, insurance, stipend, educational expenses, sports equipments, salary of coaches and other administrative staff etc. So far, Rs. 28.77 crore have been released for these NCOEs in current financial year.
