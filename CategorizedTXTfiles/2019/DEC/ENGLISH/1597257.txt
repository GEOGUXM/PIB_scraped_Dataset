      Ministry of Defence

      Army Day Parade
      Posted On: 23 DEC 2019 6:08PM by PIB Delhi


    1. Army Day Parade will be conducted at Cariappa Parade Ground on 15 Jan 2020. In addition, a press brief for Republic Day Parade and Beating the Retreat will be conducted on 23 Jan 2020. Venue for the press brief shall be intimated later.


    2. All interested media persons are requested to forward following details for necessary security clearances to armydayparade2020@gmail.com by 01 Jan 2020 :-

     1. Full Name.
     2. PIB/ID Card No
     3. Scanned photo of ID Card
     4. Name of media Agency
     5. Photo of the applicant
     6. Applying for : ADP/Press Brief/Both

    3. For any clarification, please callup 011-23019659. Mail received after 01 Jan 2020 may not be considered.


    Col Aman Anand


    PRO (Army)
