      Ministry of Women and Child Development

      Funds Sanctioned Under the Nirbhaya Fund Scheme
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2019 1:25PM by PIB Delhi


    The State/ UT-wise details of funds sanctioned/ allocated and utilized for the projects being implemented by various Ministries/ Departments out of Nirbhaya Fund are at Annexure-I.


    The Empowered Committee in the Ministry of WCD in its meeting held on 22.10.2019 has appraised a project of Ministry of Home Affairs under Nirbhaya Fund for setting up/ strengthening of Women Help Desks in Police Stations in States/UTs at a cost of Rs. 100 crore.
