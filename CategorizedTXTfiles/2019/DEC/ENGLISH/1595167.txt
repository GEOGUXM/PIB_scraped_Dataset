      Ministry of Defence

      13 Brigs MNS work study conference held to formulate ways & means to achieve health care solutions
      Posted On: 05 DEC 2019 6:42PM by PIB Delhi


    With globalisation and growing complexity of scientific and technical advancement, nursing faces tremendous challenges. The 13 Brigs MNS work study conference was held at Army hospital (Research & Referral) here on December 5-6, 2019 to provide a platform for the stalwarts of Military Nursing Service (MNS) to formulate ways and means to achieve perfect health care solutions.


    The conference was held under the aegis of Adjutant General Lt Gen Arvind Dutta and Director General Medical Services (Army) Lt Gen R S Grewal at Army hospital (Research & Referral). The conference has been organised annually since 2006 with the aim to discuss relevant nursing issues which may have direct or indirect impact on comprehensive patient care.


    Additional Director General Military Nursing Service Maj Gen Joyce Gladys Roach, brigs from all commands and other senior military officials participated in the event.
