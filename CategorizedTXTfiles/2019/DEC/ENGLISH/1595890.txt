      Ministry of Railways

      AC-III Tier Coaches
      Posted On: 11 DEC 2019 4:22PM by PIB Delhi


    The number of AC III tier coaches in service as on 31st March, 2019 on Indian Railways is 7329.


    The number of coaches including AC-III tier coaches, in the composition of trains depends, inter-alia, on factors like traffic pattern, schedule of train etc. further, the composition of a large number of trains such as unreserved trains, Antyodaya Express Train, Day time Intercity Trains, Shatabdi Express Trains does not include AC-III Tier Class.


    The number of AC-III tier coaches manufactured by the Production Units of Indian Railways are as follows:-

                                  
Year     No of AC III tier Coaches
                                  
                                  
                                  
2014-15  488                      
                                  
                                  
                                  
2015-16  597                      
                                  
                                  
                                  
2016-17  534                      
                                  
                                  
                                  
2017-18  589                      
                                  
                                  
                                  
2018-19  885                      
                                  
                                  

    The production of AC-III tier coaches, has been scaled up in the last five years, as is evident from the above given table.


    This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
