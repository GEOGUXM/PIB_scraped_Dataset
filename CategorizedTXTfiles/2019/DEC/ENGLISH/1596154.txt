      Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

      Setting up of Sports School
      Posted On: 12 DEC 2019 3:05PM by PIB Delhi


    Under Khelo India Scheme 04 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) have been adopted as State Level Khelo India Centres (SLKICs) and 10 schools are covered under National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC) Scheme of Sports Authority of India. Details of the same are at Annexure I.
