      PM congratulates Mr. Boris Johnson on electoral victory
      Posted On: 13 DEC 2019 12:00PM by PIB Delhi


    Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Mr. Boris Johnson for winning majority in the UK polls.


    “Many congratulations to P M Boris Johnson for his return with a thumping majority. I wish him the best and look forward to working together for closer India-UK ties”, the PM said.
