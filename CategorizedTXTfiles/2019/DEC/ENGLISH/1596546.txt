      President's Secretariat

      Press Communique
      Posted On: 16 DEC 2019 9:16AM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri R.N. Ravi, Governor of Nagaland to discharge the functions of the Governor of Meghalaya, in addition to his own duties, during the absence on leave of Shri Tathagata Roy, Governor of Meghalaya.
