      Prime Minister's Office

      PM salutes Indian Navy, on the Navy Day today
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2019 1:11PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has saluted the Indian Navy personnel on the occasion of Navy Day today.


    “On Navy Day, we salute our courageous navy personnel. Their valuable service and sacrifice have made our nation stronger and safer”, the Prime Minister said.
