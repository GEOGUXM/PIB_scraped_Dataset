      PM pays tributes to Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his Jayanti
      Posted On: 25 DEC 2019 10:42AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid his tributes to Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee on his Jayanti.


    “देशवासियों के दिलों में बसे पूर्व प्रधानमंत्री भारत रत्न अटल बिहारी वाजपेयी जी को उनकी जन्म-जयंती पर कोटि-कोटि नमन।”, the Prime Minister said.
