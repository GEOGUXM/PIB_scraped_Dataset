      President of India to visit Rajasthan and Odisha from December 6 to 8
      Posted On: 05 DEC 2019 5:22PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind will visit Rajasthan and Odisha from December 6 to 8, 2019.


    On December 6, 2019, the President will visit the headquarters of Brahma Kumaris in Mount Abu and grace a National Convention on Empowerment of Women for Social Transformation.


    On December 7, 2019, the President will grace the 2 nd convocation of AIIMS, Jodhpur and also inaugurate the new building of Rajasthan High Court in Jodhpur.


    On December 8, 2019, the President will lay the foundation stone of the memorial of Paika Vidroh at Barunei Hill in Khurda, Odisha. On the same day, he will also address the closing ceremony of the platinum jubilee celebrations of the Utkal University in Bhubaneswar.
