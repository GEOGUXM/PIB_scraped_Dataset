      President of India Presents Dada Saheb Phalke Award
      Posted On: 29 DEC 2019 6:59PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, presented Dada Saheb Phalke Award to Shri Amitabh Bachchan at a function held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (December 29, 2019).
