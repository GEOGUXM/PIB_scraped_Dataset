      PIB Headquarters

      DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 30 DEC 2019 11:35AM by PIB Chennai


    Office will conduct “ DAK ADALAT” on 08.01.2020 at 11.00 AM at Anna Road, HPO, Chennai 600 002. The Members of the public are requested to send their grievances, if any, regarding postal services (money order/registered articles / savings banks / saving certificate etc.) availed at Anna Road, HPO, Chennai 600 002. They can bring their grievances either in person or send them by post or through email at doannaroadhpo.tn@indiapost.gov.in , with the caption DAK ADALAT on or before 05.01.2020.


    They may also attend the Dak Adalat on 08.01.2020 at 11.00 AM, according to a press release issued by the Chief Post Master, Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002.
