      Gold,Laptops,Cigarettes worth Rs. 16.3 lakhs seized by Customs At Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 16 DEC 2019 4:43PM by PIB Chennai


    On Wednesday one Jahufer Sadiq ,39, from Ramanathapuram who had arrived from Dubai by Fly Dubai Flight FZ 447 intercepted at the exit. On his personal search, one gold cut bit weighing 10 grams and one semi-finished crude gold chain weighing 30 grams, total 40 grams valued at Rs. 1.5 lakhs were recovered from his pant pocket. On examination of his baggage, 22 Cartons (5280 Sticks) of Gudang Garam Cigarettes worth Rs. 52000 and 17 refurbished laptops worth Rs. 85000 were recovered which were concealed in his personal effects. Further, as he appeared to be nervous, on persistent questioning he admitted to have concealed 3 bundles of gold in rubbery spread form in rectum. On extraction 345 grams of gold worth Rs. 13.45 lakhs was recovered . Thus total gold and goods valued at Rs. 16.32 lakhs were recovered and the same were seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is in progress according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai
