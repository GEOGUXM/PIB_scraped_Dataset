      Gold worth Rs. 28.63 lakhs and Foreign Currency worth Rs. 8.8 lakhs seized by Customs At Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 17 DEC 2019 3:49PM by PIB Chennai


    On Sunday night based on information one Ragumad Ali, 42, Mohamed Abdullah, 27, both from Ramanathapuram arrived from Colombo by Air India flight AI 274, were intercepted at exit. On search of their person seven bundles of gold past three and four respectively, were found concealed in rectum, were recovered. On extraction 721 grams of gold valued at Rs. 28.6 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act.


    Monday early morning based on information one Mohammed Jinna, 30, of Chennai, who was bound for Dubai by Indigo Airline flight 6E65 was intercepted after he had crossed immigration. On search of his person 10000 Saudi Riyals were recovered from his trouser pocket. On search of his hand bag 30000 Saudi Arabian Riyals and 2100 US Dollars were found concealed inside. Total currency valued at Rs. 8.88 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act 1962 read with FEMA 1999.


    Further investigation is in progress , according to a press release issued by the Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai.
