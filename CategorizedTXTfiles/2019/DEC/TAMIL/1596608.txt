      27th Convocation of Pondicherry University
      Posted On: 16 DEC 2019 4:41PM by PIB Chennai


    27 th Convocation of Pondicherry University will be held on Monday, the 23 rd December 2019 at 12.40 PM at Jawaharlal Nehru Auditorium, Dr. B. R.Ambedkar Administrative Building.


    His Excellency the President of India and Visitor of Pondicherry University Shri. Ram Nath Kovind has kindly consented to be the Chief Guest and deliver the Convocation Address.


    Dr.KiranBedi, Honourable Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry, Shri V. Narayanasamy, Honourable Chief Minister of Puducherry, Shri. M. O. H. F.Shahjahan, Honourable Revenue Minister, Government of Puducherry, Honourable Shri R.Kamalakannan, Honourable Education Minister, Government of Puducherry, Shri N.Gokulakrishnan, Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha, Puducherry and Shri. Ve.Vaithilingam, Honourable Member of Parliament, LokSabha, Puducherry will be the guests of honour.


    Professor Gurmeet Singh, Vice Chancellor, Pondicherry University will preside over the Convocation, according to a press release issued by Dr.B.Chithra, Registrar, Pondicherry University.
