      Gold worth Rs. 15 lakhs seized by Customs At Chennai Airport
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2019 11:33AM by PIB Chennai


    On Tuesday one Parthiban,22, from Cuddalore and one Jabarullah Khan, 35, from Pudukkottai arrived from Sharjah by Air India Flight AI 968 and by Srilankan Airline flight UL 121 at the exit. On personal search of Parthiban semi finished crude gold chain weighing 190 gms valued at Rs.7.5 lakhs was recovered which was kept concealed in his trouser pocket. Jabarullah Khan appeared to be nervous and on questioning, if he had anything to declare he gave evasive reply. On persistent questioning, he admitted to have concealed 2 bundles of gold in rubbery spread which was recovered from rectum. On extraction 195 grams of gold valued at Rs. 7.70 lakhs was recovered.


    Total 385 grams of gold valued at Rs.15.2 lakhs was recovered and the same were seized under Customs Act 1962.


    Further investigation is in progress according to the press release issued by Commissioner of Customs, Anna International Airport, Chennai.
