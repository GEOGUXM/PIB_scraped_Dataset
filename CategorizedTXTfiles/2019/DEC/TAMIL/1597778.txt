      Junior Engineer (Civil. Electrical. Mechanical and quantity surveying & Contractors) / Combined Graduate Level (Tier-III) Examinations 2018
      Posted On: 27 DEC 2019 12:48PM by PIB Chennai


    Staff Selection Commission will conduct paper II (Descriptive Type) of Junior Engineer (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical and Quantity Surveying & Contracts) Examination 2018 on 29.12.2019 in Single Session from 02.00 pm to 04.00 pm. For Locomotor disability (LD) with dominant writing extremity affected candidates, Examination will be held from 02.00 pm to 04.40 pm.


    In Southern Region, 1007 candidates have been found qualified in Paper 1 for appearing in Paper II of the Examination.


    As like above, Staff Selection Commission will also conduct Combined Graduate Level (Tier-III) in pen and paper mode (Descriptive type) on 29.12.2019 in Single session from 11.00 am to 12.00 noon. For visually Handicapped / Cerebral Palsy / Locomotor Disability with dominant writing extremity affected candidates, Examination will be held from 11.00 am to 12.20 pm.


    Both the above said examinations will be held at 8 centres / cities viz Kurnool, Tirupati, Vijayawada and Vishakhapatnam for Andhra Pradesh; Hyderabad for Telangana; Chennai, Coimbatore and Madurai for Tamil Nadu.


    Southern Regional Office has already provided the facility to download e-Admission Certificate from its website (sscsr.gov.in) from 23.12.2019. These details are also intimated to the candidates through SMS to the phone numbers and e-mail to the mail IDs provided by the candidates in their Online application.


    PROHIBITED ITEM such as Smart / Digital watches, books, Paper chits, Magazines, Electronic gadgets (mobile phones, Blue tooth devices, head phones, Pen / buttonhole cameras, scanner, calculator, storage devices etc.,) are STRICTLY NOT ALLOWED in the examination hall. If any such item is found in the possession of a candidate in the examination hall, his/her candidature liable to be cancelled and legal / criminal proceedings could be initiated against him/her. He/she would also liable to be debarred from appearing in future examinations of the Commission for a period of 3-5 years.


    Therefore, candidates are strictly advised not to bring prohibited items and bags into the Examination Venue


    Candidates are requested to note that they shall not be permitted to take up the examination without e-Admission Certificate and Original valid identity proof having Date of Birth as enumerated in the Instructions. All the candidates are, therefore, requested to download their e-Admission Certificate without fail.
