      1.1 Kg Gold worthRs. 45 lakhs seized by Customs at Chennai Airport.
      Posted On: 30 DEC 2019 6:30PM by PIB Chennai


    On Monday morning one Dakshayini, 28, of Thanjavur who arrived from Dubai by Air India flight AI 906 was intercepted at exit of arrival hall. On personal search, one long semi finished crude chain weighing 238 grams of 24K purity worth Rs. 9.3 lakhs was found concealed in her under garments. The same recovered and seized under Customs Act.


    On Sunday night, two Srilankan nationals, Marappuli Hewawasalage,33, and Bandula Vijeweera,38, who arrived by Air India Flight AI 274 from Colombo ,were intercepted at exit of arrival hall. On their personal search 7 bundles containing gold in paste form were found concealed in rectum. On extraction 920 grams of gold valued at Rs. 36 lakhs was recovered and seized under Customs Act.


    Total 1.158Kg gold valued at Rs. 45.35 lakhs was recovered and seized under Custom Act 1962.


    Further investigation is under progress, according to press released issued by Commissioner of Customs, Chennai International Airport.
