      Visiting National Police Memorial should be on everyone's agenda when they visit Delhi: MoS, Home Affairs, G. Kishan Reddy
      Posted On: 08 DEC 2019 6:31PM by PIB Mumbai


    National P olice M emorial in Delhi should be on everyone's agenda when they visit Delhi , as it is a proud place for all of us to remember the martyrs who sacrificed their lives for us ; w e are planning to build a police memorial in every district, said Minister of State for Home Affairs , Government of India, Shri G. Kishan Reddy in Pune today. The Minister said this, while speaking to m edia , upon the conclusion of All India Conference of Director Generals / Inspector Generals of Police - 2019 at IISER , Pune. Earlier, Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi and Home Minister Shri Amit Shah addressed the conference.


    Th e Police Memorial will help improve the image of police personnel , who keep toiling 320 days in a year, without worrying about their families and relations while performing their duties. We should not discredit the entire p olice f orce for individual cases, we will try to modernise the police and impart more training to police force s , Shri Reddy added.


    Minister of State for Home Affairs , Government of India, Shri Nityanand Rai was also present.


    On the occasion, Shri Reddy and Shri Rai paid homage at Hutatma Smarak , followed by visit to Maharashtra Police Museum, at Centre for Police Research , Pune .
