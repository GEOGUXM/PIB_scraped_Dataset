      Ministry of Finance

      CBIC reaches out to BSE members on SABKA VISHWAS Scheme
      
      Legacy Dispute Resolution Scheme is open till 31st December 2019

      Posted On: 23 DEC 2019 6:11PM by PIB Mumbai

    Directorate General of Taxpayer Services, Mumbai Zonal Unit, Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs organized a Symposium on SABKA VISHWAS (Legacy Dispute Resolution) Scheme, (SVLDRS) 2019 on 23 rd December, 2019 at 4.00 pm to 5.00 pm at BSE Auditorium, 1 st Floor, BSE, Fort, Mumbai, for the members of Bombay Stock Exchange.


    The said Symposium was held to explain to the participants about SVLDRS Scheme, 2019 along with the eligibility, benefits and procedural aspects of the Scheme. This Scheme is a one time measure for liquidation of past disputes relating to Central Excise and Services Tax matters.


    The benefits include total waiver of interest, penalty and fine; immunity from prosecution; and relief in duty demand ranging from 40% to 70% with a provision for adjustment of any deposit of duty already made. In case of voluntary disclosure, only payment of full amount of disclosed duty is required to be made without any interest or penalty. June 30, 2019 is taken as cut-off date with respect to all pending cases. SVLDRS Scheme is open till December 31, 2019. The Commissioner, CGST, Mumbai South and DG Systems too attended the Symposium.
