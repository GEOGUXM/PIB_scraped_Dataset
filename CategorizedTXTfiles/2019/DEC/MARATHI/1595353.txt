      Ministry of Home Affairs

      Union Home Minister Amit Shah arrives in Pune
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2019 6:31PM by PIB Mumbai


    Union Home Minister Shri. Amit Shah has arrived in Pune to attend a National Conference of Director Generals of Police.


    

    
    The minister arrived at Lohgaon Airport by an Indian Air Force aircraft today. Air Commodore Rahul Bhasin, Director of Intelligence Bureau Arvind Kumar, Director General of Police Subodh Kumar Jaiswal, Commissioner of Police Dr. K. Venkatesham, Deputy Collector (Protocol) Amrut Natekar and Yogesh Gogavale, Jayant Yerwadekar were present.
