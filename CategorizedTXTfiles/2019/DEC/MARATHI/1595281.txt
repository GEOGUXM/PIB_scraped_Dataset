      Ministry of Communications

      Postal Pension Adalat to be held in Mumbai on February 20, 2020
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2019 4:37PM by PIB Mumbai


    The 46th Postal Pension Adalat for pensioners/family pensioners of Postal Department will be held on February 20, 2020 (Thursday) at 11 a.m. in the Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai-400 001.


    The Postal Pension Adalat will take up cases related to grievances regarding pensionary benefits pertaining to those who retired/died while in service from Department of Posts, which have not been settled within 3 months. Pensioners of Postal Department in Maharashtra and Goa States may appeal in this Pension Adalat. Cases involving purely legal points i.e. succession etc. and Grievances involving policy matters will not be considered in Pension Adalat. Pensioner can apply in the Proforma given below to Shri S.U.Pantawne, Sr. Accounts Officer/Secretary, Pension Adalat, O/o Chief Postmaster General, Maharashtra Circle, Mumbai GPO Bldg., 2 nd Floor, Mumbai-400 001 on or before January 10, 2020 . The applications received after 10 th January 2020 will not be considered in the Pension Adalat.
