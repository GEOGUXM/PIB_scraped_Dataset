      NAVY WEEK EVENTS AT HYDERABAD – DEC 2019
      Posted On: 03 DEC 2019 6:36PM by PIB Hyderabad


    India celebrates Navy Day on 04 Dec every year to commemorate the daring and successful attack on Karachi harbour by the Vidyut class Missile Boats in 'Operation Trident' during the Indo-Pak war of 1971.


    As a part of Navy Day celebrations, various events are organised across all naval stations in the country to constructively interact with veteran service personnel and citizens.


    NAVY WEEK 2019 – CALENDAR


     1. Wreath Laying 04 Dec at Parade Ground (Secunderabad)


     1. Veterans Personnel Interaction 04 Dec at Navy House, Bolarum


     1. Indian Navy Band Concert 08 Dec (Sunday) at IKEA, Hi-Tech City (6-7 PM)


    This event is Free Entry for Public
