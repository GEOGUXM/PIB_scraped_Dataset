      DR. AMBEDKARMAHAPARINIRVAN DIWAS-PHOTO EXHIBITIONORGANISED BY ROB ATCGO TOWERS
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2019 7:02PM by PIB Hyderabad


    A photo exhibition on Dr. Ambedkar has been organised today by Regional Outreach Bureau, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, at CGO Towers, Kavadiguda, on the occasion of Dr. Ambedkar Mahaparinirvan Diwas.


    
    Shri S.Venkateshwar, Director General, Press Information Bureau, Hyderabad, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, inaugurated the photo exhibition, and remembered his services to the nation on his 63rd death anniversary.


    The photo exhibition show cases rare black and white photographs of Dr Ambedkar .


    Shri P.Ratnakar,Dy Director,PIB, Dr Manas Krishnakant, Asst Director, and other officials of ROB also participated
