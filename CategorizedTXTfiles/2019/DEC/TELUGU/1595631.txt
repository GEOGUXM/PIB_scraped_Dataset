      VICE CHANCELLOR’S CONFERENCE HELD IN MEXICO FROM 30 NOV TO 02 DEC EFLU VC SURESH KUMAR PARTICIPATED IN THE CONFERENCE
      Posted On: 09 DEC 2019 6:38PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Prof. E Suresh Kumar, Vice Chancellor, The English and Foreign Languages University (EFLU) participated in the Vice Chancellor’s Conference in Mexico from 30 November to 02 December 2019. The Conference was a joint initiative by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and the National Association of Universities and Higher Education Institutions of Mexico (ANUIES).


    The aim of the conference is to provide a common platform to the dedicated group of Vice Chancellors to share experiences on innovations and to expand ideas and solutions to the problems that the participant countries are facing. The conference is intended to facilitate international academic collaborations between the universities of the two nations.


    On this occasion, The English and Foreign Languages University signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Autonomous University of Sinaloa (AUS), Mexico. Through this partnership, the universities will collaborate in academic, research, student and faculty exchange programmes. The MoU facilitates organizing of national and international symposia, conferences, specialized exhibitions and joint research projects.


    Prof. Suresh Kumar visited San José State University, San José; and Stanford University, Stanford, California in the United States to explore new trends in teaching and academic research. Prof. Kumar emphasised the importance of global partnerships in order to improve the quality of academics and to provide greater opportunities to the students and teachers of the University.
