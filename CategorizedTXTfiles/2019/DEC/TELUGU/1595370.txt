      REGIONAL LEVEL DAK ADALAT ONDECEMBER 19TH
      Posted On: 06 DEC 2019 7:00PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Department of Posts, Office of the Chief Postmaster General Head Quarter Region, Hyderabad is conducting ‘Dak Adalat’ on December 19, 2019 to hear the complaints and grievances of the public relating to postal services. The Adalat will be held at 03.00 PM at the Conference Hall, 2 nd Floor, Dak Sadan, Abids, Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Headquarters Region, Hyderabad – 500 001.


    The complaints and grievances of public from Telangana State may be sent in a postal envelop or cover, duly super scribed with the words Dak Adalat’ and addressed to Shri Santhosh Netha. K, Asst. Postmaster General, (FS & BD), Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Telangana Circle, Hyderabad – 500001. The last date for receiving complaints and grievances is December 12, 2019, according to a press note issued by the Asst. Postmaster General, O/o CPMG, Telangana Circle, Hyderabad.
