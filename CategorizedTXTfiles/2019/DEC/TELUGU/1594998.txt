      SSC TO CONDUCT EXAM FOR SUB INSPECTOR IN DELHIPOLICE, CISF
      Posted On: 04 DEC 2019 6:48PM by PIB Hyderabad

     1. Selection Commission will be conducting Sub-Inspector in Delhi Police, CAPFs and Assistant Sub- Inspector in CISF Examination, 2019 (Paper-I) in Computer Based Mode.

    In Southern Region, 51615 candidates have been admitted to appear in the examination. The examination will be conducted on 9th, 11th & 12th December, 2019 in 2 Shifts from 10.00 AM to 12.00 NOON and 02.00 PM to 04.00 PM at 21 Venues located in 11 Centers / Cities viz. Guntur, Kurnool, Rajahmundry, Tirupati, Vijayawada & Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh; Hyderabad & Warangal in Telangana and Chennai, Coimbatore & Madurai in Tamil Nadu.
