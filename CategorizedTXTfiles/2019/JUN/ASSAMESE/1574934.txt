      Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

      Yoga Shivir to be organised on International Yoga Day in SoochnaBhawan, Guwahati
      Posted On: 19 JUN 2019 1:22PM by PIB Guwahati


    On the occasion of the 5 th International Day of Yoga on June 21, 2019, the Regional Outreach Bureau under the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is going to organise a Yoga Shivir in the premises of Soochna Bhawan, Assam Khadi & Village Industries Board Complex in Guwahati. There will be special programmes to celebrate the Yoga Day. The theme of the International Day of Yoga this year is “Yoga for Heart”.


    A Yoga Teacher from Poorvanchal Yoga College, Maligaon along with Shri Pranab Kumar Nath, Field Publicity Officer, Dibrugarh, will demonstrate and lead the session on Yoga and meditation from 8 AM onwards. The session will also include interactions on various physical ailments and its management of office-goers and students.


    An exhibition on International Yoga Day will also be organised along with the Yoga session and it will be open to everyone.


    The 5 th International Yoga Day will be celebrated across the World on June 21, 2019. The officials from the Ministry of I&B and students from various colleges in Guwahati will be present in the event.


    The aim is to make Yoga an integral part of every one’s lifestyle and thereby to secure a healthy life.
