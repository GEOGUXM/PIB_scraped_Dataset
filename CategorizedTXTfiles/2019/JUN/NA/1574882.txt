      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC Approves extension of Shri Alok Vardhan Chaturvedi
      Posted On: 18 JUN 2019 6:56PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the extension in the central deputation tenure of Shri Alok Vardhan Chaturvedi, lAS (BH:86), Director General (DG), Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) beyond 18.06.2019 upto 30.09.2019 i.e. the date of his superannuation or until further orders whichever is earlier.
