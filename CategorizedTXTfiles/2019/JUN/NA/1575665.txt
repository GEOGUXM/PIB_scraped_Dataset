      Ministry of Tourism

      Several tourism projects have been sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes for Uttarakhand
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2019 6:58PM by PIB Delhi


    Several tourism projects have been sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan and Prashad Schemes for the State of Uttarakhand.

    Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of thematic tourist circuits in the country. The projects under the scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.
