      Ministry of Defence

      Raksha Mantri meets COAS, VCOAS and Army Commanders
      Posted On: 14 JUN 2019 8:01PM by PIB Delhi


    Hon'ble Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh met General Bipin Rawat, Chief of the Army Staff, Lieutenant General Devraj Anbu, Vice Chief of the Army Staff and all the GOC-in-Cs today.


    Hon'ble Raksha Mantri complimented Indian Army for ensuring the highest level of professionalism, the continuous readiness against external and internal challenges and their role in Nation building.


    Hon'ble Raksha Mantri during the meeting said that "it is matter of great pride that Indian Army is ready for full spectrum of challenges and it will be ensured that their capabilities keep getting continuously enhanced. Jointmanship of the Indian Armed Forces will be a key area and due endeavours towards the same will be ensured. Ministry of Defence will remain fully committed to speedily enhance the capabilities of Indian Armed Forces. Ensuring high morale & motivation of all ranks will be priority."


    Hon'ble Raksha Mantri wished all present success and glory.


    Lt Col Mohit Vaishnava
