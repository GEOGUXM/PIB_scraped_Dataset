      DAVP PHOTO EXHIBITION ON YOGA AT JUBILEE BUS STATION
      Posted On: 20 JUN 2019 6:23PM by PIB Hyderabad


    On the occasion International Yoga Day, Regional Outreach Bureau (ROB) under the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is organising a 3-day photo exhibition and awareness camp on yoga at Jubilee Bus Station, Secunderabad. According to press note by ROB, Director, Sri. M. Devendra, Artistes of Song & Drama Division (S & DD) will be performing cultural programmes at the venue.


    Prizes will be distributed to winners of various competitions organised by ROB in schools and colleges for the past one week.
