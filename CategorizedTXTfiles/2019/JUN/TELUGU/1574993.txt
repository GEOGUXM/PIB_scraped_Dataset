      REGIONAL LEVEL DAK ADALAT ON JULY 11
      Posted On: 19 JUN 2019 6:44PM by PIB Hyderabad


    Department of Posts, Office of the Chief Postmaster General Head Quarter Region, Hyderabad is conducting ‘2 nd Dak Adalat’ on July 11, 2019 to hear the complaints and grievances of the public relating to postal services. The Adalat will be held at 11.00 a.m. at the Conference Hall, 2 nd Floor, Dak Sadan, Abids, Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Headquarters Region, Hyderabad – 500 001.


    The complaints and grievances from the public of Districts of Hyderabad, Ranga Reddy, Malkajgiri, Sangareddy, Medak and Siddipet may be sent in a postal envelop or cover, duly super scribed with the words ‘2 nd Dak Adalat’ and addressed to Shri K. Jaya Raju, Assistant Director – II, O/o the Postmaster General, Headquarter Region, Hyderabad – 500001. The last date for receiving complaints and grievances is July 5, 2019, according to a press note issued by Asst. Director-II, O/o the Postmaster General, HQ Region, Hyderabad.
