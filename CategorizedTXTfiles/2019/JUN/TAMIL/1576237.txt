      “PF near you” to be held on July 10th
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2019 5:31PM by PIB Chennai


    Regional Office of the Employees Provident Fund Organisation is conducting “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” (PF Near You) the 10 th July, 2019 on Wednesday. Time slot for Subscribers/Members/Pensioners is 10.30 A.M. to 1.00 P.M., Employers is 3.00 P.M. to 4.00 P.M. and for Exempted establishments located in jurisdiction is 4.00 P.M. to 5.00 P.M.


    The Subscribers/Employers who desire to get their long pending unresolved grievances redressed or have any other suggestions on improvement of service/organisation, they may register their names with the Public Relations Officer duly furnishing their PF code Number/Account Number, nature of grievance etc on or before 3 rd of July 2019. The aforesaid timings are only for guidance and the employers may also present their grievance, if any, earlier during the day. “Nidhi Aapke Nikat” will take place at No.37, Royapettah High Road, Chennai 600 014 according to Press Release issued by Shri. Salil Sankar, Regional Provident Fund Commissioner-1, Regional Office, Chennai-1.
