      DAK ADALAT
      Posted On: 17 JUN 2019 5:35PM by PIB Chennai


    DAK ADALAT will be conducted on 27.06.2019 at 1600 hours at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002. The members of the Public are requested to send their grievances, if any regarding Postal services (Money order / Registered Articles / Savings Bank / Savings Certificate etc.,) availed at Anna Road HPO, Chennai 600 002, either in person or by post or through email at doannaroadhpo, tn@indiapost.govt.in . with the caption DAK ADALAT, on or before 24.06.2019. They may also attend the Dak Adalat in person on 27.06.2019 at 1600 hours according to press release issued by Shri.K. Venkatachalam, Chief Postmaster, Anna Road HO.
