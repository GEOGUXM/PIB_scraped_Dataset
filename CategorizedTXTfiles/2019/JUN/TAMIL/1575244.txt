      BSNL offers attractive ABHINANDAN – 151 Plan Voucher
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 5:56PM by PIB Chennai


    Government Telecom Company Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited has launched a very attractive plan voucher ABHINANDAN – 151 as promotional offer for a period 90 days for its prepaid mobile customers.


    Rs. 151 Plan offers unlimited calls to any network while in roaming including Delhi & Mumbai. This plan also offers free 1GB data per day & 100 SMS per day on any network with a validity of 24 days. The Plan voucher will be applicable for new connection, migration & validity extension with freebies.


    Sh. Sheetla Prasad, Director (CM), BSNL informed that BSNL is committed to offer attractive mobile plans & services at affordable price to its customers.
