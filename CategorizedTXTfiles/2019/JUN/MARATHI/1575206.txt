      Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

      International Yoga Day celebrated at AYJNISHD(D)
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 4:36PM by PIB Mumbai


    Ali Yavar Jung National Institute of Speech and Hearing Disabilities (Divyangjan), AYJNISHD (D) one of the apex National Institutes working for the rehabilitation of persons with speech and hearing disabilities across India under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Govt. of India, celebrated the International Day of Yoga today. The Yoga day was observed at the Institute campus in Mumbai today, along with the staff, students, parents and children with speech and hearing disabilities as participants in the two sessions conducted by experts from ‘Patanjali Yog Samiti’ and ‘My Home India’. Participants received guided instructions from experts in terms of Yoga performance for their day-to-day practices so as to improve their health and quality of life.
