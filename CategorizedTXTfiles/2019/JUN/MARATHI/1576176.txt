      Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation

      Statistics Day by National Statistical Office Nagpur on 29 June
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2019 4:09PM by PIB Mumbai


    National Statistical Office(Field Operations Division), Regional Office, Nagpur working under Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, is jointly organising Statistics Day with Data Quality Assurance Division, Nagpur on 29 June 2019 Saturday, at 10 A.M. at Data Quality Assurance Division (DQAD), CGO Complex, 'B' Block, 7th Floor, Seminary Hills, Nagpur. The Theme of the Statistics Day is "Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs)".


    In Recognition of the notable contributions made by late Professor Prashant Chandra Mahalnobis in the field of Economic Planning and Statistics system, the Government of India has designated his birth day 29th June as the Statistics Day in the category of Special days to be celebrated every year at the National level.


    13 th Statistical Day will be celebrated under the chairmanship of Shri Anil Patil, Deputy Director General, DQAD, Nagpur in presence of Chief Guests Smt. Shalini Bhoyar, Director General(Retd.), Shri T.K. Basu, Additional Director General(Retd.), Prof. Vinayak Deshpande, Dean, Faculty of Commerce of Management and Registrar I/C RashtraSant Tukodji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur and Shri Srinivas Uppala, Director, Regional Office, Nagpur.


    Quiz Competition and Crossword Puzzle Competition also organised on this occassion.
