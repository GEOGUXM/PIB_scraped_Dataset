      Sahitya Akademi Organises Kathasandhi With Eminent Novelist Tapan Bandyopadhyay
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2019 2:07PM by PIB Kolkata


    Kolkata, June 28, 2019


    Sahitya Akademi organised a programme for eminent fiction writer Shri Tapan Bandyopadhyay, where he read out excerpts from his novels ‘Nadi Mati Aranya’, ‘Malabkoushik’ and ‘Dvairath’ on Thursday (June 27, 2019). The name of the programme is Kathasandhi.


    Shri Bandyopadhyay even recollected his early days and shared his experiences as a novelist as he said, a novel takes nearly five to seven years to complete. The author interacted with the audience also.


    Shri Bandyopadhyay has so far published more than 50 novels including 10 for children and juveniles, nearly 300 hundred short stories and eight books of poems.
