      Ministry of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises

      Arvind Sawant takes charge as Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Minister
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2019 12:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises, Arvind Sawant being welcome by Heavy Industries Secretary, Asha Ram Sihag.


    Arvind Sawant, Union Minister of Heavy Industry and Public Enterprises assumed office today in the Ministry.


    Speaking on this occasion the Minster said that both the departments of the Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises are main contributors to employment generation in the country. He further said that he will make all efforts to re-generate and re-develop the sick industries under the Ministry and fulfil the main objective of the Government which is creation of jobs.


    The Minister was welcomed by Heavy Industries, Secretary, Public Enterprises Secretary, Seema Bahuguna, senior officers of both Departments and Public Enterprises.
