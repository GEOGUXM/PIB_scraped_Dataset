      Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

      PNG Minister Shri Dharmendra Pradhan holds telephonic discussion with Saudi Minister of Energy, Industry and Mineral Resources H.E. Dr. Khalid Al-Falih.
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 9:54PM by PIB Delhi


    Minister of Petroleum & Natural Gas and Steel Shri Dharmendra Pradhan today had a telephonic conversation with H.E. Dr. Khalid Al-Falih, Minister of Energy, Industry & Mineral Resources, Saudi Arabia and discussed about further strengthening cooperation in the hydrocarbon sector to enhance the strategic partnership that exists between India and Saudi Arabia. They discussed about the recent geo-political developments impacting crude oil prices. Shri Pradhan, referring to the developments in the Strait of Hormuz, conveyed his concern on rising crude oil prices. He also reiterated the sensitivity of Indian consumers to prevailing crude oil price volatility. Shri Pradhan has sought active role of Saudi Arabia within OPEC and in the OPEC Plus discussions for keeping oil prices at a reasonable level.


    The Ministers also discussed about Saudi interests in India’s energy sector to further bolster bilateral ties. Shri Pradhan also reviewed the progress of Saudi investments in India’s refining & petrochemical sectors. They agreed to expedite the implementation of various initiatives to transform the hydrocarbon engagement from a buyer-seller one into a comprehensive one.
