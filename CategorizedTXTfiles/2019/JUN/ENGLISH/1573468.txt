      Prime Minister's Office

      PM greets people on Eid
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2019 9:43AM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has conveyed his greetings on the festival of Eid.


    “May this special day ignite the spirit of harmony, compassion and peace in our society.May everyone be blessed with happiness”, the Prime Minister said.
