      Cabinet approves Intimation of Letter of Intent between India and Costa Rica in the field of Biotechnology
      Posted On: 12 JUN 2019 8:15PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet, chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, was apprised of signing of Lotter of Intent (LOI) between India and Costa Rica in the field of Biotechnology.


    The LOI has been signed to work out the agenda for the collaboration for Innovation in Science & Technology Diplomacy to evolve a concrete strategic plan in the area of biotechnology education, training and research.


    The LOI was signed in March, 2019.
