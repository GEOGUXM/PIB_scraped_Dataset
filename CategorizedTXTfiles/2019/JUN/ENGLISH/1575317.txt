      NITI Aayog

      PM interacts with economists and experts on the theme “Economic Policy – The Road Ahead”
      
      Groups make presentations on key economic themes
      Posted On: 22 JUN 2019 7:00PM by PIB Delhi


    The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today attended an interactive session with over 40 economists and other experts, organized by NITI Aayog, on the theme “Economic Policy – The Road Ahead.”


    During the session, participants shared their views, in five distinct groups, on the economic themes of macro-economy and employment, agriculture and water resources, exports, education, and health.


    In his intervention, the Prime Minister thanked various participants for their suggestions and observations, on various aspects of the economy.


    The meeting was also attended by Union Ministers Shri Piyush Goyal and Shri Rao Inderjeet Singh. The Vice Chairman NITI Aayog Shri Rajiv Kumar, and senior officers from the Union Government and NITI Aayog were also present.
