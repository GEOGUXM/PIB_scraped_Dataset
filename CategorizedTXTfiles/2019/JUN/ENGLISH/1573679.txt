      Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions

      ACC approves extension in service to Shri P. K. Sinha as Cabinet Secretary for three months
      Posted On: 07 JUN 2019 6:06PM by PIB Delhi


    The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved extension in service to


    Shri Pradeep Kumar Sinha, IAS (UP:1977) as Cabinet Secretary for a further period of three months beyond 12.06.2019 or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
