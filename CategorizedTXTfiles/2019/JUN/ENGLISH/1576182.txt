      Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution

      Grievances against Misleading Advertisements
      Posted On: 28 JUN 2019 4:19PM by PIB Delhi


    The Government has launched an online portal called “Grievance Against Misleading Advertisements” (GAMA) where complaints relating to misleading advertisements can be lodged.


    During the period between 01.04.2018 and 31.03.2019, a total 3884 complaints have been received on GAMA portal. Out of these, 2235 have been resolved and 1040 were rejected being incomplete or not pertaining to misleading advertisement.


    The focus is on seven sectors viz. food, agriculture, health, education, real estate, transport and financial services.


    This information was given in a written reply by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri. Danve Raosaheb Dadarao in the Rajya Sabha today
