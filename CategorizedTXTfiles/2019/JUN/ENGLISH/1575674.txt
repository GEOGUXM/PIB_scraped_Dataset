      President's Secretariat

      PRESS COMMUNIQUE
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2019 7:17PM by PIB Delhi


    The President of India is pleased to appoint Shri Padmanabha Balkrishna Acharya, Governor of Nagaland to discharge the functions of the Governor of Manipur, in addition to his own duties, during the absence, on leave, of Dr Najma A. Heptulla, Governor of Manipur.
