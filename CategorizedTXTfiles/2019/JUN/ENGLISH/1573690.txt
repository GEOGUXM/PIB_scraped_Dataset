      Ministry of Defence

      Spectacular show Thrills Parents at OTA, Gaya
      Posted On: 07 JUN 2019 6:47PM by PIB Delhi


    As a celebratory prelude to the Passing out Parade on 08 June 2019, a Multi Activity Display showcasing extraordinary military skills and breathtakingly gripping performances was held at Officers’ Training Academy, Gaya.


    Lt Gen MM Naravane, General Officer Commanding-in- Chief, Eastern Command was the Chief Guest at the event. The spellbound spectators also included proud parents and relatives of Gentlemen Cadets from the Passing Out Course, guests, school children and distinguished civilian dignitaries.


    Enthusiastic and energetic Cadets demonstrated very high standards of physical fitness, mental robustness and a rare spirit of adventure. PT event was a mesmerising and professional display of agility and physical strength by the Gentlemen Cadets. Other events included grand Equestrian Display by the Gentlemen Cadets, Fly Past by Micro-light Aircraft, Sky Diving, ‘Kalaripayattu’ Martial Arts Display, Gymnastics Display by ‘North East Warriors’ and an impressive Band Performance.
