      Top stars expected to join the campaign for environment protection
      
      Prakash Javadekar to plant sapling in premises of Films Division in Mumbai tomorrow
      
      Posted On: 05 JUN 2019 2:02PM by PIB Delhi


    Union Minister Shri Prakash Javadekar will carry forth the drive to spread awareness about environment protection through the newly launched #SelfieWithSapling campaign by planting a sapling in the premises of Films Division in Mu mbai tomorrow. He will also take a tour of the National Museum of Indian Cinema, which was inaugurated by the Honâ€™ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi earlier this year in January. During the visit, he will also be interacting with top stars from the Film Industry to exhort them to take the campaign forward in order to spread the message of sustainability, importance of reduction of carbon footprint and significance of environment protection for future generations.


    Earlier today, on the occasion of World Environment Day, Shri Javadekar planted a sapling in the premises of Paryavaran Bhawan in New Delhi, where he was joined by ace cricketer Shri Kapil dev, renowned actors Shri Jackie Shroff and Shri Randeep Hooda and famous folk singer Ms. Malini Awasthi.
