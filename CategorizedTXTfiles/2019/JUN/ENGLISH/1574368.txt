      Ministry of Power

      Shri RK Singh meets Sikkim Chief Minister; assures him all possible support for the development of power sector in Sikkim
      Posted On: 13 JUN 2019 3:48PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Prem Singh Tamang, the Chief Minister of Sikkim called on Shri RK Singh, Minister of State for Power and New & Renewable Energy (IC) and Skill Development & Entrepreneurship in Delhi today. Shri Tamang congratulated Shri Singh on being appointed as the Minister for the second term. He sought support and cooperation from the new Government for the development of Sikkim.


    Shri RK Singh assured all possible support for the development of power and renewable energy sector in the State. He also requested support of the Sikkim Government for various hydro projects under implementation by various central public sector undertakings.
