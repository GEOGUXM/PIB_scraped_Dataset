      Ministry of Electronics & IT

      Cabinet approves signing of Memorandum of Understanding between India and Finland in the area of cyber security
      Posted On: 12 JUN 2019 8:14PM by PIB Delhi


    The Union Cabinet, chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, was apprised of a Memorandum of Understanding on the cooperation in the area of Cyber Security between India and Finland.


    The MoU intends to promote closer cooperation for exchange of knowledge and experience in detection, resolution and prevention of security related incidents between India and Finland.


    The MoU was signed in January, 2019.
