      Envoys of Four Nations Present Credentials to President of India
      Posted On: 25 JUN 2019 12:51PM by PIB Delhi


    Ambassadors of Ethiopia, Tajikistan, Philippines and Nicaragua presented credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (June 25, 2019).


    The envoys who presented their credentials were: -


    1. Dr Tizita Mulugeta, Ambassador of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia


    2. Mr Rahimzoda Sulton, Ambassador of the Republic of Tajikistan


    3. Mr Ramon S. Bagatsing, Jr., Ambassador of the Republic of Philippines


    4. Mr Rodrigo Coronel Kinloch, Ambassador of the Republic of Nicaragua
