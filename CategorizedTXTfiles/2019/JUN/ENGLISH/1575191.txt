      Ministry of Railways

      Indian Railways Observes the International Day of Yoga 2019
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 4:23PM by PIB Delhi


    Indian Railways organised the International Day of Yoga today on 21st June, 2019 in Rail Bhavan, all Zonal Railway Headquarters, Divisional Railway Offices, Production Units and Railway Training Institutes and Centres, and all other Railway establishments.


    The Union Minister for Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal participated in the Yoga session along with Union Minister of Law & Justice, Communications, Electronics & Information Technology, Shri Ravi Shankar Prasad on the occasion of International Day of Yoga at Lodhi Garden, New Delhi.


    Speaking on this occasion, Union Minister, Shri Piyush Goyal said, “I congratulate the countrymen on the occasion of International Yoga Day. Hon’ble Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi’s initiative of adopting 21 st June as Yoga Day got its support from United Nations and 21 st June is recognised as International Yoga Day across the world. Hon’ble Prime Minister has asserted that we have to cure illness as well as we need to focus on wellness of countrymen. Emphasis is on preventive healthcare. By practicising Yoga, one can remain healthy and fit without incurring any cost. I think Yoga is beneficial for the society at large.”


    Minister of State of Railways, Shri Suresh C. Angadi participated in the Yoga session on the occasion of International Day of Yoga at Paschim Vihar, New Delhi.


    Speaking on this occasion, Shri Angadi said that Yoga provides a Healthy body, stable mind, spirit of oneness. On this day I urge everyone to make yoga an integral part of your lives.


    A Yoga session was organized at Officers Club in New Delhi which was attended by Railway Board Members and senior officials of Railways.
