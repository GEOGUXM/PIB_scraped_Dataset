      Ministry of Culture

      Shri Prahlad Singh Patel inaugurates Exhibition ‘Unknown Masterpieces of Himachal Folk Art' in the national capital today
      Posted On: 07 JUN 2019 7:02PM by PIB Delhi


    The Minister of State (I/c) of Culture & Tourism, Govt. of India ShriPrahaladSinghPatel, inaugurated the exhibition titled "Unknown Masterpieces of Himachal Folk Art”, at National Museum, new Delhi, today. The event was graced by Shri Akhilesh Mis h ra, Director General, ICCR, B.N Aryan, Director, Home of Tribal Folk Art, Gurugram among other dignitaries.


    Inaugurating the exhibition, Shri Prahlad Singh Patel, said that Folk Art and Folk Culture is integral to the survival of any society and that under the guidance and leadership of Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, his Ministry will strive to preserve and promote the same.


    The Exhibition is being jointly organised by the National Museum, New Delhi and Home of Folk Art (Museum of Tribal, Folk and Neglected Art), Gurugram in which more than 240 artefacts are on display. Out of this,230 objects belong to the personal lifetime collection of Late K.C. Aryan, Home of Folk Art, Gurugram. The exhibition has been Curated by Mr. B.N. Aryan, Director, Home of Folk Art, Gurugram.


    The exhibition will remain open for public viewing till 31 st July, 2019, from 10.00 AM to 6.00 PM (Tuesday to Friday) and 10.00 AM to 8.00 PM( Satuday & Sunday), Except on Mondays and National Holidays.
