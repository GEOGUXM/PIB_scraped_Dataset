      Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

      Upgradation of Medical Colleges in Kerala
      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 3:03PM by PIB Delhi


    Following 4 Government Medical Colleges in Kerala have been approved for upgradation under different phases of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY):

     1. Government Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram under Phase-I at the cost of Rs. 120 crore (Central share: Rs. 100 crore; State share: Rs.20 crore).

     1. Govt. Medical College, Kozhikode under Phase-III at the cost of Rs. 195.93 crore (Central share: Rs. 120 crore; State share: Rs.75.93 crore).


     1. T. D. Government Medical College, Alapuzzha under Phase-III at the cost of Rs. 173.18 crore (Central share: Rs. 120 crore; State share: Rs.53.18 crore).


     1. Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences & Technology under Phase-V(A) at the cost of Rs. 230 crore (MoHFW share: Rs. 120 crore; DST share: Rs.110 crore).


    The Minister of State (Health and Family Welfare), Sh Ashwini Kumar Choubey stated this in a written reply in the Lok Sabha here today.
