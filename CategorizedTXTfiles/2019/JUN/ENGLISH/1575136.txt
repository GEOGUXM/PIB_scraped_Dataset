      Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

      International Yoga Day 2019 celebrated in Parliament
      
      Yoga is a conscious process of gaining mastery over the mind: Shri Pralhad Joshi

      Posted On: 21 JUN 2019 2:07PM by PIB Delhi

    International Yoga Day 2019celebrated in the Parliament premises, here today.


    Union Minister for Parliamentary Affairs, Coal and Mines, Shri Pralhad Joshi participated in the yoga event organised by Lok Sabha Secretariat, along with Hon’ble Speaker,Shri Om Birla, other Ministers and MPs.


    Shri Joshi quoted Maharshee Patanjaliand said that, “Yoga is a conscious process of gaining mastery over the mind”.
