      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Nitin Gadkari Assumes Charge of Ministry of Road Transport and Highways
      Posted On: 04 JUN 2019 12:21PM by PIB Delhi


    Shri Nitin Gadkari took charge of the office of the Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways in New Delhi today. He also holds the portfolio of the Union Minister for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.


    Shri Gadkari was Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways, Shipping, Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation in the previous government.


    Shri Gadkari is a member of Lok Sabha since May, 2014. He was earlier a member of the Maharashtra Legislative Council during 1989-2014. He was Minister for PWD in Maharashtra Government during 1995 to 1999.
