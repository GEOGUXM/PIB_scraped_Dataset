      Ministry of Road Transport & Highways

      Gen V K Singh (Retd) takes over as Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways
      Posted On: 03 JUN 2019 5:36PM by PIB Delhi


      General V K Singh (Retd) took charge of the office of the Union Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways in New Delhi today. He was Union Minister of State for External Affairs in the previous government.


      Speaking to media, Gen Singh said he will take forward the good work done by the ministry. He said holistic development of road infrastructure in the country will be given top priority.


      General V K Singh is a member of Lok Sabha since May, 2014.
